Rule 13.91. Roll call; record votes required

           Any motion to report an instrument and any motion to adopt or reject a substantive committee amendment to which an objection is offered shall be decided by a roll call vote of the committee members. Any other vote shall be by roll call if demanded by a member. All roll call votes shall be record votes and shall appear in the records of the committee, as otherwise provided in these rules. In the case of record votes, the names of members voting for the motion, the names of members voting against the motion, and the names of absent members shall be recorded. For the purposes of this rule, a substantive amendment shall be deemed to be any amendment which is not strictly technical in nature.
