AND SEXUAL HARASSMENT
Rule 18.1. Statement of policy regarding equal employment opportunities
The Louisiana Senate seeks to aggressively recruit a highly productive and diverse
workforce, and will provide equal employment opportunities to all employees and
prospective employees. Decisions regarding the employment, assignment, reassignment,
and compensation of individuals shall be made without discriminatory regard based upon
race, gender, national origin, disability or age.
