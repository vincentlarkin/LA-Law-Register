Rule 5.7. Designation of or referral to certain former senators

           A. A former senator may be designated or referred to as "Senator Retired, District No." followed by the number of the Senatorial District represented.

           B. "Former senator" as used in this Rule, means a former senator who served three terms in the Senate and, because of this service, was prohibited by Article III, Section 4(E) of the Constitution of Louisiana from being elected to the Senate for the succeeding term.

           Added by SR 45 of 2019 RS.
