Rule 10.8. First and second reading of bills
A. Upon introduction, in the case of bills and joint resolutions originating in the
Senate, and after receipt in the Senate, in the case of House instruments, each bill or joint
resolution shall be read at least by title for the first time. This reading shall be for
information. Each such bill or joint resolution then shall be placed on the calendar for its
second reading in its regular order.
B. On the next legislative day, each such bill and joint resolution shall be read a
second time and referred to committee.
