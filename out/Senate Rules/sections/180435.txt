Rule 13.67. Interim studies
If an interim study is being undertaken by a committee on its own initiative, no
organizational meeting shall be held until all requirements of Senate Rule 13.12 have
been met.  Notice of the commencement of the interim studies shall be transmitted to all
members of the legislature, as provided in Senate Rule 13.13.
