Rule 13.68. Presession hearings of instruments

Each committee to which prefiled legislative instruments have been referred shall meet to consider such instruments upon call of the chairman or upon request of a majority of the members of the committee, but only after the requirements of Senate Rule 13.12 and Chapter 9 of these rules have been met.  Notice shall be transmitted as provided in this Part and in Senate Rule 13.74.
