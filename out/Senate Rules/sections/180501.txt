CHAPTER 2.  OATH OF OFFICE
Rule 2.1.  Members elected to full term; judging qualifications and elections; oath
Members of the Senate shall meet in the Senate Chamber at the state capitol at 10
a.m. on the second Monday in January after their election every four years and, after
judging the qualifications and elections of the members required by Article III, Section 7
of the constitution, shall take the oath or affirmation of office prescribed by the
constitution in open Senate.
