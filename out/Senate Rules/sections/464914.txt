Rule 8.4. Late approval of Non-governmental Entity Funding Request
Pursuant to Joint Rule No. 17 of the Joint Rules of the Senate and House of
Representatives, Non-governmental Entity Funding Requests submitted after November
first and not approved at a meeting of the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget or
the Joint Legislative Committee on Capital Outlay prior to the last day for introduction of
a matter intended to have the effect of law by either house of the legislature, may be
approved by the chairman of the Senate Committee on Finance for requests with respect
to bills over which the committee has jurisdiction and by the chairman of the Senate
Committee on Revenue and Fiscal Affairs for requests with respect to which that
committee has jurisdiction.
