CHAPTER 8.  AMENDMENTS

Rule 8.1. Amendments; how considered; order of consideration

A. Only one set of proposed amendments to a legislative instrument shall be considered by the Senate at any one time.

B. Proposed floor amendments shall be considered in the order in which they are received for consideration, except that amendments that have been received prior to the consideration of the instrument that are proposed by the lead author of a Senate instrument or by the Senate member handling a House instrument shall be considered prior to other proposed floor amendments.

Amended by SR 168 of 2014 RS, eff. August 1, 2014.
