Rule 7.9.  Reengrossment
A. Before a Senate bill finally passed by the Senate is sent to the House, it shall be
reengrossed to incorporate any floor amendments adopted by the Senate.
B. Before a Senate concurrent resolution finally adopted by the Senate with
amendments is sent to the House, it shall be engrossed or reengrossed to incorporate all of
the amendments.
