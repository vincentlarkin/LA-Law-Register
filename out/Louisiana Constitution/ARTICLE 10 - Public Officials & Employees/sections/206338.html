§51.  Appropriations

Section 51.  The legislature shall make adequate annual appropriations to the State Police Commission to enable it to implement this Part efficiently and effectively.  The amount so appropriated shall not be subject to veto by the governor.

Added by Acts 1990, No. 1106, §1, approved Oct. 6, 1990, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
