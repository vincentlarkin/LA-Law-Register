§16.  Public Hospitals
Section 16.  Notwithstanding any provision of this Article to the contrary, the legislature may provide by law for the supervision, operation, and management of public hospitals and their programs by the Board of Regents or by any board having powers of management over public institutions of higher education created by this constitution or pursuant to this Article.  Such laws may include but shall not be limited to laws providing for the submission and approval of capital and operating budgets, appropriations and expenditures, the supervision, management, and oversight of the hospitals and their programs, and legislative review and disapproval of related rules.  This Section shall not apply to institutions and programs operated or managed prior to January 1, 1997, by any higher education management board created by this Article.
Acts 1997, No. 1488, §1, approved Oct. 3, 1998, eff. Nov. 5, 1998.
