§6.  Use of Monies, Goods, or Services for Conduct of Elections; Prohibitions
Section 6.  No funds, goods, or services donated by a foreign government or a
nongovernmental source shall be used to conduct elections unless provided for in the election
code and subject to restrictions provided by general law.
Acts 2023, No. 200, §1, approved Oct. 14, 2023, eff. Nov. 20, 2023.
