§4.1.  Cigarette Tax Rates
Section 4.1.  To ensure revenue for the dedication provided for in Article VII, Section
10.8(C)(2)(c) of this constitution, the rate of the tax levied pursuant to R.S. 47:841(B)(3)
shall not be less than the rate set forth in that provision as it exists on January 1, 2012.
Acts 2011, No. 423, §1, approved Oct. 22, 2011, eff. Nov. 21, 2011.
