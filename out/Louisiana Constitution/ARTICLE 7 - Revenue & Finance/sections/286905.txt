§10.12.  Farmers and fishermen assistance programs; Agricultural and Seafood Products
Support Fund
(A)  The legislature is authorized to provide by law for programs to assist Louisiana
farmers and fishermen with support and expansion of their industries.
B, C.  Repealed by Acts 2023, No. 199, §1, eff. Dec. 21, 2023.
Added by Acts 2004, No. 928, §1, approved Nov. 2, 2004, eff. Dec. 7, 2004; Acts
2023, No. 199,§1, approved Nov. 18, 2023, eff. Dec. 21, 2023.
