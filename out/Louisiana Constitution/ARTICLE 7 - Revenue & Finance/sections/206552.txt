§22.  No Impairment of Existing Taxes or Obligations
Section 22.  This Part shall not be applied in a manner which will (a) invalidate taxes
authorized and imposed prior to the effective date of this constitution or (b) impair the
obligations, validity, or security of any bonds or other debt obligations authorized prior to
the effective date of this constitution.
