§17.  Legislation to Obtain Federal Aid
Section 17.  The legislature may enact laws to enable the state, its agencies, boards,
commissions, and political subdivisions and their agencies to comply with federal laws and
regulations in order to secure federal participation in funding capital improvement projects.
