Art. 575.1.  Suspension of time limitations; crimes against the Firefighters' Retirement
System
The periods of limitation established by this Chapter shall be suspended when a civil
suit is filed as provided in R.S. 11:2261.1(C).
Acts 2018, No. 115, §2.
