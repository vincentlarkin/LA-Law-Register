Art. 337.  Interruption of the period for obtaining a bond forfeiture judgment
An appearance by the defendant shall interrupt the period for obtaining a bond
forfeiture judgment. An appearance by the defendant does not relieve the surety of its bail
undertaking obligations.
Amended by Acts 1981, No. 218, §1; Acts 1987, No. 728, §1; Acts 1993, No. 834,
§1, eff. June 22, 1993; Acts 2016, No. 613, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
