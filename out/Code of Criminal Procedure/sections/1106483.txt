Art. 573.3.  Running of time limitations; exception; crimes against the Firefighters'
Retirement System
The time limitations established by this Chapter shall not commence to run as to a
crime described in R.S. 11:2261.1(C) and committed against the Firefighters' Retirement
System until the crime is discovered by the Firefighters' Retirement System.
Acts 2018, No. 115, §2.
