TITLE XVII  TIME LIMITATIONS
CHAPTER 1.  LIMITATIONS UPON INSTITUTION OF PROSECUTION
Art. 571.  Crimes for which there is no time limitation
There is no time limitation upon the institution of prosecution for any crime for which
the punishment may be death or life imprisonment or for the crime of forcible or second
degree rape (R.S. 14:42.1) or molestation of a juvenile or a person with a physical or mental
disability (R.S. 14:81.2).
Amended by Acts 1984, No. 926, §1; Acts 2001, No. 207, §1; Acts 2015, No. 184,
§6; Acts 2024, No. 698, §1.
