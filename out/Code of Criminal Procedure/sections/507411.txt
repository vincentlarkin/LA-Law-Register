Art. 25.1.  Appointment of interpreter
A. The court shall appoint an interpreter in accordance with the Code of Evidence and
the Rules of the Louisiana Supreme Court for any person who is a party or witness upon a
determination that the person is a limited English proficient or deaf individual.
B. The cost of providing a qualified court interpreter shall be paid out of the
appropriate court fund.
Acts 2008, No. 882, §2; Acts 2024, No. 32, §2.
