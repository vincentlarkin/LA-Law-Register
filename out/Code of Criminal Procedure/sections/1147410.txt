Art. 1003.1.  Public records; exception
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, any records held by the sheriff
or any other law enforcement agency pursuant to this Title shall be confidential and shall not
be considered a public record pursuant to the Public Records Law.
Acts 2019, No. 427, §3.
