Art. 981.  Judgment granting motion to expunge a record of arrest or conviction; execution
A judgment ordering expungement of  a record of arrest or of conviction of a
misdemeanor or felony offense shall be served as provided for in Article 982 of this Code.
The judgment shall not affect any persons or other entities set forth in Article 979 or 982 of
this Code who have not been served with the motion and judgment ordering the
expungement of a record.
Acts 2014, No. 145, §1.
