Art. 888.  Costs and fines; payment
Costs and any fine imposed shall be payable immediately except as provided in
Article 875.1 relative to the determination of the defendant's ability to pay; provided,
however, that in cases involving the violation of any traffic law or ordinance, the court
having jurisdiction may grant the defendant five judicial days after rendition of judgment to
pay any costs and any fine imposed.
Amended by Acts 1968, No. 368, §1; Acts 2017, No. 260, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 2018; Acts
2018, No. 137, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 2018; Acts 2018, No. 668, §4, eff. Aug. 1, 2018; Acts 2019,
No. 253, §2; Acts 2021, No. 313, §§3A and 5B.
