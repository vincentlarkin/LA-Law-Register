CHAPTER 5.  JOINDER RULES

Art. 490.  Counts

Counts are charges of crime joined in the same indictment.  Recitals in one count may be incorporated in subsequent counts by means of a clear and distinct reference.  The conclusion of the indictment applies to all counts stated therein.
