Art. 342.  Enforcement of judgment
After the delay for filing a suspensive appeal has elapsed or when a judgment
becomes final and definitive, the prosecuting attorney may file a rule to show cause in
accordance with R.S. 22:1441 or collect the judgment in the same manner as a civil
judgment.
Acts 1993, No. 834, §1, eff. June 22, 1993; Acts 1999, No. 677, §1; Acts 2010, No.
914, §1; Acts 2016, No. 613, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
