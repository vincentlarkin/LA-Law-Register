Art. 673.  Judge may act until recused
A judge has full power and authority to act, even though a ground for recusal exists,
until he is recused, or a motion for his recusal is filed.  The judge to whom the motion to
recuse is assigned shall have full power and authority to act in the cause pending the
disposition of the motion to recuse.
Acts 2010, No. 262, §2; Acts 2022, No. 42, §1.
