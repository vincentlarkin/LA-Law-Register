Art. 341.  Appeals
The rights of appeal of a bail undertaking forfeiture judgment shall be governed by
the Code of Civil Procedure Article 2081 et seq.
Acts 1986, No. 696, §1; Acts 1993, No. 834, §1, eff. June 22, 1993; Acts 2016, No.
613, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
