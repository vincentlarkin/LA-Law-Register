Rule 5.9.  Tobacco products

No person shall use any tobacco product, including without limitation any cigar, cigarette, smokeless tobacco, or smoking tobacco, in any space within the Louisiana State Capitol allocated to the House of Representatives.

HR 11, 1998; HR 69, 2008, eff. May 19, 2008.
