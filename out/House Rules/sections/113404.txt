Rule 9.8.  Motion to lay on table
A.  The motion to lay on the table shall be decided without debate.
B.  The motion to lay on the table shall be in order in accordance with this Chapter
on any matter or motion in the possession of the House regardless of the house of origin.
Mason's Manual:  Sec. 337(2)
HR 3, 1973; HR 97, 2016, eff. June 1, 2016.
