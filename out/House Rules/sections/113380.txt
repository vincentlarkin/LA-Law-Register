Rule 8.24.  Information required for consideration of appropriations bills; concurrences and
conference committee reports
Notwithstanding any other rule to the contrary, the additional information required
pursuant to Article III, Section 16(F) of the Constitution of Louisiana for purposes of
adopting a conference committee report for or concurring in Senate amendments to an
appropriations bill shall be as provided in Joint Rule 22.
HR 342, 2025, eff. June 10, 2025.
