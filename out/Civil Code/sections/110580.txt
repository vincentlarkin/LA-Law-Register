Art. 362.  Persons subject to interdiction
Persons subject to interdiction, in accordance with the provisions of Title IX  of this
Book, remain subject to interdiction as provided in Articles 389  through 399 and  any other
applicable laws.
Acts 1966, No. 496, §2; Acts 2024, No. 123, §1.
