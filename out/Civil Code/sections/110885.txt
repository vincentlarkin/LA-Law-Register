Art. 691.  Constructions
The owner of the enclosed estate may construct on the right-of-way the type of road,
railroad, or other works reasonably necessary for the exercise of the right of passage.
Acts 1977, No. 514, §1; Acts 2012, No. 739, §1; Acts 2025, No. 27, §1.
