Art. 223.  Rights and obligations of parental authority
Parental authority includes rights and obligations of physical care, supervision,
protection, discipline, and instruction of the child.
Acts 1986, No. 303, §1; Acts 2015, No. 260, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
