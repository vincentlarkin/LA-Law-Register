Art. 783.  Matters of interpretation and application
Doubt as to the existence, validity, or extent of building restrictions is resolved in
favor of the unrestricted use of the immovable.  The provisions of the Louisiana
Condominium Act, the Louisiana Timesharing Act, and the Louisiana Homeowners
Association Act shall supersede any and all provisions of this Title in the event of a conflict.

Art. 783.  Matters of interpretation and application
Doubt as to the existence, validity, or extent of building restrictions is resolved in
favor of the unrestricted use of the immovable.  The provisions of the Louisiana
Condominium Act, the Louisiana Timesharing Act, and the Louisiana Planned Community
Act shall supersede any and all provisions of this Title in the event of a conflict.
