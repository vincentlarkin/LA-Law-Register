Art. 696.  Indemnity
A.  The owner of an enclosed estate having a right of passage is bound to indemnify
the owner of the servient estate for any damage caused by the exercise of the right of passage.
B.  The right to demand indemnity against the owner of the enclosed estate may
become barred by prescription. The accrual of prescription has no effect on the right of
passage.
Acts 1977, No. 514, §1; Acts 2025, No. 27, §1.
