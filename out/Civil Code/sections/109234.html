Art. 1981. Rights of beneficiary and stipulator

            The stipulation gives the third-party beneficiary the right to demand performance from the promisor.

            Also the stipulator, for the benefit of the third-party beneficiary, may demand performance from the promisor.

            Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
