CHAPTER 2--RIGHTS IN THINGS

Art. 476.  Rights in things.

One may have various rights in things:

1.  Ownership;

2.  Personal and predial servitudes; and

3.  Such other real rights as the law allows.

Acts 1978, No. 728, §1.
