Art. 690.  Extent of passage
The right of passage for the benefit of an enclosed estate shall be suitable for the kind
of traffic that is reasonably necessary for the use of that estate.
Acts 1977, No. 514, §1; Acts 2012, No. 739, §1; Acts 2025, No. 27, §1.
