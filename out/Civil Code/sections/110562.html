Art. 3541.  Other juridical acts and quasi-contractual obligations

Unless otherwise provided by the law of this state, the law applicable to juridical acts other than contracts and to quasi-contractual obligations is determined in accordance with the principles of this Title.

Acts 1991, No. 923, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
