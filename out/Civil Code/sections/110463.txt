Art. 3440.  Protection of precarious possession
Where there is a disturbance of possession, the possessory action is available to a
precarious possessor, such as a lessee, against anyone except the person for whom he
possesses.
Acts 1982, No. 187, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983; Acts 2023, No. 421, §1.
