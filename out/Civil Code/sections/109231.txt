Art. 1979.  Revocation
The stipulation may be revoked only by the stipulator and only before the third-party
beneficiary has manifested his intention of availing himself of the benefit.
If the promisor has an interest in performing, however, the stipulation may not be
revoked without his consent.
Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
