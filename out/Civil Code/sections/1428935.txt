BOOK IV.  CONFLICT OF LAWS
TITLE I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
Art. 3514.  Multistate cases
Unless otherwise expressly provided by the law of this state, cases having contacts
with other states are governed by the law selected in accordance with the provisions of this
Book.
Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
