Art. 3493.2.  Damage to immovable property; commencement and accrual of prescription
When damage is caused to immovable property, the two-year prescription
commences to run from the day that the owner of the immovable acquired, or should have
acquired, knowledge of the damage.
Acts 2024, No. 423, §1, eff. July 1, 2024.
