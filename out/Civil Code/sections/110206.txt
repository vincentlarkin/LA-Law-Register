Art. 3157.  Indivisibility of pledge
The contract of pledge is indivisible, notwithstanding the divisibility of the secured
obligations, and the pledgor may not demand return of all or part of the thing pledged until
all secured obligations have been extinguished.
Acts 2014, No. 281, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
