Art. 226.  Parental obligation of direction
Parents have a moral obligation to provide moral, social, and material direction for
their child.
Amended by Acts 1952, No. 265, §1; Acts 1985, No. 714, §1; Acts 2015, No. 260,
§1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
