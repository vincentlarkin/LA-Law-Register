SECTION 4.  REMISSION OF DEBT
Art. 1888.  Express or tacit remission
A remission of debt by an obligee extinguishes the obligation.  That remission may be express or tacit.
Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
