SECTION 11--OF THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE TUTOR

Art. 336.  Alienation of minor's immovables.

The prohibition of alienating the immovables of a minor, does not extend to the case in which a judgment is to be executed against him, or of a licitation made at the instance of the coheir, or other coproprietor.

Acts 1966, No. 496, §1.
