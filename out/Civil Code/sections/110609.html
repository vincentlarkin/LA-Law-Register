CHAPTER 3.  EFFECTS OF INTERDICTION

Art. 394.  Pre-interdiction juridical acts

Interdiction does not affect the validity of a juridical act made by the interdict prior to the effective date of interdiction.

Acts 1997, No. 1117, §1; Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 25, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
