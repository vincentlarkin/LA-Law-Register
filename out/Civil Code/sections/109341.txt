SECTION 3.  OBLIGATIONS OF CHILDREN
Art. 227.  Parental control
A child owes assistance to his parents and may not quit a family residence without
the consent of both parents, except as otherwise provided by law.
Acts 2015, No. 260, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
