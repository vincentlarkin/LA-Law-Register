Art. 1859.  Performance in violation of seizure

A performance rendered to an obligee in violation of a seizure is not valid against the seizing creditor who, according to his right, may force the obligor to perform again.

In that case, the obligor may recover the first performance from the obligee.

Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
