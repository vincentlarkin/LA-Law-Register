Art. 2315.3. Additional damages; child pornography; child sexual abuse materials

            In addition to general and special damages, exemplary damages may be awarded upon proof that the injuries on which the action is based were caused by a wanton and reckless disregard for the rights and safety of the person through an act of child sexual abuse materials, as defined by R.S. 14:81.1, regardless of whether the defendant was prosecuted for his acts.

            Acts 2009, No. 382, §1; Acts 2025, No. 261, §7.
