Art. 1890.  Remission effective when communication is received by the obligor

A remission of debt is effective when the obligor receives the communication from the obligee.  Acceptance of a remission is always presumed unless the obligor rejects the remission within a reasonable time.

Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
