CHAPTER 6.  OBJECT AND MATTER OF CONTRACTS

Art. 1971.  Freedom of parties

Parties are free to contract for any object that is lawful, possible, and determined or determinable.

Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
