CHAPTER 6--DISSOLUTION, LIQUIDATION, AND

DIVISION OF ASSETS

Art. 2832.  Creditors of the partnership; preference.

The creditors of the partnership must be paid in preference to the creditors of the partners.

Acts 1980, No. 150, §1.
