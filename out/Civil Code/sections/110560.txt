SECTION 12 - OF CONTINUING TUTORSHIP OF
PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES
Art. 354.  Placing under continuing tutorship
Certain children with disabilities may be placed under full or limited continuing
tutorship in accordance with the following rules and the provisions of the Code of Civil
Procedure.
Added by Acts 1966, No. 496, §2; Acts 2014, No. 811, §30, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts
2024, No. 123, §1.
