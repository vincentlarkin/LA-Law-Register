Art. 3101.  Capacity of parties; authority of mandataries, tutors and curators.

They who cannot bind themselves cannot make a submission.  An attorney in fact cannot make a submission without a special power.

The tutors of minors and the curators of persons interdicted or absent, cannot do it without being authorized by the judge.

Amended by Acts 1979, No. 711, §1.
