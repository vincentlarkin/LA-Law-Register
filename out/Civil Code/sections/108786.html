Art. 1429.  Rights and obligations of income interest in trust not superseded

This Chapter does not supersede the provisions of the Trust Code governing the rights and obligations of an income interest in trust with respect to payment of estate debts.

Acts 1997, No. 1421, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
