SECTION 2--TO WHOM THE COLLATION IS DUE, AND

WHAT THINGS ARE SUBJECT TO IT

Art. 1242.  Collation; succession of donor.

The collation is made only to the succession of the donor.

Amended by Acts 1980, No. 565, §4.
