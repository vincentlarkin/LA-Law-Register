Art. 360.  Parents' rights of administration
In addition to the rights of tutorship, the parents shall retain, during the marriage and
during the minority of the child under full or limited continuing tutorship, all rights of
administration granted to parents of children not under continuing tutorship during their
minority.
Acts 1966, No. 496, §2; Acts 2014, No. 811, §30, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts 2024, No.
123, §1.
