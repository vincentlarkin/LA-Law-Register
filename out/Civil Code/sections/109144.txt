Art. 1899.  Rights acquired by third persons
Compensation can neither take place nor may it be renounced to the prejudice of
rights previously acquired by third persons.
Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
