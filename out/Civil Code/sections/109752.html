SECTION 4.  ALTERATIONS, REPAIRS, AND ADDITIONS

Art. 2690.  Alterations by the lessor prohibited

During the lease, the lessor may not make any alterations in the thing.

Acts 2004, No. 821, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2005.
