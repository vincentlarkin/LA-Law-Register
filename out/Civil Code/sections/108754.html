CHAPTER 2. INTERPRETATION OF LAWS

Art. 14. Use of gender

            Unless the context indicates otherwise, words used with reference to one gender apply to other genders.

            Acts 1991, No. 923, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
