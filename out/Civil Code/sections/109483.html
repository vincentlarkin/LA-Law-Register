Art. 2442. Recordation of sale of immovable to affect third persons

            The parties to an act of sale or promise of sale of immovable property are bound from the time that the act is made, but such an act is not effective against third persons until it is filed for registry according to the laws of registry.

            Acts 1993, No. 841, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1995; Acts 2005, No. 169, §2, eff. July 1, 2006; Acts 2005, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 13, §1, eff. Nov. 29, 2005; Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
