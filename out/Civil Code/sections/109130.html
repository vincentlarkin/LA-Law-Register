Art. 1886.  Delegation of performance

A delegation of performance by an obligor to a third person is effective when that person binds himself to perform.

A delegation effects a novation only when the obligee expressly discharges the original obligor.

Acts 1984, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
