Art. 1348.  Continuances of proceedings.

As the business of partitions sometimes requires several days, the officer may divide his proces verbal, and make as many vacations or sittings as he thinks proper.

Amended by Acts 1960, No. 30, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
