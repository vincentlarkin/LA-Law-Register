TITLE XX–SECURITY
Art. 3133.  Liability of an obligor for his obligations
Whoever is personally bound for an obligation is obligated to fulfill it out of all of
his property, movable and immovable, present and future.
Acts 2014, No. 281, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
