Art. 2813.  The right of a partner to obtain information.

A partner may inform himself of the business activities of the partnership and may consult its books and records, even if he has been excluded from management.  A contrary agreement is null.

He may not exercise his right in a manner that unduly interferes with the operations of the partnership or prevents other partners from exercising their rights in this regard.

Acts 1980, No. 150, §1.
