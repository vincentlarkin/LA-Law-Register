Art. 2841.  Contract form; registry
A contract of partnership in commendam shall be in writing and filed for registry with
the secretary of state as provided by law.  Until the contract is filed for registry, partners in
commendam are liable to third persons in the same manner as general partners.
Acts 1980, No. 150, §1; Acts 2025, No. 488, §1.
