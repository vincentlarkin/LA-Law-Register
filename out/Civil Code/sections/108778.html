Art. 1421.  Estate debts, charged

Unless otherwise provided by the testament, by agreement of the successors, or by law, estate debts are charged against the property of the estate and its fruits and products in accordance with the following articles.

Acts 1997, No. 1421, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
