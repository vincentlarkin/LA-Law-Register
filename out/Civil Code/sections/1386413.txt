Art. 358.1.  Limited continuing tutorship; authority; privileges and duties of tutor and
undertutor
The granting of a decree of limited continuing tutorship shall confer upon the tutor
and undertutor only the authority, privileges, and responsibilities required to protect the
interest of the person under limited continuing tutorship.
Acts 2024, No. 123, §1.
