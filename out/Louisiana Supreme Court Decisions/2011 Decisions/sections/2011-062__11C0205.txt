PER CURIAM

We granted defendants' application for a writ of certiorari in this case on April 8, 2011. After hearing oral arguments and reviewing the record of the matter, we conclude the judgment below does not require the exercise of this court's supervisory authority. Accordingly, we recall our order of April 8, 2011 as improvidently granted, and we deny defendants' writ application.
