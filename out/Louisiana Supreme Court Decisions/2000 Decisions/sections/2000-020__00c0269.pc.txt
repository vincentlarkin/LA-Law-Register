PER CURIAM*

Upon plaintiff's application, we granted certiorari in this case. Perkins v. Perkins, 00-0269 (La. 2/16/00), ___ So. 2d ___. After hearing oral arguments and reviewing the record of the matter, we conclude that the judgment below does not require the exercise of our supervisory authority. Accordingly, we recall our order of February 16, 2000 as improvidently granted, and deny plaintiff's application.
