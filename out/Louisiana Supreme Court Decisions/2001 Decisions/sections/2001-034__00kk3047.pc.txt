PER CURIAM*

Upon the State of Louisiana's application, we granted certiorari in this case. State v. Atkins, No. 00-3047 (La. 1/12/01), 2001 WL 49963. After hearing oral argument and reviewing the record in this case, we concluded that the judgment below does not require the exercise of our supervisory authority. Accordingly, we recall our order of January 12, 2001, as improvidently granted, and deny the State's application.
