No. 00-K-2900

STATE of Louisiana versus Kevin P. JONES

ON WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE COURT OF APPEAL

FOURTH CIRCUIT, CRIMINAL DISTRICT COURT,

PARISH OF ORLEANS

PER CURIAM

After an independent review of the record, and considering the arguments of counsel, we conclude that the decision of the Court of Appeal does not require the exercise of our supervisory authority. Accordingly, we recall our order of September 21, 2001 as improvidently granted.
