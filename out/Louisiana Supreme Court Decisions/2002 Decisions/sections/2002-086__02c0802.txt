ON WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE COURT OF APPEAL,

FIRST CIRCUIT

PER CURIAM

Upon defendant's application, we granted certiorari in this case. Williams v. Rubicon, Inc., 02-0802 (La. 6/7/02), 818 So. 2d 766. After hearing oral arguments and reviewing the record of the matter, we conclude that the judgment below does not require the exercise of our supervisory authority. Accordingly, we recall our order of June 7, 200 2 as improvidently granted, and deny defendant's application.
