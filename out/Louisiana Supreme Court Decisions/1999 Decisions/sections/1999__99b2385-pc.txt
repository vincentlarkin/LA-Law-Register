SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA

NO. 99-B-2385

IN RE: CARDELL A. THOMAS

ORDER

Having considered the Request of Cardell A. Thomas for Discipline by Consent, and the concurrence therein filed by the Office of Disciplinary Counsel, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Request of Cardell A. Thomas for Discipline by Consent be rejected pursuant to Supreme Court Rule XIX, § 20. IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that this matter be remanded to the Office of Disciplinary Counsel for further investigation and, if warranted, the filing of additional formal charges. NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA, this ____ day of September, 1999. ___________________________________________

JUSTICE, SUPREME COURT OF LOUISIANA

LEMMON, J. not on panel. Rule IV, Part II, § 3.
