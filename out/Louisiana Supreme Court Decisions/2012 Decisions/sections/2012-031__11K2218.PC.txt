ON WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE COURT OF APPEAL,

FOURTH CIRCUIT, PARISH OF ORLEANS

PER CURIAM

We granted the State's application for a writ of certiorari in this case on February 17, 2012. After hearing oral arguments and reviewing the record of the matter, we conclude the judgment below does not require the exercise of this court's supervisory authority. Accordingly, we recall our order of February 17, 2012, as improvidently granted, and we deny the writ application.
