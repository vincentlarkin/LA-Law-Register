ART.  X-A.  AD VALOREM PROPERTY TAXATION
§4.  Annual payment to Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College
Section 4.  Beginning with the fiscal year commencing on July 1, 1973, and in each such fiscal year thereafter, there shall be paid for the endowment and support of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, from the general fund of the state the sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) which amount shall be in lieu of the same amount prescribed to be paid to it for its endowment and support from the avails of the tax presently being levied on taxable property in the state pursuant to Article XII, Section 17 of this constitution.
(Added by Acts 1972, Ex.Sess., No. 17, adopted Nov. 7, 1972.)
