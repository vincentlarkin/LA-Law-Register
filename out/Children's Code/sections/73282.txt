Art. 686.  Duration of disposition
A judgment of disposition shall remain in force only until a child reaches his eighteenth birthday.  It may expire earlier by its own terms, if it is modified, or if it is vacated.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
