Art. 904.  Court transmission of reports
If the child is assigned to the custody of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections or to the custody of a public or private institution or agency, the court shall transmit with the judgment of disposition all relevant reports concerning the child.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
