Art. 1302.10.  Application of Chapter to nonresident subject to personal jurisdiction
A tribunal of this state exercising personal jurisdiction over a nonresident in a
proceeding under this Chapter, under other law of this state relating to a support order, or
recognizing a foreign support order may receive evidence from outside this state pursuant
to Article 1303.16, communicate with a tribunal outside this state pursuant to Article
1303.17, and obtain discovery through a tribunal outside this state pursuant to Article
1303.18.  In all other respects, Sections 3 through 6 do not apply, and the tribunal shall apply
the procedural and substantive law of this state.
Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
