CHAPTER 6.  PETITION AND SUMMONS

Art. 746.  Authority to file petition

If there are reasonable grounds to believe that the family is in need of services, a family in need of services proceeding shall be commenced by a petition filed by the district attorney or any other attorney authorized by the court.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §7; Acts 1999, No. 1313, §1.
