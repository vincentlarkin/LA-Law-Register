Art. 847.  Service of petition
A.  A copy of the petition and the right to counsel form specified in Article 848 shall be served upon the child.
B.  A copy of the petition and the right to counsel form specified in Article 848 shall also be served upon every parent whose address is known or can be determined after due diligence.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
