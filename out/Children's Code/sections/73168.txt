Art. 424.5.  CASA; appearance; witness
A.  The CASA volunteer shall be entitled to attend all court proceedings and hearings of any kind pertaining to a child for whom he is assigned.
B.  The CASA volunteer may be called as a witness in the proceedings by any party or by the court and may request of the court the opportunity to appear as a witness.
Acts 1999, No. 275, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
