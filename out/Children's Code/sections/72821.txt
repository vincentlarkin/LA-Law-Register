Art. 1303.3.  Application of law of state
Except as otherwise provided by this Chapter, a responding tribunal of this state shall:
(1)  Apply the procedural and substantive law generally applicable to similar
proceedings originating in this state and may exercise all powers and provide all remedies
available in those proceedings; and
(2)  Determine the duty of support and the amounts payable in accordance with the
law and support guidelines of this state.
Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 1241, §1, eff. July 15, 1997;
Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
