CHAPTER 4.  COURT PERSONNEL
Art. 418.  Juvenile court; juvenile judge
When another statute uses the term "juvenile court" or "juvenile judge" for purposes of proceedings under this Code, those terms shall be construed to mean "court" or "judge" as defined in Article 116.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
