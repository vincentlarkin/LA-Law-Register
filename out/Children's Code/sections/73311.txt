Art. 715.  Service of motion to modify
A.  A copy of a motion to modify shall be served upon the child, his parent, the petitioner, and any person, institution, or agency to whom the custody of the child has been assigned.
B.  Service shall be in the manner provided for service of the petition.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
