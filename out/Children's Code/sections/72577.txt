Art. 1043.  Adoption; voluntary registration by child and parents whose parental rights have been terminated
Parents whose rights have been terminated and an adopted child are permitted to use the services of the voluntary registry as provided in Chapter 15 of Title XII.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
