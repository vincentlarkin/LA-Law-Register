Art. 1459.  Review by MHAS

All minor patients presently unrepresented by privately retained counsel and who are the subject of involuntary commitment under any prior statute shall have their cases reviewed by attorneys provided by the MHAS within one year from the effective date of this Code, or be discharged or be committed again according to the provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §14, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
