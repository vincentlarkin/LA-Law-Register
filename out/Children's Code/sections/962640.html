Art. 1307.3. Relationship of the Department of Children and Family Services to United States central authority

            The Department of Children and Family Services of this state is recognized as the agency designated by the United States central authority to perform specific functions under the Convention.

            Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
