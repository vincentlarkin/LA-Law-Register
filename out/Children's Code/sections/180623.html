Art. 1107.9.  Notice of filing of intent to surrender for adoption; service upon identified alleged fathers, residents and nonresidents

Notice of filing of an intent to surrender for adoption shall be given by personal service or by registered or certified mail, return receipt requested, delivery restricted to the addressee, to any alleged or adjudicated and identified father.

Acts 2003, No. 564, §1.
