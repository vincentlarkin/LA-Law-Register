Art. 1285.4.  Service of process
A copy of the petition for adoption of a Hague Convention adoptee together with all exhibits shall be served by registered or certified mail, return receipt requested, postage prepaid, and properly addressed to the department and on any agency having legal custody of the child.
Acts 2013, No. 86, §1.
