Section 5.  Enforcement of Support Order
Without Registration
Art. 1305.1.  Employer's receipt of income-withholding order of another state
An income-withholding order issued in another state may be sent by or on behalf of
the obligee, or by the support enforcement agency, to the person defined as the obligor's
employer under the income withholding law of this state without first filing a petition or
comparable pleading or registering the order with a tribunal of this state.
Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 1241, §1, eff. July 15, 1997;
Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
