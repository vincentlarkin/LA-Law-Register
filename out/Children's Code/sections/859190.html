Art. 1285.8.  Interlocutory decree

Upon due consideration of the factors enumerated in Article 1285.6(B), the court may grant or refuse to grant an interlocutory decree during or after the hearing.  The basic consideration for this decree shall be the best interest of the child.

Acts 2013, No. 86, §1.
