Art. 1129.  Proof of law
A person asserting the validity of a surrender or consent executed in a foreign state shall produce sufficient proof of the laws of the foreign state governing the requirements for form and content and the force and effect accorded a properly executed act.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §11, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
