CHAPTER 14.  VISITATION RIGHTS OF GRANDPARENTS
Art. 1264.  Post-adoption visitation rights of grandparents
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the natural parents of a
deceased parent whose child is thereafter adopted and the parents of a party who has forfeited
the right to object to the adoption of the child pursuant to Article 1245 may have limited
visitation rights to the adopted child.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §12, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2023, No. 16, §1; Acts 2024, No. 92,
§1.
