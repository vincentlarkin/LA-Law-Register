Art. 752.  Service and return; resident caretaker

A.  If a caretaker resides within the state, service shall be made personally or by domiciliary service or by certified mail as soon as possible and not less than fifteen days prior to commencement of the adjudication hearing on the matter.

B.  The person effecting service shall execute a return and, if service was made by certified mail, the return receipt shall be attached thereto.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §7.
