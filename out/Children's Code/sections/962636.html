Art. 1306.16. Procedure to register child support order of a foreign country for modification

            A party or support enforcement agency seeking to modify, or to modify and enforce, a foreign child support order not under the Convention may register that order in this state under Articles 1306.1 through 1306.8 if the order has not been registered. A petition for modification may be filed at the same time as a request for registration, or at another time. The petition must specify the grounds for modification.

            Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
