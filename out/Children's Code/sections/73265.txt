CHAPTER 13.  PERMANENCY PLANNING REPORTS
Art. 671.  Applicability
If at any point in a child in need of care proceeding a child enters the custody of a child care agency, the provisions of this Chapter shall also be applicable.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
