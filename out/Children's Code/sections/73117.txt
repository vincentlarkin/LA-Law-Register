Art. 317.  Change of proper venue
Any party by contradictory motion may obtain a change of venue upon proof that he cannot obtain a fair and impartial trial because of the undue influence of an adverse party, prejudice existing in the public mind, or some other sufficient cause.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
