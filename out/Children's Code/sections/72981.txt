Art. 1513.  Venue
A proceeding brought pursuant to this Chapter may be commenced in the parish in which either:
(1)  The parents requesting transfer of custody reside.
(2)  The person, agency, or institution to whom the transfer of custody is intended resides.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §15, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
