CHAPTER 3.  CHILDREN'S ADVOCACY CLEARINGHOUSE

Art. 531.  Children's Advocacy Clearinghouse; establishment; purpose

The legislature finds that the establishment of the Children's Advocacy Clearinghouse by the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice for the processing of child sex abuse cases among the various responsible agencies is in the best interest of the children of Louisiana.

Acts 2003, No. 749, §1.
