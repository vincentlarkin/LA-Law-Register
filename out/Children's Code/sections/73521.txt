CHAPTER 3.  JUVENILE TRAFFIC REFEREES
Art. 955.  Traffic violations; referral to juvenile traffic referee
The court may, by local rule, refer cases of traffic violations to a juvenile traffic referee for adjudication and disposition.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §9, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
