Art. 811.3.  Definitions
In this Chapter:
(1)  "Juvenile crime victim" means a person under the age of eighteen against whom
a delinquent act has been committed.
(2)  "Victim" means a person against whom an offense that is a delinquent act has
been committed.
(3)  "Victim's family" means the spouse, child, stepchild, sibling, parent, grandparent,
guardian, legal custodian, or legal representative of the victim, except when the person is in
custody for an offense or is the defendant.
