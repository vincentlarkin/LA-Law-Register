CHAPTER 18.  EXPUNGEMENT
Art. 917.  Expungement and sealing; generally
This Chapter provides the exclusive procedure by which records and reports of
proceedings under Title VIII of this Code may be expunged and sealed.
Acts 1997, No. 1127, §1, eff. July 14, 1997; Acts 2017, No. 362, §1.
