Art. 1273.  Confidentiality
Documents filed with the office of children and family services, pursuant to this
Chapter, shall be confidential and shall not be available for inspection, except under the
procedures set forth in this Chapter.
NOTE: Art. 1273 as amended by Acts 2025, No. 477, eff. October 1, 2027.
Art. 1273.  Confidentiality
Documents filed with the office of child welfare, pursuant to this Chapter, shall be
confidential and shall not be available for inspection, except under the procedures set forth
in this Chapter.
