Art. 424.  Court-appointed special advocates (CASA); purpose
The purpose of CASA is to advocate for timely placement of children in permanent, safe, and stable homes.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1997, No. 904, §1, eff. July 10, 1997; Acts 1999, No. 275, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
