CHAPTER 12.  ANNULMENT OF SURRENDER
Art. 1147.  Grounds
No act of surrender shall be subject to annulment except upon proof of duress or fraud, notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §11, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
