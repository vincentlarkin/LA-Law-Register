Art. 1307.2. Applicability

            This Section applies only to a support proceeding under the Convention. In such a proceeding, if a provision of this Section is inconsistent with Sections 1 through 6, this Section controls.

            Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
