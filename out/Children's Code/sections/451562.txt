Art. 572.  Definitions
As used in this Chapter:
(1)  "Child abuse and neglect case" means a child protection proceeding conducted
by a court exercising juvenile jurisdiction involving the abuse or neglect of children as
provided specifically in Titles VI, X, and XI of this Code.
(2)  "Indigent Parents' Program" means the Indigent Parents' Representation Program
established in accordance with Article 574.
Acts 2007, No. 95, §1; Acts 2016, No. 407, §1, eff. June 5, 2016.
