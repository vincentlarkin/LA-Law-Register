Art. 1032.  Continuances

Upon a showing of good cause and notice to the opposing party, the court may grant, deny, or restrict a requested continuance of the proceeding.  The court shall avoid delays in resolving the status of the parent and in achieving permanency for the child.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1997, No. 256, §1.
