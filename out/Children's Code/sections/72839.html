Section 6. Registration, Enforcement, and Modification of

Support Order

Subsection A. Registration for Enforcement

of Support Order

Art. 1306.1. Registration of order for enforcement

           A support order or an income-withholding order issued in another state or a foreign support order may be registered in this state for enforcement.           Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 1241, §1, eff. July 15, 1997; Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
