Art. 638.  Service of petition; parent; child
A copy of the petition and the notice of the nature of the hearing and the rights of the
parent, as provided for in Article 639, shall be served, in a sealed envelope, upon every
parent of the child.  A copy of the petition and the notice of the nature of the hearing shall
be served on the child through the entity designated for the jurisdiction to provide counsel
for the child.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2006, No. 742, §1; Acts 2022, No.
272, §1.
