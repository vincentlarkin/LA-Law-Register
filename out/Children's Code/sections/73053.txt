Art. 1619.  Financial responsibility for children
NOTE:  Art. 1619 repealed by Acts 2010, No. 893, §2, eff. when the 35th state enacts the Interstate Compact for the Placement of Children.
Financial responsibility for any child placed pursuant to the provisions of this compact shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of Article 1612 in the first instance.  However, in the event of partial or complete default of performance thereunder, the provisions of Louisiana's laws fixing the responsibility for the support of children also may be invoked.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §16, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2010, No. 893, §2.
