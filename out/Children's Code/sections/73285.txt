Art. 689.  Case review reports; service
A.  A copy of the report shall be served upon counsel of record either by mail or
electronic mail and upon any unrepresented party either by certified mail at his last known
address or by the department by electronic mail at the electronic mail address expressly
designated by the party in a pleading, at the continued custody or continued safety plan
hearing, or at any other hearing at which the party personally appeared before the court prior
to or at the same time it is filed with the court.
B.  Service by electronic mail is complete upon transmission but is not effective if
the serving party learns the transmission did not reach the party to be served.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1993, No. 634, §1, eff. June 15, 1993;
Acts 2015, No. 124, §1, eff. June 19, 2015.
