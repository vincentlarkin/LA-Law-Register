Art. 1527.  Venue
Proceedings pursuant to this Chapter shall be commenced in the parish in which the offense is alleged to have been committed.  If acts constituting an offense or if the elements of an offense occurred in more than one place, the offense is deemed to have been committed in any parish in which any such act or element occurred.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §15, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
