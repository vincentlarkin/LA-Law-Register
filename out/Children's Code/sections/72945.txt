Art. 1449.  Notice of court action
Notice of any action taken by the court shall be given to the minor respondent and his attorney as well as to the director of the designated treatment facility in such manner as the court concludes would be appropriate under the circumstances.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §14, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
