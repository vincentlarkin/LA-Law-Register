TITLE XII-A.  INTERCOUNTRY ADOPTION
OF CHILDREN
CHAPTER 1.  PRELIMINARY PROVISIONS;
DEFINITIONS
Art. 1281.1.  Purpose
The purpose of this Title is to provide procedures for the adoption of foreign-born orphans by domiciliaries of Louisiana.
Acts 1999, No. 1268, §1.
