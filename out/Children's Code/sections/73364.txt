CHAPTER 10.  PREDISPOSITION INVESTIGATION
Art. 773.  Predisposition investigation and report
Following adjudication, the court may order a predisposition investigation.  The investigation shall include inquiring into the circumstances surrounding the factual allegations of the petition and shall gather the information required by Article 744(B).  A written report of the investigation and findings shall be submitted to the court prior to the disposition hearing.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §7.
