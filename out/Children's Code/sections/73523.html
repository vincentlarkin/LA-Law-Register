CHAPTER 4.  APPOINTMENT OF COUNSEL

Art. 957.  Appointment of counsel

The juvenile court or traffic referee may appoint an attorney to represent the child whenever appointment of counsel is required in the interests of justice.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §9, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
