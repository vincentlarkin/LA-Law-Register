Art. 1309.2.  Transitional provision
This Chapter applies to proceedings begun on or after the effective date of this
Chapter to establish a support order or determine parentage of a child or to register,
recognize, enforce, or modify a prior support order, determination, or agreement, whenever
issued or entered.
Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
