PART II.  LOUISIANA CHILD

REPRESENTATION SYSTEM

Art. 557.  Purpose

The purpose of this Part is to provide for an effective and efficient system of providing qualified legal representation for children in child abuse and neglect cases.

Acts 2006, No. 271, §1; Acts 2014, No. 354, §1.
