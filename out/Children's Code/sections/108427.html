Art. 420.  Law clerks

Each judge of the juvenile court is hereby authorized to employ a law clerk, provided funds are available, to perform such research duties as the judge who employs the law clerk may assign.  The salary of each such law clerk shall be fixed and paid on the warrant of the employing judge.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
