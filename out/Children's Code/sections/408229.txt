Art. 552.  Definitions
As used in this Chapter:
(1)  "Child abuse and neglect case" means a child protection proceeding conducted
by a court exercising juvenile jurisdiction involving the abuse or neglect of children as
provided specifically in Titles VI, X, XI, and XII of the Louisiana Children's Code.
(2)  "Program" means a program approved by the Louisiana Supreme Court to
provide qualified legal representation of children in child abuse and neglect cases.
Acts 2006, No. 271, §1; Acts 2014, No. 354, §1.
