CHAPTER 2-A.  INTENT TO SURRENDER PROCESS
Art. 1107.1.  Intent to surrender for adoption
During her pregnancy or thereafter, a mother of a child may execute an intent to surrender for adoption in accordance with Chapter 2-A of this Title.
Acts 2003, No. 564, §1.
