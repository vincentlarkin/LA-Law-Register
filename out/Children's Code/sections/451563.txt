PART II.  INDIGENT PARENTS' REPRESENTATION PROGRAM
Art. 573.  Purpose
The purpose of this Part is to provide for an effective and efficient system of
providing qualified legal representation, which shall include curatorship appointments, for
indigent or absent parents in child abuse and neglect cases.
Acts 2007, No. 95, §1; Acts 2016, No. 407, §1, eff. June 5, 2016.
