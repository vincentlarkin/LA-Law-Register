Art. 673.  Case plan
Within sixty days after a child enters the custody of a child care agency, the custodian
shall develop a case plan detailing the custodian's efforts toward achieving a permanent
placement for the child.  The health, welfare, and safety of the child shall be the paramount
concern in the development of the case plan.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1999, No. 449, §1, eff. July 1, 1999;
Acts 2022, No. 272, §1.
