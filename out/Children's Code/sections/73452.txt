Art. 861.  Access to evidence; reports
A.  The child shall have all rights to discovery as provided for in Article 866.
B.  In addition to the rights to review any report submitted pursuant to Article 860, the child shall have the right to review any report prepared regarding the transfer.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
