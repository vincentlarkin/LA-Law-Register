Art. 1010.  Petitions for disclosure

The child or if still a minor, his legal representative on his behalf, may file a petition for disclosure of information pertaining to the termination of parental rights proceeding.  The action shall be styled: "In re the termination of parental rights to____________________".

Acts 1991, No. 235, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
