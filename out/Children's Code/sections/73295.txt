Art. 699.  Evidence
The court or administrative review body shall consider all relevant evidence offered by the parties and may limit the admissibility or weight of any evidence which it deems unreliable, cumulative, or unduly dilatory.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
