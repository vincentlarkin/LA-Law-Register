Art. 703.  Notice
Written notice of the date, time, and place of the dispositional hearing shall be served and a return made in the same manner as a petition on all parties and counsel of record at least fifteen days prior to the hearing, unless the parties have been previously notified in open court at a prior hearing.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
