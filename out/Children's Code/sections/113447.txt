Art. 513.  Confidentiality policy
A multidisciplinary team may obtain all information necessary to perform its official duties from any public agency, department or other organization, including material otherwise made confidential or privileged.  Any confidential or privileged material or verbal information obtained by a team member during an investigation shall be disclosed only as necessary to other team members, and shall not be disclosed to any agency or individual not represented on the multidisciplinary team unless otherwise required by law.
Acts 2003, No. 749, §1.
