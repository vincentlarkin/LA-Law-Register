Art. 1303.6.  Inappropriate tribunal
If a petition or comparable pleading is received by an inappropriate tribunal of this
state, the tribunal shall forward the pleading and accompanying documents to an appropriate
tribunal in this state or another state and notify the petitioner where and when the pleading
was sent.
Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 1241, §1, eff. July 15, 1997;
Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
