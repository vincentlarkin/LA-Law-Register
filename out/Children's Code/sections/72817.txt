Art. 1303.17.  Communications between tribunals
A tribunal of this state may communicate with a tribunal outside this state in a record
or by telephone, electronic mail, or other means, to obtain information concerning the laws,
the legal effect of a judgment, decree, or order of that tribunal, and the status of a proceeding.
A tribunal of this state may furnish similar information by similar means to a tribunal outside
this state.
Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 1241, §1, eff. July 15, 1997;
Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
