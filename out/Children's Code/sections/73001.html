CHAPTER 5.  (RESERVED)

CHAPTER 6.  AUTHORIZATION OF MINORS' MARRIAGES

Art. 1543.  Purpose

The purpose of this Chapter is to set out the substantive law and procedures for securing the authorization of the juvenile court for the marriage of a minor.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §15, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
