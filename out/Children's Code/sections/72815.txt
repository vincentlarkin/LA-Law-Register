Art. 1303.15.  Nonparentage as defense
A party whose parentage of a child has been previously determined by or pursuant
to law may not plead nonparentage as a defense to a proceeding under this Chapter.
Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
