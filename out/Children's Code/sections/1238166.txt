Art. 1239.1.  Reporting requirement; statistical availability
A.  For each adopted child, the court shall ensure that the department receives all of
the following information:
(1)  Whether either of the adopted parents are related to the child.
(2)  The age of the child at placement.
(3)  The age of the child at the time of finalization.
(4)  The gender of the child.
(5)  The race of the child.
(6)  The location of placement.
(7)  Other nonidentifying information the department requests in order to maintain
statistical records of adoption finalizations.
B.  The department shall release yearly statistics on the adoptions of children in
Louisiana and placement of those children.  These statistics shall be made available to the
public.
Acts 2021, No. 6, §1.
