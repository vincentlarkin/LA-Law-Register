CHAPTER 3.  AUTHORITY TO SURRENDER
Art. 1108.  Authority to surrender
A.  Subject to the provisions for capacity found in Chapter 4 of this Title any parent may execute a voluntary act of surrender.
B.  If the parents are dead or are unknown, the child's tutor may execute the act of surrender for adoption.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §11, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
