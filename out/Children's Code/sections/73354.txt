Art. 763.  Motions to dismiss
A.  All objections to the proceedings, including objections based on defects in the
petition and defenses capable of determination as a matter of law, may be raised by motion
to dismiss.
B.  Upon a finding of grounds to dismiss the petition as provided in Paragraph A of
this Article, the court shall order that the petition be dismissed.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §7; Acts 2022, No. 176, §1.
