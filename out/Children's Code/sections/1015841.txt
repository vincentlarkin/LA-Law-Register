Art. 1015.1.  Grounds; termination of parental rights of perpetrator of a sex offense
Parental rights of a natural parent may be terminated in cases where there is a
conviction or commission of a sex offense as defined in R.S. 15:541 by that natural parent
which resulted in the conception of the child.
Acts 2023, No. 271, §1, eff. June 9, 2023.
NOTE: Former Ch.C. Art. 1015.1 redesignated as Ch.C. Art. 1015.2 by Acts 2023, No. 271,
§1, eff. June 9, 2023.
