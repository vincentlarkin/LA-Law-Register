Art. 875.  Motions to dismiss
A.  All objections to the proceedings, including objections based on defects in the petition and defenses capable of determination as a matter of law, may be raised by motion to dismiss.
B.  Upon a finding of grounds to dismiss the petition as provided for in Paragraph A of this Article, the court shall order that the petition be dismissed.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2011, No. 172, §1.
