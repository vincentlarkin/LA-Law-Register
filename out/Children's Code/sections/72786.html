Art. 1283.4.  Service of process

A copy of the petition for adoption of a foreign orphan together with all exhibits shall be served by registered or certified mail, return receipt requested, postage prepaid and properly addressed to the department and on any agency having legal custody of the child.

Acts 1999, No. 1268, §1; Acts 2013, No. 86, §1.
