TITLE III

JURISDICTION, GENERAL AUTHORITY, AND APPEALS

CHAPTER 1. DEFINITIONS

Art. 301. Definitions

            As used in this Title, "nonmetropolitan area" means a parish whose largest city has a population of fifty thousand or less and where the population of the entire parish is less than one hundred thousand.

            Acts 1991, No. 235, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2022, No. 271, §6.
