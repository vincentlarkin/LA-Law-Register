Art. 1022.  Service; nonresident parent
If a parent against whom a proceeding is instituted does not reside within this state,
service of citation shall be made by registered or certified mail to the address indicated in the
petition, return receipt required, not less than five days prior to commencement of the hearing
on the matter.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2021, No. 158, §1.
