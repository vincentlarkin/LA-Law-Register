CHAPTER 15.  CASE REVIEW PROCEDURE;
REPORTS; REVIEW HEARINGS
Art. 687.  Applicability
If at any point in child in need of care proceedings, the child is removed from his parents' care and control and placed in the custody of the department, the provisions of this Chapter shall govern the subsequent case review process until such time as the child achieves a permanent placement as defined in Article 603.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
