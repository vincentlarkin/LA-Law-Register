Art. 658. Dismissal of petition

            The court shall dismiss a petition on the motion of the petitioner.

            Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1992, No. 705, §1, eff. July 6, 1992; Acts 2022, No. 176, §1.
