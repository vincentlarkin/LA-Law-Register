CHAPTER 8.  HEARING
Art. 1031.  Time for setting termination of parental rights hearing
The termination of parental rights hearing shall commence within sixty days after the date set for the appearance to answer the petition.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1992, No. 705, §1, eff. July 6, 1992; Acts 1997, No. 256, §1.
