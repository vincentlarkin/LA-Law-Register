Art. 820.  Grounds for continued custody prior to adjudication
At the continued custody hearing, the state shall prove that there is probable cause that the child has committed a delinquent act or has violated a condition of his probation or release.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1992, No. 705, §1, eff. July 6, 1992.
