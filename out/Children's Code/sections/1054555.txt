CHAPTER 15-A.  NATURE OF PROCEEDINGS AND RECORDS
Art. 792.  Nature of proceedings
Families in Need of Services proceedings are civil in nature, and actions taken
pursuant to this Title, including taking into custody and detention, shall not be considered
juvenile delinquency or criminal matters.
Acts 2017, No. 362, §1.
