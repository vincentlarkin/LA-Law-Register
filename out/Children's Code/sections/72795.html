Art. 1301.3. State tribunal and support enforcement agency

            A. The district courts or, if applicable, the family or juvenile courts, and the division of administrative review, Department of Children and Family Services, or other administrative law division authorized by law, are the tribunals of this state for purposes of this Chapter.

            B. The Department of Children and Family Services is the support enforcement agency of this state.

            Acts 1995, No. 251, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 1241, §1, eff. July 15, 1997; Acts 2015, No. 80, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
