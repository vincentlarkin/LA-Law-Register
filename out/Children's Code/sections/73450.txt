Art. 859.  Conduct of transfer hearing
A.  The transfer hearing shall be conducted in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 13 of this Title governing the conduct of an adjudication hearing, except as otherwise provided herein.
B.  Only such evidence which pertains to the transfer criteria set out in Article 862 may be introduced.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
