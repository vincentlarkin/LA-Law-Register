CHAPTER 9.  PETITION AND SUMMONS
Art. 842.  Authority to file petition
A delinquency proceeding shall be commenced by a petition.  The district attorney may file a petition without leave of court.  Any person authorized by the court may file a petition if there are reasonable grounds to believe that the child is a delinquent child.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
