Art. 1448.  Discharge; revocation

A.  The director shall notify the court in writing when a minor patient has been discharged or conditionally discharged.

B.  The court may, if it finds it to be in the best interests of the minor, revoke the certificate or judgment of commitment.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §14, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
