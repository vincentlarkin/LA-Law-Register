Art. 648. Advice of rights at appearance to answer petition

            At the appearance to answer, the court shall advise the parent and the child of their rights and responsibilities pursuant to Articles 625 and 625.1, unless they were so advised at a previous hearing.

            Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1999, No. 449, §1, eff. July 1, 1999; Acts 2024, No. 92, §1.
