Art. 790.  Notice of modification

If a judgment of disposition is modified, a copy of the minute entry reflecting the modification shall be served upon the petitioner, the child, his parent, and any person, institution, or agency to whom custody of the child is assigned.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §7.
