Art. 424.8.  CASA; request for hearing
The CASA volunteer may request a hearing before the court  for any of the following reasons:
(1)  The case plan on behalf of the child is not being implemented or adhered to by any party.
(2)  The plan is not meeting the child's needs.
(3)  Other reasons in the best interests of the child.
Acts 1999, No. 275, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
