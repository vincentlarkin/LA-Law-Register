Art. 692.  Case review hearings
A.  A review hearing shall be conducted by the court or administrative review body three months after the disposition hearing if the child was removed prior to disposition or within six months after the disposition hearing if the child was removed at disposition, but in no case more than six months after removal of the child from his parent(s).
B.  Case reviews shall continue to be held at least once every six months thereafter until the child is permanently placed, or earlier upon the motion of a party for good cause shown or upon the court's own motion.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
