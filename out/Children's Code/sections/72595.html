CHAPTER 4.  CAPACITY TO SURRENDER

Art. 1111.  Capacity generally

Except as provided hereinafter in this Chapter, any parent has the capacity to surrender his or her child for adoption.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §11, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
