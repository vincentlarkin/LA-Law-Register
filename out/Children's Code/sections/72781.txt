Art. 1283.15.  Effect of final decree
The final decree of adoption shall have the same force and effect as any final decree of adoption rendered by a court of this state and automatically entitles the child to United States citizenship under the Intercountry Adoption Act.
Acts 1999, No. 1268, §1; Acts 2013, No. 86, §1.
