Art. 1273. Confidentiality

            Documents filed with the office of children and family services, pursuant to this Chapter, shall be confidential and shall not be available for inspection, except under the procedures set forth in this Chapter.

            NOTE: Art. 1273 as amended by Acts 2025, No. 477, eff. October 1, 2027.

Art. 1273. Confidentiality

            Documents filed with the office of child welfare, pursuant to this Chapter, shall be confidential and shall not be available for inspection, except under the procedures set forth in this Chapter.

            Acts 1991, No. 235, §12, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2010, No. 266, §1; Acts 2025, No. 477, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2027.
