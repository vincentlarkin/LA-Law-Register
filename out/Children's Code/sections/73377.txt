CHAPTER 13.  CASE REVIEW PROCEDURE;
REPORTS; REVIEW HEARINGS
Art. 785.  Applicability
If at any point in families in need of services proceedings the child is removed from his caretaker's care and control and placed in the custody of a child care agency, the provisions of Chapters 15 and 16 of Title VI shall govern the subsequent review process until such time as the child achieves a permanent placement as defined by that Title.
Acts 1991, No. 235, §7.
