Art. 106.  Number, gender

Unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:

(1)  Words used in the singular number apply also to the the plural; words used in the plural number include the singular.

(2)  Words used in one gender apply also to the other.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
