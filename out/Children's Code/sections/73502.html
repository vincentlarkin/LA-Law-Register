Art. 908.1.  AIDS and sexually transmitted diseases; victim's testing and services

When a juvenile is adjudicated a delinquent for a sexual offense as defined in R.S. 14:42 through 43.4, the provisions of R.S. 15:535(C) and (D) shall apply.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §8, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 2008, No. 678, §2.
