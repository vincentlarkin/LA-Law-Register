CHAPTER 5.  DISPOSITION

Art. 958.  Disposition immediately following adjudication

A.  In cases of traffic violations, the court or juvenile traffic referee may dispense with a predisposition investigation and enter a judgment of disposition immediately following adjudication if there is sufficient information in the record to enable the court or referee to determine a fair and just disposition of the case.

B.  Judgments of disposition in traffic cases may be evidenced by a minute entry.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §9, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
