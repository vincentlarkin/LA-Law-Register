Art. 954.  Traffic violations adjudicated on citation; petition not required

In cases of traffic violations, a petition is not required.  The child may be summoned to appear and the case may be instituted with a uniform traffic citation.  The citation shall describe in general terms the nature of the violation.  The citation or summons may direct the child to bring a parent with him when he appears.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §9, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
