Art. 711.  Simultaneous hearings

When appropriate, case review hearings and permanency hearings may be scheduled to be heard simultaneously, provided the record reflects that the appropriate findings and orders are made pursuant to this Chapter and Chapter 15 of this Title.

Acts 1991, No. 235, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1999, No. 449, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
