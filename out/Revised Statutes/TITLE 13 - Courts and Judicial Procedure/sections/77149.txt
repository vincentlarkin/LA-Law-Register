§2160.  Application of provisions of R.S. 13:5558-13:5592 to constables of
city courts
R.S. 13:5558 through 13:5592, relative to the costs of the civil sheriff
of the parish of Orleans, shall apply to and govern the costs of the constables
of the city courts of New Orleans, and the collection thereof.
Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
