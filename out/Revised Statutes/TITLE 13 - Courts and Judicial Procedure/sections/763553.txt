§5902.  Power to tax
The special districts herein created shall have the power to tax but only as provided in R.S. 13:5901 through 5909.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 689, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:9002 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
