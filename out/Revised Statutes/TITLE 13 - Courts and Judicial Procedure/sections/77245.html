§2488.57.  Collection of fines, forfeitures, penalties and costs

The city judge, or any officer of the city court designated by the city judge, shall collect all fines, forfeitures, penalties and costs, and all funds so collected, excluding costs, shall be paid into the town treasury of the town of Marksville when the prosecution is on behalf of the town, and into the parish treasury when the prosecution is on behalf of the state or the parish.

Added by Acts 1971, No. 16, §1.
