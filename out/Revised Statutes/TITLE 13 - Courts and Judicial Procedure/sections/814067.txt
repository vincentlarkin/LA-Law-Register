CHAPTER 34-A.  JUDICIAL COOPERATIVE ENDEAVORS
§ 5501.  Judicial cooperative endeavors
The various judicial district courts and district attorneys of Louisiana are authorized to enter into intergovernmental agreements with federal entities in order to share the administrative costs of funding the operations for joint programs funded by local, state, and federal assets.
Acts 2012, No. 399, §1.
