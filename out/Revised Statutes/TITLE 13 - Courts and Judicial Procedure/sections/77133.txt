§2151.2.  First City Court; term
There shall be a First City Court of the city of New Orleans, composed of three judges.  The judges of said court on September 12, 1975 shall continue to serve the terms for which they were elected.  Their successors shall be elected for terms of six years each by the qualified electors of the City of New Orleans on the left bank of the Mississippi River.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 125, §1.
