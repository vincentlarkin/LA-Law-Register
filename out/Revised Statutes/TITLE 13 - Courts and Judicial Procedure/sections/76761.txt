§1274.  Transcription for purpose of appeal
In case the transcription of the court reporter's notes shall be requested by a litigant, after judgment, for the purposes of an appeal, the whole cost of such transcription shall be paid primarily by such party, but ultimately shall be taxed as costs.
Acts 2006, No. 621, §19(A), eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 675, §1, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2008, No. 873, §1, eff. July 9, 2008; Acts 2011, No. 340, §2, eff. June 29, 2011; Acts 2012, No. 474, §4(B)(2).
