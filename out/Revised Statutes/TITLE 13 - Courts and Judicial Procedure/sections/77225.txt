§2488.29.  Seal of court
The city court shall be provided with a seal which shall contain a vignette of the state seal, with the words "Seal of the City Court of Baker, Louisiana", which seal shall be used on all process, orders and writs issuing from the court in all civil matters.
Acts 1970, No. 424, §9.
