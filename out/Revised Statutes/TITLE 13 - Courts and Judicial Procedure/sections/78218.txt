§996.8.  Judges; prohibition against payment of salaries from judicial expense fund
No salary shall be paid from the Judicial Expense Fund for the Twenty-First Judicial District to any of the judges of the Twenty-First Judicial District except as may be paid for administering the fund and then only after prior legislative approval.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 553, §1; Acts 2012, No. 452, §1.
