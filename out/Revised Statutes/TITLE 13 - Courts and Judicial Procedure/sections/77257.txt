§2488.75.  Salary of clerk
The clerk shall receive a monthly salary of one hundred fifty dollars payable in equal proportions by the governing authorities of the town of Breaux Bridge and the parish of St. Martin.  The town of Breaux Bridge and the parish of St. Martin, or either of them, may pay such additional salary to the clerk as they may deem proper.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 647, §1.
