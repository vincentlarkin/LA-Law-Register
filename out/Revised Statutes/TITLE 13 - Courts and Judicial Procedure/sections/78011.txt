§621.32.  Thirty-Second Judicial District
The Thirty-Second Judicial District Court shall have five judges.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 307.  Amended by Acts 1970, No. 124, §1; Acts 1972, No. 11, §1, emerg. eff. June 8, 1972, at 4:30 P.M.; Acts 1974, No. 84, §1, emerg. eff. July 9, 1974, at 10:25 A.M.; Acts 1974, No. 515, emerg. eff. July 12, 1974; Acts 1979, No. 516, §1, eff. July 17, 1979; Acts 1979, No. 519, §2.
