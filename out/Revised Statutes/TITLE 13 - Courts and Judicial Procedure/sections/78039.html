CHAPTER 2.  SUPREME COURT

PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§71.  Hearing cases under supervisory power

The supreme court of Louisiana may hear and decide, at chambers, either during term time or at vacation, all cases coming before them, under the supervisory powers conferred upon them by the constitution.
