§1143.  Compensation of retired judge

When a judge of the civil district court retires as authorized by the constitution, he shall receive from the city of New Orleans and from the judicial expense fund two-thirds of the remuneration which he was receiving from these sources immediately prior to his retirement.

Acts 2006, No. 621, §19(A), eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 675, §1, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2008, No. 873, §1, eff. July 9, 2008; Acts 2011, No. 340, §2, eff. June 29, 2011; Acts 2012, No. 474, §4(B)(2).
