§4251.208.  Notice of proceeding
If a petition for the appointment of a guardian or issuance of a protective order is
brought in this state and this state was not the respondent's home state on the date the petition
was filed, in addition to complying with the notice requirements of this state, notice of the
petition must be given to those persons who would be entitled to notice of the petition if a
proceeding were brought in the respondent's home state.  The notice must be given in the
same manner as notice is required to be given in this state.
Acts 2016, No. 333, §1.
