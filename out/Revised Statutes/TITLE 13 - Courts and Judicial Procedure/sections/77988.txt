§621.11.  Eleventh judicial district
The eleventh judicial district court shall have one judge.
Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 11, §1, emerg. eff. June 8, 1972, at 4:30 P.M.; Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 145, §1, eff. July 7, 1994; Acts 2007, No. 416, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
