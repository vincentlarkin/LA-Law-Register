§621.13.  Thirteenth judicial district
The Thirteenth Judicial District Court shall have two judges.
Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1980, No. 156, §1, eff. July 4, 1980; Acts 1980, No. 322, §1, eff. July 15, 1980.
