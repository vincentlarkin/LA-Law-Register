§5563.  Victims' service assistance program

The sheriff of each parish, including the criminal sheriff of the parish of Orleans, is hereby authorized to establish and maintain a victims' service assistance program.

Acts 1984, No. 370, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1463 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
