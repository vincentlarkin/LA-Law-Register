§2488.2.  Rules for conduct of business
A.  The city court may adopt and from time to time amend its rules for the conduct of the judicial business before it.
B.  The city court shall have such term and sessions for the transaction of its judicial business as shall be fixed, from time to time, by the rules adopted by the court.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 16, §2, emerg.  eff. July 4, 1968, at 10:05 A.M.
