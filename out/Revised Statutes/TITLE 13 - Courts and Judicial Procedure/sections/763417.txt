§5832.  Judicial release of constable from liability
If the court finds that the surety is solvent and possessed of the qualifications prescribed by law, the bond shall be made in favor of the parties to the case, and the constable shall be relieved of all responsibility.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 461, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1979; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1742 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
