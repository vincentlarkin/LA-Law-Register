§996.2.  Judges; prohibition against payment of salaries from Judicial Clerk's Fund

No salary shall be paid from the Judicial Clerk's Fund for the Thirty-Second Judicial District to any of the judges of the Thirty-Second Judicial District except as may be paid for administering the said fund and then only after prior legislative approval.  

Added by Acts 1980, No. 754, §1, eff. July 31, 1980.
