§2561.6.  Election of judge; terms
The first judge of this court shall be elected at the state general election to be held in 1964 and he shall serve until the next election for judges of the 24th Judicial District Court, and thereafter, shall be elected, take office and serve a six year term concurrently with that of the judges of the 24th Judicial District Court.
Acts 1962, No. 484, §6.
