§5605.  Appointment of attorney

A.  The criminal sheriff, parish of Orleans, is hereby authorized to appoint two attorneys, at no cost to the state of Louisiana.

B.  This Section shall not mandate an additional appropriation by the city of New Orleans.

Added by Acts 1960, No. 104, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 698, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1526 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
