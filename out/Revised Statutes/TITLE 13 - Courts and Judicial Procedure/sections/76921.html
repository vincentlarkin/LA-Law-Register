§1624.  Compensation; benefits; expenses

Each judge of the juvenile court shall receive the same salary as the district judges of the Nineteenth Judicial District Court, payable monthly upon his own warrant, in the same manner as the district judges are paid and shall receive the same emoluments, benefits, and expenses as are now and hereafter provided by law for judges of the Nineteenth Judicial District Court.

Acts 1990, No. 158, §1, eff. July 1, 1990; Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 145, §1, eff. July 7, 1994.
