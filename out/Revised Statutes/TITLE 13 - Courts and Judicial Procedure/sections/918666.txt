§2575.7.  Additional administrative adjudication procedures; certain parishes
A.  In any parish with a population of not less than thirty-five thousand five hundred persons and not more than thirty-six thousand persons, according to the most recent federal decennial census, the term "housing violation" as used in this Chapter shall also encompass violations of building codes, zoning, vegetation, and nuisance ordinances.
B.  In any parish with a population of not less than thirty-five thousand five hundred persons and not more than thirty-six thousand persons, according to the most recent federal decennial census, the procedures for administrative adjudication provided in this Chapter may also be utilized in matters involving licensing and permits and any other ordinance violations that may be determined by the parish governing authority.
Acts 2014, No. 828, §1.
