PART III.  MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS
§4261.301.  Reliance
A supported decisionmaking agreement that complies with Part II of this Chapter is
presumed valid.  A party may rely on the presumption of validity unless the party has actual
knowledge that the supported decisionmaking agreement was not validly executed.
Acts 2020, No. 258, §2.
