§756.5.  East Feliciana Parish; branch office
The clerk of court of the parish of East Feliciana may establish and maintain a branch office in Jackson in the parish of East Feliciana, without cost to the state, to be used to issue marriage licenses, to provide ex officio notary services, to perform microfilm services, to accept passport applications, and to be a depository of certain duplicate records.
Acts 1986, No. 356, §1.
