§2085.  Shreveport; salary of deputy marshals

The chief deputy and other deputy marshals of the City Court of Shreveport shall be paid an annual salary in an amount set by and paid by the city of Shreveport, payable monthly on their respective warrants.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 159, §1.
