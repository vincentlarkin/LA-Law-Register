§621.36.  Thirty-Sixth judicial district
The Thirty-Sixth Judicial District Court shall have two judges.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 164, §7, eff. Jan. 1, 1979; Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 56, §1, eff. May 8, 1996.
