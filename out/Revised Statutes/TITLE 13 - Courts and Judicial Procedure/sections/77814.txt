§4683.  Same; delivery to depository of copy of brief and transcript; recall
The clerk of the supreme court is directed to deliver to the Law Library of the Louisiana State University, the depository designated in R.S. 13:4682, a copy of each brief filed in the supreme court, and to deliver to this depository a copy of the transcript when each case is finally disposed of by the supreme court.  Any records so deposited may be recalled at pleasure by requisition of the chief justice, any associate justice or by the clerk of the supreme court.
