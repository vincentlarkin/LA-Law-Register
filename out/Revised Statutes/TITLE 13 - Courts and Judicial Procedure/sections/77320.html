§2562.10.  Code of Civil Procedure; applicability

The provisions of the Louisiana Code of Civil Procedure Book 8, shall be applicable to this court in all civil cases except as otherwise herein provided.  

Acts 1966, No. 5, §10, eff. June 9, 1966, at 1:45 P.M.
