§1828.  Enforcement of registered determination

A.  A court of this state may grant any relief normally available under the law of this state to enforce a registered child custody determination made by a court of another state.

B.  A court of this state shall recognize and enforce, but may not modify, except in accordance with Subpart B, a registered child custody determination of a court of another state.

Acts 2006, No. 822, §1, eff. Aug. 15, 2007.
