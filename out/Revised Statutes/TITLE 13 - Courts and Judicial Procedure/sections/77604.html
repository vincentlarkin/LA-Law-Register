§3853.  Service and recordation of notices

The sheriff shall serve one of the notices mentioned in R.S. 13:3852 on the party, or on each of the parties, whose property is to be seized, and shall file another of these notices for recordation in the office of the recorder of mortgages.  

Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
