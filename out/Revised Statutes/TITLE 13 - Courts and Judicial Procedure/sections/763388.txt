§5802.2.  Minimum salary for constables and justices of the peace in St. Landry Parish
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law to the contrary, the police jury of the parish of St. Landry shall pay the constables and justices of the peace thereof a salary of not less than one hundred dollars per month.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 703, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1702.2 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
