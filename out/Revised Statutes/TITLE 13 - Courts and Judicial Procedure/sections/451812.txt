§1860.  Duration of abduction prevention order
An abduction prevention order remains in effect until the earliest of:
(1)  The time stated in the order.
(2)  The emancipation of the child.
(3)  The child's attaining eighteen years of age.
(4)  The time the order is modified, revoked, vacated, or superseded by a court with jurisdiction under the provisions of R.S. 13:1813 through 1815 and other applicable law of this state.
Acts 2007, No. 369, §1.
