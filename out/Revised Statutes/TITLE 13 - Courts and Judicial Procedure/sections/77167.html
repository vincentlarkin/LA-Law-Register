§2485.2.  Qualifications of judge and marshal

The city judge must be licensed to practice law in the state of Louisiana for at least five years previous to his election and a qualified resident elector of the territorial jurisdiction of the court for at least two years prior to his election.  The city marshal must be a qualified resident elector of the territorial jurisdiction of the court for at least two years prior to his election.  

Acts 1962, No. 122, §2.
