§1404.  Salary of judges

The judges of the family court shall receive the same salary as the district judges of the nineteenth judicial district court, payable monthly upon their own warrants, in the same manner as the district judges are paid.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 89, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 620, §12, eff. Jan. 1, 1979.
