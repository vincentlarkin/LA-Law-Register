§621.38.  Thirty-Eighth judicial district

The Thirty-Eighth Judicial District Court shall have one judge.  

Added by Acts 1978.  No. 14, §2, eff. March 1, 1979; Acts 1978, No. 39, §22, eff. March 1, 1979.
