CHAPTER 24-A. SUPPORTED DECISIONMAKING AGREEMENT ACT

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§4261.101. Short title

            This Chapter may be cited as the Supported Decisionmaking Agreement Act, and may also be cited as the Dustin Gary Act.

            Acts 2020, No. 258, §2.
