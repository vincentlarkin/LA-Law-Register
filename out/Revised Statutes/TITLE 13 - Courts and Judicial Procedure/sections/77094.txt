§2086.  Shreveport; clerks and deputy clerks
The judges of the city court of Shreveport shall appoint one clerk for the civil section of the court, one clerk for the criminal section of the court, and not less than four deputy clerks.  Each clerk shall keep his respective minutes and dockets.
Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
