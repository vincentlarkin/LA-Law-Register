§5531.  Surplus in sheriff's civil fee account
Where a surplus of fees has accumulated in a sheriff's civil fee account, Parish of Orleans excepted, and the surplus has remained unclaimed for a period of twenty years or more, the sheriff may pay the amount of said surplus over to the general fund of his parish.
