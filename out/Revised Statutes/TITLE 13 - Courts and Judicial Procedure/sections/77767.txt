§4406.  Sections applicable to Orleans Parish
The provisions of R.S. 13:4405 apply only to Orleans Parish.  The provisions of R.S. 13:4341 have no application to Orleans Parish.  The provisions of R.S. 13:4343 through 13:4350, R.S. 13:4353 through 13:4357, and R.S. 13:4359 through 13:4366 are applicable to all parishes, including Orleans Parish.
Amended by Acts 1960, No. 32, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
