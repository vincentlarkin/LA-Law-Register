§5583.  Compensation of civil sheriff
The civil sheriff of the parish of Orleans shall receive in compensation for all services required of him by law the sum of twenty-five thousand dollars per annum as salary.
Amended by Acts 1952, No. 32, §1; Acts 1958, No. 244, §1; Acts 1960, No. 101, §1; Acts 1965, No. 69; Acts 1967, No. 60, §1; Acts 1970, No. 80, §1; Acts 1974, No. 135, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1502 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
