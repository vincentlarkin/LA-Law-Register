§621.5.  Fifth judicial district

The Fifth Judicial District Court shall have three judges.

Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1958, No. 71, §1; Acts 1972, No. 11, §1, emerg. eff. June 8, 1972, at 4:30 P.M.; Acts 1978, No. 39, §5, eff. June 1, 1978.
