§587.1.  Ninth Judicial District; judges

The judges of the Ninth Judicial District Court, by rule adopted by a majority vote of the judges sitting en banc, may designate and assign to any division of the court any or all types of matters of which the court has jurisdiction.

Acts 1997, No. 127, §1, eff. June 13, 1997.
