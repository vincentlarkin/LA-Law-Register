§4251.104.  Communication between courts
A.  A court of this state may communicate with a court in another state concerning
a proceeding arising under this Act.  The court may allow the parties to participate in the
communication.  Except as otherwise provided in Subsection B of this Section, the court
shall make a record of the communication.  The record may be limited to the fact that the
communication occurred.
B.  Courts may communicate concerning schedules, calendars, court records, and
other administrative matters without making a record.
Acts 2016, No. 333, §1.
