§5088.  Injunction

Where a case is presented under R.S. 13:5087, the judge before whom the action is pending, may issue on proper showing by rule, an injunction, without bond, restraining the defendant from continuing such practices until the termination of the suit, or from destroying or concealing its books or records.  This remedy shall not be exclusive of such other remedies as may flow from existing laws.
