§2077.  Lafayette; motor vehicles for office of marshal
The city of Lafayette shall pay for the maintenance and operation of the motor vehicles used or employed by the city marshal and his deputies in the performance of their respective duties.
Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
