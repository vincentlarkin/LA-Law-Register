§3239.  Venue in action on official bond of state officer

An action on the official bond of a state officer, against the latter or his surety, or both, may be brought in the parish where the officer performs the duties of his office.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
