§621.4.  Fourth judicial district
A.  The Fourth Judicial District court shall have eleven judges.
B.  Four judges shall be elected from election section one.  These judges shall serve in Divisions G, H, I, and J.
C.  Seven judges shall be elected from election section two.  These judges shall serve in Divisions A, B, C, D, E, F, and K.
Acts 1984, No. 56, §1(A), eff. June 8, 1984; Acts 1991, No. 8, §1(A), eff. June 6, 1991; Acts 1995, No. 377, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 2007, No. 64, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2008; Acts 2007, No. 210, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2008.
