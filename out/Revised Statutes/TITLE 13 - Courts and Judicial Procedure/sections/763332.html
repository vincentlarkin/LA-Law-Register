§5597.  Acceptance of bond without referring matter to court

The sheriff may accept the surety without referring the matter to the court, in which case the laws in reference to the assignment of the bond, its return into court and proceedings to impose liability upon the sheriff shall apply.

Redesignated from R.S. 33:1518 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
