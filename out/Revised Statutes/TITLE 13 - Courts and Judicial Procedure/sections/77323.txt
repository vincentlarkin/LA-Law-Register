§2562.13.  Sheriff as executive officer
The executive officer of this court shall be the sheriff of the Parish of Jefferson.  All writs and processes in either criminal or civil matters issued by this court shall be served by the sheriff or his deputy.  The sheriff shall charge and receive fees as provided for by law, not to exceed those applicable to the 24th Judicial District Court.
Acts 1966, No. 5, §13, eff. June 9, 1966, at 1:45 p.M.
