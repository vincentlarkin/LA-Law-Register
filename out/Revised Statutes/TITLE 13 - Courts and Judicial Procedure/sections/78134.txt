§966.1.  Twentieth Judicial District; additional court reporter; salary
A.  In addition to the court reporter appointed under the provisions of R.S. 13:961(A), there shall be appointed by each judge of the Twentieth Judicial District, comprising the parishes of East Feliciana and West Feliciana, a court reporter whose salary shall be paid solely by the state of Louisiana, but who shall in all other respects be governed by the provisions of R.S. 13:961.
B.  Commencing September 1, 2003, the salary of the court reporters provided for in Subsection A shall be thirty-five thousand dollars per annum.
