PART III.  ENFORCEMENT OF FOREIGN JUDGMENTS ACT
§4241.  Definition
In this Part "foreign judgment" means any judgment, decree, or order of a court of the United States or of any other court which is entitled to full faith and credit in this state.
Acts 1985, No. 464, §1.
