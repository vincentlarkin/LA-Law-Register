§5592.  Forfeiture of costs for overcharging any item
If the sheriff overcharges any item in any bill of costs, he shall forfeit the whole bill containing the item overcharged.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:1513 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
