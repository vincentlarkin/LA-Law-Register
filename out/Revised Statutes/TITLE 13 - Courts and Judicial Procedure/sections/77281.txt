§2501.  Fines imposed remitted monthly to city treasurer
Each judge of the Municipal and Traffic Court of New Orleans shall ensure that all
fines collected are remitted monthly to the city treasurer of New Orleans.
Acts 2011, No. 339, §1; Acts 2014, No. 845, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017; Acts 2016, No.
631, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
