§3418.  Delivery of copies of pleadings and briefs
Copies of all pleadings and briefs filed or submitted in any proceeding in which a director of personnel is a party, as herein provided, shall be delivered to the director by the party filing or submitting the same.  The director of personnel shall deliver to all adverse parties in such proceeding a copy of all pleadings and briefs filed or submitted on his behalf.
Acts 1962, No. 57, §3.
