§2072.  Bogalusa; vacation of judge; compensation of judge ad hoc

The judge of the city court of Bogalusa is entitled to an annual vacation of two weeks with pay.  The judge ad hoc appointed to serve in his place shall be paid an amount proportionate to the salary of the city judge for the time served, payable in equal proportions by the city of Bogalusa and the parish of Washington.  

Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
