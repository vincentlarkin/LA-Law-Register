§2590.2.  Clerk of court appointment in Jefferson Parish
Notwithstanding the provision of R.S. 42:63(D), the elected clerk of the court of the parish of Jefferson shall serve as ex officio clerk of court for a consolidated Justice of the Peace Litter Court of Jefferson Parish.
Acts 2001, No. 749, §1, eff. June 25, 2001.
