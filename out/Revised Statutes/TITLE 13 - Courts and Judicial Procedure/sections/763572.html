§5973.  Disbursements from district funds

All funds collected by a district from indebtedness shall be paid into a special fund to be established by the clerk of court and applied in accordance with law.  Expenditures from the fund shall be subject to the public bid laws of the state of Louisiana.

Acts 2006, No. 674, §1, eff. June 29, 2006; Redesignated from R.S. 33:9603 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
