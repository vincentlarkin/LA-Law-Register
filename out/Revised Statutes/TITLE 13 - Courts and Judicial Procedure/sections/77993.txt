§621.16.  Sixteenth judicial district
The sixteenth judicial district court shall have eight judges.  Two judges shall be elected from election section one and six judges shall be elected from election section two.
Acts 1984, No. 56, §3(A), eff. June 8, 1984; Acts 1993, No. 214, §1.
