CHAPTER 4.  DISTRICT COURTS ORLEANS PARISH EXCEPTED
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§471.  Scope of Chapter 4
The provisions of R.S. 13:472 through 13:967 inclusive shall not apply to the district courts of Orleans parish, except as provided in Title 13, Chapter 5.
Acts 2006, No. 621, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 873, §1, eff. July 9, 2008; Acts 2012, No. 474, §5(A).
