§5602.  City and state not liable for costs of criminal sheriff

Neither the state nor the city of New Orleans shall be liable for or make any appropriation to pay any costs of the criminal sheriff in any criminal case, except as provided in R.S. 15:566.

Amended by Acts 1974, No. 550, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1522 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
