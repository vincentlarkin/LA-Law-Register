§1836.  Appeals
An appeal may be taken from a final order in a proceeding under this Subpart in accordance with expedited appellate procedures in other civil cases.  Unless the court enters a temporary emergency order under R.S. 13:1816, the enforcing court may not stay an order enforcing a child custody determination pending appeal.
Acts 2006, No. 822, §1, eff. Aug. 15, 2007.
