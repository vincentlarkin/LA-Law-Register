§3043.  Jury commission

The membership of the jury commission, the qualifications, appointment, term of service, oath, and quorum of the commissioners, and the requirement of public meetings are governed by Article 404 of the Louisiana Code of Criminal Procedure.  Notice of jury commission meetings is governed by Article 405 of the Louisiana Code of Criminal Procedure.  

Amended by Acts 1966, No. 313, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1967.
