§1144.  Provision in budget
The city of New Orleans shall provide in its budget a sum sufficient for the payment of the remuneration to retired judges of the civil district court.  This sum shall be payable to the retired judge monthly upon his own warrant.
Acts 2006, No. 621, §19(A), eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 675, §1, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2008, No. 873, §1, eff. July 9, 2008; Acts 2011, No. 340, §2, eff. June 29, 2011; Acts 2012, No. 474, §4(B)(2).
