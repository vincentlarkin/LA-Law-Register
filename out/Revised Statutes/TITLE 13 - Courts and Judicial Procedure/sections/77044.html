§1951.1.  Pineville City Court; additional jurisdiction

In addition to all other jurisdiction now vested in the Pineville City Court, said court shall have territorial jurisdiction over juvenile and civil matters of Ward Eleven of Rapides Parish.  

Acts 1991, No. 16, §1.
