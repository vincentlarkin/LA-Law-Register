§3053.  Compensation of jury commissioners

All jury commissioners are entitled to recover compensation at the rate of seventy-five dollars per day during actual attendance at meetings and an additional fee of twenty-one cents per mile for each mile going to and returning from their place of meeting.  

Amended by Acts 1958, No. 525, §1; Acts 1971, No. 130, §1; Acts 1974, No. 139, §1; Acts 1974, No. 272, §1; Acts 1975, No. 193, §1; Acts 1981, No. 832, §1.
