§2562.14.  Prosecution of criminal cases

All criminal cases shall be prosecuted by the district attorney for the Parish of Jefferson, or through an assistant appointed by him.  

Acts 1966, No. 5, §14 eff. June 9, 1966, at 1:45 P.M.
