§4354.  Omission of any formality required by R.S. 13:4353 does not affect validity of sheriff's sale

The omission of the sheriff to set out in his act of sale any of the recitals required by R.S. 13:4353 does not affect the validity of the judicial sale made by the sheriff.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
