§2152.1.  Additional salary for judges of the first city court of New Orleans
In addition to all salary now paid by the state to the judges of the First City Court of New Orleans, the city of New Orleans may pay to each of said judges an additional salary payable monthly on his own warrant, and the city of New Orleans shall provide in its annual budget a sum sufficient for the payment of such additional salary.
Added by Acts 1961, No. 83, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 636, §1.
