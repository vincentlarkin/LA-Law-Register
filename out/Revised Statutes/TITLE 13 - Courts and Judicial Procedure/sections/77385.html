PART II.  PARTICULAR COURTS

§2601.  Justice of the peace ward; West Feliciana Parish

Effective upon the expiration in 1972 of the existing terms of office of the justices of the peace in all wards of the parish of West Feliciana, there shall be one justice of the peace ward for the parish of West Feliciana, which shall encompass the entire parish, with one justice of the peace therein.  

Added by Acts 1970, No. 12, §1.
