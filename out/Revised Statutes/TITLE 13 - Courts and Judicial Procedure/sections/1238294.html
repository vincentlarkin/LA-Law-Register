§2575.9. Additional administrative adjudication procedures in Tangipahoa Parish

            A. As applied in Tangipahoa Parish, the term "housing violation" as used in this Chapter shall also encompass building codes, zoning, vegetation, and nuisance ordinances.

            B. In Tangipahoa Parish, the procedures for administrative adjudication provided in this Chapter may also be used in matters involving licensing and permits and any other ordinance violations that may be determined by the parish governing authority.

            Acts 2021, No. 153, §1.
