§2562.3.  Criminal jurisdiction
This court shall have criminal jurisdiction concurrent with that of the district court, for the trial of all persons charged with offenses or crimes committed within its territorial boundaries, but shall not include capital crimes or those crimes or offenses which are punishable by imprisonment at hard labor under the laws of this state.
Acts 1966, No, 5, §3, eff. June 9, 1966, at 1:45 P.M.
