§996.15.  Judges; prohibition against payment of salaries from judicial clerk's fund
No salary shall be paid from the judicial clerk's fund for the Twenty-Ninth Judicial District to any of the judges of the Twenty-Ninth Judicial District except as may be paid for administering the said fund and then only after prior legislative approval.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 511, §1, eff. July 19, 1981.
