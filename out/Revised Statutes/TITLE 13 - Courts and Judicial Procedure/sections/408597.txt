§1842.  Transitional provision
A motion or other request for relief made in a child custody proceeding or to enforce a child custody determination which was commenced before August 15, 2007, is governed by the law in effect at the time the motion or other request was made.
Acts 2006, No. 822, §1, eff. Aug. 15, 2007.
