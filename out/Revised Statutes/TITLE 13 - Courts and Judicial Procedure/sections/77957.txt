§5130.  Bonds invalidated only for substantial defects; matters of form disregarded
No court in which a proceeding to invalidate or sustain bonds is brought shall invalidate the bonds unless it finds substantial defects, material errors and omissions in the incidents of such bond issue.  Matters of form shall be disregarded.
Acts 1972, No. 385, §1, eff. July 26, 1972.
