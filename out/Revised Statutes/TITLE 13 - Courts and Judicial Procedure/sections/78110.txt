§847.1.  Traffic violations; fees to clerk of court; Twenty-seventh Judicial District
In addition to any other fees authorized by law, the clerks of the Twenty-seventh Judicial District may assess a fee not to exceed five dollars for the processing of payment of a citation issued for any offense involving the operation of a motor vehicle in violation of any state or local law.
Acts 1995, No. 970, §1.
