§5565.  Remittance of tax collections; duties of sheriff or tax collector

Each sheriff or other official responsible for tax collection shall deduct the amount certified by the legislative auditor pursuant to R.S. 24:513(O), and remit that amount of money to the appropriate public retirement system as provided by law.

Acts 2012, No. 218, §1.
