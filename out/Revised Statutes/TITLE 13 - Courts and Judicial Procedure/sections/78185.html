§966.31.  Judges; prohibition against payment of salaries from judicial expense fund

No salary shall be paid from the Judicial Expense Fund for the Twenty-Second Judicial District Court to any of the judges of the district except as may be paid for administering the said fund and then only after prior legislative approval.  

Added by Acts 1982, No. 617, §1.
