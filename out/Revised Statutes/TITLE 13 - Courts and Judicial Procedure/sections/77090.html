§2082.  Natchitoches; additional duties of marshal

The marshal of the city court of Natchitoches shall serve as deputy city tax collector and deputy city license tax collector of the city of Natchitoches, which is authorized to reimburse him his automobile travel expenses in connection with his duties as such, in an amount not to exceed $50 a month.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
