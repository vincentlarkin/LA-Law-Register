§4002.  Sequestration without security available to enforce privilege of farm or plantation laborer for unpaid wages
Sequestration may issue to enforce the privilege for unpaid wages of a farm or plantation laborer on the crop, and to prevent the removal thereof from the farm or plantation, without the necessity of the laborer furnishing security therefor.
Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
