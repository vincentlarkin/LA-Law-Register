§848.1.  Criminal cases; disbursement
The sheriff, clerk, marshal, or other agency or office whose duties include receiving court fines, fees, costs, assessments, and forfeitures for courts within their jurisdictions shall disburse all sums due on a monthly basis and shall provide an itemized detail of the sources of the sums.
Acts 2011, No. 366, §1.
