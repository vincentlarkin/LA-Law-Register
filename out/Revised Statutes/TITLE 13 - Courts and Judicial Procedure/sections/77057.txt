§2003.  Bossier City
Fines, forfeitures, costs, and penalties imposed in criminal prosecutions by the city court of Bossier City shall be paid into the general fund of the city of Bossier City.
Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.  Acts 1986, No. 594, §1.
