§3822.  Same; interpretation and citation

R.S. 13:3821 and 3823 shall be so interpreted and construed as to effectuate its general purposes to make uniform the law of those states which enact it, and may be cited as the Uniform Foreign Depositions Law.

Acts 2014, No. 793, §1.
