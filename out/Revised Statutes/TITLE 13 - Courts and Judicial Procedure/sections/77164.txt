§2485.17.  Seal
The court shall be provided with a seal which shall contain a vignette of the state seal, with the words "Seal of the City Court of Leesville, Louisiana", which shall be used on all orders, writs and processes issuing from the court.  However the absence of the seal shall not affect the validity of such documents.
Acts 1962, No. 122, §17.
