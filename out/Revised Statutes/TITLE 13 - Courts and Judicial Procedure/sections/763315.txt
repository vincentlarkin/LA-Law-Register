§5575.  Excess insurance
The interlocal risk management agency may enter into contracts for excess insurance, with the payment of premiums being made from the contributions of the participating sheriffs to the group self-insurance fund.  Any excess insurance purchased under this Section shall not be subject to the provisions of R.S. 22:439.
Acts 1986, No. 662, §1; Acts 1993, No. 710, §1; Acts 2008, No. 415, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1485 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
