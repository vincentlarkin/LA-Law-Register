§2488.37.  Court facilities
The city of Baker, Louisiana, shall furnish a suitable place, together with the necessary books, records, equipment and supplies for holding said court and shall provide suitable furnished offices for the city judge, city marshall, and city clerk; said offices to be equipped with fire-proof vaults or filing systems to insure preservation of city court records.
Acts 1970, No. 424, §17.
