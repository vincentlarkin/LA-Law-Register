§621.30.  Thirtieth Judicial District
The thirtieth judicial district court shall have three judges.
Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 11, §1, emerg. eff. June 8, 1972, at 4:30 P.M.; Acts 1976, No. 46, §14, eff. June 30, 1976; Acts 1977, No. 164, §7, eff. Jan. 1, 1979; Acts 2001, No. 25, §1, eff. May 24, 2001.
