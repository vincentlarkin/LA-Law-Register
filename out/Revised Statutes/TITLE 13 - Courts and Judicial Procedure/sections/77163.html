§2485.16.  Court room and offices

The town or city of Leesville shall furnish a suitable city court room and suitable offices for the city clerk and marshal.  It shall also furnish adequate fireproof vaults or other filing equipment for the preservation of the records of the court.  

The expenses of operation and maintenance of the court room and offices shall be borne by the town or city of Leesville and the police jury of Vernon Parish, Louisiana, in equal proportions.  

Acts 1962, No. 122, §16.
