§5525.  Sheriff's general funds; reimbursement by the Department of Public Safety and Corrections in certain parishes

The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, out of its appropriated funds, shall annually reimburse certain sheriff's general funds for services rendered to state correctional institutions in certain parishes.  The following sheriff's general funds in the following parishes shall be reimbursed annually in the following amounts:

(1)

West Feliciana Parish (sheriff's general fund)

$6,000
