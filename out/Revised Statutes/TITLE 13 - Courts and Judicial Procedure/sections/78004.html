§621.26.  Twenty-Sixth Judicial District

The Twenty-Sixth Judicial District Court shall have six judges.

Acts 1989, No. 174, §1, eff. June 22, 1989; Acts 2002, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 61, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2003.
