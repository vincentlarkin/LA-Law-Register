§621.10.  Tenth judicial district

The tenth judicial district court shall have two judges.

Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1960, No. 3, §1; Acts 1972, No. 11, §1, emerg. eff. June 8, 1972, at 4:30 P.M.
