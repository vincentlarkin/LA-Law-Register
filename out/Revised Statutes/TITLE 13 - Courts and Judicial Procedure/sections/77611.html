§3860.  R.S. 13:3851 through 13:3861 provide an additional mode of seizing immovable property

The provisions of R.S. 13:3851 through 13:3861 provide an additional mode of seizing immovable property in all parishes.  Nothing contained therein prevents an actual seizure of the immovable property by the sheriff, and his taking possession thereof through the appointment of a keeper therefor, or otherwise.  

Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
