§5828.  Liability for costs
The fees of the constable shall be advanced or paid by the party requiring the service to be performed.  The party may recover the same from the other party on the final termination of the cause, if cause should be decreed in his favor, and shall be protected by the bond or security as provided in R.S. 13:1215.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 461, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1979; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1738 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
