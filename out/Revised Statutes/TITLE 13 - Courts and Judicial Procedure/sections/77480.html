PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§3415.  Party not liable for slander or libel uttered by his attorney

No party to an action or proceeding is liable for any slanderous or libellous words uttered by his attorney at law.  

Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961; Acts 1990, No. 989, §5, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
