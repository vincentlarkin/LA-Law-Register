§849.  Microfilm suit records; services; fees

Whenever the Department of State forwards to the clerks of court requests for services or inquiries relating to records in suits previously transferred to the department for copying by microphotographic process, the clerks of court, in response to the request or inquiry, shall provide the necessary copying or other service and charge therefor the appropriate fees as provided in R.S. 13:841 or R.S. 13:844, or, in Orleans Parish, by R.S. 13:1213 or R.S. 13:1381.  

Added by Acts 1978, No. 397, §1.
