§2563.13.  Prosecution of criminal cases
All criminal cases shall be prosecuted by the district attorney for the Twenty-Third Judicial District, or through an assistant appointed by him.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 307, §1, eff. Jan. 3, 1977.
