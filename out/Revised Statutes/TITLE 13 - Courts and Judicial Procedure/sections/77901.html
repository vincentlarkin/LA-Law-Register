§5002.  Employment of counsel to aid state's attorney

The citizens and taxpayers, at whose request the suit is instituted, at their own exclusive cost, may employ counsel to aid the state's attorney in instituting and conducting the suit.  

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 127, §3.
