§4727.  Head of corporation; liability
Where a gambling house as defined in R.S. 13:4721 is owned by a corporation, the president or executive head thereof shall be as fully amenable to the provisions of this Sub-part and the penalties hereunder as though such president or executive head were actually the owner of the gambling house.
