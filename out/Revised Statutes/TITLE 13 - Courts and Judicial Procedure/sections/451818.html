SUBPART E.  MISCELLANEOUS PROVISION

§1862.  Relation to electronic signatures in Global and National Commerce Act and the Louisiana Uniform Electronic Transactions Act

This Part modifies, limits, and supersedes the federal Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. Section 7001, et seq., and the Louisiana Uniform Electronic Transactions Act, R.S. 9:2601 et seq., but does not modify, limit, or supersede Section 101(c) of the Act, 15 U.S.C. Section 7001(c), of that Act or authorize electronic delivery of any of the notices described in Section 103(b) of that Act, 15 U.S.C. Section 7003(b) or R.S. 9:2603(B)(4).

Acts 2007, No. 369, §1.
