CHAPTER 1A.  JUDICIARY COMMISSION

§32.  Judiciary Commission

As used in this Chapter, "commission" means the Judiciary Commission provided for by Article V, Section 25 of the Constitution of Louisiana.

Added by Acts 1968, No. 152, §1; Acts 2012, No. 811, §3, eff. July 1, 2012.
