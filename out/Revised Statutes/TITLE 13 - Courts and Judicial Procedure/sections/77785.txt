§4442.  Costs and conditions of transfer or retransfer of appeals
A court which orders the transfer or the retransfer of an appeal under the provisions of R.S. 13:4441 may regulate the costs thereof, and the costs of the prior transfer if retransferred, and may prescribe the delay within which the transfer or retransfer must be effected, and such other conditions as the court may deem proper.
Amended by Acts 1960, No. 38, §1, eff. June 20, 1960.
