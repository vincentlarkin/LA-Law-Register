§621.3.  Third Judicial District

The third judicial district court shall have three judges.

Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 620, §2, eff. June 1, 1978; Acts 2001, No. 190, §1, eff. May 31, 2001.
