§996.21.  Judges; prohibition against payment of salaries from district court fund

No salary shall be paid from the district court fund for the Seventeenth Judicial District Court to any of the judges, except as may be paid for administering the fund and then only after prior legislative approval.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 863, §1, eff. Aug. 4, 1982.
