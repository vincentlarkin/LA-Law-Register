§2603.  Justice of the peace and constable districts; Jackson Parish
The justices of the peace and constables districts for Jackson Parish shall encompass the following police jury/school board wards:
District A - Ward 1
District B - Ward 2
District C - Wards 3 and 4
District D - Wards 5, 6, 7, and 8
District E - Wards 9 and 10.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 627, §1.
