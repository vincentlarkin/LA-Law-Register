§1442.  Civil jurisdiction; in general

The civil jurisdiction of a parish court is limited by the amount in dispute and by the nature of the proceeding, as set forth in R.S. 13:1443 and R.S. 13:1444.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 328, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.
