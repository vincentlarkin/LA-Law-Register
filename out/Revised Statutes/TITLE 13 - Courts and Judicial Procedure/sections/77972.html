§5212.  Applicability of Part

The provisions of this Part, except as otherwise specifically provided by Book VIII of the Louisiana Code of Civil Procedure, shall govern and regulate the procedure in proceedings in small claims divisions of city courts.  

Acts 1987, No. 256, §1.
