§5532.  Funds for other law enforcement purposes; Vernon Parish
The sheriff of Vernon Parish may use any available funds of his office or of the law enforcement district to engage in cooperative endeavors with other law enforcement agencies within the parish for law enforcement purposes.  In furtherance of such cooperative endeavors, he may provide funding to other law enforcement agencies in the parish.
