§4251.205. Exclusive and continuing jurisdiction

            Except as otherwise provided in R.S. 13:4251.204, a court that has appointed a guardian or issued a protective order consistent with this Chapter has exclusive and continuing jurisdiction over the proceeding until it is terminated by the court or the appointment or order expires by its own terms.

            Acts 2016, No. 333, §1.
