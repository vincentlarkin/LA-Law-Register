§1622.  Evidence; pleadings; appeals
The rules of evidence, the pleadings, and other matters in the trial of cases in the juvenile court shall be the same as are provided for the trial of cases in the juvenile courts of the state.  Appeals from judgments or orders of the juvenile court shall lie in the same manner as is provided by law governing appeals in similar matters from the juvenile courts of the state.
Acts 1990, No. 158, §1, eff. July 1, 1990.
