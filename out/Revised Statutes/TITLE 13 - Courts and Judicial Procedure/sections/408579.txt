§1824.  Enforcement under Hague Convention
Under this Subpart a court of this state may enforce an order for the return of the child made under the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction as if it were a child custody determination.
Acts 2006, No. 822, §1, eff. Aug. 15, 2007.
