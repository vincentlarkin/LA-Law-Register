§621.18.  Eighteenth judicial district

The eighteenth judicial district court shall have four judges.  

Acts 1953, Ex. Sess., No. 9, §§1 to 3.  Amended by Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1; Acts 1968, No. 86, §1; Acts 1982, No. 21, §3(a), eff. July 9, 1982.
