§621.34.  Thirty-fourth Judicial District

The Thirty-fourth Judicial District Court shall have five judges.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 620, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1979; Acts 1989, No. 611, §2(A); Acts 1999, No. 489, §1, eff. June 21, 1999.
