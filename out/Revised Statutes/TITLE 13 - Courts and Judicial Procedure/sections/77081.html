§2076.  Lafayette; compensation of deputy marshal

The deputy marshal shall receive an annual salary of $3,000 payable monthly on his own warrant by the city of Lafayette and by the parish of Lafayette in the proportion of $1,650 by the city and $1,350 by the parish.

Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.
