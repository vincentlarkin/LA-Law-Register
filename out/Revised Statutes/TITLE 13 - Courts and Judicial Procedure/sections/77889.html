§4950.  Time for payment by purchaser

Nothing contained in R.S. 13:4941 through 13:4951 shall be construed or understood to authorize the purchaser at a judicial sale to refuse carrying the same into effect, or to delay the payment of the price for a greater space of time than is now allowed by law.
