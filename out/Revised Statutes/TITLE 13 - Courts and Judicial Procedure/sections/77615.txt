§3864.  Seizure of rights in pending suit; rights of seizing creditor
When the rights and interests of a litigant in a pending lawsuit are seized, the seizing creditor shall not interfere with the progress of the suit nor thereby acquire control of the action nor be permitted to sell these rights and interests under the seizure except as provided in R.S. 13:3863 through 13:3867.
