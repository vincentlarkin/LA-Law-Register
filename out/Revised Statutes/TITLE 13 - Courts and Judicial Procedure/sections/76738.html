PART III.  CLERKS AND DEPUTY CLERKS

§121.  Number, appointment, and removal

There shall be one clerk of the Supreme Court, and five deputy  clerks, who shall be appointed by the court and removable at its pleasure.

Acts 1999, No. 994, §1.
