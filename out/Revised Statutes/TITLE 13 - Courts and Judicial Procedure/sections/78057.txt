§74.  Crier for Supreme Court; salary
There shall be a crier for the supreme court, who shall be appointed by the judges of the supreme court, and be commissioned by the civil sheriff for the parish of Orleans as a deputy sheriff.  The crier shall receive from the state treasury on his own warrant approved by the chief justice the sum of thirty-one hundred dollars, per annum, payable monthly.
Amended by Acts 1950, No. 477, §1; Acts 1952, No. 22, §1.
