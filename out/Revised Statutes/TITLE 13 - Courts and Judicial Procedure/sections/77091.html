§2083.  Shreveport; vacation of judges

Each judge of the city court of Shreveport is entitled to an annual vacation of thirty days each calendar year, during which time at least one of the divisions of the court must continue in operation.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1961; Acts 1985, No. 375, §1.
