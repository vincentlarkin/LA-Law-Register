§2575.3.  Additional administrative adjudication procedures in Livingston Parish

A.  In the parish of Livingston, the term "housing violation" as used in this Chapter shall also encompass building codes, zoning, vegetation, and nuisance ordinances.

B.  In the parish of Livingston, the procedures for administrative adjudication provided in this Chapter may also be used in matters involving licensing and permits and any other ordinance violations that may be determined by the Livingston Parish Council.

Acts 2009, No. 316, §1.
