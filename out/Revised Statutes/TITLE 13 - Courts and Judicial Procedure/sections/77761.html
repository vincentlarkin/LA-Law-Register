§4364.  Sheriff to appoint appraiser if party does not appoint; delivery of appraisal

A.  If a party neglects to appoint an appraiser or to notify the sheriff within the time designated, the sheriff shall appoint an appraiser for him.  

B.  The appraisal of any appraiser appointed by the sheriff shall be made and delivered to the sheriff at a time prior to the sale.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 32, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1961.  Acts 1986, No. 214, §1.
