§122.  Salaries

The clerk and five deputy clerks of the supreme court shall receive from the state annual salaries which shall be established by the supreme court, subject to appropriation of funds for such purpose.

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 23, §1; Acts 1956, No. 306, §1; Acts 1963, No. 4, §1; Acts 1966, No. 469, §1; Acts 1970, No. 307, §3; Acts 1973, No. 167, §1; Acts 1999, No. 994, §1.
