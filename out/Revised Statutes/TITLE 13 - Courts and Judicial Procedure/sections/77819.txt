§47.  Legislative authority for judicial salaries
Any increase in salaries may be enacted by the legislature only after submission of the aforesaid report and must be approved by a favorable vote of the majority of the elected members of each house, whether in an odd-numbered or even-numbered year, or at any extraordinary session if included within the objects of that session.
Acts 1995, No. 1077, §1; Acts 2001, No. 225, §1, eff. June 1, 2001.
