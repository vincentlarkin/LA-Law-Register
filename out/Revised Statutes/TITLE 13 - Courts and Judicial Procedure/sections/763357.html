§5708.  Orleans Parish; coroners, employees; compensation

The salaries of the coroner, assistant coroners, and other employees of the office of the coroner of Orleans Parish shall be set by the city council and paid by the city of New Orleans.

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 151, §2.  Acts 1984, No. 570, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1557 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
