§621.23. Twenty-Third Judicial District

            The Twenty-Third Judicial District Court shall have six judges. One judge shall be elected from election section one, and five judges shall be elected from election section two.

            Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1. Amended by Acts 1958, No. 391, §1; Acts 1968, No. 464, §1; Acts 1982, No. 21, §4(a), eff. July 9, 1982; Acts 1992, No. 1069, §1, eff. July 14, 1992; Acts 1993, No. 780, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 1993; Acts 2024, No. 496, §1.
