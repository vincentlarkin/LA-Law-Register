§2104.  Eunice; surplus in civil fee account
In the instance in which a surplus of filing fees and costs has accumulated in the Eunice City Court civil fee account and the surplus has remained unclaimed for a period of five years or more, the judge of the court may transfer the amount of the surplus to the general operational fund of the court.  This Section shall not affect the payment of civil fees to which the judge is entitled pursuant to R.S. 13:1874(A).
Acts 1991, No. 903, §1, eff. July 23, 1991.
