§4108.3.  Relation to Chapter 9 of the Louisiana Commercial Laws

The rights or absence thereof of a secured creditor to pursue and collect a deficiency from a debtor, guarantor, or surety in connection with a secured transaction subject to Chapter 9 of the Louisiana Commercial Laws (R.S. 10:9-101, et seq.) shall be exclusively subject to the provisions of Chapter 9.  

Added by Acts 1989, No. 137, §8, eff. Sept. 1, 1989.
