§2487.15.  Salary of clerk

The salary of the clerk shall be a minimum of four hundred dollars per month.  Such salary shall be paid in equal proportions by the city of Slidell and the police jury of the parish of St. Tammany, state of Louisiana.

Acts 1964, No. 102, §15.  Amended by Acts 1973, No. 180, §1.
