§10.1.  Completion of term to which elected; certain judges reaching mandatory retirement age within twelve months of election
Any judge who was in office on December 31, 1974, and who reaches his mandatory retirement age within twelve months prior to the primary election at which his successor is to be elected, may serve through the end of the term to which he was elected.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 483, §1.
