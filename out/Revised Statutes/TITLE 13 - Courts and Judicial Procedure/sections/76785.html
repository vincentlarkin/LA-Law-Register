§1341.2.  Office expenses, reimbursement to judges

The provisions of R.S. 13:698 shall be applicable to judges of the criminal district court and to judges of the civil district court.

Added by Acts 1970, No. 166, §1.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 605, §1; Acts 2006, No. 621, §19(A), eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 675, §1, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2008, No. 873, §1, eff. July 9, 2008; Acts 2011, No. 340, §2, eff. June 29, 2011; Acts 2012, No. 474, §4(B)(2).
