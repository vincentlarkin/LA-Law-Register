§2488.23.  Civil jurisdiction and procedure

The civil jurisdiction, and the pleadings, practice, and procedure of the court, shall be as provided by law.  

Acts 1970, No. 424, §3.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 716, §1.  Acts 1986, No. 226, §1.
