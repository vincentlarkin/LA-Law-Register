§2575.8.  Additional administrative adjudication procedures; city of Monroe
A.  As applied in the city of Monroe, the term "housing violation" as used in this
Chapter shall also encompass building codes, zoning, vegetation, and nuisance ordinances,
and ordinances that provide for the regulation of sewerage and drainage systems.
B.  In the city of Monroe, the procedures for administrative adjudication provided in
this Chapter may also be used in matters involving licensing and permits and any other
ordinance violations as determined by the governing authority of the city of Monroe.
Acts 2021, No. 256, §1, eff. June 14, 2021.
