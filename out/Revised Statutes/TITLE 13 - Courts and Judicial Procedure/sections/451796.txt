SUBPART B.  JURISDICTION
§1855.  Jurisdiction
A.  A petition under this Part may be filed only in a court that has jurisdiction to make a child-custody determination with respect to the child at issue under the provisions of R.S. 13:1813 through 1816.
B.  A court of this state has temporary emergency jurisdiction under the provisions of R.S. 13:1816, if the court finds a credible risk of abduction.
Acts 2007, No. 369, §1.
