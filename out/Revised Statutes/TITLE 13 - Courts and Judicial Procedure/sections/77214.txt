§2487.9.  Salary of marshal
The marshal of the city court shall receive as compensation for his services a minimum salary of forty-eight hundred dollars annually, payable monthly on his own warrant and which, except as otherwise indicated, shall be paid in equal proportions by the city of Slidell, Louisiana and the police jury of St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana.
Acts 1964, No. 102, §9.  Amended by Acts 1973, No. 180, §1.
