§5907.  Rollback of millage by multi-parish districts
Each taxing district whose jurisdiction encompasses more than one parish shall adjust their millage so that the adjusted millage is uniform throughout the district.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 605, §1, eff. July 19, 1977; Redesignated from R.S. 33:9005.2 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
