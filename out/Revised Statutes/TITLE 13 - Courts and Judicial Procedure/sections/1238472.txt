PART XVIII-A.  ONLINE DISPUTE RESOLUTION PILOT PROJECT PROGRAM
§5213.  Declaration of purpose
The purpose of the Online Dispute Resolution Pilot Project Program, hereinafter
referred to as "the program" is to further the primary objectives of the small claims division
pursuant to R.S. 13:5200. In an effort to improve access to justice, the Louisiana Supreme
Court has initiated the program in the City Court of East St. Tammany to include all small
claims cases filed in the court beginning January 1, 2022.
Acts 2021, No. 281, §1.
NOTE: See R.S. 13:5226 regarding termination of this Part on Aug. 1, 2025.
