§5596.  Court's finding relieves sheriff of liability

If the court finds that the surety is solvent and possessed of the qualifications prescribed by law, the bond shall be made in favor of the parties to the case, and the sheriff shall be relieved of all responsibility.

Redesignated from R.S. 33:1517 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
