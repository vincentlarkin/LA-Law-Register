§2563.15.  Office space
Each governing authority of the municipalities of Donaldsonville, Gonzales and Sorrento shall provide suitable space within each municipality, respectively, for the operation of the court.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 307, §1, eff. Jan. 3, 1977.
