CHAPTER 33-C. SWIFT AND CERTAIN PROBATION PILOT PROGRAM

§5371. Creation

            The provisions of this Chapter are to create a pilot program in the Twenty-fourth Judicial District Court entitled the Swift and Certain Probation Pilot Program.

            Acts 2016, No. 676, §1, eff. June 17, 2016.
