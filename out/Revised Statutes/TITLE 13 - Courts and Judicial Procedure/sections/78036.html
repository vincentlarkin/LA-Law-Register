§700.1.  First Judicial District, law clerks

Each judge of the First Judicial District Court may employ a law clerk, provided funds are available, to perform such research duties as the judge who employs the law clerk may assign.  The salary of each such law clerk shall be fixed and paid on the warrant of the senior judge.  

Acts 1984, No. 145, §1.
