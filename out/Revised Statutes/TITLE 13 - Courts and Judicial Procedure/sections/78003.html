§621.25.  Twenty-fifth judicial district

The twenty-fifth judicial court district shall have four judges.  

Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1960, No. 4, §1; Acts 1964, No. 314, §2; Acts 1970, No. 60, §1.
