CHAPTER 6.  JUVENILE COURTS
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§1561.  Gun court docket; probation supervision program
Each court exercising juvenile jurisdiction may by local rule designate as a gun division one or more divisions to which gun and weapon-related offenses are assigned and may establish an intensive probation program to be administered by the presiding judge or judges thereof or by an employee designated by the court under the direction of the office of juvenile justice.
Acts 2013, No. 352, §1, eff. June 17, 2013.
