§5975.  Powers of clerk of court not diminished
It is the purpose of this Chapter to create special districts to provide an additional method for financing the clerks of courts' offices.  Nothing in this Chapter shall be interpreted as diminishing any other authority of the clerks of court as delegated to them in the constitution and laws of this state.
Acts 2006, No. 674, §1, eff. June 29, 2006; Redesignated from R.S. 33:9605 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
