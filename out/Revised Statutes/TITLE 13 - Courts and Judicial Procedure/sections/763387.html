§5802.1.  Minimum salary for constables and justices of the peace in Ouachita parish

Any other provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the police jury of the parish of Ouachita shall pay the constables and justices of the peace thereof a salary of not less than thirty dollars per month.

Added by Acts 1962, No. 77, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1702.1 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
