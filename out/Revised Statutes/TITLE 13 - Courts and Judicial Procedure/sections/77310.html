§2561.17.  Appellate jurisdiction

Any other law to the contrary notwithstanding, all appeals allowed by law from justice of the peace courts to district courts in civil and criminal matters shall, in the parish of Jefferson for justice of the peace courts east of the Mississippi River, be to the First Parish Court for the Parish of Jefferson instead of to the district court.  

Added by Acts 1977, No. 295, §1.
