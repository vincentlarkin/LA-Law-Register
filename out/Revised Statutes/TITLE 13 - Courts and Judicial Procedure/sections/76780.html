PART III.  CRIMINAL DISTRICT COURT

SUBPART A.  JUDGES

§1335.  Criminal district court for the parish of Orleans

There shall be one criminal district court for the parish of Orleans, which shall be composed of twelve judges.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 143, §1.  Amended by Acts 1980, No. 86, §1; Acts 1995, No. 217, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1997; Acts 2006, No. 621, §19(A), eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 675, §1, eff.
