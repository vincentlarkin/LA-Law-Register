§2575.11.  Additional administrative adjudication procedures in Acadia Parish
A.  As applied in the parish of Acadia, the term "housing violation" as used in this
Chapter shall also encompass building codes, zoning, vegetation, and nuisance ordinances.
B.  In the parish of Acadia, the procedures for administrative adjudication provided
in this Chapter may also be used in matters involving licensing and permits and any other
ordinance violations that may be determined by the parish governing authority.
C.  In the parish of Acadia, any owner or mortgagee of record of property determined
to be blighted or abandoned, or any party determined by a hearing officer to be in violation
of any ordinance enacted pursuant to this Chapter, may appeal the determination to the
appropriate district court.
Acts 2023, No. 223, §1.
