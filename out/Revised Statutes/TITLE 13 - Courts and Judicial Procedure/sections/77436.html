§3106.  Provisions applicable to Orleans Parish

The provisions of R.S. 13:3041, 3042, 3042.1, 3050, 3051, 3052, 3055, and 3056 shall apply to all parishes of the state, including Orleans Parish.  The provisions of R.S. 13:3043 through 3046, both inclusive, 3048, 3049, 3053, and 3054, shall have no application to Orleans Parish.

Acts 2003, No. 678, §1.
