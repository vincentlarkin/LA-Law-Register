§103.  Expenses of chief justice and associate justices of the supreme court; reimbursement
The chief justice and each associate justice of the supreme court shall be reimbursed for reasonable housing and other expenses incurred while on official duty, payable out of the self-generated funds provided for in R.S. 13:124 in amounts not to exceed the amounts allocated under the authority of this Section as of May 1, 1989.
Acts 1990, No. 336, §1, eff. July 10, 1990.
