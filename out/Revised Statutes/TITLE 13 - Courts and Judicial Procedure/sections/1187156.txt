§4261.103.  Purpose
The purpose of this Chapter is to recognize a less restrictive decisionmaking process
and empowers supported decisionmaking as an option over interdiction for adults with
disabilities who need assistance with decisions regarding daily living.
Acts 2020, No. 258, §2.
