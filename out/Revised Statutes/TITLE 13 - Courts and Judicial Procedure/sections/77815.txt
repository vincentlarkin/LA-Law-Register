§4684.  Same; clerk to turn over duplicate copies to law library
The clerk of the supreme court is further directed to turn over to the Law Library of the Louisiana State University, a depository, any duplicate copies of each transcript and brief in the files of the supreme court and the depository shall catalogue, index and preserve the documents.
