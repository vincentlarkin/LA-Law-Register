§993.  Judges; prohibition against payment of salaries from judicial expense fund
No salary shall be paid from the Judicial Expense Fund for the Nineteenth Judicial District to any of the judges of the Nineteenth Judicial District except as may be paid for administering the said fund and then only after prior legislative approval.
Added by Acts 1969, No. 135, §1.
