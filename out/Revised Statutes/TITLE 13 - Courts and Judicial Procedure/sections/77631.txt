§3880.  Same; costs of keeping
So long as any vessel, steamer, or other water craft shall remain in the hands of the constable, the court shall not allow costs for more than one or two keepers, if the case require it.  The costs of said keeping, in the case of the dissolution of the writ or order under which the vessel, steamer or water craft was seized, shall be borne in proportion by the parties having wrongfully prayed for it.
