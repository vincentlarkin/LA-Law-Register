CHAPTER 31.  COURT RECORDS
§4681.  Withdrawal of district court records
It is unlawful to withdraw from any district court the record, or any part thereof, of any civil or criminal proceeding heretofore or hereafter filed in such court, except as may be allowed to attorneys or parties interested in any cause by order of the clerk of court, or, in his absence, by the deputy clerk of court, as custodian of such court records.
