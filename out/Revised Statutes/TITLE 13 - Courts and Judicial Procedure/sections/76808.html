§1381.6.  Filing of pleadings and documents in criminal cases by facsimile transmission

Any pleading or document in a traffic or criminal action may be filed with the court by facsimile transmission in compliance with the provisions of the Code of Criminal Procedure Article 14.1 if permitted by the policy of the clerk of court.

Acts 2001, No. 319, §1; Acts 2006, No. 621, §19(A), eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2008, No. 675, §1, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2008, No. 873, §1, eff. July 9, 2008; Acts 2011, No. 340, §2, eff. June 29, 2011; Acts 2012, No. 474, §4(B)(2).
