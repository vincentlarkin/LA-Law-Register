§5562.  Public bid laws applicable to assessor's office
The assessor's office in each parish shall be subject to the public bid laws of the state.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 206, §3, eff. July 23, 1976; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1462 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §3.
