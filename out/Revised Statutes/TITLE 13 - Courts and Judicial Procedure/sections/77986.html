SUBPART B.  NUMBER OF JUDGES

§621.1.  First judicial district

The First Judicial District Court shall have eleven judges.  Four judges shall be elected from election section one, three judges shall be elected from election section two, and four judges shall be elected from election section three.

Acts 1956, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §1.  Amended by Acts 1966, No. 2, §1; 1973, No. 117, §1; Acts 1976, No. 46, §1; Acts 1978, No. 39, §1, eff. June 1, 1978; Acts 1982, No. 21, §1(a), eff. July 9, 1982; Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 145, §1, eff. July 7, 1994; Acts 1997, No. 241, §1, eff. June 16, 1997.
