§3046.  Selecting a new general venire; revising and supplementing a general venire

The selection of a new general venire, the revising and supplementing of a general venire, and the functions of the jury commission and of the clerk of court in connection therewith, are governed by the Louisiana Code of Criminal Procedure.  

Amended by Acts 1966, No. 313, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1967.
