§9-521.  Uniform form of written financing statement and amendment
(a)  Initial financing statement form.  A filing office that accepts written records may not refuse to accept a written initial financing statement in any form or format approved by the secretary of state, except for a reason set forth in R.S. 10:9-516(b):
(b)  Amendment form.  A filing officer that accepts written records may not refuse to accept a written record in any form approved by the secretary of state, except for a reason set forth in R.S. 10:9-516(b).
Acts 2001, No. 128, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
