CHAPTER 4-A.  FUNDS TRANSFERS

PART 1.  SUBJECT MATTER AND DEFINITIONS

§4A-101.  Short Title

This Chapter may be cited as Uniform Commercial Code -- Funds Transfers.

Added by Acts 1990, No. 1079, §4, eff. Sept. 1, 1990; Acts 2006, No. 533, §4.
