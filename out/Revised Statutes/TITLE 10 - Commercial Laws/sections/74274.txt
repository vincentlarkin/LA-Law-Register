§5-104.  Formal requirements
A letter of credit, confirmation, advice, transfer, amendment, or cancellation may be
issued in any form that is a signed record.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 92, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975; Acts 1999, No. 171, §1, eff. Jan.
1, 2000; Acts 2024, No. 773, §1.
