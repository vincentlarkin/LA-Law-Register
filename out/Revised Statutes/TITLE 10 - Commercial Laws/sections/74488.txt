§9-603.  Agreement on standards concerning rights and duties
(a)  Agreed standards.  The parties may determine by agreement the standards measuring the fulfillment of the rights of a debtor or obligor and the duties of a secured party under a rule stated in R.S. 10:9-602 if the standards are not manifestly unreasonable.
