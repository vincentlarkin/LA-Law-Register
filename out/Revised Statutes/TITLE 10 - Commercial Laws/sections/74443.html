§9-341. Bank's rights and duties with respect to deposit account

            Except as otherwise provided in R.S. 10:9-340(c), and unless the bank otherwise agrees in a signed record, a bank's rights and duties with respect to a deposit account maintained with the bank are not terminated, suspended, or modified by:

            (1) the creation, attachment, or perfection of a security interest in the deposit account;

            (2) the bank's knowledge of the security interest; or

            (3) the bank's receipt of instructions from the secured party.

            Acts 2001, No. 128, §1, eff. July 1, 2001; Acts 2024, No. 773, §1.
