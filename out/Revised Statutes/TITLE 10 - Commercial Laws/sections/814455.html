§9-809.  Priority

Chapter 9 as amended by the Act determines the priority of conflicting claims to collateral; however, if the relative priorities of the claims were established before the Act takes effect, Chapter 9 as it existed before amendment determines priority.

Acts 2012, No. 450, §2, eff. July 1, 2013.
