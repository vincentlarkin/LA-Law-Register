§9-410.  Relation to the Louisiana Trust Code
Nothing under this Chapter shall supersede the provisions of the Louisiana Trust Code that prohibit a beneficiary from alienating or encumbering a beneficial interest in the trust if the trust instrument so provides.
Acts 2001, No. 128, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
