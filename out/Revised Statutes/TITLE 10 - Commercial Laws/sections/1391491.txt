PART 2.  GENERAL TRANSITIONAL PROVISION
§13-201.  Saving clause
Except as provided in Part 3 of this Chapter, a transaction validly entered into before
August 1, 2024, and the rights, duties, and interests flowing from the transaction remain valid
thereafter and may be terminated, completed, consummated, or enforced as required or
permitted by law other than this Title or, if applicable, this Title, as though this Act had not
taken effect.
Acts 2024, No. 773, §1.
