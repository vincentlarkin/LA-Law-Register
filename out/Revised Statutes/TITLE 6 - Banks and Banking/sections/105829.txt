PART X.  CONSERVATORSHIPS, RECEIVERSHIPS, AND
INVOLUNTARY LIQUIDATIONS
§1321.  Conservatorships of savings banks
The commissioner in his sole discretion may take custody of, and appoint a conservator for, the property, liabilities, books, records, business, and assets of every kind and character of any savings bank in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 6:381 et seq.
Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
