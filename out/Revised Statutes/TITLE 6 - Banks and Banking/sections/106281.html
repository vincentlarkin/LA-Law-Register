§969.13.  Leap years

The effects of a leap year may be disregarded for purposes of determining whether the annualized loan finance charge rate or credit service charge rate exceeds the maximum rate limitations provided in this Part.

Acts 1999, No. 794, §1, eff. July 2, 1999.
