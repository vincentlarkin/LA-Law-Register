§1142.  Safety deposit boxes and safekeeping
The provisions of R.S. 6:321 through 328 relative to safety deposit boxes and
safekeeping shall be applicable to savings banks chartered under the provisions of the
Louisiana Savings Bank Act of 1990 in the same manner as they are applicable to state
banks.
Acts 2020, No. 236, §1.
