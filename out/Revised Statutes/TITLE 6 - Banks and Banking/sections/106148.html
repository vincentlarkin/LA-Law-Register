§793.  Directors' examination of association

The commissioner shall require at least once in each year that each association cause its books, records, and accounts to be examined in accordance with a regulation promulgated by the commissioner.

Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Amended by Acts 1982, No. 368, §1; Acts 1983, No. 675, §1; Acts 1986, No. 818, §1; Acts 1995, No. 248, §§1, 2, eff. June 14, 1995; Acts 1997, No. 49, §1; Acts 2001, No. 530, §1.
