TITLE 6

BANKS AND BANKING

CHAPTER 1.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§1.  Short title

This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Banking Law."

Amended by Acts 1972, No. 747, §1; Acts 1984, No. 719, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
