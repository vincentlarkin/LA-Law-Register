§1232.  Prohibited loans

No loan shall be made by any savings bank to any person owning ten percent or more of the capital stock or to any affiliated person, agent, or attorney of that savings bank either for himself or as agent, or as partner of another, except in accordance with laws and regulations to which national banks are subject, applicable to a similar transaction.

Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
