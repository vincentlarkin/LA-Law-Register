§771. Disposition of abandoned safety deposit box contents

            The provisions of R.S. 6:327 and 327.1 relative to the disposition of abandoned safety deposit box contents shall be applicable to savings and loan associations chartered under the Louisiana Savings and Loan Association Law in the same manner as they are applicable to state banks.

            Acts 2020, No. 236, §1.
