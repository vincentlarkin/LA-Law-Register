§1423.  Violations
The commissioner may enforce the provisions of this Chapter using the
commissioner's authority pursuant to R.S. 6:121.1.  Each violation of this Chapter, including
any order, rule, or regulation made or issued pursuant to this Chapter, shall constitute a
separate offense.
Acts 2022, No. 715, §1.
