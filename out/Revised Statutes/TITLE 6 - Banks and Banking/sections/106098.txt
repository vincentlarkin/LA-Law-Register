CHAPTER 9.  HOMESTEAD AND SAVINGS AND LOAN
§701.  Applicability
This Chapter is enacted under the legislature's police power and reserved constitutional power to enact law for the regulation of associations, and shall be effective as to all associations heretofore or hereafter incorporated or authorized to transact business in this state.
Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
