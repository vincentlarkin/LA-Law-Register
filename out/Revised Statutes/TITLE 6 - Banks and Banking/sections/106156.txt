§806.  Board to determine amounts to be withdrawn
The attribution to be made from receipts or other assets for the payment of withdrawals shall be under the sole control and administration of the board of directors, which shall determine the amount to be used for the payment of said withdrawals.  Before making any appropriation of the receipts which may be applied to the liquidation of the claims of withdrawing members or depositors, the board may make use of such receipts and funds as are needed for all operating expenses, the maintenance and improvements of all repossessed property, the payment or renewal of obligations, and the creation of cash reserves needed for the declaration of dividends or interest.
Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
