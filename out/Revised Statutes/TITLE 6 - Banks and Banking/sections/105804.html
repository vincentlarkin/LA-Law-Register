§1280.  Merger; commissioner's expenses

The expenses of any examination made by or at the direction of the commissioner of financial institutions in connection with a proposed merger shall be paid for by the merging institutions.

Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
