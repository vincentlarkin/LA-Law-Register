§1310.  Annual directors' examinations
The commissioner shall require at least once in each year that each savings bank cause its books, records, and accounts to be examined in accordance with a regulation promulgated by the commissioner.
Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990; Acts 2001, No. 530, §1.
NOTE:  See Acts 2001, No. 530, §2, relative to effective date.
