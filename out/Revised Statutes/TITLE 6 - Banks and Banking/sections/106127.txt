§762.  Access to books and records
The right of inspection and examination of the books and records shall be exclusively vested in:
(1)  The commissioner or his duly authorized representative as provided in this Chapter; and
(2)  Any federal or state instrumentality or agency authorized to inspect or examine the books and records of an insured association.
Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
