§1085.  Rules and regulations

The commissioner shall promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act as may be necessary to effectuate the purposes, administration, and enforcement of the provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 1999, No. 1098, §1, eff. July 9, 1999.
