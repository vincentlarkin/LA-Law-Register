§627.  Interstate supervision and regulation

The commissioner may rely upon applications, notifications, and examination reports provided by the appropriate home states or federal regulator, or enter into agreements with other state regulators to supervise and regulate the trust activities of interstate trust companies.

Acts 2003, No. 573, §1, eff. June 27, 2003.
