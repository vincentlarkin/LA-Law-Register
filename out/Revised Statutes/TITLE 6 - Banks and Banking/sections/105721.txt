PART III.  INCORPORATION AND ORGANIZATION
§1161.  Application for permit to organize
A.  Savings banks shall be organized and incorporated in the same manner as state banks under the provisions of Chapter 3 of Title 6 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
B.  Not less than five natural persons may organize a savings bank under this Chapter.
Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
