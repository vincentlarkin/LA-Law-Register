PART VI.  MEMBERS, DEPOSITORS, AND OTHER MATTERS

§761.  Members of mutual associations

Members shall be admitted to membership of a mutual association in such manner as may be prescribed in the articles of incorporation or the bylaws.

Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
