§641.1.  Public policy

It is the declared public policy of the state of Louisiana to encourage and to foster the development of credit unions under a dual chartering system of the state and federal governments.  In order to carry out this policy, the office of financial institutions of the state of Louisiana is authorized and requested to use its resources in the promotion and development of credit unions chartered under the laws of Louisiana.

Added by Acts 1978, No. 480, §1.
