CHAPTER 10-B. LOUISIANA MOTOR VEHICLE SALES

FINANCE ACT

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS AND DEFINITIONS

§969.1.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Motor Vehicle Sales Finance Act".

Acts 1999, No. 794, §1, eff. July 2, 1999.
