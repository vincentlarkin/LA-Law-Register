§5.  Public policy
It is the declared public policy of the state of Louisiana to encourage and to foster the
development of financial institutions under a dual chartering system of the state and federal
governments.  In order to carry out this policy, the office of financial institutions is
authorized and requested to use its resources in the promotion and development of the dual
chartering system under the laws of Louisiana.
Acts 1990, No. 203, §1, eff. July 3, 1990; Acts 1993, No. 960, §1; Acts 2021, No. 17,
