§650.2.  Educational committee

The credit union may establish, in a manner provided for in the bylaws, an educational committee.  The committee shall plan, and, with approval of the board of directors, carry out programs to inform members and potential members of the objectives, procedures and services of the credit union, and to encourage maximum participation in all activities of the credit union.

Added by Acts 1968, No. 180, §1.
