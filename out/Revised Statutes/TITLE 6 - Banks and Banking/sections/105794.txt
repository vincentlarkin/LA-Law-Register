PART VIII.  VOLUNTARY CORPORATE CHANGES
§1271.  Amendment of articles
A savings bank may amend and restate its articles of incorporation in accordance with the procedure set forth in Part III of Subchapter A of Chapter 3 of this Title.
Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990; Acts 2003, No. 577, §1, eff. June 27, 2003.
