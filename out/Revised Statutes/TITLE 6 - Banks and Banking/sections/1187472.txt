§1389.  Virtual currency kiosks; maximum daily transaction limit; cancellation and refund
A.  There is a maximum daily transaction limit of three thousand dollars for each user
of a virtual currency kiosk.
B.  The owner or operator of a virtual currency kiosk shall wait seventy-two hours
before processing a user's virtual currency kiosk transaction or shall, at such owner's or
operator's cost and within seventy-two hours after a virtual currency kiosk transaction, allow
the user to cancel and receive a full refund for the virtual currency kiosk transaction.
Acts 2025, No. 369, §1.
