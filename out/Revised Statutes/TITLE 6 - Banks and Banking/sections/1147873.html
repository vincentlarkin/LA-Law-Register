§659.2. Federally insured credit unions; abandonment of safety deposit box

            The provisions of R.S. 6:327 and 327.1 shall apply to abandonment of a safety deposit box leased from a federally insured credit union.

            Acts 2019, No. 188, §1; Acts 2020, No. 236, §1.
