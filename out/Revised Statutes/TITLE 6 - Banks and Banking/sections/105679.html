§1050.  Retention of surety bond

The commissioner shall retain the surety bond required by this Chapter until the list required by R.S. 6:1049 has been supplied and thirty days have passed since the financial institution or institutions have been notified in accordance with R.S. 6:1051.

Acts 1990, No. 827, §1.
