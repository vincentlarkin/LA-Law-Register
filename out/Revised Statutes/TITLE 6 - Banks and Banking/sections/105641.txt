§1003.  License; certificates
Beginning January 1, 2000, a person engaging in currency exchange shall obtain a license from the commissioner. A person may engage in such business at more than one location with one license.  The commissioner shall issue a certificate for each currency exchange location in addition to the license.
Acts 1999, No. 1208, §1, eff. July 9, 1999.
