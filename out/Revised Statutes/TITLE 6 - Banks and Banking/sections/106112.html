PART III.  AMENDMENT AND RESTATEMENT OF ARTICLES

§721.  Method of amending articles generally

An association may, in the manner provided for state banks in Part III of Subchapter A of Chapter 3 of  this Title, amend and restate its articles of incorporation.

Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1; Acts 2003, No. 17, §1, eff. May 23, 2003; Acts 2003, No. 60, §1, eff. May 23, 2003; Acts 2003, No. 577, §1, eff. June 27, 2003.
