§898.  Supervision and examination

The commissioner shall examine and supervise all foreign associations doing any such business in this state in the same manner as he examines and supervises associations of this state.  Foreign associations shall pay the supervision and examination fee imposed by R.S. 6:911, plus all fees imposed by R.S. 6:912, plus any additional costs as determined by the commissioner.  The commissioner, in his discretion, may rely on such official examination, public and private audits, and copies of reports which are supplied to him.  The commissioner is hereby authorized and directed to obtain an injunction or take any other action necessary to prevent any foreign association from doing any business of an association in this state without approval.

Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
