§1208.1.  Applicability of statute, rules and regulations
The provisions of R.S. 6:416 and the rules and regulations promulgated thereunder shall be applicable to savings banks chartered under the provisions of this Title in the same manner as they are applicable to state banks.
Acts 1997, No. 1003, §1.
