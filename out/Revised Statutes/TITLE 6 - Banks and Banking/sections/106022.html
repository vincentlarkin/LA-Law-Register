PART V.  FINANCIAL SERVICE CENTER BANKS

§541.  Declaration of policy

In order to encourage the development of the financial services industry within this state, thereby increasing employment opportunities and broadening the tax base, it is declared to be the policy of the state of Louisiana to promote the organization of financial service center banks and the retention of existing financial services within the state.

Acts 1987, No. 808, §1.
