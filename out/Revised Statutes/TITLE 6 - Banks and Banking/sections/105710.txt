§1133.  Administration
This Chapter shall be administered by the commissioner of financial institutions under the authority granted to him by Chapter 2 of Title 6 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
