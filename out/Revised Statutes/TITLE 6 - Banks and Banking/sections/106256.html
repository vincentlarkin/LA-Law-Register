§950.8.  Savings clause

Nothing in this Part shall be interpreted or construed as approving any act, actions, or conduct which is or has been or may be in violation of any existing law, nor shall anything herein contained constitute a defense to any action, suit, or proceeding pending or hereafter instituted on account of any prohibited antitrust or monopolistic act, action, or conduct.

Added by Acts 1986, No. 595, §1, eff. July 2, 1986.
