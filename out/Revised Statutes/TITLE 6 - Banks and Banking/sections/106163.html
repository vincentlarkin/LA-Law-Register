§813.  Prohibition against institution of action

No suit shall be instituted by a withdrawing member or by a depositor against an association, its officers, or representatives, except for a violation of the provisions of this Part or to enforce compliance therewith.

Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
