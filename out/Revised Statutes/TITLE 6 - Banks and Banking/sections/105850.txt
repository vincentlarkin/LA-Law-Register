CHAPTER 19.
AUTOMATED TELLER MACHINES/NIGHT
DEPOSITORY CUSTOMER SAFETY ACT
§1361.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Automated Teller Machines/Night Depository Customer Safety Act".
Acts 1995, No. 588, §1, eff. July 1, 1996.
