§1303.  Examiners
The commissioner shall appoint competent examiners who are not officers or agents of, or in any manner interested in, any savings bank, savings bank holding company, or subsidiaries or affiliates of either, except that they may be depositors therein.
Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
