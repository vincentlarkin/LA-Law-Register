§1229.1.  Applicability of rules and regulations
The provisions of R.S. 6:243 and 246, and the rules and regulations promulgated
thereunder shall be applicable to savings banks in the same manner as they are applicable to
banks.
Acts 1995, No. 248, §1, eff. June 14, 1995; Acts 2019, No. 16, §1.
