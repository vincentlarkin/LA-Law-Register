§663.  Money deposited by minors

Money deposited in shares in credit unions doing business in this state by minors themselves may be drawn out by them upon their own signature without other authorization.  The credit union shall prescribe limitations and procedures for minor accounts in the bylaws.

Added by Acts 1950, No. 391, §1; Acts 1995, No. 293, §1.
