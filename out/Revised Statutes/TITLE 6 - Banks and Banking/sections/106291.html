PART IV.  LIMITATIONS ON AGREEMENTS

AND PRACTICES

§969.22.  Attorney fees

A motor vehicle credit contract may provide for the payment of collection and enforcement attorney fees by the consumer in an amount not to exceed twenty-five percent of the total amount payable under the transaction.

Acts 1999, No. 794, §1, eff. July 2, 1999.
