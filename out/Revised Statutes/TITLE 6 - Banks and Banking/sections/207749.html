PART V.  TRUST DEPOSITS LIABILITIES

AND PLEDGE OF ASSETS

§621.  Borrowing limit

Except with the prior written approval of the commissioner, a trust company may not have outstanding liabilities exceeding an amount equal to three times its capital.  However, the commissioner may impose a lower limit consistent with the safety and soundness of the trust company.

Acts 2003, No. 573, §1, eff. June 27, 2003.
