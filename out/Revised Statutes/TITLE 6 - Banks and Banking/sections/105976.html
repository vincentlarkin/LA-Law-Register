§401.  Exclusive procedures

This Part provides the full and exclusive powers and procedures for the liquidation of state banks under the laws of this state, and no receiver or other liquidating agent shall be appointed for such purpose except as expressly provided in this Part.

Acts 1984, No. 719, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
