§924.  Holder of note may enforce

Any pledgee or other lawful holder of any note or other evidence of indebtedness due to any association may enforce in its own name or in the name of the association all appropriate remedies to enforce collection, whether or not the shares or savings account described in connection with the note is held by the pledgee or holder.

Acts 1970, No. 234, §1.  Acts 1983, No. 675, §1.
