§1135.  Federal deposit insurance

Any savings bank chartered under the supervision of this Chapter shall obtain and maintain federal deposit insurance of accounts through an insurance corporation created by an Act of Congress.

Acts 1990, No. 816, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1990.
