§551.2.  Foreign banking activities; authorized

A foreign bank may open and operate a representative office, administrative office, agency, branch, subsidiary bank, or an Edge Act or agreement corporation or operate as an international banking facility in this state subject to the provisions of this Chapter and applicable federal law.

Acts 2004, No. 420, §1.
