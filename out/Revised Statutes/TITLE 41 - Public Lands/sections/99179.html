PART IV.  ACTIONS INVOLVING SCHOOL LANDS

SUBPART A.  ACTIONS BY THE STATE

§921.  Actions for recovery of title and damages for trespass

The Attorney General may contract with and employ attorneys at law to sue for and recover from any person, firm or corporation, claiming under color of title, sixteenth sections known as school lands, title to which belongs to the State of Louisiana, and to sue for and recover damages for trespass upon sixteenth sections known as school lands except where the school boards have sold the timber thereon, whether the trespasser claims under color of title or not.
