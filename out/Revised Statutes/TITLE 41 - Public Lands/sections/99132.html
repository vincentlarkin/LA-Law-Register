§7.  Annual list of lands entered or sold to be sent to parish assessors

The commissioner shall furnish, annually, on or before March first, to the assessors of each parish, the list of all the lands that may have been entered or sold during the preceding year, together with the names of the persons entering or purchasing them, in order that they may be assessed for taxes.

Acts 1989, No. 282, §4, eff. June 27, 1989.
