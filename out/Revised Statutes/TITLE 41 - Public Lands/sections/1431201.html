§1215.4. Lamar-Dixon Expo Center; lease

            Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the governing authority of Ascension Parish is hereby authorized and empowered to enter into a lease of all or any portion of the Lamar-Dixon Expo Center located in Ascension Parish at 9039 St. Landry Road in Gonzales, Louisiana. The lease shall provide for the uses of the property and the lease shall be subject to approval by the governing authority of Ascension Parish.

            Acts 2025, No. 329, §1.
