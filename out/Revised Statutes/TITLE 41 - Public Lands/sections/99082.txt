§5.  Receipt to purchaser
The state treasurer shall deliver to the purchaser or his agent a receipt, which shall contain a correct description, by section, township and range, of the lands sold by the state.  The state treasurer shall receive no extra compensation for this service.
Acts 1983, No. 705, §3, eff. Sept. 1, 1983.
