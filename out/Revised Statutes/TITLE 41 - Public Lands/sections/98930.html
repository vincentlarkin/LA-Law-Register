§1009. Cutting or sale, or both, of cypress timber on state property; prohibition

            The cutting or sale, or both, of standing cypress timber located on any property owned by the state of Louisiana is hereby prohibited pursuant to R.S. 3:4278.5.

            Added by Acts 1980, No. 795, §1; Acts 2018, No. 242, §2; Acts 2021, No. 190, §2, eff. June 11, 2021.
