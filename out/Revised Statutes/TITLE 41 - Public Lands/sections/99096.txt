§52.  Records transferred to this state by the former office of the United States Surveyor General of the District of Louisiana
The maps, books, records, field notes and documents appertaining to land title in this state which were transferred to this state by the former office of the United States Surveyor General of the District of Louisiana and accepted by this state by Act No. 6 of 1910 and Act No. 183 of 1928, are public records and are a part of the permanent archives of the state land office.
