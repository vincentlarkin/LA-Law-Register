§1089.  Transfer of former Interagency Recreation Board land to Department
of Public Safety and Corrections
Notwithstanding any provision of this Part or of any other law to the
contrary, the supervision, management, and use of the surface of any state-owned lands in East Feliciana Parish previously transferred to the former
Interagency Recreation Board, pursuant to authorization of Act No. 846 of the
1984 Regular Session, and subsequently transferred to the Louisiana
Department of Health by Act No. 662 of the 1989 Regular Session of the
Legislature shall be transferred to the Interagency Recreation Board and the
board shall have and may exercise any powers, duties, and functions provided
for it in this Chapter.
Acts 1989, No. 662, §6, eff. July 7, 1989; Acts 1999, No. 1148, §3.
