§1505.  Cancellation of lease
The register of the State Land Office shall have the power to cancel any lease issued under the provisions of this Chapter for violation of the purposes, terms, or conditions of such lease.
Acts 1960, No. 583, §5.  Amended by Acts 1968, No. 626, §1; Acts 1970, No. 596, §1.
