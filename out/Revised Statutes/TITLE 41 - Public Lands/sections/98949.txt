PART IV.  TIMBER ON SIXTEENTH SECTION SCHOOL LANDS
§1111.  Sale of timber on sixteenth section school lands
Parish school boards may sell timber on sixteenth section lands owned in townships wherein no qualified electors reside.  Sales of the timber shall be made only to the highest bidder and after due advertisement in the official journal of the parish.
