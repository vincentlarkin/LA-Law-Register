§1323.4.  Sales, transfers or exchanges of public property by cities, towns, villages and police juries prior to twelve o'clock, noon, July 28, 1948

All sales, transfers or exchanges of public property made by cities, towns, villages and police juries made prior to twelve o'clock, noon, July 28, 1948 are hereby validated, ratified and confirmed unto the original purchasers or transferees and their successors in title, notwithstanding any informalities provided there was a valid consideration therefor.

Added by Acts 1962, No. 205, §1.
