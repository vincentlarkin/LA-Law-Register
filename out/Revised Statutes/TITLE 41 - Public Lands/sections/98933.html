§1043.  Advertisement

The secretary of the levee board shall cause to be published an advertisement in the official journal of the parish wherein the land is located setting forth the description of the land on which the timber to be sold is located, the type and quantity of timber, and the time, place, and terms of the sale.  The advertisement shall be published for a period of not less than fifteen days and at least once a week during three consecutive weeks.

Acts 1978, No. 405, §1.
