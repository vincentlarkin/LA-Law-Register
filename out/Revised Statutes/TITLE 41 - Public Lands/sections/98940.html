§1082. Information to be secured as to quantity and value of timber

            On receipt of an application to have timber offered for sale, the secretary of the board shall request the assistant commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry, office of forestry to evaluate the current market value and quantity that could be harvested based on sound forestry practices and the goals of the board. The board may on its own initiative advertise for bids for sale of timber as provided in this Part.

            Acts 1984, No. 846, §2; Acts 2018, No. 242, §2.
