§964.  Lands to which applicable
The authority given by this Subpart shall apply to all sixteenth sections
donated by congress to this state in trust for public school purposes, and to
which the state has never legally parted with the title.  The suits herein
authorized may be brought against those who claimed the right to cut and
remove timber from any such lands, under color of title.
