§1293.  Execution of lease in triplicate, disposition of copies
Any such lease shall be executed in triplicate, and shall be disposed of as follows: one copy shall be furnished to the lessee; one copy shall be recorded in the conveyance records of the parish in which the land lies; and one copy shall be retained in the records of the lessor.
Acts 1954, No. 571, §3.  Amended by Acts 1954, No. 712, §3.
