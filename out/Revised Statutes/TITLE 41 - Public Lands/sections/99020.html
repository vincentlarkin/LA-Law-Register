§136.  Expenses

The newspapers publishing the advertisement of the sale of land shall receive therefor not more than the legal rate of advertising.  All bills for expenses incurred by the register of the state land office, or the president of the levee board, shall be paid from the proceeds of the land sold, when realized.

Amended by Acts 1960, No. 568, §1.
