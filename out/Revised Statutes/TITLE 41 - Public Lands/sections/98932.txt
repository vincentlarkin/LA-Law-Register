§1042.  Information to be secured as to quantity and value of timber
On receipt of an application to have timber offered for sale, the secretary of the levee
board shall request the assistant commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and
Forestry, office of forestry to evaluate the current market value and quantity that could be
harvested based on sound forestry practices. The levee board may on its own initiative
advertise for bids for sale of timber as provided in this Part.
Acts 1978, No. 405, §1; Acts 2018, No. 242, §2.
