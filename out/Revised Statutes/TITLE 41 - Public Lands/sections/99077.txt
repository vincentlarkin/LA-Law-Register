§426.  Records and maps to show lands recovered
The lands recovered shall be placed upon the official records and maps of the state land office as lands belonging to the state, and copies of the final judgment decreeing them to be the property of the state shall be kept on file and of record in the office and shall so remain as muniments of title to the lands.
