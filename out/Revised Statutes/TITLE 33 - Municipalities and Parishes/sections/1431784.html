§4712.26. Naming of medical office building; St. Martin Parish Hospital District No. 2

            Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, Hospital District No. 2 of St. Martin Parish may name the medical office building having the municipal address of 220 Champagne Boulevard in honor of Burton D. Dupuis, a living person who has served St. Martin Parish for more than fifty years.

            Acts 2025, No. 315, §1.
