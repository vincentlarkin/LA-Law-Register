§130.446.  Governmental functions

A.  No bonds, other debt obligations, or contracts of the district, or of any subdistrict, shall be a charge upon the income, property, or revenue of Madison Parish or the state of Louisiana.

B.  The district or any subdistrict created by the board shall not be deemed to be a public utility and shall not be subject in any respect to the authority, control, regulation, or supervision of the Louisiana Public Service Commission or any other regulatory body of the state, or any political subdivision thereof.

Acts 2010, No. 558, §1, eff. June 25, 2010.
