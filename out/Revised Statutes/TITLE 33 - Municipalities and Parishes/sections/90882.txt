§4545.32.  Restriction as to transmission facilities
The Authority shall not construct facilities for the transmission of electric power and energy unless it cannot provide for such by contract with investor-owned electric power companies with available existing transmission facilities under fair and reasonable terms and conditions or unless ordered to do so by a regulatory authority having jurisdiction.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 772, §1, eff. July 20, 1979.
