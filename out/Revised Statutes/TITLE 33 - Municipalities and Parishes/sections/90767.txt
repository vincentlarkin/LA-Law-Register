§4362.  State highways; consent of highway engineer
No franchise shall be granted over any state highway without the written consent of the Louisiana highway engineer.  Work shall be done in pursuance to a franchise granted on a state highway in a manner consistent with the requirements of the laws with reference to public highways and particularly Chapter 2 of Title 48.
