§8044.  Commissioners; compensation
The members of the commission shall receive compensation in the form of a per diem for attendance at meetings of the board and actual mileage expenses incurred in the performance of their duties as approved by the governing authorities of the member parishes of the district.
Acts 1990, No. 781, §1.
