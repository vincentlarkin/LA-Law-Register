§4872.  Election to approve parking meters

Where the parking meters are installed, an election shall be held by the municipality to decide whether the parking meters shall remain on the streets or be removed if a petition signed by fifteen percent of the qualified voters of the municipality is presented to the governing authority after a six months trial period and prior to nine months after meters are installed.  A majority of the qualified votes cast in the election shall determine the issue.
