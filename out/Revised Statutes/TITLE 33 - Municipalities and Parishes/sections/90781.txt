§4407.  Separate rather than collective disposition of franchise or lease in cities of 50,000
When any municipality of over fifty thousand inhabitants has for sale or lease more than one of the same kind of franchise lease or privilege pertaining to the comfort or convenience of its residents, it shall sell, lease, or grant each franchise or lease separately and not collectively.
