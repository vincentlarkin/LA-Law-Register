§226.  Naming of bridge by governing authority of East Baton Rouge Parish

Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing authority of East Baton Rouge Parish may name a bridge that is part of the Central Thruway and crosses over Beaver Creek in honor of a living person who represented East Baton Rouge Metropolitan Council District Four from 1992 until 2008 and served as mayor pro tem of the East Baton Rouge Parish Metropolitan Council.

Acts 2009, No. 439, §1.
