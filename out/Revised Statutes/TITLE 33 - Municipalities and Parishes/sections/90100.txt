§2932.  "Depositing authority" and "fiscal agent bank" defined
"Depositing authority" as used in R.S. 33:2928 through R.S. 33:2931 means the commissioner of public finance or the finance officer, or any department, board, commission, or institution, of cities having a population exceeding one hundred fifty thousand.  "Fiscal agent bank" or "depository" means any bank selected and designated by the governing body of cities having a population exceeding one hundred fifty thousand, for deposit of funds belonging to or held in custody for such cities or any department, board, commission, or institution thereof.
