§447.9.  Mayor's court; town of Sterlington; additional court costs

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the mayor of the town of Sterlington may impose additional court costs not to exceed ten dollars for each offense, as defined by ordinance, on any defendant convicted of a violation of a municipal ordinance.  Any additional amount in court costs collected pursuant to this Section shall be allocated to the Sterlington Police Department.

Acts 2012, No. 39, §1.
