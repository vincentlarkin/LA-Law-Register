§4555.  Joint maintenance of recreation system by political subdivisions

Any two or more municipalities or parishes or wards, may jointly establish, maintain and conduct a recreation system and facilities under this Chapter.  Any school board may join with any such subdivisions for this purpose.

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 512, §1.
