§7627.  Authority of parishes, municipalities and subdivisions thereof to appropriate and dedicate funds to a new community trust fund
Notwithstanding any other provision of general or special law, the purposes for which a parish or municipality, or any agency or subdivision of either, may levy taxes or appropriate funds to a new community trust fund include the preservation and enhancement of the tax base of such parish, municipality, subdivision or agency and the furthering of the purposes of such parish, municipality, subdivision or agency as provided by law.
Acts 1987, No. 815, §1, eff. July 20, 1987.
