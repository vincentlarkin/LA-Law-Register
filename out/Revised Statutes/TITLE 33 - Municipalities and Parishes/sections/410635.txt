PART VIII.  LOUISIANA INCLUSIONARY ZONING
AND WORKFORCE AFFORDABLE HOUSING ACT
§5001.  Short title
This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Inclusionary Zoning and Workforce Affordable Housing Act".
Acts 2006, No. 810, §1, eff. July 1, 2006.
NOTE:  See HCR 123 of the 2007 RS for recommended inclusionary zoning ordinance.
