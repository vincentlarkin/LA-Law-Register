§130.254.  Advisory committee
A.  The board may establish advisory committees and may appoint members to any
such committee.  Advisory committees shall meet with the board upon the request of a
majority of the board membership.
B.  Elected officials are prohibited from serving on the advisory committee.
Acts 1989, No. 2, §1, eff. June 1, 1989; Acts 2015, No. 191, §1.
