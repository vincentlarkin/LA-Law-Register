§3675.15.  Certificates not included in computing municipal indebtedness
Certificates issued under the provisions of this Sub-part shall not be included in computing the indebtedness of the municipality for the purpose of determining any constitutional or statutory limitation of indebtedness.
Added by Acts 1956, No. 517, §1.
