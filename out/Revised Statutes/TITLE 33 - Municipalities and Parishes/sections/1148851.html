§140.50.37. Commission recommendations

            The commission may prepare and recommend to the town council and the police jury for adoption such codes, ordinances, plans, or other measures as, in its judgment, may be necessary to accomplish the purpose of this Subpart.

            Acts 2019, No. 75, §1.
