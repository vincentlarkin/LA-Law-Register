§4361.1.  Cable television system; report
Each cable television system franchised in accordance with law shall make an annual report, not later than December 30 of each year, to the local governing authority from whom such franchise was obtained regarding the current state of the art in descrambling any signal carried by that cable television system.  Such report shall be a public record and made available to the public in accordance with the public records laws of this state.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 723, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1984.
