§4712.19.  Naming of police complex; Kenner
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing authority
of the city of Kenner may name the police complex in Kenner in honor of Nick Congemi.
Acts 2018, No. 298, §1, eff. May 15, 2018.
