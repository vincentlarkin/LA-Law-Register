§423.11.  City of Winnfield; disciplinary action by chief of police
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, in and for the city of Winnfield, the chief of police may take whatever steps are necessary to effect disciplinary action concerning police personnel, including but not limited to suspension for not longer than three days.  Any such disciplinary action shall be taken without regard to race, creed, or color.
