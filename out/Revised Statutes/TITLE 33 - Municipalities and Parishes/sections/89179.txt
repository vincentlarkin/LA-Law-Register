§140.34.  Commission
The commission may prepare and recommend to the city council for adoption such
codes, ordinances, plans, or other measures as, in its judgment, may be necessary to
accomplish the purpose of this Subpart.
Acts 1962, No. 52, §34; Acts 2020, No. 294, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2022.
