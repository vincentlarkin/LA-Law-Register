§450.  Mayor's court, St. Gabriel; establishment; jurisdiction

A.  There is hereby created the Mayor's Court of the Town of St. Gabriel, the territorial jurisdiction of which shall extend throughout the town of St. Gabriel in Iberville Parish.

B.  The general provisions of R.S. 33:441 and 442 shall be applicable to and shall govern and regulate the Mayor's Court of the Town of St. Gabriel and its officers.

C.  The mayor acting as a court magistrate shall have such power and authority and the mayor's court shall have such jurisdiction as provided by law.

Acts 2004, No. 478, §1.
