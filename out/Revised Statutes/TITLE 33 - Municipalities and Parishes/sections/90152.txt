§3338.  Ratification of prior paving and assessments
All paving done and all assessments made in the manner permitted or authorized by R.S. 33:3301, 33:3305, 33:3306, 33:3331, and 33:3334, as amended by Act 192 of 1950, are hereby ratified and confirmed to the same extent and with the same effect as if specifically authorized prior to such Act.
Acts 1950, No. 192, §6.
