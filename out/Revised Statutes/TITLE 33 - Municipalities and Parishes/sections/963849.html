§130.863. Purpose

            The district is created to provide for cooperative economic and community development among the district, the city, the state, and the owners of property in the district, to enhance the development of and improvement to the property within the area of the district, and to promote economic growth, safety, and development.

            Acts 2015, No. 420, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
