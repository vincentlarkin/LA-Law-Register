§2536.1.  St. Landry Parish Fire District No. 3 Civil Service Board; compensation of members
Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Part to the contrary, the members of the St. Landry Parish Fire District No. 3 Civil Service Board shall receive from funds available to the board compensation in an amount equal to and the same as the members of the Fire Board of Commissioners of St. Landry Parish Fire District No. 3, plus the same amount as said commissioners for mileage going to and from the meetings for the board.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 148, §1.
