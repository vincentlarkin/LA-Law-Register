§4561.1.  Naming of civic center theater; city of Monroe

The governing authority of the city of Monroe may name a civic center theater in honor of a former living mayor of the municipality who served in such office for more than fifteen years and his service in such office ended prior to 1980.

Acts 2003, No. 442, §1, eff. July 1, 2003; Acts 2011, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 20, §1, eff. June 12, 2011.
