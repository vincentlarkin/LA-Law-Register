§4832.  Hawking and peddling in cities of over 25,000
Municipalities with over twenty-five thousand population, may enact ordinances regulating the hawking and peddling in the streets and thoroughfares of market produce, vegetables, meat, fish, game, fruit and oysters, and may enforce the ordinances by fine or imprisonment.
Acts 1988, No. 539, §1.
