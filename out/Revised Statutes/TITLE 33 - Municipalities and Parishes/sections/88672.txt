§120.1.  Civil actions
Nothing contained in any subdivision regulation shall relieve, lessen, or otherwise diminish the responsibility, liability, or duty of any person engaged in the design or development of a subdivision for damage to property or injury to another caused by or resulting from any defects of any nature in any work performed or acts done by said person.
Acts 1990, No. 699, §1, eff. July 20, 1990.
