§120.13.  Planning and zoning board serving as planning commission
The planning and zoning board shall operate in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 33:101 through R.S. 33:120.1 except where there is conflict with the provisions of this Subpart.  If a conflict exists, the provisions of this Subpart shall govern.
Acts 1997, No. 1274, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
