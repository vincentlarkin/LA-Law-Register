§130.16.  Inclusion of industrial area within certain newly created special service districts prohibited
No portion of an industrial area that provides any of the services enumerated in R.S. 51:1202 shall be included within any newly created special service district.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 406, §1; Acts 2008, No. 644, §1.
