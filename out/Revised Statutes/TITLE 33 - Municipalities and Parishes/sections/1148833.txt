§140.50.19.  Effect of plat approval on status of dedications
The approval of a plat by the commission shall not be deemed to constitute or affect
any acceptance by the municipality or parish or public body of the dedication of any street
or other ground shown upon the plat.
Acts 2019, No. 75, §1.
