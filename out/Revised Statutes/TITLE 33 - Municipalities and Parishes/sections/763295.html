§447.8.  Mayor's court; town of Basile; additional court costs

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, if the local governing authority by ordinance authorizes an increase in court costs for the mayor's court of the town of Basile, the mayor of the town of Basile, as the presiding officer of the mayor's court of the town of Basile, may impose court costs not to exceed seventy-five dollars for each offense, as defined by ordinance, on any defendant convicted of a violation of a municipal ordinance.  The court costs authorized by this Section shall be in addition to the court costs authorized by R.S. 33:441.

Acts 2011, No. 6, §1.
