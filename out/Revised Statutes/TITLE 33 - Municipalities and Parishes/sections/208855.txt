§4770.8.  Maintenance
All buildings, both existing and new, and all parts thereof, shall be maintained in a safe and sanitary condition.  All devices or safeguards which are required in a building when erected, altered or repaired shall be maintained in good working order.  The owner, or his designated agent, shall be responsible for the maintenance of the building.
Acts 2003, No. 546, §1.
