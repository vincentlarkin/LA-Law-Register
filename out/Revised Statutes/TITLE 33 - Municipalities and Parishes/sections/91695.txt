§9054.  Purpose
The purpose of an ambulance service district created under the provisions of this Chapter shall be to own and operate ambulances for the transportation of persons suffering from illnesses or disabilities which necessitate ambulance care.
Acts 1985, No. 121, §1, eff. June 29, 1985.
