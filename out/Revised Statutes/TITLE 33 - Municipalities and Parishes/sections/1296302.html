§5083. Governance of risk management organizations

            The insurance committee of the Louisiana Housing Council, Inc., shall constitute the board of trustees of the organization established as provided in R.S. 33:5082 and shall be authorized to adopt bylaws for the administration of the organization, subject to approval of the executive committee of the Louisiana Housing Council, Inc.

            Acts 2022, No. 686, §1, eff. June 18, 2022.
