§4158.  Short term revenue notes
The Sewerage and Water Board of New Orleans is hereby authorized to issue short-term revenue notes in the manner provided by Chapter 15-A of Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 and subject to the provisions of R.S. 33:4096(B) and R.S. 33:4121(B).
Acts 1985, No. 731, §1, eff. July 16, 1985.
