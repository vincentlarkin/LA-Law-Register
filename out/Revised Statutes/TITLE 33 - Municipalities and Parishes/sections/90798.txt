§441.23.  Village of Hessmer; appointment of court magistrate
The board of aldermen of the village of Hessmer shall, upon request of the mayor, appoint an attorney who shall be designated as court magistrate and who shall serve at the pleasure of the mayor and may from time to time be designated by the mayor to serve in his stead as the presiding official over the mayor's court.  Whenever the magistrate is so designated by the mayor to preside over the mayor's court he shall exercise the powers and authority of the mayor over said court.  The magistrate shall receive a salary fixed and paid by the board of aldermen.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 203, §1.
