PART I-A.  SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE CHARTER MUNICIPALITIES

§481.  Municipalities governed by a special legislative charter; silent charter

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, in any municipality governed by a special legislative charter, if the provisions of the special legislative charter are silent on a particular matter, then the provisions of Part I of Chapter 2 of Title 33 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 shall govern.  If a conflict exists between the provisions of the special legislative charter and the provisions of Part I of Chapter 2 of Title 33 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, then the provisions of the special legislative charter shall govern.

Acts 2010, No. 52, §1, eff. May 26, 2010.
