CHAPTER 10-D.  LOUISIANA LOCAL GOVERNMENT

ENVIRONMENTAL FACILITIES AND COMMUNITY

DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY

§4548.1.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Local Government Environmental Facilities and Community Development Authority Act".

Acts 1991, No. 813, §1; Acts 1997, No. 1151, §1, eff. July 14, 1997.
