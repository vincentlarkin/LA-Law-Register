§9122.  Primary emergency telephone number
The digits 911 shall be the primary emergency telephone number, but the involved agencies may maintain a separate secondary backup number and shall maintain a separate number for nonemergency telephone calls.
Acts 1995, No. 477, §1, eff. June 17, 1995.
