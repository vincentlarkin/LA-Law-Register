§142.  Police jury to arrange for special election; appointment of commissioners and clerk

All qualified electors in the two parishes may vote at the special election.  The police juries of the respective parishes shall appoint for each polling place three commissioners and one clerk of election (all of whom shall be registered voters); designate the polling places; and provide the ballot boxes, ballots and necessary blanks for tally sheets.
