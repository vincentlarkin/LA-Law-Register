§4564.6.  Livingston Parish Recreation District No. 2; per diem
Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 33:4564(B) or any other law to the contrary, the members of the board of commissioners of Recreation District No. 2 of Livingston Parish shall each receive a per diem of one hundred dollars for each meeting they attend but shall not be paid for more than twelve meetings in each year.  Such per diem shall be paid out of the funds of the district.
Acts 2008, No. 430, §1.
