SUBPART B.  CLASSIFICATION OF MUNICIPALITIES

§341.  Division into cities, towns, and villages

Municipal corporations shall be divided into three classes: cities, towns, and villages.  Those having five thousand inhabitants or more are cities; those having less than five thousand but more than one thousand inhabitants are towns; and those having one thousand or fewer inhabitants are villages.

Acts 1986, No. 1076, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1987.
