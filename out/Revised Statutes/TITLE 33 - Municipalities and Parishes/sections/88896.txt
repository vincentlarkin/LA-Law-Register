§130.383.  Officers; meetings; quorum
Officers of the board of commissioners shall be selected and perform their duties, meetings shall be held, and a quorum shall be required, all as provided in the articles of incorporation and bylaws of the Northeast Economic Development Foundation on September 1, 1992.
Acts 1992, No. 929, §1, eff. July 9, 1992.
