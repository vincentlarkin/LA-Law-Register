§4720.143.  Agencies to have no power of taxation

The Jefferson Parish Redevelopment Authority created by this Chapter shall not have any power to levy or assess any ad valorem taxes, personal property taxes, or any other forms of taxes, including special assessments against any property.

Acts 2007, No. 390, §1, eff. July 10, 2007.
