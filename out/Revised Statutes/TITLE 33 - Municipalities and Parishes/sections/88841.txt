§130.273.  Officers; meetings; quorum
Officers of the board of commissioners shall be selected and perform their duties, meetings shall be held, and a quorum shall be required, all as provided in the articles of incorporation and bylaws of the Tri-Parish Economic Development Foundation on July 9, 1989.
Acts 1989, No. 775, §1, eff. July 9, 1989.
