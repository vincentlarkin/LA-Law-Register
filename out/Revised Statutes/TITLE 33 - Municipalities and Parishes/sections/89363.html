§1654.  Duties

The parish treasurer shall receive the money of the parish, disburse the same agreeably to law, and take receipts therefor.  He shall keep regular accounts of all receipts and expenditures, and of all debts due to or from the parish, and direct prosecutions ordered by the police jury, for all debts due the parish.
