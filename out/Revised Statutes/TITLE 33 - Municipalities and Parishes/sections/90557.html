§4092.  Extension of systems

The extension of the sewerage, water and drainage systems shall be made, as far as practicable, in areas adjacent to inhabited portions of the city, divided into squares and lots, and where dedicated streets are open and in use as such, or wherever hereafter opened, dedicated and in use.

Added by Acts 1952, No. 262, §8.
