§4712.28.  Naming of the soccer complex at Moore Park
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing authority
of the city of Lafayette may name the soccer complex at Moore Park in honor of Deanna G.
Bostick.
Acts 2025, No. 323, §1.
