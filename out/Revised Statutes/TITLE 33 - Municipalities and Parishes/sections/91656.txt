§9039.17.  Budget; reports and reviews
A.  The district is required to comply with R.S. 39:1301 et seq.
B.  At least sixty days prior to adoption, the district board shall submit to the local governing authorities having jurisdiction over the area included in the district, for purposes of disclosure and information only, the proposed annual budget for the ensuing fiscal year and any proposed long-term financial plan or program of the district for future operations.
Acts 2001, No. 992, §1.
