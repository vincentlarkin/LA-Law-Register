§9507.  Federal and state aid

Any animal control district created pursuant to this Chapter may do such things and enter into such contracts and agreements with the United States or with any state or federal agency or instrumentality as may be necessary to procure aid and grants to assist the district in carrying out the purposes for which it is created.  

Acts 1993, No. 816, §1, eff. June 22, 1993.
