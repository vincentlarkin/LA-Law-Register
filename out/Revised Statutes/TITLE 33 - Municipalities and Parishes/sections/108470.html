PART II.  WAR MEMORIAL CIVIC CENTERS

§4641.  Parishes authorized to establish war memorial civic center

The governing authority of any parish may create, establish, equip, maintain, operate, and support a war memorial civic center in the parish or in any ward thereof at any site selected by a vote of a majority of the governing authority.
