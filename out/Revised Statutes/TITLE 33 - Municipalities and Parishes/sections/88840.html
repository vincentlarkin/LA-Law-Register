§130.272.  Board of commissioners; members

The district shall be governed by the board of directors of the Tri-Parish Economic Development Foundation, hereinafter referred to as the "board of commissioners".  Said board shall be appointed as provided by the articles of incorporation and bylaws of said foundation on July 9, 1989.  The members of the board of commissioners shall serve terms, be removed from or resign membership on the board, and be compensated in accordance with such articles and bylaws.

Acts 1989, No. 775, §1, eff. July 9, 1989.
