§2740.30.  New Iberia; downtown development district
The New Iberia Downtown Commercial Historic District as created and provided for by city ordinance 459-93 adopted November 16, 1993, and any amendment thereto shall be deemed to be and shall be recognized as a downtown development district.
Acts 1995, No. 359, §1.
