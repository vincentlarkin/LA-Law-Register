PART V-A.  ALLEN PARISH AMBULANCE SERVICE DISTRICTS

§1261.  Creation; authority of Allen Parish Police Jury

The Allen Parish Police Jury is authorized and empowered, upon its own initiative, to form and create one or more ambulance service districts within Allen Parish.  The police jury is further authorized to alter the boundaries of any ambulance service district, provided that no such boundary change shall cause an impairment of the obligations of any contract of the ambulance service district.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 39, §1.
