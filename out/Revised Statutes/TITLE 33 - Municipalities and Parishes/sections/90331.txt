PART IV.  BUILDING LINES
§3721.  Street defined
The word "street" as used in this Part shall mean any public highway, boulevard, esplanade, square or street, or any part or side of the same, within the limits of any incorporated municipality having a population in excess of fifty thousand inhabitants.
