§1362.  Local laws unaffected until promulgation of code

R.S. 33:1361 through R.S. 33:1363 shall have no effect on the laws, by-laws, or ordinances of such municipalities or parish governing authorities until such time as the code has been promulgated.

Amended by Acts 1975, No. 635, §1.
