§441.32.  Village of Ida; mayor's court
A.  There is hereby created the Mayor's Court of the Village of Ida, the territorial jurisdiction of which shall extend throughout the village of Ida in Caddo Parish.
B.  The general provisions of R.S. 33:441 and 442 shall be applicable to and shall govern and regulate the Mayor's Court of the Village of Ida, the jurisdiction of the court, and the power and authority of the mayor and other officers of the court.
Acts 2009, No. 127, §1, eff. June 25, 2009.
