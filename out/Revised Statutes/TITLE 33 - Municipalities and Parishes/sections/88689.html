§1225.  Qualifications

Members of the police juries shall possess the same qualifications at the time of their election as members of the house of representatives and in addition thereto shall not hold or exercise the office of mayor of any municipality having a population of 5000 or more according to the United States census for the year 1960.

Amended by Acts 1962, No. 267, §1.
