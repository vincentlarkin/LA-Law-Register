§9127.  Additional funding

In order to provide additional funding for the district, the  governing authority may receive federal, state, parish, or municipal funds as well as funds from private sources and may expend such funds for the purposes of this Part.

Acts 1995, No. 447, §1, eff. June 17, 1995.
