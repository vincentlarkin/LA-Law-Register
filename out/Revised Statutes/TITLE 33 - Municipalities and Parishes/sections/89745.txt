§261.  Disposition of surplus funds of municipality
If after the payment of all the debts of a municipality dissolved under this Subpart,
any balance of money remains, it shall be turned over to the school board of the parish to be
used in the education of the children of school age residing within the territory covered by
the municipality.
Acts 2016, No. 313, §1, eff. June 2, 2016.
