§4574.14.  Allen Parish Tourist Commission; per diem

Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 33:4574.1.1 or any other law to the contrary, the members of the board of commissioners of the Allen Parish Tourist Commission shall each receive a per diem not to exceed fifty dollars for each meeting they attend but shall not be paid for more than twelve meetings in each year.  Such per diem shall be paid out of the funds of the commission.

Acts 2005, No. 33, §1, eff. July 1, 2005.
