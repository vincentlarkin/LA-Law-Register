§130.12.  Territory included within industrial area
Subject to the limitation contained in R.S. 33:130.11, an industrial area may include
any compact body of land which is used exclusively for industrial purposes or which is
primarily suited for industrial development. For the purposes of this Subpart, "industrial
purposes" shall include the construction and operation of data centers and commercial
operations directly related thereto.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 406, §1; Acts 2025, No. 36, §1.
