§4262.  No restriction of acts authorized

This Sub-part shall constitute full authority for the accomplishment of all acts herein authorized to be done.  No other law restricting the carrying out of the acts authorized to be done shall be construed as applying to any proceedings had or acts done pursuant to this Sub-part.
