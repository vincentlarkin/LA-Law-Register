§2740.29.  Abbeville; downtown development district

The Community and Historic Preservation District of the city of Abbeville as created and provided for by city ordinance 93-20, adopted December 7, 1993, and any amendment thereto, shall be deemed to be and shall be recognized as a downtown development district.

Acts 1995, No. 181, §1; Acts 1995, No. 494, §1.
