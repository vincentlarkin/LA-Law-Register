§4129.  New Orleans; sale of bonds
All bonds issued under the authority of R.S. 33:4125 shall be sold in the manner provided for sewerage service revenue bonds in R.S. 33:4121(B)(4).
Added by Acts 1976, No. 167, §1.  Acts 1985, No. 731, §1, eff. July 16, 1985.
