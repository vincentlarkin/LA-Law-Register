§4066.2.  Procedure for creation of individual sewerage system districts
The governing authority of any parish which desires to create an individual sewerage system district shall substantially follow the procedures set forth in R.S 33:3882 and 3888.
Acts 1987, No. 919, §1, eff. July 20, 1987.
