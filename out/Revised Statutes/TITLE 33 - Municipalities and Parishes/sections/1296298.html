§4712.25. Naming of Kenner council on aging building

            Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing authority of the city of Kenner may name the Kenner council on aging building in honor of Mable Trepagnier Brown.

            Acts 2022, No. 327, §1, eff. June 10, 2022.
