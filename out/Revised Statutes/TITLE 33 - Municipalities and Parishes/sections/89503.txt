§2024.  Salary deductions; donations; source of fund
The municipality shall each month deduct from the salary of each paid fireman one per cent of the amount thereof and pay the amount deducted into the fund.  The municipality may receive any donations or bequests to the fund.  The fund shall be maintained by the salary deductions, by any donations that are made to it, and by the appropriations provided for in R.S. 33:2023.
