§2037.  Effect of receipt of workers' compensation or social security payments
When a person entitled to benefits from the fund is covered by a
workers' compensation insurance policy or any other type of insurance policy
paid for by the municipality or fire company, or is entitled to benefits under
social security legislation, and receives benefits from the insurance policies or
social security legislation equal to or greater than the benefits herein provided,
he shall not receive any benefits from the fund.
Acts 1983, 1st Ex.Sess., No. 1, §6.
