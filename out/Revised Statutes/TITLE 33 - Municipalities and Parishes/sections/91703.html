§9058.3.  Use of revenues; DeSoto Parish

Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, all revenues from any tax levied or assessment or charge imposed by a parishwide ambulance service district in DeSoto Parish shall be made available to such district and shall not be diverted for use by any other entity or for any purpose other than those of such district.

Acts 2001, No. 365, §1.
