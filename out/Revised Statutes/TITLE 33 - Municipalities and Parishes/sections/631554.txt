§9100.2.  East New Orleans Neighborhood Advisory Commission established
The East New Orleans Neighborhood Advisory Commission is hereby created.  The commission shall constitute a body corporate in law, with all the powers of a corporation, and with all the powers and rights of a political subdivision of the state as provided by the constitution and laws of this state.
Acts 2008, No. 887, §1.
