§4569.  Iberville Parish Parks and Recreation District; creation; limits; purpose
There is hereby created the Iberville Parish Parks and Recreation District, referred to
in this Section and R.S. 33:4569.1 through 4569.3 as the "district", whose authority shall
encompass all territory within the geographic limits of the parish of Iberville,
notwithstanding the provisions of any law to the contrary.  The district is created for the
purposes set forth in this Section and R.S. 33:4569.1 through 4569.3 and shall be responsible
for the acquisition, construction, development, maintenance, and operation of parks and
recreational facilities and programs in Iberville Parish.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 557, §1; Acts 2019, No. 61, §1.
