SUBPART D.  MUNICIPALITIES OF LESS THAN 1,000
§3951.  Formation of sewerage districts by municipalities of less than 1,000
In addition to other powers relating to the creation of sewerage districts or the construction of sewers and sewerage disposal plants, the governing authority of any municipality having a population of one thousand or less may create sewerage districts to construct and maintain sewers and sewerage disposal works within their territorial limits in accordance with the provisions of this Sub-part.
