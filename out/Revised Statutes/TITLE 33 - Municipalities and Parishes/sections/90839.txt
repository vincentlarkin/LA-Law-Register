§4505.  Scope of power and duties
The power, authority, and duties of the municipality shall affect and include all matters and things connected with, concerning, and growing out of the service to be given or rendered by the public utility operating trackless trolleys and motor busses under a franchise granted by said municipality.
Acts 1956, No. 234, §2.
