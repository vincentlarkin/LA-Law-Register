SUBPART F-1.  LAKE CATHERINE

SEWAGE AND WATER DISTRICT

§4065.1.  Creation; purpose; boundaries

A.  The Lake Catherine Sewage and Water District, hereinafter in this Subpart referred to as "the district", is hereby created as a political subdivision of the state with power to sue and to be sued in its corporate name.  The district shall be responsible for the protection of public health through the control, monitoring, and inspection of sewerage and water systems in the Lake Catherine area of the parish of Orleans and through enforcement of all ordinances and state and local regulations relative to such systems, all as further provided in this Subpart.  The district may also construct, purchase, own, maintain, operate, and improve sewerage and water systems as further provided in this Subpart.

B.  The boundaries of the district shall consist of the area between Chef Menteur Pass and Rigolets Pass along both sides of U.S. Highway 90 for approximately 46,000 lineal feet, including, those camps along the Old Hospital Road and on Lake Catherine in the vicinity of the Old Hospital Road in Sections 24 and 25, Township 10, Range 14 and the Fort Pike Subdivision including the surrounding area of the subdivision.
