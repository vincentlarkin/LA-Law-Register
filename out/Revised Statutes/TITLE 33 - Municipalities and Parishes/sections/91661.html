§9039.21.  Purchase, management or sale of water or wastewater utility  by district

No community development district may purchase or sell a water or wastewater utility that provides service to the public, or enter into a management contract for such facilities, until the governing body of the community development district has held a public hearing on the purchase, sale, or management contract and made a determination that the purchase, sale, or management contract is in the public interest.

Acts 2001, No. 992, §1.
