PART VIII.  ORDINANCES AND LAWS
§1361.  Power to adopt code of laws
On or before January 1, 1977, all municipalities, or parish governing authorities, regardless of population, shall adopt a code embracing all of the laws which they have enacted, pursuant to and in accordance with the provisions of this Part.
Amended by Acts 1972, No. 61, §1; Acts 1975, No. 635, §1.
