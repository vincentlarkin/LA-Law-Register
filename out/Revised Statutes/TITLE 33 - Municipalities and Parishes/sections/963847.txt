SUBPART B-48.  NEW ORLEANS EXHIBITION HALL AUTHORITY
ECONOMIC GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT DISTRICT
§130.861.  Title
The provisions of this Subpart shall hereafter be known as and may be cited as the
"New Orleans Exhibition Hall Authority Economic Growth and Development District Act".
Acts 2015, No. 420, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
