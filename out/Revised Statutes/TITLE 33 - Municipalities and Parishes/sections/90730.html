§4307.1.  Winn Parish; authorization to sell certain gas and utility lines

The governing authority of Winn Parish is authorized to sell or lease, without voter approval, a gas utility system owned by a gas utility district created pursuant to this Subpart.

Acts 1991, No. 440, §1.
