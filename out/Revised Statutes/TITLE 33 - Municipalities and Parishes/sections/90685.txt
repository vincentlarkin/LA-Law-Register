§423.10.  Village of Tickfaw; personnel authority of chief of police
Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 33:423 or any other provision of law to the contrary, in and for the village of Tickfaw, the chief of police shall appoint, promote, discipline, and discharge police personnel subject to the budgetary limitations of the mayor and board of aldermen pertaining to the number of allotted positions for the police department.
Acts 1999, No. 441, §1.
