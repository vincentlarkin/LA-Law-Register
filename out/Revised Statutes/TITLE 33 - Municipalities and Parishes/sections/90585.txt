§4119.  New Orleans; R.S. 33:4106 to R.S. 33:4118 self-operative
The provisions hereof are self-operative.  The sewerage and water board of New Orleans and the city of New Orleans shall carry the same into effect.  No proceedings or approvals, not required by the provisions of those sections, shall be necessary to the validity of any certificates issued hereunder.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 167, §1.
