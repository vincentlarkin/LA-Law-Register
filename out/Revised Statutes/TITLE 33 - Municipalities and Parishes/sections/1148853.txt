§140.50.39.  Enforcement and remedies
The town council and the police jury may provide, in their respective jurisdictions,
for the enforcement of any code, ordinance, or other measure enacted under this Subpart.  A
violation of any such code, ordinance, or other measure is hereby declared a misdemeanor.
In case any building or structure is or is proposed to be constructed, reconstructed, altered,
extended, converted, or maintained in violation of any code or ordinance enacted under this
Subpart, the building inspector, permit or zoning clerk, municipal or parish counsel, or other
appropriate authority of the municipality or of the parish, or any adjacent or neighboring
property owner who would be specifically damaged by such violation, may, in addition to
other remedies, institute injunction, mandamus, or other appropriate action or proceeding to
prevent such unlawful construction, reconstruction, alteration, extension, conversion,
maintenance, or use, or to correct or abate such violation, or to prevent the occupancy of such
building.
Acts 2019, No. 75, §1.
