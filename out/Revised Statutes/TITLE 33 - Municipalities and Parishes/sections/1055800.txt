§4712.17.  Naming of St. John the Baptist Sheriff's Office law enforcement training facility
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the St. John the Baptist
Parish Sheriff may name the sheriff's office law enforcement training facility located on
Cambridge Drive in LaPlace in honor of Lloyd B. Johnson, a living person who served as
sheriff of St. John the Baptist Parish for more than twenty years.
Acts 2017, No. 130, §1.
