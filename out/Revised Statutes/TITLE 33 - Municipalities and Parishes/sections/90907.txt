§4546.19.  Sale of natural gas by the authority
The authority may sell, exchange, or otherwise dispose of the natural gas from a project not then required by participating political subdivisions under their contracts with the authority for such consideration and for such period and upon such other terms and conditions as may be determined by the authority, to any person, firm, association, or corporation, public or private.
Acts 1990, No. 666, §1.
