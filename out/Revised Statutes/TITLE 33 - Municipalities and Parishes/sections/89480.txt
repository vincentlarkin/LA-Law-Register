§1995.1.  Sick pay reduced by workers' compensation
A fireman employed by any municipality, parish or fire protection
district who draws full pay during sickness or incapacity shall have such pay
decreased by the amount of workers' compensation benefits actually received
by the employee.
Acts 1966, No. 165, §1.  Acts 1983, 1st Ex.Sess., No. 1, §6.
