§4122.  New Orleans; no obligation to provide funds
There shall be no obligation on the part of the city of New Orleans to provide funds
for the operation and maintenance of the sewerage system of the city of New Orleans.
Revenue generated from automated speed enforcement devices within the parish of Orleans
outside of a school zone, mobile speed cameras, or red light cameras shall be only used for
the purposes of stormwater drainage infrastructure, maintenance, and improvements.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 167, §1; Acts 2025, No. 452, §1.
