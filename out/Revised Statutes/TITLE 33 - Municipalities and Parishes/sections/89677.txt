§252.  Procedures; notice of election
The procedures for the election and notice of the election shall be in compliance with
the provisions of Chapter 6-A and Chapter 6-B of Title 18 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes
of 1950.
Acts 2016, No. 313, §1, eff. June 2, 2016.
