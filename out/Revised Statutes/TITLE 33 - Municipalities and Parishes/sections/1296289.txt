§4564.8.  Recreation districts in St. Mary Parish; per diem
Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 33:4564(B), the members of the boards of
commissioners of recreation districts in St. Mary Parish may each receive a per diem of not
less than ten dollars nor more than sixty-five dollars for attending meetings of the board for
a maximum of twelve meetings each year.  Per diem paid pursuant to this Section shall be
paid out of the funds of the district.
Acts 2022, No. 211, §1.
