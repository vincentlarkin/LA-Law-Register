§1419.3.  Alternative Fuel Vehicle Revolving Loan Fund Program
A.  There shall be an Alternative Fuel Vehicle Revolving Loan Fund Program within
the Department of Conservation and Energy through which the state may provide financial
assistance to a local governing authority in the manner provided for in this Subpart for the
costs of converting all or a portion of the local governing authority's fleet of motor vehicles
to qualified clean fuel vehicles propelled by an alternative fuel.
B.  The Department of Conservation and Energy may promulgate rules and
regulations as are necessary to implement the provisions of this Subpart, in accordance with
the Administrative Procedure Act, subject to oversight by the Senate Committee on Natural
Resources and the House Committee on Natural Resources and Environment.
Acts 2010, No. 118, §1, eff. July 1, 2010; Acts 2023, No. 150, §8, eff. Jan. 10, 2024.
