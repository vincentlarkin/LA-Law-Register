§4876.1.  Junk, wrecked, or used automobile or motor vehicle; public nuisance
A junk, wrecked, or used automobile or motor vehicle, including part of a
junk, wrecked, or used automobile or motor vehicle, that is visible at any time of year
from a public place or public right-of-way is a public nuisance when it:
(1)  Is detrimental to the safety and welfare of the public.
(2)  Tends to reduce the value of private property.
(3)  Invites vandalism.
(4)  Creates a fire hazard.
(5)  Is an attractive nuisance creating a hazard to the health and safety of
minors.
(6)  Produces urban blight adverse to the maintenance and continuing
development of municipalities.
Acts 2024, No. 718, §3.
