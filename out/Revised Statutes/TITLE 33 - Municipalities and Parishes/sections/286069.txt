§4570.12.  Oswald-Dubea Arboretum and Nature Center of LaSalle Park, Jefferson Parish
A portion of LaSalle Park located in Jefferson Parish is hereby named and shall be known as the Oswald-Dubea Arboretum and Nature Center of LaSalle Park.  The provisions of this Section shall be exempt from the provisions of R.S. 14:316
1
.
Acts 2004, No. 55, §1.
1
R.S. 14:316 was repealed by Acts 2004, No. 577, §2.  See now R.S. 42:267, enacted by Acts 2004, No. 577, §1.
