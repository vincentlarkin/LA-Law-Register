§9039.23.  Bonds and notes; miscellaneous
A.  The district may issue bond anticipation notes in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 39:1460.1 et seq.
B.  The district may issue short-term revenue bonds for paying any of the expenses of the district in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 39:1470 et seq.
C.  The district may issue refunding bonds in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 39:1444 et seq.
Acts 2001, No. 992, §1.
