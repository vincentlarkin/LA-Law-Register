§4690.3.  Purpose of authority
The purpose of an agricultural arena authority shall be to promote all aspects of agriculture, through the construction and operation of an agricultural arena for activities such as shows and exhibits of an agricultural nature.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 515, §1, eff. July 13, 1979.
