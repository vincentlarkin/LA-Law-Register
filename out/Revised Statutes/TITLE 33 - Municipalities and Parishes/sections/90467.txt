§3995.  Validation of present projects
Where prior to July 30, 1958 any sewerage district has issued the notice of intention provided for in R.S. 33:3982 and has thereby initiated a sewerage project under this Sub-part, it is expressly provided and declared that such district may proceed with such project and complete same under this Sub-part as amended.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 395, §9.
