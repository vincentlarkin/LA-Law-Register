§1407.  Period in which authority may be exercised
Officials authorized to act as emergency interim successors are empowered to exercise the powers and discharge the duties of an office as herein authorized only after an attack upon the United States, as defined herein, has occurred.  The governing body of the political subdivision, by resolution, may at any time terminate the authority of said emergency interim successors to exercise the powers and discharge the duties of office as herein provided.
Acts 1963, No. 114, §7.
