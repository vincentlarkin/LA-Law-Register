§140.32. Existing zoning ordinances

            Existing zoning ordinances of the municipality shall continue in effect until repealed by ordinances enacted under the authority of this Subpart.

            Acts 1962, No. 52, §32. Amended by Acts 1976, No. 268, §1; Acts 1991, No. 166, §1; Acts 2020, No. 294, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2022.
