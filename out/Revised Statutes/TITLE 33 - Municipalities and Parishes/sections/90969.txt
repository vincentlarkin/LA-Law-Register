§4568.  Nibletts Bluff Park Commission; creation
There is hereby created the Nibletts Bluff Park Commission, whose territorial limits shall be the entire area within the limits of Ward Seven of Calcasieu Parish.  The objectives and purposes of the authority shall be the development, maintenance and operation of Nibletts Bluff Park.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 489, §1.
