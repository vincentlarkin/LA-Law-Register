§4526. Construction of Subpart; surrender of powers

            Nothing contained in this Subpart shall be construed as surrendering to the office of conservation of the Department of Conservation and Energy any of the powers of supervision, regulation, or control over any local public utility by any town or city, other than those specifically provided in this Subpart.

            Added by Acts 1970, No. 352, §1. Amended by Acts 1981, No. 754, §1; Acts 2023, No. 150, §8, eff. Jan. 10, 2024.
