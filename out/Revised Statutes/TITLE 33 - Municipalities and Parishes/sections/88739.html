§1246.  Powers of the police jury of Ascension Parish; ambulance service district

The police jury is authorized and empowered, upon their own initiative, to form and create one or more ambulance service districts within the parish of Ascension.  The police jury is further authorized to alter the boundaries of any ambulance service district, provided that no such boundary change shall cause an impairment of the obligations of any contract of the ambulance service district.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 369, §1.
