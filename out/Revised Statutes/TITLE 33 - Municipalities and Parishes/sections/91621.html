§9031.  Cooperative endeavors with public body

For the purpose of aiding in the implementation of an economic development plan, any local governmental subdivision may cooperate or engage in cooperative endeavors with any economic development corporation or other local governmental subdivision or any other private or public entity or person.

Added by Acts 1978, No. 617, §1; Acts 2002, No. 78, §2, eff. June 25, 2002.
