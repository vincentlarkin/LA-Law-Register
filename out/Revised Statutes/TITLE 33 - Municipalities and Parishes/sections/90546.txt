§4082.1.  Authorization to furnish water and other services to adjoining parishes
The sewerage and water board is authorized to contract with parties having franchises
for that purpose to supply water and other services to consumers in the adjoining parishes,
at rates to be fixed by the sewerage and water board.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 60, §1; Acts 2017, No. 311, §1.
