§140.78.  Exemption from taxation
The district and all properties at anytime owned by the district and the income therefrom and all bonds, certificates, and other evidences of indebtedness issued by the district under this Subpart and the interest or income therefrom shall be exempt from all taxation by the state of Louisiana.
Acts 2004, No. 435, §1, eff. June 24, 2004.
