§4568.5.  Annual report

The board shall make an annual report to the police jury of Calcasieu Parish showing all receipts and disbursements of the board; setting forth the general condition of the park, its buildings, structures and other facilities; and making such recommendations as it deems advisable for the development, maintenance and operation of the park.  A copy of this report shall be published in the official journal of Calcasieu Parish.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 489, §1.
