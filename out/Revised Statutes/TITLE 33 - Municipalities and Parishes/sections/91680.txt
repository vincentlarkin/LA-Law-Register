CHAPTER 28.  MUNICIPAL AMBULANCE SERVICE DISTRICTS
§9040.  Municipal ambulance service districts
Municipal governing authorities are authorized and empowered, upon their own initiative, to form and create one or more ambulance service districts within and without their respective municipalities.  The municipal governing authorities are further authorized to alter the boundaries of any ambulance service district, provided that no such boundary change shall cause an impairment of the obligations of any contract of the ambulance service district.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 304, §1.
