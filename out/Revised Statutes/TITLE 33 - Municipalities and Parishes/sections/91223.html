§4851.1.  Municipalities with population of 100,000 inhabitants or less

All municipalities in the state of Louisiana having a population of one hundred thousand inhabitants or less are hereby authorized and empowered to pass laws prohibiting within said municipalities gambling or games of chance for money or for value in any form whatsoever, and to define what shall constitute gambling and to provide penalties for the violation of any laws passed under the power hereby delegated.

Acts 1952, No. 327, §1.
