§130.807.  Lincoln Parish information technology

If the governing authority of Lincoln Parish creates a district as authorized by this Subpart, it shall provide for the transfer of all duties, obligations, property, and employees of the Lincoln Parish Information Technology Department or the successor of that department and any parish agency responsible for geographic information systems to the district.

Acts 2009, No. 69, §1, eff. June 16, 2009.
