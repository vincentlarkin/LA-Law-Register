§4066.4.  Powers of individual sewerage system districts

Any individual sewerage system district shall have all the powers and authority granted to sewerage districts pursuant to any provision of law, but may only own, install, construct, lease, finance, maintain, and inspect individual sewerage systems and shall additionally have the powers and authority granted pursuant to this Subpart relating to individual sewerage systems.

Acts 1987, No. 919, §1, eff. July 20, 1987.
