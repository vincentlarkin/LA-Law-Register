SUBPART C.  CONSTRUCTION, REPAIR, AND REMOVAL OF
STRUCTURES IN CERTAIN PARISHES AND MUNICIPALITIES
§4751.  Construction and repair of buildings and walls
The governing body of any municipality of one hundred thousand or more inhabitants may adopt ordinances relating to the construction, equipment, alteration, and repair of buildings, structures, walls, and party walls, and provide in the ordinances penalties for their violation.  Such ordinances shall supersede any laws existing prior to 1910.
