§4570.12.  Oswald-Dubea Arboretum and Nature Center of LaSalle Park, Jefferson Parish

A portion of LaSalle Park located in Jefferson Parish is hereby named and shall be known as the Oswald-Dubea Arboretum and Nature Center of LaSalle Park.  The provisions of this Section shall be exempt from the provisions of R.S. 14:3161.

Acts 2004, No. 55, §1.
