§1236.22. Pointe Coupee Parish Poydras Fund; authority to expend revenue
The governing authority of the parish of Pointe Coupee may expend any interest and other revenues earned by the Pointe Coupee Parish Poydras Fund for any lawful educational or cultural purpose as well as to maintain the grave site of Julien Poydras.
Acts 1995, No. 407, §1.
