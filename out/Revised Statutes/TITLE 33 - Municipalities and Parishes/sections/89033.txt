PART VII.  INTERGOVERNMENTAL FUNCTIONS
SUBPART A.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§1321.  Terms defined
For the purposes of this Part "Municipality" shall include cities, towns, villages, or other special districts or other political subdivisions created to perform one or more public functions or services.  "Governing body" shall mean the body with authority to enact ordinances and resolutions and in which is vested responsibility for the public policy; and "bonds" shall mean any bonds issued pursuant to any law by any parish or municipality, whether such bonds are payable from revenues of a revenue producing project, or from ad valorem taxes on real estate, acreage taxes, or from any other source whatever.
Amended by Acts 1954, No. 665, §1.
