§447.4.  Mayor's court; town of Vinton; additional court costs
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the mayor in the town of Vinton may also impose additional court costs not to exceed twenty dollars for each offense, as defined by ordinance, on any defendant convicted of a violation of a municipal ordinance.
Acts 1999, No. 392, §1.
