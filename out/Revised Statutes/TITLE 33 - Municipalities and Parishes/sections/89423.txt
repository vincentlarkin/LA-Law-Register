§1818.  New appointments on change of appointing authorities
Nothing in R.S. 33:1815 through R.S. 33:1817 shall prohibit the appointment of new deputy commissioners, heads of departments, or employees, subject to any applicable civil service law, whenever the terms of office of the governmental authorities expire and their successors are elected and qualified.
