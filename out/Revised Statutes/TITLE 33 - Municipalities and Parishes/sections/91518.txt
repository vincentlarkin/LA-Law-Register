§7836.  By-laws; rules and regulations
The commission may adopt such by-laws, rules, and regulations as it may deem necessary for its own government and the conduct of its business not in conflict with this Chapter.  Vote by proxy shall not be permitted.
Acts 1988, No. 259, §1, eff. July 7, 1988.
