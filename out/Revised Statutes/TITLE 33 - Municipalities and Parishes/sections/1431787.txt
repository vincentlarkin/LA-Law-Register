§4712.29.  Naming of the recreational complex located at Graham Brown Memorial Park
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing authority
of the city of Lafayette may name the recreational complex located at Graham Brown
Memorial Park in honor of Merlin Leger.
Acts 2025, No. 324, §1.
