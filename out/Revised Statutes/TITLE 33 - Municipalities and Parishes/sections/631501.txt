§4712.15.  Naming of courthouse annex building; Beauregard Parish
The governing authority of the parish of Beauregard may name a building used as a courthouse annex located in the parish seat as the M. Bolivar Bishop Courthouse Annex Building in honor of M. Bolivar Bishop, a living person who has served as sheriff for more than thirty-five years commencing in 1972.
