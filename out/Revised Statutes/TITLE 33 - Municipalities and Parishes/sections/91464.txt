CHAPTER 22.  CONSOLIDATED SPECIAL SERVICE DISTRICTS
§7701.  Definition
The term "consolidated special service district" shall mean consolidated waterworks districts, consolidated sewerage districts, consolidated fire protection districts, consolidated hospital service districts, consolidated recreation districts, and consolidated garbage districts created under the authority conferred by this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 94, §1.
