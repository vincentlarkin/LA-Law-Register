§2476.2.  City of Opelousas Municipal Fire and Police Civil Service Board; compensation of members
Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Part to the contrary, the members of the City of Opelousas Municipal Fire and Police Civil Service Board, except the two members elected from the municipal fire and the municipal police departments, shall receive from funds available to the board compensation in the amount of twenty-five dollars for attendance at meetings of the board not to exceed two meetings per month, for a maximum of twenty-four days per year.
Acts 2001, No. 683, §1, eff. June 25, 2001.
