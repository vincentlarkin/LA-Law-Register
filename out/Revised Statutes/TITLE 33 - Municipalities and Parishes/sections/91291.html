§5065.  Maintenance of abandoned residential properties in municipalities and parishes

The governing authority of any parish with a population between four hundred thousand and four hundred forty thousand based on the latest federal decennial census, and any governing authority of a municipality that lies within such a parish, may enact ordinances requiring that abandoned residential property be maintained in safe and sanitary condition to maintain the stability of the neighborhood.

Acts 2012, No. 692, §2, eff. Oct. 1, 2012.
