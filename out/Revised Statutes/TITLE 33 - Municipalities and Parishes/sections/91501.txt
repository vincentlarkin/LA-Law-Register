§7803.  Purpose
The commission is hereby created for the object and purpose of providing economy and efficiency in the furnishing of water, sewer, and sewage disposal services to the citizens of the parish, and it shall have the authority to plan, finance, construct, acquire, improve, operate, and maintain water, sewer, and sewage disposal improvements and facilities within the parish.
Acts 1986, No. 377, §1, eff. July 2, 1986.
