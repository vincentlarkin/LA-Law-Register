§4712.13.  Naming of park behind Mandeville City Hall
The Mandeville City Council may name the park located behind Mandeville City Hall and generally known as Mandeville City Park in honor of Paul D. Cordes.
Acts 2005, No. 173, §1, eff. June 28, 2005.
