§447.14.  Mayor's court; town of Anacoco; additional court costs
In addition to the court costs authorized by R.S. 33:441, the mayor of the town of Anacoco, as the presiding officer of the mayor's court of the town of Anacoco, may impose additional court costs not to exceed twenty dollars for each offense, as defined by ordinance, on any defendant convicted of a violation of a municipal ordinance.
Acts 2014, No. 367, §1.
