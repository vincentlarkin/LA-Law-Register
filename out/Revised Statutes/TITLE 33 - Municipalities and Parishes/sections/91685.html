§9045.  Staff

The commission shall appoint such staff as is necessary to provide adequate ambulance service in the municipality.  The staff shall assist the director in the operation of the service district, promulgate rules and regulations, and approve the appointment of a director made by the commission.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 304, §1.
