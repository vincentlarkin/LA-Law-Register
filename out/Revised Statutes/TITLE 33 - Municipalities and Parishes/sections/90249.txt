§364.  Town of Gueydan; municipal officials and employees; increase in compensation; certain prohibition
The compensation of any municipal official or employee of the town of Gueydan shall not be increased:
(1)  During the period from six months prior to any regularly scheduled municipal election of municipal officials until the term of the municipal officials in office expires.
(2)  Prior to any special election of any municipal official after the election date is set, in the town of Gueydan.
Acts 1991, No. 87, §1.
