§7557.  Administration of chapter; reservation of powers and duties

The board of commissioners of the district shall enforce and administer the provisions of this Chapter and the rules, regulations and orders of the board of commissioners.  It may employ such agents and enforcement officers as it deems necessary for this purpose.

Added by Acts 1970, No. 589, §1.
