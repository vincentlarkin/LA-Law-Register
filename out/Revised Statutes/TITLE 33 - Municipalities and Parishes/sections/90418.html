SUBPART C.  DISTRICTS UNDER ACT 285 OF 1908

§3931.  Application of Sub-part

This Sub-part applies only to those sewerage districts which were created under Act 285 of 1908, and the Acts amendatory thereof, and which are operating thereunder on the effective date of the Revised Statutes.
