§3811.1.  Authority of Lafourche Parish Police Jury to expand powers of Lafourche water district

In addition to the provisions of R.S. 33:3811, the Lafourche Parish Police Jury may authorize the Lafourche water district to do and perform all acts in its corporate capacity and in its corporate name which are necessary and proper for the purpose of constructing and maintaining sewers and sewerage disposal works within its territorial limits.

Added by Acts 1974, No. 440, §1.
