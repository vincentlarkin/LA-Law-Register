PART V.  MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS
§9099.1.  Renewal of parcel fee
A.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an election to renew a parcel fee
imposed within any crime prevention and security district or improvement district, created
pursuant to Chapter 29 of Title 33 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, may be held
at an election held for that purpose in accordance with the Louisiana Election Code.
B.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any crime prevention district
imposing a parcel fee may spend proceeds of the parcel fee for the following purposes if such
use of the proceeds is approved by a majority of the voters of the district who vote on a
proposition that authorizes such use:
(1)  To provide for the beautification of the public spaces within the district.
(2)  To promote and encourage beautification of private spaces within the district.
Acts 2010, No. 535, §1, eff. June 25, 2010; Acts 2018, No. 704, §1.
