§3653.  Plans and specifications

The petitioning property owners may provide in their petition, plans, and specifications, including the kinds and types of poles and lights and other material to be used in the special lighting system.  The governing authorities in such cases may order the specified material used in the construction of the system, or may reject any and all of the plans and specifications and have them prepared by the city or a special engineer who shall submit them to the governing authority for approval or rejection.
