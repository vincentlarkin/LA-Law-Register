§4834.  Protection given only to farmers raising produce

R.S. 33:4833(A) shall apply only to farmers selling produce raised by them or on their farms and shall in no way be construed to protect peddlers who did not themselves raise the produce which they peddle.

Acts 2005, No. 354, §1.
