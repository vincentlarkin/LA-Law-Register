§4786.  Hearing; requirement of majority vote for suspension or revocation
Before any permit is suspended or revoked the holder thereof shall be entitled to a hearing as hereinafter provided, and no permit shall be revoked unless such a hearing has been held and a majority of the governing body of the municipality or parish, or a municipal alcoholic beverage control board thereafter votes for such suspension or revocation.
Acts 1952, No. 493, §2.  Amended by Acts 1958, No. 302, §1; Acts 1975, No. 359, §1.
