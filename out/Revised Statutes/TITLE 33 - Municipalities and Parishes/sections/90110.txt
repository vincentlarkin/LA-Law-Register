§3003.  Appropriations to develop tourist attractions; St. Mary Parish; Lafayette Parish
The police juries of St. Mary and Lafayette Parishes are hereby authorized and empowered at their discretion to appropriate an annual sum not to exceed fifty thousand dollars for the purpose of developing tourist attractions in the parishes.
Such police juries may make such appropriations through any agency or agencies they may deem fit and proper.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 318, §1.
