§9100.3.  Commission's neighborhood area; domicile

A.  The commission shall include each neighborhood in New Orleans East that is represented on the commission by the president or appointed representative of the neighborhood association or organization as provided in this Chapter.

B.  The commission shall designate its domicile at a place within that area.

Acts 2008, No. 887, §1; Acts 2009, No. 394, §1.
