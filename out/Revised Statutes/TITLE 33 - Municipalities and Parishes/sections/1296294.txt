§4712.24.  Naming of facilities that are part of the district headquarters; St. George Fire
Protection District No. 2
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing board
of St. George Fire Protection District No. 2 may name facilities that are part of the district's
headquarters in honor of a living person.
Acts 2022, No. 104, §1, eff. May 25, 2022.
