SUBPART G-1.  IMPROVEMENT OF DRAINAGE DITCHES

§3521.  Power to order improvements

Municipalities shall have the power to order the improvement of drainage ditches, including the paving thereof, or otherwise.

Acts 1952, No. 379, §1.
