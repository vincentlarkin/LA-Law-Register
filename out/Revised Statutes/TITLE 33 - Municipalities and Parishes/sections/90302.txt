§3689.16.  Exemption of certificates from taxation; acceptability as security
All paving certificates issued under the provisions of R.S. 33:3689.1-33:3689.17 shall be exempt from taxation.  They shall be acceptable as security for any deposits of public funds.
Acts 1952, No. 430, §16.
