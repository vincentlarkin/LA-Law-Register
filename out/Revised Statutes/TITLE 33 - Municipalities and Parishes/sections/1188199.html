§3080. Liberal construction

            This Part does and shall be construed to provide a complete method for the doing of the things authorized by this Part. The provisions of this Part shall be liberally construed for the accomplishment of its purposes.

            Acts 2020, No. 361, §1, eff. June 12, 2020.
