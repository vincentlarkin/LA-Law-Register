§2193.  Substitutes; continuous duty during emergency
Salaries of the members of the department shall not be reduced below the level paid on July 27, 1920.  Substitutes shall be furnished whenever necessary.  In cases of emergency, the officer having the management of the department may summon and keep on continuous duty any members of the department as long as the emergency exists.
