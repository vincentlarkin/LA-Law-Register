RS 33:1392
Terrebonne Parish; charter commission; plan of government
https://www.legis.la.gov/legis/Law.aspx?d=89096

NOTE:  Former §1392, dealing with the charter commission and plan
of government for Terrebonne Parish, was removed from the Louisiana
Revised Statutes of 1950 pursuant to Acts 2014, No. 158, §4 but
remains in effect as a Local and Special Act; see Acts 1979, No. 341
for content.
