§4780.10.  Conflicting regulations; higher standards to apply

Whenever the regulations made under authority of this Subpart impose higher standards than are required in any other statute or local ordinance or regulation, the provisions of the regulations made under authority of this Subpart shall govern.  Whenever the provisions of any other statute, local ordinance, or regulation impose higher standards than are required by the regulations made under authority of this Subpart, the provisions of such statute, local ordinance, or regulation shall govern.

Acts 1987, No. 355, §1, eff. July 6, 1987.
