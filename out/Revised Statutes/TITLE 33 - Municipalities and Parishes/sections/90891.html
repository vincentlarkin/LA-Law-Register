§4545.4.3.  Alternate form for approving changes in board membership

With the approval of a majority of the board of directors, mail ballots may be utilized to record the vote of the board of directors on any ordinance proposing to increase or decrease the membership of the board of directors of the authority.

Acts 1988, No. 520, §1, eff. July 8, 1988.
