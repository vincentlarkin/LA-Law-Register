§4159.4.  Estimated billing; prohibited
Any bill for services submitted by the Sewerage and Water Board of New
Orleans to a customer shall reflect the charge for actual services provided to the
customer, unless the customer voluntarily enrolls in a fixed billing program.  No
customer shall receive a bill based on estimated services.
Acts 2024, No. 703, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2025.
