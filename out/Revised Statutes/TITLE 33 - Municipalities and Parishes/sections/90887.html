§4545.37.  Laws governing contracts

All contracts let for purposes of this Chapter shall be subject to the public contract laws contained in R.S. 38:2211-2225, except contracts entered into between the Authority and a participating municipality, contracts between municipalities, and contracts between the Authority and investor-owned electric power companies and electric power cooperative associations or corporations for the purchase of an interest in a facility or facilities existing or under construction.

Added by Acts 1979, No. 772, §1, eff. July 20, 1979.
