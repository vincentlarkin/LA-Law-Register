§4877.1.  Parish zoning, notice to property owners, prohibited rezoning
The governing authority of any parish with a population of less than three hundred fifty thousand authorized to zone its territory shall, at least ten days prior to the initial hearing on the matter, notify the owner or owners of record of the properties to be zoned or rezoned in parochial zoning of the time and place of the hearing and subject matter of the regulations and restrictions.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 175, §2.
