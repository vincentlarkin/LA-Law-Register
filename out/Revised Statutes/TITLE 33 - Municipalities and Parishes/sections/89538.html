§2192.  Division of policemen into platoons; transfer

A.  The officer having management of the department shall divide the members thereof into three platoons.

B.  The proper authority in the department may transfer a member from one platoon to another, but only for the good of the service.

Amended by Acts 1982, No. 221, §1.
