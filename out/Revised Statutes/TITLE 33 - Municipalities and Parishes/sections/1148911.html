§4712.21. Naming of community center; Church Point

            Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267, the governing authority of the town of Church Point may name a community center in honor of Gertrude Wimberly Broach.

            Acts 2019, No. 132, §1, eff. June 6, 2019.
