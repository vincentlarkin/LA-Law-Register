§8045.  Corporate status

The district shall constitute a body corporate in law with all the powers of a corporation, shall have perpetual existence, shall have the power and right to incur debts and contract obligations, to sue and be sued, and to do and perform any and all acts in its corporate capacity and its corporate name necessary and proper for carrying out of the objects and purposes for which the district was created.

Acts 1990, No. 781, §1.
