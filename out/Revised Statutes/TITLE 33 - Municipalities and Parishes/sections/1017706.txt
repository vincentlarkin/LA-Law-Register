§2541.5. Winn Parish Fire Protection District No. 3; fire chief and assistant fire chief;
unclassified service
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the positions of fire chief and
assistant fire chief for Winn Parish Fire Protection District No. 3 are in the unclassified
service, and the right of selection, appointment, supervision, and discharge for such positions
is vested in the governing board of the district.
Acts 2016, No. 238, §1, eff. May 26, 2016.
