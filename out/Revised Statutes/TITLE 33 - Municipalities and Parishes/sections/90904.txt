§4546.16.  Bonds exempt from taxation
Bonds, their transfer and the income therefrom, including any profit made on the sale thereof, shall at all times be exempt from all taxation by the state or any political subdivision thereof.
Acts 1988, No. 412, §1; Acts 2010, No. 175, §4.
