§120.6.  Calcasieu Parish Planning and Zoning Board, creation

The police jury may combine the Calcasieu Parish Planning Commission and the Calcasieu Parish Board of Adjustment into one board. The new board shall be called the "Calcasieu Parish Planning and Zoning Board" and in this Subpart shall be referred to as the "planning and zoning board".

Acts 1997, No. 1274, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
