§2740.77.  Disclosure of public financing
The district shall take affirmative steps to provide for the full disclosure of information relating to the public financing and maintenance of improvements to immovable property undertaken by the district.  Such information shall be made available to all existing residents, and to all prospective residents, of the district.
Acts 2003, No. 904, §1.
