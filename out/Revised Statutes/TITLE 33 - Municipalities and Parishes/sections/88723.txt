§1236.4.  Powers of the police juries of St. Charles, St. John the Baptist, Iberville, West Baton Rouge, and Tangipahoa Parishes; ambulance services
The police juries of St. Charles, St. John the Baptist, Iberville, West Baton Rouge, and Tangipahoa Parishes shall have authority to purchase ambulances and operate and maintain an ambulance service in their respective parishes and to expend parish funds for said purpose.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 216, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 123, §2.
