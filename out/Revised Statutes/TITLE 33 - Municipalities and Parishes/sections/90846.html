SUBPART C.  REQUIREMENT OF MALODORANTS IN GASES

§4521.  Application of Subpart

This Subpart applies only to any town or city exercising powers of supervision, regulation, and control over any local public utility.  Nothing in this Subpart applies to or affects:

(1)  Gas transported out of the state;

(2)  Gas transported in gas lines for industrial or commercial purposes or for resale;

(3)  Liquefied petroleum gas.

Added by Acts 1970, No. 352, §1.
