§5003.  Inclusionary zoning

Any municipality or parish in the state that adopts land use or zoning ordinances, resolutions, or regulations may adopt ordinances to provide for inclusionary zoning to increase the availability of affordable dwelling units within the jurisdiction of the respective municipality or parish.

Acts 2006, No. 810, §1, eff. July 1, 2006.
