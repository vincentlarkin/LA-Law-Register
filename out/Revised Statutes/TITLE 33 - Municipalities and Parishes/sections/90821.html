SUBPART E.  GRANT OF FRANCHISES BY POLICE JURIES,

MUNICIPALITIES, AND OTHER LOCAL GOVERNING

AUTHORITIES

§4461.  Limitation on cable television fees and charges

No police jury, municipality, or other local governing authority empowered to grant cable television franchises shall charge any franchise fee of any kind in excess of that authorized by federal law.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 793, §1.  Amended by Acts 1988, 2nd Ex.  Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. Oct. 27, 1988; Acts 1990, No. 57, §1.
