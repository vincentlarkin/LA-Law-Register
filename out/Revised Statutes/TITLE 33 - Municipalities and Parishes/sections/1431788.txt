§4712.30.  Naming of Heymann Park Complex
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the governing authority
of the city of Lafayette may name the Heymann Park Complex in honor of John "Cush"
Leblanc.
Acts 2025, No. 330, §1.
