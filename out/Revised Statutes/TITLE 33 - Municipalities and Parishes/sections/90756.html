§4341.1.  Hospital Service District No. 1 of Terrebonne Parish; exception

The sale or lease of Terrebonne General Medical Center by the governing authority of Hospital Service District No. 1 of Terrebonne Parish shall be in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 46:1064.1.

Acts 1997, No. 1083, §1.
