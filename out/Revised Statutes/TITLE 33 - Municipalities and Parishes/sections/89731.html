§2585.1.  Call of election

Commencing on January 1, 1981, and, except as otherwise provided by law, every five years thereafter, the governing authority of the parish or municipality shall issue the call for an election for the classified employee member of the municipal or parochial civil service commission.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 563, §1.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 253, §1.
