CHAPTER 13-D.  NEW ORLEANS COMMUNITY IMPROVEMENT ACT

§4720.51.  Short title

This Chapter shall be referred to as the "New Orleans Community Improvement Act".

Acts 2004, No. 349, §1.

Sess., Nos. 65 and 135, Acts 1995, Nos. 30 and 375, Acts 1997, No. 101, and Acts 2003, Nos. 266 and 968.
