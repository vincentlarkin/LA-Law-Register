§1236.31. Lincoln Parish hospital proceeds; investments

            The governing authority of Lincoln Parish may invest funds that it received from the sale of a hospital and that it placed in a permanent trust in the same manner as post-employment benefits trusts may be invested pursuant to R.S. 33:5162.

            Acts 2023, No. 160, §1.
