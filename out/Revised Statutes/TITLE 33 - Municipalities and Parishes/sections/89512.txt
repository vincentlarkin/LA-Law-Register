§2032.  Compensation of board; expenditures to preserve fund
The members of the board shall receive no compensation from the fund.  The board may make payments from the fund for its preservation and administration.  These expenditures are limited to what is necessary to safeguard and administer the fund by means of court proceedings, and stenographic and clerical assistance.
