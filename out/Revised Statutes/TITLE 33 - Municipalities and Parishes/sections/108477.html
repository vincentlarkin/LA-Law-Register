§4648.  Charges for use of space; gifts

The board of control may fix and collect charges or rent for the use by the public or by organizations for space used or occupied, and may receive unconditional gifts.  All moneys collected or received by the board of control shall be turned over to the governing authority of the parish, and disbursed for the benefit of the war memorial civic center.
