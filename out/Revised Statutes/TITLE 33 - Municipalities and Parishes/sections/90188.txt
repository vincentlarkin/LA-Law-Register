§3429.  Contribution by municipality
The governing authorities may contribute or appropriate out of any available funds a portion not exceeding one-third of the cost of any improvement undertaken under the provisions of this Sub-part, in which event the balance of the entire cost shall be assessed against and borne by abutting property owners in the manner hereinbefore indicated.
