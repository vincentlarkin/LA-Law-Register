§1364.  Revision of ordinances

Any municipality or parish may, through its governing authority amend and revise on any date its existing ordinances, in whole or in part, may compile the revision in book or pamphlet form and may adopt and promulgate the same.

Amended by Acts 1975, No. 635, §1.
