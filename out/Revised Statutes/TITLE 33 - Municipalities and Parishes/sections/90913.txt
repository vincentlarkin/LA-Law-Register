§4546.5.  Applicability of antitrust laws
All immunity of the state of Louisiana from liability under antitrust law is hereby extended to the authority acting within the scope of the grants of authority contained in this Section and when so acting, the authority shall be presumed to be acting in furtherance of state policy.
Acts 1987, No. 658, §1.
