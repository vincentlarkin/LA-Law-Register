§4152.  New Orleans; sale of bonds

All bonds issued under the authority of R.S. 33:4148 shall be sold in the manner provided for sewerage service revenue bonds in R.S. 33:4121(B)(4).

Added by Acts 1981, No. 247, §1, eff. July 1, 1981.  Acts 1985, No. 731, §1, eff. July 16, 1985.
