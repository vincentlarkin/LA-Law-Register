§1236.17.  Gathering or cutting of Spanish moss on Lake St. John; prohibition
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the gathering or cutting, for sale, of Spanish moss located on Lake St. John is hereby prohibited.
Acts 1992, No. 634, §1.
