§7756.  Combination of administrative personnel of two or more districts; approval of ordinances by state health officer
To effect economy of operation, any two or more districts may combine the use of administrative and operative personnel and equipment upon such basis of compensation therefor as is mutually agreed to by all such boards of commissioners participating.
Acts 1992, No. 198, §1.
