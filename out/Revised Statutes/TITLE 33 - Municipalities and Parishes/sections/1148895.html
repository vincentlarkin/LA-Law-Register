§4710.21. Superdome; certain exhibits

            If an exhibition be suitable for and the exhibitor be willing to hold the exhibition in the Superdome, no contract for such exhibition in any facility of the authority shall be entered into without the prior approval of the operating management of the Superdome, which approval shall not be unreasonably withheld.

            Acts 2019, No. 172, §1.
