§3886.  Domicile of districts; seal; depository

The domicile of the sewerage district shall be designated at some place within its boundaries in the resolution of the police jury creating said district.  The governing authority of the sewerage district shall, at its first meeting, adopt an official seal for said district and shall designate the official journal thereof.  They shall select a solvent chartered bank within the parish where the district is organized in which all of the money of the district shall be deposited.

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 495, §2.
