§3834.1.  Engineer not required

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the governing authority of a waterworks system shall not be required to employ an engineer for any public works project the total cost of which is less than fifty thousand dollars including labor and materials.

Acts 2004, No. 895, §1.
