§140.50.8.  Budget
The commission shall prepare an annual budget of its operating expenses, the total
amount of which, exclusive of gifts, shall be within the total amounts appropriated for the
purpose by the town council and the police jury.  The municipality shall act as fiscal agent
for the commission.
Acts 2019, No. 75, §1.
