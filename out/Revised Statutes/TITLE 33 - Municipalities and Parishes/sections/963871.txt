§2541.4.  Parishwide Fire Protection District No. 1 of the Parish of Tensas; fire chief and
assistant fire chief; unclassified service
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the positions of fire chief and
assistant fire chief for Parishwide Fire Protection District No. 1 of the Parish of Tensas are
in the unclassified service, and the right of selection, appointment, supervision, and discharge
for such positions is vested in the governing board of the district.
Acts 2015, No. 89, §1, eff. June 19, 2015.
