§4082.  Leasing of conduits; fiber optic cable

The board shall have authority to lease and install conduits attached to pipe for housing fiber optic cable for the generation and transmission of electricity to board facilities as a part of its authority to operate, install, and repair the sewer, water and drainage systems under its control.

Acts 2005, No. 308, §1, eff. June 29, 2005.
