§3390.  Transfer of assessments and notes to contractor
The assessments and the notes executed by the property owners, may be transferred by the municipality, without recourse, to the contractor at their face value and the contractor or any of his transferees shall have the lien, privilege and all other rights herein provided, accruing to the municipality.
