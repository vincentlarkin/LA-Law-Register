§3317.  Construction and maintenance by parish government of streets and roads within municipality

Wherever streets and roads within a municipality are constructed and maintained by the parish government and not by the city government, the parish government shall have all of the powers and duties otherwise imposed on municipalities by the provisions of R.S. 33:3301 through 33:3316.

Added by Acts 1950, No. 397, §1.
