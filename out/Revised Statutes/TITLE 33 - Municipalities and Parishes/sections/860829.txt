§447.12.  Mayor's court; town of Mansura; additional court costs
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the mayor of the town of Mansura may impose court costs not to exceed eighty-five dollars for each offense, as defined by ordinance, on any defendant convicted of a violation of a municipal ordinance.  The court costs authorized by this Section shall be in addition to the court costs authorized by R.S. 33:441.
Acts 2013, No. 382, §1.
