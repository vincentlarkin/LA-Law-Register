§7839.  Construction

This Chapter shall be liberally construed to promote the purposes for which it is enacted and shall constitute full authority for the accomplishment of all acts being authorized.  No proceedings shall be necessary except such as are required herein.

Acts 1988, No. 259, §1, eff. July 7, 1988.
