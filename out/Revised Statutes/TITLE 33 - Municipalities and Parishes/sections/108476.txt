§4647.  Payment of expenses
The expenses and costs of maintenance of any parish or other memorial, including the salaries of employees, shall be paid monthly by the governing authority of the parish upon the approval of the board of control, out of the funds specially collected by taxes duly budgeted, or otherwise collected, for the support and maintenance of the memorial.
