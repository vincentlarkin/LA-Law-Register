§4780.23.  Periodic review; termination or modification of agreement
Procedures established pursuant to R.S. 33:4780.22 shall include provisions requiring periodic review at least every twelve months, at which time the applicant or his successor in interest thereto shall be required to demonstrate good faith compliance with the terms of the agreement.  If, as a result of such periodic review, the municipality or parish finds and determines, on the basis of substantial evidence, that the applicant or successor in interest thereto has not complied in good faith with terms or conditions of the agreement, the municipality or parish may terminate or modify the agreement.
Acts 1988, No. 505, §1.
