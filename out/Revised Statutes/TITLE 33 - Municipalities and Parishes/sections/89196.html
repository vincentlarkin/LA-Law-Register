§140.6. Budget

            The commission shall prepare an annual budget of its operating expenses, the total amount of which, exclusive of gifts, shall be within the total amounts appropriated for the purpose by the city council. The municipality shall act as fiscal agent for the commission.

            Acts 1962, No. 52, §6; Acts 2020, No. 294, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2022.
