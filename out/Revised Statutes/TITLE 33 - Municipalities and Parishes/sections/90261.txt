§3661.  Form of the certificates; registration
The certificates shall be signed by the Chief Executive Officer of the
municipality and by its Clerk or Secretary, on behalf of and in the name of the
municipality and under the seal thereof.  The interest coupons shall be signed
with the facsimile signature of said officers, and before delivery, shall be
registered in the mortgage records of the parish in which the municipality is
located as to the serial number, or numbers, amount, rate of interest, date due,
and the streets, roads, or parts thereof, along which the street lighting facilities
have been installed.  Each of these certificates shall bear the following
endorsement:
"This certificate registered in the Mortgage Records of the Parish of
________ on this, the ______ day of ________, 20__."
Added by Acts 1967, No. 117, §5.
