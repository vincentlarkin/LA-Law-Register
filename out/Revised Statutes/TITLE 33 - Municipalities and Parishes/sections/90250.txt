§365.  Naming of town hall; town of Jonesboro
The governing authority of the town of Jonesboro may name its town hall after Richard Zuber, its former long-time mayor.
Acts 1999, No. 1306, §1; Acts 2011, 1
st
Ex. Sess., No. 20, §1, eff. June 12, 2011.
