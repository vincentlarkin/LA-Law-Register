§2663.  Ordinance levying tax
If a majority in number and value of the taxpayers of the parish or municipality vote in favor of the special tax, the governing authority shall immediately pass an ordinance levying the tax for the time specified in the petition and designating the years in which the tax shall be levied and collected.
