CHAPTER 10-B.  LOUISIANA MUNICIPAL NATURAL GAS
PURCHASING AND DISTRIBUTION AUTHORITY
§4546.1.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Municipal Natural Gas Purchasing and Distribution Act".
Acts 1987, No. 658, §1.
