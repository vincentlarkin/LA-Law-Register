§7573.  Beach; definition
For purposes of this Chapter, a "beach" shall be defined as a shore of any body of salt water, a shore of a lake, being a large inland body of fresh or salt water, or the bank of a river covered, at least in part, with sand, shell, gravel, or larger rock fragments.
Acts 1992, No. 59, §1.
