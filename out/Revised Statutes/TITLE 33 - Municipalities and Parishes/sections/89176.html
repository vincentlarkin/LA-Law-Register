§140.31. Conflict with other laws

            A. If regulations made under authority of this Subpart require a greater width or size of yards, courts, or other open spaces, or require a lower height of buildings or less number of stories, or require a greater percentage of lot to be left unoccupied, or impose other higher standards than are required in any other statute, the provisions of the regulations made under authority of this Subpart shall govern.

            B. If any other provision of law requires a greater width or size of yards, courts, or other open spaces, or require a greater percentage of lot to be left unoccupied, or impose other higher standards than are required by the regulations made under authority of this Subpart, the provisions of such statute shall govern.

            Acts 1962, No. 52, §31; Acts 2020, No. 294, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2022.
