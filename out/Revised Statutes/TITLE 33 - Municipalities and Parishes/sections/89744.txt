§260.  Liquidation of other affairs of municipality
The governing authority of a municipality dissolved under this Subpart, in office at
the time of its dissolution shall liquidate the other affairs of the municipality as speedily as
possible and shall continue in office until the final settlement thereof.
Acts 2016, No. 313, §1, eff. June 2, 2016.
