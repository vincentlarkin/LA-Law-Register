§455.  Mayor's court; village of Elizabeth
A.  There is hereby created the Mayor's Court of the Village of Elizabeth, the
territorial jurisdiction of which shall extend throughout the village of Elizabeth in Allen
Parish.
B.  The general provisions of R.S. 33:441 and 442 shall be applicable to and shall
govern and regulate the Mayor's Court of the Village of Elizabeth, the jurisdiction of the
court, and the power and authority of the mayor and other officers of the court.
Acts 2016, No. 36, §1.
