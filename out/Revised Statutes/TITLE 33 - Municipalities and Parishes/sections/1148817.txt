§140.50.3.  Conflict with other laws
A.  If other laws relating to the physical planning, zoning, airport zoning, effectuation
of plans, platting, and other purposes of this Subpart are in conflict with the provisions of
this Subpart, the provisions of this Subpart shall prevail; however, where such other laws or
provisions thereof provide for other and additional duties, powers, authority, and
responsibility, such other laws shall be applicable insofar as they are not in conflict with the
provisions of this Subpart.
B.  If the town council or police jury fails to make use of the provisions of this
Subpart, the provisions of this Subpart shall not be construed to displace, amend, supersede,
or affect existing planning or zoning laws of the state of Louisiana or of either jurisdiction.
