§152.  Proces verbal and plat of survey
Such petition shall be accompanied with a proces verbal and plat of survey of such lots or land desired to be annexed to and included in the territorial corporate limits of such city or town made by the parish surveyor of the parish in which the city or town is situated.  Such survey and proces verbal and plat thereof, to be first made by such surveyor for such purpose, shall set forth the boundaries and accurate description of such lots or land desired to be so annexed, and shall be by such surveyor certified to be correct.
