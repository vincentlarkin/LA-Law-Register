§253. Form of ballot

            The ballot for the election shall provide as follows:

"PROPOSITION ELECTION

            Shall the Charter of the municipality of __________________be surrendered and the municipality dissolved?".

            Acts 2016, No. 313, §1, eff. June 2, 2016.
