§3991.  Certificates as negotiable paper

Sewerage certificates issued hereunder shall have all the requisites of negotiable paper under the law merchant, and shall not be invalid for any irregularity or defect in the proceedings for their issuance, sale or delivery, and shall be incontestable in the hands of bona fide purchasers or holders for value thereof.

Added by Acts 1950, No. 462, §11.
