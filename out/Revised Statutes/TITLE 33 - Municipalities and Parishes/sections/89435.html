PART IX.  MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES MERIT AWARD SYSTEMS

§1891.  Merit awards

The governing body of any municipality shall have authority to grant merit awards to full-time permanent employees of the municipality in accordance with the provisions of this Part.  Nothing herein shall be in conflict with Civil Service rules and regulations.

Added by Acts 1979, No. 535, §1.
