§130.546.  Exemption from taxation
NOTE:  R.S. 33:130.546 repealed by Acts 2001, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 2, §3, eff. upon transfer to the military department of the state pursuant to R.S. 29:33.2.  See Section 5 of Act 2.
The district and all properties at any time owned by the district and the income therefrom shall be exempt from all taxation by the state of Louisiana.
Acts 1995, No. 986, §1; Acts 2001, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 2, §3.
