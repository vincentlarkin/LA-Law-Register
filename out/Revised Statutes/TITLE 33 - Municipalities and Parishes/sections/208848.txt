§4770.2.  Authority to abate nuisances
A.  The city of Monroe may abate nuisances in accordance with the procedures set forth in this Subpart and any other state law or city ordinance.
B.  Part I of Chapter 32 of Title 13 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 shall specifically be included in the authority to abate nuisances provided to the city of Monroe in this Subpart. In the case abatement is sought under such provisions, and there is a conflict with the provisions of this Subpart, the provisions of such Part in Title 13 shall control.
Acts 2003, No. 546, §1.
