SUBPART C.  CONNECTION WITH SYSTEM
§4041.  Compelling connection with sewerage system
Municipalities and sewerage districts having a public system of sewerage may compel the connection therewith by owners of premises within three hundred feet of the public sewer and may compel owners to connect with water mains or provide other means for flushing purposes.
