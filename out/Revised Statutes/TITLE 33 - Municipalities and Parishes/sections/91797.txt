§9502.  Purpose
The purpose of any animal control district created pursuant to this Chapter shall be to provide for the regulation and control of canines and felines within the district, and all activities incidental thereto, as set forth in the ordinance creating the district adopted by the governing authority of St. Tammany Parish.
Acts 1993, No. 816, §1, eff. June 22, 1993.
