§7810.  Period of existence
The commission shall exist and continue and shall have and enjoy existence and succession for a period of the later of ninety-nine years from November 13, 1985, or until all of its contractual obligations have been performed and all debt securities issued by it fully paid.
Acts 1986, No. 377, §1, eff. July 2, 1986.
