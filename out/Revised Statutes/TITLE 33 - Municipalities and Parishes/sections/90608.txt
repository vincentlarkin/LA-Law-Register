§4142.  New Orleans; sale of bonds
All bonds issued under the authority of R.S. 33:4138 shall be sold in the manner provided for sewerage service revenue bonds in R.S. 33:4121(B)(4).
Added by Acts 1978, No. 419, §1, eff. July 1, 1978.  Acts 1985, No. 731, §1, eff. July 16, 1985.
