§4780.82.  Uniform regulations within district

A.  Within the district, the governing authority of the parish may regulate and restrict the erection, construction, alteration, or use of buildings, structures, or land.

B.  All regulations shall be uniform for each class or kind of land and structure throughout the district.

Acts 2012, No. 422, §1, eff. May 31, 2012.
