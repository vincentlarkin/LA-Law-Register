§102.  State psychiatric hospitals; return of escaped patients
Any escaped patient from a state psychiatric hospital shall be returned at the expense
of the state psychiatric hospital from which he left without authorization unless his discharge
is granted before his return.
Amended by Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
