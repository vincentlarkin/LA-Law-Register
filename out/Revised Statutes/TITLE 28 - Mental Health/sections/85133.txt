§22.9.  Rosenblum Mental Health Center
The name of the Hammond Mental Health Center is changed to the Rosenblum
Mental Health Center and under such name it shall continue to serve as an outpatient center
for the care, treatment, and rehabilitation of persons who have a mental illness and  persons
with intellectual or developmental disabilities at the region level.
Acts 1986, No. 776, §1; Acts 2014, No. 811, §14, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts 2017, No.
369, §2.
