§148.  Expenses of deportation

Expenses for deporting a nonresident patient shall be paid by the department out of appropriations for persons who are indigent and have a mental illness.

Amended by Acts 1974, No. 294, §1; Acts 1978, No. 786, §3, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2014, No. 811, §14, eff. June 23, 2014.
