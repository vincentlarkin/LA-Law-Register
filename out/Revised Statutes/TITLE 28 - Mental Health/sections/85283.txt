§756.  Council bylaws
The council shall adopt and amend bylaws governing its proceedings, activities, and organization, including but not limited to, provisions for election of officers, rotation of members, quorum, procedure, frequency, and location of meetings, and establishment, function, and membership on council committees.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 528, §1.
