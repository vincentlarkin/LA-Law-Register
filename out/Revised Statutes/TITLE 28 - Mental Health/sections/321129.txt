PART V.  QUALITY ASSURANCE
§455.1.  Quality standards
The office shall adopt standards for the quality of developmental disabilities services, shall disseminate those standards to persons with developmental disabilities and their families, stakeholders, and other interested parties, and shall establish mechanisms for feedback on the quality of services from persons with developmental disabilities and their families, stakeholders, and other interested parties.  Activities concerning quality standards developed pursuant to R.S. 28:382.1 may be conducted concurrently with the requirements of this Section.
Acts 2005, No. 128, §1, eff. June 22, 2005.
