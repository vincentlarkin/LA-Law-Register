§725.  Proposed transferees
The compact administrator is hereby directed to consult with the immediate family of any proposed transferee and, in the case of a proposed transferee from an institution in this state to an institution in another party state, to take no final action without approval of the coroner and judge of the parish from which the proposed transferee was committed.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 336, §1.
