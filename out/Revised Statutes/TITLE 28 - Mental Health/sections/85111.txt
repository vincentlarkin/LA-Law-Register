§184.  Furnishing intoxicants
Any person who knowingly makes available any intoxicant to any client of any
treatment facility, except with the permission of the director or administrator, shall be fined
not more than five hundred dollars,  imprisoned for not more than one year, or both.
Amended by Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
