PART I-A. BEHAVIORAL HEALTH SERVICES

PRESERVATION ACT

§11. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Behavioral Health Services Preservation Act".

            Acts 2012, No. 506, §1; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
