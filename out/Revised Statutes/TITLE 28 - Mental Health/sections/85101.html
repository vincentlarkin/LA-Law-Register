§145. Costs of transfer

            The person requesting the transfer shall pay the costs of transferring a patient between hospitals. The department shall pay the costs of transfers made at its request.

            Amended by Acts 1974, No. 294, §1; Acts 1978, No. 786, §3, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
