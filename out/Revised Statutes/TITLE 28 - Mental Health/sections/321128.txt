§454.18.  Advertisement and award of lease bid
The office and administrative units thereof are exempt from the requirement of R.S. 39:1643, regarding advertisement and award of lease bids, except that such exemption shall only be to lease privately owned buildings or space for the purpose of establishing living options.
Acts 2005, No. 128, §1, eff. June 22, 2005.
