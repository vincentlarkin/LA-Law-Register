PART IX-A.  CORONER'S STRATEGIC INITIATIVE FOR A
HEALTH INFORMATION AND INTERVENTION PROGRAM
§215.1.  Coroner's Strategic Initiative for a Health Information and Intervention Program;
creation
The office of the coroner in the parish of St. Tammany is hereby authorized to
establish within his office a Coroner's Strategic Initiative for a Health Information and
Intervention Program, referred to hereafter in this Part as "CSI/HIP".
