CHAPTER 5. GROUP HOME FOR PERSONS

WHO HAVE MENTAL ILLNESS OR

DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES ACT

§475. Short title

            This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Group Home for Persons Who Have Mental Illness or Developmental Disabilities Act".

            Added by Acts 1981, No. 892, §1, eff. Aug. 2, 1981; Acts 2014, No. 811, §14, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
