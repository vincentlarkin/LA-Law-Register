§73.  Application to stay, vacate, or modify
In addition to any right or remedy available by law, the respondent may apply to the
court to stay, vacate, or modify the order based on a change in circumstances and he shall
notify the director of the local governing entity or designee of his application.
Acts 2008, No. 407, §2; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2; Acts 2018, No. 375, §1; Acts 2021,
No. 329, §1.
