§726.  Distribution of copies of law
Duly authorized copies of this law shall, upon its approval, be transmitted by the compact administrator to the governor of each state, the attorney general and the administrator of the general services administration of the United States, and the council of state governments.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 336, §1.
