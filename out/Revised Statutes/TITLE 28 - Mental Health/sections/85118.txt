§205.  Allocation of funds
The department shall have final authority in determining the percentage not to exceed seventy-five percent of state and federal funds or either that may be allotted to any community behavioral health center as contemplated by R.S. 28:204 but the allocation may be cancelled at any time the department finds a community behavioral health or developmental disabilities center is violating any of the standards of operation and maintenance adopted under the provisions of R.S. 28:203.
Acts 1978, No. 786, §3, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2014, No. 811, §14, eff. June 23, 2014.
