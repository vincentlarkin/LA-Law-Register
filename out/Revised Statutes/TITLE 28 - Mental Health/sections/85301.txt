§825.  Administering medication
Personal care attendants, home- and community-based service provider workers, as
defined in R.S. 40:2120.2, and respite care workers providing in-home services to persons
with developmental disabilities pursuant to a Medicaid waiver may administer medication
to patients as provided in R.S. 37:1024 after successful completion of the drug administration
course offered by the Louisiana Department of Health in accordance with R.S. 37:1021 et
seq.
Acts 1995, No. 668, §2; Acts 2008, No. 839, §2, eff. July 8, 2008.
