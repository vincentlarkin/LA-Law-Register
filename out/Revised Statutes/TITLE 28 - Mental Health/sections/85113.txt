PART VIII.  COMMUNITY BEHAVIORAL HEALTH AND
DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES
CENTERS, FACILITIES, AND SERVICES
§200.  Promotion of a community-based system of care
It is hereby declared to be a function of the Louisiana Department of Health to
promote the establishment and administration of a community-based system of care,
including but not limited to community behavioral health clinics for persons who have a
mental illness, persons with developmental disabilities, or persons with both conditions as
contemplated by the provisions of R.S. 40:2013.
Acts 1978, No. 786, §3, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1997, No. 166, §1; Acts 2014, No.
811, §14, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
