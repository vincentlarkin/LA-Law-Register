§233.  Individuals prohibited from serving as representative
The following individuals shall be prohibited from serving as a representative:
(1)  The treating physician, provider, or an employee of the physician or provider if
the physician, provider, or employee is unrelated to the principal by blood, marriage, or
adoption.
(2)  An owner, operator, or employee of a treatment facility in which the principal is
a patient, client, or resident if the owner, operator, or employee is unrelated to the principal
by blood, marriage, or adoption.
Acts 2001, No. 755, §1; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
