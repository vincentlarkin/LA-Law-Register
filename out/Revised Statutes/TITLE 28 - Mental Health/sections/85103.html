§147. Method of collection

            The department may demand and receive any sums assessed as costs against a patient or his legal guardian, and in the case of nonpayment may sue to enforce collection.            Amended by Acts 1974, No. 294, §1; Acts 1978, No. 786, §3, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2017, No. 369, §2.
