§755.  Council remuneration

Members of the council shall not be entitled to compensation for their services as members but shall be reimbursed for actual travel expenses as provided by law and travel regulations issued by the Division of Administration.  Those members of the council serving in an ex officio capacity shall not be reimbursed for such actual travel expenses from grant monies specifically designated for the developmental disabilities program.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 528, §1.
