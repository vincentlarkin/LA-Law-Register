§242.  Article 142.  Uniformity of interpretation
This code shall be so construed as to effectuate its general purpose to make it uniform insofar as it does not expressly conflict with the Uniform Code of Military Justice, Chapter 47 of Title 10, United States Code.
Acts 1974, No. 621, §1.
