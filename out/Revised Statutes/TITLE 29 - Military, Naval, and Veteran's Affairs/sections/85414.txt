§183.  Article 83.  Malingering
Any person subject to this Code who for the purpose of avoiding work, duty, or
service in the state military forces:
(1)  feigns illness, physical disablement, mental lapse, or derangement; or
(2)  intentionally inflicts self-injury; shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.
