§170.  Article 70.  Appellate counsel
The accused shall have the right to appointed military defense counsel as appellate counsel.  The state shall be represented by the trial counsel or his designee.
Acts 1974, No. 621, §1; Acts 2007, No. 309, §1, eff. July 1, 2007.
