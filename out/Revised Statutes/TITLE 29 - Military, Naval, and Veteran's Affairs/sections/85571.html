§38.4.  Military police; extra compensation

Any person employed as a military policeman by the Military Department, State of Louisiana, who is certified pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 40:2405, after completion of one year of service, shall be paid by the state additional salary at the same rate provided for police officers and deputy sheriffs, provided funds are available.

Acts 2001, 2nd Ex. Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. Oct. 16, 2001.
