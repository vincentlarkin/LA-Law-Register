§231c.  Article 131c.  Misprision of serious offense
Any person subject to this Code who knows that another person has committed a
serious offense and wrongfully conceals the commission of the offense and fails to make the
commission of the offense known to civilian or military authorities as soon as possible shall
be punished as a court-martial may direct.
Acts 2022, No. 672, §1.
