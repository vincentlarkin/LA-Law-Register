§358.  Citation
It shall not be necessary to cite the person for whom the curator is sought, and there shall be no pronouncement of interdiction in the case, but the said person shall at all times have the right to have the appointment revoked and an accounting made to him or her by the curator upon producing proof that he has been pronounced cured.
