§423.  Award of attorney fees
In any proceeding employed to enforce any of the provisions of the Military Service Relief Act, the court may award reasonable litigation expenses, including but not limited to the payment of reasonable attorney fees.
Acts 1991, 1st E.S., No. 6, §1, eff. April 17, 1991; Acts 1995, No. 716, §1, eff. June 21, 1995.
