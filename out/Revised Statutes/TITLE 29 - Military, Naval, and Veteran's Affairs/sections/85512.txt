§287.  Limited construction
R.S. 29:285 and 29:286 shall be construed solely as an enabling law for the purpose of permitting a minor eligible for benefits under the Servicemen's Readjustment Act of 1944
1
to obtain these benefits and the effect of these Sections is specifically restricted to that purpose.
1
38 U.S.C.A. §1801 et seq.
