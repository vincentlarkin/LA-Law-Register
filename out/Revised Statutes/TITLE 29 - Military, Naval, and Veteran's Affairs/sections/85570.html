§38.3.  Award of attorney's fees

In those proceedings employed to redress prohibited practices under R.S. 29:38, 29:38.1 and 29:38.2, the court may award reasonable litigation expenses, including, but not limited, to attorney's fees upon good cause being shown, which cause may include the failure, refusal, or inability, of the district attorney to act as the service member's attorney pursuant to this statute.  

Added by Acts 1988, No. 187, §1, eff. July 1, 1988.
