§68.  Coordination; state government
All departments, commissions, boards, offices, entities, agencies, and officers of the state of Louisiana, or any political subdivision thereof, are authorized and directed to cooperate with the council in implementing the provisions of this Subpart.
Added by Acts 2012, No. 810, §2.
