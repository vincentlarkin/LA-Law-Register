§182.  Article 82.  Solicitation

A.  Any person subject to this code who solicits or advises another or others to desert in violation of R.S. 29:185 or mutiny in violation of R.S. 29:194 shall, if the offense solicited or advised is attempted or committed, be punished with the punishment provided for the commission of the offense, but, if the offense solicited or advised is not committed or attempted, he shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.  

B.  Any person subject to this code who solicits or advises another or others to commit an act of misbehavior before the enemy in violation of R.S. 29:199 or sedition in violation of R.S. 29:194 shall, if the offense solicited or advised is committed, be punished with the punishment provided for the commission of the offense, but, if the offense solicited or advised is not committed, he shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.  

Acts 1974, No. 621, §1.
