§220a.  Article 120a.  Mails; deposit of obscene matter
Any person subject to this Code who, wrongfully and knowingly, deposits obscene
matter for mailing and delivery shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.
Acts 2022, No. 672, §1.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 29:220a redesignated as R.S. 29:230 by Acts 2022, No. 672, §1.
