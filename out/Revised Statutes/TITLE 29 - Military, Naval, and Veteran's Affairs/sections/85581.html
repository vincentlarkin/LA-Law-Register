§40. Leasing for military purposes

            The public advertising and bidding procedures governing the leasing of airport space, military facilities, or reservations shall not apply to the Military Department of the state of Louisiana when leasing of such airport space, military facilities, or reservations is for military purposes.

            Acts 1984, No. 340, §2, eff. July 2, 1984; Acts 2017, No. 75, §1, eff. June 8, 2017.
