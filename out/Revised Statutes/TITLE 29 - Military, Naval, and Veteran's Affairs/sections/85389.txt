§160.  Article 60.  Initial action on the record
After a trial by court-martial the record shall be forwarded to the convening authority, and action thereon may be taken by the person who convened the court, a commissioned officer commanding for the time being, a successor in command, or the person exercising general court-martial jurisdiction.
Acts 1974, No. 621, §1.
