§382.  Title to property and facilities; contracts

Subject to the mutual agreement and consent of the Department of Veterans Affairs and the Louisiana Health and Human Resources Administration, such administration shall transfer the title to any lands, property, and facilities now owned by such administration to the Department of Veterans Affairs which may be deemed to be necessary for the effective implementation of the provisions of this Part.  Any contracts entered into for the operation and maintenance of the property and facilities used in connection with the purposes of this Part shall be contracted for by the Department of Veterans Affairs and the department is authorized to contract for such operation and maintenance.  

Acts 1965, No. 152, §2.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 294, §1; Acts 1974, No. 687, §1; Acts 1976, No. 300, §1.
