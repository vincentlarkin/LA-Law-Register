§174.  Article 74.  Remission and suspension
A.  The convening authority may remit or suspend any part or amount of the unexecuted part of any sentence, including all uncollected forfeitures.
B.  The convening authority may, for good cause, substitute an administrative form of discharge for a discharge or dismissal executed in accordance with the sentence of a court-martial.
Acts 1974, No. 621, §1; Acts 2007, No. 309, §1, eff. July 1, 2007.
