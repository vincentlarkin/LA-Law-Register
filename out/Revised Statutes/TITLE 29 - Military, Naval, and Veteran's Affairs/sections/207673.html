§763.  Preparation of Bioterrorism Response Plan; Homeland Security Advisory Council

The Subcommittee on Chemical and Biological Terrorism of the Homeland Security Advisory Council, as established by the Governor's Office of Homeland Security and Emergency Preparedness, or its successor, shall have responsibility for the preparation and updating of Annex M of the State Emergency Operations Plan.

Acts 2003, No. 1206, §1; Acts 2006, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 35, §8, eff. March 1, 2006; Acts 2006, No. 442, §3, eff. June 15, 2006.
