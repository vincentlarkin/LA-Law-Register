PART V-A.  FACILITIES FOR VETERANS CONFINED IN

FACILITIES OF DIVISION OF HOSPITALS OF THE

HEALTH AND HUMAN RESOURCES ADMINISTRATION

§391.  Operation of facilities

The Department of Veterans Affairs is authorized to establish and operate whatever facilities are necessary for the treatment and maintenance of Louisiana veterans with psychiatric and geriatric conditions.  The Department of Veterans Affairs is hereby authorized to enter into contracts for the operation of such facilities.  

Acts 1968, No. 648, §1.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 294, §1; Acts 1976, No. 300, §1.
