§171.  Article 71.  Execution of sentence; suspension of sentence

All court-martial sentences, unless suspended or deferred, may be ordered executed by the convening authority when approved by him.  The military judge, summary court officer, or the convening authority may suspend the execution of any sentence.

Acts 1974, No. 621, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 503, §1; Acts 2007, No. 309, §1, eff. July 1, 2007.
