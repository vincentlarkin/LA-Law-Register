§67.  Working groups
The council may create working groups composed of council members, stakeholders,
and non-council members which meet in accordance with the open meetings laws as
provided for in R.S. 42:11 et seq.
Added by Acts 2012, No. 810, §2; Acts 2018, No. 197, §1.
