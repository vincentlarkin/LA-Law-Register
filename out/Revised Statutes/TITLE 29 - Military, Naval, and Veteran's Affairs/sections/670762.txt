§783.  Applicability to volunteer health practitioners
This Chapter applies to volunteer health practitioners registered with a registration system that complies with R.S. 29:785 and who provide health services in this state for a host entity while an emergency declaration is in effect.
Acts 2009, No. 397, §1.
