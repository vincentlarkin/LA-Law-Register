§212.  Article 112.  Drunk on duty--sleeping on post--leaving post before relief

Any person subject to this code who is found drunk on duty or sleeping upon his post, or who leaves his post before he is regularly relieved, shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.  

Acts 1974, No. 621, §1.
