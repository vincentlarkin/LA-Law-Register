SUBPART G.  RETIREMENT SYSTEM AND PENSION
BENEFIT PLAN CREDIT
§411.  Retirement credit
A.  Any employee, who completes his service in the uniformed services and applies
for reemployment upon release from service in the uniformed services or discharge from
hospitalization incidental to his service in uniformed service, shall be entitled to receive
creditable service for such period of service in the uniformed services toward vesting and
computation of benefits in the retirement system, pension fund, or employee benefit plan
applicable to his employment, as provided in this Subpart.
B.  Except as required pursuant to federal law, no employee shall receive more than
a total of four years of military service credit in the retirement system, pension fund, or
employee benefit plan applicable to his employment, pursuant to this Part.
Acts 1991, 1st E.S., No. 6, §1, eff. April 17, 1991; Acts 1995, No. 716, §1, eff. June
21, 1995; Acts 2018, No. 225, §2, eff. May 15, 2018.
