SUBPART E.  APPOINTMENT AND COMPOSITION
OF COURTS-MARTIAL
§122.  Article 22.  Who may convene general courts-martial
General courts-martial may be convened by the governor or the adjutant general.
Acts 1974, No. 621, §1; Acts 2007, No. 309, §1, eff. July 1, 2007.
