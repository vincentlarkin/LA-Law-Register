§725.5.  Emergency Communications Interoperability Fund
A.  There is hereby created in the state treasury, as a special fund, the Emergency
Communications Interoperability Fund, hereinafter referred to in this Section as the "fund".
B.  All unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund at the end of the fiscal year
shall remain in the fund.  Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as monies
in the state general fund.  Interest earned on the investment of monies in the fund shall be
deposited in and credited to the fund.
C.  Monies in the fund shall be used to provide funding for the Louisiana Wireless
Information Network.
