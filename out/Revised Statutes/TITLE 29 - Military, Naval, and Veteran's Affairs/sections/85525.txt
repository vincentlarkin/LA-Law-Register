PART III.  AGENT ORANGE DIRECTORY
§301.  Purpose
The legislature finds a need for a program to compile information for the assessment of the effects of Agent Orange and other chemical defoliants or herbicides used in the Vietnam conflict and to provide assistance to persons who may be affected thereby.
Acts 1985, No. 878, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1986; Acts 1995, No. 746, §1.
