§365.  Purchase of property for home
Any curator or tutor appointed under this Part may purchase for his ward a home with the funds of the ward whenever the said purchase meets with the approval of the interested parties, the court of appointment, and the veterans administration, provided that sufficient time be given the veterans administration to investigate the property to be purchased.
