§425.  Vacancies in elected office
No vacancy in the office of an elected official shall be deemed to have occurred by reason of his absence when the official is called to service in the uniformed services as defined by R.S. 29:403(6).  No change in the residence of an elected official is deemed to have occurred by reason of his service in the uniformed services.
Acts 1991, 1st E.S., No. 6, §1, eff. April 17, 1991; Acts 1995, No. 716, §1, eff. June 21, 1995.
