§2316.  Port development

In order to facilitate the accomplishment of its objects and purposes, the commission is hereby authorized to cooperatively assist in developing ports in conjunction with the port commissions within the district.

Added by Acts 1965, No. 17, §16.  Amended by Acts 1993, No. 550, §1.
