§366.  Board of advisors

The governing authorities of the several municipalities or parishes are authorized to appoint three responsible citizen tax payers as a board of advisors, to serve without compensation, to assist them in letting contracts, performing and supervising the works authorized and in the expenditures of public funds therefor.
