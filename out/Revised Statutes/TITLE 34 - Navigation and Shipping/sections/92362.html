§340.6.  Nothing to affect Port of New Orleans

Nothing in this Subpart shall add to or detract from the provisions of law relative to the Board of Commissioners of the Port of New Orleans, the rights under all of said provisions being preserved.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 101, §1.
