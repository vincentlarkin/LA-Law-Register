§248.  Letting contracts; emergency procedures

Whenever any work is to be let by contract under the provisions of this Part, the board shall comply with the provisions of R.S. 38:2211 et seq.

Amended by Acts 1980, No. 165, §1; Acts 1982, No. 150, §1.
