CHAPTER 6.  PILOTS
PART I.  PORT OF NEW ORLEANS
SUBPART A.  BAR PILOTS
§941.  Definitions
As used in this Subpart, the following terms shall have the meaning ascribed to them in this Section, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:
(1)  "Board of examiners" or "board" means the Board of Examiners of Bar Pilots for the Port of New Orleans, established in R.S. 34:942.
(2)  "Bar pilot" or "pilot" means a Bar Pilot for the Port of New Orleans, as designated in R.S. 34:943.
(3)  "Services" of a bar pilot shall mean any advice or assistance with respect to pilotage by the commissioned bar pilot or by his authorized representative, including but not limited to advice concerning weather, channel conditions, and other navigational conditions.
Acts 1983, No. 331, §1.
