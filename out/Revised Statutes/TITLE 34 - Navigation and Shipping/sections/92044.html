§2205.  Advertisement; R.S. 43:111 not applicable

The prohibition contained in R.S. 43:111 against advertising in any newspaper, book, pamphlet or periodical, shall not apply to the Terrebonne Port Commission in carrying out its functions and duties under the constitution and laws of this state.  

Added by Acts 1964, No. 485, §1.
