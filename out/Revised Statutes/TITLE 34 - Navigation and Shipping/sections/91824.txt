§1046.  Oath of office and bond
The pilots shall take an oath of office and furnish bond in favor of the governor
conditioned upon the faithful performance of the duties imposed by this Part, to the extent
of one thousand dollars, approved by the board of examiners.
Acts 2015, No. 18, §1, eff. May 26, 2015.
