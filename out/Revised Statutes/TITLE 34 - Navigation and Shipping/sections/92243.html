§3255.  Limits of the district

A.  The commission shall exercise the powers herein conferred upon it, within the port area, consisting of the entire town of Grand Isle as the boundaries and limits of said town are presently or hereafter fixed by law and all of Ward 11 of Jefferson Parish.  

B.  The commission shall be domiciled in the town of Grand Isle.  

Added by Acts 1982, No. 864, §2.
