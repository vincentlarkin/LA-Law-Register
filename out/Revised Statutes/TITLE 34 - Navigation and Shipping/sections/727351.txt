PART IX. SAMUEL B. "SAMMY" NUNEZ, JR., BUILDING
§340.31.  Samuel B. "Sammy" Nunez, Jr., Building
Notwithstanding R.S. 42:267 or any other law to the contrary, the board of
commissioners of the St. Bernard Port, Harbor and Terminal District may name the
administration/security building of the port located at 100 Port Boulevard in Chalmette in
honor of Samuel B. "Sammy" Nunez, Jr., a living person who contributed to the growth and
well-being of St. Bernard Port, Harbor and Terminal District; served as a state representative
from St. Bernard Parish from 1964 through 1969; served as a state senator from 1969
through 1996 from St. Bernard Parish; and was inducted into the Louisiana Political Hall of
Fame on January 30, 2010.
Acts 2010, No. 35, §1, eff. May 26, 2010.
