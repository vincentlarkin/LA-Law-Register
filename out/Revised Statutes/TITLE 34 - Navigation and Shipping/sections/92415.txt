§364.  Acquisition of property and donation to federal government
When the works authorized in this Part, are undertaken solely by the federal government, the governing authorities of the municipalities or parishes are authorized to acquire by purchase, donation or expropriation, any property necessary for the federal government in the construction of such works, and to donate to the federal government the property so acquired.
