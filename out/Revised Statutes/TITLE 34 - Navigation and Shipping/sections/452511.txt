§852.18.  Forms; investigations
A.  The department shall prescribe and provide suitable forms of applications, financing statements, certificates of title, and all other notices and forms necessary to carry out this Part.
B.  The department may make necessary investigations to procure information required to carry out this Part.
Acts 2007, No. 319, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; H.C.R. No. 25, 2008 R.S., eff. May 28, 2008.
