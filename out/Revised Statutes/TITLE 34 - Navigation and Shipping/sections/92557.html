§946.  Members of board of examiners failing to perform duties; removal from office

Members of the board of examiners failing or refusing to perform their duties as such, may, upon written statement signed by a majority of the commissioned bar pilots stating their cause of complaint, be removed from office by the governor and the vacancy or vacancies filled by the governor as an original appointment.
