§3482. Report of any misuse of funds

            If the legislative auditor determines that any expenditures by the recipient governmental entity have not been made in accordance with this Chapter, he shall promptly report the facts of such expenditure to the Legislative Audit Advisory Council. The council shall make further investigation of the matter as it deems necessary.

            Acts 2017, No. 382, §1, eff. July 1, 2017.
