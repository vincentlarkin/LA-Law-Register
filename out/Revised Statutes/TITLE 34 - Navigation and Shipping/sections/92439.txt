§423.  Levee boards, provisions not to affect
Nothing contained in this Chapter shall deprive the levee boards of their authority with respect to levees within their respective districts.
Added by Acts 1975, 1st Ex.Sess., No. 48, §1, eff. Feb. 20, 1975.
