CHAPTER 52.  CAMERON PARISH PORT COMMISSION
§5201.  Creation of district
The Cameron Parish Port, Harbor, and Terminal District, hereinafter referred to as
the "district", is hereby created as a political subdivision of the state with full corporate
powers.  The territorial limits and territorial jurisdiction of the district shall be composed of
all of the area comprising Wards 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 of the parish of Cameron.  The district
shall have such authority, power, and jurisdiction as provided in this Chapter.
Acts 2016, No. 331, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
