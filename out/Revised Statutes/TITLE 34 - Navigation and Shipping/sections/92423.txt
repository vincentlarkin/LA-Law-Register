§382.  Areas requiring facility; connection to public road
Publicly owned access sites consisting of at least one boat launching ramp and one parking area shall be constructed or provided where the tracts of land are comprised of at least five acres and shall be located on the shoreline of the new lake, adjacent to public roads or connected to a public road by right of way acquired for public use.
Acts 1968, No. 652, §2.
