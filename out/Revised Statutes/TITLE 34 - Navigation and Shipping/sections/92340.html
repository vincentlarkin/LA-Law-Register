§335.6.  Advertisement

The prohibition contained in R.S. 43:111 against advertising in any newspaper, book, pamphlet, or periodical shall not apply to the board of commissioners of the Alexandria Regional Port in carrying out its functions and duties under the constitution and laws of this state.

Acts 1987, No. 440, §1; Acts 1992, No. 770, §1.
