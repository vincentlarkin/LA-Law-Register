SUBPART D.  RODENT CONTROL AND
PREVENTION OF INFECTIOUS DISEASES
§340.12.  Purpose
It is the intent of the legislature by means of this Subpart to establish a procedure to promote public health and safety and prevent the spread of infectious diseases by controlling rodent populations entering Louisiana from vessels and barges.
Acts 1997, No. 554, §1, eff. July 3, 1997.
