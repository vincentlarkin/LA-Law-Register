§3474.  Supplemental list of projects
The office also shall provide to the joint committee annually a supplemental list of
projects proposed to be commenced or authorized within the ensuing four years which are
in various stages of planning and preparation.  The supplemental list shall be subject to
change by the office until the office finally approves each project.
Acts 2017, No. 382, §1, eff. July 1, 2017.
