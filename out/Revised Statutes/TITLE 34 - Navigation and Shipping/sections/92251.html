§3266.  Bylaws, rules and regulations; quorum; proxy

The commission shall adopt such bylaws, rules and regulations as it may deem necessary for its own government and the conduct of its business, including the terms of its officers, not in conflict with this Chapter.  A quorum for the transaction of business at meetings shall consist of not less than three members.  With reference to salaries and compensation of employees, per diem and travel allowance of members, the incurring of funded, general or bonded debt and the levy of taxes, the affirmative vote of three members shall be necessary.  Vote by proxy shall not be permitted.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 391, §1.
