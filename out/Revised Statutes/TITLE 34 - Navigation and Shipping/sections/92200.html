§3163.  Right of expropriation

The Caddo-Bossier Parishes Port Commission may acquire by expropriation, and in accordance with the applicable expropriation laws of the state of Louisiana, any property necessary for the benefit and advantage of the commerce of the port; however, private wharves or landings in existence on November 8, 1966 shall not be subject to expropriation.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 66, §1.
