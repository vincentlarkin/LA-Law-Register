§2477.  Transitional
R.S. 34:2471 through 2476 shall be construed as continuations of and as substitutes for the provisions of Article VI, Section 33.1 of the Louisiana Constitution of 1921 and not as new enactments, and shall be amended or repealed only in accordance with Article VI, Section 43 of the Louisiana Constitution of 1974.
Acts 1975, No. 65, §1.
