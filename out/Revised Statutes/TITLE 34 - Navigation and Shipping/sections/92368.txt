§3406.  Construction of Chapter; controlling law
To the extent that the provisions of this Chapter are inconsistent with any other provisions of any general statute or special act or parts thereof, the provisions of this Chapter shall be deemed controlling.
Acts 1986, No. 83, §1, eff. June 17, 1986.
