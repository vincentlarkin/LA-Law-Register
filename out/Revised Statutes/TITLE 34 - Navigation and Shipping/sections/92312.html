PART VII.  VINTON HARBOR AND TERMINAL DISTRICT

§334.1.  Creation and territorial limits

The Vinton Harbor and Terminal District is hereby created as a political subdivision of the state of Louisiana, and its territorial limits are fixed as follows:

All of the area presently embraced within the limits of ward seven of the parish of Calcasieu, state of Louisiana.

Added by Acts 1956, No. 466, §1.
