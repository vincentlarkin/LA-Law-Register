§852.21.  Fees

A.  The department shall charge the following fees:

(1)  Certificate of title-eighteen dollars.

(2)  Duplicate certificate of title-fifteen dollars.

(3)  Notation of a security interest, whether a chattel mortgage, other security agreement, or other financing statement evidencing a security interest is recorded, on a vessel certificate of title-five dollars.

(4)  Cancellation of a notation of a security interest-five dollars.

(5)  The fees authorized under R.S. 10:9-525 et seq.

B.  In addition, the department shall collect a handling fee of eight dollars for each certificate of title issued.

Acts 2007, No. 319, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; H.C.R. No. 25, 2008 R.S., eff. May 28, 2008.
