§2312.  Revenue bonds
In addition to the power and authority to issue bonds payable from the capital outlay tax herein authorized in the manner provided by R.S. 34:2311, the commission shall have the right, power and authority to issue revenue bonds for the purposes and in the manner provided by Part XII, Chapter 4, Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, except that it shall not be necessary to hold an election to authorize the issuance of such bonds.
Added by Acts 1965, No. 17, §12.
