§3267.  Meetings

The commission shall meet annually at its domicile on a date to be fixed by the commission.  Other regular and special meetings may be held upon such call, after such notice, at such times and at such places within the district as the commission may prescribe and fix in its bylaws, not in conflict with general law.  The commission shall cause a record to be kept of all its proceedings and it shall select a newspaper or newspapers of general circulation within the district as its official journal or journals, in which all minutes and official notices shall be published promptly.  

Added by Acts 1982, No. 391, §1.
