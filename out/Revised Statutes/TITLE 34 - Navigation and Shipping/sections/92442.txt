PART II.  RED RIVER NAVIGATION DISTRICT
§441.  Creation of district; purpose
There is created a navigation and river improvement district which shall be known as the "Red River Navigation District of Louisiana" composed of the Parishes of Caddo and Bossier, for the purpose of improving Red River with respect to the navigation thereof within the State of Louisiana.
