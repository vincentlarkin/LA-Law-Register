§1506.  Right to expropriate
The Lake Providence Port Commission may acquire by expropriation in accordance with expropriation laws of the state of Louisiana, any wharves, landings or any other property necessary for the benefit and advantage of the commerce of the said commission.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 450, §1.
