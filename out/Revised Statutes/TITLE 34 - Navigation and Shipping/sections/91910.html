§1652.4.  Advertisement; exemption

The prohibition, contained in R.S. 43:111, against advertising in any newspaper, book, pamphlet or periodical, shall not apply to the Greater Lafourche Port Commission in carrying out its functions and duties under the constitution and laws of this state.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 22, §1.
