PART III.  LAKES CREATED BY STATE AGENCIES;
LAUNCHING FACILITIES
§381.  Duty of agency to construct facility
Whenever any agency of the state undertakes the construction or creation of a lake it shall construct adequate permanent public access and boat launching facilities and parking areas on or for the lake.  Such facilities and parking areas shall be constructed at the time of the creation or construction of the lake.
Acts 1968, No. 652, §1.
