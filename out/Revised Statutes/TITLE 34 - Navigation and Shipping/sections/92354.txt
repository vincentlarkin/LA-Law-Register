§340.13.  Control, testing, and monitoring center; establishment
Each port and terminal accepting vessel and barge traffic entering Louisiana waters may establish and maintain a rodent control, testing, and monitoring center.  The purposes of the center shall be the detection, evaluation, control, and prevention of infectious diseases spread by rodents from the vessels and barges.  The port and terminal may impose reasonable inspection requirements to implement the provisions of this Subpart.
Acts 1997, No. 554, §1, eff. July 3, 1997.
