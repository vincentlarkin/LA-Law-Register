§340.14.  Fees
Each port authority participating in this program may promulgate rules and regulations, including the charges to be levied on vessels and barges to pay for the necessary costs of administration and enforcement of the provisions of this Subpart.
Acts 1997, No. 554, §1, eff. July 3, 1997.
