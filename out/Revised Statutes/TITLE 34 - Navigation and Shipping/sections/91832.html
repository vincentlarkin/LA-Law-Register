§1054.  Offering of services

As used in this Part, reference to the offering of a New Orleans and Baton Rouge steamship pilot or the offering of services by a New Orleans and Baton Rouge steamship pilot shall mean any offering of any advice or assistance with respect to pilotage by the commissioned New Orleans and Baton Rouge steamship pilot or by his authorized representative, including but not limited to advice concerning weather, channel conditions, and other navigational conditions.

Added by Acts 1983, No. 331, §1.
