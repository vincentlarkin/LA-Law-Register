CHAPTER 40.  MERMENTAU RIVER HARBOR

AND TERMINAL DISTRICT

§3221.  Creation of district and territorial limits thereof

The Mermentau River Harbor and Terminal District, hereinafter referred to as the district, is hereby created as a political subdivision of the state of Louisiana, and its territorial limits shall extend to the limits of Ward 5 of Acadia Parish.  

Added by Acts 1976, No. 196, §1.
