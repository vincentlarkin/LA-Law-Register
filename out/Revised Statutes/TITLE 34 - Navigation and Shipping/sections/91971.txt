§1956.  Right to expropriate
The commission may acquire by expropriation in accordance with expropriation laws of the State of Louisiana, any wharves, landings or any other property necessary for the benefit and advantage of the commerce of the said commission.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 299, §1.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 497, §1.
