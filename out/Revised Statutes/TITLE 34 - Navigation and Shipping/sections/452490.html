PART IV-A.  VESSEL AND MOTOR TITLING ACT

§852.1.  Short title

This Part may be cited as the "Vessel and Motor Titling Act".

Acts 2007, No. 319, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; H.C.R. No. 25, 2008 R.S, eff. May 28, 2008; Acts 2009, No. 508, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
