§852.19.  Adoption of rules and regulations

The department shall adopt the necessary rules and regulations to implement the provisions of this Part.

Acts 2007, No. 319, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; H.C.R. No. 25, 2008 R.S., eff. May 28, 2008.
