§1157.3.  Rapides Parish industrial development board; bond issuance prohibited

Beginning August 1, 1993, the Industrial Development Board of Rapides Parish shall be prohibited from issuing any bonds pursuant to the authority provided by Chapter 7 of Title 51 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.  The board shall honor all bonds issued previous to August 1, 1993.  

Acts 1993, No. 735, §1.
