§2390.  Reports

Each licensee, each affiliate of a licensee, and each subsidiary of a licensee shall file with the commissioner such reports as and when the commissioner may reasonably require.  A report under this Section shall be in such form and shall contain such information as the commissioner may reasonably require.

Acts 1991, No. 506, §1.
