CHAPTER 4-B.
STRATEGIC PLAN TO COMBAT POVERTY
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§1031.  Short title
This Chapter shall be named and may be cited as the Louisiana Strategic Plan to Combat Poverty Act.
Acts 2002, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 44, §1, eff. April 18, 2002; Acts 2002, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 162, §3, eff. April 26, 2002.
