CHAPTER 36.  LOUISIANA CONTINUING CARE PROVIDER
REGISTRATION AND DISCLOSURE ACT
§2171.  Title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Continuing Care Provider Registration and Disclosure Act".
