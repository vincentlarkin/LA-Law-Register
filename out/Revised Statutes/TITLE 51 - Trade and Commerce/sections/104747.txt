§929.  Domicile of the department and the board; branch offices
Louisiana Economic Development and the Board of Commerce and Industry
shall be domiciled in the city of Baton Rouge.  Branch offices may be established at
such other locations as the board may deem advisable.
Acts 1964, No. 403, §1.
