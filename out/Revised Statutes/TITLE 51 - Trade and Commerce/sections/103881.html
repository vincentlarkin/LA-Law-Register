TITLE 51 TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 1.  IN GENERAL

PART I.  COMMISSION MERCHANTS

§1.  Definitions

As used in this Part:

Commission merchant means every person who sells in this state on commission or buys as broker or agent for or on account of any person, farm products, fish, oysters, shrimp, crabs, game, or fur skins.  

Farm products means all agricultural, horticultural, and floricultural products, live stock, meats, hides, poultry, eggs, butter, milk, nuts, honey, and seeds.
