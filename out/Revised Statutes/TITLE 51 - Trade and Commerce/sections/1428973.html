§3275.5. Implementation

            A. The commission shall promulgate any rules and regulations necessary for the collection and publication of the information outlined in this Chapter.

            B. This Chapter does not require an energy utility to provide an annual consumption report, but an energy utility shall disclose to the commission whether it does or does not provide this service.

            Acts 2025, No. 311, §1, eff. June 24, 2025.
