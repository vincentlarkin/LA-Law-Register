§2765.  Nonconformity disclosure requirement
No assistive device returned by a consumer or assistive device lessor in this state or any other state may be sold or leased again in this state unless full disclosure of the reason for return is made to any prospective buyer or lessee.
Acts 1993, No. 907, §1, eff. June 23, 1993.
