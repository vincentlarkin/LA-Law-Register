§149.  Discovery; transcript; governor to have custody

The officer designated under the authority of R.S. 51:144 shall cause the testimony to be recorded by competent stenographers.  Each witness shall sign his testimony.  After the close of the examination, the officer shall certify and deliver all testimony to the Attorney General or district attorney.  The Attorney General or district attorney shall forthwith seal and forward it to the governor who shall be the custodian thereof.
