§724.  Fees and expenses
All fees and charges payable under the provisions of this Part and rules promulgated thereunder, shall be collected by the commissioner, shall be nonrefundable and shall be immediately transmitted by him to the state treasurer.
Acts 1985, No. 722, §1; Acts 1986, No. 465, §1, eff. July 1, 1986; Acts 2006, No. 544, §1, eff. June 22, 2006.
