CHAPTER 13.  UNFAIR TRADE PRACTICES AND CONSUMER
PROTECTION LAW
§1401.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Unfair Trade Practices and Consumer Protection Law."
Added by Acts 1972, No. 759, §1.
