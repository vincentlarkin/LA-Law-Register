§938.  Advisory committees; functions
The secretary may appoint advisory committees composed of representatives of the businesses and industries and labor of the state and fix the number of members of each.  These committees shall conduct such studies and investigations as the secretary may direct and shall report their findings and recommendations thereon to the secretary.
Acts 1964, No. 403, §1; Acts 1989, No. 316, §1.
