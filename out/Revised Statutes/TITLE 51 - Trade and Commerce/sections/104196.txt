§1930.  Other department responsibilities
The department, in addition to other grants of authority to promote the economic development of the state, is hereby authorized to serve as a clearinghouse for information relevant to potential incorporators or organizers of certified Louisiana capital companies and for the locating and promoting of qualified Louisiana businesses seeking infusions of capital.
Acts 1989, No. 496, §1.
