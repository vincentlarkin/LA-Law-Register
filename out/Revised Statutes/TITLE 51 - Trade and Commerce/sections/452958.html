§3104.  Designation of banking development districts; application

A.  A local government, in conjunction with a financial institution, may submit an application, on a form prescribed by the commissioner, to the commissioner for the designation of a banking development district within a specified geographic area.

B.  A financial institution may apply to open a branch in the banking development district at the time the local government submits an application for the designation of such a district.

Acts 2007, No. 255, §1.
