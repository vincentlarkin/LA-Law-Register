CHAPTER 43.  LOUISIANA

EQUAL HOUSING OPPORTUNITY ACT

§2601.  Title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Equal Housing Opportunity Act".

Acts 1991, No. 527, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1997, No. 1141, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
