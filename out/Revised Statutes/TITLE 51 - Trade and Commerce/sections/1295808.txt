CHAPTER 62.  IMPORT RESTRICTIONS
§3221.  Imports from the Russian Federation
The importation of any product into this state produced in or originating from the
Russian Federation is prohibited. This prohibition includes imports of oil and gas, iron and
steel, precious metals, and agricultural products.
Acts 2022, No. 236, §1, eff. May 31, 2022.
