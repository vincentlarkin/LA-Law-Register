CHAPTER 18.  RENTAL REFERRAL AGENCIES

§1700.  Definition

A "rental referral agency" is a person or business which advertises, offers, or attempts to offer for a prepaid fee, listings of residential rental property.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 407, §1.
