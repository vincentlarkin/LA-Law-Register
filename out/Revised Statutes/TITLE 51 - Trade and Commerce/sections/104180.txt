§1916.  Application
The provisions of this Chapter shall apply in all circumstances in which any party to the contract conducted any contractual activity, including but not limited to advertising, solicitation, discussion, negotiation, offer, acceptance, signing, or performance, in this state.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 811, §1.
