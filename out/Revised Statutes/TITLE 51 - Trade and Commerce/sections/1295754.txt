§772.  Prohibition on the sale or offer for sale of certain cosmetics
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, it is unlawful for a
manufacturer to sell or offer for sale in this state a cosmetic that utilized cosmetic animal
testing during the development or manufacture of the cosmetic, if the cosmetic animal testing
was conducted by the manufacturer, any supplier of the manufacturer, or any person or
business hired or contracted by the manufacturer.
Acts 2022, No. 712, §1.
