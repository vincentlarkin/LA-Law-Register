§2260.  Documentary evidence; effect

The production of a written, printed, or visual communication, advertisement, or other form of publication, or a written inquiry, record, or other document purporting to have been made by an individual shall be prima facie evidence in a proceeding under this Chapter that it was authorized by the individual.

Acts 1988, No. 886, §1.
