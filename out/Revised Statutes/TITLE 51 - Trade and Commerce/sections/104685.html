PART XIV. MANUFACTURED AND MODULAR HOUSING

§911.21. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as "Uniform Standards Code for Manufactured and Modular Housing".

            Acts 1974, No. 281, §1. Amended by Acts 1982, No. 565, §1, eff. July 22, 1982; Acts 1982, No. 211, §1; Acts 1984, No. 576, §3; Acts 2001, No. 718, §2; Acts 2017, No. 221, §1.
