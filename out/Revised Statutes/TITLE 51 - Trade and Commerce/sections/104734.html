§913.1.  Dealers

A person whose primary business is the sale and purchase of the equipment defined in R.S. 51:913 shall maintain a copy of each purchase and each sale receipt or invoice and shall make them available in an easily retrievable form for inspection by any law enforcement officer.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 549, §1.
