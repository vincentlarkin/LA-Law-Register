§2377.  Termination of Chapter
The provisions of this Chapter shall terminate on June 30, 2023, and thereafter shall
be null, void, and without effect.
Acts 2018, No. 327, §1; Acts 2020, No. 21, §1, eff. June 4, 2020.
NOTE:  This Section is updated through the 2020 First Extraordinary
Session, but is subject to final technical revisions by the Louisiana State Law
Institute.
