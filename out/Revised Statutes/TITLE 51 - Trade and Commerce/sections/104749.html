§931. Certification of small entrepreneurships

            The secretary of Louisiana Economic Development shall certify businesses as small entrepreneurships for purposes of the Louisiana Initiative for Small Entrepreneurships (the Hudson Initiative) as provided in R.S. 39:2006.

            Acts 2005, No. 440, §2, eff. July 11, 2005.
