§350.  Independent truckers; prohibit requirement of broker
A.  It shall be unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation to require that an independent trucker utilize the services of a broker in contracting for work with that person, firm, or corporation.
B.  If any firm or corporation, foreign or domestic, authorized to do business in this state or any officer, agent, or receiver of any such firm or corporation knowingly and willfully violates the provisions of this Section, his license or permit to do business in this state shall be revoked immediately.
Acts 1984, No. 856, §1.
