§1365.  Termination
The provisions of this Chapter shall terminate at twelve o'clock midnight on June 30,
2028, and shall thereafter have no effect.
Acts 2020, 2nd Ex. Sess., No. 24, §3, see Act; Acts 2022, No. 760, §1, eff. June 18,
2022.
