§2370.25.  Records; limitations
A.  Notwithstanding any provision of this Subpart to the contrary, all records related
to the GUMBO 2.0 program are public records as provided by the Public Records Law, R.S.
44:1 et seq., except for a broadband service provider's trade secret and proprietary
information, including coverage data, maps, and shapefiles.
B.  The office shall treat any information submitted with a broadband availability
challenge or grant award protest that is not publicly available as confidential and subject to
the trade secrets protections of state law upon a challenging broadband service provider's
request for confidential treatment.
Acts 2023, No. 383, §1.
