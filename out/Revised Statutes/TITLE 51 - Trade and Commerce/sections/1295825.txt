§3266.  Preemption
No political subdivision may establish, mandate, or otherwise require an online
marketplace to collect or verify information from a high-volume third-party seller or disclose
information to a consumer on a one-time or ongoing basis.
Acts 2022, No. 316, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2023.
