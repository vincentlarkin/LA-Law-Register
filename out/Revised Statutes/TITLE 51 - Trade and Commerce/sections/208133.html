CHAPTER 49. PROHIBITION ON CONTRACTS WITH CERTAIN FOREIGN-OWNED COMPANIES IN CONNECTION WITH

CRITICAL INFRASTRUCTURE

§3051. Short title

            This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Transparency in Ownership of Critical Infrastructure Law".

            Acts 2022, No. 766, §1, eff. June 18, 2022.
