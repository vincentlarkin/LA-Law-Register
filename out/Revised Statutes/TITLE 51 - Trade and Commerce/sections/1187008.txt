§2115.  Prohibition on contracting with a provider
A public body shall not enter into a contract for managed security services with a
provider that has not registered with the secretary of state or has failed to renew its
registration with the secretary of state.  Such a contract shall be null and void.
Acts 2020, No. 117, §2, eff. Feb. 1, 2021.
