§3138.  Exceptions

A.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, nothing in this Chapter shall be construed to affect the Port Construction and Development Priority Program, R.S. 34:3451 et seq.

B.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, nothing in this Chapter shall be construed to affect the Airport Construction and Development Priority Program, R.S. 2:801 et seq.

Acts 2012, No. 687, §2, eff. June 7, 2012.
