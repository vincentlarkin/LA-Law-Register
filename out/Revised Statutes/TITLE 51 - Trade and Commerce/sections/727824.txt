CHAPTER 13-B.  LOUISIANA CHILD SUPPORT
COLLECTION PROTECTION ACT
§1441.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Child Support Collection Protection Act".
Acts 2010, No. 872, §2, eff. July 2, 2010.
