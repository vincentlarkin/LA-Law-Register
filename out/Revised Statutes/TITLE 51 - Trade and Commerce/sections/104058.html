§145.  Discovery; service; place of examination

The order to appear as a witness under the provisions of R.S. 51:144 shall be served and return made as in the case of subpoenas.  

The place fixed for the examination shall be that most convenient to the person being investigated, whether within or without the parish of his domicile.
