§947.  Executive branch agency participation in mentor-protégé program

Any state agency may develop a plan for participation in the mentor-protégé program which plan shall be consistent with the rules and regulations adopted pursuant to R.S. 51:946.  Additionally, each plan shall specify the conditions for participation for that agency in accordance with the statutes governing public bid and procurement.

Acts 2006, No. 434, §1.
