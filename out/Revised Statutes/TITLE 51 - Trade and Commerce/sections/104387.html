§2398.  General provisions

A.  This Chapter shall be liberally construed to accomplish its purposes.

B.  A proceeding to promulgate rules or regulations regarding civil penalties under R.S. 51:2396(I) shall be subject to the Administrative Procedure Act.

C.  Except as otherwise provided in this Subsection, the provisions of a licensee's incorporating statute apply to the licensee.  If a provision of the licensee's incorporating statute conflicts with any provision of this Chapter, this Chapter controls.

Acts 1991, No. 506, §1.
