CHAPTER 5.  EMPOWERING FAMILIES TO LIVE WELL LOUISIANA
§1055. Short title
This Chapter shall be named and may be cited as the "Empowering Families to Live
Well Louisiana Act".
Acts 2018, No. 581, §1, eff. May 31, 2018.
