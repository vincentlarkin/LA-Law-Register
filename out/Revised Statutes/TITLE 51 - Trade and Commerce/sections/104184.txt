§1922.1.  Administration of the program
Louisiana Economic Development shall maintain and interpret policy for the
Louisiana Capital Companies Tax Credit Program.  The Office of Financial
Institutions shall perform the regulatory and examination functions of the program.
Acts 2001, No. 1122, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
