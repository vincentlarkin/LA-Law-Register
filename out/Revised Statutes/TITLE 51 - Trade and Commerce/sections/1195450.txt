§3213.  Powers and duties
The Louisiana Gaming Control Board, the Louisiana State Racing Commission, and
the office of alcohol and tobacco control have authority to take actions, not inconsistent with
law, as deemed necessary to ensure a respective licensee's compliance with the provisions
of this Chapter.
