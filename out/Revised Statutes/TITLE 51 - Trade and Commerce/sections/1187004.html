CHAPTER 31-A. MANAGED SERVICE PROVIDERS

AND MANAGED SECURITY SERVICE PROVIDERS

FOR PUBLIC BODIES

§2111. Scope and purpose

            The purposes of this Chapter are:

            (1) To create a registration for managed service providers and managed security service providers doing business in this state with a public body.

            (2) To provide access for public bodies to obtain information on managed service providers and managed security service providers.

            (3) To require managed service providers and managed security service providers to report cyber incidents and the payment of cyber ransom or ransomware.

            Acts 2020, No. 117, §2, eff. Feb. 1, 2021.
