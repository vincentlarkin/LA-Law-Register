§2370.16. Records; limitations

            Notwithstanding any provision of this Subpart to the contrary, all records related to the GUMBO program shall be public records as provided by the Public Records Law, except for a provider's trade secret and proprietary information, including coverage data, maps, and shapefiles.

            Acts 2021, No. 477, §1, eff. July 1, 2021; Acts 2022, No. 288, §2; Acts 2023, No. 383, §3A.
