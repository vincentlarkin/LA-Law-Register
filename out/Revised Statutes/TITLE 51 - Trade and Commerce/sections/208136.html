§3054. Designation of country as a threat to critical infrastructure

            A. The governor, after consultation with the director of the Governor's Office of Homeland Security and Emergency Preparedness, may designate a country as a threat to critical infrastructure for purposes of this Chapter.

            B. The governor shall consult the Senate and House select committees on homeland security to assess a threat to critical infrastructure for purposes of making a designation under this Section.

            Acts 2022, No. 766, §1, eff. June 18, 2022.
