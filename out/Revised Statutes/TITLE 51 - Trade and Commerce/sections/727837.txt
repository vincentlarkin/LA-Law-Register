§2363.  Accountability requirements; reports; Rapid Response Fund
A.  The secretary of Louisiana Economic Development shall develop a uniform
accountability report for economic development created by the Rapid Response Fund.  The
secretary shall also develop a formula for measuring the return on investment for each Rapid
Response Fund project.
B.  Louisiana Economic Development shall compile and make available a list of the
cooperative endeavor agreements, the name of the entity receiving funds, and the amount of
the incentive received for all Rapid Response Fund projects in both written and electronic
form.
Acts 2010, No. 420, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
