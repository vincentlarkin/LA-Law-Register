§3283.  Financial privacy
A.  Except for those records kept during the regular course of a criminal investigation
and prosecution, or as otherwise required by law, no government entity or official and no
agent or employee of a governmental entity shall knowingly keep or cause to be kept any list,
record, or registry of privately owned firearms or the owners of those firearms.
B.  A payment card network shall not require or incentivize the use of a merchant
category code in a manner that distinguishes a firearm retailer from other retailers.
C.  A covered entity shall not assign a firearm retailer a merchant category code that
distinguishes the firearm retailer from other retailers.
Acts 2024, No. 585, §1.
