§2320.3.  Penalty for false statements
Whoever knowingly files a false statement concerning an application for any program administered by the corporation shall be guilty of the offense of filing false public records and shall be subject to the penalty provided for in R.S. 14:133.
Acts 1992, No. 1136, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
