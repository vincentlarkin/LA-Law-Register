§3275.2. Definitions

            For the purposes of this Chapter, the following definitions apply:

            (1) "Annual consumption report" means a detailed report of the aggregate energy usage for all the meters associated with a property during the previous calendar year, including but not limited to total usage, peak usage periods, and cost.

            (2) "Commission" means the Public Service Commission.

            (3) "Energy utility" means any public utility providing electricity or natural gas services within the state.

            Act 2025, No. 311, §1, eff. June 24, 2025.
