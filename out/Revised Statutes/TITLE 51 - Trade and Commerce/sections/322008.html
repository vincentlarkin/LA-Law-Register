PART IV.  PLAN; COORDINATION AND IMPLEMENTATION

§1045.1.  Legislative findings

The legislature finds that poverty is one of the greatest enemies of human dignity and undermines the general welfare of the people of Louisiana.  It destroys the liberty our ancestors fought so hard to preserve, a fight that continues today.  The legislature continues to promote the health, safety, education, and welfare of the people of Louisiana by enacting this Part to combat poverty.

Acts 2005, No. 404, §1.
