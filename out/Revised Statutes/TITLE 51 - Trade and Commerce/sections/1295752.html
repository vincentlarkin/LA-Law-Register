§470.6. Construction and applicability

            A. The rights granted by this Subpart are cumulative and shall be in addition to any others provided by law.

            B. The property rights granted by this Subpart vest with an individual or the individual's authorized representative on August 1, 2022.

            Acts 2022, No. 425, §1.
