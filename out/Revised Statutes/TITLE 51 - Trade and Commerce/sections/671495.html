§2399.5. Promulgation of rules

            Louisiana Economic Development, Louisiana Works and the Department of Revenue shall each promulgate such rules, in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, as may be necessary to implement the provisions of this Chapter.

            Acts 2009, No. 447, §1.
