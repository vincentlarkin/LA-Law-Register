§2135.  Written permit required
No minor's employment regulated under the provisions of this Chapter shall occur without
the issuance of a written permit from Louisiana Works as provided in Chapter 3 of Title 23 of the
Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
Acts 2005, No. 147, §2; Acts 2008, No. 743, §7, eff. July 1, 2008.
