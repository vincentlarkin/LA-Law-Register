§193.  Motor vehicle dealer exemption

No motor vehicle dealer licensed pursuant to Title 32 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 who is engaged in the sale of new or used cars or trucks may be open on Sunday.  

Acts 1986, No. 1, §1.
