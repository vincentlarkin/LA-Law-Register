§3205.  Insignia; creation; use
The department shall create an insignia for use by certified veteran-owned businesses
to indicate to the public that the business is part of The Veterans First Business Initiative.
After certification, a veteran-owned business shall have the right and privilege of displaying
the insignia on its storefront and on any promotional or marketing materials.  The display of
the insignia serves to distinguish the business as being veteran-owned.
Acts 2019, No. 160, §1.
