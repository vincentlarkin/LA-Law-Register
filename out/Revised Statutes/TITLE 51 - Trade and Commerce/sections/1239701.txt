§2370.6.  Consultation
The office may consult with Louisiana Economic Development to determine if a
broadband project proposed under this Subpart will benefit a potential economic
development project relevant to the proposed area outlined in the broadband project.
Acts 2021, No. 477, §1, eff. July 1, 2021; Acts 2023, No. 383, §3A.
