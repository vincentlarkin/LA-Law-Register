§2370.18. Coordination with parishes and municipalities

            Beginning August 1, 2024, broadband providers receiving funding through the GUMBO 1.0 program shall provide a thirty-day advanced written notice prior to the commencement of GUMBO 1.0 construction activities to the relevant parish or municipality. Nothing in this Section relieves the GUMBO 1.0 grantee of its obligations to comply with local regulations and ordinances.

            Acts 2024, No. 632, §1.
