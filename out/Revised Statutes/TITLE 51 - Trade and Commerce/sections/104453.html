§283. Corporation; partnership

           This Subpart does not apply to any corporation duly organized under the laws of Louisiana or lawfully doing business in Louisiana. This Subpart shall not prevent the lawful use of a partnership name or designation, if the partnership name or designation includes the real name of at least one of the persons transacting the business.
