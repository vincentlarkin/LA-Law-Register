§692.1.  Sale on the open market prohibited
It shall be unlawful for the Department of Public Safety and Corrections to sell or offer for sale on the open market of this state any articles, goods, wares, or merchandise produced, manufactured, or mined, wholly or in part, in this or any other state by convicts and prisoners of this state or any other state, except convicts and prisoners on parole or probation.
Acts 1950, No. 350, §1; Acts 1999, No. 741, §1.
