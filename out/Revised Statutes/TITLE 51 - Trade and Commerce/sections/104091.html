§1578.  Prohibited clauses in contracts

No contract for physical fitness services may:

(1)  Have a duration of longer than thirty-six months or be measured by the life of the buyer or the life of the center.  

(2)  Waive the required provisions of this Chapter.  

(3)  Provide that a right of action or defense of the member shall be terminated by assignment of the contract to a third person.  

Added by Acts 1982, No. 808, §1.
