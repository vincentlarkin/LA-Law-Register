§1011.  Cooperation of departments, agencies, and officers

The departments, agencies, and officers of this state and its subdivisions are hereby authorized to cooperate with the Southern States Energy Board in the furtherance of any of its activities pursuant to the Compact.  

Acts 1979, No. 576, §1.
