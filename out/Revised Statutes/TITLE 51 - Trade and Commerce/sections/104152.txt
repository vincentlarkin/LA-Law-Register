§1790.  Management
The governing authority of an enterprise zone or economic development zone may contract with a community organization or a volunteer or non-profit charitable organization to provide management services for the zone.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 120, §1.  Acts 1995, No. 581, §2, eff. June 18, 1995.
