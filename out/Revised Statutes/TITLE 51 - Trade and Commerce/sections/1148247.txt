§3207.  Outreach
The department shall work in cooperation with the Louisiana Department of Veterans
Affairs for outreach to regional economic development organizations, local economic
development organizations, and chambers of commerce throughout the state.  The
cooperation shall serve to encourage business owners who are veterans to seek certification
as part of The Veterans First Business Initiative and to promote veteran-owned businesses
that have been certified pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 2019, No. 160, §1.
