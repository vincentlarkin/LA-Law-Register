§1966.  Enforceability
The provisions of this Chapter are not intended to limit in any manner the effectiveness or enforceability of any of the provisions of such a license agreement under any other provisions of the laws of this state, nor is this Chapter intended to limit in any manner the laws of this state or any other laws.
Acts 1984, No. 744, §1.
