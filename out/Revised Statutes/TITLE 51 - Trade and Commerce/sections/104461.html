§294.  Issuance by secretary of state of certificates of registration of trademarks, service marks and trade names reserved to Louisiana Stadium and Exposition District

The secretary of state shall not register any trade name, (including corporate names), or trademark or service mark reserved by this Subpart to the Louisiana Stadium and Exposition District, except for said district or with the written approval of said district.  

Added by Acts 1973, No. 132, §1.
