§1581.  Preservation of rights of action and defenses

Any right of action or defense which the member could raise based on the contract for physical fitness services is preserved against any assignee or successor to the contract or to any credit contract executed by the member in connection therewith.  

Added by Acts 1982, No. 808, §1.
