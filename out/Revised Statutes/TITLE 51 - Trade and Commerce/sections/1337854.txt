§2370.31.  Receipt and disbursement of federal grant funds
The division of administration shall be the designated agency for receipt and
disbursement of state and federal funds received to implement the BEAD program.  All
federal grant funds received for this purpose shall be disbursed in accordance with R.S.
51:2370.21(A).
Acts 2023, No. 383, §1.
