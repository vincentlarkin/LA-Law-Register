§911.29. Motor vehicle inspection; exception

            The provisions of Chapter 7 of Title 32 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 relative to inspections shall not apply to manufactured or modular housing.

            Acts 1974, No. 281, §1. Amended by Acts 1982, No. 211, §1, eff. July 15, 1982; Acts 2001, No. 718, §2; Acts 2017, No. 221, §1.
