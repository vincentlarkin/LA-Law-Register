§2330.  Qualifications for tenants

A.  The tenants of a small business incubator created by this Part shall be limited to technology based start-up companies.

B.  The corporation shall define by rule a qualified technology based start-up company and establish eligibility qualifications for inclusion in a small business incubator.

C.  In addition to the rules established by the corporation, the applicant may also establish additional qualifications for inclusion in a small business incubator which are not inconsistent with this Part.

Acts 2003, No. 1170, §1.
