§935.  Information clearinghouse
The department shall serve as the information clearinghouse in Louisiana for all data related to economic development, economic modeling, and strategic economic development planning.
Acts 1995, No. 482, §1; Acts 2003, No. 183, §8; Acts 2010, No. 1034, §2.
