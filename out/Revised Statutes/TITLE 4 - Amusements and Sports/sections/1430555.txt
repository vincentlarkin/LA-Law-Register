§290.  Dissolution of corporation
Upon dissolution of the corporation, all of the funds, property, both movable and
immovable, and both tangible or intangible, assets, interests, rights, and all other property
whatsoever, shall become owned by and shall inure to the benefit of the state.
Acts 2025, No. 512, §1.
