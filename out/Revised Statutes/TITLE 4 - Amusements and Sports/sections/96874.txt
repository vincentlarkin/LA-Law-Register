§364.  Refusal to comply; punishment
Whenever the owner of a dance hall refuses to comply with the
provisions of this Chapter, he shall be fined not less than twenty-five dollars
nor more than one hundred dollars, and imprisoned for not less than thirty
days, for each violation.
