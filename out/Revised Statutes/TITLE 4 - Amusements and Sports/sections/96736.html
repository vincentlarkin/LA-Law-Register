§10.5.  Exemptions

The provisions of R.S. 4:10 through 10.6 shall not apply to games operated by volunteers of churches, educational, charitable, eleemosynary, veteran or senior citizen organizations, or associations and civic or nonprofit organizations or associations.

Added by Acts 1981, No. 918, §1, eff. Aug. 2, 1981.
