§145.2.  Exclusive venue for judicial review of adjudication
Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 49:978.1(B), proceedings for judicial review
of a final decision or order in an adjudication proceeding by the commission may be
instituted by filing a petition in the district court in the parish where the matter arose that was
the subject of the final decision or order within thirty days after mailing notice of the final
decision by the commission or, if a rehearing is requested, within thirty days after the
decision thereon.  Copies of the petition shall be served upon the commission and all parties
of record.
Acts 2010, No. 675, §1.
