§82.  Noncompliance with Chapter; penalty

Whoever conducts, promotes, or in any manner participates in any boxing, mixed technique event, or wrestling contest or exhibition without first complying with the provisions of this Chapter shall be fined not more than five hundred dollars.

Acts 1974, No. 553, §1; Acts 2007, No. 90, §1.
