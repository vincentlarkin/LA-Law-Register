§173.  Commissioners and stewards not liable in damages

Both commissioners and stewards in the exercise of their duties and use of their discretion shall not be held liable for damages to an aggrieved person, even in the event of a mistake, as long as they acted in good faith, without malice or improper motive.

Acts 1968, No. 554, §1.
