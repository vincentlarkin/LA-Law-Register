§145.1.  Exclusive venue for law suits against the commission
The commission may be sued only in the city of New Orleans, parish of Orleans or in the parishes of Bossier, Calcasieu, or St. Landry.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 229, §1, eff. June 30, 1977; Acts 2007, No. 129, §1.
