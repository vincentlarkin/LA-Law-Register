§10.6.  Violations; penalties
Anyone who intentionally operates, who intentionally hires anyone to
operate, or intentionally permits anyone to operate any games of chance or
skill as listed in R.S. 4:10.3 or any other game of chance or skill for pay which
defrauds or attempts to defraud the player, or who intentionally violates any
other provision of R.S. 4:10 through 10.3 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor
and, upon conviction, shall be fined not less than one hundred dollars nor more
than two hundred fifty dollars for each violation.  Each day on which a
violation occurs shall be considered a separate offense.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 918, §1, eff. Aug. 2, 1981.
