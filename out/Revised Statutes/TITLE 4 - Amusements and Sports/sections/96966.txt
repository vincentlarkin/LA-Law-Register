§84.  Administrative procedure
The commission's hearings, practice and procedure and rule and
regulation making procedure are as provided in the Administrative Procedure
Act, R.S. 49:950 et seq., except as otherwise provided in this Chapter.
Acts 1974, No. 553, §1.
