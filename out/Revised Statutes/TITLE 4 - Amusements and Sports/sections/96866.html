§276.  Louisiana compact committee official

The governor shall appoint one official to represent Louisiana on the compact committee for a term of four years.  No official may serve more than three consecutive terms.  A vacancy shall be filled by the governor for the unexpired term.

Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 149, §1.
