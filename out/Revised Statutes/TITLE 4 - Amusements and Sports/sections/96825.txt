PART I-A.  LOUISIANA CHAMPIONS DAY
§201.  Purpose
The purpose of this Part is to focus national attention on and enhance the horse breeding industry in the state by establishing a special day of racing to display Louisiana bred champions.
Acts 1990, No. 884, §1.
