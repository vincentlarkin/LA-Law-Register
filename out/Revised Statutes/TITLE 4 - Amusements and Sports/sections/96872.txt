§362.  Ordinance or resolution
Whenever a municipality or the department of police desires to avail itself of this privilege it shall pass an ordinance or resolution fixing the compensation to be paid to the policewomen or matrons who shall be assigned to the dance halls, cabarets, or places where public dances are held.
