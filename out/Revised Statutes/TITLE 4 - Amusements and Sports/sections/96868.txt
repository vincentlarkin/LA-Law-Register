§278.  Louisiana State Racing Commission powers preserved
Nothing in this Part shall be construed to diminish or limit the powers and responsibilities of the commission established by this Chapter or to invalidate any action of the commission previously taken, including but not limited to any regulation promulgated.
Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 149, §1.
