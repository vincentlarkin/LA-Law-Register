§1457.  Quorum; voting

A quorum necessary for the conduct of business by the board shall exist whenever a majority of the members is present.  Except as may be otherwise provided by this Chapter, the public meeting law, or any other law, board action shall require the affirmative vote of a majority of the members present and voting.

Acts 2005, No. 463, §1, eff. Jan.1, 2006.
