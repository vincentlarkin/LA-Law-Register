§1616.  Taxing power
The authority is hereby authorized to participate in any new or additional tax related to transit and/or transportation that may be imposed in the future by the state legislature, provided, however, that the proceeds from said statewide tax will be prorated to all parishes in the state on a population formula for use by said parishes for transit and/or transportation purposes.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 638, §1.
