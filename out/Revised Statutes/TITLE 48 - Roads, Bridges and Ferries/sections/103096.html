§256.2.  Reduce speed limit signs; removal

Any contractor who is under contract for the construction, maintenance, or improvements to highways, bridges, or other public works under the control of the Department of Transportation and Development shall remove signs which indicate a reduced speed limit in construction areas not later than thirty days after final acceptance of the project by the department.  In the event that the project work is performed by the Department of Transportation and Development personnel, then the reduced speed limit signs shall be removed not later than thirty days after the work activity on the project ceases.

Acts 1993, No. 383, §1; Acts 1999, No. 232, §1; Acts 2010, No. 45, §1.
