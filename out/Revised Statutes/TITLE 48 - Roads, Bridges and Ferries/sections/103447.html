§945.1.  Commuter tickets; Louisiana registration as a qualification

The state, or any political subdivision thereof, which operates a toll bridge may require that in order to qualify for a commuter discount the motor vehicle shall be registered and licensed in Louisiana.

Added by Acts 1993, No. 177, §1.
