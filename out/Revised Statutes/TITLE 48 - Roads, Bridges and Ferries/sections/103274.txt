CHAPTER 2.  PARISH AND LOCAL ROADS
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBPART A.  RESTRICTION
§471.  Application of Chapter
This Chapter does not apply in any manner to roads, streets, or alleys which are part of the state highway system.
