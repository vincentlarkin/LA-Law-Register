§381.4.  Rights-of-way; wireless telecommunications tower managers
The assistant secretary for project delivery or his duly authorized representative may
enter into agreements with a wireless telecommunications tower manager to manage such
towers for specified periods as follows:
(1)  One or more telecommunication towers owned and operated solely by the
department may be managed for a period less than set forth in Civil Code Article 3473.
(2)  Telecommunication towers in either of the following categories may be managed
for a period less than that set forth in Civil Code Article 3486:
(a)  One or more telecommunication towers owned or operated by the department
which have been strengthened by the wireless telecommunications tower manager.
(b) One or more telecommunication towers erected by the wireless
telecommunications tower manager on public or highway rights-of -way.
Acts 1999, No. 1382, §1, eff. July 13, 1999; Acts 2025, No. 380, §1.
