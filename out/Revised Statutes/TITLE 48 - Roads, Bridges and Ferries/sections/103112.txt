SUBPART E.  MISCELLANEOUS PROCEDURES
§263.  Department owns repair shops; standby equipment
The department shall establish and maintain its own shops for the servicing, repair, and upkeep of its equipment and shall provide, at these shops and elsewhere, adequate standby units for emergency use and replacement of units under repair.
Acts 1997, No. 1112, §5.
