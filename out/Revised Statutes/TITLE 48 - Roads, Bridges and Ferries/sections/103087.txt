§255.4.  Cost-plus contracts prohibited; exceptions
Except for a duly certified emergency, no contract of the department shall be let on a cost-plus basis. Plan changes which cannot be negotiated and for which no item was provided in the original contract may be ordered on a cost-plus basis, if such basis was specified in the original contract as advertised.
Acts 1997, No. 1112, §1, eff. July 14, 1997.
