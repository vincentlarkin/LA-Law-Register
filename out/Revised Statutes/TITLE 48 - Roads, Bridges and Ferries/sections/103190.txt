§389.1.  Parish public roadways; designation as public crossings
Each parish governing authority is authorized to designate parish public roadways, as defined in R.S. 48:753(F), intersecting a railroad right of way as public crossings.  Upon a formal designation as a public crossing, the parish public roadways and public crossings may be eligible for the same safety and crossings improvements as are other public crossings.
Acts 1993, No. 479, §1.
