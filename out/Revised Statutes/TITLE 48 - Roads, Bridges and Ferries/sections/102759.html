§1163.  Transfer of records, money and equipment

All books, papers, records, money, choses in action and other property heretofore possessed, controlled or used by any authority in the exercise of functions herein transferred are hereby transferred to the board of highways.  

Added by Acts 1972, No. 726, §1.
