§953.  Organized runs, walks, marches, or parades on bridges prohibited on weekdays; exceptions

Organized runs, walks, marches, or parades shall be prohibited from crossing the Greater New Orleans Bridges Nos. one and two and the Huey P. Long Bridge in Orleans and Jefferson Parishes on weekdays from midnight Sunday evening until midnight on Friday evening except on any holiday recognized by federal, state, or local government.  

Acts 1985, No. 176, §1.
