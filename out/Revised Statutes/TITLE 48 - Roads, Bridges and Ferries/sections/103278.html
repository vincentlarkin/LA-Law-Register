§484.  Employment of engineers

The governing authority of any parish may employ an engineer to design blacktop, concrete or other hard surfaced roads, highways, bridges and tunnels in the parish to be constructed with funds from the royalty road fund whenever the department of highways is unable to assign engineers for the purpose; provided that the design must be approved by the state department of highways.  

Acts 1956, No. 418, §1.
