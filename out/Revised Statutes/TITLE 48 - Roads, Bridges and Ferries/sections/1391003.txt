§1303.2.  Terrebonne Parish road lighting districts; tax levy
Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 48:1303, the governing authority of any road
lighting district within the parish of Terrebonne may levy taxes not to exceed in any year ten
mills on the dollar on all of the taxable property within the boundaries of the district.  All
other provisions and limitations of R.S. 48:1303 shall apply to any tax so levied.
