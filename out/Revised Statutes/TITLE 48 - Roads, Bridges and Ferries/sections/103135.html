§280.  Directional signs in advance of Louisiana byways

The department shall erect signs indicating the approaches to designated Louisiana byways.  The points of location of such signs shall be  determined by the traffic engineer for the highway district in which the Louisiana byway is located.  The department shall erect a directional sign on Interstate 20 indicating the approaches to the United Theological Seminary.  The signs shall conform to a standard size and design prepared by the department.

Acts 1999, No. 938, §1.
