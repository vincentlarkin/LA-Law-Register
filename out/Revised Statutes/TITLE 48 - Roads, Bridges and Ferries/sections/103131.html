§277.  Signs in advance of driveways for churches

The department may erect signs indicating the location of churches located along state highways.  The points for location of such signs shall be determined by the traffic engineer of the highway district in which the church is located.  The signs shall conform to a standard size and design prepared by the department.

Acts 1997, No. 1230, §1.
