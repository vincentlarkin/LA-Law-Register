§425.  Purchasing, rules and regulations; intentional violation; penalty
A.  No person shall intentionally violate the Louisiana Procurement Code or any rule or regulation promulgated by the secretary of the Department of Transportation and Development with respect to purchasing.
B.  Any person who intentionally violates such law, rule, or regulation shall be fined not more than five hundred dollars, or imprisoned for not more than six months, or both.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 252, §2.
