CHAPTER 31.  LAFAYETTE METROPOLITAN

EXPRESSWAY COMMISSION

§2091.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be referred to as the "Lafayette Metropolitan Expressway Commission Act".

Acts 2003, No. 893, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
