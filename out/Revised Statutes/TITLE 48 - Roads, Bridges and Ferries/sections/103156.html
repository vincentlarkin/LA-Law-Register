§305.  Agreements with each other or with federal government

The highway authorities may enter into agreements with each other, or with the federal government, respecting the financing, planning, establishment, improvement, maintenance, use, regulation, or vacation of controlled-access facilities or other public ways in their respective jurisdictions, to facilitate the purposes of this Part.  

Added by Acts 1955, No. 129, §1.
