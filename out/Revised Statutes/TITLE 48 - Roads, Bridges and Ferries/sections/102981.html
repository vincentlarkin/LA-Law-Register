§2026.  Conflict of interest; ethics code

Any authority created pursuant to this Chapter, and all directors and officers thereof, shall be subject to the Code of Governmental Ethics (R.S. 42:1101 et seq.).

Acts 1997, No. 1017, §1, eff. July 11, 1997.
