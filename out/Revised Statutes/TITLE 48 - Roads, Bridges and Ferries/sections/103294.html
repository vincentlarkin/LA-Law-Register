PART II.  FORMATION AND CONSOLIDATION

OF ROAD DISTRICTS

§571.  Creation authorized; political subdivision

Parish governing authorities may create road districts and sub-road districts in the manner and subject to the limitations and restrictions contained in this Part.  

Road districts so created are sub-divisions of the state within the meaning of the laws of Louisiana relating to incurring debt and issuing bonds therefor.
