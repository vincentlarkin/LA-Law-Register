§2156.  Feasibility; advice
A.  The commission may construct projects under the terms and conditions set forth in this Chapter. The commission shall conduct an economic feasibility study prior to initiation of any project to substantiate project need and feasibility.
B.  The Rapides Area Planning Commission, and all subcommittees thereof, shall serve in an advisory capacity to the commission, relative to any project or projects contemplated by or to be undertaken by the commission.
Acts 2009, No. 518, §1, eff. July 10, 2009.
