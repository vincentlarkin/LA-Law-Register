§2084.1.  Public-private partnership projects; service contract

In addition to any authority otherwise conferred by law, the authority may contract with a private entity for transportation services to be provided for a qualifying transportation facility in exchange for such service payment and other consideration as the authority may deem appropriate.

Acts 2006, No. 304, §1.
