PART VII.  PARISH TRANSPORTATION FUND

§751.  Parish transportation

There is hereby established in the state treasury a special fund to be designated the "Parish Transportation Fund" to consist of the funds and to be used for the purposes hereinafter provided in this Part.

Acts 1990, No. 221, §1.
