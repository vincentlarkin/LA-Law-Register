PART IV.  FREE PASSAGE
§971.  Boy scouts, girl scouts, and camp fire girls to have free passage over toll bridges
Members of the Boy Scouts of America, of the Girl Scouts of America, and of the Camp Fire Girls when assembled in uniform in a parade or group consisting of not less than fifteen and under the supervision of a scout master or other responsible person, have free and unhampered passage at all times over toll bridges in this state.
