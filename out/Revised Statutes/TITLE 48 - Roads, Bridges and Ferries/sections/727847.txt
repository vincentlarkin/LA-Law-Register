CHAPTER 35.  THE LOUISIANA INTRASTATE RAIL COMPACT
§2170.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be referred to as "The Louisiana Intrastate Rail Compact Act".
Acts 2010, No. 838, §2, eff. June 30, 2010; Acts 2010, No. 858, §1, eff. June 30, 2010.
