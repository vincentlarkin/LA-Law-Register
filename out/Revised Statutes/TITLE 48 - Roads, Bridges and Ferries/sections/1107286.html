§94. District office transparency; publication of district work

            A. Each department district office shall publish weekly on the department's internet website information by parish regarding the construction and maintenance work performed, including but not limited to a description and location of the construction project or maintenance work performed.

            B. The department shall make available to the public an interactive online system to conveniently and accurately monitor the status of the department's projects statewide.

            Acts 2018, No. 662, §1; Acts 2025, No. 380, §1.
