§718.  Exchange
If the governing authority decides to dispose of the property by exchange, it may be exchanged for other property of approximately equal value, provided that the governing authority may pay or receive any difference in value in cash.
Acts 1968, No. 432, §6.  Amended by Acts 1970, No. 648, §1; Acts 1972, No. 417, §1.
