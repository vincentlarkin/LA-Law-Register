§461.13.  Unlawful junkyards; injunction

Any junkyard which violates the provisions of this Part is hereby declared to be illegal and the department may apply to the court in the parish in which such junkyard is located for an injunction to require compliance with the provisions of this Part.  

Added by Acts 1966, No. 474, §1.
