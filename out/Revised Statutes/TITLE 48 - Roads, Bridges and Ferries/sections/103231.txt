§451.5.  Qualification and exemption of jurors
The qualifications and exemptions of jurors and the method of choosing and summoning the general venire in jury cases are provided by special laws.
Acts 1974, Ex.Sess., No. 30, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
