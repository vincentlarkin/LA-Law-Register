§227.  Lands beneath elevated expressways; lease of
The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby authorized to lease after public bid for commercial and other purposes not repugnant to the state, lands beneath elevated expressways owned and maintained by the department.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 432, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 291, §1.
