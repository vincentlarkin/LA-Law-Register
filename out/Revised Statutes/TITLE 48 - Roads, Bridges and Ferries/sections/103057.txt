§23.  Engineering and other services
In order to properly discharge its functions, the department may employ engineering,
drafting, accounting, legal, and other help and labor, to the fullest extent permitted by
applicable law.
Acts 1991, No. 540, §1; Acts 2014, No. 811, §26, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts 2025, No.
380, §1.
