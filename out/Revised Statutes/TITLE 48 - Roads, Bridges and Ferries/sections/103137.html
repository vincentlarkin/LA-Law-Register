§286. Retention of consultants

            There are no conditions which shall limit or prohibit the retention of the services of consultants by the department.

            Acts 1991, No. 784, §1; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 35, §1; Acts 2009, No. 255, §1, eff. July 1, 2009; Acts 2025, No. 380, §1.
