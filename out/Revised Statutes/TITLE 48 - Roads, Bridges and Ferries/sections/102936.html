§1806.  Advice and services of the Department of Transportation and Development; interstate cooperation

The department is authorized to work jointly or in cooperation with the state of Mississippi, and to provide such advice and services to the commission as will, in the opinion of the secretary of the department, tend to achieve the objectives of this Chapter.  

Acts 1992, No. 288, §1; Acts 1993, No. 230, §1.
