§102.  Functions of commission
The commission shall be an affiliate of the National Commission and shall cooperate with and assist it in promoting interest in and the development of the Great River Road as designated by the federal authorities and shall serve in an advisory capacity to the department.  The chairman of the commission shall be the Louisiana representative on the National Commission.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 760, §1.
