CHAPTER 9.  TOLEDO BEND FOREST SCENIC

DRIVE COMMISSION

§1401.  Commission created

There is hereby created a scenic drive commission to be known as the Toledo Bend Forest Scenic Drive Commission.  

Acts 1968, No. 232, §1.  Amended by Acts 1971, No. 157, §1.
