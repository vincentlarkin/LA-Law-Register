§1458.  Conduct of meetings and affairs; bylaws

The board is authorized to create, amend, and repeal bylaws, rules and regulations, and resolutions for the government of the meetings and affairs of the system, not inconsistent with this Chapter or other law.

Acts 2005, No. 463, §1, eff. Jan.1, 2006.
