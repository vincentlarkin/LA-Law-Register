§272.  Access roads to rural cemeteries or graveyards
The department is authorized to grade and keep in repair access roads leading from roads or highways under regular maintenance of the department to cemeteries or graveyards situated within one mile or less of such regularly maintained roads or highways.
Acts 1950, No. 440, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 291, §1; Acts 2006, No. 11, §5.
