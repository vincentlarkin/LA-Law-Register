CHAPTER 34.  CENTRAL LOUISIANA REGIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE
BELTWAY COMMISSION
§2151.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be referred to as the "Central Louisiana Regional Infrastructure Beltway Commission".
Acts 2009, No. 518, §1, eff. July 10, 2009.
