§1844.  T.J. Hawkins, Craig E. Lear, Cooper Road USA Expressway
Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, that portion of Interstate 49 from its
intersection with Interstate 220 to its intersection with Louisiana Highway 1 in the city of
Shreveport, Louisiana, shall be known and designated as the "T.J. Hawkins, Craig E. Lear,
Cooper Road USA Expressway".
Acts 2017, No. 157, §1.
