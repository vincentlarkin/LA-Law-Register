§892.  Expenses of enforcing Sub-part; how paid
All expenses incurred in carrying out the provisions of this Sub-part shall be paid solely from funds provided under the authority of this Sub-part, and no liability or obligation shall be incurred hereunder beyond the extent to which money has been provided under the authority of this Sub-part.
