§1842.  West Florida Republic Parkway
Interstate 12 shall be known and designated as the West Florida Republic Parkway.  Commemorative signs along the roadway shall include a circular emblem including the words "West Florida Republic Parkway" and the Bonnie Blue Flag of the Republic of West Florida.
Acts 1993, No. 513, §1.
