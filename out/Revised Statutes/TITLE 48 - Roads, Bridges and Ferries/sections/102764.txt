§12.  Domicile
The domicile of the department is in the city of Baton Rouge, parish of East Baton Rouge.  Service of citation and other process directed to the department shall be made by handing the citation or other process to the secretary or to the undersecretary.
Amended by Acts 1977, No. 291, §1.
