§1662.  Conflict of interests

All members of the board and every employee of the board or of the authority shall be subject to the provisions of state law governing governmental ethics in all matters relating to any interest or business, direct or indirect, of the authority.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 439, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 1979.
