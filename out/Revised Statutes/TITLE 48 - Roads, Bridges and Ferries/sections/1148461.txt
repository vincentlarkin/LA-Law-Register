§2202.  Records
A company shall maintain the following records:
(1)  Individual trip records for at least three years from the date each trip was
provided.
(2)  Individual records of drivers for at least three years after the date which a driver's
relationship with the company has ended.
Acts 2019, No. 286, §2, eff. July 1, 2019.
