§142.  Financial services administrator has general charge of accounting, disbursing and cost accounting of the department

The financial services administrator has general charge of the accounting, disbursing and cost accounting of the department.  

Amended by Acts 1970, No. 414, §1; Acts 1972, No. 428, §1; Acts 1979, No. 413, §5, eff. July 11, 1979.
