§95.1.4.  Illegal transfer of a firearm to a prohibited possessor
A. Illegal transfer of a firearm to a prohibited possessor is the intentional giving,
selling, donating, lending, delivering, or otherwise transferring a firearm to any person
known to the offender to be a person prohibited from possessing a firearm under state or
federal law.
B.  Whoever commits the crime of illegal transfer of a firearm to a prohibited
possessor shall be fined not more than two thousand five hundred dollars, imprisoned with
or without hard labor for not more than one year, or both.
Acts 2018, No. 367, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2018; Acts 2019, No. 427, §1.
