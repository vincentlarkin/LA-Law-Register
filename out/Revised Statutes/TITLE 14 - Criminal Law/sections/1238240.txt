§68.4.2.  Aggravated staging of a motor vehicle collision
A.  Aggravated staging of a motor vehicle collision is the staging of a motor vehicle
collision, as defined in R.S. 14:68.4.1, which causes death or serious bodily injury to another
person.
B.  Whoever commits the crime of aggravated staging of a motor vehicle collision
shall be imprisoned, with or without hard labor, for not less than five years nor more than
thirty years, fined not more than fifteen thousand dollars, or both.
Acts 2021, No. 248, §1.
