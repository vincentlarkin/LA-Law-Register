§329.8.  Applicability of other acts
The provisions of R.S. 14:329.1 to 329.8 shall be supplemental and in addition to any other applicable laws of this state, including but not limited to the Louisiana Disaster Act of 1974, R.S. 29:701 through 716.
Amended by Acts 1990, No. 152, §1, eff. July 1, 1990.
