§365.  Election ballots
No organization finally determined under the provisions of R.S. 14:358
through 373 to be a communist organization or a subversive organization shall
have its name or the name of any of its nominees printed on any election ballot
used in any primary or general election in the state of Louisiana.
Amended by Acts 1965, No. 45, §1.
