§223.2.  Exceptions

R.S. 14:223 through 223.8 shall not apply to any person engaged in radio or television broadcasting who transfers or causes to be transferred any such sounds intended for or in connection with broadcast transmissions or related uses or for archival purposes.  

Acts 1972, No. 350, §3; Acts 1990, No. 122, §1.
