§335.  Checks; requiring certain information to be recorded on; prohibited; penalties

A.  No retail or wholesale merchant shall demand the race of any customer for the purposes of recording such on the customer's personal check which has been tendered for the payment of goods or services or for cashing.  

B.  No retail or wholesale merchant shall cause the race of any customer to be recorded on a personal check tendered by such customer for the payment of goods or services or for cashing.  

C.  Any person convicted of a violation of this Section may be assessed civil damages in an amount not to exceed two hundred fifty dollars.

Acts 1993, No. 399, §1.
