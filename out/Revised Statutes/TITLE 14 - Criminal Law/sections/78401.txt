§312.  Jumping from state bridge for publicity prohibited; penalty
A.  No person shall dive or jump off of any public bridge, constructed or owned by the state or any of its political subdivisions, where the object and purpose of the act is to gain publicity.
B.  Whoever violates this Section shall be fined not more than twenty-five dollars, or imprisoned for not more than thirty days, or both.
Acts 2014, No. 791, §7.
