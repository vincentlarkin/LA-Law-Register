§95.1.1.  Illegally supplying a felon with a firearm
A.  Illegally supplying a felon with a firearm is the intentional giving, selling,
donating, providing, lending, delivering, or otherwise transferring a firearm to any person
known by the offender to be a person convicted of a felony and prohibited from possessing
a firearm as provided for in R.S. 14:95.1.
B.  Whoever commits the crime of illegally supplying a felon with a firearm shall be
imprisoned with or without hard labor for not more than five years and may be fined not less
than one thousand dollars nor more than five thousand dollars.  At least one year of the
sentence imposed shall be served without benefit of parole, probation, or suspension of
sentence.
Acts 2004, No. 385, §1; Acts 2018, No. 124, §1.
