TITLE 35
NOTARIES PUBLIC AND COMMISSIONERS
CHAPTER 1.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§1.  Appointment of notaries public
The governor may appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, and upon their meeting the qualifications for office provided for in this Title, notaries public in the different parishes.
Amended by Acts 1964, No. 359, §1; Acts 1964, No. 359, §1; Acts 1966, No. 539, §1; Acts 1976, No. 677, §1; Acts 1977, No. 451, §1; Acts 2008, No. 856, §1.
