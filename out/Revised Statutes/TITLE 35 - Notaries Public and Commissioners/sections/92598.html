§132.  Notaries in military service, leave of absence

A leave of absence may be granted by the secretary of state on behalf of the governor to any notary public upon his application to the secretary of state in writing certifying that he is a member of the Army, Navy, Marine Corps, or any other branch of the military service of the United States, or of the state of Louisiana, and stating the expiration date of his bond.

Acts 2006, No. 423, §1, eff. June 15, 2006.
