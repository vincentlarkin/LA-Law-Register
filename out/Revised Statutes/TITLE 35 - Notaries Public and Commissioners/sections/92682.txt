§76.  Release of surety company upon acceptance of personal surety bond
The Secretary of State for the state of Louisiana, upon filing and recordation of a notarial bond with the Secretary of State, with personal surety in lieu of a surety company, shall upon request execute a release of the surety company effective as of the date of the acceptance of the personal surety bond in lieu thereof.
Acts 1952, No. 497, §2.
