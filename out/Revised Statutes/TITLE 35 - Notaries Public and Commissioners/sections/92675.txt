§6.  Foreign notaries; acts and other instruments; effect
All acts passed before any notary public and two witnesses in the District of
Columbia, or any state of the United States other than Louisiana, except those performed by
remote online notarization, shall be authentic acts and shall have the same force and effect
as if passed before a notary public in Louisiana.
Acts 2020, No. 254, §3.
