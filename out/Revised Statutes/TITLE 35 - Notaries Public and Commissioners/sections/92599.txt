§133.  Notaries in military service, period of leave
The period of the leaves of absence granted in accordance with R.S. 35:132 shall date from the day the leave is granted and shall terminate sixty days after the date of discharge of the notary from the military service of the United States or the state of Louisiana.
Acts 2006, No. 423, §1, eff. June 15, 2006.
