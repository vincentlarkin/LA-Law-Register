§192.  Execution and recordation of bond; filing of certificate of competency
A.  The bond required of notaries by R.S. 35:71 shall be filed in the office of the
secretary of state.  The bond shall be subscribed in favor of the governor and if a special
mortgage or secured by personal surety, recorded  in the mortgage office of the parish.
B.  The provisions of Subsection A of this Section shall not affect the validity of
bonds given or recorded in the mortgage or conveyance office of any parish prior to
September 9, 1977.
C.  Notwithstanding any provisions of law to the contrary, in all cases where a notary
furnishes a bond signed by a surety company authorized to do business in this state, it shall
not be necessary to record the bond with the clerk of court or have the bond approved by the
clerk of court.
Amended by Acts 1977, No. 451, §1; Acts 2012, No. 279, §1; Acts 2025, No. 258,
§1, eff. Feb. 1, 2026.
