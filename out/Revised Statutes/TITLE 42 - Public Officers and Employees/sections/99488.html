§55.  Definitions

As used in this Part: (1) "Public employee" shall mean and include every officer or employee of any department, board, commission, or agency of the State of Louisiana, or of any political subdivision or municipal corporation of the State of Louisiana.

(2)  "Student in a public educational institution" shall mean and include every student enrolled in any public school of the State, or in any state-supported trade school, vocational school, college, or university.

Acts 1950, No. 284, §4.
