§1193.  Effect of expungement
A.  The Board of Ethics and the Ethics Adjudicatory Board shall remove from public
access all ethics enforcement records that are ordered to be expunged. The records shall not
be destroyed.
B.  An expunged ethics enforcement record shall be privileged, confidential, no
longer be considered a public record, and not be made available to any person or other entity,
except that any such record shall be available to each member of the Board of Ethics and the
Ethics Adjudicatory Board and to their respective staff members.
Acts 2015, No. 316, §1.
