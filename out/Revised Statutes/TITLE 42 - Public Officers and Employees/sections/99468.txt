§450.5.  Employing agency discretion
Nothing contained herein shall be construed to require any employing agency to grant a public employee's request for voluntary disaster service leave if the appointing authority determines that the grant of leave would pose a hardship on the employing agency.
Acts 1995, No. 455, §1.
