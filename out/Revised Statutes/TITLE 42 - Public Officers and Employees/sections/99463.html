§447.  Leave remaining after termination of personal emergency

Transferred annual, sick, or compensatory leave remaining to the credit of a leave recipient when the personal emergency terminates shall be restored to the pool account.

Acts 1992, No. 1008, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993; Acts 2012, No. 590, §1, eff. June 7, 2012; Acts 2013, No. 220, §18, eff. June 11, 2013.
