§444. Pool account leave request

            Employees of a state agency requesting leave from the pool leave account under the provisions of this Part shall submit a written leave request to the employing agency with the following information included:

            (1) The name, employing agency, position, title, and classification of the employee.

            (2) A brief description of the nature, severity, and anticipated duration of the personal emergency.

            Acts 1992, No. 1008, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993; Acts 2022, No. 649, §1.
