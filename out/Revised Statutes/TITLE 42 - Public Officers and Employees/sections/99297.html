§1264.  Review of needs and requirements

The Department of State Civil Service shall conduct, at least every four years, a complete review of the needs and requirements for in-service training and education and shall adjust the training programs provided by this Chapter to meet the needs revealed by said reviews.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 400, §1.  Amended by Acts 1979, No. 656, §1; Acts 1986, No. 313, §1, eff. June 30, 1986; Acts 2010, No. 825, §1, eff. July 1, 2010.
