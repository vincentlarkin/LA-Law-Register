§395.  Governor may grant leave to appointive officials in armed forces; salary to cease
The governor may grant, upon request, a leave of absence for a period not to exceed the term of the respective appointment to any appointive state official who is serving in the armed forces of the United States.
The salary of any official obtaining a leave of absence under the provisions of this Section shall cease during the time the leave of absence is in effect.
