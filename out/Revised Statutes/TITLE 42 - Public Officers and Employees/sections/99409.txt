§303.  Power of governor to remove for cause is not modified
The provisions of R.S. 42:301 and 42:302 shall not be construed as modifying, limiting, or restraining the power of the governor, under existing laws to remove for cause any tax collector appointed by him, or as in any way affecting the penalties provided for failure to make settlement within the time prescribed by law.
