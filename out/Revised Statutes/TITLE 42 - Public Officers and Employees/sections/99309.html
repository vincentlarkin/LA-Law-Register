§1309.  District attorney deferred compensation program

A.  The district attorneys and assistant district attorneys are hereby authorized to establish a deferred compensation program.  The executive director of the Louisiana District Attorneys Association shall be the trustee of said program and the funds needed for the administration of the program shall be limited to one percent of the total amount in the fund including additional salary or compensation for the trustee which shall be set by the Board of Directors of the Louisiana District Attorneys Association.

B.  Both state and local funds paid to district attorneys or assistant district attorneys may be deferred for the purposes of this program.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 1, §1, eff. May 6, 1982.
