§1305.  Audits

The legislative auditor is authorized to audit annually the books and records of the plan administrator, and is authorized and directed to audit annually the plan.

Added by Acts 1981, No. 550, §1.  Amended by Acts 1982, No. 1, §1, eff. May 6, 1982.
