§1008.  Rules and regulations
The Department of the Treasury shall make, adopt, and promulgate such rules and regulations, not inconsistent with the provisions of this Chapter, as it finds necessary or appropriate to the efficient administration of the functions with which it is charged under this Chapter.
Acts 1952, No. 204, §8; Acts 2004, No. 280, §1, eff. July 1, 2004.
