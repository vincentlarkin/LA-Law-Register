CHAPTER 8.  LEAVE OF ABSENCE, REINSTATEMENT
AND ACCRUED LEAVE
PART I.  GRANTING OF LEAVE
§391.  Leave of absence for state and parish officers
The governor may grant leave of absence to state and parish officers, not to exceed ninety days at any one time, at his discretion.
