CHAPTER 1-A.  OPEN MEETINGS LAW
§11.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Open Meetings Law".
Acts 2010, No. 861, §18.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 42:11 was redesignated as R.S. 42:26 by Acts 2010, No. 861, §23.
