§446. Agency considerations

            Unless the qualifying circumstance is the birth or adoption of a child or directly involves a medical condition affecting the leave recipient, the employing agency may consider the likely impact on morale and efficiency within the agency in considering a leave recipient's request to use leave transferred from the pool account.

            Acts 1992, No. 1008, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993; Acts 2022, No. 649, §1.
