§1473.  Penalties
An agency head who fails to properly implement a policy developed and approved
pursuant to this Chapter shall be subject to a civil penalty not to exceed five hundred dollars
per violation.  The agency head shall be personally liable for the payment of the penalty.  The
civil penalty may be recovered by the state in a civil action instituted by the attorney general.
Acts 2023, No. 308, §1, eff. June 13, 2023.
