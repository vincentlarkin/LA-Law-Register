§1160.  Electronic response required
The board shall send a communication electronically in a timely manner to a filing
party who files a report through the Computerized Data Management System in the manner
provided by this Subpart.  This communication shall not take the place of any other type of
notice required by law.
Acts 2023, No. 241, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2024.
