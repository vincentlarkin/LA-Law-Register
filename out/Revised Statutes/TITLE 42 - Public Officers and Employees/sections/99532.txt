§806.  Claims
The office shall be responsible for the processing of all claims under policies for group benefits for state employees contracted for under the provisions of this Chapter.  The office shall be responsible for the disbursement of all payments of such claims to program participants.
Acts 2001, No. 1178, §5, eff. June 29, 2001.
