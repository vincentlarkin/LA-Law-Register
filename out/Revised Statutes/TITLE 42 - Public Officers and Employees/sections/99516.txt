§729.  Statement of policy
The State of Louisiana recognizes that intergovernmental cooperation is an essential factor in resolving problems affecting the state and that the interchange of personnel between and among the governmental agencies at the same or different levels of government is a significant factor in achieving such cooperation.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 375, §1.
