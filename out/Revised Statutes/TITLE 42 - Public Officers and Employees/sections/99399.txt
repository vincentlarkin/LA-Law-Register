§286.  Time of making report
The annual reports required by R.S. 42:283 shall be made within thirty days after the close of the fiscal year employed by the respective offices, boards or commissions, and shall be filed and kept by the secretaries of the respective police juries and respective clerks of court, as public records, for one year.
