§1352.  Call of election

The director of the Department of State Civil Service shall issue the call for an election for the classified member of the State Civil Service Commission in January, 1975, and every six years thereafter, on the first Monday after January first of the election year.

Added by Acts 1974, Ex.Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.  Acts 1988, No. 164, §1.
