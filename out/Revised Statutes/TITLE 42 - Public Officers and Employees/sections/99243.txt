§1143.  Procedure and rules of evidence
Except as otherwise provided in this Chapter, all proceedings conducted by  the board or panel shall be subject to and in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 1979, No. 443, §1, eff. April 1, 1980; Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 64, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
