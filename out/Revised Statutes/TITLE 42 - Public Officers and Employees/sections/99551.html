§858.  Contracts to be subject to other laws concerning group insurance

All group insurance contracts effected pursuant hereto shall conform and be subject to all the provisions of any existing or future laws concerning group insurance.

Acts 2001, No. 1178, §5, eff. June 29, 2001.
