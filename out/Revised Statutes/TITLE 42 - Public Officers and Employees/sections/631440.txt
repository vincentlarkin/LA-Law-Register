§1130.4.  False statements by candidate or political committee
No candidate in an election or any political committee shall, with the intent to
mislead the voters, distribute or cause to be distributed any oral, visual, digital, or written
material containing any statement which he knows or should be reasonably expected to know
makes a false statement about another candidate in the election.  "Political committee" shall
have the same meaning as provided in R.S. 18:1483.
Acts 2008, No. 812, §1, eff. July 7, 2008; Acts 2022, No. 110, §1; Acts 2024, No.
589, §1.
