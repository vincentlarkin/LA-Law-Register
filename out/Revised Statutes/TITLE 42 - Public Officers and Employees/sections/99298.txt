§1265.  Cooperative arrangements with educational institutions
In addition to the in-service training and educational programs provided for in this
Chapter, all state agencies may engage in cooperative arrangements or agreements with the
Department of State Civil Service and with state educational institutions for specialized
training, work-study programs, internships, and research projects designed to be useful in
making long-range training and educational plans.  State educational institutions are hereby
directed to give special consideration to such projects and programs.
