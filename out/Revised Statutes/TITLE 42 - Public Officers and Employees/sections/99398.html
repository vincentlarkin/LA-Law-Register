§285.  Names of deputies, employees, etc., and compensation to be reported

The first report of each year, made under the provisions of R.S. 42:283 and 42:284 shall include the names of all deputies, employees and officers of the respective offices, boards and commissions, for the preceding year, whether they were employed full time or only temporarily, and shall also show the total salary or compensation paid to each of them during that year.
