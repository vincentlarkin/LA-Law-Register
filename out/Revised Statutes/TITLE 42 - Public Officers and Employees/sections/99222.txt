§1116.1.  Repealed by Acts 2003, No. 1292, §1.
NOTE:  Statute requiring elected officials to submit to random drug testing violated search and seizure clause and therefore is unconstitutional; no special need which would justify departure from Fourth Amendment's requirement of individualized suspicion for search.  O'Neill v. La., E.D. La. 1998, 61 F.Supp.2d 485, affirmed 197 F.3d 1169, cert. denied 120 S.Ct. 2740, 530 U.S. 1274, 147 L.Ed.2d 1005.
