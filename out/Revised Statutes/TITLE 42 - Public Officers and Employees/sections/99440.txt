§396.  Temporary appointments
The governor may in each instance where a leave of absence is granted under the terms of R.S. 42:395 name and designate a person having the necessary legal qualifications to serve during the temporary absence.  The person so serving shall be vested with all of the rights, duties and emoluments of the office during the temporary appointment.
