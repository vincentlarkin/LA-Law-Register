CHAPTER 19.  STATE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION

PART I.  EMPLOYEE MEMBER

§1351.  Qualifications; term of office

The classified employee member of the State Civil Service Commission provided for in Article X, Section 3(C) of the Constitution of 1974 shall be a full time, permanent employee in the classified state service for a period of one year prior to the date on which he qualifies as a candidate.  He shall serve a term of six years, which shall commence on May 1 of the year in which he is elected.

Added by Acts 1974, Ex. Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
