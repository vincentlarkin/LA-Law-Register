§398.  Leave not accrued; unclassified health care professional employee pool

Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, no employee who fills a position in an unclassified health care professional employee pool established by a Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center medical center pursuant to R.S. 17:1519.16 shall earn or accrue any type of leave.  The appointing authority for such position shall notify each person being employed in such a pool position of his ineligibility to earn or accrue leave.  Each person employed in a pool position shall sign an affidavit acknowledging his ineligibility to earn or accrue leave and stating that he has full knowledge that he is never to earn or accrue any leave for time worked in a position in a pool.

Acts 2005, No. 223, §3, eff. July 1, 2005.
