CHAPTER 3-A.  SUSPENSION FROM BOARD

§91. Purpose

The purpose of this Chapter is to prohibit a person who has his professional license or certificate suspended or revoked from serving as a member of a board or regulatory entity if membership on the board requires a valid professional license or certificate.

Acts 1995, No. 64, §1, eff. June 12, 1995.
