§809. Payroll deductions for payment of premiums, surcharges, and other voluntary
contributions
State boards, commissions, municipalities, and other public bodies may deduct from
the employee's pay, salary, or compensation, such parts of the premiums, surcharges, and
other voluntary contributions for life, health, or other benefit programs offered by the office
as are payable by the employee and as may be authorized in writing by the employee.
Acts 2001, No. 1178, §5, eff. June 29, 2001; Acts 2018, No. 676, §1.
