§56.  Dismissal from employment; expulsion from educational institution

Any public employee found guilty of committing any act prohibited by R.S. 42:54 hereof shall be dismissed from his public employment.  Any student in a public educational institution found guilty of committing any act prohibited by R.S. 42:54 hereof shall be expelled from the public educational institution in which he is enrolled.

Acts 1950, No. 284, §5.
