§1304.  Meetings

The Commission shall meet at least twice yearly, and all meetings of the Commission shall be held in compliance with the Louisiana Open Meetings Law.

Added by Acts 1981, No. 308, §2.  Amended by Acts 1982, No. 1, §1, eff. May 6, 1982.
