§1356.  Cost of election
All costs incurred in connection with the election provided for by this Chapter shall be paid out of funds available to the Department of State Civil Service for its operations.
Added by Acts 1974, Ex.Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.  Acts 1988, No. 164, §1.
