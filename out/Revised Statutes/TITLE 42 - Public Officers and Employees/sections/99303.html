§1303.1.  Section 401(a) plan

The commission shall be authorized to establish a Section 401(a) plan in accordance with the provisions of the Internal Revenue Code of 1954, as amended, under such rules and regulations as the commission shall adopt.

Acts 2001, No. 1068, §1, eff. June 28, 2001.
