§1306.  Deferred amounts as compensation
Any compensation deferred pursuant to the plan operated under this Chapter shall be deemed regular compensation for the purpose of computing contributions or benefits under existing retirement, pension, or social security systems applicable to participants.  This provision shall not supersede the provisions of R.S. 42:861.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 1, §1, eff. May 6, 1982.
