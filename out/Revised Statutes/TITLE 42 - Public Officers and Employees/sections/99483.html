PART II.  SUBVERSIVE ACTIVITIES BY PUBLIC

EMPLOYEES OR BY STUDENTS

§51.  Declaration of policy

The Legislature of Louisiana does hereby take cognizance of the fact that much of the world is in a state of political unrest; that foreign agents and others who seek the overthrow or destruction of the government of the United States are known to be working in our country, and that such a situation presents a clear and present danger; and it is hereby declared to be public policy that subversive activities by any public employee, or by any student in a public educational institution in this state, should not be permitted, and this act is enacted to prohibit such subversive activities.

Acts 1950, No. 284, §1.
