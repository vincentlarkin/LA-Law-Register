§603.  Assistant district attorneys
The offices of first, second, and third assistant district attorneys for the
Twenty-Seventh Judicial District of Louisiana are hereby created.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 445, §1.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 421,
§1.
