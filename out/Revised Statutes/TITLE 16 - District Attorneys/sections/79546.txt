§274.  Assistant district attorneys
The offices of first and second assistant district attorneys for the
Twelfth Judicial District are created.
Added by Acts 1967, No. 5, §1; Acts 1967, No. 38, §1.  Amended by
Acts 1973, No. 118, §5.
