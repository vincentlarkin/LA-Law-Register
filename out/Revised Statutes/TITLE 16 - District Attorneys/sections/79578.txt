§357.  Third and fourth assistant district attorneys
The offices of third and fourth assistant district attorneys are hereby
created in and for the Sixteenth Judicial District of Louisiana under the
provisions of Article VII, Section 60 of the Constitution of Louisiana.

Added by Acts 1966, No. 150, §2.
