§358.1.  Fifth assistant district attorney

The office of fifth assistant district attorney is hereby created in and for the Sixteenth Judicial District of Louisiana under the provisions of Article VII, Section 60 of the Constitution of Louisiana.1

Added by Acts 1970, No. 55, §1.

1See, now LSA-Const. Art. 5, §26.
