§925.  Budget to include additional salary
The police jury of DeSoto Parish shall annually, and at the same time as they prepare their general budgets of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary of the district attorney as set forth in this Part.
Acts 2007, No. 416, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
