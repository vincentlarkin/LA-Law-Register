§64.  Powers of district attorney not diminished
It is the purpose of this Chapter to create judicial enforcement districts and subdistricts for the additional financing of the district attorney's office for each such district.  Nothing contained herein shall be interpreted as diminishing any of the authority of the district attorney as delegated to him in the constitution and laws of this state.
Acts 1988, No. 688, §1, eff. July 15, 1988.
