§586.  Budget to include additional salary and expense allowance of district attorney

The police juries of each of the parishes comprising the Twenty-Sixth Judicial District shall annually and at the same time as they prepare their general budgets of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary and expenses of the district attorney as herein fixed.  

Added by Acts 1959, No. 113, §9.  Amended by Acts 1968, No. 366, §1.
