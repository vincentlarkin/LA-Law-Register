§108.  Second assistant district attorney
The office of second assistant district attorney is hereby created for the Second Judicial District of Louisiana.  The second assistant district attorney shall not be a resident of the parish in which the district attorney of said district resides, nor shall he be a resident of the parish in which the first assistant district attorney resides.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 152, §2.
