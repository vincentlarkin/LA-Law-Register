PART III-A.  THIRD JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§111.  Assistant district attorney
The office of assistant district attorney is hereby created in and for the Third Judicial District of the State of Louisiana composed of the parishes of Lincoln and Union.
Added by Acts 1950, No. 148, §1.
