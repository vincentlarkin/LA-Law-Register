PART XXXIV.  THIRTY-EIGHTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT

§891.  Additional salary of district attorney

The district attorney of the Thirty-Eighth Judicial District shall receive, in addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual salary, to be fixed by the governing authority of the parish of Cameron, of not less than three thousand six hundred dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of Cameron.  

Added by Acts 1978, No. 14, §4, eff. March 1, 1979.
