§85.  Budget to include additional salary
The police jury of the parish of Caddo shall annually, and at the same time as it prepares the general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the additional salary of the district attorney as herein fixed.
Added by Acts 1959, No. 113, §1.
