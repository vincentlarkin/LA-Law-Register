§358.2.  Additional salary of fifth assistant district attorney
The fifth assistant district attorney shall receive, in addition to the salary
paid by the state of Louisiana, an additional salary of five thousand five
hundred fifty dollars per year, payable monthly, said sum to be paid by the
parishes of Iberia, St. Mary, and St. Martin, as set forth in R.S. 16:359.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 55, §1.
