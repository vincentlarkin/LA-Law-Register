PART XXX.  THIRTY-THIRD JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§831.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the Thirty-Third Judicial District of the state
shall receive, in addition to the salary paid him by the state and when funds are
made available for the payment of said salary by the state, an annual salary of
three thousand six hundred dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the
parish of Allen.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 550, §1.
