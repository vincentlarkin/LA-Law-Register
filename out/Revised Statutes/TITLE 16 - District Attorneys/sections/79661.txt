§583.  Budget to include additional salary and expenses
The governing authority of each of the parishes composing the Twenty-Sixth Judicial District shall annually and at the time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salaries and expenses fixed in R.S. 16:582.
Amended by Acts 1968, No. 366, §1.
