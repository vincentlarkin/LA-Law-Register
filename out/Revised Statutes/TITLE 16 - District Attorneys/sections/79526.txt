§228.  Third assistant district attorney; budget to include additional salary
The police jury of Rapides Parish shall annually, and at the same time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary of the third assistant district attorney herein fixed.
Added by Acts 1967, No. 39, §1.
