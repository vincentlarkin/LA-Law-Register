§272.  Budget to include additional salary
The governing authority of the parish of Avoyelles, shall annually and at the time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary fixed in R.S. 16:271.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 231, §1.  Amended by Acts 1957, No. 13, §1.
