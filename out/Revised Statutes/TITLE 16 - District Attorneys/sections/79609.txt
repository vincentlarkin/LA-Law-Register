§434.  Additional salary of assistant district attorney
The assistant district attorney of the Twentieth Judicial District shall
receive as compensation for his services, in addition to the annual salary paid
by the state, such additional salary as the police juries of the parishes of East
Feliciana and West Feliciana may determine, but such additional compensation
shall not be in excess of one thousand dollars per annum from each parish.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 56, §1.
