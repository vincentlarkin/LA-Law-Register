§330. Compensation

           The fifth and sixth assistant district attorneys shall receive as compensation for their services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state, such additional salary as may be provided by the parishes comprising the Fifteenth Judicial District or each of them.

           Added by Acts 1971, No. 63, §1.
