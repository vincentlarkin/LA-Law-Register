§604.  Additional salary of assistant district attorneys
A. The first assistant district attorney shall receive as compensation for
his services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state of Louisiana, an
annual salary of not less than six thousand dollars, payable monthly on his own
warrant.
B. The second assistant district attorney shall receive as compensation
for his services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state of Louisiana,
an annual salary of not less than one thousand dollars, payable monthly on his
own warrant.
C. The third assistant district attorney shall receive as compensation for
his services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state of Louisiana, an
annual salary of not less than one thousand dollars, payable monthly on his
own warrant.
D. These salaries shall be paid by the parish of St. Landry.  The police
jury of St. Landry Parish shall annually budget an amount sufficient to pay the
salaries herein fixed.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 445, §§2, 3.  Amended by Acts 1962, No.
432, §1; Acts 1972, No. 421, §1.
