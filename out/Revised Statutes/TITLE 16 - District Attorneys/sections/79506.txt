§165.  Expense allowance
The district attorney of the Sixth Judicial District, in addition to any
other funds provided for the purpose, shall receive an expense allowance of
two thousand four hundred dollars for expenses incurred in the discharge of
his official duties, payable monthly upon his own warrant by the parishes of
East Carroll, Madison, and Tensas in the proportion of eight hundred dollars
by each parish.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 153, §1.
