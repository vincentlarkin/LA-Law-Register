PART XXV.  TWENTY-EIGHTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT

§621.  Additional salary of the district attorney

The district attorney of the Twenty-Eighth Judicial District of this state shall receive, in addition to the salary paid him by this state, in lieu of all other fees, an additional salary of four thousand two hundred dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of LaSalle.  

Amended by Acts 1958, No. 212, §1; Acts 1959, No. 113, §16; Acts 1977, No. 620, §26, eff. Jan. 3, 1979.
