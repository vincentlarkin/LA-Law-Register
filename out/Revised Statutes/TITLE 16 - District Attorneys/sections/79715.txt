§872.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the Thirty-Sixth Judicial District shall receive,
in addition to the salary paid him by the state, an annual salary to be fixed by
the governing authority of the parish of Beauregard, of not less than nine
thousand dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of
Beauregard.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 164, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1979.
