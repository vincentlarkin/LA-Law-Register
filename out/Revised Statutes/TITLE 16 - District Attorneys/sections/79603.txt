§423.  Assistant district attorneys
The offices of first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth, seventh, eighth,
ninth, and tenth assistant district attorneys for the Nineteenth Judicial District
are created.
Amended by Acts 1956, No. 468, §1; Acts 1961, 2nd Ex.Sess., No. 8,
§1; Acts 1966, No. 33, §1; Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 54, §1.
