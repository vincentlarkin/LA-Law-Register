PART VI.  SIXTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§161.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the Sixth Judicial District of this state shall
receive, in addition to the salary paid him by this state, in lieu of all other fees,
an annual salary of four thousand five hundred dollars, payable monthly on his
own warrant by the parishes comprising the Sixth Judicial District, apportioned
as follows:
(1)  By the parish of East Carroll, the sum of one thousand five hundred
dollars per annum.
(2)  By the parish of Madison, the sum of one thousand five hundred
dollars per annum.
(3)  By the parish of Tensas, the sum of one thousand five hundred
dollars per annum.
Amended by Acts 1959, No. 113, §11.
