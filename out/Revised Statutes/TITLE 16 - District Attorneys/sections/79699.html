PART II.  FIRST JUDICIAL DISTRICT

§81.  Assistant district attorneys

The offices of first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth, seventh, eighth and ninth assistant district attorneys for the First Judicial District of Louisiana are hereby created.  

Amended by Acts 1954, No. 105, §1; Acts 1967, No. 49, §1; Acts 1970, No. 42, §1; Acts 1973, No. 118, §2.
