§895.  Budget to include additional salary
The governing authority of the parish of Cameron shall annually, at the time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary fixed in R.S. 16:894.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 694, §2, eff. July 20, 1981.
