§752. First assistant district attorney; additional salary

           A. The office of first assistant district attorney for the Thirty-First Judicial District is created.

           B. The first assistant district attorney shall receive as compensation for his services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state, an annual salary of three thousand four hundred fifty dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant. This additional salary shall be paid by the parish of Jefferson Davis.

           Added by Acts 1952, No. 382, §§1 to 4. Amended by Acts 1968, No. 548, §1.
