PART XXXII.  THIRTY-SIXTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§871.  Additional salary of assistant district attorneys
Each assistant district attorney of the Thirty-Sixth Judicial District shall
receive as compensation for his services, in addition to the annual salary paid
by the state, an annual salary to be fixed by the governing authority of the
parish of Beauregard of not less than three thousand two hundred fifty dollars,
payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of Beauregard.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 164, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1979.
