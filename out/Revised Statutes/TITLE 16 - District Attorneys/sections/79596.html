§406. Second assistant district attorney

           The office of second assistant district attorney is hereby created for the Eighteenth Judicial District of Louisiana, under the provisions of Article 7, Section 60 of the Constitution of Louisiana.1

           Added by Acts 1964, No. 251, §1.

1See, now, LSA-Const. Art. 5, §26.
