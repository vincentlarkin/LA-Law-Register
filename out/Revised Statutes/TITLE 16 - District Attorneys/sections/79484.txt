PART IV.  FOURTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§121.  Assistant district attorneys
The offices of first, second, third, fourth, fifth, and sixth assistant district attorneys for the Fourth Judicial District are created.
Amended by Acts 1950, No. 76, §1; Acts 1956, No. 77, §1; Acts 1965, No. 147, §1; Acts 1970, No. 336, §1; Acts 1972, No. 264, §1.
