PART X-A.  TWELFTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§271.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the Twelfth Judicial District shall receive, in
addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual salary of three
thousand six hundred dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the
parish of Avoyelles.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 231, §1.  Amended by Acts 1957, No. 13, §1.
