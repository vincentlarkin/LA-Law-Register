§383.  Assistant district attorneys

The offices of first, second, third, and fourth assistant district attorneys for the Seventeenth Judicial District are hereby created.  

Amended by Acts 1961, 2nd Ex.Sess., No. 8, §2; Acts 1967, No. 63, §1; Acts 1968, No. 309, §1; Acts 1979, No. 247, §2.
