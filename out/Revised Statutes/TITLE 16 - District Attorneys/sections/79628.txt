PART XIX.  TWENTY-SECOND JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§481.  Additional salary of the district attorney
The district attorney of the Twenty-Second Judicial District of this state
shall receive, in addition to the salary paid him by the state, in lieu of all other
fees, an annual salary of seven thousand two hundred dollars, payable monthly
on his own warrant, by the parishes comprising the Twenty-Second Judicial
District, apportioned as follows:
(1)  By the parish of Washington, the sum of three thousand six hundred
dollars per annum.
(2)  By the parish of St. Tammany, the sum of three thousand six
hundred dollars per annum.
Amended by Acts 1959, No. 113, §14; Acts 1963, No. 125, §1.
