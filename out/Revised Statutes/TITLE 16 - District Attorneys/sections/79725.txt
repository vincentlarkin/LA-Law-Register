PART XXXV.  THIRTY-NINTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§901.  Additional salary of district attorney
A.  The district attorney of the Thirty-Ninth Judicial District shall
receive, in addition to the salary paid by the state, an annual salary of two
thousand four hundred dollars, payable monthly by the governing authority of
the parish of Red River.
B.  The governing authority of the parish of Red River shall annually
budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary fixed in Subsection A of this
Section.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 635, §5, eff. March 1, 1980.
