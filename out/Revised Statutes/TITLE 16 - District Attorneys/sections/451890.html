PART XXXVII.  FORTY-SECOND JUDICIAL DISTRICT

§921.  Assistant district attorney

The office of assistant district attorney for the Forty-Second Judicial District is created.

Acts 2007, No. 416, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
