§433.  Assistant district attorney
A. The office of assistant district attorney for the Twentieth Judicial
District is created.
B. The assistant district attorney may be a resident of either the parish
of East Feliciana or the parish of West Feliciana.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 56, §1.
