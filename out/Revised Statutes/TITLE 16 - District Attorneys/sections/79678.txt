§63.  Disbursements
All funds received by the districts or subdistricts herein created shall be paid into the district attorney's general fund which shall be disbursed by the district attorney in accordance with law.  Expenditures from the district attorney's general fund shall be subject to the public bid laws of the state of Louisiana.
Acts 1988, No. 688, §1, eff. July 15, 1988.
