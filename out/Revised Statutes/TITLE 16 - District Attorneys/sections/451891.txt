§922.  Additional salary of assistant district attorney
Each assistant district attorney of the Forty-Second Judicial District shall receive as compensation for his services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state, an annual salary of four thousand eight hundred dollars, payable monthly by the parish of DeSoto.
Acts 2007, No. 416, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
