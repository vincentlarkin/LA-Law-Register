PART XXIII.  TWENTY-SIXTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§581.  Assistant district attorneys
The offices of first, second and third assistant district attorneys for the Twenty-Sixth Judicial District of Louisiana are hereby created.
Amended by Acts 1968, No. 366, §1.
