§839.2. Assistant district attorneys

           The offices of assistant district attorneys of the Thirty-Fourth Judicial District are hereby created. The minimum number of said assistant district attorneys shall be the number as set out in R.S. 16:51 and all additional assistant district attorneys as shall be appointed by the district attorney of the Thirty-Fourth Judicial District, all of which assistants shall serve at the pleasure of the district attorney.

           Added by Acts 1979, No. 672, §1.
