§806. Third assistant district attorney; additional salary

           A. The office of third assistant district attorney for the Thirty-Second Judicial District is hereby created.

           B. The third assistant district attorney shall receive as compensation for his services an annual salary of not less than seven thousand dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of Terrebonne.

           Added by Acts 1973, No. 118, §9.
