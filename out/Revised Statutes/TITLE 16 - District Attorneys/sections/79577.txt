§356.  Additional salary of second assistant district attorney
The second assistant district attorney shall receive as compensation for
his services, in addition to the annual salary paid by the state, an annual salary
of five thousand five hundred fifty dollars, payable monthly.  The additional
salary shall be paid by the parishes of Iberia, St. Mary, and St. Martin as set
forth in R.S. 16:359.
Added by Acts 1954, No. 560, §§2, 3.  Amended by Acts 1955, No. 51,
§1; Acts 1962, No. 348, §4; Acts 1966, No. 150, §1.
