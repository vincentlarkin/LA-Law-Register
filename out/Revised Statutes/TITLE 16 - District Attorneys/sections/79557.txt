§303.  Budget to include additional salary
The governing authority of the parish of Calcasieu shall annually, and at the time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salaries fixed in R.S. 16:302.
Amended by Acts 1978, No. 14, §3, eff. March 1, 1979.
