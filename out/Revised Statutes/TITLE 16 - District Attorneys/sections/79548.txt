§276.  Budget to include additional salaries
The governing authority of the parish of Avoyelles shall annually budget an amount sufficient to pay the salaries fixed by R.S. 16:275.
Added by Acts 1967, No. 5, §1; Acts 1967, No. 38, §1.  Amended by Acts 1973, No. 118, §5.
