§923.  Budget to include additional salary
DeSoto Parish shall annually, and at the time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary fixed in R.S. 16:922.
Acts 2007, No. 416, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
