§382.  Budget to include additional salary

The governing authority of the parish of Lafourche shall annually and at the same time it prepares its general budget of expenses budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary fixed in R.S. 16:381.  

Amended by Acts 1968, No. 308, §1.
