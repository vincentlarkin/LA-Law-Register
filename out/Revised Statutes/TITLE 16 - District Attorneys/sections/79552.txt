§284.  Additional salary of assistant district attorney
The assistant district attorney of the Thirteenth Judicial District shall
receive as compensation for his services, in addition to the annual salary paid
by the state, an annual salary of two thousand four hundred dollars, payable
monthly.  The additional salary shall be paid by the parish of Evangeline.
