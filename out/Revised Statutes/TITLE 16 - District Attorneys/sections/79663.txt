§585.  Additional salary and expenses of district attorney
The district attorney of the Twenty-Sixth Judicial District of this state
shall receive, in addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual
salary of seven thousand two hundred fifty dollars, payable monthly on his
own warrant by the parishes comprising said district and apportioned as
follows:
(1) By the parish of Bossier, the sum of three thousand six hundred
twenty five dollars per annum.
(2) By the parish of Webster, the sum of three thousand six hundred
twenty five dollars per annum.
Added by Acts 1959, No. 113, §9.  Amended by Acts 1967, No. 46, §1;
Acts 1968, No. 366, §1.
