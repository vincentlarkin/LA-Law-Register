§326.  Third and fourth assistant district attorneys
The offices of the third and fourth assistant district attorneys for the
Fifteenth Judicial District of Louisiana are hereby created.
Added by Acts 1956, No. 449, §1.  Amended by Acts 1966, No. 256,
§1.
