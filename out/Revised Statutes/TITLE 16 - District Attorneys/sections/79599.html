§409.  Third assistant district attorney

The office of third assistant district attorney is hereby created for the Eighteenth Judicial District of Louisiana, under the provisions of Article V, Section 26 of the Constitution of Louisiana.

Added by Acts 1968, No. 162, §1; Acts 1997, No. 110, §1.
