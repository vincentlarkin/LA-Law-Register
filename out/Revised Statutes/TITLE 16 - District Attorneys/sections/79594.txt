§404.  Additional salary of assistant district attorney
The assistant district attorney for the Eighteenth Judicial District of
Louisiana shall receive as compensation for his services, in addition to the
annual salary paid by the state, an annual salary of four thousand fifty dollars,
payable monthly.  The additional salary shall be paid by the parishes
comprising the Eighteenth Judicial District apportioned as follows:
(1)  By the parish of Iberville, the sum of one thousand three hundred
fifty dollars per annum.
(2)  By the parish of Pointe Coupee, the sum of one thousand three
hundred fifty dollars per annum.
(3)  By the parish of West Baton Rouge, the sum of one thousand three
hundred fifty dollars per annum.
Amended by Acts 1952, No. 91, §1.
