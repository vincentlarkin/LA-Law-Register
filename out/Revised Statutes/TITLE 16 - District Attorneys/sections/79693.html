PART XXIX. THIRTY-SECOND JUDICIAL DISTRICT

§801. Additional salary of district attorney

           The district attorney of the Thirty-Second Judicial District of the state shall receive, in addition to the salary paid him by the state, an annual salary of ten thousand dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of Terrebonne.

           Added by Acts 1968, No. 306, §1.
