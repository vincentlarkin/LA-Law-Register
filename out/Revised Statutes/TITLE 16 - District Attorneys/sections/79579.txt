§358.  Additional salaries of third and fourth assistant district attorneys
The third and fourth assistant district attorneys shall receive, in addition
to the salary paid by the state of Louisiana, an additional salary of five
thousand five hundred fifty dollars per year each, payable monthly, said sum
to be paid by the parishes of Iberia, St. Mary, and St. Martin as set forth in R.S.
16:359.
Added by Acts 1966, No. 150, §2.  Amended by Acts 1968, No. 310,
§1.
