§355.  Second assistant district attorney
The office of second assistant district attorney is hereby created in and
for the Sixteenth Judicial District of Louisiana, under the provisions of Article
VII, Section 60 of the Constitution of Louisiana.
1
Added by Acts 1954, No. 560, §1.
