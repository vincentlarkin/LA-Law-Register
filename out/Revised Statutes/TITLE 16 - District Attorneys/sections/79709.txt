§84.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the First Judicial District of this state shall receive, in addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual salary of eleven thousand seven hundred and fifty dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of Caddo.
Added by Acts 1959, No. 113, §1.
