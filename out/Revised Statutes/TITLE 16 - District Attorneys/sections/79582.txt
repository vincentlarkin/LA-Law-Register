§359.  Payment of additional salaries to assistant district attorneys
The additional annual salaries of the first, second, third, fourth, and fifth
assistant district attorneys of the Sixteenth Judicial District shall be prorated
as follows: The parish of Iberia shall pay ten thousand dollars, the parish of St.
Mary shall pay ten thousand dollars, and the parish of St. Martin shall pay
seven thousand seven hundred fifty dollars.
Added by Acts 1966, No. 150, §2.  Amended by Acts 1968, No. 310,
§1; Acts 1970, No. 55, §2.
