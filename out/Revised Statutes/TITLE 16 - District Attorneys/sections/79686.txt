§692.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the Thirtieth Judicial District of this state shall
receive, in addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual salary to
be fixed by the governing authority of the parish of Vernon, of not less than
nine thousand dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of
Vernon.
Acts 1977, No. 164, §10, eff. Jan. 1, 1979.
