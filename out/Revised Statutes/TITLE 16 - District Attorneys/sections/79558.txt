§304.  Additional salary of district attorney
The district attorney of the Fourteenth Judicial District of this state shall
receive, in addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual salary of
nineteen thousand dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish
of Calcasieu.
Added by Acts 1959, No. 113, §7.  Amended by Acts 1968, No. 266,
§1; Acts 1970, No. 426, §1; Acts 1973, No. 197, §1; Acts 1978, No. 14, §3,
eff. March 1, 1979.
