§166.  Budget to include additional salary
Each of the parishes composing the Sixth Judicial District shall
annually, and at the same time it prepares its general budget of expenses,
budget an amount sufficient to pay the salaries fixed in R.S. 16:164 and the
expense allowance fixed in R.S. 16:165.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 153, §1.
