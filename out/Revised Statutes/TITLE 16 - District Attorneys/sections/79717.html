PART XXXIII. THIRTY-SEVENTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT

§881. Additional salary of district attorney

           The district attorney of the Thirty-Seventh Judicial District shall receive, in addition to the salary paid him by the state, an annual salary to be fixed by the parish of Caldwell, of not less than four thousand two hundred dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the parish of Caldwell.

           Added by Acts 1977, No. 620, §27, eff. Jan. 3, 1979.
