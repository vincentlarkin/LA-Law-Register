§642.  Assistant district attorneys

The offices of the first, second, third, fourth, and fifth assistant district attorneys for the Twenty-Ninth Judicial District of Louisiana are hereby created.  

Acts 1952, No. 68, §1.  Amended by Acts 1960, No. 211, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 111, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.  Acts 1984, No. 84, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
