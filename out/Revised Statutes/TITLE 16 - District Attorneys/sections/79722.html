§894.  Additional salary of assistant district attorney

The first assistant district attorney of the Thirty-Eighth Judicial District shall receive, in addition to the salary now paid him by the state, an annual salary, to be fixed by the governing authority of the parish of Cameron, of not less than three thousand six hundred dollars, payable monthly on his own warrant by the governing authority of the parish of Cameron.  

Added by Acts 1981, No. 694, §2, eff. July 20, 1981.
