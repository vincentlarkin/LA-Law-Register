§572.  Assistant district attorneys
The offices of assistant district attorneys for the Twenty-Fifth Judicial District are hereby created.  The minimum number of said assistant district attorneys shall be the number as set out in R.S. 16:51 and all additional assistant district attorneys as shall be appointed by the district attorney of the Twenty-Fifth Judicial District, all of which assistants shall serve at the pleasure of the district attorney.
Acts 1986, No. 198, §1; Acts 1986, No. 223, §1; Acts 1997, No. 782, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
