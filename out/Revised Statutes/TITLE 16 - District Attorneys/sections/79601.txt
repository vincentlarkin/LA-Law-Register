PART XVII.  NINETEENTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT
§421.  Additional salary of the district attorney for the Nineteenth Judicial
District
The district attorney of the Nineteenth Judicial District of this state shall
receive, in addition to the salary paid him by the state, in lieu of all other fees,
an annual salary of not less than eleven thousand seven hundred fifty dollars,
nor more than such sum as may be established from time to time by the
governing authority of the parish of East Baton Rouge, Louisiana, the amount
so established to be payable monthly by said parish on the warrant of the said
district attorney.
Amended by Acts 1957, No. 41, §1; Acts 1959, No. 113, §13; Acts
1967, No. 84, §1.
