§263.  Budget to include additional salary
Sabine Parish, composing the Eleventh Judicial District shall annually, and at the time it prepares its general budget of expenses, budget an amount sufficient to pay the salary fixed in R.S. 16:262.
Acts 2007, No. 416, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
