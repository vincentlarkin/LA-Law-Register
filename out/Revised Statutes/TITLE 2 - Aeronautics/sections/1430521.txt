SUBPART E.  CLAIBORNE PARISH AIRPORT DISTRICTS
§349.1.  Claiborne Parish Airport District No. 31
There is hereby created the "Claiborne Parish Airport District No. 31", hereinafter
referred to in this Subpart as the "district".  The district shall be a political subdivision of the
state within the meaning of Article VI, Sections 30 through 33 of the Constitution of
Louisiana and those provisions of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 applicable to
airport districts and the levy of taxes, the incurring of debt, and the issuing of bonds by those
districts.
Acts 2025, No. 214, §1, eff. June 8, 2025.
