§7.1.  Operation and maintenance of aeronautical navigation aids
The Department of Transportation and Development may, subject to legislative appropriation of sufficient funds, enter into a contract or contracts to maintain, repair, and use such air navigation aids as the department deems necessary to operate a statewide aeronautical navigation system.  A plan for such a system shall be presented to the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget for approval before the program is put into effect.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 216, §1.
