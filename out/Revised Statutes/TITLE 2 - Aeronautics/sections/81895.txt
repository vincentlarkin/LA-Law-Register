§330.  Part is full authority for creation of district
This Part is full authority for the creation of airport districts without reference to any other Part.  No proceedings with respect to the creation of airport districts are necessary except as required by this Part.  Publication of notices required by this Part may be in any newspaper conforming to the terms of this Part without regard to designation thereof as the official journal of the airport district.
Amended by Acts 1975, No. 659, §1.
