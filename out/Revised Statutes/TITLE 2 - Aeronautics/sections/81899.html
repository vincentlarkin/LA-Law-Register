§343.  Issuance of bonds

No bonds shall be issued by the Ascension-St. James Airport and Transportation Authority under this Part until they are approved by a vote of a majority in number of the qualified electors of the district, voting at an election held in the district for that purpose.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 819, §1; Amended by Acts 1988, No. 68, §1.
