§11.  Powers and duties of the department; aeronautics; regulations filed for inspection
The department shall keep on file with the secretary of state and at the principal office of the department, a copy of all its rules and regulations pertaining to aeronautics for public inspection.
Acts 1985, No. 889, §1.
