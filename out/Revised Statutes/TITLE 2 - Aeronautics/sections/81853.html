PART III.  FINANCING BY PARISHES AND MUNICIPALITIES

SUBPART A.  INDEBTEDNESS AND BONDS; GENERAL

TAX REVENUE

§181.  Authority of municipalities and parishes to incur and refund debt and issue bonds

Airports and landing fields being works of public improvement, municipal corporations or parishes of the state may incur and refund debt and issue negotiable bonds for acquiring, building, equipping or maintaining such airports and landing fields, or for any one or more of such purposes, by such municipal corporations or parishes, as provided by Section 14 of Article XIV of the Constitution of Louisiana.
