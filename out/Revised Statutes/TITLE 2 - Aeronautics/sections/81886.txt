§321.  Board of commissioners; tenure and compensation
Louisiana members of boards of commissioners for airport districts shall be appointed for a term of not more than five years from the date they assume their duties and shall serve without compensation; however, all members of boards of commissioners shall serve at the will of the governing bodies of the respective parishes appointing such commissioners.
Amended by Acts 1975, No. 659, §1.  Acts 1983, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 26, §1, eff. Jan. 19, 1983; Acts 1999, No. 836, §1.
