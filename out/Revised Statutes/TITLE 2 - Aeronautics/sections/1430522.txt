§349.2.  Domicile
The domicile of the district shall be in Claiborne Parish.  The domicile, provided
there is appropriate action of the board of commissioners, may be at any place located within
the boundaries of the district, or may be the seat of any political subdivision a portion of
which is included within the boundaries of the district.
Acts 2025, No. 214, §1, eff. June 8, 2025.
