CHAPTER 8.  GENERAL AVIATION AND RELIEVER

AIRPORT MAINTENANCE GRANT PROGRAM

§901.  Creation and administration

The General Aviation and Reliever Airport Maintenance Grant Program is hereby created within the division of aviation of the Department of Transportation and Development for the purpose of providing for airport maintenance to assist Louisiana general aviation and reliever airports and to enhance their safety.

Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 145, §1.
