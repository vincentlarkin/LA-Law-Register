§324.  Abolition of district or change of boundaries

The parish governing authorities may repeal any ordinance creating an airport district, may abolish a district, or may change its boundaries; neither the existence of a district nor the boundaries thereof shall be affected in a manner to impair any obligations incurred by the district during its existence.

Amended by Acts 1975, No. 659, §1.
