§323.  Domicile of district
The domicile of the airport district shall be designated in the resolution or resolutions creating said district.  The domicile so designated may be at any place located within the boundaries of the airport district, or may be the seat of any political subdivision a portion of which is included within the boundaries of the airport district.
Amended by Acts 1975, No. 659, §1.  Acts 1983, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 26, §1, eff. Jan. 19, 1983.
