§317.  Creation of airport district in more than one parish; joint resolution

When a proposed airport district includes territory situated in more than one political subdivision, it shall be created by joint resolutions of the governing authority of each political subdivision whose territory is included, or by resolution of one approved by the other.

Amended by Acts 1975, No. 659, §1.  Acts 1983, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 26, §1, eff. Jan. 19, 1983.
