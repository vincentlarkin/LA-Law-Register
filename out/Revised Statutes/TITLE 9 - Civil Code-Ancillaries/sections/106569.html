§1123.107. Upkeep of the condominium

           Except to the extent provided by the declaration, or R.S. 9:1123.112, the association is responsible for maintenance, repair, and replacement of the common elements, and each unit owner is responsible for maintenance, repair and replacement of his unit.

           Acts 1979, No. 682, §1.
