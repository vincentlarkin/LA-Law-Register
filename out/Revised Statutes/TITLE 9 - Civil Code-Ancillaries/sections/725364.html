§359.9.  Duty to cooperate; disclosure of information

A.  When military necessity precludes court adjudication prior to deployment, the parties shall cooperate in custody or visitation matters.

B.  Within ten days of receipt, a copy of the deployment orders shall be provided to the other parent.  When the deployment date is less than ten days after receipt of the orders, a copy shall immediately be provided.

Acts 2010, No. 739, §1.
