§355.18.  Posting security
If relocation of a child is permitted, the court may require the person relocating the child to provide reasonable security guaranteeing that the court-ordered physical custody or visitation with the child will not be interrupted or interfered with by the relocating party.
Acts 2012, No. 627, §1.
