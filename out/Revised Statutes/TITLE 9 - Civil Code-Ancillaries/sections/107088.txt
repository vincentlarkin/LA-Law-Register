PART IV.  DELAYS AND CEREMONY
SUBPART A.  TWENTY-FOUR-HOUR DELAY
§241.  Premature ceremony prohibited
An officiant may not perform a marriage ceremony until twenty-four hours have
elapsed since the issuance of the marriage license.
Acts 1987, No. 886, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Acts 2018, No. 276, §1.
