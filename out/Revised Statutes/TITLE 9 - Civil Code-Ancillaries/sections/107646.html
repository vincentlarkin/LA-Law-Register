§355.13.  Priority for trial

A trial on the proposed relocation shall be assigned within sixty days after the filing of the motion to obtain court approval to relocate.

Acts 1997, No. 1173, §1; Acts 2003, No. 676, §1; Acts 2012, No. 627, §1.
