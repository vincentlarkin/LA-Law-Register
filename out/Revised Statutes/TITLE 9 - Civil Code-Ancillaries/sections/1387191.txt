§377.12.  Disclosure of information
Except as provided by law other than this Part, during the collaborative family law
process, upon the request of another party, a party shall make timely, full, candid, and
informal disclosure of information related to the collaborative family law matter without
formal discovery.  A party also shall update promptly previously disclosed information that
has materially changed.  The parties may define the scope of disclosure during the
collaborative family law process.
Acts 2024, No. 98, §1.
