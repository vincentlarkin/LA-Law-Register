§315.47.  Reissuance of license

The licensing authority shall issue, reissue, renew, or otherwise extend an obligor's license, in accordance with any applicable reinstatement fees or applicable rules, upon receipt of a certified copy of a compliance or partial compliance release from the department.

Acts 1995, No. 751, §1; Acts 2001, No. 612, §1, eff. June 22, 2001; Acts 2012, No. 613, §1.
