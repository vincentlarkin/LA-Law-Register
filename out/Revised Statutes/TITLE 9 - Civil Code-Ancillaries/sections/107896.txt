§407.  Uncontested paternity proceedings; proof by affidavit; adoption of court rules
The court vested with jurisdiction may provide, by local rule, that in uncontested
proceedings to establish paternity, proof may be submitted by affidavit.
Acts 2015, No. 260, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
