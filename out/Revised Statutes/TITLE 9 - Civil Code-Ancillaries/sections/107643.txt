§355.10.  Burden of proof
The person proposing relocation has the burden of proof that the proposed relocation is made in good faith and is in the best interest of the child.
Acts 1997, No. 1173, §1; Acts 2003, No. 1209, §1; Acts 2012, No. 627, §1.
