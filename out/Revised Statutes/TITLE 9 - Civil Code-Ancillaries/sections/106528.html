§1110. Ownership of land adjacent to False River

            The title of the owners of land adjacent to that body of water in Pointe Coupee Parish known as False River shall extend to a line delineated by the "Final Map showing survey of the property boundary of the State of Louisiana along False River located in T 4 S - R 10 E & T 5 S - R 10 E Pointe Coupee Parish, Louisiana for The State of Louisiana" dated February 21, 2018, and filed with the Pointe Coupee Parish Clerk of Court. The line delineated by the map shall be the line that marks the division between land owned by the state and land owned by private persons along the banks of False River.

            Added by Acts 1975, No. 285, §1; Acts 2015, No. 402, §1; Acts 2018, No. 95, §1, eff. May 10, 2018.
