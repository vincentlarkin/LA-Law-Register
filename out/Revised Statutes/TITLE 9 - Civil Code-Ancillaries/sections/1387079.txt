§59.  Application of definitions; distinctions between the sexes
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, any provision of law
enacted by the legislature or any rule adopted by a state agency or other entity subject to the
Administrative Procedure Act when applicable to an individual's sex shall apply those
definitions provided in R.S. 9:58.
Acts 2024, No. 436, §1.
