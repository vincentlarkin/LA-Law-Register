§357.  Use of technology

The court shall consider ordering persons awarded custody or visitation to use technology, including video calling, telephone, text messaging, Internet communications, or other forms of technology, to facilitate communication with the child when it is in the best interest of the child.

Acts 2012, No. 627, §2.
