§124.  Identification and confidentiality
As a juridical person, the in vitro fertilized human embryo shall be given an
identification by the medical facility.  The confidentiality of the in vitro fertilized human
embryo and the patient from which it came shall be maintained.
Acts 1986, No. 964, §1; Acts 2025, No. 116, §1.
