§602.  Tutor, under tutor, curator, under curator; family meeting dispensed with
The judge shall act, without the convocation of a family meeting, in all matters pertaining to the appointment, recognition, or confirmation of all tutors, under tutors, curators, or under curators for minors or interdicts.
