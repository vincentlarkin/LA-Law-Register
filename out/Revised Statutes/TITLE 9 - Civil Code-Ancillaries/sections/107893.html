§404.  Father's paternity action; time period; exception

The peremptive periods in Civil Code Article 198 shall apply to the Department of Children and Family Services when providing services in accordance with 42 U.S.C. 666.

Acts 2006, No. 344, §4, eff. June 13, 2006.
