§770.  Termination of custodianship
The custodian shall transfer in an appropriate manner the custodial property to the
minor or to the minor's estate upon the earlier of the following:
(1)  The minor's attainment of twenty-two years of age.
(2)  The minor's judicial emancipation.
(3)  The minor's death.
Acts 1987, No. 469, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Acts 2023, No. 60, §1.
NOTE:  See Acts 2024, No. 194, §1, regarding retroactive application of Acts 2023,
No. 60.
