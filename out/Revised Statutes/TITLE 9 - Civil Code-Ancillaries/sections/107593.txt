SUBPART D.  ACCESS TO RECORDS
§351.  Access to records of child
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, access to records and information pertaining to a minor child, including but not limited to medical, dental, and school records, shall not be denied to a parent solely because he is not the child's custodial or domiciliary parent.
Acts 1984, No. 788, §1; Acts 1985, No. 789 §1, eff. July 22, 1985; Acts 1989, No. 188, §2; Acts 1993, No. 261, §5, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
