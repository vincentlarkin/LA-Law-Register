§64.  Standard of review
Any law that distinguishes between the sexes in furtherance of the provisions of this
Part is subject to intermediate scrutiny which forbids discrimination against similarly situated
individuals but allows the law to distinguish between the sexes when there is an important
governmental interest.
Acts 2024, No. 436, §1.
