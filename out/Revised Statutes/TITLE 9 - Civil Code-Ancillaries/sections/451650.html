§358.7.  Access to information

The court shall order the parties to cooperate with the parenting coordinator and to provide relevant non-privileged records and information requested by the parenting coordinator.  The parenting coordinator may communicate with the child and other persons not a party to the litigation.

Acts 2007, No. 265, §1.
