§368. Other remedies not affected

            This Part shall in no way affect the remedies set forth in R.S. 46:2131 through 2142, the Criminal Code, the Children's Code, the Civil Code, or elsewhere; however, the court, in any case brought under R.S. 46:2131 et seq., may impose the remedies provided herein.

            Acts 1992, No. 1091, §1; Acts 2015, No. 221, §3.
