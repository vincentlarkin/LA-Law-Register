§359.8.  Lack of existing order of custody or visitation
When an order establishing custody or visitation has not been rendered and deployment is imminent, upon the motion of either parent, the court shall expedite a hearing to establish a temporary order in accordance with this Subpart.
Acts 2010, No. 739, §1.
