§758.  Receipt for custodial property

A written acknowledgment of delivery by a custodian constitutes a sufficient receipt and discharge for custodial property transferred to the custodian pursuant to this Subpart.  

Acts 1987, No. 469, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
