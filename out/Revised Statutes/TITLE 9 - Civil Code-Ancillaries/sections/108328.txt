§734.  Investment of funds
Tutors of minors and curators of interdicts may invest the funds of their wards by depositing them at interest in savings accounts or on time deposit in banks domiciled in this state, in amounts not exceeding the amounts insured under the Acts of Congress by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation or insured under any other Act of the Congress of the United States.
Acts 1952, No. 292, §1.
