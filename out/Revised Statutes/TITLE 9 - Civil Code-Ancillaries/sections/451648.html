§358.5.  Testimony and report

A.  The parenting coordinator shall not be called as a witness in the child custody proceeding without prior court approval.

B.  The parenting coordinator shall distribute all reports to the court, the parties, and their attorneys.

Acts 2007, No. 265, §1.
