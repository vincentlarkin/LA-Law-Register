§255.  Tabulation of marriage statistics; annual report
A. The state registrar of vital records shall annually prepare, from the information
filed with him under the provisions of R.S. 9:224, 252, and 253, abstracts and tabular
statements of the facts relating to marriages in each parish, and embody them, with the
necessary analysis, in his annual report to the state.  His annual report to the state shall
include a state of marriage report.
B. The annual state of marriage report shall include the number of minors married in
each parish, the number of marriages approved by parental consent, and the number of
marriages approved by judicial authorization.
C. The annual state of marriage report shall be submitted to the speaker of the House
of Representatives and the president of the Senate.
Acts 1987, No. 886, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Acts 2019, No. 401, §2.
