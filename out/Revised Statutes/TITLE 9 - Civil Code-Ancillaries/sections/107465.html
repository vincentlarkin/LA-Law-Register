§324.  Cancellation of record following payment

A recorder of mortgages shall forthwith cancel and erase from his records any judgment or order recorded in his office as provided in R.S. 9:323 upon the order of the person in whose favor said judgment or order was rendered or upon proper evidence showing payment in full by the person against whom said judgment or order was rendered.

Acts 1952, No. 488, §1; Acts 1997, No. 1078, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
