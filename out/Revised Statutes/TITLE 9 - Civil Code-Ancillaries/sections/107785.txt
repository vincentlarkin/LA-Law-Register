§365.  Qualification of licensed mental health professional
Any licensed mental health professional appointed by the court to conduct a custody
evaluation in a case where family violence is an issue shall have current and demonstrable
training and experience working with perpetrators and victims of family violence.
Acts 1992, No. 1091, §1; Acts 2022, No. 614, §1.
