§355.16.  Application of factors at initial hearing

If the issue of relocation is presented at the initial hearing to determine custody of and visitation with a child, the court shall consider also the factors set forth in R.S. 9:355.14 in making its initial determination.

Acts 1997, No. 1173, §1; Acts 2012, No. 627, §1.
