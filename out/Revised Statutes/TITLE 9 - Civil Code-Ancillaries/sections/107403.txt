§315.43.  Administrative hearing
Upon receipt of a timely written objection, the department shall conduct an administrative hearing in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.  Such hearing may be conducted telephonically or by means of any other such electronic media.  The sole issue at the administrative hearing shall be whether the obligor is in compliance with an order of support.
Acts 1995, No. 751, §1; Acts 2001, No. 612, §1, eff. June 22, 2001.
