CODE TITLE IV - HUSBAND AND WIFE

CHAPTER 1.  MARRIAGE: GENERAL PRINCIPLES

PART I.  OFFICIANTS

§201.  Definition

An officiant is a person authorized by law to perform marriage ceremonies.  

Acts 1987, No. 886, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
