§1122.119.  Amendment of declaration
Except in cases of amendments that may be executed by declarant under
R.S. 9:1122.110(C) or (D), or by the Association under R.S. 9:1121.107,
1122.107(B),  1122.114(A), or 1122.115, the declaration, including the plats
and plans, may be amended only by vote or agreement of unit owners of units
to which at least sixty-seven percent of the votes of the association are
allocated or any other percent of votes which the condominium declaration
specifies.
Acts 1979, No. 682, §1.
