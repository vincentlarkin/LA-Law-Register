PART V.  BOOKS AND INDEXES

§185.  Recordation books; indexes

The clerk of court as the parish recorder shall keep a book of conveyances, a book of mortgages, and other recordation books as provided by law.  He shall keep indexes as required by law.

Acts 2006, No. 621, §5, eff. June 23, 2006.
