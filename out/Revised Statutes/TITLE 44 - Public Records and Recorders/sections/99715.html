§370. Restricted application

           Nothing in the provisions of this Sub-part shall be construed as giving or affording evidence superior to titles, instruments, or other documents restored or revived, after issue joined, under and by virtue of the provisions of Subpart A of this Part.

           The effect hereof shall be restricted to titles, instruments, or other documents re-established or restored under and pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 44:361 through R.S. 44:366.
