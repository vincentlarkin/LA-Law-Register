§415. Imaging and preservation services

            The imaging and preservation services program shall provide conversion services for the state. All agencies shall contract with the division for conversion services or shall comply with the conversion standards and disposal request procedures established by the division in accordance with R.S. 44:405.

            Acts 1956, No. 337, §15; Acts 1985, No. 238, §1, eff. July 6, 1985; Acts 1986, No. 913, §1; Acts 2019, No. 46, §1; Acts 2024, No. 508, §1.
