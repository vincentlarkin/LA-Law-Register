§325.  Parol evidence may be used
The provisions of this Subpart shall not prevent the establishment of
any judgment or any other instrument of writing, by parol evidence, where the
original record has been destroyed by the burning of the courthouse or any
other place of deposit of public records, or destroyed in any other way in the
parish.
