§406. Collection of records

            The state archivist may collect and accept records of any format, papers, and artifacts from agencies or the public, provided that these records adhere to the mission of the division as provided in R.S. 44:401. The division shall prepare inventories, indexes, catalogs, and other aids to facilitate the access and use of these records.

            Acts 1956, No. 337, §6; Acts 1985, No. 238, §1, eff. July 6, 1985; Acts 2024, No. 508, §1.
