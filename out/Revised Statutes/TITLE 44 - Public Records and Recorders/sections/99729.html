§409.  Appointment of advisory groups

The secretary may from time to time appoint advisory groups to more effectively obtain the best professional thinking of groups including but not limited to the bar, historians, political scientists, accountants, genealogists, patriotic groups, associations of public officials, et cetera, concerning the steps to be taken with regard to any particular group or type of records.  Advisory group members shall receive no salary.

Acts 1956, No. 337, §9; Acts 1985, No. 238, §1, eff. July 6, 1985.
