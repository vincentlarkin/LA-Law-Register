§3.4. Records of the Board of Ethics

            Nothing in this Chapter shall be construed to require the disclosure of records, or the information contained therein, held by the Board of Ethics pertaining to enforcement proceedings. However, any such record shall be public record and subject to the provisions of this Chapter when introduced as evidence before the Ethics Adjudicatory Board or a court or when the enforcement proceedings are concluded, subject to the limitations in R.S. 42:1141.4.

            Acts 2015, No. 316, §2, eff. August 1, 2015.
