§410. §410. Records management programs

            A. The secretary shall maintain a statewide records management program that prescribes policies and principles to be followed by state and local agencies for the economical and efficient management of state and local government records, to improve preservation and access to records, to ensure the maintenance and security of records, and to facilitate the appropriate retention or disposal of records.

            B. The secretary shall employ records analysts to assist agencies in their compliance with the requirements of this Chapter and to provide training regarding compliance with the statewide records management program to state and local agencies.

            Acts 1956, No. 337, §10; Acts 1985, No. 238, §1, eff. July 6, 1985; Acts 2024, No. 508, §1.
