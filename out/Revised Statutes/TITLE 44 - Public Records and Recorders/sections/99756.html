§72.  Seal

Each recorder shall have an official seal, which he shall attach to all acts, certificates, and other instruments, executed or granted by him.  Failure to affix the seal does not affect the validity or effect of the instruments.

Acts 2005, No. 169, §6, eff. July 1, 2006; Acts 2005, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 13, §1, eff. Nov. 29, 2005.
