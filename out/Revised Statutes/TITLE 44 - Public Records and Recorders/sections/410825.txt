§182.1.  Retention of powers
The clerk of court as the parish recorder shall be the successor to and shall exercise all powers, duties, functions, and responsibilities of the offices of the custodian of notarial records, the register of conveyances, and the recorder of mortgages, consistent with the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 2006, No. 621, §5, eff. June 23, 2006.
