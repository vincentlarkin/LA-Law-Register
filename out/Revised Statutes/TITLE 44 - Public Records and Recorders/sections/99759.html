§75.  Deputies

Except in the parish of Orleans, the recorder may appoint deputies whose duties shall be the same as those of the recorder.  Before entering upon the duties of his office the deputy shall take the oath that the law prescribes for the recorder.

Acts 2005, No. 169, §6, eff. July 1, 2006; Acts 2005, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 13, §1, eff. Nov. 29, 2005.
