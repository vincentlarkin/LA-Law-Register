§18.  Geophysical survey information

All information and records of geophysical or geological surveys furnished to the State Mineral and Energy Board or the office of mineral resources pursuant to R.S. 30:213 shall be confidential and exempt from the provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 2000, 2d Ex. Sess., No. 8, §2; Acts 2009, No. 196, §8, eff. July 1, 2009.
