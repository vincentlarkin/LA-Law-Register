PART II.  POWERS AND DUTIES OF PARISH RECORDER
§182.  General powers
The clerk of court as the parish recorder shall have the same general powers as provided in R.S. 44:101 for recorders throughout the state.
Acts 2006, No. 621, §5, eff. June 23, 2006.
