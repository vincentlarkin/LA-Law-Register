§424.  Authority of state financial officers and others not limited
Nothing in this Chapter shall be construed as limiting the authority of the commissioner of administration, or other officers charged with prescribing accounting systems, forms, or procedures or of lessening the responsibility of collection and disbursal officers for rendering of their accounts for settlement.
Acts 1956, No. 337, §24; Acts 1985, No. 238, §1, eff. July 6, 1985.
