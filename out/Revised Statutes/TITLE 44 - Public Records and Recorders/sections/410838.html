§184.1.  Recordation of agreement

Any person, upon paying the fees otherwise fixed by law, may at any time request that the agreement be filed and copied in full into the records and the clerk of court as the parish recorder upon request also note in the margin of the recorded copy of the declaration a reference to the place where the agreement is recorded.

Acts 2006, No. 621, §5, eff. June 23, 2006.
