§419. §419. Preservation, conservation, and exhibition of records

            A. The secretary shall maintain a conservation and exhibition program and shall provide for the preservation, conservation, and exhibition of records at the Louisiana State Archives.

            B. The secretary shall provide for the exhibition of copies in lieu of the original records if the secretary deems it necessary to protect old, fragile, or damaged records.

            Acts 1956, No. 337, §19; Acts 1985, No. 238, §1, eff. July 6, 1985; Acts 2024, No. 508, §1.
