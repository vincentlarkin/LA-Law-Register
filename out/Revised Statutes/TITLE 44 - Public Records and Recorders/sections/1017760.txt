§33.1.  Custodian; contact information; notice to public
A.  Each public body that has a custodian of public records shall make the contact
information of the custodian available to the public in a manner that will allow a member of
the public to quickly determine the appropriate person to whom a public records request
should be submitted, including by placing such information on the internet.
B.  Each custodian shall use reasonable means to notify the public that information
submitted to the public body may become public record pursuant to the provisions of this
Chapter.
Acts 2016, No. 654, §1; Acts 2019, No. 256, §1.
