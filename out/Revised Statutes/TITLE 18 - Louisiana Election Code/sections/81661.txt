§59.1.  Payment of state portion of salaries and expenses of registrars; secretary of state
A.  All monies made available from state funds for the payment of the state portion of the salaries and expenses of registrars of voters and their confidential assistants, deputy registrars, and other personnel shall be appropriated to and be paid to each such person by the secretary of state in accordance with each appropriation for the purpose.  The secretary of state shall include in his annual budget an amount necessary for the purpose, and the monies so appropriated shall be withdrawn from the treasury upon warrants drawn by the secretary of state and payments therefrom shall be made in accordance with applicable law.
B, C.  Repealed by Acts 1985, No. 754, §2.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 292, §1, eff. July 6, 1978; Acts 1985, No. 754, §2; Acts 2001, No. 451, §6, eff. Jan. 12, 2004.
