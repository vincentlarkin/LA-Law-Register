§1434.  Waiver of objections to voter qualifications when voter is not challenged at the election
An objection to the qualifications of a voter, except for an objection to a voter who should have been removed from the voter registration rolls pursuant to R.S. 18:173, or to an irregularity in the conduct of the election, which with the exercise of due diligence could have been raised by a challenge of the voter or objections at the polls to the procedure, is deemed waived.
Acts 1976, No. 697, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1978; Acts 1997, No. 591, §1.
