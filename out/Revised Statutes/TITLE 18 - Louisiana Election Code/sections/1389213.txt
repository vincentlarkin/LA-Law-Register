§119.  Voter registration drives; mandatory registration
A.  Each third-party voter registration organization that intends to conduct a voter
registration drive shall first register with the secretary of state either directly or through a
registrar of voters in the manner prescribed by the secretary of state.
B.  The registration required by this Section shall require acknowledgment of the
requirements applicable to voter registration activities as well as the election offenses
applicable to voter registration and voter registration activities.  The registration required by
this Section shall not require a response from the secretary of state.
C.  This Section shall not apply to a voter registration drive conducted using the
electronic voter registration application provided for in R.S. 18:115.1, provided that the
third-party voter registration organization only provides access to the electronic voter
registration application and does not capture the information provided for any purpose.
Acts 2024, No. 701, §1.
