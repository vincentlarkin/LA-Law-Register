§153.  Tax rolls

A copy of the latest filed tax roll shall be made available to the registrar of each parish upon his request, so that he may prepare the list to be used in any election in which property ownership is a legally valid requisite for voting.

Acts 1976, No. 697, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1978.
