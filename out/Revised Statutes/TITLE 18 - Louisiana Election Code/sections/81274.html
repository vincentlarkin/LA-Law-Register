§1265.  Compensation

Presidential electors shall be paid fifty dollars for attendance at the meeting of electors and shall be paid the same mileage allowance as is provided for state officers and employees on official state business.  Such compensation and mileage allowance shall be paid out of funds appropriated to the office of the secretary of state for such purpose.

Acts 1976, No. 697, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1978.
