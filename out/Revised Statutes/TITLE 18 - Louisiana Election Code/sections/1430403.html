§1367.5. Authority of the commissioner of administration

            The commissioner of administration shall have the authority to review and determine any appeal by an aggrieved person from a determination by the state chief procurement officer or his designee which is authorized by R.S. 18:1367 or 1367.1.

            Acts 2025, No. 208, §1, eff. June 8, 2025.
