§1280.23.  Conduct of election
Except as otherwise specifically provided in this Part, the election for which provision is made in this Part shall be conducted and the returns thereof published and promulgated as provided in the election laws of the state.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 684, §1, eff. Dec. 1, 1979.
