§1511.9.  Immunity from prosecution; prohibition
No person shall be granted immunity from prosecution for testimony or for providing information in connection with any investigation or proceeding conducted under the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 1980, No. 786, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1981.
