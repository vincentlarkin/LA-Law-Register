§1274.  Declaration of candidacy by congressional candidates
Any person desiring to become a candidate in a primary election for United States senator or representative in Congress shall file notification of his candidacy and declaration of his qualifications as provided for state candidates in Chapter 5 of this Title.
Acts 1976, No. 697, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1978.
