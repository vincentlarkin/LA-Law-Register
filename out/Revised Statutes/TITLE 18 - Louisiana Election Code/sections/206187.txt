§567.6.  Judicial review
The final decision of the board or the final resolution of the administrative law judge may be judicially reviewed by filing a petition in the Nineteenth Judicial District Court, Parish of East Baton Rouge.  The petition shall be filed within thirty days after the mailing of the notice of the final decision of the board or the final resolution of the administrative law judge.
Acts 2003, No. 423, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
