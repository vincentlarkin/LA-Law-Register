PART IV. LOUISIANA ELECTIONS INTEGRITY

§41. Louisiana Elections Integrity; administration of Part

           A. The Board of Ethics, hereafter in this Part referred to as the "board", shall administer the provisions of this Part.

           B. Board members shall be paid the same per diem as members of the legislature for each day of attendance at board meetings and shall be reimbursed actual expenses incurred in attending board meetings and in conducting board business.

           C. The board shall hold such meetings as are necessary to effectuate its purposes and shall meet upon call of the chairman or upon the request of any three members.

           Acts 1989, No. 45, §1; Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 64, §11, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
