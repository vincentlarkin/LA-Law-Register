§63.  Ineligibility to hold elective or appointive public office

A registrar, deputy registrar, or employee of a registrar shall not be eligible to hold any elective or appointive public office or position with the United States or the state or any of its agencies or political subdivisions, except as otherwise specifically provided by law.

Acts 1976, No. 697, eff. Jan. 1, 1978.
