§1300.12.  Results of election
The majority of the votes cast shall determine the result of the recall election.  If the proposition for the recall does not receive a majority of the votes cast, the effort to recall shall have failed.  Votes shall be tabulated and returns made, declared, and promulgated as at a general election.
Acts 1995, No. 555, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
