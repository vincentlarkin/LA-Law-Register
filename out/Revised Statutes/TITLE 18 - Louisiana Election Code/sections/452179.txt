§1400.8.  Expenses incurred by registrars of voters conducting early voting; payment by
secretary of state
Election expenses incurred by a registrar of voters and his permanent employees to
perform election duties and responsibilities associated with early voting on any day during
the week between 4:30 p.m. and the time that early voting terminates that day and on any
Saturday on which early voting is conducted shall be paid by the state from funds
appropriated to the secretary of state for that purpose.
Acts 2007, No. 229, §1; Acts 2008, No. 167, §1, eff. June 12, 2008; Acts 2015, No.
269, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016, and §2, eff. Dec. 31, 2017.
