PART V.  CANVASS, CANCELLATION
§191.  Permanent registration
The registration of any person as provided in this Chapter shall remain in effect for so long as the registration is not canceled for a cause and in the manner set forth in this Chapter.
Acts 1989, No. 652, §1.
