§113.1.  Denial or cancellation of registration; correction of errors
If a person's registration was denied or cancelled and the registrar of voters
determines that the registration was not processed correctly or was cancelled through an error
of the registrar of voters, the registrar of voters shall process and approve the registration or
correct the error and reinstate the registration.
Acts 2020, No. 28, §1, eff. June 4, 2020.
