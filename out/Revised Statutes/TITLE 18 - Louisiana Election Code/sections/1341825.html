§410.2. Party primary office; party primary qualifications

            Major political parties shall make nominations of candidates for party primary office as provided in this Part. Only candidates affiliated with a major political party may participate in the party primary election of the political party of the candidate's party affiliation.

            Acts 2024, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 1, §1, eff. See Act; Acts 2024, No. 640, §2, eff. See Act; Acts 2025, No. 386, §2, eff. See Act.
