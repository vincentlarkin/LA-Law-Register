§1367.10. Commencement of actions

            A. Protested solicitations and awards. Any action under R.S. 18:1367.9(A) shall be commenced within fourteen days after receipt of the decision of the commissioner under R.S. 18:1367.6(C).

            B. Debarments and suspension for cause. Any action under R.S. 18:1367.9(B) shall be commenced within sixty days after receipt of the decision of the commissioner under R.S. 18:1367.7(C).

            C. Actions under contracts or for breach of contract controversies. Any action under R.S. 18:1367.9(C) shall be commenced within sixty days after receipt of the decision of the commissioner under R.S. 18:1367.8(C).

            Acts 2025, No. 208, §1, eff. June 8, 2025.
