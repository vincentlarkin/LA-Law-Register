§1923.  Failure to comply; misfeasance; sanctions

A.  Failure to meet any of the requirements of R.S. 18:1922 shall be misfeasance in office.

B.  Failure to meet any of the requirements of R.S. 18:1922 may subject the local governing authority to the sanction of having the amount of any state revenue sharing funds payable to that governing authority withheld, reduced, or both.

Acts 1984, No. 672, §1.
