§1367.12.  Computation of time
Computation of all time intervals in this Chapter shall include Sundays and other
legal holidays.  However, if the time interval ends on a Sunday or other legal holiday, then
noon of the next legal day shall be deemed to be the end of the time interval.
Acts 2025, No. 208, §1, eff. June 8, 2025.
