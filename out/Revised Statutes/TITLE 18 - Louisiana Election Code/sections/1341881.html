§410.10. Party primary elections

            Party primary elections shall be held in an election year on the spring election dates established in R.S. 18:402.

            Acts 2024, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 1, §1, eff. See Act; Acts 2024, No. 640, §1, eff. See Act; Acts 2025, No. 386, §5(A)(3), eff. See Act.
