PART VIII.  SPECIAL PROVISIONS FOR DECEASED CANDIDATES
§1551.  Exception
Notwithstanding any contrary provision of this Chapter, the provisions of this Part
shall apply when a candidate dies leaving a deficit or surplus which would have otherwise
required reports to be filed if the candidate were not deceased.
Acts 2011, No. 208, §1; Acts 2024, No. 390, §1.
