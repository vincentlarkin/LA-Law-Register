§1400.6. Costs and expenses of primary and general elections

            A. Any local governing authority or school board which receives a request for reimbursement of election costs pursuant to this Title shall pay such reimbursement promptly.

            B. Repealed by Acts 2019, No. 374, §3, eff. June 19, 2019.

            C. If payment for any such reimbursement is not received by the secretary of state on the one hundred twentieth day following the first billing by the secretary of state, the account shall be forwarded to the attorney general for collection. The attorney general is additionally authorized to collect the actual expenses his office incurs in the collection of such account.

            Added by Acts 1983, No. 682, §1; Acts 1986, No. 669, §1; Acts 1988, No. 909, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1989; Acts 2001, No. 451, §1, eff. Jan. 12, 2004; Acts 2001, No. 1181, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2002; Acts 2019, No. 374, §3, eff. June 19, 2019.
