§1288.  Election officers; oaths

Commissioners-in-charge and commissioners shall take the oath or affirmation as provided by R.S. 18:424 and R.S. 18:425.  A commissioner may administer any oath and receive any affidavit provided for in this Chapter.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 545, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1978.
