§1300.17.  Right to contest preserved
Nothing contained in this Chapter shall be construed to deny to any public officer recalled, or whose recall is sought, the right to contest the recall, or any proceedings in relation thereto, in any court of competent jurisdiction, for fraud or other illegality.  The procedural provisions of Chapter 9, Part I, of this Code shall be applicable to such actions.
Acts 1986, No. 669, §1.
