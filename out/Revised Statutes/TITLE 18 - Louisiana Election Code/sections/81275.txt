PART II.  UNITED STATES CONGRESS
§1271.  Applicable laws
Except as otherwise specifically provided in this Part, United States senators and representatives in Congress shall be elected as provided in this Title for the election of public officers.
Acts 1976, No. 697, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1978.
