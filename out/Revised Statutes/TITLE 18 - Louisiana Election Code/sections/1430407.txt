§1367.8.  Contract and breach of contract controversies
A.  Scope.  This Section applies to a review by the commissioner of a decision under
R.S. 18:1367.4.
B.  Time limitation on filing an appeal.  The aggrieved contractor shall file an appeal
with the commissioner within seven days of the receipt of the determination under R.S.
18:1367.4(C).
C.  Decision. The commissioner or his designee shall decide within seven days the
contract or breach of contract controversy.  Any prior determination by the state chief
procurement officer or his designee shall not be final or conclusive.
D.  Notice of decision.  A copy of the decision under Subsection C of this Section
shall be mailed or otherwise furnished immediately to the contractor.
E.  Finality of decision.  A decision under Subsection C of this Section shall be final
and conclusive unless one of the following applies:
(1) The decision is fraudulent.
(2) The contractor has timely appealed an adverse decision of the commissioner to
the court in accordance with R.S. 18:1367.9(C).
Acts 2025, No. 208, §1, eff. June 8, 2025.
