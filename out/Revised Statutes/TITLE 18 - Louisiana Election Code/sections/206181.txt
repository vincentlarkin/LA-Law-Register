SUBPART G.  ADMINISTRATIVE COMPLAINT PROCEDURE
§567.  Administrative complaint procedure; scope
This Subpart provides a uniform, nondiscriminatory procedure for the resolution of any complaint alleging a violation of any provision of Title III of the Help America Vote Act of 2002 for an election for federal office, including a violation that has occurred, is occurring, or is about to occur.
Acts 2003, No. 423, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
