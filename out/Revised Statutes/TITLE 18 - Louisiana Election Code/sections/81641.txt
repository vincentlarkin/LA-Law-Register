§56.  Salary supplement
The salaries provided by law for the registrar, the chief deputy, and any
other unclassified employees may be supplemented by the parish governing
authority.
Acts 1976, No. 697, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1978; Acts 1991, No. 395, §1, eff.
July 1, 1991; Acts 1999, No. 972, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
