§1300.15.  Recall proceedings involving governor or Secretary of State
In all cases where the governor's office is involved in a recall election, the Secretary of State shall issue the proclamation and act in all matters where it is made the duty of the governor to act, and in case the office of Secretary of State is involved, then the governor shall act instead of the Secretary of State.
