§1905.  Cooperation of local governments and officials

All parish and municipal governing authorities and officials shall cooperate with the secretary of the Senate and clerk of the House of Representatives in carrying out the purposes of this Chapter and shall furnish to the secretary and clerk upon request such information as may be required by the census bureau, including but not limited to precinct maps.

Added by Acts 1976, No. 626, §1, eff. Aug. 4, 1976.  Amended by Acts 1982, No. 559, §1, eff. July 22, 1982.
