§143.  Appointment of estimators; restrictions in selection
The plaintiff shall select two or more persons to make the estimate, but at least one of them must be a licensed realtor who is familiar with land values in the vicinity of the property to be taken.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 204, §1.  Amended by Acts 1962, No. 40, §3.
