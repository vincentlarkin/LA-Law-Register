PART III.  EXPROPRIATION BY MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS
§101.  Property defined
As used in this Part, the term "property" means immovable property, including servitudes and other rights in or to immovable property, and any electric light, gas or waterworks plants.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 453, §1.
