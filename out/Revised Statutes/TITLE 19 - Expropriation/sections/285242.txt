§138.7.  Grant as additional authority
The right to take possession and title as provided in this Part is in addition to any right or authority conferred by the laws of this state under which expropriation proceedings may be conducted and shall not be construed as abrogating, eliminating, or modifying any such right or authority.
Acts 2004, No. 59, §1, eff. May 28, 2004.
