PART III-B.  EXPROPRIATION OF PROPERTY BY

A DECLARATION OF TAKING BY EAST BATON ROUGE

PARISH AND THE CITY OF BATON ROUGE

§131.  Property defined

As used in this Part, the term "property" means any portion of immovable property, including servitudes and other rights in or to immovable property, and the term "governing authority" means the governing authority of the parish of East Baton Rouge and the city of Baton Rouge.

Acts 1989, No. 224, §1, eff. June 26, 1989; Acts 2001, No. 228, §1.
