PART III-C.  EXPROPRIATION OF PROPERTY BY

A DECLARATION OF TAKING BY CITY OF ALEXANDRIA

OR CITY OF NEW ORLEANS

§132.  Property, governing authority defined

As used in this Part, the term "property" means any portion of immovable property, including servitudes, rights-of-way, and other rights in or to immovable property, where there are no buildings or structures for support or shelter; the term "governing authority" means the governing authority of the city of Alexandria or the governing authority of the city of New Orleans.

Acts 1991, No. 101, §1; Acts 2004, No. 90, §1, eff. May 28, 2004.
