PART X.  EXPROPRIATION BY A DECLARATION
OF TAKING BY CERTAIN PARISHES
§351.  Property, governing authority defined
As used in this Part:
(1)  "Governing authority" means the governing authority of the parish of St. Tammany.
(2)  "Property" means any portion of immovable property, including servitudes, rights-of-way, and other rights in or to immovable property.
Acts 2006, No. 542, §1, eff. June 22, 2006; Acts 2010, No. 198, §1, eff. June 9, 2010.
