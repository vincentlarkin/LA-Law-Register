§107.  Service of answer on plaintiff
The defendant shall file an answer within fifteen days after the service upon the defendant of the notice of the time fixed for the trial.  The answer shall be served personally or by mail on either the plaintiff or his attorney of record in the suit.
Added by Acts 1977, No. 453, §1.
