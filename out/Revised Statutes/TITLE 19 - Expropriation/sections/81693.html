§104.  Property not subject to expropriation

A.  No graveyard or cemetery shall be expropriated unless the court finds that the route of the proposed improvement cannot be diverted from that proposed by the plaintiff without great public loss or inconvenience.

B.  No mortgage shall be expropriated.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 453, §1; Acts 2014, No. 632, §1.
