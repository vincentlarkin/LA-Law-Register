PART XI. EXPROPRIATION BY A DECLARATION OF TAKING

BY THE CITY OF CENTRAL

§371. Definitions

            As used in this Part, the following terms shall mean:

            (1) "Governing authority" means the city council for the city of Central.

            (2) "Property" means any portion of immovable property, including corporeal property, servitudes, rights-of-way, and other rights in or to immovable property located within the corporate limits of the city of Central.

            Acts 2020, No. 229, §1, eff. June 11, 2020.
