TITLE 19

EXPROPRIATION

PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§1.  "Property" defined

As used in this part, the term "property" means immovable property, including servitudes and other rights in or to immovable property.

Amended by Acts 1974, Ex.Sess. No. 11, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
