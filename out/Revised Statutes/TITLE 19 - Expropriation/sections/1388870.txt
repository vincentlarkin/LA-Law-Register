PART XII.  EXPROPRIATION BY A DECLARATION OF TAKING
BY THE CITY OF MONROE
§386.  Definitions
As used in this Part, the following terms shall mean:
(1)  "Governing authority" means the city council for the city of Monroe.
(2)  "Property" means any portion of immovable property, including corporeal
property, servitudes, rights-of-way, and other rights in or to immovable property located
within the corporate limits of the city of Monroe, but excluding all public electrical utility
infrastructure, whether movable or immovable, located on or within such immovable
property.
Acts 2024, No. 717, §1, eff. June 19, 2024.
