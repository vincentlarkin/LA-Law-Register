§136.6.  Notice to defendant

Upon receipt of the deposit and the ex parte order, the clerk of court shall comply with all laws governing citation as to each named defendant.

Acts 2003, No. 984, §1; Acts 2004, No. 755, §1, eff. July 6, 2004.
