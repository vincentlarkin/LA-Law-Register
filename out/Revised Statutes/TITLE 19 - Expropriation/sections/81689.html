§10.  Transfer of ownership

Payment by the plaintiff to the owner of the compensation fixed in the final judgment to be due or the deposit thereof in the registry of the court for the benefit of the persons entitled thereto entitles the plaintiff to the property rights described in the judgment of expropriation.  

Amended by Acts 1970, No. 477, §1; Acts 1974, Ex.Sess., No. 11, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
