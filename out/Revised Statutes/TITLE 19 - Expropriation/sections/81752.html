PART III-F. EXPROPRIATION OF PROPERTY BY A DECLARATION

OF TAKING BY LINCOLN PARISH AND THE CITY OF RUSTON

§135. Property, governing authority defined

            As used in this Part, the term:

            (1) "Governing authority" means the governing authority of the parish of Lincoln or the city of Ruston.

            (2) "Property" means any portion of immovable property, including servitudes, rights-of-way, and other rights in or to immovable property.

            Acts 2003, No. 1212, §1; Acts 2008, No. 809, §1; Acts 2025, No. 484, §1.
