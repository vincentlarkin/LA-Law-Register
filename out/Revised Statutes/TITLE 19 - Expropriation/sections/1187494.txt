§381.  Grant as additional authority
The right to take possession and title in advance of final judgment, provided herein,
is in addition to any right or authority conferred by the laws of this state under which
expropriation proceedings may be conducted by municipalities, and shall not be construed
as abrogating, eliminating, or modifying any such right or authority.
Acts 2020, No. 229, §1, eff. June 11, 2020.
