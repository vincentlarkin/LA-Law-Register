§404.  Falsification of records
Whoever knowingly makes any false statement or falsifies or permits to be falsified any record or records of this system in an attempt to defraud the system shall be fined not more than five hundred dollars or imprisoned for not more than six months, or both.
Acts 1972, No. 135, §1, eff. July 26, 1972; Redesignated from R.S. 42:544 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
