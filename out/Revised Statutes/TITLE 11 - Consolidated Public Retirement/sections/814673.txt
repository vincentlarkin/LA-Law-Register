PART III.  NONHAZARDOUS DUTY SUBPLAN
§2242.1.  Creation of Nonhazardous Duty Subplan
A.  The Nonhazardous Duty Subplan is created within the Municipal Police Employees' Retirement System for members whose first employment making them eligible for membership in  the system occurred on or after January 1, 2013, in nonhazardous duty positions as defined in this Part.
B.  Any other provisions of this Chapter or any other laws to the contrary notwithstanding, the retirement of Nonhazardous Duty Subplan members shall be governed by the provisions of this Part; however, if provisions of this Chapter cover matters not specifically addressed by the provisions of this Part or if any of the provisions of this Chapter are made applicable in this Part, then those provisions shall apply to members governed by this Part.
Acts 2012, No. 522, §1.
