§2177.  Duties of executive director
The executive director shall be the fund administrator and shall perform those duties delegated and assigned by the board of trustees for the efficient operation of all business of the fund.
Acts 1991, No. 61, §1, eff. July 1, 1991; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1455 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
