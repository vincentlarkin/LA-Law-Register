§1548.  Payment of warrants

All monies paid from the fund shall be paid by the secretary of the board only upon warrants signed by the president of the board and countersigned by the secretary of the board.  No warrants shall be drawn except by orders of the board, duly entered upon the records of the proceedings of the board.  

Acts 1986, No. 1063, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 1986; Redesignated from R.S. 13:958 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
