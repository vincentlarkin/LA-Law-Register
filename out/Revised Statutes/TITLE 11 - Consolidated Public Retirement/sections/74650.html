§1331.  Exemption from state income taxes; garnishment

All retirement income payable to retirees and their beneficiaries shall be exempt from state income taxes.  All retirement benefits payable to retirees shall be subject to garnishment or court-ordered assignment only as provided for in R.S. 11:292.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 243, §2; Acts 1986, No. 767, §6; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1442.1 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2007, No. 333, §1, eff. July 1, 2007.
