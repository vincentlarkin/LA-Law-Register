§2165.3.  Eligibility for membership

Each person who would be eligible for Tier 1 membership but whose first employment making him eligible for membership in this system occurred on or after January 1, 2013, shall become a member of the Tier 2 of the system as a condition of employment.

Acts 2012, No. 719, §1, eff. June 30, 2012.
