PART III.  CREDITABLE SERVICE

§751.  Rounding of service credit at the time of retirement

At the time of retirement or beginning participation in the Deferred Retirement Option Plan, any fractional part of a year of the member's total service credit shall be rounded to the closest one-tenth of a year but shall not exceed one hundred percent of a year.

Acts 1997, No. 105, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Acts 1997, No. 1353, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Acts 2001, No. 1174, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
