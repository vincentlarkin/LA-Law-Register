§1164.  Voting power of trustees; votes necessary for action of board
Each trustee shall be entitled to one vote in the board.  Six votes shall be necessary for a decision by the trustees at any meeting of the board.
Redesignated from R.S. 17:934 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2012, No. 113, §1, eff. June 30, 2012.
