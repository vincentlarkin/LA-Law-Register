§1764.  Maximum benefit
The actual retirement benefit paid to any retiree of this system at the time of retirement, whether for normal or disability retirement and whether the benefit is the full maximum allowable or reduced due to the selection of an optional mode of benefit payments as allowed by R.S. 11:1757, shall not exceed one hundred percent of the retiree's final salary or final compensation, whichever is greater.
Acts 1992, No. 1027, §1.
