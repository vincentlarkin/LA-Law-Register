§1345.5.  Retirement benefit

A member shall receive a retirement equal to three and one-third percent of average compensation for every year of creditable service in the plan, not to exceed one hundred percent of the member's average compensation.

Acts 2010, No. 992, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
