§923.  Administration

The Teachers' Retirement System of Louisiana or any successor shall provide for the administration and maintenance of the optional retirement plan.  

Acts 1989, No. 90, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 17:773 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
