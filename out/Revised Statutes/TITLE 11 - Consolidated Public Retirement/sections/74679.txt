CHAPTER 6.  PENSIONS FOR CONFEDERATE VETERANS
AND WIDOWS OF CONFEDERATE VETERANS
§1391.  Pension system merged with system of assistance to the aged
The system of pensions for Confederate veterans and widows of Confederate veterans is merged and consolidated with the system of financial assistance to aged, needy persons.
Redesignated from R.S. 29:461 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
