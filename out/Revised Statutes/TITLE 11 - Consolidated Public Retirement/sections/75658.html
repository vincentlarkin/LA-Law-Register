§3854.  Coverage by other plans; effect

In the event any such official or employee is protected or covered by any other pension plan or by Federal Social Security, then, and in that event, such official or employee shall be entitled to the difference between the payments made by such other plan or Social Security and the payments due under the municipal plan established by said ordinance.  

Acts 1954, No. 511, §4; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1784 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
