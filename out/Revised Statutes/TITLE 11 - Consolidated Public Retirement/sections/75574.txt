§3688.1.  Amendments
A.  An action of the board of trustees with respect to employee contributions as
provided in R.S. 11:154 and actuarial assumptions as provided in R.S. 11:3688(D), shall be
considered amendments to the provisions of this plan.
B.  No amendment to the retirement system or plan shall operate to deprive any
member of a benefit to which he is already entitled.
Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 97, §1, eff. May 5, 1998; Acts 2014, No. 648, §2, eff.
July 1, 2015.
