§2267.  Limitations of membership
No other provisions of law in any other statute which provides wholly or partly at the expense of the state of Louisiana for pensions or retirement benefits for employees of the several parishes or any parish of the state of Louisiana, their widows or other dependents shall apply to members or beneficiaries of the retirement system established by this Chapter, their widows or other dependents.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 434, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2163 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
