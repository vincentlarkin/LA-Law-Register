§901.23.  Monthly payments; scope of part
The supplement provided by this Part shall be paid monthly in equal payments.
Nothing in this Part is intended to limit in any way the benefit provided for a member by either the teachers' retirement system of Louisiana or the teachers' retirement fund for Orleans Parish.
