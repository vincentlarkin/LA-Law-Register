§3803.  Special benefits; vote at special or annual meeting

The Board of Trustees shall have complete discretion and authority to secure information regarding worker's compensation and Social Security benefits.  If the employee fails to furnish full and complete information regarding Social Security benefits after having been requested to do so by the Board of Trustees, the Board shall have authority to terminate the annual pension or disability allowance or survivor's benefit until such time as the Social Security information is furnished.  In all such cases the decision of the Board of Trustees shall be final.  

Designated from Acts 1956, No. 283, §13 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
