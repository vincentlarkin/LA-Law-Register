§2063.  Service on which retirement allowances are based
Creditable service at retirement on which the retirement allowance of a member shall
be based shall consist of the membership service rendered by him since he last became a
member, and also, if the member has a prior service certificate which is in full force and
effect, the amount of service certified on his prior service certificate.  Any unused sick leave
and any unused annual leave at the date of retirement shall be used in the calculation of the
retirement allowance; however, for any person whose system membership first begins on or
after July 1, 2024, unused leave shall not be used for purposes of attaining eligibility.
Acts 1954, No. 215, §4(5).  Amended by Acts 1971, No. 79, §1; Acts 1974, No. 388,
