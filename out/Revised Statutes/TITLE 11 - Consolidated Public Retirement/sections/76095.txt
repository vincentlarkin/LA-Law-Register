§952.62.  Actuary; appointment; duties and powers
The board of supervisors shall designate an actuary who shall be the technical advisor of the board of supervisors on matters regarding the operation of the funds created by the provisions of this part, and shall perform such other duties as are required in connection therewith.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1643 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
