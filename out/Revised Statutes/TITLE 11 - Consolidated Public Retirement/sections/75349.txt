§3297.  Member participation
No fireman shall be eligible to participate in any of the benefits of this Part or to vote on any matter arising hereunder, unless he is participating or has satisfactorily completed participation in the fund herein referred to by making the contributions hereinabove set forth and provided for in this Part.
Acts 1976, No. 615, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2144 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
