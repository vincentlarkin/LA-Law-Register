§1395.  Payment of pensions

Pensions granted to Confederate veterans and to widows of Confederate veterans shall be paid monthly or quarterly on the warrant or order of the division out of money appropriated for pensions.  

Amended by Acts 1974, No. 294, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 29:466 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
