§807.  Employee contributions
Each member covered by R.S. 11:801 shall contribute an amount equal to five percent of his earnable compensation to the retirement system as the employee contribution.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 718, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 17:642.6 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
