§1751.2.  Membership in lieu of participation in another retirement system
Any person who is a member of the Municipal Employees' Retirement System, who
has creditable membership service of at least five years in this system, and who becomes
employed in a position where he is no longer eligible for membership in this system, but is
eligible for membership in the Louisiana State Employees' Retirement System, shall have the
right to remain a member of this system in lieu of membership in the Louisiana State
Employees' Retirement System by filing a notice of election to remain in this system, in
writing, with the board of trustees within thirty days after the effective date of employment.
Such election shall be irrevocable.
Acts 2025, No. 232, §1, eff. June 11, 2025.
