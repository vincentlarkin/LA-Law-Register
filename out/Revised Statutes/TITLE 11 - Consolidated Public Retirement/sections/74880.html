BENEFITS; CONTRIBUTIONS

§1781.  Eligibility for normal retirement

Any member of Plan A shall be eligible to retire if he has at least:

(1)  Twenty-five years of creditable service, regardless of age.

(2)  Ten years of creditable service, and is at least age sixty.

Acts 1978, No. 788, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7231 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 1999, No. 240, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
