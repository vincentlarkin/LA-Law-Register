§125.  Meetings

The committee shall meet as necessary, and shall assist and report to the  House and Senate committees on retirement and the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget.

Acts 1988, No. 81, §2, eff. July 1, 1988; Acts 1991, No. 1038, §1; Acts 2010, No. 874, §1, eff. July 1, 2010.
