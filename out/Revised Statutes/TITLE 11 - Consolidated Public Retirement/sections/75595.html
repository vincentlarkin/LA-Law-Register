§3721.  Disbursements upon warrants

Money shall be paid by the treasurer from the fund only upon warrants signed by the president and countersigned by the secretary.  No warrant shall be drawn except by order of the board.  

Acts 1968, No. 641, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2361 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
