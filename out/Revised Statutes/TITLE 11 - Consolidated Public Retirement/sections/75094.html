§2217.  City of Shreveport; mandatory membership for certain employees

Notwithstanding the provision of R.S. 11:2214(A)(1), R.S. 11:3711, or any other law to the contrary, all persons who become employees, as defined in R.S. 11:2213, of the city of Shreveport on and after July 12, 1977, shall become members of this system as a condition of their employment, if they are under fifty years of age on the date of employment.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 496, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2373.3 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
