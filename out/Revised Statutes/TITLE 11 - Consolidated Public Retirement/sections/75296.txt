§3204.  Authority to alienate property
The Board of Trustees of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund of the City of Bossier City, shall have, and the same is hereby granted unto them, full power and authority to sell, mortgage, lease, let or otherwise alienate or dispose of any and all property, real or personal, tangible or intangible, which is now in the possession of or belongs to or shall become the property or come into the possession of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund of the City of Bossier City and/or of the Board of Trustees thereof.
Designated from Acts 1952, No. 15, §15 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
