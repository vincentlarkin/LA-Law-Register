§2055.  Termination of membership

Should any member in any period of six consecutive years after becoming a member be absent from service for more than five years, or should he withdraw his accumulated contributions, or should he become a beneficiary or die, he shall thereupon cease to be a member.  

Acts 1954, No. 215, §3(6); Redesignated from R.S. 18:1685 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
