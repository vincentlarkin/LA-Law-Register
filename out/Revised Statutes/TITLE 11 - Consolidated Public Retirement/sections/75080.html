§2183.  Time for filing claim

Any claim for death or disability benefit shall be filed with the board of trustees within one year from the date of disability or death.  

Added by Acts 1954, No. 239, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1459 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
