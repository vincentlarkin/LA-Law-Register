§1904.  Agreement amendments

If an employer wishes to transfer its employees from Plan B or Plan C to Plan A, then an amendment to the agreement must be executed subject to the same procedures and payments for existing accrued liability as if a new agreement had been executed.

Acts 1979, No. 765, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980; Redesignated from R.S. 33:6104 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 1997, No. 867, §1, eff. July 1, 1997.
