§2052.  Persons not eligible for membership
No elected or appointed official of this state or of any parish thereof who under any provisions of the constitution or laws of this state is subject to retirement with pay is eligible for membership in this system.
Acts 1954, No. 215, §3(36).  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 526, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 18:1682 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
