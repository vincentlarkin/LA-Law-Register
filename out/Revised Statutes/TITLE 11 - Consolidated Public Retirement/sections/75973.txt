PART VII-D.  RETIREMENT AND DISABILITY PAYMENTS
PAYABLE BY PARISHES (ORLEANS EXCEPTED)
§901.35.  Limitations of provisions
The provisions of this Part shall not apply to the Parish of Orleans.
