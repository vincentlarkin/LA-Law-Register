§3387.  Record of retired firefighters
There shall be kept in the office of the board of trustees by the secretary-treasurer, a book to be known as the "list of retired firefighters." This book shall give a full and complete history and record of the action of the board of trustees in retiring any person under this Part.  The records shall give names, date of joining the department, date of retirement, and the reason thereof, if any, and all persons retired.
Amended by Acts 1969, No. 120, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2118 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
