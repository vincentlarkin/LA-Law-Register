§951.50.  Pensions payable from accumulation account; transfers to reserve account

A.  All pensions payable on account of participants entitled to prior service credit shall be payable from the pension accumulation account.

B.  Upon the retirement of a participant not entitled to credit for prior service, an amount equal to his pension reserve shall be transferred from the pension accumulation account to the pension reserve account.
