PART II.  MEMBERSHIP

§721.  Eligibility requirement for membership

All teachers shall become members of this system as a condition of their employment.

Acts 1988, No. 11, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 17:591 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 1992, No. 555, §§1 and 2, eff. July 1, 1992; Acts 1995, No. 572, §1, eff. July 1, 1995.
