§951.44.  Disposition of employee contributions upon termination of employment or death of employee

The regular contributions of a participant withdrawn in the event he ceases to be an employee, or paid in the event of his death to his succession or designated beneficiary, shall be paid from the annuity savings account.

Redesignated from R.S. 17:1074 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
