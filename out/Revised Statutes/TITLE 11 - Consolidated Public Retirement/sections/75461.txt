§3503.  Fund not to be commingled
All funds derived and held or invested under the provisions of this Subpart shall be administered as a sacred trust for the sole purposes stated in this Subpart and at no time commingled or combined with any other fund.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2223 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
