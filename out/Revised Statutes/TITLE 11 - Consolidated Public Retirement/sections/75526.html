§3614.  Computation of length of service

The length of service for retirement or disability benefits under this Subpart shall commence on the first day of employment by the police department and shall terminate on that date of retirement, unless otherwise provided by this Subpart.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 549, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2385.13 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
