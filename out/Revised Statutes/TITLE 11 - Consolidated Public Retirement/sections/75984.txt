§922.  Purpose
The purpose of the optional retirement plan is to provide retirement and death benefits to the participants while affording the maximum portability of these benefits to the participants.
Acts 1989, No. 90, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 17:772 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 1999, No. 1320, §1, eff. July 12, 1999.
