§3318.  Secretary; duties; powers
The secretary of the said board of directors shall keep in books provided for the purpose, a full and complete record of all proceedings of the board of directors, particularly with reference to investment of funds belonging to the said Pension and Relief Fund as hereinafter provided.  He shall file and keep all correspondence of the Board, keep minutes of all of its meetings, and shall perform such other duties as may be assigned him by the board of directors, including the preparation of warrants for the various disbursements from the said Fund and the keeping of an accurate record thereof.  The said Board shall fix at their discretion a salary to be received by said Secretary as compensation for his services.
Designated from Acts 1944, No. 186, §8(1) by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
