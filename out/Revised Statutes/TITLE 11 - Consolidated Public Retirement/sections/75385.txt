§3368.  Proceeds of sales of condemned apparatus paid into fund
All proceeds of sales of condemned apparatus and other personal property in use by the said department, shall be paid into the pension and relief fund.
Amended by Acts 1969, No. 120, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2105 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
