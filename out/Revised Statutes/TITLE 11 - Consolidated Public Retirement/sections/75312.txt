§3235.  Member participation
Members of the said fire department, as used in this Part shall constitute every man actively in the employ of said fire department of the City of Houma and who is qualified under the Municipal Fire and Police Civil Service System of the City of Houma, Louisiana.
Designated from Acts 1973, No. 139, §15 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
