§3800.  Exemption from seizure and attachment

 Except as provided in R.S. 11:292, no portion of the Electrical Workers' Pension and Relief Fund shall, before or after its order for distribution is issued by the Board of Trustees to the person or persons entitled thereto under the provisions of this Part, be held, seized, taken, subjected to, detained, or levied upon by virtue of any attachment, garnishment, execution, injunction, writ, order, decree, or any other process whatsoever, issued out of, or by, any court of this state for the payment or satisfaction, in whole or in part, of any debt, damage, demand, claim, judgment, or decree, against any beneficiary of such fund, but shall be exempt therefrom.  The fund shall be sacredly kept, held, and distributed for the purposes named in this Part, and for no other purposes whatsoever.

Designated from Acts 1956, No. 283, §10 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2010, No. 634, §1, eff. July 1, 2010.
