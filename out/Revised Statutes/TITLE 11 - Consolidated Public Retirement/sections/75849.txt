§769.  Payment of employee's accumulated contributions guaranteed
In no case shall the system pay total benefits of an amount less than the total of the employee's accumulated contributions.  Where the sum of the regular benefits, Deferred Retirement Option Plan payments, and survivor benefits is less than the employee's total contributions, the difference shall be paid to the estate of the deceased member.
Acts 2004, No. 747, §1, eff. July 6, 2004.
