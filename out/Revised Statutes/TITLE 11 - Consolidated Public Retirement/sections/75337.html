§3286.  Treasurer; duties; bond

The director of administration of the city of Lafayette, shall be the treasurer of said board of directors and custodian of all funds belonging to the fund.  He shall deposit the funds in such depositories as are designated by the board and shall disburse funds belonging to the fund only upon warrant of the secretary of the board, countersigned by the president thereof.  The official bond of said director of administration shall be security for the faithful performance, by the director of administration, of his duties as treasurer of the board of directors, or in lieu thereof he may furnish a valid surety bond in the sum of ten thousand dollars.  In that event, the premium for the bond shall be paid out of the fund.  

Acts 1976, No. 615, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2135 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
