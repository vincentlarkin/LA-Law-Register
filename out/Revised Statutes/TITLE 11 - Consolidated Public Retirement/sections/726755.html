§1345.6.  Back-Deferred Retirement Option Program

A member, except for a member who retires pursuant to R.S. 11:1345.4(3), shall have the option of participating in the Back-Deferred Retirement Option Program in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 11:1312.1.

Acts 2010, No. 992, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011; Acts 2013, No. 220, §3, eff. June 11, 2013.
