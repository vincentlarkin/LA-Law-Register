§1653.  Board of trustees; rules and regulations

Subject to the limitations of this Chapter, the board of trustees shall, from time to time, establish rules and regulations for the administration of the funds created by this Chapter for the transaction of its business.  

Added by Acts 1956, No. 56, §6(7); Redesignated from R.S. 16:1063 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
