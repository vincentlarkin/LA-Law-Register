§1354.  Retirement pay and source to remain the same

Any judge who is or may hereafter become retired under the provisions of this Part shall continue to receive the same retirement pay which was received at the time of retirement and from the same sources.  

Redesignated from R.S. 13:30(D) by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
