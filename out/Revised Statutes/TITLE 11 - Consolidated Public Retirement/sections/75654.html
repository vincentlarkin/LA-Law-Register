§3846.  Liberal construction

This Part and any ordinance adopted in pursuance thereof shall be liberally construed in order to carry out the beneficent provisions of this Part and of such ordinance.  

Redesignated from R.S. 33:1766 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
