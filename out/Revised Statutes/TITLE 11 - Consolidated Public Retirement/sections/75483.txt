§3543.  City of Alexandria; composition of board of trustees
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the mayor, president of the city council, superintendent of the police department, and four members of the police department, elected by a majority vote of the members of the department, shall constitute a board of trustees for the fund in the city of Alexandria.  The mayor shall be president of the board.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 539, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2224.1 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
