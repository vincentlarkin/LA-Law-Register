§1803.  Eligibility for disability retirement
A member shall be eligible to retire and receive a disability benefit if he has at least ten years of creditable service, is not eligible for normal retirement, and suffers disability.
Acts 1978, No. 788, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7273 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
