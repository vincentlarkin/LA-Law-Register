PART VII.  METHOD OF FINANCING

§1691.  Funds to which assets credited

All of the assets of the retirement system shall be credited according to the purpose for which they are held to one of five funds, namely, the Annuity Savings Fund, the Pension Accumulation Fund, the Pension Reserve Fund, the Deferred Retirement Option Plan Account, and the Expense Fund.

Added by Acts 1956, No. 56, §8; Redesignated from R.S. 16:1101 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 1993, No. 234, §1, eff. June 1, 1993.
