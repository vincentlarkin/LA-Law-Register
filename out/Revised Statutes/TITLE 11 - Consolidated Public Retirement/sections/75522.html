§3610.  First payment by city

The first payment, or contributions, to be made by the city of Lafayette shall be paid as soon as the first board of directors are elected and have begun to function; provided that payments shall have been collected from the members of the police department.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 549, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2385.9 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
