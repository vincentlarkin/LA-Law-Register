§951.5.  Receipt of both salary and retirement allowances prohibited

No beneficiary of the retirement system shall be paid for any service as an employee of the Orleans Parish School Board after the date of the first payment of such retirement allowance except as provided for in this Part.

Redesignated from R.S. 17:1015 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
