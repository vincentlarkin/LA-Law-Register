§951.74.  Service on which retirement allowances are based
Creditable service at retirement, on which the retirement allowance of a participant is based, shall consist of subsequent service rendered by him since last becoming a participant, and also if he has prior service credit, which is in full force and effect, the amount of service certified on his prior service certificate.
