SUBPART I.  POLICE PENSION FUND OF THE

CITY OF SHREVEPORT

§3711.  Police pension fund for the police department of the city of Shreveport; creation

There is created a police pension fund for the police department of the city of Shreveport for pensioning all police officers of the police department of the city of Shreveport, their widows and children.  For purposes of this Subpart police officers shall be defined as those members of the Shreveport Police Department who meet the definition of police officers for the purpose of civil service rating and are so rated.  

Acts 1968, No. 641, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2351 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
