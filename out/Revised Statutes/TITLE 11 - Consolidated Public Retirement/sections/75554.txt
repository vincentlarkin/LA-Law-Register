§3655.  Records of retired policemen
There shall be kept in the office of the board a book to be known as "The list of retired police officers." This book shall give a complete record of all pensions being paid at the time of the organization of the board and of the action of the board in retiring any persons under this Subpart.  Such records shall give names, date of joining the department, date of retirement, and the reason therefor, as to any person retired.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2299 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
