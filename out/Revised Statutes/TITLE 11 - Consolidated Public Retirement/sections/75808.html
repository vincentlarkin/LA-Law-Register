§706.  Application of other laws

No other provision of law which provides wholly or partly at the expense of the State of Louisiana for pensions or retirement benefits for teachers, their widows, or other dependents, shall apply to members or beneficiaries of the retirement system established by this Chapter, their widows or other dependents.  

Redesignated from R.S. 17:575 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
