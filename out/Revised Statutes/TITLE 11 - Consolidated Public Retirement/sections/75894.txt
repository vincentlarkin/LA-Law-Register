PART V.  ADMINISTRATION
§821.  Board of trustees; administrative powers in general
The general administration and responsibility for the proper operation of the retirement system and for making effective the provisions of this Chapter are vested in a board of trustees which shall be organized immediately after a majority of the trustees provided for in this Part shall have qualified and taken the oath of office.
Redesignated from R.S. 17:651 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
