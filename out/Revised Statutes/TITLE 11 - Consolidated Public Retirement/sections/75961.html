§901.24.  Administration of benefits by board of trustees of state teachers' retirement system

The board of trustees of the teachers' retirement system of Louisiana, under such rules and regulations as the said board of trustees may adopt not inconsistent herewith, shall administer all the benefits provided by this Part.
