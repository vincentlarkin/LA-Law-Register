§3181.  Return to service; cancellation of pension

The Board shall at all times have the power to summon any pensioner retired for disability, inquire as to whether his disability has continued, cancel his pension if it finds he is capable of returning to active service.  

Designated from Acts 1944, No. 193, §10 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
