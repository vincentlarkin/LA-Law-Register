§1822.  Board officers
The board shall elect from its members a chairman and vice chairman to serve at the board's pleasure.  They shall perform the duty designated by the board and shall serve without compensation.
Acts 1978, No. 788, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7312 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
