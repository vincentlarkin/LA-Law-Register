§1754.  Termination of membership

An employee shall cease to be a member upon the occurrence of any of the following circumstances:

(1)  He resigns, is dismissed, or is otherwise separated from service.

(2)  He withdraws from active service with a retirement allowance granted under the provisions of this Chapter.  

Acts 1978, No. 788, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7193 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
