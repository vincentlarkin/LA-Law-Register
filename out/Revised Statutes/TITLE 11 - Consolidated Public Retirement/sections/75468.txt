§3510.  Money available for pensions
The interest received from the investments after the fund has reached fifty thousand dollars or fifteen thousand dollars, as the case may be, and all money received from any source, shall be applicable to the payment of pensions and relief under this Subpart.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2230 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
