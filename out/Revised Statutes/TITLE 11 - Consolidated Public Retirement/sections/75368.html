§3326.  Computation of length of service

The methods of computing the periods of service and seniority under this Part shall be determined by the board of directors and rules shall be passed by it relative thereto.  

Designated from Acts 1944, No. 186, §18 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
