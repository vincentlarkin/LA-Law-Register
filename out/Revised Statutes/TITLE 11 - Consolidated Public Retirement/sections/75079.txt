§2182.  Exemption from execution
Any annuity, retirement allowance or benefits, or refund of
contributions, or any optional benefit or any other benefit paid to any person
under the provisions of the Sheriffs' Pension and Relief Fund is exempt from
any state or municipal tax and is exempt from levy and sale, garnishment,
attachment, or any other process whatsoever, except as provided in R.S.
11:292, and is unassignable.
Added by Acts 1954, No. 239, §1.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 261,
§1; Acts 1986, No. 767, §5; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1458 by Acts 1991,
No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
