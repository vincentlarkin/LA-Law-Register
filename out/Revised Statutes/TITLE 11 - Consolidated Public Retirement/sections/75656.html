§3852.  Eligibility

Such pension plans shall apply to any employee or official, elected or appointed, who has reached the minimum age of sixty years and has been an officer or employee of such municipality for at least twenty-four years.

Acts 1954, No. 511, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1782 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
