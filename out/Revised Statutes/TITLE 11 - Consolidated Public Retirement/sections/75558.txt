§3659.  Board may pay benefits monthly
All of the benefits provided in this Subpart whether referred to as an "annuity" or "per annum", may, in the discretion of the board, be paid in equal monthly installments.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2303 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
