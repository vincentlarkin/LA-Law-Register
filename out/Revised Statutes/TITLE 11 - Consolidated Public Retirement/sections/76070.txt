SUBPART C.  CREDITED SERVICE
§952.21.  Service after effective date
All service as a regular employee, whether on a full-time or part-time basis, rendered by a member prior to retirement, since he became a member, or since he last became a member in the event of a break in membership, for which he has made contributions to the system in accordance with R.S. 11:952.52, shall be recognized as credited service.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1619 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
