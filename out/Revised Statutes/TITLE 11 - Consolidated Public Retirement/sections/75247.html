§3118.  Exemption of benefits

Any annuity, retirement allowance or benefits, or refund of contributions, or any optional benefit or any other benefit paid to any person under the provisions of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund for the city of Alexandria is exempt from any state income tax.  

Designated from Acts 1990, No. 972, §1 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
