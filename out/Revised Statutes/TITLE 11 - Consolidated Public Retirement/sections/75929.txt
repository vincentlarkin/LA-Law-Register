§883.  Unexpended balance in expense fund
The unexpended balance in the expense fund on June thirtieth of each year shall be paid by the board of trustees into the pension accumulation fund.
Added by Acts 1960, No. 401, §2.  Amended by Acts 1966, No. 81, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 17:701.1 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
