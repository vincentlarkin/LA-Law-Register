§3657.  Records to be kept by board; annual audit
All records shall be kept by the board through their properly designated officer, who shall maintain a complete record of all meetings and proceedings.  The commissioner of finance of the city of New Orleans shall annually audit the records of the fund.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2301 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
