§3566.  City of Alexandria; exemption of benefits

Any annuity, retirement allowance or benefits, or refund of contributions, or any optional benefit or any other benefit paid to any person under the provisions of the Policemen's Pension and Relief Fund for the city of Alexandria is exempt from any state income tax.  

Acts 1990, No. 972, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2224.3 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
