§952.22.  Service prior to the effective date
For any employee who elected pursuant to R.S. 11:952.12 to become a member of the funded system as of the effective date or for former employees who are subsequently reemployed, credited service shall also include any service prior to the effective date which was recognized under the nonfunded university retirement plan as service for computation of a benefit allowance, except as provided in R.S. 11:952.41.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1620 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
