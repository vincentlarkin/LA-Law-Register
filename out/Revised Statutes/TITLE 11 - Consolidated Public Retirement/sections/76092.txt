§952.54.  Operating expenses
The board of supervisors shall include an estimated amount required for operating expenses of the system in the appropriation for the operating expenses of the university.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1639 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
