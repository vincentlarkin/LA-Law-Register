§1738.  Application of amendments
Amendments to this Chapter which have been or may be enacted shall be applicable only to persons who, on or after the effective date thereof, are in service as participating members, unless the amendatory act specifies otherwise.
Acts 1978, No. 788, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7158 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
