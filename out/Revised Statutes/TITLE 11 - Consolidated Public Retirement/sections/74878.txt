§1768.  Direct rollover of eligible rollover distributions
A distributee may elect, at the time and in the manner prescribed by the board, to have any portion of an eligible rollover distribution paid directly to an eligible retirement plan specified by the distributee in a direct rollover, notwithstanding any other provision of this system to the contrary that would otherwise limit a distributee's election under this Section.
Acts 1999, No. 398, §1.
