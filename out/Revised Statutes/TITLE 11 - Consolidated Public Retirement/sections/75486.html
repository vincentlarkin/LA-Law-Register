§3546.  City of Lake Charles; salary deduction

With respect to the Police Pension and Relief Fund for the city of Lake Charles, each member of the fund shall be assessed seven percent of his salary monthly.  

Amended by Acts 1954, No. 460, §1; Acts 1962, No. 236, §1; Acts 1976, No. 607, §1; Acts 1981, No. 320, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2225(A)(3) by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
