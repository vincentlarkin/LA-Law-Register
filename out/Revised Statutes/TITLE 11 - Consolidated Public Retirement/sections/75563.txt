SUBPART G.  HARBOR POLICE RETIREMENT PLAN
(PORT OF NEW ORLEANS) IN THE LOUISIANA STATE EMPLOYEES'
RETIREMENT SYSTEM
§3681.  Name and date of establishment
A retirement plan is hereby established and placed under the management of the
Board of Trustees for the purpose of providing retirement allowances and other benefits
under the provisions of this Subpart for Commissioned Members of the Harbor Police
Department of the Port of New Orleans hired on or before June 30, 2014.
Acts 1971, No. 80, § 1, designated by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts
2014, No. 648, §2 eff. June 30, 2014.
