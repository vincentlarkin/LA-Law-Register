§610. Retirement; purchase of service firearm

            Any member of the Louisiana State Employees' Retirement System who is a commissioned officer employed by the Department of Agriculture and Forestry pursuant to R.S. 3:734 or 4274.1, who has at least ten years of active service, and who retires under the provisions of R.S. 11:441 shall be entitled to purchase his service firearm at fair market value upon retirement, subject to the approval of the commissioner of agriculture and forestry.

            Acts 2025, No. 58, §1, eff. June 4, 2025.
