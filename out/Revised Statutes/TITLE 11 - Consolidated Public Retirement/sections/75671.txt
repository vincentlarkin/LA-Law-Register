SUBTITLE II.  STATE SYSTEMS
CHAPTER 1.  LOUISIANA STATE EMPLOYEES'
RETIREMENT SYSTEM
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§401.  Establishment of retirement system
There shall be a retirement system which shall be under the management of a board of trustees for the purpose of providing retirement allowances and other benefits under the provisions of this chapter for state officers and employees and their beneficiaries.
Acts 1972, No. 135, §1, eff. July 26, 1972; Redesignated from R.S. 42:541 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
