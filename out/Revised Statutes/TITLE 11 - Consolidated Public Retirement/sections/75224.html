§3052.  Error

Should any change or error in the records result in any member or beneficiary receiving from the system more or less than he would have been entitled to receive had the record been correct, or a mistake not made, the board shall correct such error and as far as practicable shall adjust the payment so that the benefit to which such member or beneficiary was correctly entitled shall be paid.

Designated from Acts 1952, No. 184, §22, by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
