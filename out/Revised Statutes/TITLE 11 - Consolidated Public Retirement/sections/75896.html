§823.  Restriction on election of parish or city superintendent

No parish or city superintendent of schools shall henceforth be elected to any vacancy or recurring term of any position provided in R.S. 11:822(A)(7) on the Board of Trustees of the Teachers' Retirement System of Louisiana.  

Added by Acts 1978, No. 111, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 17:652.1 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
