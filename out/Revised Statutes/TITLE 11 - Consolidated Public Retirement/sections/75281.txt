§3180.  Member participation; limitations
After the first Tuesday in December, 1944 no member who enters the service of said Fire Department when over the age of thirty years shall be entitled to retirement pension or disability payments; nor shall the widow and children of said member be eligible for any benefits under this Part.
Designated from Acts 1944, No. 193, §9 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
