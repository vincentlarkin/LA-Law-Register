§3299.  Violations; penalties

Whoever willfully violates any provision of this Act shall be fined not less than one thousand dollars nor more than ten thousand dollars or imprisoned not longer than one year, or both.  

Acts 1976, No. 615, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2146 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
