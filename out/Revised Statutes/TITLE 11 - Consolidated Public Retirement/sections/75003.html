§2035.  Limitation of membership

No other provisions of law in any other status which provides wholly or partly at the expense of the state of Louisiana for pensions or retirement benefits for employees of the several parishes or any parish of the state of Louisiana, their widows, or other dependents, shall apply to members or beneficiaries of the retirement system established by this Chapter, their widows or other dependents.  

Acts 1954, No. 215, §11; Redesignated from R.S. 18:1655 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
