§264.1.  Liabilities; discretionary control

Legislators, state officials, system attorneys, accountants, and actuaries shall not be considered fiduciaries unless they exercise discretionary control over the management or administration of the system or some authority or control over system assets.  

Acts 1992, No. 1046, §1, eff. July 1, 1993.
