§3717.  Deposit of money collected for fund
Not later than fifteen days after the close of the fiscal year, the treasurer of the board shall deposit to the credit of the fund, in the bank designated by the board, the amounts prescribed in R.S. 11:3502 and all money collected under this Subpart and turned into the municipal treasury during the preceding year.
Acts 1968, No. 641, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2357 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
