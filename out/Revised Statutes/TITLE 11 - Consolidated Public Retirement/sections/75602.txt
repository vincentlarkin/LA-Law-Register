§3728.  Credit for military service
Time served in the military service of the United States government to fulfill military obligation shall constitute "service" in the police department as used in this Subpart.  Any re-enlistment time shall not be considered "service" under the terms of this Subpart upon re-enlistment such persons shall forfeit any rights under this Subpart.  Such time in military service must interrupt the member's employment in the Shreveport Police Department.
Acts 1968, No. 641, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2368 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
