SUBPART E.  PARTICIPATION
§951.61.  New employees; participation at time of employment
All persons who become employees of the Orleans Parish School Board after the effective date of the establishment of this Retirement System shall become participants as of the date of their employment.
Redesignated from R.S. 17:1101 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
