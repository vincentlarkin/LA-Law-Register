§23.  Funded percentage; state systems
Except as otherwise provided in this Title, "funded percentage" for each state public
retirement system shall mean the valuation assets used to determine the actuarially required
contributions pursuant to R.S. 11:102 divided by the accrued liability of the system
determined by utilizing the funding method established in R.S. 11:22.
Acts 2016, No. 95, §1, eff. June 30, 2016.
