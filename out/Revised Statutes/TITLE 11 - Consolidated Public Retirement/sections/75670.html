§3870.  Officers of city court

The provisions of this Part shall include and be extended to any officer of any city court of any city of over twenty-five thousand population, whose salary is paid by such city and who is not a member of any other retirement system.  

Acts 1958, No. 65, §11; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1800 by Acts  1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
