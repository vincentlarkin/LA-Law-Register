§3375.  Report to council; appropriation by council to make good deficit
The board shall make report to the council of the city of New Orleans showing the condition of the pension fund on the first day of January of each year.  If at any time there is not sufficient money in the fund to pay each person entitled to the benefits thereof the full amount, then the council of the city of New Orleans shall appropriate and pay into the fund an amount sufficient to make good the deficit, and the fund shall be thus replenished to warrant the payment in full of each beneficiary.
Amended by Acts 1962, No. 403, §1; Acts 1969, No. 120, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2112 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
