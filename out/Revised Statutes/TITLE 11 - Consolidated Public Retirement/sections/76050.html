§951.65.  Absence or death of employee; effect on right to participate

A person shall cease to be a participant if he absents himself from service for more than five years in any period of six consecutive years, or if he withdraws his accumulated contributions, becomes a beneficiary, or dies.

Redesignated from R.S. 17:1105 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
