§951.64.  Temporary employees; denial of participation rights
The board of trustees has discretionary power to deny the right to participate to any employee who is serving on a temporary or other than regular basis, or the board of trustees may make optional with such employees their right to participate.
Redesignated from R.S. 17:1104 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
