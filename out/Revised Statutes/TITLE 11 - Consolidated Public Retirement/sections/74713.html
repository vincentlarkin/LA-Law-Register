§1454.  Dual retirement benefits prohibited

No member of this fund shall be eligible to draw more than one retirement from this fund at the same time, except that a surviving spouse of a member may draw retirement benefits and also surviving spouse benefits from this fund at the same time.

Acts 1989, No. 545, §1, eff. July 5, 1989; Redesignated from R.S. 47:8068 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2001, No. 96, §2, eff. July 1, 2001.

NOTE:  See Acts 2001, No. 96, §4 relative to retroactivity of Act.
