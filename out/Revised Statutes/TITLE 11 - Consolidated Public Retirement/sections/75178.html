§3.  Conflicts; provisions of Title to control

Except as is specifically otherwise provided, the provisions of this Title do not repeal comparable provisions contained within separate laws governing state and statewide public retirement systems.  However, the provisions of this Title shall be controlling in case of conflict with the separate laws.  The separate laws shall continue to be operable to the extent they are not in conflict with the provisions of this Title.  

Acts 1988, No. 81, §2, eff. July 1, 1989.
