§727.  Reciprocity of credits

The board of trustees may provide for reciprocity of credits between this system and any other state, parish or city retirement system of Louisiana supported partly or wholly by public funds.  

Redesignated from R.S. 17:597 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
