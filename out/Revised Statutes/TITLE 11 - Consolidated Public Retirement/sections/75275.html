§3174.  Officers and employees; bond

It shall be the duty of the said Board of Trustees to carry a blanket fidelity insurance policy covering all officers and employees handling the money or securities of said fund in an amount equal to the largest amount of said fund; and to pay the premiums therefor out of the funds of said Board.  

Designated from Acts 1944, No. 193, §4 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
