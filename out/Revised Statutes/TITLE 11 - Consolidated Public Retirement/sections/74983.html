§2.  Purpose of Title

The purpose of this Title is to consolidate public retirement law in order to effectively comply with the mandate of Article X, Section 29(E) of the Constitution of Louisiana to maintain public retirement systems on a sound actuarial basis.  

Acts 1988, No. 81, §2, eff. July 1, 1989.
