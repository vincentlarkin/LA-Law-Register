§1184.  Bond for funds on deposit

The funds and properties of this retirement system held in any bank of the state shall be safeguarded by a fidelity and surety bond, the amount of which shall be determined by the board of trustees.  

Redesignated from R.S. 17:964 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
