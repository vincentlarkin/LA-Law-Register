§3117.  Authority to alienate property
Said Board of Trustees shall have, and the same is hereby granted, full power and authority to sell, mortgage, lease, or let or otherwise alienate or dispose of any and all property, real or personal, tangible or intangible, which becomes the property of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund of the City of Alexandria.
Designated from Acts 1940, No. 12, §17 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
