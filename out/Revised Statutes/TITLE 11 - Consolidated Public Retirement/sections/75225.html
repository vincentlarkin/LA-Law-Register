§3053.  Legislative intent

It is the legislative intent and purpose of this Part to provide certain retirement, disability, death and other benefits to appointive officers and employees of the City in the amounts and under the terms and conditions herein set forth.  It is the further legislative intent and purpose of this Part that when and if the Federal Social Security Act is amended so as to permit the inclusion of municipal employees therein, then and in that event an adjustment of the benefits and contributions herein provided may be made so that the system hereby created may become integrated with the benefits under the Federal Social Security Act.

Designated from Acts 1952, No. 184, §21, by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
