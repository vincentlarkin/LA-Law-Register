§882.  Expense fund

The expense fund is the fund from which the expenses of the administration of the retirement system shall be paid, exclusive of amount payable as retirement allowances or other benefits provided in this Part.  The board of trustees shall determine annually the amount required to defray the expenses for the ensuing fiscal year.  The amount required, as determined by the board, shall be paid into the expense fund from the interest earnings of the system.  

Amended by Acts 1960, No. 401, §1; Acts 1966, No. 81, §1; Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 28, §1; Acts 1973, No. 165, §1; Acts 1976, No. 145, §1, eff. July 16, 1976; Redesignated from R.S. 17:701 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
