§1399.6. Acts 2012, No. 483, §1, eff. June 30, 2012; HCR 2 of the 2013 R.S., eff. May 23, 2013.

NOTE: Acts 2012, No. 483, which enacted Chapter 7 of Subtitle II of Title 11 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, comprised of R.S. 11:1399.1 through 1399.7 was held unconstitutional by the state Supreme Court in Retired State Employees Association, et al. v State of Louisiana No. 2013-CA-0499 (La. 6/28/13); 119 So.3d 568.
