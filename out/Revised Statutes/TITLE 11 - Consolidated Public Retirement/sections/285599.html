PART IX.  EXCESS BENEFIT PLAN

§2161.  Establishment of excess benefit plan

There is hereby created a separate, unfunded, nonqualified excess benefit plan containing the terms and provisions set forth in this Part and intended to be a qualified governmental excess benefit arrangement, as defined in Section 415(m)(3) of the Code.

Acts 2004, No. 259, §1, eff. July 1, 2004.
