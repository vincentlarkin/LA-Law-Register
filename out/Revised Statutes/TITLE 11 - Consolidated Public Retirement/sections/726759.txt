§1345.9.  Survivors' benefits for death other than in the line of duty
The surviving spouse or children of any active member whose death occurs other than in the performance of his duties shall have the same pension rights as provided in R.S. 11:1323.
Acts 2010, No. 992, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011; Acts 2011, No. 368, §1, eff. July 1, 2011.
