§3175.  Board of trustees; duties; meetings
It shall be the duty of the Board of Trustees of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund to meet at least every three months and to announce the results of their meetings within three days thereafter.  All meetings shall be public.  Decisions of the Board shall be final unless appeal to the Court from said decisions shall be filed within sixty days from the date the decisions are announced.
Designated from Acts 1944, No. 193, §8 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
