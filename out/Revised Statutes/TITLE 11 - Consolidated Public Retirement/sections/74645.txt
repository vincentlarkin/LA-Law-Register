§1327.  Investment of funds
The board may invest, in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 11:263, any portion of the State Police Retirement Fund in the judgment of the board, not required to pay current pensions and retirement salaries payable under the provisions of this Subpart.
Acts 1984, No. 867, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1440 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
