§1521.2.  Maximum compensation
A.  For the purposes of calculating any benefit payable under this plan, any compensation in excess of the limits set forth in 26 U.S.C. 401(a)(17) shall be disregarded.
B.  Subsection A of this Section shall not apply to members who enrolled in the plan on or before January 1, 1996.
Acts 2012, No. 481, §1, eff. June 5, 2012.
