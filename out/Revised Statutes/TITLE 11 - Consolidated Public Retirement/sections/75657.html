§3853.  Maximum pension

The amount of said pension shall not exceed one-half of the salary received by the official or employee at the time of his retirement, but in no case shall it be in excess of one hundred fifty dollars a month.  

Acts 1954, No. 511, §3; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1783 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
