§1563.  Deficiencies

If at any time the monies of the fund are insufficient to pay each retiree and beneficiary the full amount to which he is entitled, equal percentages of the full amount shall be paid to each retiree and beneficiary until the fund is replenished so as to warrant resumption of the payment of the full amount to each retiree and beneficiary.  

Acts 1986, No. 1063, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 1986; Redesignated from R.S. 13:960.2 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
