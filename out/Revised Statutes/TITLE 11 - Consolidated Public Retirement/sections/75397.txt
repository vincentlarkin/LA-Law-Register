§3379.  Surviving widows to participate
The surviving widows of members of the fire department of New Orleans who have died since January 1, 1904, may participate in the fund and shall be placed upon the rolls of the fund for the amounts and under the rules established by the board.
Amended by Acts 1969, No. 120, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2115 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
