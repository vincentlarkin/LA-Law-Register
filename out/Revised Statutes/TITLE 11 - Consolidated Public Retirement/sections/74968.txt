§1966.  Employee contributions
Each member of Plan B shall contribute an amount equal to a percentage of his monthly earnings from each and every payment of earnings, which contributions shall be credited to annuity savings fund B. The percentage shall be set by the board for each calendar year in a range from three percent to five percent and shall become effective as of the first payroll period reportable on the employee's W-2 for that year.
Acts 1989, No. 557, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:6176 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2001, No. 695, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2002; Acts 2010, No. 996, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
