§408.4.  Ability to contest signatures
Nothing in this Part shall be construed to limit or otherwise affect the rights of any person to assert that an electronic signature is a forgery, is used without authority, has been altered after the signature was applied to the electronic document, or otherwise is invalid for reasons that would invalidate the effect of a signature in written form.
Acts 2001, No. 234, §1, eff. June 1, 2001.
