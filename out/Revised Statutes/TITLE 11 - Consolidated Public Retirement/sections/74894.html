§1806.  Employee contributions

Each member of Plan B shall contribute an amount equal to a percentage of his monthly earnings from each and every payment of earnings, which contributions shall be credited to the Annuity Savings Fund B, and which percentage shall be set by the board for each fiscal year in a range from five percent to six percent and shall become effective as of the next payroll period reportable on the employee's W-2 for that year.

Acts 1978, No. 788, §1.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 519, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7276 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2012, No. 717, §1, eff. July 1, 2012.
