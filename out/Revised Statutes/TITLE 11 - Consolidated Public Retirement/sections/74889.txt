PART IV.  PLAN B.  RETIREMENT ELIGIBILITY;
RETIREMENT COMPUTATION; SURVIVOR
BENEFITS; CONTRIBUTIONS
§1801.  Eligibility for normal retirement
Any member of Plan B shall be eligible to retire if he has at least:
(1)  Thirty years of creditable service, regardless of age.
(2)  Ten years of creditable service, and is at least age sixty.
Acts 1978, No. 788, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:7271 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2001, No. 873, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
