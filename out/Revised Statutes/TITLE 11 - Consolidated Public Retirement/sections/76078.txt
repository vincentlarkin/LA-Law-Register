§952.34.  Early service retirement allowance
The early service retirement allowance shall be computed as a service retirement allowance as provided in R.S. 11:952.32.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 519, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1626 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
