§901.33.  Administration of benefits
The board of trustees of the teachers' retirement system of Louisiana shall administer all of the benefits provided by this law, including those benefits already accrued under and in accordance with Act 216 of the Legislature of Louisiana for the year 1940, under such rules and regulations as it may adopt, not inconsistent herewith.
