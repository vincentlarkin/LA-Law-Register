§3613.  Member participation

No policeman shall be eligible to participate in any of the benefits of this Subpart or to vote on any matter arising hereunder, unless he is participating or has satisfactorily completed participation in the fund herein referred to by making the contributions hereinabove set forth and provided for in this Subpart.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 549, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2385.12 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
