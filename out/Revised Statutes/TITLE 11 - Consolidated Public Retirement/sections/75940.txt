§892.  Contributions; limitation
The retirement benefits provided by this Part shall not exceed one hundred percent, and when a member has earned a benefit accrual equal to one hundred percent, no further contributions shall be required of him.  However, the state shall continue to pay to the system the employer's contribution.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 484, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 17:706 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991; Acts 2003, No. 218, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
