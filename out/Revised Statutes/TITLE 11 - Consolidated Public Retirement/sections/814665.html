§2241.2.  Application; definitions

Terms not specifically defined in this Section shall have the meanings provided in R.S. 11:2213 unless a different meaning is clearly required by the context.  For purposes of this Part:

(1)  "Member" or "members" shall mean all persons otherwise qualifying as a member under R.S. 11:2213 whose first employment making them eligible for membership in this system occurred on or after January 1, 2013, and who by virtue of their employment are eligible to receive state supplemental pay, notwithstanding any temporal restrictions relative to qualifying for such pay.

(2)  "Subplan" means the Hazardous Duty Subplan created by this Part for certain hazardous duty service employees within the system.

(3)  "System" means the Municipal Police Employees' Retirement System.
