§951.18.  Secretary-treasurer of board, election and compensation

The board of trustees shall by a majority vote of all its members, fix the term of office and appoint a secretary-treasurer who may be, but need not be, either a member of the board or one of the participants, and fix the amount of his compensation.

Redesignated from R.S. 17:1038 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
