§3640.  Payments to constitute fund
The foregoing payments so dedicated, collected, and made, or revenue so described, shall be deposited in and shall constitute the police pension fund.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2288 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
