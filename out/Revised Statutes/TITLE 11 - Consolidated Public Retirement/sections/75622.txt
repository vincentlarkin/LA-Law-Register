§3777.  Compulsory retirement prohibited
No member of this organization shall ever be compelled by this organization to retire from active service in the department because of age, length of service, physical disability or infirmity; the acceptance of said pension benefits shall always be optional with the member.
Designated from Acts 1952, No. 187, §18 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
