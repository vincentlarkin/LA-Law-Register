§901.14.  Administration of benefits

The board of trustees of the teachers' retirement system of Louisiana under such rules and regulations as the said board of trustees may adopt, not inconsistent herewith, shall administer all the benefits provided by this Sub-part.

Added by Acts 1960, No. 440, §2.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:720.2 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
