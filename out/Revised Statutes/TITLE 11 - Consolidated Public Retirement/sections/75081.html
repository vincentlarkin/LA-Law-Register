§2184.  Preservation of rights

The failure to re-enact all or any part of Subsections D, E, F and L of R.S. 11:2178 shall not affect the rights of the recipients to continue to receive the benefits presently being paid thereunder.  

Added by Acts 1954, No. 239, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1460 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
