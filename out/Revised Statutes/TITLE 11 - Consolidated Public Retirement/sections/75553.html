§3654.  Benefits to be paid in strict conformity with Subpart

No benefits, pensions, annuities, or refunds shall ever be made from the fund for any reason whatsoever, except in strict conformity with the provisions and requirements of this Subpart.  Any person who separates from the police department for any cause prior to retirement, shall have no claim to the benefits, pensions, or annuities provided herein, nor to refunds of any amounts paid into the fund.  This provision shall be retroactive.  

Redesignated from R.S. 33:2298 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
