§414.  Transfers with federal civil service retirement system
The board may negotiate and effect transfers with the United States Civil Service Retirement System.
Acts 1972, No. 135, §1, eff. July 26, 1972; Redesignated from R.S. 42:555 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
