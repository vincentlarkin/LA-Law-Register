§3855.  Subsequent employment or income from other sources; effect

Any pension granted under the provisions of this Part shall not be affected by earnings derived from subsequent employment or by income from other sources, but shall be paid regardless of the amount or source of any such future earnings or income.  

Acts 1954, No. 511, §5; Redesignated from R.S. 33:1785 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
