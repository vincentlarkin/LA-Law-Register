§951.63.  Employees not electing to participate; right to future election

An employee whose participation in this retirement system is contingent on his own election and who elects not to become a participant may thereafter apply for and be admitted to participation; no such participant shall receive prior service credit, but shall participate on the same basis as a new participant.
