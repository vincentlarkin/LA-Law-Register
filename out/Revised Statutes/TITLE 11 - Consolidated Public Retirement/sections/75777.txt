§567.  Existing members of system
Any person covered by this Subpart who on August 2, 1976, is a member of the Louisiana State Employees' Retirement System shall have the option to avail himself of the provisions of this Subpart as though he were not such a member.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 518, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 13:23 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
