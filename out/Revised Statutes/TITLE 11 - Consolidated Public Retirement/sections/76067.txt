§952.13.  Termination of membership
Membership in the funded system shall cease when a member receives a refund of his accumulated contributions, or receives a retirement benefit under this system, or dies.
Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1617 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
