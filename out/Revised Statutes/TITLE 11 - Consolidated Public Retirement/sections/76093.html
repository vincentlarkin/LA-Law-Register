§952.55.  Appropriations by state

The board of supervisors shall include in its annual operating budget submitted to the state an estimate of the contributions of the state to the system necessary for the ensuing fiscal year.

Added by Acts 1971, No. 26, §1.  Redesignated from R.S. 17:1640 pursuant to R.S. 24:253.
