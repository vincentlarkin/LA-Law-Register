§517.  Investments
The board may invest available funds and may hold, purchase, sell, assign, transfer, and dispose of any of the securities of the system, in the name of the system or a nominee name, provided the action is taken in compliance with the rules and regulations established by the board and in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 11:263.
Acts 1984, No. 867, §2; Acts 1991, No. 863, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 42:647 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
