§502.6.  Implementation
The Board of Trustees of the Louisiana State Employees' Retirement System shall implement the optional retirement plan no later than July 1, 2000, and the employer agencies, institutions, and boards shall implement the optional retirement plan on July 1, 2000.
Acts 1999, No. 1320, §1, eff. July 12, 1999.
NOTE:  See Acts 1999, No. 1320, §2(B), relative to termination of the optimal retirement plan.
