§3519.  Filing names of persons entitled to death benefits
Every member of the department shall file with the secretary of the board the name of any beneficiary to whom death benefits shall be paid and his relation to the member.
Redesignated from R.S. 33:2239 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
