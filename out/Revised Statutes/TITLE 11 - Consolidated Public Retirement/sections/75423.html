§3413.  Member participation

The employees of Fire Protection District No. One, as used in this Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund Act, means every man actively in the employ of Fire Protection District No. One who is qualified as a classified employee under the state civil service system.  

Designated from Acts 1968, No. 31, §13 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
