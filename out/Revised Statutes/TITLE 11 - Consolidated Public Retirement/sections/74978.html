§1982.  Board officers

The board shall elect from its members a chairman and vice chairman to serve at the board's pleasure.  They shall perform the duties designated by the board and shall serve without compensation, except for the per diem as provided herein.  

Acts 1979, No. 765, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980; Redesignated from R.S. 33:6192 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
