§3236.  Authority to alienate property

The board of trustees of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund of the City of Houma shall have, and the same is hereby granted unto them, full power and authority to sell, mortgage, lease, let or otherwise alienate or dispose of any and all property, real or personal, tangible or intangible, which is now in the possession of or belongs to or shall become the property or come into the possession of the Firemen's Pension and Relief Fund of the City of Houma, Louisiana, and/or of the board of trustees thereof in furthering the requirements and purposes of this Part.  

Designated from Acts 1973, No. 139, §17 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
