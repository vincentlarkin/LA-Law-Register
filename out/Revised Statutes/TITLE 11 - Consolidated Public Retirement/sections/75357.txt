§3315.  Election of officers; term
The President and Secretary of the board of directors shall be chosen by a majority vote of the members of said Board and shall be elected for a two year term.
Designated from Acts 1944, No. 186, §5 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
