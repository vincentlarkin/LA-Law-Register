§825.  Voting powers of trustees; quorum
Each trustee shall be entitled to one vote in the board.  A majority of the members of the board shall constitute a quorum.
Amended by Acts 1991, No. 73, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 17:654 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §3, eff. June 25, 1991.
