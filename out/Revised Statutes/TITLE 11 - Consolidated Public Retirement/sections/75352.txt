§3300.  Exemption from state income tax
Any annuity, retirement allowance or benefits, or refund of contributions, or any optional benefit or any other benefit paid to any person under the provisions of this Part shall be exempt from any state income tax.
Acts 1991, No. 218, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2140.1 by Acts 1991, No. 74, §5, eff. June 25, 1991.
