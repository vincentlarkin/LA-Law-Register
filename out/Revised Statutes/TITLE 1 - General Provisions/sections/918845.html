§58.6.  Purple Heart Recognition Day

The seventh day of August shall be recognized as "Purple Heart Recognition Day" and shall be dedicated to the remembrance and recognition of those members of the armed forces of the United States who have earned the Purple Heart Medal for wounds received in combat.

Acts 2014, No. 22, §1.
