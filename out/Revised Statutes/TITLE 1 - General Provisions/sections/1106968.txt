§58.9. Diffuse Intrinsic Pontine Glioma
The seventeenth day of May shall be recognized as "Diffuse Intrinsic Pontine Glioma
Awareness Day" to recognize those individuals who have been diagnosed with diffuse
intrinsic pontine glioma and to increase awareness of the disease throughout the state of
Louisiana.
Acts 2018, No. 252, §1, eff. May 17, 2018.
