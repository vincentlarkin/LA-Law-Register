§324.  Menhaden seines; restrictions

Anyone legally taking menhaden shall not have in their possession more than five percent, by weight, of any species of fish other than menhaden and herring-like species.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 593, §1.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 838, §1; Acts 1986, No. 904, §3.
