§659.  Penalties

Any violation by any person, firm, or corporation of any of the provisions of this Part or the failure to comply with said provisions, including the securing of the license herein provided, shall constitute a class two violation.  

Added by Acts 1958, No. 455, §9.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 837, §3.
