§79.  Participation of non-coastal state; limitation

When any state other than those named specifically in R.S. 56:73 becomes a party hereto for the purpose of conserving its anadromous fish or marine species in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 56:73, the participation of the state in the action of the commission shall be limited to such species of fish.  

Acts 1981, No. 837, §2.
