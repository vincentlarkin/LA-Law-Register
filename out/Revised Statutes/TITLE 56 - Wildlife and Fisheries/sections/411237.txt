§1950.12.  Definitions
As used in this Part the "Old Metairie Road Business and Cultural District" means that corridor or area along Metairie Road, also designated as Louisiana Highway 3119, beginning at the Jefferson Parish-Orleans Parish line at the Seventeenth Street Canal and extending to the west along Metairie Road to its intersection with Severn Avenue near Airline Drive and including the properties on both sides of such portion of Metairie Road.
Acts 2006, No. 245, §1, eff. July 1, 2006.
