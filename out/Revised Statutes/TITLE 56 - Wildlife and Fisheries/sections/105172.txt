§307.2.  Buying and selling prohibition by transporters; exceptions
A.  A person transporting fish under privilege of a transport license is prohibited from buying or selling, by any means whatsoever, any species of fish.
B.  The provisions of this Section shall not apply to a person transporting fish under the privilege of a Louisiana transport license purchased in connection with a Louisiana wholesale/retail dealer's license when that person buys fish for or on behalf of the wholesale/retail dealer to whom such transport license was issued and only transports such fish to that wholesale/retail dealer.
Acts 1986, No. 904, §1; Acts 1990, No. 261, §1, eff. July 4, 1990.
