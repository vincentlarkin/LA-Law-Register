§764.  Penalty for violation of R.S. 56:761, R.S. 56:762 or R.S. 56:763
Violation of the provisions of R.S. 56:761, R.S. 56:762, or R.S. 56:763 constitutes a class two violation.
Amended by Acts 1970, No. 651, §1; Acts 1981, No. 837, §3.
