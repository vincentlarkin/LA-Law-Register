§67.  Enforcement of civil judgment

Judgments recovered on civil demands may be enforced according to law.  No license for any purpose whatsoever shall be issued to the defendant by the commission or by any commission or office required to enforce the laws protecting natural resources, until such judgment is satisfied.  

Acts 1985, No. 876, §3, eff. July 23, 1985.
