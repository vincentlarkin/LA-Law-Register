§676.  Reciprocity; license fees; seasons; limits

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, where the commission fails to enter into a reciprocal agreement with a state as allowed in R.S. 56:673, 674, and 675, residents of that state shall be granted or sold privileges and licenses equal to those privileges and licenses granted or sold to Louisiana residents by the nonresident's state.  

Acts 1984, No. 843, §1.
