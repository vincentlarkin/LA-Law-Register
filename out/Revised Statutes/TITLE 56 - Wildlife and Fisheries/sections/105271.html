§407.  Use of trammel nets, gill nets, and seines in certain areas of Lake Borgne prohibited

A.  No person shall use any trammel net, gill net, or seine for the taking of fish, within one-half mile of the shoreline of Grand Island, commonly known as Half Moon Island, in Lake Borgne, or within one-half mile of the shoreline of Grassy Island, in Lake Borgne.  

B.  Violation of any of the provisions of this Section constitutes a class two violation.  

Acts 1974, No. 223, §2.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 213, §1; Acts 1992, No. 528, §1, eff. June 29, 1992.
