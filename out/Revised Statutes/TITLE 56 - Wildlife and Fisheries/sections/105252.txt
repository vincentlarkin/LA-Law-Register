§37.1.  Class eight violation
The following penalties shall be imposed for a class eight violation:
For each offense, the fine shall not be less than five thousand dollars nor more than seven thousand dollars and the violator may be imprisoned in jail for not less than sixty days nor more than six months.
Acts 1991, No. 868, §1.
