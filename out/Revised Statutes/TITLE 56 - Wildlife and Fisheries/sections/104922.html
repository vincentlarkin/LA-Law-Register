§1684.1.  Cypremort Point State Park; design standards; applicability

A.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, no rule or regulation of the office of state parks regulating design and aesthetic quality standards, construction, or usage of facilities at state parks shall be applicable to the development, planning, and construction of facilities in Cypremort Point State Park without the review of the oversight committee as provided for in Subsection B of this Section.  However any such plans shall be approved by the lieutenant governor prior to commencing any development or construction.

B.  An oversight committee comprised of the state representatives and state senators representing the parishes of St. Mary and Iberia, or their designees, and the parish presidents of the parishes of St. Mary and Iberia, or their designees, shall review the proposed plans before any construction begins as provided in Subsection A of this Section.

Acts 1999, No. 1028, §1.
