§697.  Special fund

For the payment of the additional salary provided by R.S. 56:696, there is hereby created a special fund in the treasury of the state of Louisiana, to which the appropriations hereinafter made shall be credited, and the funds so credited shall be used solely for the payment of such additional salary as provided in R.S. 56:696.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 314, §1, eff. July 17, 1975.
