§174.  Shipment of carcasses or parts thereof; tag or label
Any common carrier may receive and transport carcasses of such animals or birds, or parts thereof.  However, to every package containing such carcasses or parts thereof shall be affixed a tag or label, upon which shall be plainly printed or written the name and license number of the person to whom the breeder's license was issued who is thus transporting; the number of carcasses or portions thereof contained therein; and a statement that the animals or birds were killed and tagged in accordance with the provisions of this Sub-part.
