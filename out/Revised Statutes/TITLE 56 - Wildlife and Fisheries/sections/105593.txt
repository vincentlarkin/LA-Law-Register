§762.  Imported wild birds and animals protected in general
No person shall catch, kill, snare, or pursue any wild animal or bird imported into this state by the federal government, or by any person under the supervision and consent of the commission for the purpose of experimentation or propagation.
Amended by Acts 1981, No. 736, §1.
