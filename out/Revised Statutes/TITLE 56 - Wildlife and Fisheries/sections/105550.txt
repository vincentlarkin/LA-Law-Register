§7.1.  Jesse J. Guidry Center for Wildlife and Fisheries Resources
The official name of the state building in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, wherein the primary administrative offices for the Department of Wildlife and Fisheries are located shall be "Jesse J. Guidry Center for Wildlife and Fisheries Resources".
Added by Acts 1988, No. 17, §1.
