§432.2.  Annual reporting of coastal protection, conservation, and restoration project status
Once per year, in coordination with the Louisiana Oyster Task Force, the Coastal Protection and Restoration Authority shall provide information to the Oyster Task Force regarding the nature, location, and status of current or planned projects for integrated coastal protection to the extent practical.
Acts 2006, No. 425, §1, eff. June 15, 2006; Acts 2009, No. 523, §4, eff. July 10, 2009; Acts 2012, No. 604, §4, eff. June 7, 2012.
