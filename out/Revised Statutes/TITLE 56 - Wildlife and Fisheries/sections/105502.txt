§653.  Information to accompany applications; annual fee
A.  The application for such a license shall be accompanied by a fee of four hundred
twenty dollars to be the annual charge for the issuance of such license, and shall be further
accompanied by information as to both of the following:
(1)  The location and description of the premises, including a legal description of the
land.
(2)  Copies of parish or ward maps with the area or areas clearly outlined and a
description of the facilities thereon, together with the interest of the applicant therein.
B.  It shall further be accompanied by information on the applicant as to his place of
residence or, if a corporation, the place of incorporation, and the location of its principal
place of office, and if a partnership, the names and addresses of each partner, the address of
its principal office, together with full details as to the intended operations on said hunting
preserve.  Also, the applicant shall further furnish to the commission any additional
information required or which may be requested by said commission.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 455, §3; Acts 1988, No. 87, §1; Acts 2021, No. 356, §2,
eff. Nov. 15, 2021.
