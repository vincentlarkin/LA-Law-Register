§410.  Trawling at night prohibited on White Lake and Grand Lake
A.  Trawling, skimming, or butterflying on White Lake in Cameron and Vermilion parishes and Grand Lake in Cameron Parish from official sunset to official sunrise is hereby prohibited.
B.  Violation of this Section constitutes a class two violation.
Acts 1986, No. 748, §1; Acts 1992, No. 528, §1, eff. June 29, 1992; Acts 2007, No. 296, §1.
