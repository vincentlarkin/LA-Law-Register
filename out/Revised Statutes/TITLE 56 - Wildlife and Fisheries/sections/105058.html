CHAPTER 13.  TERREBONNE PARISH ARTIFICIAL REEF ACT

§2021.  Title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Terrebonne Parish Artificial Reef Act".  

Acts 1991, No. 179, §1, eff. July 2, 1991.
