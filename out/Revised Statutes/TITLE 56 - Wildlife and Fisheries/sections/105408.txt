§578.13.  Labeling of "crawfish"
A.  No product labeled "crawfish" may be sold in Louisiana unless it consists of, or is derived from, the red swamp crawfish
(Procambarus clarkii)
, the white river crawfish
(Procambarus zonangulus)
, or Louisiana pond-raised crawfish.
B.  Any violation of the labeling requirements shall subject the distributor or packager to the penalties provided in R.S. 56:578.10(B).
Acts 2001, No. 200, §1.
