§303.9.  Presumption of fish used for commercial purposes only
As a condition of granting wholesale licenses,  retail licenses, or transportation licenses, all fish in the possession on the premises of the licensed facility or licensed vehicle shall be deemed to be used for commercial purposes only.
Acts 1997, No. 670, §1.
