§57.1.  Forfeitures for fraud

Where the proof shows that a license as a resident has been obtained by fraud or subterfuge, the vessels and equipment used under that license shall be forfeited by proceedings maintained as provided for the forfeiture of vessels in this Subpart.  

Acts 1985, No. 876, §3, eff. July 23, 1985.
