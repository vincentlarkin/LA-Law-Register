§3005.  Lifetime licenses
A.  Resident
(1)  Infant (under three years)
$500.00
(2)  Adult (ages three through sixty-four years)
$500.00
(3)  Senior (sixty-five years or older)
$100.00
B.  Nonresident
(1)  Infant (under three years)
$1,000.00
(2)  Adult (three years and older)
$4,000.00
Acts 2021, No. 356, §3, eff. June 1, 2022; Acts 2024, No. 653, §1.
