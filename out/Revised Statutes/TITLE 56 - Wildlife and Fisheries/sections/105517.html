§677.  Reciprocal agreements; Toledo Bend; Caddo Lake; special fishing licenses; fees

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the Wildlife and Fisheries Commission may enter into reciprocal agreements with the state of Texas to establish special fishing licenses and fishing license fees on the Toledo Bend Reservoir and Caddo Lake.

Acts 1995, No. 117, §1.
