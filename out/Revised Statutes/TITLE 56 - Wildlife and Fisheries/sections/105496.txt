§649.7.  Infant combination lifetime hunting and fishing license
Beginning January 1, 2025, the department shall make available for persons under
the age of three years a combination lifetime hunting and fishing license which shall entitle
the licensee to all of the privileges conferred by basic hunting, deer, waterfowl, and turkey
hunting licenses; basic fishing, saltwater, crab, and recreational fishing gear licenses; and
basic wildlife management area access permits.
Acts 2000, 2d Ex. Sess., No. 1, §3, eff. June 26, 2000; Acts 2021, No. 356, §3, eff.
June 1, 2022; Acts 2024, No. 653, §1.
