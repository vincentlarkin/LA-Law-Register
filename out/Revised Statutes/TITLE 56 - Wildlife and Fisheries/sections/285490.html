§799.5. Commission duties and responsibilities

            In addition to duties, responsibilities, and powers provided by the constitution and statutory law, the commission:

            (1) Shall establish a conservation management plan for the White Lake Property.

            (2) May authorize the conduct of lottery hunts on the White Lake Property by the department or through a cooperative endeavor agreement.

            Acts 2004, No. 613, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2005; Acts 2016, No. 203, §1.
