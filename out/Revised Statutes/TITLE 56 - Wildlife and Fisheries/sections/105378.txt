§54.1.  Prima facie evidence of violation of law
In all cases of arrest for violation of the game and fish laws, the possession of game, fish, nets, seines, or lines, tackle, or the possession or operation of any other device prohibited, is prima facie evidence of the violation.
Acts 1985, No. 876, §3, eff. July 23, 1985.
