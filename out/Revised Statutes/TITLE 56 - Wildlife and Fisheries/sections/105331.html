SUBPART D-1.  CLAMS

§471.  Ownership of water bottoms

A.  All water bottoms suitable for clamming within the state shall remain public with respect to clams and shall be subject to any and all restrictions contained herein.  

B.  Nothing in this Subpart shall in any way affect the dredging of Rangia clam shells as presently authorized.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 616, §1.
