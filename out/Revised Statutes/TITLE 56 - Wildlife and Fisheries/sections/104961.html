§1764.  Secretary; staff

The commissioners shall have the power to select a secretary and to fix his salary at a reasonable sum.  The commission may employ such other agents and employees as may be required for the discharge of its functions and to prescribe and define their respective duties, authority, and compensation.  

Acts 1983, No. 485, §1, eff. July 27, 1983.
