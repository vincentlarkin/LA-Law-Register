§700.11.  Definitions
As used in this Part, unless the context requires otherwise, the terms set forth below
shall have the following meanings:
(1)  "Biological test data" means surveys of oyster beds and grounds by a certified
biologist to determine the quality, condition, and value of oyster beds and grounds.
(2)  "Board" means the Oyster Lease Damage Evaluation Board.
(3)  "Department" means the Department of Conservation and Energy.
(4)  "Leaseholder" means an owner of an oyster lease granted by the Department of
Wildlife and Fisheries.
(5)  "Mineral activity" means exploration (including all seismic operations),
production, transportation (of equipment or product), and any other activity associated with
the production of oil and gas.
(6)  "Owner" means an owner or operator of a mineral activity.
(7)  "Secretary" means the secretary of the Department of Conservation and Energy
or his designee.
Acts 1995, No. 1304, §1, eff. June 29, 1995; Acts 2023, No. 150, §22, eff. Jan. 10,
2024.
