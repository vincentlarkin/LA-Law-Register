§641.2.  Certification of certain not-for-profit organizations involved in charitable hunting
and fishing activities; exemption from license requirements
The secretary of the Department of Wildlife and Fisheries is hereby authorized to
certify a not-for-profit organization as an "organization involved in charitable hunting and
fishing activities".  Individuals engaged in hunting or fishing activities conducted by a not-for-profit organization certified by the secretary as an"organization involved in charitable
hunting and fishing activities" shall be exempt from the requirement for a hunting or fishing
license while engaged in activities sponsored by that organization.
Acts 2020, No. 178, §1.
