§652.  Rights under license

Any license issued hereunder shall permit the licensee to propagate, possess, and release for shooting or taking on the preserve covered by such license any pen-raised quail and pen-raised mallard and in addition thereto, import, propagate, possess, and release for shooting or taking on the preserve covered by such license any foreign game bird not a native of the state of Louisiana, if approved by the Louisiana Wildlife and Fisheries Commission.  

Added by Acts 1958, No. 455, §2; Acts 1988, No. 87, §1.
