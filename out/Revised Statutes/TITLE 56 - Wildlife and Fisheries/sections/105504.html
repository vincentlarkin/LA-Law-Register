§655.  Regulation of operations

The Louisiana Wildlife and Fisheries Commission shall have the authority to regulate the operations of hunting preserves licensed hereunder, and to set seasons, bag limits, and any other rules and regulations on the operation of said hunting preserves as are deemed necessary for the best interest of the public.  

Added by Acts 1958, No. 455, §5.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 682, §1; Acts 1988, No. 846, §1.
