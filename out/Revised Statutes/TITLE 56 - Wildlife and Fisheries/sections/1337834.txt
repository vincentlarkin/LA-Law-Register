PART VII.  LOUISIANA MUSIC TRAIL
§1950.21.  Purpose
The purpose of the Louisiana Music Trail is to commemorate and to promote
awareness and encourage enjoyment of the stories, biographies, and points of interest in
blues, rock and roll, country, zydeco, cajun, jazz, rhythm and blues, hip-hop, swamp pop,
gospel, and other music types throughout Louisiana.
Acts 2023, No. 283, §1.
