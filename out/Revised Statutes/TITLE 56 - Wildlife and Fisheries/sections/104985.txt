§1831.  Agreements; land conservation organizations
The department is authorized to enter into agreements with national, nonprofit membership land conservation organizations to conduct programs; manage, preserve, and conserve land; and to purchase lands.
Acts 1987, No. 324, §1, eff. July 6, 1987.
