§1704.  Bayou Segnette State Park; operation of the wave pool
All fees and other revenues generated through operation of the wave pool at Bayou Segnette State Park shall be appropriated as self-generated revenues to the Bayou Segnette State Park to be used solely for the purpose of funding the operation and maintenance of the wave pool.
Acts 1992, No. 121, §1, eff. July 1, 1992.
