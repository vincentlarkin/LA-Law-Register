§304.5.  Transferability; change of vessel name
A.  Vessel licenses are not transferable except as provided by rule or regulation of the department.
B.  The name of a vessel for which a vessel license has been issued cannot be changed without prior notification to the department.
Acts 1986, No. 904, §1.
