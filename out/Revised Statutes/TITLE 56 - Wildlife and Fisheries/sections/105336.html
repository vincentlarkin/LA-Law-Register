§476.  License display

The vessel license number shall be painted on the cabin roof or any other place which is easily visible from the air.  The letters shall be at least eight inches high and of appropriate width and prefaced with the letter "C".  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 616, §1.  Acts 1986, No. 904, §3.
