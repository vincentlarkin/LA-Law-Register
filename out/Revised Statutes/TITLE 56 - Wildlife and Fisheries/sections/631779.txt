§1685.2.  J. D. "Prof" Lafleur Visitor Center at the Louisiana State Arboretum
The visitor center located at the Louisiana State Arboretum State Preservation Area is hereby designated and shall hereafter be known as the J. D. "Prof" Lafleur Visitor Center.
Acts 2008, No. 238, §1.
