§410.6.  Calcasieu Lake in Calcasieu Parish; use of trammel nets, gill nets, strike nets, and seines
A.  No person shall set or use any trammel net, gill net, strike net, or seine for the taking of fish in that portion of Calcasieu Lake which is situated in Calcasieu Parish.
B.  Violation of this Section constitutes a class two violation.
Acts 1995, No. 583, §1.
