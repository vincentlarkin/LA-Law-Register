§3006. Violations

            Except as otherwise provided in this Title, hunting, fishing, or accessing WMAs without the license required by this Part shall constitute a Class II violation punishable under the provisions of R.S. 56:32.

            Acts 2021, No. 356, §3, eff. June 1, 2022.
