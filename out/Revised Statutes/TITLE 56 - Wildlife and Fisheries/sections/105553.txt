§70.3.  Louisiana Help Our Wildlife Fund; uses of fund
The Louisiana Help Our Wildlife Fund is hereby abolished in the state treasury.  Any
monies in the fund shall be transferred to the Conservation Fund.
Acts 1995, No. 77, §1; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 148, §8; Acts 2018, No. 612,
§18, eff. July 1, 2020; Acts 2019, No. 404, §1, eff. July 1, 2020.
