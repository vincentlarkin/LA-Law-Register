SUBPART I-1.  WHITE LAKE PROPERTY

§799.1.  Purpose

The purpose of this Subpart is to provide for the administration, control, management, and funding for the White Lake Property located in Vermilion Parish.  The legislature acknowledges that the White Lake Property provides an opportunity for, and an obligation on, the state to preserve a scarce fresh water ecosystem and to conserve and protect in perpetuity a relatively natural habitat of fish, wildlife or plants or similar ecosystem.

Acts 2004, No. 613, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2005.
