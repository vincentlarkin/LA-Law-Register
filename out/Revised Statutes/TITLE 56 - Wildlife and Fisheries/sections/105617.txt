§80.  Signatory states not precluded from additional restrictions
Nothing in this compact limits the powers or the proprietary interest of any signatory state or repeals or prevents the enactment of any legislation or the enforcement of any requirement by a signatory state imposing additional conditions and restrictions to conserve its fisheries.
Acts 1981, No. 837, §2.
