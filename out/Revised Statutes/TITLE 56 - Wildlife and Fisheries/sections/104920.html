§1683.  Office of state parks; authority to classify

The office of state parks is hereby authorized to maintain a system for classifying all holdings within its jurisdiction.  Such classification system will provide objectives which will encompass preservation, commemoration, and recreation as identified in the statement of purpose in R.S. 56:1682.  

Acts 1980, No. 827, §1.
