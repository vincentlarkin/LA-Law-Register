§1947.  Definitions
As used in this Part, the "Highland Road" means the road extending, on its northerly terminus, from South Boulevard in Baton Rouge to the road's point of junction with Jefferson Highway at the southerly terminus of the road, all of which is designated an historic road.  The portion of the Highland Road extending from the north gate of Louisiana State University to the point of junction with Jefferson Highway is designated a scenic parkway.
Acts 1989, No. 467, §1.
