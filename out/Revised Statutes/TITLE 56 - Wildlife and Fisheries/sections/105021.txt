§1907.  Penalties and enforcement
Any violation of the provisions of this Part or any regulation adopted pursuant to the
provisions of this Part shall constitute a class six violation punishable under the provisions
of R.S. 56:36.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 473, §1.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 837, §3; Acts 2017,
No. 228, §1.
