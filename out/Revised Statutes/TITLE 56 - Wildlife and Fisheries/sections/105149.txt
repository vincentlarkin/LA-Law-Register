§304.1.  Vessel license; license year; purchase period
A.  A vessel license is valid for one year, beginning on January first of each calendar
year and expiring on December thirty-first of the same calendar year.
B.  A vessel license may be purchased at any time of the year for the current license
year and from November fifteenth for the immediately following license year.
Acts 1986, No. 904, §1; Acts 2021, No. 356, §2, eff. Nov. 15, 2021.
