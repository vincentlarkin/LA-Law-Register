§264.  Scientific research

The commission shall conduct scientific researches into the life habits of the nongame quadrupeds through technically trained officers and employees and, when practicable, give full publicity to such biological findings.  

Acts 1970, No. 550, §1; Acts 1990, No. 194, §1, eff. July 2, 1990.
