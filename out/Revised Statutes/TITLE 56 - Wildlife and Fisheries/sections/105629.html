§87.  Necessary travel expenses; payment

All necessary expenses incident to the membership of Louisiana in the commission are payable out of the general fund by the treasurer of this state.  Such expenses shall include travel cost and necessary expenses of the commissioners and other representatives of Louisiana to and from meetings of the commission or its duly constituted sections or committees.  

Acts 1981, No. 837, §2.
