§57.  Seizure of vessels, airplanes, vehicles, and equipment
Any enforcing officer shall seize vessels, airplanes, vehicles, and all other means of transport and the equipment used or employed in the commission of offenses prohibited by this Chapter and taken into possession by the department.
Acts 1981, No. 837, §1.
