§301.6.  Special licenses and permits
A person taking or possessing fish with a permit or license pursuant to R.S. 56:17, 318, 319, 412, 571, or any other Section of the laws of Louisiana, and unless otherwise prohibited by law, may be required to be licensed pursuant to this Part.  A decision to require licensing shall be made at the sole discretion of the secretary.
Acts 1986, No. 904, §1.
