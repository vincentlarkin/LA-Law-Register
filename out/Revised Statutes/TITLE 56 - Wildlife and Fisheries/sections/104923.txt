§1685.  Classification of holdings
A.  The office of state parks shall classify all holdings according to the classification
most closely representing their character or potential use, or both.  The official names of each
existing and future office of state parks holding will include the classification term
representing its function and basic purpose.  In the event one holding represents several
classification functions, the dominant function will determine the primary classification, and
the name will represent this consideration.
