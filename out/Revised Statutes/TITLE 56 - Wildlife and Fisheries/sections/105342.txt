§481.  Size limits
It shall be illegal to possess clams of less than one inch measured from hinge to edge of shell.  Clams shall be culled where taken and all clams of less than legal size shall be immediately returned to the waters.  A tolerance of ten percent by count of any catch shall be allowed.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 616, §1.
