§410.10.  Use of certain fishing gear on Poverty Point Reservoir; prohibition

A.  No person shall possess, set, or use any recreational hoop nets, recreational wire nets, yo-yos, trotlines, or slat traps in Poverty Point Reservoir.

B.  Violation of this Section constitutes a class two violation.

Acts 2005, No. 95, §1.
