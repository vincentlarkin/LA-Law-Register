SUBPART C.  LOUISIANA ACRES FOR WILDLIFE
§191.  Title
The cooperative environmental action program to save wildlife cover and provide abundant game for future generations mandated by this Subpart shall be known as "Louisiana Acres for Wildlife."
Added by Acts 1979, No. 481, §1.
