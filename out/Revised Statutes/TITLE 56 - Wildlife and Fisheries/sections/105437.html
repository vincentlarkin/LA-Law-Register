§613.  Location; cooperation with federal agencies

The secretary shall provide for cooperation with the United States Fish and Wildlife Service, in connection with the investigations of the station, and offer its facilities, under proper regulations, to special investigation and students in the biologic sciences.  He shall publish the results of any investigations that may be of value in the development of the biologic resources of the state.  

Amended by Acts 1974, No. 717, §1; Acts 1981, No. 736, §1.
