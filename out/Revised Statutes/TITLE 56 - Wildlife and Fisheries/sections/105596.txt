§765.  Donations for wildlife refuges, wildlife management areas, and public hunting
grounds; applicability of certain laws
The provisions of R.S. 30:148.1 through 148.7, 149.1, and 209.3 and R.S. 47:648.1
shall not authorize the breach of any term or condition of any donation which was accepted
by the state prior to August 1, 2025, involving any state wildlife refuge, wildlife management
area, or public hunting ground.
Acts 1986, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 36, §1, eff. Dec. 24, 1986; Acts 2025, No. 508, §2.
