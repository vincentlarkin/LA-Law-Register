SUBPART A-1.  LOUISIANA DUCK LICENSE, STAMP,

AND PRINT PROGRAM

§151.  Purpose

The hunting of migratory waterfowl has long been a source of recreation and tourism in Louisiana.  In order to protect and preserve this most valuable asset, the Louisiana Duck License, Stamp, and Print Program is created as a means of funding approved projects through sale of a migratory waterfowl license, hereinafter known as the "duck license", and sale of duck stamps and prints.

Acts 1988, No. 632, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1989; Acts 1990, No. 392, §1; Acts 2001, No. 270, §1.
