§319.1.  Control of importation of fish; department's regulatory authority

The department shall have the authority to regulate or prohibit, by rule in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, the possession, sale, or transportation of any fish into Louisiana.  

Acts 1988, No. 614, §1, eff. July 14, 1988; Acts 1990, No. 196, §1.
