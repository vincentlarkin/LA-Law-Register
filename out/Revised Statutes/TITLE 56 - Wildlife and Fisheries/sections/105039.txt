Aycock and Homer west on LA 146, plus a short link on LA 79 from Homer to LA 2.

designated on LA 22 from its intersection with LA 70 at the Ascension Parish Visitor Center
to the intersection of LA 22 with US 51-X in Ponchatoula and then south on US 51-X and
continuing on US 51 to the Byway's intersection with I-10.
