§1689.  Violation of rules and regulations; penalty
In addition to any penalties otherwise provided by law, violation of any of the rules and regulations promulgated by the secretary of the Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism shall be punishable by fine of not less than fifteen dollars nor more than two hundred fifty dollars.
Acts 1980, No. 827, §1.
