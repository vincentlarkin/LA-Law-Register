§62.  Time limit for retention of things seized
Things which have been seized by the department may in any case be disposed of by the department by the methods authorized in R.S. 56:61 after having been held by the department for two years.
Acts 1981, No. 837, §1; Acts 1997, No. 263, §1, eff. June 17, 1997.
