§55.3.  Boats maintained for enforcement; vehicle equipment
A.  The department shall keep and maintain suitable boats for the purpose of enforcing the provisions of this Title.
B.  Each state owned vehicle, operated by a classified wildlife enforcement agent or officer of the enforcement division who has enforcement authority as defined by R.S. 56:55.2, while acting in his official capacity or performing his duties, may be equipped with such devices as are provided for authorized emergency vehicles in R.S. 32:318(A), (C), (D), (E), and (F).  However, the secretary may, at his discretion, exempt special vehicles from the provisions of this Section.
Acts 1985, No. 876, §3, eff. July 23, 1985.
