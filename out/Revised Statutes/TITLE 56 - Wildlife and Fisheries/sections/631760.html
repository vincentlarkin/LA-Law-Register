§69.25.  Reciprocal recognition of suspension

A.  A participating state shall recognize the suspension of license privileges of a person by another participating state as though the violation resulting in the suspension satisfies each of the following:

(1)  Had occurred in the home state.

(2)  Could have been the basis of the suspension of license privileges in the home state.

B.  A participating state shall communicate suspension information to other participating states in the form and with the content as contained in the compact manual.

Acts 2008, No. 363, §1.
