§326.1.  Size limits set by commission
The commission shall have the authority to set, by rule in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, size limits for all freshwater and saltwater fish for which no limits have been set by law.
Acts 1986, No. 492, §1; Acts 2008, No. 23, §1, eff. July 1, 2008.
