PART VII.  LOUISIANA CONSERVATION INCENTIVE PROGRAM
§1938.1.  Louisiana Conservation Incentive Program; fund
There is hereby established within the Department of Wildlife and Fisheries the
Louisiana Conservation Incentive Program for the purpose of assisting private landowners
to enhance wildlife habitat and to manage native species on private lands.  The
administration of the program and the projects funded to assist private landowners related
to improving and providing wildlife habitats on their property shall be funded by the
Conservation Incentive Program Account as provided for in R.S. 56:10(B)(20).
Acts 2025, No. 87, §1, eff. See Act.
