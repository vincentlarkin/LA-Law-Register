§326.3.  Possession limits; size limits, seasons, quotas, times, and daily take
limits, set by commission; penalties
Subject to the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act and
specifically R.S. 49:966 and R.S. 56:325.3, the commission may set possession
limits, quotas, places, seasons, times, size limits, and daily take limits based
upon biological and technical data, for all freshwater and saltwater finfish
taken or possessed in Louisiana waters.  Penalties for violation of any rule or
regulations adopted and promulgated by the commission under this Section
constitutes a class two violation.
Acts 1988, No. 954, §1, eff. July 27, 1988; Acts 1989, No. 486, §1, eff.
July 1, 1989; Acts 1990, No. 376, §1; Acts 1991, No. 157, §1, eff. July 2,
1991.
