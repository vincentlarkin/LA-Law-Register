§1936.  Program termination
The Louisiana Outdoors Forever Program shall terminate on July 1, 2033.
Acts 2022, No. 714, §2, eff. June 18, 2022.
NOTE:  Pursuant to this Section, the Louisiana Outdoors Forever Program shall
terminate on July 1, 2033.
