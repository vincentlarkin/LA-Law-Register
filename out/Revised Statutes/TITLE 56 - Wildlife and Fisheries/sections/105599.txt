§768.  Private property surrounded by wildlife management areas; access
The Department of Wildlife and Fisheries shall ensure land access by the owner or owners thereof to privately owned property which is located within the boundaries of a wildlife management area and which is totally surrounded by that wildlife management area.
Acts 1999, No. 716, §1.
