§1563.5. Inspection fees

            The state fire marshal may charge inspection fees pursuant to R.S. 23:537 and 541, R.S. 27:44.2, and R.S. 51:911.22, 911.28, and 911.32.

            Acts 1993, No. 687, §2; Acts 2023, No. 126, §2.
