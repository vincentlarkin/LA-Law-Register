§1085.7.  Collusion to hide facts prohibited
No official and no person named in this Subpart shall collude with any person to
misstate or conceal any facts the correct reporting of which, under this Subpart, is essential.
Amended by Acts 1952, No. 127, §15; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1107 by HCR 84
of 2015 R.S.
