§1281.3.  Classification of water and sewerage facilities
Within one hundred twenty days of adoption of this
Subpart
*, the state health officer
will classify all water production facilities, water treatment plants, water distribution systems,
sewage collection systems, and sewage treatment plants actually used or intended for use by
the public with due regard to the size, type, character of water or wastewater to be treated,
and other physical conditions affecting such treatment plants and collection or distribution
systems and prescribe the skill, knowledge, and experience that the operator in charge must
have to supervise successfully the operation of the facilities so as to protect the public health,
prevent unlawful pollution, and protect the water resources of the state.
Acts 1972, No. 538, §3; Acts 1986, No. 983, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Redesignated from
R.S. 40:1143 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
