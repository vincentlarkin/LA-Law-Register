§2017.5.  Promotion of nurse education program
The department is further authorized and directed to cooperate with all agencies, both public and private, in promoting a program of nurse education, including the establishment wherever feasible of additional schools for training student nurses in state owned institutions where such schools do not exist and in private hospitals.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 38, §1.
