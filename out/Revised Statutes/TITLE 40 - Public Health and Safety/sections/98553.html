§5.2. Limitations

           Nothing in this Part shall be construed to authorize the state health officer or the Louisiana Department of Health to impose on any person any mode of treatment inconsistent with1 creed or tenets of any religious denomination of which he is an adherent, provided that sanitary and quarantine regulations are complied with by such person.

           Added by Acts 1982, No. 619, §1.

1The words "with creed" are as they appear in the enrolled Act.
