PART VI.  REGULATION OF TRANSPORTATION OF OIL AND

OIL FIELD EQUIPMENT

§1481.  Purpose

The legislature finds that the production of oil for the energy needs of the state and the nation is of vital concern and that a serious problem of theft of both the production and the means of production increases both the cost to the public and the danger to the public on the roads of the state.  Therefore, the legislature imposes certain requirements upon the transportation of oil and oil field equipment.  

Added by Acts 1982, No. 550, §1.
