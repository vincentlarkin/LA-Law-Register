§1139.8.  Domicile; service of process
A.  The domicile of the district shall be fixed by the commission and may be changed
at the pleasure of the commission.  All books and records of the district shall be kept at the
domicile.
B.  Service of process shall be made upon the director, and in his absence, upon the
chairman of the commission, and in his absence or their absence, then upon the vice-chairman of the commission.
