§31.67.  Funding

The provisions of this Part shall be implemented only to the extent that funds become available.  The office of public health, a public health unit, or their agents or providers may solicit funding from any and all sources, including private or federal funds, to implement these provisions.

Acts 2005, No. 210, §1.
