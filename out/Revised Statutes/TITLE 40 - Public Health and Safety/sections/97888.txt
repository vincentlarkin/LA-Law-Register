PART II.  MALODORANTS
§1891.  Application of Part
This Part applies only to retail distributing systems, subject to the jurisdiction of the Louisiana Public Service Commission.  Nothing in this Part applies to or affects:
(1)  Gas transported out of the state.
(2)  Gas transported in gas lines for industrial or commercial purposes or for re-sale.
(3)  Liquefied petroleum gas.
