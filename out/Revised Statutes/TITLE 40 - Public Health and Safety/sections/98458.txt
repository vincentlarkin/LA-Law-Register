§418.  Notification of mayor of adoption of consolidated authority
When the governing body of a municipality adopts a resolution declaring that there is a need within it for a consolidated housing authority, it shall promptly notify the mayor of the municipality of that adoption.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
