§1558.2.  Definitions

As used in this Part, the following words shall have the following meanings:

(1)  "Board" means the Volunteer Firefighters' Tuition Reimbursement Board.

(2)  "Combination fire department" means a department whose personnel is composed of both volunteer and professional firefighters.

(3)  "Recipient" means a person who has met the eligibility requirements and  is awarded tuition reimbursement from the Volunteer Firefighters' Tuition Reimbursement Fund.

Acts 2013, No. 310, §2.
