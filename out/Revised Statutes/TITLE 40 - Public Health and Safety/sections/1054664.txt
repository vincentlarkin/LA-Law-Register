§1105.8.2.  Cancer data; electronic notifications and reports
The Louisiana Tumor Registry shall develop and publish on its website a mechanism
by which individuals may elect to receive in electronic format notifications and reports
issued by the tumor registry.
Acts 2017, No. 373, §1.
