SUBPART B.  HEALTHCARE WORKPLACE VIOLENCE PREVENTION
§2199.11.  Purpose
The purpose of this Subpart is to protect public health and safety by establishing
policies and practices through which incidents of workplace violence in healthcare settings
can be properly addressed, mitigated, and, wherever possible, prevented.
Acts 2022, No. 461, §§1, 2B.
