§2010.5.  Confidentiality of records; liability

The ombudsman and any agency that receives information from the ombudsman shall maintain as confidential all matters relating to any inquiry or referral, including the identities of the residents, unless the resident or a legal representative consents in writing to the disclosure or unless ordered by a court to do so.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 515, §1.  Acts 1988, No. 1003, §1.
