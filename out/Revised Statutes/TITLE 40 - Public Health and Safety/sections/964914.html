§1263.2. Louisiana Organ Transplant Assistance Program

            The secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health shall establish in the office of the secretary a Louisiana Organ Transplant Assistance Program.

            Acts 1984, No. 849, §1; Acts 1992, No. 206, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.122 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
