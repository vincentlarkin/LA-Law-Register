§1113.7. Autologous donations

            The provisions of this Part shall not be construed or interpreted to prohibit or exclude autologous donations or preparation, handling and storage of autologous donations of blood, blood products, body tissue, or body fluids for the intended purpose of transfusions or reimplantation into the original donor.

            Acts 1987, No. 234, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.147 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
