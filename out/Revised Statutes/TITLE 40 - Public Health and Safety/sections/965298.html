§1295.3. Public information concerning smoking cessation

            A. The department shall establish and maintain on its internet website a link to the website of the Smoking Cessation Trust, along with summary information on programs and services offered by the trust.

            B. The department may feature the link to the website of the Smoking Cessation Trust and information on programs and services of the trust on any web page it maintains for any program or office if the link and information are posted in a conspicuous location.            Added by Acts 2015, No. 55, §§1, 2, eff. June 5, 2015.
