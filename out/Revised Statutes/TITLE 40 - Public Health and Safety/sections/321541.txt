§1730.43.  Purpose
It is the intent and purpose of this Part to institute minimum energy conservation
standards for new construction and all applicable alterations and repairs of state-owned and
state-funded buildings within Louisiana.
Acts 2005, No. 91, §1, eff. June 21, 2005; Acts 2022, No. 635, §1.
