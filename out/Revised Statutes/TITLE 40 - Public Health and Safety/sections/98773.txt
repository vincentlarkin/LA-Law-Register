§700.1.  Rules and regulations
The secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health shall have the authority to
promulgate rules and regulations, with the advice and recommendations of the Advisory
Committee on Water Treatment Devices, for the efficient enforcement of this Subpart.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 507, §1; Acts 2018, No. 206, §4.
