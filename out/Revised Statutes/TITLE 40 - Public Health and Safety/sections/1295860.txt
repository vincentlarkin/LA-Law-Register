SUBPART B.  SICKLE CELL DISEASE REGISTRY
§1125.11.  Definitions
As used in this Subpart, the following terms have the meanings ascribed to them in
this Section:
(1)  "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Health.
(2)  "Registry" means the sickle cell disease registry established by the provisions of
this Subpart.
Acts 2022, No. 647, §2.
