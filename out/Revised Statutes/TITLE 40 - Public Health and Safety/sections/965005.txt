§1061.12.  Born-Alive Infant Protection Act
A.  An infant at any stage of development who has survived an abortion procedure
resulting in his or her live birth shall be given reasonable and immediate medical care as
provided in R.S. 40:1061.13(C), whether the abortion was considered legal or illegal under
the law at the time that the abortion was performed.
B.  The provisions of R.S. 40:1061.1 shall apply to this Section.
