§425.  Disposal of property held in eliminated area

Any property held by regional or consolidated housing authority within a parish or municipality excluded from the area of operation of the particular authority shall be disposed of by the authority in the public interest as soon as practicable after the exclusion.

Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
