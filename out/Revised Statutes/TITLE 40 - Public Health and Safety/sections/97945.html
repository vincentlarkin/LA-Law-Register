§2009.43.  Culinary therapy authorized

Notwithstanding any contrary provision of law, rule, or regulation of any department or agency, or any local ordinance or code, licensed nursing homes or long-term care facilities may provide culinary therapy as part of a planned patient activity program for residents of said facilities.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 474, §1.
