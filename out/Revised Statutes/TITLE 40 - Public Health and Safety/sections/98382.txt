PART VI.  PUBLIC HEALTH FEES AND PERMITS
§31.31.  Definitions
As used in this Part:
(1)  "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Health except in instances of
the issuance of licenses and the collection of fees from transporters and haulers of sewage
sludge or biosolids, when "department" means the Department of Environmental Quality.
(2)  "Office" means the Louisiana Department of Health, office of public health.
Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 125, §1, eff. July 1, 2000; Acts 2008, No. 56, §3, eff.
July 1, 2009.
