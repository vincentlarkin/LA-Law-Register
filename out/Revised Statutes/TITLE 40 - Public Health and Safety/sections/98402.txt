§38.  Certified copies; issuance
A certified copy of a vital record in the custody of the vital records registry shall be
issued in accordance with other provisions of law governing disclosure of records under this
Title and regulations duly promulgated in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 1979, No. 776, §1.  Amended by Acts 1986, No. 876, §1; Acts 2024, No. 257,
§1, eff. May 24, 2024.
