§4.16.  Water systems; iron and manganese control
The office of public health of the Louisiana Department of Health shall promulgate
and adopt rules in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to implement iron and
manganese control requirements for water systems.
Acts 2014, No. 861, §2.
