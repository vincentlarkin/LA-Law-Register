§1193.2.  Definitions
As used in this Part, the following terms have the meaning ascribed in this Section:
(1)  "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Health.
(2)  "Legal representative" means a legal guardian or a legally appointed substitute
decision-maker who is authorized to act on behalf of a nursing home resident.
(3)(a)  "Monitoring device" means a surveillance instrument that transmits and
records activity and is not connected to the facility's computer network.
(b)  The term "monitoring device" shall not include a camera that records still images
exclusively.
(4)  "Nursing home" means a nursing facility or nursing home as defined in R.S.
40:2009.2.
(5)  "Ombudsman" means the administrator of the office of the state long-term care
ombudsman established within the office of elderly affairs by the provisions of R.S.
40:2010.2.
(6)  "Resident" means a person who is a resident of a nursing home.
