§2002.3.  Huey P. Long Medical Center
The state operated general hospital located in Pineville, Louisiana, heretofore known as Huey P. Long Charity Hospital, is hereby designated and shall hereafter be known as Huey P. Long Medical Center.
Acts 1987, No. 43, §2.
