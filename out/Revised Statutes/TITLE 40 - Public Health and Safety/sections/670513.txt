§2120.55.  Duties of the nursing facility
Prior to employing or contracting with a certified nurse aide, a nursing facility shall verify through the Louisiana's Certified Nurse Aide Registry that the certified nurse aide is currently certified and has a current state registration.
Acts 2009, No. 357, §1, eff. Aug. 15, 2010.
