PART II.  CHOLESTEROL SCREENING BY MOBILE
CHOLESTEROL SCREENING UNITS
§1213.1.  Purpose
It is a public policy of the state to promote the health, welfare, and safety of the
people of this state by attempting to ensure high standards for cholesterol screening by
mobile cholesterol screening units.
