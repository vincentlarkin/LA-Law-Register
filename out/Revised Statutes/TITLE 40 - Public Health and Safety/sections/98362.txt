§30.  Provisions of this Chapter not to affect Louisiana Department of Agriculture
Anything to the contrary notwithstanding, the provisions hereof shall not be construed to affect present laws and regulations administered by the Louisiana Department of Agriculture, particularly the provisions of R.S. 40:2277.*
Acts 1976, No. 346, §1.
*See, now, R.S. 3:4208.
