PART VI-E. LICENSING OF INTERMEDIATE CARE FACILITIES

FOR PERSONS WITH

DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES (ICF/DD)

§2180. Definitions

            As used in this Part, the following terms shall have the following meanings, except when the context clearly indicates otherwise:

            (1) "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Health.

            (2) "ICF/DD" means an intermediate care facility for people with developmental disabilities.

            Acts 2005, No. 128, §1, eff. June 22, 2005; Acts 2006, No. 163, §2.
