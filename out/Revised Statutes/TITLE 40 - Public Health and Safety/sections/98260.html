§25.  Entry on and inspection of premises

The parish health officer and every person duly authorized may enter, examine and inspect all grounds, structures, public buildings and public places in execution of a warrant issued in accordance with the constitution and the laws of Louisiana.  

Acts 1976, No. 346, §1.
