§2004.2.  Contract for sewer service for Central Louisiana State Hospital
The Louisiana Department of Health is authorized to enter into a
contract with the city of Pineville for sewerage services to be furnished by the
city of Pineville to the Central Louisiana State Hospital.  In consideration of
the expenditures made by the city of Pineville in providing said sewerage
services, the Louisiana Department of Health may agree to pay a minimum
charge of three thousand three hundred thirty-three dollars per month for one
hundred twenty months to the city of Pineville.
Added by Acts 1969, No. 98, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 680, §2.
