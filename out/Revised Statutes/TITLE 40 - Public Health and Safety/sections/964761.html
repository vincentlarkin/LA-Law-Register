§1201.2. Clinical preceptor training program

            A. The Louisiana Community and Technical College System is hereby authorized to develop and maintain a clinical preceptor nurse aide training program.

            B. Any program developed under the provisions of this Part shall be in compliance with all federal laws and regulations.

            Acts 2011, No. 50, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.22 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
