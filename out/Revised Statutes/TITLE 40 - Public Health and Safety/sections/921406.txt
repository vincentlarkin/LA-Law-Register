§1666.6.  Compensation; expenses
The members of the board shall receive per diem in the amount of fifty dollars per
day and shall be reimbursed for actual mileage in attending to official business.  The per
diem and the reimbursement of mileage expenses shall be made by the Department of Public
Safety and Corrections and shall be payable out of the special fund provided in R.S.
40:1666.2, provided, however, that no such expense shall be payable unless a residual
remains in the special fund after payment of all amounts due as extra compensation under
the provisions of R.S. 40:1666.1.
Acts 1967, No. 82, §3.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 21, §1; Acts 1973, No. 159, §1;
