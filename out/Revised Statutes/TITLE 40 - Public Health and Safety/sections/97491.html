SUBPART C.  ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION FOR STATE POLICE

OFFICERS AND DRIVERS LICENSE EXAMINERS

§1451.  Purpose

This law is enacted by the legislature in the exercise of the police power of the state, to promote the public peace and safety by providing better enforcement of the law.  

Added by Acts 1959, No. 50, §1.
