§1564.  Cost of performing functions

The cost of performing the functions assigned to the fire marshal under the provisions of this part shall be borne by the fire marshal fund in the state treasury, created by R.S. 22:835 and with other such funds as may be appropriated by the legislature for such purposes.

Amended by Acts 1968, No. 418, §1; Acts 1974, No. 234, §1; Acts 2008, No. 415, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
