§2024.7.  Financial and human resources obligations
The Louisiana Department of Health may, at its discretion, secure financial and
human resources from, or create formal partnerships with, external entities, in order to meet
its obligations as described in this Part.
Acts 2021, No. 320, §2, See Act.
