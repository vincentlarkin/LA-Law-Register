§1227.7.  Remote monitoring equipment and network requirements
Any telehealth equipment and network used for remote patient monitoring services
shall meet all of the following requirements:
(1)  Compliance with applicable standards of the United States Food and Drug
Administration.
(2)  Maintenance of telehealth equipment in good repair and free from safety hazards.
(3)  Installation of only new or sanitized equipment in the patient's home setting.
(4)  Availability of technical and clinical support services for the patient user.
Acts 2024, No. 749, §1.
