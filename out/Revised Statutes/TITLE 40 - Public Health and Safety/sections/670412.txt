PART X-F.  EPHEDRINE, PSEUDOEPHEDRINE, AND
PHENYLPROPANOLAMINE MONITORING ACT
§1049.1.  Short title
This Part may be referred to and may be cited as the "Ephedrine, Pseudoephedrine, and Phenylpropanolamine Monitoring Act".
Acts 2009, No. 314, §1.
