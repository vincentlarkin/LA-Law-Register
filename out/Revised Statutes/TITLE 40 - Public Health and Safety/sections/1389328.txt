§31.74.  Report
The department shall produce an annual report on the results obtained through the
surveillance system to be submitted to the secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health
and the House and Senate committees on health and welfare.
Acts 2024, No. 511, §1, eff. June 10, 2024.
