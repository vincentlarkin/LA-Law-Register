§2002.1.  Lallie Kemp Regional Medical Center
The state operated general hospital located in Independence, Louisiana, heretofore known as Lallie Kemp Charity Hospital, is hereby designated and shall hereafter be known as Lallie Kemp Regional Medical Center.
Acts 1987, No. 43, §2.
