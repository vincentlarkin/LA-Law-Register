§1217.11. Funding

            The medical center may seek and receive monies from any source, including federal funds, grants, and gifts which shall be expended for the purposes designated in this Part.

            Acts 1995, No. 951, §1; Acts 1997, No. 557, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.91 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
