§5.1.  Sanitation; inspection of railroad property
The secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health or his designee shall have the
authority to go upon railroad property and inspect the premises for sanitary conditions and
enforce the rules and regulations pertaining to sanitation.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 241, §1.
