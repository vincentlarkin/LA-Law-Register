§599.23. Conclusive and binding determination of authority

            Notwithstanding any other provision of this Part, in a proceeding involving the validity or enforceability of a bond or the security for a bond, the determination of an authority under this Part is conclusive and binding.

            Acts 2025, No. 443, §1.
