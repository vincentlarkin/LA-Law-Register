§792.  Penalty
Whoever violates any of the provisions of this Subpart, or the orders, rules, or regulations promulgated by the department under authority hereof, shall be fined, for each offense, not more than one hundred dollars or imprisoned for not more than thirty days, or both.
Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
