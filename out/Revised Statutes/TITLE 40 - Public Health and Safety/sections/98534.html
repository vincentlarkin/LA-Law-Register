§486.  Rights of action

Nothing contained in this Chapter shall create, expressly or by implication, any right, claim, or cause of action in favor of anyone in connection with any failure of a housing authority to exercise any one or more of its discretionary powers.

Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
