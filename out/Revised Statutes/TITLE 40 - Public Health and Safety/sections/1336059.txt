§2886.  Federal preemption
A.  Nothing in this Chapter is to be construed or applied to be less restrictive than
federal law for a person or entity regulated by this Chapter.
B.  Nothing in this Chapter is to be construed or applied to be in conflict with any of
the following:
(1)  Applicable federal law and related regulations.
(2)  Other laws of this state if the state law is compatible with applicable federal law.
C.  Limited distribution of a drug required under 21 U.S.C. 355-1 is not to be
construed as a violation of this Chapter.
Acts 2023, No. 358, §1.
