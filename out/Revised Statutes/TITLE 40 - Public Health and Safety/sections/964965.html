§1291.23. Development of smoke-free policies

            A.(1) Public postsecondary education institutions shall develop smoke-free policies for its campuses.

            (2) For the purposes of this Section, "smoke-free" means the prohibition of smoking as defined in R.S. 40:1291.3(14).

            B.(1) Nothing in this Section shall prohibit a public postsecondary education institution from developing a tobacco-free policy for its campus.

            (2) For the purposes of this Section, "tobacco-free" means the prohibition on the use of tobacco derived or containing products, including but not limited to cigarettes, cigars, cigarillos, pipes, hookah-smoked products, and oral tobacco products.

            C. The provisions of this Section shall not supersede the provisions of R.S. 40:1291.11(A).
