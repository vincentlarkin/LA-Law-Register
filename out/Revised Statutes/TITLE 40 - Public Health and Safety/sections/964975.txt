§1300.36.  Duty of others
A.  Any person, other than a law enforcement officer or medical practitioner, who
finds a disabled person shall make a reasonable effort to notify a law enforcement officer or
medical practitioner.  If a law enforcement officer or medical practitioner is not present, any
person who finds a disabled person may make a reasonable search for an identifying device,
and, if the identifying device is found, may make a reasonable search for an identification
card of the type described in Section 1300.33(B).  If a device or card is located, the person
making the search shall attempt promptly to bring its contents to the attention of a law
enforcement officer or medical practitioner.
B.  There shall be no cause of action arising from a reasonable search to locate an
identifying device or identification card as authorized by Subsection A of this Section.
C.  The duties imposed by this Section shall be in addition to, and not in limitation
of, any other duties existing under the laws of this state.
