§451.  Indemnification

A local housing authority may indemnify any commissioner, officer, or employee of the authority as provided in R.S. 40:516.

Amended by Acts 1950, No. 401, §8; Acts 1960, No. 278, §4; Acts 1968, No. 236, §1; Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 19, §§1, 2; Acts 1988, No. 103, §1; Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
