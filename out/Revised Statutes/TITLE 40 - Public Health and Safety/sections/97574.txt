CHAPTER 7.  FIRE PREVENTION OR PROTECTION
PART I.  FIRE PROTECTION DISTRICTS
§1491.  Definitions
When used in this Part, the following terms have these meanings:
(1)  "District" means a fire protection district created under the provisions of this Part.
(2)  "Board" means a board of commissioners, governing a fire protection district.
Amended by Acts 1975, No. 17, §1.
