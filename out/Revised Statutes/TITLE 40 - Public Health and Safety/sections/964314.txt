§1121.10.  Rules and regulations
The secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health shall make all necessary rules
and regulations for the carrying out of the purposes of this Subpart.  These rules and
regulations shall be printed in pamphlet or folder form in sufficient numbers for free
distribution among physicians, sanitariums, and the general public.
Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Redesignated from R.S.
40:1067 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
