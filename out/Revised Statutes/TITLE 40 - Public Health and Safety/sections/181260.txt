§940.52.  Commercial fisherman's catch; transportation and sale
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law to the contrary, a commercial fisherman shall be exempt from having to possess a "mobile food vendor's permit" while selling his catch directly from either his boat or his place of residence.  No commercial fisherman shall be allowed to sell oysters under the provisions of this Section.
Acts 2003, No. 176, §1.
