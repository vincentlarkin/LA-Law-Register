§1251.4.  Definitions
As used in this Part, the following terms shall have the following meanings:
(1)  "Medicaid Buy-In Program" means the Louisiana Medicaid Optional program
to provide the opportunity for working persons with disabilities to buy access to needed
health care and support services.
(2)  "Persons with disabilities" means those citizens of Louisiana who meet the Social
Security Disability criteria.
