§1211.2.  Notification of results
A.  Each patient shall be given the opportunity to name a physician to receive the
results of any screening mammogram performed without the direction by prescription of a
licensed practitioner.
B.  The report of results mailed to the patient and to any physician named pursuant
to Subsection A of this Section shall clearly state whether the need for any follow-up care
is indicated by the mammogram.
