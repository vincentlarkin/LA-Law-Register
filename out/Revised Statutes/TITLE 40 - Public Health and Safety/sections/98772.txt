PART III-A.  WATER
SUBPART A.  WATER TREATMENT DEVICES
§700.  Definition of terms
As used in this Subpart, unless the context otherwise indicates, the
following terms shall have the meaning ascribed to them in this Section:
(1)  "Advertisement" includes all representations of fact or opinion
disseminated to the public in any manner or by any means.
(2)  "Dealer" means any person or entity engaged in the business of
selling water treatment devices to the public.
(3)  "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Health and
"secretary" means the secretary thereof.
(4)  "Water treatment device" means any instrument or contrivance sold
at retail for residential use and designed to be added to the plumbing system
of a water supply intended for human consumption to improve that water
supply by any means including, but not limited to, filtration, purification,
distillation, or conditioning.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 507, §1.
