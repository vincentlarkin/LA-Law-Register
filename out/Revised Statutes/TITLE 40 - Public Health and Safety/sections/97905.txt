§2002.7.  E. A. Conway Medical Center
The state operated general hospital located in Monroe, Louisiana heretofore known as E. A. Conway Memorial Hospital, is hereby designated and shall be hereafter known as E. A. Conway Medical Center.
Acts 1991, No. 390, §3, eff. July 8, 1991.
