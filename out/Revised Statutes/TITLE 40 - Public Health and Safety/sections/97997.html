§2017.8.  Administration of oaths and taking of testimony

The secretary, the general counsel, the personnel officer in the central office, and all superintendents and business administrators shall have the power to administer oaths and take testimony of witnesses anywhere in the state on matters relating to the business of the department.

Added by Acts 1958, No. 38, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 680, §53.
