§1169.6.  Action against physician license prohibited
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Louisiana State Board of
Medical Examiners shall not revoke, fail to renew, or take any other action against the
license of a physician issued pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 37:1261, et seq. based solely
upon the recommendation of the physician to an eligible patient regarding, or prescription
for, or treatment with, an investigational drug, biological product, or device when such
recommendation, prescription, or treatment is undertaken in strict conformance with the
provisions of this Subpart.
