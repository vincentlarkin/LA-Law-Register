§1631.  Implementation

The state fire marshal may promulgate rules and regulations, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act, necessary to effectuate the purposes of this Act*.

Acts 2007, No. 409, §1, eff. Aug. 31, 2009.

*Acts 2007, No. 409
