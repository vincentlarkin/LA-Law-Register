§1248.2.  Purpose
The purpose of this Subpart is to generate revenue by collecting from certain
institutional providers a local hospital assessment payment to be used to provide the
nonfederal share of a Medicaid payment program directly benefitting the residents of a
parish.
Acts 2020, No. 330, §1.
