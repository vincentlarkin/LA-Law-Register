§1250.12.  Annual review of rates
A. The department may conduct annual reviews of all rates by service category and
shall make a determination of the level of sufficiency of each rate based on a review of all
pertinent data.
B.  Any reduction in disability rates to providers must be approved by the Joint
Legislative Committee on the Budget.
Acts 2019, No. 381, §1.
