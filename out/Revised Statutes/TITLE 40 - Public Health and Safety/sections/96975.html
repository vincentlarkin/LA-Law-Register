§1032.  Permit

No animal control agency or facility shall purchase, possess, or administer sodium pentobarbital to sick, homeless, injured, or unwanted pets or other domestic and wild animals for their humane euthanasia without the permit required by this Part.

Acts 1987, No. 225, §2; Acts 2006, No. 676, §3, eff. July 1, 2006.
