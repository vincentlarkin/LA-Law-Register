§1139.8. Domicile; service of process

            A. The domicile of the district shall be fixed by the commission and may be changed at the pleasure of the commission. All books and records of the district shall be kept at the domicile.

            B. Service of process shall be made upon the director, and in his absence, upon the chairman of the commission, and in his absence or their absence, then upon the vice-chairman of the commission.

            Acts 2010, No. 887, §1, eff. July 2, 2010; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1236.28 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
