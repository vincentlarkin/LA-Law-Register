PART X. VENEREAL DISEASES

SUBPART A. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§1121.1. Definition

            In this Subpart, "venereal disease" means syphilis, gonorrhea, chancroid, or any other infectious disease primarily transmitted from one person to another by means of a sexual act.

            Acts 2007, No. 153, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1061 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
