§2014.1.  Superintendents to furnish reports on patients
The superintendents of all general hospitals administered by the
Louisiana Department of Health are hereby authorized and directed to furnish,
upon written request of the doctor who refers the patient to such hospital, a
report to the doctor on such patient when the patient is discharged showing the
diagnosis, laboratory and x-ray findings and treatment prescribed and are
further authorized and required to furnish the patient or his attorney or the
patient's heirs or their attorney, upon written request, a full report on such
patient.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 36, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 680,
§48.
