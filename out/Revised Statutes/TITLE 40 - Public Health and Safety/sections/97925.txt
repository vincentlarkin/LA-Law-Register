§2009.19.  Notification of change
It shall be the duty of the department to apprise all nursing homes licensed under R.S. 40:2009.6 in writing of any state law, minimum standard, rule, regulation, or order promulgated by the department and of any changes therein with which the nursing homes will be expected to comply.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 687, §2.
