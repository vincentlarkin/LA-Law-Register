TITLE 40

PUBLIC HEALTH AND SAFETY

CHAPTER 1. DIVISION OF HEALTH AND HEALTH OFFICERS

PART I. STATE DIVISION OF HEALTH

§1. Assistant secretary

            The office of public health shall be administered by an assistant secretary appointed by the governor in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 36:257.

            Acts 1976, No. 346, §1. Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2018, No. 206, §4.
