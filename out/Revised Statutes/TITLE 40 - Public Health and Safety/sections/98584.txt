§520.  Insurance
A housing authority may purchase and maintain insurance on behalf of a representative against liability asserted against or incurred by a representative who is or was a representative of a housing authority or who, while a representative, is or was serving at the request of the housing authority as a director, officer, partner, trustee, employee, or agent of a partnership, joint venture, employee benefit plan, or other enterprise.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
