§1381.  Concealed weapons; employees; highway patrol
A.  All employees, officers, or agents of the division of state police may carry arms and weapons, concealed or openly, while in the actual performance of any duty or while under assignment to any duty under this Subpart.
B.  The police employees of the division shall be the highway patrol of the state and as such shall perform only real police duties as provided by R.S. 40:1379.
Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 4, §1, eff. April 19, 1996.
