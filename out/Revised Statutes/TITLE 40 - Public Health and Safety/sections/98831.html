§782.  Rules and regulations

The department shall make, amend, and rescind such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Subpart, including, but without being limited to, such orders, rules, and regulations as it is specifically authorized and directed to make in this Subpart.  

Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
