§2141.  Denial, suspension or revocation of license; appeal

The procedure of denial, suspension, or revocation of licenses, and appeals therefrom, will be the same as provided for in the licensing of hospitals, as contained in Louisiana R.S. 40:2110.

Added by Acts 1976, No. 596, §1.
