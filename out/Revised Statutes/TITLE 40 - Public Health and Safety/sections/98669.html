§592.7.  Title; conclusive proof of compliance

Any instrument executed by the governing authority purporting to convey all right, title, or interest in any property under this Part shall be conclusively presumed to have been executed in compliance with the provisions of this Part insofar as title or other interest of any bona fide purchasers, lessees, or transferees of the property is concerned.  

Acts 1984, No. 571, §1.
