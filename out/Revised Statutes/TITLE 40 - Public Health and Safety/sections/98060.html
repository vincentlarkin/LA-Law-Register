§2117.7.  Civil fines for violations; appeals

Civil fines for violations by applicants and licensees shall be assessed and collected by the department, and administrative appeals with respect to such fines shall be available, in accordance with R.S. 40:2199.

Acts 1999, No. 650, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
