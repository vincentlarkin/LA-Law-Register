§505.  Change in family composition

In the event of a change in the composition of tenant household, the local housing authority may determine which member or members of the household are entitled to continued occupancy for the unexpired lease term or periodic tenancy.  A local housing authority may adopt such rules and procedures governing such determinations as it deems appropriate.

Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
