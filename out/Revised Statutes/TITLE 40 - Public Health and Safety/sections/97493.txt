§1453.  Credit to special fund
All appropriations made for the payment of the supplemental salaries provided by this Sub-part shall be credited to the special fund in the state treasury created by R.S. 40:1667.2, and the funds so credited shall be and remain dedicated to the payment of the supplemental salaries provided in R.S. 40:1452.
Added by Acts 1959, No. 50, §1; Acts 2014, No. 158, §§3 and 7.
