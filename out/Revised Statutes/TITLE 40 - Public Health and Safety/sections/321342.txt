§2180.3.  Input from the office for citizens with developmental disabilities
Any office, bureau, or agency that licenses ICF/DD's shall prepare proposed rules, regulations, and standards in consultation with the office for citizens with developmental disabilities.  Such consultation will include assurance that all federal and state laws and regulations, including the provisions of this Chapter regarding program requirements, are included in the licensing requirements.
Acts 2005, No. 128, §1, eff. June 22, 2005.
