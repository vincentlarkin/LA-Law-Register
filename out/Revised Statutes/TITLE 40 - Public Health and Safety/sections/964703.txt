§1159.7.  Right of adult to refuse treatment as to his own person not abridged
Nothing contained herein shall be construed to abridge any right of a person eighteen
years of age or over to refuse to consent to medical or surgical treatment as to his own
person.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 798, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.56 by HCR 84
of 2015 R.S.
