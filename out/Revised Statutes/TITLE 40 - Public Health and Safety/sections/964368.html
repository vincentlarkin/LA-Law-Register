§1079.17. Authorized resources

            A school or facility may solicit and use any available state, federal, or private resources, which may include but shall not be limited to funds, in-kind resources, or volunteer services, for the purpose of providing preventive counseling services or treatment for alcohol or drug abuse.

            Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 18, §1, eff. July 6, 1994; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1098.7 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
