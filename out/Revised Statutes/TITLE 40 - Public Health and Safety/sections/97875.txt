§1844.  Meetings
The commission shall hold not more than one meeting per month at its domicile and no meeting shall be for longer than is absolutely necessary to transact the business of the commission, provided, however, that the commission may hold three of its monthly meetings at places in the state elsewhere than at its domicile.
Acts 1950, No. 63, §1.
