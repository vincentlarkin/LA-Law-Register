§713.  Contents of application; fee
A.  The application for the permit required under R.S. 40:711 shall contain the name of the applicant, his address, the location of the manufacturing plants, the name of the drink or beverage, and any other pertinent information prescribed by the department in pursuance of the provisions of this Part.
B.  Repealed by Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 125, §2, eff. July 1, 2000.
Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 125, §2, eff. July 1, 2000.
