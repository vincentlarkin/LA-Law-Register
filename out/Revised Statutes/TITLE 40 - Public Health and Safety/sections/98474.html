§433.  Issue subpoenas and commissions

A local housing authority may issue subpoenas requiring the attendance of witnesses or the production of books and papers and commissions for the examination of witnesses who are out of state, unable to attend before the authority, or are excused from attendance.

Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
