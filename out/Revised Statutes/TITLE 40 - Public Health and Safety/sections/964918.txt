PART IV.  POISON CONTROL CENTER
§1267.1.  Poison control center; establishment
The Louisiana Department of Health shall provide for the establishment of a
statewide poison control center at the University of Louisiana at Monroe.  The university
may cooperate with public and private agencies and health care institutions in the operation
of the center.  The center shall include operation of a statewide toll-free telephone number.
