§599.5. Board of directors
An ordinance that creates an authority shall establish a board to govern the authority
and shall include provisions for:
(1) Appointment of procedures.
(2) Powers of the board.
(3) Removal procedures.
(4) Term lengths.
(5) The election of a chair.
Acts 2025, No. 443, §1.
