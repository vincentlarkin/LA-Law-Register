§1567.1.  Fees for copies of fire and investigation reports

The fire marshal shall furnish copies of fire and investigation reports to any person upon the payment of two dollars per page for fire reports and other reports on letter size paper and five dollars per page for investigation reports and other reports on legal size paper.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 524, §1, eff. July 19, 1977.
