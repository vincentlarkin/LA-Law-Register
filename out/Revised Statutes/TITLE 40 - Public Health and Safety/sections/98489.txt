§447.  Forgiveness or compromise of indebtedness
A local housing authority may forgive, compromise, or forbear from collecting or enforcing, wholly or partially, temporarily or permanently, any debt or obligation owed to the local housing authority.
Amended by Acts 1960, No. 278, §3; Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
