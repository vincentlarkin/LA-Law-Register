§964.l.  Treatment of controlled analogues

A controlled substance analogue shall be treated, for the purposes of any state law and to the extent intended for human consumption, as a controlled dangerous substance in either Schedule I or Schedule II of R.S. 40:964.

Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 34, §2; Acts 2001, No. 1036, §1.
