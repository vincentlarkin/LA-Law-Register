SUBCHAPTER C. SMOKING

PART I. LOUISIANA SMOKEFREE AIR ACT

SUBPART A. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§1291.1. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Smokefree Air Act."

            Acts 2006, No. 815, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.251 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
