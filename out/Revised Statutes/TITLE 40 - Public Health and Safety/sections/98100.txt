PART VI-A.  LICENSING OF OUTPATIENT ABORTION FACILITIES
§2175.1.  Short title
A.  This Part may be cited as the "Outpatient Abortion Facility Licensing Law".
B.  The provisions of R.S. 40:1061.1 shall apply to this Part.
