§2620.  Bar to collateral action
No person claiming an interest in property subject to forfeiture may commence or maintain any action against the state concerning the validity of the alleged interest other than as provided in this Chapter.
Acts 1989, No. 375, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
