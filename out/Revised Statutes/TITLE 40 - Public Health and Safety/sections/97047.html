CHAPTER 5-B. HEALTH PROVISIONS:

DISEASES AND CONDITIONS

PART I. ALZHEIMER'S SPECIAL CARE DISCLOSURE

§1101. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Alzheimer's Special Care Disclosure Act".

            Acts 1997, No. 909, §1, eff. July 10, 1997; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.121 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.

NOTE: Former R.S. 40:1101 redesignated to R.S. 40:1085.1 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
