§1730.60.  Approval of design
Each page of the designs, plans, and specifications shall bear a stamp of approval by the state fire marshal if the designs, plans, and specifications satisfy the requirements set forth in R.S. 40:1730.59.
Acts 2007, No. 364, §1; Acts 2009, No. 514, §1.
