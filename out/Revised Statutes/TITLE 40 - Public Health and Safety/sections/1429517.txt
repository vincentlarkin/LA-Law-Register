§599.17. Civil action
A. An authority may bring a civil action to prevent, restrain, or enjoin unlawful
removal of any property from real property held by the authority.
B.(1) An authority shall be made a party to any action to set aside:
(a) Title to property the authority holds.
(b) The sale of property by the authority.
(2) A hearing in an action under this Subsection may not be held until the authority
is served.
Acts 2025, No. 443, §1.
