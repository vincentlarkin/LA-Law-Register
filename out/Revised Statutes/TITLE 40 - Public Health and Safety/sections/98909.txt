§987.  Injunctions
Any district court of this state shall have jurisdiction in proceedings in accordance
with the rules of such courts to enjoin violations of this Part and in accordance with the Code
of Civil Procedure and other laws of this state.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 634, §1; Acts 2018, No. 206, §4.
