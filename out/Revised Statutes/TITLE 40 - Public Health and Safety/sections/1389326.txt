§31.72.  ALS disease surveillance system
A.  The Louisiana Department of Health shall establish an amyotrophic lateral
sclerosis (ALS) surveillance system within the office of public health to collect, analyze,
interpret, and disseminate data relative to individuals living with ALS in Louisiana.
B.  In establishing the surveillance system, the department shall require reporting
sources to report information on ALS to the office of public health.
Acts 2024, No. 511, §1, eff. June 10, 2024.
