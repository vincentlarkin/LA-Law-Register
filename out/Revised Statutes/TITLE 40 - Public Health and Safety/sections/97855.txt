§1788.  Identification with number or other mark; obliteration or alteration of number or
mark
A.  Each manufacturer, importer, and dealer in any firearm shall identify it with a
number or other identification mark approved by the department and shall mark or stamp or
otherwise place the number or mark thereon in a manner approved by the department.
B. No one shall obliterate, remove, change, or alter this number or mark.
Acts 2016, No. 340, §1.
