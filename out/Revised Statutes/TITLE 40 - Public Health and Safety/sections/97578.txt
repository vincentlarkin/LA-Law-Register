§1493.1.  Fire District Number 2 of Rapides Parish; extension of boundaries
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the governing authority of Rapides Parish is authorized to extend the boundaries of Fire District Number 2 of Rapides Parish so that it includes all of Wards 1 and 8 outside the city of Alexandria and Wards 2, 3, 5, and 7.
Acts 1984, No. 536, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1984.
