§404.1.  Housing authorities of the cities of Gonzales and St. Martinville; per diem
Each commissioner of the housing authorities of the cities of Gonzales and St. Martinville may receive a per diem, to be paid from funds of the respective housing authority, for a maximum of twenty-four days per year for attendance at meetings of the respective housing authority in amounts not to exceed the following:
(1)  Fifty dollars for the chairman of the authority.
(2)  Twenty-five dollars for all other members.
