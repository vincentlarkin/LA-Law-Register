§1098.3.  Healthcare professionals; disciplinary actions
If a professional or occupational licensing board finds by a preponderance of the
evidence that a healthcare professional licensed or certified by the board has violated the
provisions of this Part, the board shall revoke any professional or occupational license or
certificate held by the healthcare professional for a minimum of two years.
Acts 2023, No. 466, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2024.
