§2009.10.  Department reports relating to recipients of public assistance
The department shall promptly report to the office of family security, the licensing status or any change thereof, of any nursing home that has any patrons who are recipients of public assistance.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 188, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 680, §11; Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
