PART II. CHOLESTEROL SCREENING BY MOBILE

CHOLESTEROL SCREENING UNITS

§1213.1. Purpose

            It is a public policy of the state to promote the health, welfare, and safety of the people of this state by attempting to ensure high standards for cholesterol screening by mobile cholesterol screening units.

            Acts 1991, No. 411, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.191 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
