§2198.13.  Annual fee; use of proceeds

There shall be an annual license fee to be set by the department not to exceed one thousand dollars for any license issued in accordance with the provisions of this Part.  Monies collected for annual fees shall be used for the investigation and enforcement of the provisions of this Part.

Acts 2005, No. 488, §1, eff. July 11, 2005.
