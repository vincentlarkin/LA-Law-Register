§2140.  Application
An application for a license shall be made to the licensing agency on forms provided by it and shall contain such information as the department reasonably requires, which may include affirmative evidence of ability to comply with such reasonable standards, rules, and regulations as are lawfully prescribed hereunder and information required for health planning pursuant to R.S. 36:256(B).  Additional information required by the licensing agency or for health planning shall be supplied on supplemental forms as needed.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 596, §1; Acts 1985, No. 342, §2, eff. July 9, 1985.
