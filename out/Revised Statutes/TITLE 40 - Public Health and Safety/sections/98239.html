§2405.3.  Unlawful activity by non-accredited training centers

It shall be unlawful for any training center to offer, conduct, award any certificates or documents purporting to evidence completion of, or solicit prospective trainees for a basic law enforcement training course, unless same is approved and accredited by the council.

Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 108, §1.
