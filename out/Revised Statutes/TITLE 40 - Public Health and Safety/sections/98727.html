§620.  Certification of coal-tar colors for drugs

The department may promulgate regulations for the certification of coal-tar colors which are harmless and suitable for use in drugs for purposes of coloring only.  

Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
