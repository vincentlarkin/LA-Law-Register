§1666.2.  Special fund

For the payment of the supplemental salaries and expenses of administration provided by this Part, there is hereby created a special fund in the treasury of the state of Louisiana, to which the appropriations hereinafter made shall be credited, and the funds so credited shall be and remain dedicated to the payment of such supplemental salaries and expenses of administration, as provided in R.S. 40:1666.1 and R.S. 40:1666.6.

Acts 1963, No. 82, §3.  Amended by Acts 1967, No. 82, §1; Acts 1973, No. 187, §1; Acts 1974, No. 296, §1; Acts 2014, No. 158, §3, eff. May 22, 2014; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2003.  See Acts 2014, No. 158, §§3 and 7.
