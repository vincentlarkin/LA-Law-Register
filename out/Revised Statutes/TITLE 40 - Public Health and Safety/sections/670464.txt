§653.  Food processing plants; recall plan
A.  Beginning January 1, 2011, every food processing plant operating within this state shall maintain a written food recall plan which shall be immediately available for review by the department upon request.
B.  The secretary shall promulgate rules and regulations, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act, establishing minimum standards and requirements for a written food recall plan.
C.  Any person or firm operating a food processing plant that violates the provisions of this Section shall be subject to a civil fine of not more than five hundred dollars.
Acts 2009, No. 341, §1, eff. July 6, 2009.
