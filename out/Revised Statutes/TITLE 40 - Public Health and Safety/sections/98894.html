§977.  Order forms

Controlled dangerous substances in Schedules I and II shall be distributed only pursuant to an order form.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 634, §1.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 2006, No. 834, §1.
