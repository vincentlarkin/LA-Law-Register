§1269.4.  Penalty
Whoever violates any provisions of any rule or regulation of the department adopted
hereunder, shall be fined not more than one hundred dollars, or imprisoned for not more than
thirty days, or both, for each offense.
Added by Acts 1954, No. 663, §1.  Amended by Acts 1974, No. 601, §1; Acts 1978,
No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1278 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
