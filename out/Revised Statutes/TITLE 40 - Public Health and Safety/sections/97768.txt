§1674.  Construction of scaffolds, etc., to bear certain weight; overcrowding or overloading
All swinging or stationary platforms, scaffolding, or similar devices shall be so constructed as to bear four times the maximum weight required to be dependent thereon when in use.  These platforms, scaffolds, or similar devices shall not be so loaded or crowded as to render them unsafe or dangerous.
