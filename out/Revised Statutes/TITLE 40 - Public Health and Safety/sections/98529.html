§481.  Issuance of debt instruments on behalf of others

A local housing authority may issue bonds, notes, certificates, or other instruments evidencing indebtedness on behalf of others to carry out any purpose authorized by this Chapter.

Added by Acts 1950, No. 401, §23.  Amended by Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
