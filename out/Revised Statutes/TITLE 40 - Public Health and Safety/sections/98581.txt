§518.  Advance for expenses
A.  A housing authority may in its discretion pay for or reimburse the reasonable expenses incurred by a representative who is a party to a proceeding in advance of final disposition of the proceeding.
B.  Any authorization of payments under this Section shall be made by resolution of the housing authority.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
