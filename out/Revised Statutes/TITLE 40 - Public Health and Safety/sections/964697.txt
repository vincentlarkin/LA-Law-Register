§1159.2.  Subpart not applicable to abortion and sterilization
The provisions of this Subpart shall not apply in any manner whatsoever to the
subjects of abortion and sterilization, which subjects shall continue to be governed by
existing law independently of the terms and provisions of this Subpart.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 798, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.51 by HCR 84
of 2015 R.S.
