§1730.42.  Definitions
(1)  "Historic buildings" means those buildings specifically designated as historically
significant by the state historic preservation officer or by official action of a local
government.
(2)  "International Energy Conservation Code" means the document developed by the
International Code Council, Inc., entitled "International Energy Conservation Code", also
referred to as "IECC".
