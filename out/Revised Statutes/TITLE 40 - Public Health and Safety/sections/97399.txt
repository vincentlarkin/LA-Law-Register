§1309.  Rules and regulations
The governor may prescribe such general administrative rules for the conduct of the affairs of the department, not inconsistent with law, as he thinks necessary or expedient.
The director of public safety may prescribe such administrative rules as he thinks necessary or expedient for the proper conduct of the work of the department and for making effective the provisions of law, so long as these rules are not inconsistent with the law or with any general rules prescribed by the governor.
