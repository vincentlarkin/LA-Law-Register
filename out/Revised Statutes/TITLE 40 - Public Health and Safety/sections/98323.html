§2719.  Fees

The department shall establish and collect a permit fee of one hundred fifty dollars and an annual permit renewal fee of one hundred ten dollars.

Acts 1990, No. 587, §1, eff. July 19, 1990; Acts 1992, No. 432, §1; Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 125, §1, eff. July 1, 2000.
