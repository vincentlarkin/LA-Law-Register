PART II-D.  LICENSING OF HOME- AND COMMUNITY-BASED

SERVICE PROVIDERS

§2120.1.  Purpose

The purpose of this Part is to further provide for the operation and maintenance of home- and community-based services for elderly persons and people with disabilities or mental illness.

Acts 2005, No. 483, §1, eff. July 12, 2005; Acts 2008, No. 839, §3, eff. July 8, 2008.
