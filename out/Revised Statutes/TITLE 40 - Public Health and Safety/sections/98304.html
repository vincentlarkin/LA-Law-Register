§27.  Salaries and personnel benefits of health unit

Where a health unit is established or partially maintained as provided in the preceding Section, the salaries and personnel benefits may be paid in part by funds from the state and parish, except, that if said employees are parish or municipal employees, all salaries and personnel benefits shall be paid entirely by parish funds including any increases in salaries or personnel benefits.  

Acts 1976, No. 346, §1.
