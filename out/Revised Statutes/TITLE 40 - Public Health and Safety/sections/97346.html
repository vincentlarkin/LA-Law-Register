PART IV. UNIFORM DUTIES TO DISABLED PERSONS

§1300.31. Title

            This Part may be referred to as the Uniform Duties to Disabled Persons Act.

            Added by Acts 1978, No. 399, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.71 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.

NOTE: Former R.S. 40:1300.31 redesignated as R.S. 40:1191.1 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
