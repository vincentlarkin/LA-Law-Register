§1113.7.  Autologous donations
The provisions of this Part shall not be construed or interpreted to prohibit or exclude
autologous donations or preparation, handling and storage of autologous donations of blood,
blood products, body tissue, or body fluids for the intended purpose of transfusions or
reimplantation into the original donor.
