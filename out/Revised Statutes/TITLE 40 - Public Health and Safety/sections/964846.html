§1217.15. Rules and regulations

            The medical center shall adopt and promulgate such rules and regulations as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this Part in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.

            Acts 1995, No. 951, §1; Acts 1997, No. 557, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.95 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
