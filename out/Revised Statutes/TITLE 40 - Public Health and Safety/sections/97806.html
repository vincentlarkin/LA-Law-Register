§1730.32.  Imposition of fees

Nothing in this Part shall prohibit the governing authority of a parish or municipality from imposing fees necessary to implement and continue the provisions required by this Part as provided by law.

Acts 2005, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 12, §2, eff. Nov. 29, 2005.
