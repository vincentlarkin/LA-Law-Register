§2134.  Applicability
The provisions of this Part shall apply only to ambulatory surgical centers and shall not apply to hospitals, as defined by R.S. 40:2102, or nursing homes, as defined in R.S. 40:2009.2.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 596, §1; Acts 1999, No. 696, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
