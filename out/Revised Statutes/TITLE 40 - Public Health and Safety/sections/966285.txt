§1300.62. Definitions
As used in this Part, the following terms have the following meanings ascribed to
them unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:
(1) "Local government" means a parish or municipality, as provided pursuant to
Article VI, Sections 1 and 2 of the Constitution of Louisiana.
(2) "Mitragynine speciosa" shall have the same meaning as defined in R.S. 14:91.10.
Acts 2023, No. 416, §2.
