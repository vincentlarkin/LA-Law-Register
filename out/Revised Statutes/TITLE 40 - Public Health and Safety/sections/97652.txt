§1562.1.  Organization of fire marshal's office
The fire marshal's office shall be organized into the following sections:
(1)  Administrative.
(2)  Engineering.
(3)  Inspection.
(4)  Investigation.
(5)  Fire and emergency training.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 231, §1; Acts 2022, No. 231, §3.
