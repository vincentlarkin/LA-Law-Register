§2200.4.  Approved dementia training
Any dementia specific training received in a nursing or nursing assistant program
approved by the Louisiana Department of Health or the Department of Children and Family
Services may be used to fulfill the training hours required pursuant to this Part.
Acts 2008, No. 571, §1, eff. June 30, 2008.
