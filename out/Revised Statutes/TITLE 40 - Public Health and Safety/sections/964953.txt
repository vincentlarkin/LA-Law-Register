§1289.2.  Definition
For purposes of this Subpart, the term "mold" means any form of multicellular fungi
that live on plant or animal matter and in indoor environments.  Types of mold include but
are not limited to cladosporium, alternaria, aspergillus, trichoderma, memnoniella, mucor,
and stachybotrys chartarum.
