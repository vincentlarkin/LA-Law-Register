§790.  Inspection and analyses

The secretary of the department may collect samples for analysis and conduct examinations and investigations for the purposes of this Subpart through any officers or employees under his supervision.  All such officers and employees may enter any factory, mill, warehouse, shop, or establishment where flour or bread is manufactured, processed, packed, sold, or held, or any vehicle and inspect any flour, bread, and pertinent equipment, materials, containers, and labeling therein.  

Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
