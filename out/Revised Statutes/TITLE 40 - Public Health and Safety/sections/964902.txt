§1251.5.  Buy-In Program Appropriations
The provisions of this Part shall not be implemented by the department unless
funding and performance standards, including a specified target for enrollment, are included
in the General Appropriation Act
;1
enacted during the 2003 Regular Session of the
Legislature. If included in that Act, the Medicaid Buy-In Program for Persons with
Disabilities shall be implemented by the department no later than January, 2004.
