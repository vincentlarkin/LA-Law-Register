§2116.38.  Records; reports

Every home health agency and licensee shall keep such records and make such reports as the department shall prescribe, and all such records shall be open to inspection by the department.

Acts 1966, No. 25, §1, eff. July 11, 1966 at 3:15 P.M.; Acts 1985, No. 912, §1; Acts 1995, No. 1252, §1.
