§1159.3. Subpart not applicable to care and treatment of persons with mental illness; exception

            Except as provided in R.S. 40:1159.9, the provisions of this Subpart shall not apply to the care and treatment of persons with mental illness, which subject shall continue to be governed by existing law independently of the terms and provisions of this Subpart.

            Added by Acts 1975, No. 798, §1. Amended by Acts 1978, No. 607, §1; Acts 2014, No. 811, §22, eff. June 23, 2014; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.52 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
