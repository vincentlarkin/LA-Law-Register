§753.  Conformity to rulings of federal security agency

The department shall change or add to the specifications for ingredients and the amounts thereof so as to conform to any changes in the ruling of the Federal Security Agency concerning the addition of vitamins to oleomargarine.  

Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
