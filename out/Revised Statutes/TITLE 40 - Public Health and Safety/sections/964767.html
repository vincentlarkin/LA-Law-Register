§1203.6. Ineligible for unemployment compensation

            A nonlicensed person or any licensed ambulance personnel hired on a temporary basis and terminated pursuant to the provisions of this Part shall not be eligible for unemployment compensation.

            Acts 1993, No. 594, §1, eff. August 15, 1994; Acts 1995, No. 917, §1, eff. June 28, 1995; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.56 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
