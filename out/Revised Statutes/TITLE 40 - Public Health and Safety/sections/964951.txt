§1285.10.  Liability of owners of residential property; damages
The owner of any residential property shall be liable for all damages caused by his
failure to perform the duties required of him pursuant to R.S. 40:1285.6 or R.S. 40:1285.7.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 371, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.29 by HCR 84
of 2015 R.S.
