§1485.9.  Applicability
The provisions of Subpart A of Part VII of Title 40 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes
of 1950, comprised of R.S. 1484.1 through 1484.28, are applicable in their entirety to the
provisions of this Subpart.
Acts 2016, No. 462, §2.
