§1715.  Penalty

Whoever violates the provisions of this Part shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction thereof, shall be sentenced to pay a fine of not less than one hundred dollars or more than one thousand dollars, or to imprisonment of not more than six months, or both.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 719, §1.
