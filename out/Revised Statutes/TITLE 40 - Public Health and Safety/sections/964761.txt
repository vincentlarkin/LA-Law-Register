§1201.2.  Clinical preceptor training program
A.  The Louisiana Community and Technical College System is hereby authorized
to develop and maintain a clinical preceptor nurse aide training program.
B.  Any program developed under the provisions of this Part shall be in compliance
with all federal laws and regulations.
