§34.7.  Original birth certificate; required contents; other information regarding the parents
The following information regarding the parents shall also be entered on the original
birth certificate:
(1)  Respective ages of parents on their last birthday, in years.
(2)  Races of parents as reported by the parents.
(3)  Ethnicities of parents as reported by the parents.
(4)  Residences of parents.
(5)  Birthplaces of parents, at least the state or foreign country, if known.
(6)  Number of children born to this mother.
(7)  Number of children born to this mother living.
(8)  Social security account numbers issued to the parents, if obtainable; however,
these numbers shall not be printed on the birth certificate of the child, but shall be entered
only as a part of vital records.
Acts 2016, No. 434, §3.
