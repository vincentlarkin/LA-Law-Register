§1644.  Cost

A.  No work shall begin until the plans and specifications have been submitted and reviewed by the state fire marshal's office as required by R.S. 40:1574.

B.  There shall be a review charge assessed by the state fire marshal as detailed in R.S. 40:1574.1.

Acts 1988, No. 422, §2.
