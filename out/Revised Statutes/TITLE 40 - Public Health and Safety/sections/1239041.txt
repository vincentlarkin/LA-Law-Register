§2552. Use of motor vehicle dash cameras
No later than January 1, 2022, any law enforcement motor vehicle that is equipped
with a dash camera that has the technology to automatically record upon the activation of the
motor vehicle's police emergency lights shall utilize that technology.
Acts 2021, No. 430, §1.
