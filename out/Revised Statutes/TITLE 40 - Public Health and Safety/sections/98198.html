§2262.  Domicile of the North Louisiana Criminalistics Laboratory Commission

The domicile of the North Louisiana Criminalistics Laboratory Commission shall be the parish seat of Caddo Parish, unless changed by a vote of two-thirds of the membership of the commission.

Acts 1981, No. 168, §1.
