§1217.13.  Scientific investigations
A.  The medical center may develop and conduct scientific investigations into the
cause, prevention, detection, methods of treatment, and cure of kidney disease, including
research in kidney transplantation.
B.  The medical center may develop techniques for an effective method of mass
testing to detect kidney disease and urinary tract infections.
