§1472.15.  Exceptions

Nothing contained in this Part shall apply to the regular military forces of the United States, or to the duly organized military force of any state or territory thereof, or to police or fire departments in this state, provided they are acting within their respective official capacities and in the performance of their duties.

Acts 1995, No. 1139, §1.
