PART II.  BREAST CANCER:  ORAL AND WRITTEN SUMMARY OF
TREATMENT ALTERNATIVES
§1103.1.  Short title
This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Carter Stokes Oral and Written
Summary of Breast Cancer Treatment Alternatives and Access to Breast Reconstruction
Surgery Information Law".
