§2175.4.  License required
A.  An outpatient abortion facility may not be established or operated in this state
without  an appropriate license issued under this Part.
NOTE:  Subsection B eff. until conditions per Acts 2018, No. 498.
B. A license issued to an outpatient abortion facility is valid for only one location.
NOTE:  Subsection B eff. upon conditions per Acts 2018, No. 498.
B.  A license issued to an outpatient abortion facility is valid for only one location
that shall be physically and financially separate from any facility where publicly funded
medical assistance program services are provided, in accordance with R.S. 49:200.51.
C.  A license issued to an outpatient abortion facility shall be valid for one year from
the date of issuance, unless revoked prior to that date.
D.  A license issued to an outpatient abortion facility is not transferable or assignable.
E.  A license issued to an outpatient abortion facility shall be posted in a conspicuous
place on the licensed premises.
F.  The provisions of R.S. 40:1061.1 shall apply to this Part.
