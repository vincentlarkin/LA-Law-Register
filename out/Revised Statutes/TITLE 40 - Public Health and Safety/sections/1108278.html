§1193.7. Immunity; unauthorized use

            A. In any civil action against a nursing home, material obtained through the use of a monitoring device shall not be used if the device was installed or used without the knowledge of the nursing home, or installed or used without the prescribed form.

            B. Compliance with the provisions of this Part shall be a complete defense to any civil or criminal action brought against the resident, legal representative, or nursing home for the use or presence of a monitoring device.

            Acts 2018, No. 596, §1.
