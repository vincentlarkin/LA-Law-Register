§1667.2.  Special fund
A.  For the payment of the supplemental salaries provided by R.S. 40:1667.1, there is hereby created a special fund in the treasury of the state of Louisiana, to which the appropriations hereinafter made shall be credited, and the funds so credited shall be and remain dedicated to the payment of such supplemental salaries, as provided in R.S. 40:1667.1.
B.  For the payment of the supplemental salaries provided by R.S. 40:1667.7, there is hereby created an additional special fund in the treasury of the state of Louisiana, to which the annual appropriations hereafter made shall be credited, and the funds so credited shall be and remain dedicated to the payment of such supplemental salaries as provided in R.S. 40:1667.7.
Acts 1956, No. 323, §3.  Amended by Acts 1971, No. 120, §2.  Redesignated from R.S. 33:2218.3.  See Acts 2014, No. 158, §§3 and 7.
