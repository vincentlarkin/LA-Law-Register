§1555.  Federal, state, and private aid and grants
A district created pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter shall have full power to do such things and enter into such contracts and agreements with the federal government, the state, or other public or private sources as may be necessary to procure aid and grants to assist the district in carrying out the purpose for which it was created.
Acts 2001, No. 188, §1, eff. May 31, 2001.
