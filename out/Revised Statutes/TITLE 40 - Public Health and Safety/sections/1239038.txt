CHAPTER 25-A. RESPONSIBILITIES OF LAW ENFORCEMENT
OFFICERS WHILE INTERACTING WITH THE PUBLIC
§2551. Use of body-worn cameras
No later than January 1, 2022, any law enforcement agency that utilizes body-worn
cameras shall adopt a policy regarding the activation and deactivation of such cameras by the
officer.
Acts 2021, No. 430, §1.
