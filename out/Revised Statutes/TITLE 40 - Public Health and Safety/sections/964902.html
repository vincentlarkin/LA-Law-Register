§1251.5. Buy-In Program Appropriations

            The provisions of this Part shall not be implemented by the department unless funding and performance standards, including a specified target for enrollment, are included in the General Appropriation Act;1 enacted during the 2003 Regular Session of the Legislature. If included in that Act, the Medicaid Buy-In Program for Persons with Disabilities shall be implemented by the department no later than January, 2004.

            Acts 2003, No. 207, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.78.4 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
