SUBPART D-3.  LIFE SAFETY AND PROPERTY PROTECTION LICENSING
§1664.1.  Short title
This Subpart shall be known as and may be cited as the "Life Safety and Property Protection Licensing Act".
Acts 2006, No. 307, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
