§1730.42. Definitions

            (1) "Historic buildings" means those buildings specifically designated as historically significant by the state historic preservation officer or by official action of a local government.

            (2) "International Energy Conservation Code" means the document developed by the International Code Council, Inc., entitled "International Energy Conservation Code", also referred to as "IECC".

            Acts 2005, No. 91, §1, eff. June 21, 2005; Acts 2007, No. 110, §1, eff. June 22, 2007; Acts 2012, No. 562, §1; Acts 2022, No. 635, §1.
