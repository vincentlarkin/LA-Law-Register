§1281.4.  Responsibility for operator certification
The state health officer shall upon recommendations of the committee certify persons
as to their qualifications to supervise successfully the operation of such water supply and
sewerage systems.
Acts 1972, No. 538, §4; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1144 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
