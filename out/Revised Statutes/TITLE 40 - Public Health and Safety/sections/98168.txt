§2210.  Prohibited discrimination; prenatal test results; genetic information
Every preferred provider organization authorized under this Part shall comply with the provisions of R.S. 22:1022 and 1023, relative to the use of prenatal test results and genetic information.
Acts 1997, No. 1418, §2; Acts 2008, No. 415, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
