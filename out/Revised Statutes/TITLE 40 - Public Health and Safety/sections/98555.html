§5.4.  Testing of private water wells

Upon the request of the owner of any private water well, the state health officer or his representative is authorized to test the purity of the water produced by such well and shall report the results of such testing to the owner.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 698, §1; Acts 1999, No. 289, §5.
