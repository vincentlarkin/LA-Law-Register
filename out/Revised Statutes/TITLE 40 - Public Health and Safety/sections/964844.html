§1217.13. Scientific investigations

            A. The medical center may develop and conduct scientific investigations into the cause, prevention, detection, methods of treatment, and cure of kidney disease, including research in kidney transplantation.

            B. The medical center may develop techniques for an effective method of mass testing to detect kidney disease and urinary tract infections.

            Acts 1995, No. 951, §1; Acts 1997, No. 557, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.93 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
