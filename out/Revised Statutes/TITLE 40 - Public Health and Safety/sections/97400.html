§1310.  Reports of director and governor

The Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall not authorize reports and recommendations which include the issue, publication, or distribution of general information documents or pamphlets, which are published on a regular basis and are generally known as newsletters.  

Acts 1986, No. 817, §1.
