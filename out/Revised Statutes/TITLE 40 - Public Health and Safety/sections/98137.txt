§2195.3.  Rural referral centers
The Louisiana Department of Health shall, subject to federal approval,
provide for adjustments in the disproportionate share Medicaid reimbursement
formula to provide increased support for hospitals which are classified as rural
referral centers.
Acts 1991, No. 394, §1.
