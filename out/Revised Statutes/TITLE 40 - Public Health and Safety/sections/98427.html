§4.5.  Exemptions

Parishes shall be exempt from that portion of the special event food and beverage preparation regulations of the department that require any equipment, design, construction, utensils, supplies, preparation, or services of any organization directing or operating a celebration of any recognized Louisiana festival or fair or any participant who prepares, serves, or sells any food or beverage at the celebration.  

Acts 1984, No. 906, §1, eff. July 20, 1984.
