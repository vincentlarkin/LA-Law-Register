§2120.17.  Renewal of a license
At least thirty days prior to license expiration, the licensee shall submit an application
which meets the requirements of this Part for renewal of the license.  If the application is
approved, the license shall be renewed for an additional two-year period.
Acts 2022, No. 577, §1.
