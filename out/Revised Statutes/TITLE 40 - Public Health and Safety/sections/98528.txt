§480.  Essential governmental purpose
All bonds, notes, certificates, and other instruments evidencing indebtedness or conveying equity participations issued by a local housing authority or any subsidiary thereof are deemed to be issued for an essential governmental purpose.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
