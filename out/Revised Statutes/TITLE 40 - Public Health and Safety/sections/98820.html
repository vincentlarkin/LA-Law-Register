PART V. OLEOMARGARINE

§751. Enforcement of Part

           The Louisiana Department of Health, hereinafter referred to in this Part as the "department," shall enforce this Part through the secretary of the department or its officers or agents.

           Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
