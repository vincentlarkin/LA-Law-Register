§1532.  Transfer of administration
The administration of the State Fire College is hereby transferred from the Department of Public Safety to the Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College and the expenses of the same shall be included in the annual budget submitted by the Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College.
Acts 1963, No. 84, §3.
