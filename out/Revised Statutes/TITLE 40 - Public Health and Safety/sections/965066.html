PART II. BREAST CANCER: ORAL AND WRITTEN SUMMARY OF

TREATMENT ALTERNATIVES

§1103.1. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Carter Stokes Oral and Written Summary of Breast Cancer Treatment Alternatives and Access to Breast Reconstruction Surgery Information Law".

            Acts 1999, No. 199, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1300.151 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S; Acts 2018, No. 580, §1, eff. May 31, 2018.
