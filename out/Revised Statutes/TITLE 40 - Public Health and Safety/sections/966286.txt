§1300.63. Local government prohibition or regulation
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a local government may
enact an ordinance to prohibit the sale or distribution of mitragynine speciosa products or to
regulate the sale or distribution of mitragynine speciosa products in a manner that is more
restrictive than provided for in R.S. 14:91.10.
Acts 2023, No. 416, §2.
