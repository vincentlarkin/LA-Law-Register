§1743.  Penalties

Any person who violates, or fails to comply with, the provisions of this Part except those violations under R.S. 40:1742(B) shall be subject to penalties described in R.S. 40:1621.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 625, §1.  Acts 1995, No. 880, §1.
