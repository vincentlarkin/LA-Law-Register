PART II-J. LICENSING PROGRAM FOR ALL-INCLUSIVE CARE OF THE ELDERLY (PACE)

§2120.61. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Licensing Program for All-Inclusive Care of the Elderly (PACE)".

            Acts 2024, No. 444, §1, eff. June 3, 2024.
