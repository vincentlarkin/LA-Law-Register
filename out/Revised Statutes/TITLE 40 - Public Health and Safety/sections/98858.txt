§923.  Imported milk and milk products
All milk and milk products shipped into this state from another state shall meet the sanitary standards and requirements according to the grade required of the product in which it is used as provided in the rules and regulations promulgated by the department.
Acts 1954, No. 93, §1.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
