§1472.17.  Administration of Part; personnel

The deputy secretary is authorized to employ such persons as he may deem qualified, consistent with applicable civil service regulations, and incur such other expenses as may be required in connection with the administration of this Part.

Acts 1995, No. 1139, §1; Acts 1999, No. 1202, §1.
