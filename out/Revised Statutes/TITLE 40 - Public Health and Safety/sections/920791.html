§1730.40.2.  Plumbing provisions; manufactured housing

Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary, all manufactured homes that are built to federal construction standards shall only be subject to the plumbing provisions in federal law. Manufactured homes shall not be subject to state plumbing regulations, regardless if the manufactured home is connected to a public or private sewer system; however, such connection shall be completed and maintained by a Louisiana licensed plumber.

Acts 2014, No. 836, §1, eff. June 23, 2014.
