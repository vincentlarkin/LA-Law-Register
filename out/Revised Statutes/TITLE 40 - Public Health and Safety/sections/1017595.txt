§46.13.  Paternity establishment services
The state registrar shall provide voluntary paternity establishment services in
accordance with regulations prescribed by the secretary of the United States Department of
Health and Human Services.  The state registrar may designate specific employees in the
offices of the vital records registry who shall possess notarial powers to administer an oath
to any person in connection with any document required in the course of establishing
paternity.
Acts 2016, No. 434, §3.
