§1191.4.  Limitation of liability
The provisions of this Part shall not be construed to create a cause of action, nor shall
the health facility, or any person authorized to act on behalf of the health facility, be held
civilly liable for any damages when a health facility makes a final disposition of the fetal
remains in accordance with the provisions of this Part.
Acts 2016, No. 59, §1, eff. May 10, 2016.
