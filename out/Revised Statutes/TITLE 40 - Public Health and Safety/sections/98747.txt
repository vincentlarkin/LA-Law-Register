§639.  Penalties
Except as provided in R.S. 40:971.3, whoever violates any provision of this Part
shall be fined, for the first offense, not more than one thousand dollars or imprisoned for not
more than one year, or both. For the second or subsequent offense, he shall be fined not more
than three thousand dollars or imprisoned for not more than two years, or both. But any
person who violates the provisions of R.S. 40:625(C) shall only be fined not more than one
thousand dollars for each violation if the violation does not involve gross deception or
imminent danger to health, and is established by opinion evidence only.
Acts 2017, No. 108, §1.
