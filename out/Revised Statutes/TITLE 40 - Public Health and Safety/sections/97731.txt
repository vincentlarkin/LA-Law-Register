§1646.1.  Inspectors
The inspections and tests conducted pursuant to the provisions of this Subpart may be conducted by employees of the fire marshal, who may be reimbursed at reasonable rates determined by the fire marshal, or by qualified employees of certified fire protection sprinkler contractors, as such term is defined in R.S. 40:1625(2), or by licensed engineers or licensed architects as provided by law.
Acts 1991, No. 664, §1.
