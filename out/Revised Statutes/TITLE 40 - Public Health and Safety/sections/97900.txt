§2002.2.  Washington-St. Tammany Regional Medical Center
The state operated general hospital located in Bogalusa, Louisiana, heretofore known as Washington-St. Tammany Charity Hospital, is hereby designated and shall hereafter be known as Washington-St. Tammany Regional Medical Center.
Acts 1987, No. 43, §2.
