§1484.26.  Local regulation
Nothing contained in this Part shall prevent any local governmental subdivision of
this state from licensing or regulating any inflatable amusement device, amusement
attraction, or amusement ride, carnival, or circus as otherwise provided by law.
Acts 2016, No. 462, §2.
