§1281.24. Approval of individual sewage treatment and disposal systems

            No parish, municipality, or planning commission shall enact a sewerage permit ordinance or similar regulation authorizing the installation of individual sewage treatment and disposal systems without written approval by the office of public health of the Louisiana Department of Health.

            Acts 1985, No. 569, §2, eff. July 12, 1985; Acts 1999, No. 289, §4; Acts 2013, No. 220, §17, eff. June 11, 2013; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1155 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
