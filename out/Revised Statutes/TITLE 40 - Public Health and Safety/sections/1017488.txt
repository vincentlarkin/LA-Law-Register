§1484.17.  Notice; hearing; revocation of certificate or license
Licenses, as provided for in this Part, may be revoked or suspended after notice and
hearing in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act and upon a finding that a
person or firm acted in any of the following manners:
(1)  Willfully violated any provision of this Part or any rule, regulation, or adopted
order.
(2)  Used deceit or false or misleading information to obtain any certificate or license
pursuant to this Part.
(3)  Demonstrated professional incompetence or gross negligence.
(4)  Assisted any person attempting to evade the provisions of this Part or any related
adopted rules or regulations.
Acts 2016, No. 462, §2.
