SUBPART C.  PENALTIES
§1621.  Violation of fire marshal's orders; penalty
Whoever fails to comply with any order issued by the fire marshal or his authorized representative, under any provisions of Part III of Chapter 7 of Title 40, R.S. 40:1569 excepted, shall be fined not more than five hundred dollars or imprisoned for not more than six months, or both.  Each day's violation of an order constitutes a separate offense and may be punished as such at the discretion of the court.
Acts 1989, No. 181, §1; Acts 1990, No. 86, §1.
