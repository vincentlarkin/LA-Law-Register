§924.  Revocation or cancellation of permit or license
The department shall have the power and authority to revoke or cancel the permit, or license, of any person, firm, or corporation doing business in the state who violates any of the provisions of this Subpart.
Acts 1954, No. 93, §1.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
