§1131.3. Medical assistance program; ground ambulance transportation to alternative
destination; reimbursement
The department shall promulgate rules and regulations to provide for a
reimbursement methodology to cover alternative destination transport by a ground
ambulance service provider to ensure that reimbursement rates are reasonable and adequate.
Acts 2018, No. 565, §2.
