§1596.2.  Administration
The assistant secretary of the Department of Public Safety in charge of the office of state fire protection, commonly known as the State Fire Marshal, shall administer this Subpart and may issue rules and regulations which he considers necessary to its administration.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 495, §1, eff. July 13, 1979.
