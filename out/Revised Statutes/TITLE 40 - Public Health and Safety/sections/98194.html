§2254.7.  Appeal

A party to a cooperative agreement may appeal, in the manner provided in the Administrative Procedure Act, a final decision by the department to deny an application for a certificate or a decision by the department to revoke a certificate.  A revocation of a certificate pursuant to R.S. 40:2254.6 does not become final until the time for appeal has expired.  If a decision to revoke a certificate is appealed, the decision is stayed pending resolution of the appeal by the courts.

Acts 1997, No. 1331, §1.
