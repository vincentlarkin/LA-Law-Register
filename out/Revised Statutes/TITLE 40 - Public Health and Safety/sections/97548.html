PART VII. REGULATION OF AMUSEMENT ATTRACTIONS

AND RIDES

SUBPART A. REGULATION

§1484.1. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the Amusement Rides Safety Law.

            Added by Acts 1985, No. 733, §1; Acts 2003, No. 928, §2; Acts 2016, No. 462, §2.
