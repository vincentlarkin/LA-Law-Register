PART XII. PERINATAL MENTAL HEALTH POLICY

§1123.1. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as "The Perinatal Mood and Anxiety Disorders Act".

            Acts 2022, No. 188, §1.

            NOTE: Former R.S. 40:1123.1 redesignated as R.S. 40:1300.41 by Acts 2022, No. 271, §9A.
