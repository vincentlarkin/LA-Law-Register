§600.102.  Trust funds

All monies received by the corporation pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter shall be deemed to be trust funds, to be held and applied solely to or for the public functions and corporate purposes of the corporation.

Acts 2011, No. 408, §1, eff. July 5, 2011.
