§533.  Vacancies in local authorities
Any vacancy in an unexpired term shall be filled for the remainder of the term. Vacancies shall be filled by the same authority and in the same manner as the original appointment.
Amended by Acts 1950, No. 401, §28; Acts 1952, No. 127, §15; Acts 1954, No. 80, §1; Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
