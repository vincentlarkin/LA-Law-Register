§1124.2. Discharge information; hospital and birthing center requirements

            A. All hospitals and birthing centers that provide labor and delivery services shall, prior to discharge following birth, provide the mother and her family members information about post-birth warning signs, including symptoms and available resources.

            B. The Louisiana Department of Health shall make the information available to hospitals and birthing centers and shall include the information on the department's website.

            Acts 2024, No. 122, §1.
