§1079.17.  Authorized resources
A school or facility may solicit and use any available state, federal, or private
resources, which may include but shall not be limited to funds, in-kind resources, or
volunteer services, for the purpose of providing preventive counseling services or treatment
for alcohol or drug abuse.
