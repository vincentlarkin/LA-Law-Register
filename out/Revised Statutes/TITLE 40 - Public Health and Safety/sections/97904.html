§2002.6.  Earl K. Long Medical Center

The state operated general hospital located in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, heretofore known as Earl K. Long Memorial Hospital, is hereby designated and shall be hereafter known as Earl K. Long Medical Center.

Acts 1991, No. 390, §3, eff. July 8, 1991.
