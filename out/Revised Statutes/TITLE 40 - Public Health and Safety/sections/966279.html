§1300.53. Applicability

            The provisions of this Part shall apply to all of the following:

            (1) Licensed centers that provide care for persons with developmental disabilities as defined in R.S. 28:751.

            (2) Hospitals as defined in R.S. 40:2102.

            (3) Licensed facilities that provide inpatient hospice care as defined in R.S. 40:2182.

            (4) Nursing homes as defined in R.S. 40:2009.2.

            Acts 2023, No. 367, §1.
