§1749.22.  Preemption
Except as provided in this Part, no parish, municipal, local, or state governing
authority may enact any ordinance or promulgate any rules or regulations which are in
conflict with the provisions of this Part.
Acts 1988, No. 923, §1; Acts 2019, No. 344, §1.
