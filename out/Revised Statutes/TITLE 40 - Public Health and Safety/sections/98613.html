§546.  Meetings of commissioners

Nothing contained in this Part prevents meetings of the commissioners of a housing authority anywhere within the perimeter boundaries of the area of operation of the authority or within any additional area where the housing authority is authorized to undertake a housing project.

Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
