§419.  Transfer of property to regional or consolidated authority; registration
When any immovable property of a housing authority vests in a regional or consolidated housing authority under the provisions of R.S. 40:417, it shall execute an act transferring the property to the regional or consolidated authority.  The regional or consolidated authority shall thereupon register the act as provided by law in the case of sales.  However, nothing contained in this Section affects the vesting of property in the regional or consolidated housing authority as provided in R.S. 40:417.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
