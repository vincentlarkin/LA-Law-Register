§543.  Area of operation after dissolution
After any dissolution and transfer, notwithstanding anything contained in R.S. 40:396, the area of operation of the receiving local housing authority shall include, nonexclusively, the area of operation of the transferring local housing authority.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
