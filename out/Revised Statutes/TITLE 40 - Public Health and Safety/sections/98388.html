PART VII. LOUISIANA BIRTH DEFECTS SURVEILLANCE SYSTEM

§31.41.  Legislative intent

It is the intent of the legislature to establish a system to collect, analyze, and disseminate data regarding birth defects in the state and to provide information to families of children born with birth defects regarding services available in their community and the development of appropriate prevention programs.

Acts 2001, No. 194, §1.
