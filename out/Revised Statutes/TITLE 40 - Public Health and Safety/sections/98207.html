§2267.2.  Domicile of the Acadiana Criminalistics Laboratory Commission

The domicile of the Acadiana Criminalistics Laboratory Commission shall be the parish seat of the parish where the laboratory is located unless later changed by a two-thirds vote of the membership of the commission.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 554, §1.
