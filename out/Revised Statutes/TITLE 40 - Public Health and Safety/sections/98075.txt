§2138.  Confidential nature of financial information and financial records
Notwithstanding the provisions of the Public Records Act, financial information and financial records received by the licensing agency under the provisions of this Part shall be held confidential and shall not be disclosed except when subpoenaed in a proceeding for the revocation, suspension, or denial of a license.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 872, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1984; Acts 1999, No. 1289, §1.
