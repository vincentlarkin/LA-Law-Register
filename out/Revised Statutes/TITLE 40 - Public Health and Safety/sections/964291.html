§1121.5. Isolation, quarantine, or internment of persons affected

            Any person affected with a venereal disease is subject to isolation, quarantine, or internment, on the order of the department, and shall submit to any treatment for such a time and under such restrictions as seems reasonable and proper to the department.

            Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1064 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
