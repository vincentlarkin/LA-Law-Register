§2135.  Provisional licenses
A provisional license may be issued to an ambulatory surgical center for a period of six months in cases where sufficient compliance with regulations, rules, and minimum standards require an extension of time.  The failure to comply must not be detrimental to the health and safety of the public.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 596, §1.
