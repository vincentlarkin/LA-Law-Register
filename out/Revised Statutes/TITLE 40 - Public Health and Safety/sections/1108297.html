§1664.17. Local governing authorities; exemption

            A municipality or parish that has adopted and is enforcing a nationally recognized standard or code for conveyance devices may continue to enforce such standard or code, and in such instances, no additional inspections shall be required under the provisions of this Subpart; however, such standard or code shall contain requirements that are substantially equal to the fire marshal's code with respect to conveyance devices.

            Acts 2018, No. 598, §1, eff. January 1, 2019.
