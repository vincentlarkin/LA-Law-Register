§2012.1. Admission of indigent patients

           Any indigent person suffering with tuberculosis who has resided in Louisiana for a period of one year may be admitted to any tuberculosis hospital operated by the Louisiana Department of Health upon the recommendation of any registered physician and upon being found by the superintendent of the hospital to be a fit subject entitled to such admission.

           Added by Acts 1958, No. 34, §1. Amended by Acts 1977, No. 518, §1; 1977, No. 680, §20.
