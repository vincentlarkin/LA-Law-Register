§1217.16.  Report
The medical center shall prepare and submit an annual report concerning its findings,
progress, and activities to the governor, the president of the Senate, the speaker of the House
of Representatives, and the chairmen of the House and Senate committees on health and
welfare at least sixty days prior to the legislative session and shall report on the state's total
need in the area of kidney health care.
Acts 1995, No. 951, §1; Acts 1997, No. 557, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Redesignated from
R.S. 40:1300.96 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S; Acts 2018, No. 206, §4.
