SUBPART B. LOUISIANA MEDICAL CONSENT LAW

§1159.1. Short title

            This Subpart shall be known as and may be cited as the "Louisiana Medical Consent Law."

            Added by Acts 1975, No. 798, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.50 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
