CHAPTER 3-F.  LOUISIANA HOUSING COUNSELING ASSISTANCE ACT
§600.71.  Title
This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Housing Counseling
Assistance Act."
Acts 2007, No. 383, §1, eff. July 10, 2007.
NOTE: Pursuant to Acts 2007, No. 383, §3, the provisions of this Section ceased to be
effective on July 1, 2010.
