§2707.  Prohibited acts; advertisement; minors

A.  A tanning facility shall not claim or distribute promotional materials that claim use of a tanning device is safe or free from risk.  No person shall state or imply that any activity under such registration has been approved by the department.

B.  A tanning facility shall not allow any person under eighteen years of age to use any tanning equipment.

Acts 1990, No. 587, §1, eff. July 19, 1990; Acts 2014, No. 193, §1.
