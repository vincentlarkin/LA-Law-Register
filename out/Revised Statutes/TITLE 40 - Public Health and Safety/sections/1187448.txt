§1255.2.  Telehealth services; alignment of reimbursement with Medicare policy
A.  The department shall periodically review policies regarding Medicaid
reimbursement for telehealth services to identify variations between permissible
reimbursement under that program and reimbursement available to healthcare providers
under the Medicare program.
B.  To the extent practicable, notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, after
conducting a review provided for in Subsection A of this Section, the department may
modify its administrative rules, policies, and procedures applicable to Medicaid
reimbursement for telehealth services as necessary to provide for a reimbursement system
that is comparable to that of the Medicare program for those services.
Acts 2020, No. 88, §1.
