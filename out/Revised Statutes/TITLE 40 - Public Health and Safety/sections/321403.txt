§2120.40.  Operating without a license or in violation of departmental regulations; injunctive relief
If any adult brain injury facility operates without a valid license issued by the department, or in violation of departmental regulations, the department may cause a civil suit to be instituted in a district court in the parish in which the facility is located for injunctive relief, including a temporary restraining order, to restrain the institution, society, agency, corporation, person or persons, or any group operating the facility from continuing the violation.
Acts 2005, No. 245, §1, eff. June 29, 2005.
