§1057.9.  Regulations

The authority to promulgate regulations for the efficient enforcement of this Chapter is hereby vested in the secretary.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 590, §1, eff. July 17, 1975.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978.
