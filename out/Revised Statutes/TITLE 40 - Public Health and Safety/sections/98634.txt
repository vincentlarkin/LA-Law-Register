§574.  Exception of contracts from requirements of appraisal; advertising and bidding
Any sale, lease, or other agreement provided for in R.S. 40:573 may be made by a state public body without appraisal, public notice, advertising, or public bidding.
Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
