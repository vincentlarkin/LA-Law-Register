§2186.  Complaints

A.  It shall be the duty of the department, through its duly authorized agents, to investigate all complaints against hospices as defined in this Part.  The department may take such action as is authorized by this Part.

B.  The department shall receive, record, and dispose of complaints in accordance with rules and regulations promulgated in accordance with the provisions of this Part.

Acts 1988, No. 941, §2.
