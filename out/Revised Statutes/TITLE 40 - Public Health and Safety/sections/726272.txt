§1917.  Penalty
Whoever violates a provision of this Part shall be fined not less than one hundred nor more than one thousand dollars.
Acts 1993, No. 586, §1; Acts 1999, No. 585, §1; Redesignated by Acts 2010, No. 579, §4 from R.S. 3:1357.
