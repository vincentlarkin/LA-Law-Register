§16.  Parish health units to act in harmony with state health officer
All parish health units act under the supervision and advice of the state
health officer and the office of health services and environmental quality of the
Louisiana Department of Health.
Acts 1976, No. 346, §1.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July
17, 1978.
