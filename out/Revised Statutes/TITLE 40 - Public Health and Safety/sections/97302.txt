PART II.  MASS MEETINGS
§1300.11.  Definitions
For the purposes of this Part "mass meeting" shall mean any gathering or the
prospective gathering of over five thousand people; provided that the term "mass meeting"
shall not include meetings and assemblies of members of bona fide labor organizations when
said meetings are held in furtherance of any legitimate collective bargaining activity,
business activity, or political activity of such labor organizations.
