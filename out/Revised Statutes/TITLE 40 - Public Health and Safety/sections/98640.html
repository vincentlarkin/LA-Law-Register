§58.  Fee of clerk of court for divorce and annulment report

Every clerk of court acting under the previous Section shall receive a fee for each certificate prepared and forwarded by him to the state registrar.  This fee shall be included and collected as court costs for the divorce or annulment.  

Acts 1979, No. 776, §1.
