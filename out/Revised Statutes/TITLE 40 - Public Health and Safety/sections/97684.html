§1578.5.  Appeals from decision of board of review

Any interested person may appeal a decision of the board of review as provided in R.S. 40:1578.1 to a court of competent jurisdiction within five days of the rendering of the decision of the board of review.

Added by Acts 1974, No. 236, §1.
