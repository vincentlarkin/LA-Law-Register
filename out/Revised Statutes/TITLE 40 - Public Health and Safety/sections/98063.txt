§2123.  Eligibility for assistance
Any child whose physical, emotional, mental, or neurological condition
so deviates from the normal that he cannot, without special therapy, protective
care, or training satisfactorily participate in community living is eligible for
assistance under this Part, provided that said child shall be a resident of the
state at the time of the application for assistance and a diagnostic workup has
been performed pursuant to Louisiana Department of Health rules and
regulations.
Acts 1981, No. 673, §1.
