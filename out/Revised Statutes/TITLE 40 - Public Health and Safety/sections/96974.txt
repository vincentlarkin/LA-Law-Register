PART X-C.  ANIMAL EUTHANASIA WITH
SODIUM PENTOBARBITAL
§1031.  Purpose
It is the purpose of this Part to establish a permit system to allow animal control facilities to acquire and administer sodium pentobarbital for the humane euthanasia of sick, homeless, and abandoned animals.
Acts 1987, No. 225, §2; Acts 2006, No. 676, §3, eff. July 1, 2006.
