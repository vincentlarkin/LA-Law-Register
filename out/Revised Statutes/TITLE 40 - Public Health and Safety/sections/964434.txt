§1281.24.  Approval of individual sewage treatment and disposal systems
No parish, municipality, or planning commission shall enact a sewerage permit
ordinance or similar regulation authorizing the installation of individual sewage treatment
and disposal systems without written approval by the office of public health of the Louisiana
Department of Health.
