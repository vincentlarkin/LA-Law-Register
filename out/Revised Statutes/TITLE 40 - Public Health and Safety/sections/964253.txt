PART X.  VENEREAL DISEASES
SUBPART A.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§1121.1.  Definition
In this Subpart, "venereal disease" means syphilis, gonorrhea, chancroid, or any other
infectious disease primarily transmitted from one person to another by means of a sexual act.
