§1071.5.  Funds
The department may receive monies from any source, including state and federal
funds, grants, and gifts which shall be expended for the purposes designated in this Part.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 690, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.116 by HCR 84
of 2015 R.S.
