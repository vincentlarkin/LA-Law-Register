§1893. Method of use and containers and equipment regulated

            The method of using the malodorant and the containers and equipment used in connection therewith are under the direction and subject to the approval of the office of conservation of the Department of Conservation and Energy.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 535, §1. Amended by Acts 1981, No. 754, §2; Acts 2023, No. 150, §14, eff. Jan. 10, 2024.
