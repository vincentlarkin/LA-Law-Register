§1542.  Standards
The National Firefighting Standards shall be used as the basis for classroom instruction and testing for certification of firemen.
Acts 1970, No. 481, §2.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 19, §1; Acts 1975, No. 576, §1; Acts 1979, No. 528, §1, eff. July 18, 1979.
