§1500.1.  Emergency medical services and emergency medical transportation in the parishes of Caddo, Cameron, and Morehouse
Any fire protection district in the parishes of Caddo, Cameron, or Morehouse may enter into public service contracts with other public service entities for emergency medical services or emergency medical transportation; or in the absence of public service entities, the fire protection district may provide such services.
Acts 1991, No. 908, §1; Acts 1992, No. 20, eff. May 18, 1992; Acts 1995, No. 15, §1; Acts 1995, No. 22, §1.
