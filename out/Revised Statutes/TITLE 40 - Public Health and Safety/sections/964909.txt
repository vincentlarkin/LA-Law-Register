PART II.  SMOKING REGULATION
§1293.1.  Louisiana Superdome; smoking regulation
A.  It is the public policy of this state to protect the health, comfort, and environment
of the people of this state.
B.  Smoking shall be prohibited in all areas of the Louisiana Superdome.
C.  "Smoking" means the carrying of a lighted cigar, cigarette, pipe, or any other
lighted smoking equipment, in addition to the inhalation and exhalation of smoke by a person
from any form of lighted tobacco.
D.  Any person who violates the provisions of this Section shall be advised of the
prohibition by Superdome personnel.  In addition, the board of commissioners of the
Louisiana Stadium and Exposition District may adopt rules to provide for ejection from the
Superdome of any person who continues such violation after being so advised of the
prohibition, such rules to be enforced by security personnel in accordance with board rules.
