§953.  Seizure
Any article or product found in violation of the labeling requirements in R.S. 40:952
shall be subject to seizure and condemnation by the state health officer or by any duly
authorized representative he designates for that purpose.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 140, §1; Acts 2018, No. 206, §4.
