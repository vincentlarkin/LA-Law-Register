§34.6.  Original birth certificate; required contents; name of mother
The full name of the mother of the child, including her maiden name and current
surname, shall be entered on the original birth certificate; however, if the child was born of
a surrogate birth parent as a result of an enforceable gestational carrier contract, as provided
in R.S. 9:2720, the maiden name of the biological mother shall be listed as the mother.
Acts 2016, No. 434, §3; Acts 2016, No. 494, §2.
