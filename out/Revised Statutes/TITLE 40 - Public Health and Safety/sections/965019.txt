§1061.26.  Instructions to be provided subsequent to abortion
A.  Any physician who shall perform or induce an abortion shall, subsequent to the
abortion being performed or induced, provide his patient with specific oral and written
medical instructions to be followed by that patient in order to ensure her safe recovery from
the abortion.
B.  The provisions of R.S. 40:1061.1 shall apply to this Section.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 435, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.35.15 by HCR
84 of 2015 R.S.; Acts 2022, No. 545, §3.
