§448.  Mixed-income housing
A local housing authority may develop, acquire, own, hold, lease, rent, and operate mixed-income housing developments, subject to the limitations contained in R.S. 40:466.
Amended by Acts 1960, No. 278, §3; Acts 1978, No. 786, §5, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1997, No. 1188, §1.
