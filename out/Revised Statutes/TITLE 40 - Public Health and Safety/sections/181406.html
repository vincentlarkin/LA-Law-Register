§2118.  Fluoroscopy procedures in a hospital setting

With regard to the protection of the public health, in a hospital setting, fluoroscopy procedures may be conducted by a  radiologist licensed in the state or by a radiology technician licensed in the state and who is under the direct supervision of a radiologist or other physician.

Acts 2003, No. 1226, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 2003.
