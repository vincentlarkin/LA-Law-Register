§1544. Administration of federal funds

            The office of state fire marshal is designated as administrator for all federal fire training and education funds; however, this Section shall not prohibit local governmental subdivisions or Delgado Community College from applying for, receiving, and administering federal funds for fire training and education.

            Acts 1970, No. 481, §4. Amended by Acts 1975, No. 576, §1; Acts 1979, No. 528, §1, eff. July 18, 1979; Acts 2001, No. 212, §1; Acts 2022, No. 231, §3.
