§1049.7.  Board of pharmacy access to information
The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of state police, shall provide
access to the information regarding the sale of products containing ephedrine,
pseudoephedrine, or phenylpropanolamine as authorized by the provisions of this Part to the
Louisiana Board of Pharmacy.
Acts 2009, No. 314, §1; Acts 2018, No. 206, §4.
