PART II. MASS MEETINGS

§1300.11. Definitions

            For the purposes of this Part "mass meeting" shall mean any gathering or the prospective gathering of over five thousand people; provided that the term "mass meeting" shall not include meetings and assemblies of members of bona fide labor organizations when said meetings are held in furtherance of any legitimate collective bargaining activity, business activity, or political activity of such labor organizations.

            Added by Acts 1970, No. 3, §1, eff. May 28, 1970; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.11 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
