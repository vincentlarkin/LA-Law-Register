SUBPART D.  CLINICS AND LOCAL PROGRAMS
§1125.31.  Sickle cell anemia; clinic established
The Louisiana Department of Health is hereby authorized and directed to establish
and operate clinics in cooperation with the medical schools of Louisiana State University and
Tulane University for the treatment of sickle cell anemia, in Charity Hospital of Louisiana
in New Orleans and in Louisiana State Medical Center in Shreveport.  The clinics shall be
maintained for the treatment of victims of sickle cell anemia regardless of geographic and
economic family situation without regard to the standard eligibility requirements of charity
hospitals and for the training of physicians and medical students in methods of treatment and
for research into the causes of the disease and its cure.
