PART IV-C.  STATE BUILDING ENERGY CONSERVATION CODE
§1730.41.  Short title
This Part shall be known, and may be cited and referred to, as the "State Building
Energy Conservation Code".
Acts 2005, No. 91, §1, eff. June 21, 2005; Acts 2022, No. 635, §1.
