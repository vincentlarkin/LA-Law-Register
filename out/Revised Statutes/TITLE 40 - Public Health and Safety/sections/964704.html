§1159.8. Consent to medical arbitration agreements

            The persons authorized and empowered in R.S. 40:1159.4 (A) and (B) to consent to surgical or medical treatment or procedures for others as provided therein are also authorized and empowered, for and on behalf of such others, and without court approval, to enter into binding medical arbitration agreements.

            Added by Acts 1976, No. 269, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.57 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
