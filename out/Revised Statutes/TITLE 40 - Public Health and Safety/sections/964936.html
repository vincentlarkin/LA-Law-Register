§1275.4. Rules and regulations

            The department shall promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to implement the requirements of this Part.

            Acts 1995, No. 350, §1, eff. June 16, 1995; Redesignated from R.S. 40:1299.174 by HCR 84 of 2015 R.S.
