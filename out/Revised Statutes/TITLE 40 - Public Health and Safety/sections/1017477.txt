§1226.7.  Compensation of caregiver
A caregiver designated under the provisions of this Part shall not accept
compensation in exchange for aftercare provided to the patient.  A licensed medical
professional may be designated as a caregiver as long as the individual does not accept
compensation for aftercare provided to the patient.
Acts 2016, No. 351, §1.
