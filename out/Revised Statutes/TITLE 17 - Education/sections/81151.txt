§544.  Rules and regulations impairing provisions prohibited
Nothing contained in the Louisiana Revised Statutes shall be construed as conferring upon the Board of Elementary and Secondary Education the authority to make rules and regulations which may impair or nullify the provisions of this Subpart.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 661, §1.
