§417.  Courses of study and school regulations; enforcement by teachers
Teachers shall faithfully enforce the school courses of study and the regulations prescribed in pursuance of law; and if any teacher wilfully refuses or neglects to comply with such requirements, the parish superintendent shall withhold the salary of such teacher until the teacher properly performs his duties in such respect.
