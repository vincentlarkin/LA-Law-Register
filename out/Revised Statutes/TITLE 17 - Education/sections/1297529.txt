§419.4.  Required teacher training and student instruction; effectiveness
A.  No requirement that increases the uncompensated training burden on professional
teachers shall become effective.  A requirement for additional teacher training shall become
effective only if provision is made for teachers to receive the training at a time when they are
being compensated and not participating in local professional development activities or the
burden of the requirement is offset by the elimination of another training requirement,
completion of which requires at least the same amount of time as the additional requirement.
B.  A requirement for additional student instruction shall become effective only if the
requirement is offset by the elimination of another instructional requirement, completion of
which requires at least the same amount of time as the additional requirement.
Acts 2022, No. 569, §1; Acts 2024, No. 716, §1.
