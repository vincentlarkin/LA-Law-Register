§1994.2.  Margaret Surles Branch of Louisiana Technical College - Tallulah Campus
The Lake Providence branch of Louisiana Technical College - Tallulah Campus shall be renamed and shall be hereafter known as the "Margaret Surles Branch of Louisiana Technical College - Tallulah Campus".
Acts 1992, No. 132, §1; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 103, §1.
