§1601.8.  Cador Baseball Operational Center
The baseball operational center located on the Baton Rouge campus of Southern
University, operated by and under the authority of the Board of Supervisors of Southern
University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, is designated and shall be known as the
"Cador Baseball Operational Center".
Acts 2018, No. 46, §1, eff. May 10, 2018.
