PART V.  FUNDING OF PROGRAM
§3661.  Funding of program
The Louisiana Educational Employees Professional Improvement Program established herein shall be funded with monies specifically appropriated therefore by the Louisiana Legislature, except as otherwise provided in this Chapter; however, no funds shall be appropriated for this purpose until after funds have been appropriated to fully fund the teachers' minimum salary schedule provided for in R.S. 17:421.3 and any cost-of-living increases granted by the legislature.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 207, §1.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 337, §1, eff. July 15, 1981.
