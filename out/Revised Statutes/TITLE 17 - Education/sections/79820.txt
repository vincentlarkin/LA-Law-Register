§127.  Revenues of parish school board; duty to secure
The Orleans Parish School Board or its successor in law, shall secure its revenues as provided in the Constitution of the state.
Acts 1960, 3rd Ex.Sess., No. 4, §1, emerg.  eff. Jan. 12, 1961.
