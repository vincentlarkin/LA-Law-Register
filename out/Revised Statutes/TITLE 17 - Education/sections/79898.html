§1553.  Multipurpose assembly center; operational authority; advisory committee

Any multipurpose assembly center constructed on the east campus of the University of New Orleans shall be owned by and under the operational authority of the Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, which body shall make the facility available to the community at large, and particularly to all educational institutions in the New Orleans area, for academic, cultural, and other appropriate uses.

Acts 1989, No. 662, §2, eff. July 7, 1989.
