§1673. Scholarship program to be administered by state Department of Education

            The scholarship program established and authorized by this Subpart shall be administered by the state Department of Education.

            Added by Acts 1974, No. 695, §1, eff. July 1, 1975; Acts 2022, No. 374, §1.
