§3086.  ABLE account funds; investment
The state treasurer shall invest monies deposited in the ABLE accounts in the same
manner as prescribed by R.S. 17:3091 et seq. for the investment of monies deposited in
START Program accounts.  Funds in ABLE accounts shall be invested in such investment
options and subject to the same requirements and limitations for funds deposited in START
Program accounts provided in R.S. 17:3091 et seq., except as otherwise provided in this
Chapter.
Acts 2016, No. 604, §1.
