§2826.  Fees

Each cooperative shall pay annually, on or before the first day of October, to the department of revenue, a fee of ten dollars for each one hundred persons or fraction thereof to whom educational services are supplied within the state by it, but shall be exempt from all other excise and income taxes whatsoever.

Added by Acts 1958, No. 257, §26.
