§535.  Rules and regulations impairing provisions prohibited
Nothing contained in the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, as amended, shall be construed as conferring upon the Iberville Parish School Board the authority to adopt rules and regulations which impair or nullify the provisions of this subpart.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 194, §1.
