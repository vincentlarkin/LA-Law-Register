§2121. Voter registration; high schools

            A. Each public school governing authority shall provide an opportunity for each high school senior who is at least seventeen years old to register to vote by using a school computer to fill out an electronic voter registration application or by using the state mail voter registration application form.

            B. No public school governing authority shall allow the involvement of any political or partisan group or organization in the registration process.

            C. Each public school governing authority shall adopt policies for the implementation of this Section and shall review the policies, at minimum, every five years for the purpose of considering any needed revisions or updates.

            D. Each public school governing authority may follow guidance provided by the secretary of state in implementing the provisions of this Section.

            Acts 2022, No. 624, §1.
