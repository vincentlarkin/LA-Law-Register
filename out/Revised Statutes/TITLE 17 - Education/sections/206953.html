§85.4.  Naming of curriculum center; St. Tammany Parish School Board

 The parish school board in St. Tammany Parish may name a curriculum center in the parish in honor of a former educator and coach who meets all of the following criteria:

(1)  He served as an educator in Louisiana schools for more than forty years.

(2)  He was a teacher and school principal in the parish where the curriculum center is located.

(3)  He served as a football coach in the parish school system and the team he coached won its first state football championship.

(4)  He retired from the parish school system before 1980.

Acts 2003, No. 909, §1, eff. July 1, 2003; Acts 2011, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 41, §1, eff. June 12, 2011.
