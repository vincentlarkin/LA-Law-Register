§3363.  Amendment of rules and regulations
From time to time each board may amend its rules, regulations, and requirements adopted in accordance with this Subpart, but no such amendments shall affect leases previously granted.  However, each board shall have the authority to change its rules, regulations and requirements as to past or present leases to fraternities or sororities.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 313, §2, eff. July 17, 1975.
