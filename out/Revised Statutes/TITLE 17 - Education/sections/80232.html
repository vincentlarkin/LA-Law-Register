§2150.  Applicability

A.  Nothing in this Part shall be construed to affect academic teaching personnel.

B.  Nothing in this Part shall authorize or require the reduction of any sick leave benefits presently available to any unclassified, non-teaching academic employee under any contract, law, agreement, custom or policy in effect at twelve o'clock noon on July 31, 1974.

Added by Acts 1974, No. 339, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 723, §1.
