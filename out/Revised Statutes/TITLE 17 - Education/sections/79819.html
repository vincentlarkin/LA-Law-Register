§126.  Courses of study and text books; authority to adopt

The Orleans Parish School Board has authority to prepare courses of study for use in public schools of Orleans Parish, subject to the approval of the state board of education, and to adopt text books for use in the public schools of Orleans Parish, subject to the approval of the state board of education.  

Acts 1960, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 4, §1, emerg. eff. Jan. 12, 1961.
