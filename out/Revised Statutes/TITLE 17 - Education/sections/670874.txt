§2931.  Rules and regulations
The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education, in consultation with the
Board of Regents and Louisiana Works, shall adopt rules and regulations to implement the
provisions of this Chapter in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 2009, No. 257, §1, eff. July 1, 2009.
