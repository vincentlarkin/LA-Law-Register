§182.  Protective custody
A.  No school administrator shall release a minor into protective custody unless the
official to whom the minor is being released provides the following information about the
minor:
(1)  First and last name.
(2)  Address.
(3)  Date of birth.
B.  The provisions of this Section shall not apply to an arrest for which there is
probable cause.
