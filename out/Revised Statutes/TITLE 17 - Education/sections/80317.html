§2279.  Transportation of bodies

In the distribution and transportation of bodies as provided in R.S. 17:2274 and 17:2275, the board may employ any public carrier having a transmittal permit issued by the local registrar of vital statistics authorizing the removal thereof.  All bodies being transported shall be properly encased and delivered free from public observation.
