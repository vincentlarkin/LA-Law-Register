§2.  Reports of the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education and state
Department of Education
A.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, any provision of the
Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 requiring the State Board of Elementary and Secondary
Education or the state Department of Education to submit a report as defined in R.S. 24:771
shall cease to be effective on August first two years following the date of the first publication
of the report unless renewed by the legislature.
B.  If a bill providing for the renewal of a reporting requirement does not become
law, the statute creating and continuing such report shall be construed as repealed on the
applicable termination date.
Acts 2025, No. 210, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 2026.
