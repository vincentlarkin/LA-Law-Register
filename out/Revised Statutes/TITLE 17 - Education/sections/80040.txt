§1925.  Executive director; appointment; functions
The governance board may appoint a chief administrative officer who shall be the executive director of the center.  The executive director of the center shall serve at the pleasure of the governance board and shall, with the approval of the governance board, appoint and remove all professional, research, technical, clerical, and other employees of the center.  The compensation of the executive director shall be fixed by the governance board.
Acts 2002, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 41, §1.
