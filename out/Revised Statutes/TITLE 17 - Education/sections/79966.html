§1757.  Donations to loan fund from private sources

Each parish school loan fund may be augmented by donations by private individuals, companies, corporations, or from any other source, and, such donations, when made, shall be used and accounted for in the same manner as will be the moneys made available and appropriated by the governing authority of the parish.
