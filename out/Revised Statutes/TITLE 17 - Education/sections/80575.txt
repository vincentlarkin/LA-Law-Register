§3045.7.  Repayment of loans
All repaid principal and interest resulting from repayment of a loan shall be credited to the commission for the commission's use in making further loans.
Acts 1991, No. 799, §1, eff. July 22, 1991; Acts 1991, 3rd E.S., No. 3, §1, eff. Aug. 5, 1991.
