§377.  Quality Science and Mathematics Program; oversight
A.  The activities of the council and the implementation of the program may be reviewed by the House Committee on Education and the Senate Committee on Education and the rules and regulations shall be subject to review as provided by the Administrative Procedure Act.
B.  Repealed by Acts 2001, No. 1185, §8, eff. July 1, 2001.
Acts 1992, No. 1093, §1; Acts 2001, No. 1185, §8, eff. July 1, 2001.
