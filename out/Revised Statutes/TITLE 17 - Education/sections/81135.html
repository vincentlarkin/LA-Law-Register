SUBPART D.  EMPLOYEES OTHER THAN TEACHERS

IN ORLEANS PARISH

§521.  Definitions

As used in this Sub-part the term "employee" means any person in the employ of the Orleans Parish school board who is not a teacher or whose legal employment does not require the holding of a teacher's certificate.
