§2119.  Celebrate Freedom Week
Each September, in conjunction with Constitution Day and Constitution Week, each
public school shall observe Celebrate Freedom Week.  As part of this observance, the public
school governing authority shall ensure that all students receive age- and grade-appropriate
instruction, in a social studies or other appropriate course, on topics related to freedom.  Such
topics may include but are not limited to instruction related to the nation's founding and the
intent, meaning, and importance of the Declaration of Independence and the Constitution of
the United States of America, including the Bill of Rights.
Acts 2022, No. 370, §1.
