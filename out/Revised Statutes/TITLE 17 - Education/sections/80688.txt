§3225.  Louisiana Delta Community College; creation
The Louisiana Delta Community College is hereby created in the area of the Monroe Regional Planning and Economic Development District as a comprehensive, two-year institution of higher education as part of the Louisiana Community and Technical College system under the management of the Board of Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges. Such community college shall offer classes at appropriate satellite locations, including in East Carroll Parish.
Acts 1997, No. 1369, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 151, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
NOTE:  See Acts 1997, No. 1369, §§3, 4.
