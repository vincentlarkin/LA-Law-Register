§274.  Free enterprise; elective course
Public high schools may give instruction in an elective course in free enterprise in accordance with a course of study prescribed by the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education for at least one semester, equal to one-half unit of credit.  The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall prescribe suitable teaching materials for the instruction.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 83, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 324, §1, eff. July 13, 1977.  Acts 1984, No. 283, §1, §2; Acts 2003, No. 296, §1, eff. June 13, 2003; Acts 2010, No. 327, §1.
