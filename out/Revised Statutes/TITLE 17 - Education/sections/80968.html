CHAPTER 42. LOUISIANA PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL LAW

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§3971. Short citation

            This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Public Charter School Law".

            Acts 1995, No. 192, §1, eff. June 14, 1995; Acts 1997, No. 477, §1, eff. June 30, 1997; Acts 2024, No. 334, §1, eff. May 28, 2024.
