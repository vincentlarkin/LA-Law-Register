SUBPART F.  FULL-TIME EMPLOYEES OF
VOCATIONAL-TECHNICAL SCHOOLS
§540.  Definitions
As used in this Subpart, the following terms have the following meanings:
(1)(a)  "Board" means the Board of Supervisors of Community and Technical
Colleges.
(b)  Further, any reference in this Subpart to the State Board of Elementary and
Secondary Education insofar as such reference is related to the exercise of jurisdiction over
employees of a vocational-technical school shall mean and be interpreted to mean the Board
of Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges.
(2)  "Employee" means any full-time teacher or instructional personnel of a
vocational-technical school.
(3)  "Vocational-technical school" means any vocational-technical school under the
jurisdiction of the Board of Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 661, §1; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 151, §1, eff. July 1,
1999; Acts 2022, No. 374, §1.
