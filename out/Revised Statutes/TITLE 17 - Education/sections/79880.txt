SUBPART D.  JUNIOR COLLEGE, EUNICE
§1521.  Establishment
The Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College is hereby authorized to establish, as a branch or extension of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College a two-year junior commuters' college which shall be located at Eunice in the Eunice, Crowley, Jennings and Ville Platte area.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 74, §1.
