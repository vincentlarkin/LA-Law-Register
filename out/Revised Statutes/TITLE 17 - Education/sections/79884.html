SUBPART E.  JUNIOR COLLEGE IN CATAHOULA, CONCORDIA,

TENSAS, LASALLE, FRANKLIN AND CALDWELL PARISH AREA

§1531.  Establishment

The Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College is hereby authorized to establish, as a branch or extension of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, a two-year junior commuters' college which shall be located in one of the parishes of Catahoula, Concordia, Tensas, LaSalle, Franklin and Caldwell at a location which the board of supervisors determines as in the best interests of the state, after consideration of geographical factors and potential attendance factors.

Added by Acts 1966, No. 163, §1.
