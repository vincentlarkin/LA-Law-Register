§162.  School busses purchased for private use
Every school bus sold or transferred to any use other than school activities in this state shall be painted by the new owner a color other than national school bus chrome yellow, all lettering of school bus identification, and all semaphore arms and alternate flashing signal lights shall be removed therefrom.
Added by Acts 1950, No. 505, §2.  Amended by Act 1966, No. 109, §1.
