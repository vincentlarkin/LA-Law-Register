PART II.  TAXES FOR SUPPORT OF SCHOOLS

SUBPART A.  LOUISIANA STATE UNIVERSITY AND

AGRICULTURAL AND MECHANICAL COLLEGE

§2201.  Ad valorem tax for university

There shall be an annual tax of one half of one mill on the dollar of all taxable property in the state as provided in R.S. 47:1701 and R.S. 47:1704.
