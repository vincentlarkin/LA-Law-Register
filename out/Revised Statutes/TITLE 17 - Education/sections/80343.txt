§2358.  Uniformity of application and construction
In applying and construing this Part, consideration shall be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to this subject matter among states that enact it.
Acts 1968, No. 651, §1; Acts 2010, No. 937, §2, eff. July 1, 2010.
