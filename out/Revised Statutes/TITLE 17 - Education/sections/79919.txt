PART IV.  MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS
SUBPART A.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§1601.1.  Pete Maravich Assembly Center
The multipurpose assembly center located on the main campus of Louisiana State University, operated by and under the authority of the Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, is designated and shall be known hereafter as the "Pete Maravich Assembly Center".
Acts 1988, No. 901, §1.
