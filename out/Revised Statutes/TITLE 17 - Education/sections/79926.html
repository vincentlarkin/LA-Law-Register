§163.  Penalties

Penalties for violation of R.S. 17:161 and 17:162 shall be twenty-five dollars for the first offense, seventy-five dollars for the second offense, and one hundred twenty-five dollars or ninety days for the third offense.  

Added by Acts 1950, No. 505, §3.
