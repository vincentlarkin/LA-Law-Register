§407.82. Definitions.

            As used in this Part, the following terms have the following meanings:

            (1) "Department" means the state Department of Education.

            (2) "Early learning staff" means a person employed as a full-time staff member in a licensed early learning center or a registered family child day care home.

            Acts 2014, No. 868, §3, eff. Feb. 1, 2015; Acts 2015, No. 297, §1; Acts 2022, No. 374, §1.
