§407.34.  Requirement of licensure

All early learning centers shall be licensed prior to beginning operations in Louisiana.  Early learning center licenses shall be of three types: Type I, Type II, and Type III.

Acts 2014, No. 868, §2, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
