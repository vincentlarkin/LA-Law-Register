§1989.2. Definitions

            As used in this Part, the following terms have the following meanings unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:

            (1) "Director" means the director of the program.

            (2) "Program" means the Governor's Program for Gifted Children.

            (3) "University" means the college or university at which the program's activities are held, if any.

            Acts 1999, No. 595, §1, eff. June 30, 1999; Acts 2022, No. 374, §1.
