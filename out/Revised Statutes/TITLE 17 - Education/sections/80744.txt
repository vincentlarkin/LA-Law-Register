§3383.  Public postsecondary education institutions; events; payments accepted
Each public postsecondary education institution and each management board of such
institutions shall accept or require the acceptance of cash as payment for goods sold at
athletic or other public events including but not limited to payments for concessions and
merchandise.
Acts 2024, No. 593, §1.
