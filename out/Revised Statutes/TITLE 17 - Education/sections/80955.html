PART VII.  LOUISIANA REMEDIAL EDUCATION

§394.  Short title

This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Remedial Education Act."

Added by Acts 1980, No. 433, §1, eff. July 21, 1980; Acts 1986, No. 698, §1, eff. June 1, 1987.
