§94.  Oaths of office; superintendents' duty to administer

The parish superintendents of schools or their designees may administer the oath required of any of the officials of the public schools or of any person required to take oath in any manner thereto except to qualify school board members.  

Amended by Acts 1976, No. 39, §1.
