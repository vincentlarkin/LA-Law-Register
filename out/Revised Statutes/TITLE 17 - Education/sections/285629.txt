§85.7.  Naming of Reserve Officers' Training Corps building at West Monroe High School
The Ouachita Parish School Board may name the Reserve Officers' Training Corps building at West Monroe High School in honor of James Donald Halsell, Jr.
Acts 2005, No. 215, §1, eff. June 29, 2005.
