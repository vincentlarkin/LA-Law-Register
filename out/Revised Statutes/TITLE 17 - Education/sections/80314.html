§2276.  Records showing receipt and disposition of bodies

The secretary of the board shall keep an accurate record of all bodies received by the board, showing the dates of receipt and distribution, or other disposition made of them, the sources from which received, and the educational institutions to which sent.  This record shall be open to inspection by the members of the board and by the persons, or their authorized representatives, from whom the bodies were received.
