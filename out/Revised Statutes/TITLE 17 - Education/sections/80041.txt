§1926.  Reports
The governance board shall present a strategic plan and annual progress
report to the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget, the Louisiana Board of
Regents, and Louisiana Economic Development not later than February first of each
year.  The governance board shall make such other reports as are required by the
Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget.
Acts 2002, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 41, §1.
