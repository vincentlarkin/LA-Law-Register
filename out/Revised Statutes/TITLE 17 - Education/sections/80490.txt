§3023.3.  Loan of reserve funds
The board may authorize the granting of guaranteed student loans made pursuant to
the provisions of this Chapter out of reserve funds available to the board for such purpose,
and may sell or discount, or both, such loans to banks, credit unions, and other authorized
lending institutions, at such consideration and upon such terms as may be agreed upon by the
board.
Acts 1978, No. 135, §2; Acts 1980, No. 815, §4; Acts 2016, No. 314, §3(A).
