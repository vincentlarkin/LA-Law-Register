§3045.8.  Loan funds; availability
The receipt of a LA-OP loan by an eligible borrower is subject to the availability of lending capital provided by appropriation.
Acts 1991, No. 799, §1, eff. July 22, 1991; Acts 1991, 3rd E.S., No. 3, §1, eff. Aug. 5, 1991; Acts 2012, No. 834, §2, eff. July 1, 2012.
