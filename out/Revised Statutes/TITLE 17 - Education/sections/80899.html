CHAPTER 37. TEACHER INCENTIVE PROGRAMS

§3831. Definitions

            As used in this Chapter, the following terms have the following meanings unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:

            (1) "Local system" means the appropriate city or parish school board or system.

            (2) "Local superintendent" means the appropriate city or parish school superintendent.

            (3) "Program" means a program limited to providing incentive pay to teachers for not using sick leave days.

            Acts 1999, No. 1231, §1, eff. July 9, 1999; Acts 2022, No. 374, §1.
