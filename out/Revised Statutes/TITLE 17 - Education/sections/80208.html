§2114.  Furnishing protective glasses or goggles

Any student using in the course of his studies any device dangerous or hazardous to the eyes such as welding equipment, acid or abrasives, or any other dangerous devices shall be furnished with and shall wear safety or protective glasses or goggles made of a material suitable to protect the eyes from such hazards.

Added by Acts 1966, No. 75, §1.
