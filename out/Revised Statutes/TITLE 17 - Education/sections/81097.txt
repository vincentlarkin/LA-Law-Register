§445.  Rules and regulations impairing provisions prohibited
Nothing contained in the Revised Statutes of Louisiana shall be construed as conferring upon any parish or city school board the authority to make rules and regulations which may impair or nullify the provisions of this Subpart.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 494, §2.
