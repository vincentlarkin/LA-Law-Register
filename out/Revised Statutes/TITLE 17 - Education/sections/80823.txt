§362.  Applications for reimbursement
Each school which seeks reimbursement pursuant to this Part shall submit to the superintendent an application therefor, together with such additional reports and documents as the superintendent may require, at such times, in such form, and containing such information as the superintendent may prescribe in order to carry out the purposes of this Part.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 205, §1, eff. July 11, 1980.
