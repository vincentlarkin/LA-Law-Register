PART V.  PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND SALARIES

OF UNCLASSIFIED EMPLOYEES

§2049.  Applicability

The provisions of this Part shall apply to all unclassified persons employed at all public vocational-technical institutes in this state.

Acts 1991, No. 612, §1, eff. July 17, 1991.
