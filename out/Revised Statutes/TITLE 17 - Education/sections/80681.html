§3220.  Purposes

The college, technical, community colleges, and university systems are established and maintained to serve the educational needs of the people of the state.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 313, §2, eff. July 17, 1975; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 151, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
