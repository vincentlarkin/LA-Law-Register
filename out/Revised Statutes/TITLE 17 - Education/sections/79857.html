§1501.1.  Louisiana State University at Alexandria; baccalaureate degrees authorized

A.  The Board of Regents may provide for the conversion of Louisiana State University at Alexandria to an institution offering baccalaureate degrees.

B.  Louisiana State University at Alexandria is recognized and defined as an institution offering baccalaureate degrees.  The Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College and the Board of Regents shall provide for implementation accordingly, subject to the provisions of this Section.

Acts 2000, 2d Ex. Sess., No. 4, §1, eff. June 28, 2000; Acts 2001, No. 402, §1, eff. June 14, 2001.
