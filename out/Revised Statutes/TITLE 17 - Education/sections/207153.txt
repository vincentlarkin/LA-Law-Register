§1601.2.  The Louisiana Museum of Natural History
The Louisiana State University Museum of Natural History, including all constituent collections thereof as designated by the Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, is hereby declared to be the official museum of natural history for the state of Louisiana and shall be known as the "Louisiana Museum of Natural History".
Acts 1999, No. 143, §1.
