CHAPTER 20-E.  LOUISIANA OPPORTUNITY LOAN PROGRAM
§3045.  Purpose
It is the purpose of this Chapter to assist middle income students in meeting the rising costs of higher education and thereby increase the educational opportunities of such students by providing low interest rate student loans to assist Louisiana students in furthering their higher education goals.
Acts 1991, No. 799, §1, eff. July 22, 1991.
