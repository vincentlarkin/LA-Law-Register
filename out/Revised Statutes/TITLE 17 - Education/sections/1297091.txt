§1945.3.  Louisiana Schools for the Deaf and Visually Impaired; year-round operation;
continuing service
The Louisiana Schools for the Deaf and Visually Impaired may operate year-round
under the provisions of this Section, subject to the approval of the superintendent of the
Special School District and subject to the availability of funds.
Acts 2022, No. 323, §1.
