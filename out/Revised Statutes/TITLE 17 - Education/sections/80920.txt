SUBPART C.  SCHOOL PERSONNEL EVALUATION
§3901.  Applicability
This Subpart and the program provided herein shall apply to all teachers and administrators.
Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 1, §2, eff. June 22, 1994; Acts 2010, No. 54, §1, eff. May 27, 2010.
