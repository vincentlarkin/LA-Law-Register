§1518.2.  Huey P. Long Medical Center; administration
The LSU Health Sciences Center at Shreveport, through its chancellor, shall enter into a cooperative endeavor agreement with Tulane University Health Sciences Center to include the current residency program in obstetrics offered by Tulane University Health Sciences Center.
Acts 2007, No. 220, §1, eff. July 2, 2007.
