§2506.  Political prohibitions; Federal Communications Commission Regulations

No station licensed to the Authority shall be used for, nor shall any network or local programs be devoted to, the presentation of biased or one-sided aspects of partisan politics, or to advocating or opposing any present or prospective political candidacy, or to advocating or opposing any legislation currently being considered or prepared.  All stations licensed to the Authority must be used in compliance with the rules and regulations of the Federal Communications Commission.

Added by Acts 1971, No. 13, §2.
