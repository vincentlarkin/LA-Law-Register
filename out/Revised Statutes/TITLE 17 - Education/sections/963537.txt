§5063.  Notice of Program Changes
In addition to any other requirements of this Chapter, the administering agency shall
notify all appropriate public and nonpublic school personnel, including school counselors,
of any changes in law or agency rules relative to the Taylor Opportunity Program for
Students no later than sixty days after such change.
Acts 2015, No. 227, §1.
