§407.52.  Coordination
The department shall coordinate with the office of state fire marshal and the
Louisiana Department of Health to align standards for licensing of early learning centers with
the standards for early childhood education programs.
Acts 2014, No. 868, §2, eff. Oct. 1, 2014; Acts 2015, No. 297, §1.
