§3041.12.  Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center; contracts

The commission shall enter into such contracts for students who have been admitted to the medical school, and the money obligations of such contract as made by the commission with the medical school shall be paid for out of funds to be provided in accordance with law for such purposes.

Acts 1997, No. 281, §1; Acts 1999, No. 802, §1, eff. July 2, 1999.
