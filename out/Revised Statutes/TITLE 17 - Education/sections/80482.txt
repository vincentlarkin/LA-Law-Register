§3003.  Authorization for employment
The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education is authorized and directed
to use these funds for the purpose of providing employment for school counselors and
counseling directors in each city, parish, or other local public school system.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 319, §3; Acts 2021, No. 275, §4.
