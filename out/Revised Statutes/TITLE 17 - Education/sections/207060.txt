§1601.5.  Isaac Greggs Band Hall
Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 14:316 or any other law to the contrary, the band hall located on Elton C. Harrison Drive on  the Baton Rouge campus of Southern University, operated by and under the authority of the Board of Supervisors of Southern University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, is designated and shall be known hereafter as the "Isaac Greggs Band Hall".
Acts 2003, No. 429, §1, eff. June 18, 2003.
