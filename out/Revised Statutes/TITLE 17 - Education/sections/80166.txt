§2035.  Necessary expenditures; board's authority to make
The Board of Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges may make such expenditures for the actual expenses of the board and for the salaries of assistants and for office and other expenses as in its judgment are necessary for the proper administration of this Subpart.
Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 151, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
