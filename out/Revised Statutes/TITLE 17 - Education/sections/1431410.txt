§3138.13.  Return on investment analysis; industry-based credentials
A.  Louisiana Works shall perform an annual return on investment analysis, including
but not limited to employment rates and wage outcomes, for the purpose of evaluating the
relationship between industry-based credentials earned by high school students and the
students' attainment of high-wage, high-demand jobs that are aligned with state workforce
priorities.
B.  The department shall submit a written report on the analysis to the House
Committee on Education, the House Committee on Labor and Industrial Relations, the
Senate Committee on Education, the Senate Committee on Labor and Industrial Relations,
and the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education not later than February first
annually.  The department shall report student information only in the aggregate and shall not
include any student's personally identifiable information as defined in R.S. 17:3914(B)(1)
in the report.
Acts 2025, No. 187, §1.
