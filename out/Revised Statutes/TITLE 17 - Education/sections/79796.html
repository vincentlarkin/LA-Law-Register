§1216.  Substitute teachers to fill vacancies; appointment

The position vacated by a teacher who has been granted a military leave, or by a teacher transferred to the position vacated by a teacher granted a military leave, in accordance with R.S. 17:1215, may be filled by the respective school boards by the appointment of substitute teachers.

Added by Acts 1950, 2nd Ex.Sess., No. 14, §1.
