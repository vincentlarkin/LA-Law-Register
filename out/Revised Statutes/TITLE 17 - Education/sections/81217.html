§87.3.  Disposition of revenues received from grants

All moneys received as consideration for grants authorized by R.S. 17:87.1 shall be deposited by the school board and credited to the general school fund of the parish wherein the sixteenth section or part thereof is located.

Added by Acts 1950, Ex. Sess., No. 9, §1.
