§229.  Appointment of visiting teachers, or supervisors of child welfare and attendance
The local school superintendent shall appoint visiting teachers and supervisors of child welfare and attendance; but no person shall be so recommended or so appointed unless certified by the state board of education.
Acts 1964, No. 109, §2; Acts 2012, No. 1, §1, eff. July 1, 2012.
