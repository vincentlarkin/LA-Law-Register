CHAPTER 29.  LOUISIANA EDUCATIONAL EMPLOYEES
PROFESSIONAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§3601.  Purpose
It is the purpose of this Chapter to provide a mechanism by which professional educational employees in public elementary and secondary schools of this state will have an opportunity for continuing professional growth and improvement in order to enable them to acquire and bring into the classroom innovative ideas, insight, greater competence, and better methods of instruction.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 207, §1.
