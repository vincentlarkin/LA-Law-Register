§416.5.  Same; attorney fees

If a judgment is rendered in favor of the teacher in an action provided for in R.S. 17:416.4, it may include an award for attorney fees if the court finds that the action was brought in bad faith or was otherwise brought without sufficient merit.  

Added by Acts 1983, No. 378, §2.
