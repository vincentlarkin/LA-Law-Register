§3042.6.  Reports
The commission shall make an annual report to the legislature of its activities under the provisions of this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 934, §1, eff. Aug. 2, 1981; Acts 1986, No. 583, §1, eff. July 2, 1986.
