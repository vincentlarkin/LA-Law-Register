PART II.  MANAGEMENT BOARDS
§3205.  Qualifications of members of boards
No member of the faculty and no officer or employee of a university or college shall be eligible hereafter for appointment to any board which has authority for his/her appointment.  However, this provision shall not prohibit the appointment of any person serving in an emeritus capacity to a board other than the board under which he served.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 313, §2, eff. July 17, 1975.
