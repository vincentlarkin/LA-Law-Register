§68.4.  Unfunded accrued liability; payment
In the event that the establishment or the operation of the St. George Community
School District results in the elimination of one or more positions of current members of the
Louisiana School Employees' Retirement System by means of privatizing, outsourcing, or
contracting the service with a private employer, vendor, or by any other means, the St.
George Community School District shall remit that portion of the unfunded accrued liability
attributable to that position or positions pursuant to R.S. 11:1195.2.
Acts 2025, No. 455, §1, eff. See Act.
