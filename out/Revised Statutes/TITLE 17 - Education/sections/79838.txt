§1379.  Consolidation of school systems; call of election
An election shall be called for the purpose of consolidating two or more school systems, in accordance with Article VIII, Section 10(C) of the Constitution of Louisiana, pursuant to an act of the legislature or a resolution adopted by each of the affected school boards. Consolidation of two or more school systems shall be subject to the approval by a majority of the electors voting, in each school system affected, in such election held for this purpose.
Acts 2012, No. 691, §1.
