§2121.1. Voter registration day; high schools

            A. The first Tuesday after the first Monday in May annually shall be known as Louisiana High School Seniors Voter Registration Day.

            B. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to create any duty or obligation for the secretary of state or any registrar of voters.

            Acts 2023, No. 79, §2.
