§165.  Penalty for violation of regulations
The Louisiana state board of education may issue orders prohibiting the operation of any school bus which does not comply with the rules and regulations adopted hereunder and such orders shall be enforced by the Louisiana state department of public safety.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 399, §2.
