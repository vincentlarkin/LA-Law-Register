§3041.13.  Cancellation

The commission shall have authority to cancel any contract made between it and any applicant upon cause deemed sufficient by the commission, and the commission shall have authority to cancel such contracts which it may lawfully cancel made with any of the schools as herein provided.

Acts 1997, No. 281, §1.
