CHAPTER 19-A.  SCHOOL CHAPLAINS
§3011.  School chaplains; employment; volunteer
Each city, parish, or other local public school board may employ or accept as a
volunteer a certified chaplain to provide support, services, and programs for students, staff,
and parents as assigned by a school board pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 2024, No. 293, §1, eff. May 28, 2024; Acts 2024, No. 688, §1, eff. June 19,
2024.
