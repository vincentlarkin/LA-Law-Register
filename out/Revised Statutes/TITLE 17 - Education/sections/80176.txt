§2049.1.  Employment standards; evaluation
Each vocational-technical employee shall meet all employment standards required by the Board of Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges, and shall be evaluated annually pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 17:2049.2.
Acts 1991, No. 612, §1, eff. July 17, 1991; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 151, §1, eff. July 1, 1999.
