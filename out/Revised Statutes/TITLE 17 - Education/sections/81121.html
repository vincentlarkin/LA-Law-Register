§497.2. Remuneration for participation in in-service training programs

            Any school bus operator who participates in the school bus operator's instructional program provided through the state Department of Education shall receive as remuneration the sum of six dollars for each hour of instruction in such program, not to exceed forty-eight dollars per school year.

            Added by Acts 1978, No. 37, §2. Amended by Acts 1980, No. 408, §1; Acts 2017, No. 335, §1.
