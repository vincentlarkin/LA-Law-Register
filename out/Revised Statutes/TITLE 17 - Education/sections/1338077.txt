§4103.  Rules
The administering agency shall adopt rules in accordance with the Administrative
Procedure Act for the implementation of the provisions of this Chapter. When developing
the rules, the administering agency shall consult with the Louisiana National Guard.
Acts 2023, No. 279, §2.
