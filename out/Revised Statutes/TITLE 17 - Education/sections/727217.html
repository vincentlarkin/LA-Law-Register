PART II. LOUISIANA GRANTING RESOURCES

AND AUTONOMY FOR DIPLOMAS ACT

§3139. Louisiana Granting Resources and Autonomy for Diplomas Act; title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Granting Resources and Autonomy for Diplomas Act".

            Acts 2010, No. 741, §1, eff. June 29, 2010; Acts 2011, No. 418, §1, eff. July 12, 2011.
