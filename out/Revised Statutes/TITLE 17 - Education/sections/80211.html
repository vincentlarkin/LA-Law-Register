§2115.10.  Classroom time

Nothing in R.S. 17:2115.1 through 2115.10 shall prevent a city or parish school board from adopting rules to reasonably limit the amount of classroom time that may be devoted to the student expression protected by the provisions of R.S. 17:2115.1 through 2115.10.

Acts 1995, No. 264, §1.
