§1601.7.  Dr. Dolores Richard Spikes Honors College Building
Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Honors College building located on B.A. Little Drive on the Baton Rouge campus of Southern University, operated by and under the authority of the Board of Supervisors of Southern University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, is designated and shall be known hereafter as the "Dr. Dolores Richard Spikes Honors College Building".
Acts 2007, No. 350, §1, eff. July 9, 2007.
