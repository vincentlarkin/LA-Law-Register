§407.44.  Denial, refusal to renew, or revocation of license; written notice
The department shall have the power to deny, revoke, or refuse to renew a license for an early learning center if an applicant has failed to comply with the provisions of this Part or any applicable, published rule or regulation of the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education relating to early learning centers. If a license is denied or revoked, or renewal is refused, the action shall be effective when made, and the department shall notify the applicant or licensee of such action in writing immediately, and of the reason for the denial or revocation, or refusal to renew the license.
Acts 2014, No. 868, §2, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
