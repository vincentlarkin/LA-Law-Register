§2115.1.  Official prayer; prohibition
No public elementary or secondary school shall adopt or use any official or standard prayer and when a voluntary, student-initiated, student-led prayer is offered, it shall be done in accordance with the religious views of the student offering the prayer.
Acts 1995, No. 264, §1.
