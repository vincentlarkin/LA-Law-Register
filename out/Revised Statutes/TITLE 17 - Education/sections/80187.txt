§2072.  St. Paul's College
The faculty of St. Paul's College, under the control of the Benedictine Fathers of Covington, Louisiana, or any corporation which may be established under their management, are authorized to graduate students and to confer degrees of bachelor of science, bachelor of arts, and master of arts, upon graduates of the college, under such rules and regulations and over the signatures of such person or persons as the said faculty or corporation may adopt.
