§3396.1.  Construction of part; supplemental and additional nature

This Part shall be deemed to provide a complete, additional, and alternative method for doing the things authorized hereby and shall be regarded as supplemental and additional to powers conferred by other laws.

Acts 1992, No. 882, §1.
