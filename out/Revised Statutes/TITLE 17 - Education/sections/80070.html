PART II. JIMMY D. LONG, SR., LOUISIANA SCHOOL FOR MATH,

SCIENCE, AND THE ARTS

§1961. Legislative intent

           It is the intent of the legislature to establish an independent, residential school for certain high school students with the express purpose of providing a more challenging educational experience for the gifted and talented children of this state and of developing such children to their full potential.

           Added by Acts 1981, No. 932, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1982.
