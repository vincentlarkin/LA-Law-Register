SUBPART B-1.  DEAN LEE AGRICULTURAL CENTER
§1502.  Name of school
The Louisiana State University School of Vocational Agriculture at Chambers in Rapides Parish, Louisiana, shall hereafter be known as the "Dean Lee Agricultural Center".
Added by Acts 1956, No. 57, §1.
