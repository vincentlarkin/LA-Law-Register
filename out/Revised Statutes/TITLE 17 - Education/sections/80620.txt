§3107.  Prohibition against enrolling or employing dismissed student, faculty or employee
No student, faculty member, member of the administrative staff or other employee expelled or dismissed under the provisions of this Chapter shall be eligible to be enrolled or employed in any capacity in any state owned and operated institution of higher learning in this state for a period of one year from the date of the expulsion or dismissal.
Added by Acts 1969, No. 59, §1.
