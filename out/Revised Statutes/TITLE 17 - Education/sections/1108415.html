§3100.4. Cooperation of state agencies

            The authority shall develop a plan for the establishment of education savings accounts. The Board of Regents, and any other state agency requested to do so, shall cooperate with the authority and provide technical assistance upon request. To facilitate participation in the START K12 Program, such plan shall allow for the deposit of small sums of money on a regular, incremental basis.

            Acts 2018, No. 687, §2, eff. May 30, 2018.
