§266.  Cursive writing; required instruction
Cursive writing shall be taught in all public schools.  Each public school shall ensure
that such instruction is introduced by the third grade and incorporated into the curriculum in
grades four through twelve.
Acts 2016, No. 482, §1, eff. July 1, 2017.
