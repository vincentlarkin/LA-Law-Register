§3106.  Recording the fact of dismissal

Thirty days after expulsion or, if a hearing is demanded, the completion of the hearing, if the governing authority sustains the order expelling a student, a notation shall be made on the student's transcript reflecting the date of and the reasons for the expulsion.

Added by Acts 1969, No. 59, §1.
