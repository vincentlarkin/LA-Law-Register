§1512.  Location; land acquisition and construction
The Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College shall determine the location for the college, shall acquire the necessary land therefor and shall construct, equip and furnish the buildings necessary therefor.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 41, §2.
