§407.37.  Operating without a license; penalties
The department may take action against a person who operates an early learning
center without a valid license issued by the department.  Such actions may include the
following:
(1)  Assessing a civil fine of up to one thousand dollars for each day of the knowing
and continued unlicensed operation of an early learning center.
(2)  Filing a suit in the district court in the parish in which the center is located for
injunctive relief, including a temporary restraining order, to enjoin the institution, agency,
corporation, person or persons, or any other group from continuing the violation.
Acts 2014, No. 868, §2, eff. Oct.1, 2014; Acts 2016, No. 659, §1.
