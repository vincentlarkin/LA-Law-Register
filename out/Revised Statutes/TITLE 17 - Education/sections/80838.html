§3653.  Failure by participants to successfully complete program

If a participating teacher fails to complete his or her approved program during the five-year period or within an additional year if there were acceptable extenuating circumstances, his or her certificate shall be renewed without the endorsement and the additional compensation shall cease.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 207, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 257, §1, eff. July 13, 1984.
