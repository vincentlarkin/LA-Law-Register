§407.53.  Rules

The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act to carry out the provisions of this Part.

Acts 2014, No. 868, §2, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
