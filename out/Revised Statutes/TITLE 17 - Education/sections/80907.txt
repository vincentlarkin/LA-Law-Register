CHAPTER 39.  THE CHILDREN FIRST ACT
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§3871.  Short title
This Chapter may be referred to and may be cited as "The Children First Act".
Acts 1988, No. 659, §1, eff. July 15, 1988.
{{NOTE:  ACTS 1988, NO. 659, §3, PROVIDES THAT IF R.S. 17:421.3 IS NOT FUNDED, THE PROVISIONS OF THIS CHAPTER, AS ENACTED BY SAID ACT, SHALL BE NULL AND VOID.}}
