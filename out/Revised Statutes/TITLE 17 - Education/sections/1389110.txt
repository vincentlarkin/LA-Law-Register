§3399.40.  Due process requirements; right to an attorney
A public postsecondary education management board shall be prohibited from
denying any student the right to have an attorney present at any disciplinary hearings to
which the student is a party if the student desires to engage an attorney at the student's
expense.
Acts 2024, No. 584, §1, eff. June 11, 2024.
