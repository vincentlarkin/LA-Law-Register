§2081.  South Central College

South Central College in Lake Charles, an interdenominational institution for the education of young men and women, situated in the parish of Calcasieu, Louisiana, shall have the power and is hereby granted authority to graduate students and confer such literary honors and degrees, and to grant such diplomas, as are conferred and granted by any colleges, universities or seminaries of learning in the United States and Europe, to such graduates of such institution, or other persons, as may be provided for under such rules and regulations and over the signature of such persons as such corporation may adopt.

Added by Acts 1958, No. 165, §1.
