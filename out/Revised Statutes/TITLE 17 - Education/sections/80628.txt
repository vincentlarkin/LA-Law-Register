§3125.  Power to revise or eliminate programs
The Board of Regents shall have the power to revise or eliminate an existing degree program, department of instruction, division, or similar subdivision of any public institution of higher education.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 313, §1, eff. July 17, 1975.
