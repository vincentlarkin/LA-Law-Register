§3974.  Prohibitions; persons convicted of felony offenses
No person who has been convicted of or has pled nolo contendere to a crime listed
in R.S. 15:587.1(C) shall be hired by a charter school as a teacher, substitute teacher, bus
operator, substitute bus operator, or janitor, or as a temporary, part-time, or permanent school
employee of any kind.
Acts 2012, No. 2, §1; Acts 2016, No. 497, §2, eff. June 14, 2016; Acts 2017, No. 335,
§1.
