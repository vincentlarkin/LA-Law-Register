§1552.  Acquisition of land and buildings
The Board of Supervisors of said Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College is vested with full authority to determine the location of and acquire the land and buildings deemed necessary for the establishment of such branch.
Added by Acts 1956, No. 60, §2.  Amended Acts 1966, No. 159, §1.
