§2273.  Meetings of the board

There shall be at least one regular meeting of the board each year, to be held at such time and place as the board may determine.  Each department of anatomy represented in the board will be entitled to one vote only, in any matter to be voted upon.

Amended by Acts 1968, No. 250, §1.
