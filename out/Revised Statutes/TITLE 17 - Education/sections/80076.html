§1967.  Eligibility limited to high school sophomores, juniors, and seniors.

The school shall  admit high school sophomores, juniors, and seniors.

Added by Acts 1981, No. 932, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1982; Acts 2010, No. 121, §1, eff. June 8, 2010.
