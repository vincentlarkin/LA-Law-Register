SUBPART D-2.  BALANCED TREATMENT FOR
CREATION-SCIENCE AND EVOLUTION-SCIENCE
IN PUBLIC SCHOOL INSTRUCTION
§286.1.  Short title
This Subpart shall be known as the "Balanced Treatment for Creation-Science and
Evolution-Science Act."
Added by Acts 1981, No. 685, §1.
NOTE:  The Balanced Treatment for Creation-Science and Evolution-Science
Act, R.S. 17:286.1 to 286.7, was held unconstitutional in
Edwards v.
Aguillard
, La. 1987, 107 S.Ct. 2573, 96 L.Ed.2d 510.
