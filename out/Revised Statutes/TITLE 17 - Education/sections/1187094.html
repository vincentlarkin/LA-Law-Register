§410.1. Interior blueprints and floor plans of public school buildings and facilities; exemption from public records law

            Blueprints and floor plans of the interior of a public school building or facility shall not be subject to the Public Records Law.

            Acts 2020, No. 211, §1, eff. June 11, 2020.
