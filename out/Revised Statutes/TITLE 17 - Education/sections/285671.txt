§3397.3.  Corporation defined; status
A.  As used in this Subpart, "corporation" means a nonprofit corporation organized under and pursuant to the Nonprofit Corporation Law, as provided in Chapter 2 of Title 12 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
B.  In the exercise of the powers, functions, and activities authorized by this Subpart, a corporation shall not constitute an instrumentality of the state, a state agency, board, or commission, or a political subdivision.
C.  Repealed by Acts 2008, No. 815, §5.
Acts 2004, No. 672, §1, eff. July 5, 2004; Acts 2008, No. 815, §5.
