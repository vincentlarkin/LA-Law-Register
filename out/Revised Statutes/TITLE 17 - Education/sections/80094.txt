§1970.8.  Funding; acceptance of other funds
A.  Funds for the operation of the school and the programs provided by the school
shall come from monies appropriated therefor by the legislature.
B.  Nothing in this Part shall be so construed as to prohibit or prevent the board
from accepting federal funds or monies from any corporation or other private contributor
for use in establishing school facilities, operating the school, maintaining the school, or
providing programs by the school.
Acts 1997, No. 1350, §1.
