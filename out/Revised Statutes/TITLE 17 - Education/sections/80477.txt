§2990.5.  Reimbursement
Upon complying with the conditions as set forth in R.S. 17:2990.4, the superintendent of education, or a person or persons duly appointed by him, shall reimburse to the parent a sum amounting to $50.00 per student in grades 1 through 8 and $75.00 per student in grades 9 through 12.  This sum shall be reduced by the superintendent of education in the event it should exceed 30% of the total cost of educating a public school student in the state.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 94, §6.
