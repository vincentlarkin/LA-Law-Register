§424.1.  Credit for teaching service in the United States armed forces institute school system
Any Louisiana certified teacher in the United States armed forces institute school system, who has taught academic subjects consecutively in Louisiana, and who transferred from the United States armed forces institute to a parish or city school system of this state, shall be given full credit on the teachers salary schedule of the parish or city school system concerned, for the years and months of satisfactory teaching service previously rendered in the United States armed forces institute school system of the state.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 81, §1.
