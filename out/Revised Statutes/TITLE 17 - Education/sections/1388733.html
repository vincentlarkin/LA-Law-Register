§17.8. Health Education requirement; virtual course

            A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a one-half credit course in Health Education that is required for a high school diploma shall be offered virtually to any student that is required to enroll in a physical education course for at least two consecutive school years.

            B. No policy or administrative rule promulgated by the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education that is contrary to this Section shall be enforced.

            Acts 2024, No. 530, §1, eff. June 10, 2024.
