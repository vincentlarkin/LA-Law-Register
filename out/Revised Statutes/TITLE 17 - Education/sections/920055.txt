§407.93.  Responsibilities of approved local early learning enrollment coordinators
In accordance with R.S. 17:407.22, local early learning enrollment coordinators shall support families in accessing high-quality publicly-funded early childhood care and education by:
(1)  Conducting a process for disseminating information about early childhood care and education programs and collecting family preferences in order to make distribution recommendations.
(2)  Using a common application process, annually determine family demand for publicly-funded early childhood care and education within the coverage area.
(3)  Providing a recommendation to the department of the distribution of available publicly-funded early childhood care and education slots within the coverage area.
Acts 2014, No. 717, §1, eff. June 18, 2014.
