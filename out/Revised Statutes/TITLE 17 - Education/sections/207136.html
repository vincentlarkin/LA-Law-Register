§3050.3. Administration

            The GO-Youth ChalleNGe Program shall be under the administration of the Board of Regents, through the office of student financial assistance, which shall adopt rules and regulations to implement the provisions of this Chapter.

            Acts 2003, No. 826, §1, eff. July 1, 2003; Acts 2019, No. 402, §1.
