§407.83.  Early learning staff; training
Early learning staff who participate in an early childhood educational training program approved by the department may be eligible for a scholarship funded by the state. Such scholarship shall be awarded only if funds are appropriated by the federal government for such purpose.
Acts 2014, No. 868, §3, eff. Feb. 1, 2015.
