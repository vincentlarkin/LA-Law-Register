§2944.  Dual Enrollment Innovation and Equity Grant; purpose; management
A.  The Dual Enrollment Innovation and Equity Grant is hereby established to
conduct capacity building for implementation of a statewide dual enrollment framework with
the purpose of assisting and encouraging high schools and public postsecondary education
institutions to implement new or conduct existing dual enrollment programs that provide
equitable access and healthy participation.
B.  A portion of funds appropriated to the Higher Education Initiatives Fund pursuant
to R.S. 17:3129.6 shall be allocated to the grant created by this Section.
Acts 2020, No. 204, §1, eff. June 11, 2020.
