§2083.  Mt. Carmel Junior College
The Mount Carmel Junior College in the city of New Orleans, owned and operated by a corporation known as The Congregation of Our Lady of Mount Carmel, shall have the power and is hereby granted authority to graduate students and confer such certificates, literary and academic honors and diplomas as are conferred and granted by any other junior colleges within the state of Louisiana, to such graduates of said institution, or other persons, as may be provided for under such rules and regulations as said corporation may adopt and over the signature of such persons as said corporation may designate.
Added by Acts 1961, No. 74, §1.
