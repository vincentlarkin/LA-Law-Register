§3041.23.  Tulane University School of Medicine; contracts
The commission shall enter into such contracts for students who have been admitted to the medical school, and the money obligations of any such contract as made by the commission with the medical school shall be paid for out of funds to be provided in accordance with law for such purposes.
Acts 2004, No. 894, §1.
