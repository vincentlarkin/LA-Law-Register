§122.  Application of general laws and regulations of state board of education
All laws, and all rules and regulations of the state board of education governing other parish school boards throughout the state, shall govern the Orleans Parish School Board, except as otherwise specifically provided in this Title.
Acts 1960, 3rd Ex.Sess., No. 4, §1, emerg.  eff. Jan. 12, 1961.
