§3024.1. Student loans as security for deposit of public funds

            Notes representing loans to students which are guaranteed by this board in accordance with a contract agreement under the provisions of this Chapter, may be pledged as collateral security for deposit of public funds as required in R.S. 39:1221.

            Acts 1978, No. 135, §2; Acts 2016, No. 314, §3(A).
