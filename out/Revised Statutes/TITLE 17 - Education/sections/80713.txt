§3326.  Compensation while on leave
Each person granted sabbatical leave shall receive and be paid compensation for the period of leave approved at the rate and according to procedures promulgated by the appropriate management board.  The employer and employee shall contribute to the retirement system on the basis of the annual salary rate while the employee is on leave.
Acts 1991, No. 858, §1.
