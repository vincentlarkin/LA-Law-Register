§1532.  Land acquisition; construction
The Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College may acquire the necessary land therefor, and shall construct, equip and furnish the buildings necessary therefor.
Added by Acts 1966, No. 163, §1.
