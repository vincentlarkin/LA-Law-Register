PART III.  TEACHING CERTIFICATES
§3652.  Teaching certificates
A.  The provisions of this Chapter shall have no effect on the existing requirements for initial certification or the designated types of certificates.
B.  Certificates subsequent to the initial certificate issued to a participating teacher shall contain an endorsement of participation in a professional improvement program.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 207, §1.
