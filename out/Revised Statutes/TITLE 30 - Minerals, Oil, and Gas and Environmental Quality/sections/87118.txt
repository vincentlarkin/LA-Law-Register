§2055.  Permits; licenses
No person shall conduct any activity which results in the discharge of air contaminants without the appropriate permit or license as required under the regulations of the secretary adopted pursuant to this Chapter.
Acts 1983, No. 97, §1, eff. Feb. 1, 1984; Acts 1999, No. 348, §1, eff. June 16, 1999.
