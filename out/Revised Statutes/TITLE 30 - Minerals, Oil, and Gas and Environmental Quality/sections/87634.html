§596.  Waiver of requirements

The commissioner may waive any requirement of this Part when upon reasonable cause shown he finds that the application of enforcement thereof will work an undue hardship on the person affected or will seriously impede the efficiency of his administration of this Part, and that the application or enforcement thereof is not necessary to the accomplishment of the purposes of this Chapter.

Added by Acts 1973, Ex.Sess., No. 16, §1, emerg. eff. Dec. 8, 1973, at 9:55 A.M.
