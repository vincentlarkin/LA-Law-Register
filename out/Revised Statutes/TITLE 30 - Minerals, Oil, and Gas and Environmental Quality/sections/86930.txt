§1159.  Contracts for materials and services
For purposes of carrying out the provisions of this Chapter, the secretary may, subject
to departmental contract management, contract with any person for materials and services
that cannot be performed by his staff or other state agencies and may apply for federal grants
or any other funding.
Acts 1983, No. 705, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1983; Acts 2023, No. 455, §2, eff. June 28,
2023.
