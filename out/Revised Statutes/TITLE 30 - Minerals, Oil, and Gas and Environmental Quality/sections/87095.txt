§2050.20.  Record of decision
For matters not handled by the division of administrative law, within the Department of State Civil Service, the secretary shall adopt rules requiring that the record of a decision be assembled in a uniform and consistent order.
Acts 1995, No. 947, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 1997, No. 17, §1.
