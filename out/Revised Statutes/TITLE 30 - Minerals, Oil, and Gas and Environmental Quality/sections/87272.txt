§2244.  Tax collectible from disposers and generators
The one time tax levied in this Chapter shall be collectible from and shall be paid by the generators or disposers who have contributed to the hazardous waste content of land in Louisiana to the extent of their contribution to such hazardous waste content as determined in this Chapter.
Acts 1984, 1st.  Ex. Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. March 27, 1984.
