§906.3.  Department of Revenue; surface mining and reclamation fees
A.  Funds received by the Department of Revenue in the form of surface mining and reclamation fees shall be deposited immediately upon receipt to the state treasury.
B, C.  Repealed by Acts 1992, No. 984, §18.
Acts 1986, No. 351, §1; Acts 1992, No. 984, §18; Acts 1997, No. 658, §2.
