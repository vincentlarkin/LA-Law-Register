§2134.  Compact funding
The legislature shall appropriate funds as required by Article III, Section d.  and Article IV, Section h.  of the Central Interstate Low-Level Radioactive Waste Compact (R.S. 30:2131).
Added by Acts 1982, No. 791, §1.
