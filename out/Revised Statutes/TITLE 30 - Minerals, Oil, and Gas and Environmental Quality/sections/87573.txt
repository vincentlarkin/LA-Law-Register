§44.  Gas to be measured by meter
All gas produced from the deposits of this state when sold shall be measured by meter
and the secretary shall, upon notice and hearing, relieve any common purchaser from
purchasing gas of an inferior quality or grade, and the secretary shall from time to time make
such regulations for delivery, metering and equitable purchase and taking as conditions may
necessitate.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
