§2159.  Landfill siting near airports

In parishes with populations of at least seventy thousand persons and less than seventy-five thousand persons according to the latest decennial figures, the Department of Environmental Quality may recognize and adhere to all Federal Aviation Administration safety guidelines, rules, regulations, or recommendations before issuing any permit for a solid waste facility located within five thousand feet of any main runway of an airport and that may interfere with or endanger air traffic safety.

Acts 2008, No. 526, §1, eff. June 30, 2008.
