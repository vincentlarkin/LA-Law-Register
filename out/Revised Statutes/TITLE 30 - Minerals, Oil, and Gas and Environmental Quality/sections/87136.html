§2075.  Permits, variances, and licenses

No person shall conduct any activity which results in the discharge of any substance into the waters of the state without the appropriate permit, variance, or license required under the regulations of the department adopted pursuant to this Chapter.

Acts 1979, No. 449, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980; Acts 1991, No. 236, §1; Acts 1999, No. 348, §1, eff. June 16, 1999.
