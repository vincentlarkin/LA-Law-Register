CHAPTER 16-A LOUISIANA COMMUNITY AIR

MONITORING RELIABILITY

§2381.1. Short title

            This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Community Air Monitoring Reliability Act".

            Acts 2024, No. 181, §1, eff. May 23, 2024.
