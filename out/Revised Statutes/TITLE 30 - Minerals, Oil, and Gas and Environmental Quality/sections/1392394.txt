§148.10.  Applicability
Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary contained in Subpart A of this Part,
the provisions of this Subpart shall apply to leases by the State Mineral and Energy Board
for the purposes set forth in R.S. 30:148.2.
Acts 2024, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 17, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2025.
