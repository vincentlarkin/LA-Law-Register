§2050.24.  Subpoenas and witnesses; fees; expenses; notice

A.  The secretary shall establish fees to be paid by the party for whom a subpoena is issued.  The fees shall be sufficient to defray fees and expenses due a witness in a civil proceeding and to reimburse the department for administrative costs of issuing and serving subpoenas.

B.  Notice of a subpoena may be given by certified mail return receipt requested or by other means authorized by law.

Acts 1995, No. 947, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
