§76.  Oilfield waste site cleanup
Except as provided in R.S. 30:77 in any cases where monies are expended to conduct investigative, testing, control, pit, or other cleanup activities at a waste site, the commissioner shall institute a civil action to recover from any responsible person all such monies so expended.  Any monies so recovered shall be paid into the state general fund.
Acts 1986, No. 892, §1, eff. July 10, 1986; Acts 1992, No. 984, §9.
