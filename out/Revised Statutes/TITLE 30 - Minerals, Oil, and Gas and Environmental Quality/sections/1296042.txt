§101.15.  Termination date
The provisions of this Part shall terminate and have no effect after June 30, 2027.
Acts 2022, No. 100, §2, eff. July 1, 2023.
NOTE: Pursuant to its own terms, R.S. 30:101.15 will terminate after June 30, 2027.
