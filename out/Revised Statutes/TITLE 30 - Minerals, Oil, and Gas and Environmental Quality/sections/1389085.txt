§2381.11.  Severability
If any provision of this Chapter or the application thereof to any person or
circumstance is held invalid, the invalidity shall not affect other provisions or applications
of the Chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to
this end the provisions of this Chapter are declared to be severable.
Acts 2024, No. 181, §1, eff. May 23, 2024.
