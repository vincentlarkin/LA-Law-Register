§2242.  Imposition

There is hereby levied a one time tax upon the hazardous waste content, as of July 1, 1984, of land in Louisiana, as determined in this Chapter.  

Acts 1984, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. March 27, 1984.  Acts 1984, No. 108, §1, eff. June 15, 1984.
