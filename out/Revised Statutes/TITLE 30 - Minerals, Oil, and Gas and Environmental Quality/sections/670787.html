CHAPTER 11.  LOUISIANA GEOLOGIC SEQUESTRATION

 OF CARBON DIOXIDE ACT

§1101.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Geologic Sequestration of Carbon Dioxide Act".

Acts 2009, No. 517, §2.
