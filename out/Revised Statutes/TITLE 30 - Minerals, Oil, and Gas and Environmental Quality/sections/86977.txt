§141.  Power of mineral board not derogated - Other rights and remedies not modified
R.S. 30:137 through 141 are not intended in any way to derogate from or question the
power and authority of the State Mineral and Energy Board to enter into any agreements of
any type whatsoever pursuant to its power and authority heretofore expressly or impliedly
granted by law; and the provisions hereof shall not modify in any way the right of any lessee
or other party to invoke the rights and remedies available under existing laws.
Acts 1963, No. 13, §5; Acts 2009, No. 196, §2, eff. July 1, 2009.
