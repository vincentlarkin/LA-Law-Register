§159.  State banks in liquidation, leases subject to approval, how

All mineral leases entered into by state banks in liquidation shall be subject to the approval of the State Mineral and Energy Board and of the district court having jurisdiction of the liquidations.

Acts 2009, No. 196, §2, eff. July 1, 2009.
