§2247.  Extension of time to file Contribution Report

Upon timely written application therefor, the secretary may grant an extension of time in which to file the contribution report not in excess of sixty days.  

Acts 1984, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. March 27, 1984.
