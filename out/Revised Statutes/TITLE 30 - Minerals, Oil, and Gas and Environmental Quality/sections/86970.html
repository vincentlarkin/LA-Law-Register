§138.  Agreements to offset, etc., to be made against any payable royalty

The agreements referred to in R.S. 30:137 may provide for such offset, compensation and recovery by such lessees or other parties to be made against any royalty payable to the state of Louisiana by such lessees or other parties.

Acts 1963, No. 13, §2.
