§208.  Exploration of public lands

The State Mineral and Energy Board may explore and develop the mineral resources of lands belonging to the state which might lease under Subpart A of Part II of Chapter 2 of this Title.

Acts 2009, No. 196, §2, eff. July 1, 2009.
