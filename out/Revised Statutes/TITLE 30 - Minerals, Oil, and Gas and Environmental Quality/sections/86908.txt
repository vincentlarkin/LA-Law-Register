CHAPTER 2.  LEASES AND CONTRACTS
PART I.  LEASES IN GENERAL
§102.  Lessees to notify lessors of the termination of mineral leases
Within ninety days after the expiration of:
(1)  Production under a mineral lease previously maintained by production and
(2)  all other rights to maintain that lease,
the lessee or his assigns shall notify the lessor or his representative that the lease has terminated, unless such notification is already provided in the lease.
Acts 1984, No. 559, §1.
