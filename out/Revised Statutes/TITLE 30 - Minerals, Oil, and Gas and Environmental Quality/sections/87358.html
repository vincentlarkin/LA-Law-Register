CHAPTER 15.  LOUISIANA SCHOOL ASBESTOS

ABATEMENT ACT

§2341.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana School Asbestos Abatement Act".  

Acts 1985, No. 394, §1, eff. July 10, 1985.
