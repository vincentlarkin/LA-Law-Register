§98.13.  Severability
If any provision of this Chapter or its application is held invalid, the invalidity shall
not affect other provisions or applications that can be given effect without the invalid
provision or application, and to this end, the provisions of this Chapter are severable.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
