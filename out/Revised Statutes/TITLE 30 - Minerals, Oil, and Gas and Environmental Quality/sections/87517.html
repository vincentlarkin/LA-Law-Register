§2531.7.  Rules and regulations

The deputy secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of public safety services, shall promulgate rules and regulations to provide for a uniform citation document which shall be used for issuing citations for violations of this Part.

Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 148, §3.
