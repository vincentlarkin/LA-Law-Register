§98.5.  Comprehensive water resource plan
A.  The department, utilizing the Natural Resources Commission, shall develop,
implement, and periodically update a comprehensive statewide water resource management
plan, addressing current and future water demands, conservation strategies, infrastructure
needs, environmental impacts, and sustainable water management practices.
B.  The comprehensive plan shall be developed through a transparent public process,
including stakeholder input, interagency collaboration, and review by the Natural Resources
Commission.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
