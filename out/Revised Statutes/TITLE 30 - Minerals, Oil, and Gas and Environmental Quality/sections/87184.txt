§2118.  Preemption
With the exception of Section 2117 and Section 2111(A) of this Chapter, none of the provisions of this Chapter shall be applicable to any activity which is regulated by the United States government or which is otherwise subject to federal law.
Acts 1979, No. 449, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.
