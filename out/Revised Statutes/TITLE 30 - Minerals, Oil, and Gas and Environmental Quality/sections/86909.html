§103.  Operators to report to owners amount of oil or gas produced

Operators taking or producing oil or gas from lands who do not market through a pipeline company shall report monthly to each owner of an oil or gas interest in the lands.  These monthly reports shall show the amount of oil or gas produced from the lands during the previous calendar month, the amount disposed of, and the amount which has not been disposed of.  Reports shall be sent by certified mail to each owner of a royalty, oil or gas interest, who has furnished his name and address to the operator.

Acts 2001, No. 973, §1.
