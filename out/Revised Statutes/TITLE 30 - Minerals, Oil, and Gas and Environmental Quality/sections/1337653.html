§1166. Demands by lessor; effect on assignee or sublessee

            An assignee or sublessee is bound by any notice or demand by the lessor on the lessee unless the lessor has been given written notice of the assignment or sublease. If notice has been given, any subsequent notice or demand by the lessor shall be made on the assignee or sublessee.

            Acts 2023, No. 455, §1, eff. June 28, 2023.
