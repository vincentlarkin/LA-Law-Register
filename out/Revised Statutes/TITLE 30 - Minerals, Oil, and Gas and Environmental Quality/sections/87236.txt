§2194.1.  Prohibitions
No person shall place or dispense a regulated substance into an underground storage
tank that has not been registered with the Louisiana Department of Environmental Quality.
Acts 1995, No. 336, §1, eff. June 16, 1995; Acts 2016, No. 521, §1; Acts 2024, No.
167, §1.
