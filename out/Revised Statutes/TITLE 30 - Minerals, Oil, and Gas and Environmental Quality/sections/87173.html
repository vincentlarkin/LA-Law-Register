§2108.  Impounding of materials by the department

In the event of emergency, the department may impound or order the impounding of any sources of radiation in the possession of any person who is not equipped to comply with or fails to comply with the provisions of this Chapter or any rules or regulations issued hereunder.

Acts 1979, No. 449, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980; Acts 1999, No. 303, §1, eff. June 14, 1999.
