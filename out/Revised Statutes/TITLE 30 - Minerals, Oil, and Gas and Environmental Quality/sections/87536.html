CHAPTER 22.  LOUISIANA ENVIRONMENTAL

REGULATORY INNOVATIONS PROGRAMS

§2561.  Citation

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Environmental Regulatory Innovations Programs Act".

Acts 1997, No. 992, §1.
