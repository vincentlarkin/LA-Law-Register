§2351.58.  Interim procedures
In developing a program to implement this Chapter, the secretary may provide for interim licensing and certification procedures to ensure a transition period of not less than one hundred eighty days before the application of the requirements established in this Chapter.
Acts 1993, No. 224, §1.
