§2196.  Manifest system
Within its comprehensive hazardous waste control program, the secretary shall require the use of a manifest system for the orderly tracking of hazardous wastes from the generation site to the site of treatment, storage, and disposal.  The system shall at a minimum require the designation of the generator, each transporter, the disposal facility, and the type and quantity of waste involved.  The secretary may establish additional criteria to accommodate the manifest system to internal record keeping and to facilitate the monitoring of hazardous wastes activity within the state.  Any requirements of the secretary shall be consistent with regulations promulgated by the Department of Public Safety which govern the transportation of hazardous wastes.
Acts 1983, No. 97, §1, eff. Feb. 1, 1984.
