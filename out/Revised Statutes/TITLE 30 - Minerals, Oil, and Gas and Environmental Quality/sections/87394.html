§2351.8.  Accreditation of training providers

A.  The secretary shall establish standards for accreditation of training providers under this Chapter, including provisions for training program quality control.  The secretary shall issue a certificate of accreditation to training providers that meet the secretary's accreditation standard and pay the accreditation fee provided in R.S. 30:2351.59.  The certificate of accreditation shall identify specifically the categories for which the training provider is accredited.

B.  Training providers shall be accredited for all categories for which they qualify.
