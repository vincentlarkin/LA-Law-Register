§98.6. Enforcement and compliance

            A. The department shall have the exclusive authority to enforce the provisions of this Part consistent with its authority under law.

            B. The department shall have the authority to collaborate with federal, state, and local agencies to ensure coordinated enforcement efforts.

            Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
