§1303.  Incentives, consumption of gasohol by state vehicles

A.  All existing industrial incentives are to be applied to projects consistent with the purposes of this Chapter.  

B.  All state-owned motor fuel supply stations shall supply gasohol for use by state vehicles whenever possible.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 793, §1.
