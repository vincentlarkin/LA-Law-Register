§954.  Expenses

Dues and expenses incurred as a consequence of membership by the state in the Interstate Mining Compact shall be paid only pursuant to appropriations therefor by the legislature.

Added by Acts 1979, No. 714, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1979.
