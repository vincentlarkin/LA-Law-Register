§2582.  Disclosure for mercury-containing formulated products used in health care facilities

On and after July 1, 2007, the manufacturers of formulated mercury-added products that are offered for sale or use to a health care facility in Louisiana shall provide both the Department of Environmental Quality and the recipient health care facility a notice of the mercury content of the product, down to a 1 part per million level.  The notice shall report the result of an analysis performed for mercury on the specific batch or lot of that product offered for sale.  The batch or lot number of the product shall be clearly identified on the product and on the notice.

Acts 2006, No. 126, §1, eff. June 2, 2006.
