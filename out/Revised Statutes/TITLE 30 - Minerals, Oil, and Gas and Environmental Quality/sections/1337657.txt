§1168.  Right to relief for violation
If a renewable energy lease is violated, an aggrieved party shall be entitled to any
appropriate relief provided by law.  Parties may stipulate what constitutes appropriate relief.
Acts 2023, No. 455, §1, eff. June 28, 2023.
