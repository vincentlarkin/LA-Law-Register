PART II. TERMINATION AND REMEDIES FOR VIOLATION

§1167. Termination of renewable energy lease

            A renewable energy lease terminates at the expiration of the agreed term or upon the occurrence of an express resolutory condition.

            Acts 2023, No. 455, §1, eff. June 28, 2023.
