§528.  Agencies created at same session
In the event that any other laws are enacted at this 1973 Extraordinary Session which create and provide for boards or other agencies charged with the functions of the administration, enforcement, management or operation of matters and facilities governed by this Chapter, each such board or agency and its functions are hereby merged, consolidated and transferred into the State Department of Conservation in the same manner and to the same extent as are the boards, commissions, agencies and offices enumerated in this Part, such merger, consolidation and transfer to be completed no later than April 1, 1974.
Added by Acts 1973, Ex.Sess., No. 16, §1, emerg. eff. Dec. 8, 1973, at 9:55 A.M.
