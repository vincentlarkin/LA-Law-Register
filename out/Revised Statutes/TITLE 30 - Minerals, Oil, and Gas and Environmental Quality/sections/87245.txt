§2195.7.  No inference of liability on the part of the state
Nothing in R.S. 30:2194 through 2195.11 shall establish or create any liability or
responsibility on the part of the department or the state of Louisiana to pay any cleanup cost
or third-party claims from any source other than the Tank Trust Account created by R.S.
30:2195, nor shall the department or the state of Louisiana have any liability or responsibility
to make any payments for cleanup costs or third-party claims if the Tank Trust Account
created herein is insufficient to do so.
Acts 1988, No. 767, §2, eff. July 15, 1988; Acts 1995, No. 336, §1, eff. June 16,
1995; Acts 2004, No. 692, §1, eff. July 6, 2004; Acts 2021, No. 114, §18, eff. July 1, 2022.
