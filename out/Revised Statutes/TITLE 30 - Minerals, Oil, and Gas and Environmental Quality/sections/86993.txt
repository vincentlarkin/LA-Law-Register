§15.  Appeal
In proceedings brought under authority of, or for the purpose of contesting the
validity of, a provision of this Chapter, or of an oil or gas conservation law of this state, or
of a rule, regulation, or order issued thereunder, appeals may be taken in accordance with the
general laws relating to appeals. In appeals from judgments or decrees in suits to contest the
validity of a provision of this Chapter, or a rule or regulation of the secretary hereunder, the
appeals when docketed in the proper appellate court shall be placed on the preference docket
of the court and may be advanced as the court directs.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
