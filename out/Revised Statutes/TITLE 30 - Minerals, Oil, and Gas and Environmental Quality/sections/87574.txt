§45.  Secretary to enforce Part
The secretary shall see that the provisions of this Part are fully and properly complied
with and the district attorney in whose district a violation takes place shall, on application,
bring suit if necessary to enforce the provisions of this Part. Any injunction which may be
necessary shall be furnished without bond.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
