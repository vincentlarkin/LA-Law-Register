§2496.  Exclusive authority

The provisions of this Chapter shall be the exclusive authority on oil spill prevention, response, removal, and the limitations of liability.

Acts 1991, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §1, eff. April 23, 1991; Acts 1995, No. 740, §1.
