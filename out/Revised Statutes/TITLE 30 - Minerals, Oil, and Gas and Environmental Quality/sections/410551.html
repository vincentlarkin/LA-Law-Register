§2588.  Enforcement

A violation of any of the provisions of this Chapter or of any rule or regulation promulgated pursuant thereto shall be punishable as provided in R.S. 30:2025 and any other law providing for the protection of human health and the environment.

Acts 2006, No. 126, §1, eff. June 2, 2006.
