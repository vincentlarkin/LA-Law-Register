§1353.  Purpose

The purpose of this Chapter is to provide financial assistance to eligible governmental units which must expand the availability of public facilities and services as a consequence of large scale lignite resource development.  

Added by Acts 1981, No. 824, §1, eff. Aug. 2, 1981.
