§101.8. Liability

            The secretary shall not be liable for any damages arising from an act or omission if the act or omission is part of a good faith effort to carry out the purpose of this Part.

            Acts 1997, No. 666, §2; Acts 1997, No. 666, §2; Acts 2022, No. 100, §2, eff. July 1, 2023; Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.

NOTE: See R.S. 30:101.15 regarding the termination of Part after June 30, 2027.
