PART X.  REGULATION OF COMPRESSED NATURAL GAS
USED AS A VEHICULAR FUEL
§731.  Definitions
As used in this Part, the following words and phrases shall have the meanings
hereinafter ascribed to them:
(1)  "Compressed natural gas" means natural gas designated for vehicular use that is
under pressures exceeding twenty-four hundred pounds per square inch.
(2)  "Compression and conversion equipment" means all equipment used in the
compression, storage, transmission, and decompression of natural gas for the purpose of
powering motor vehicles.
(3)  " Secretary" means the secretary of the Department of Conservation and Energy.
Acts 1990, No. 927, §1; Acts 2023, No. 150, §5, eff. Jan. 10, 2024; Acts 2025, No.
