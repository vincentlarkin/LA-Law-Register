PART IV.  OIL SPILL PREVENTION AND RESPONSE
§2461.  Coordinator; notification
On notification of an actual or threatened unauthorized discharge of oil, the coordinator shall take immediate action to assess the discharge and prevent, abate, or contain any pollution from the discharge.
Acts 1991, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §1, eff. April 23, 1991.
