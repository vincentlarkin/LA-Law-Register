§1173.  Notice; manner of delivery
For any written notice required under this Chapter, the parties to a renewable energy
lease may stipulate the conditions, manner, and method by which delivery of the written
notice to the lessee shall be made.
Acts 2023, No. 455, §1, eff. June 28, 2023.
