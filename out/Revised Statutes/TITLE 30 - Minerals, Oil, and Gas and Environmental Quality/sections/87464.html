§2477.  Audits, inspections, and drills

The coordinator, in conjunction with the United States Coast Guard, may subject a vessel covered by this Chapter as a condition to being granted entry into any port in this state, or a terminal facility to an announced or unannounced audit, inspection, or drill to determine the discharge prevention and response capabilities of the terminal facility or vessels.  

Acts 1991, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §1, eff. April 23, 1991.
