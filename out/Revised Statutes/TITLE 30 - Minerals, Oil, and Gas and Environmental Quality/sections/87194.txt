CHAPTER 8.  LOUISIANA SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT
AND RESOURCE RECOVERY LAW
§2151.  Citation
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Solid Waste Management and Resource Recovery Law."
Acts 1979, No. 449, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.
