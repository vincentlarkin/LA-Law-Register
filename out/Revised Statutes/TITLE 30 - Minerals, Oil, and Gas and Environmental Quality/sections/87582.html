CHAPTER 7.  NATURAL RESOURCES AND ENERGY ACT

OF 1973

PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§501.  Citation

The provisions of this Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the Natural Resources and Energy Act of 1973.

Added by Acts 1973, Ex.Sess., No. 16, §1, emerg.  eff. Dec. 8, 1973, at 9:55 A.M.
