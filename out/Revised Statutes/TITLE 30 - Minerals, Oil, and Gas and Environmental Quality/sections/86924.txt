§1153.  Technical assessment studies
The secretary shall carry out technical assessment studies on all forms of solar energy
and energy-related problems in order to influence federal research and development priorities
and to be informed on future energy options and their impacts.
Acts 1983, No. 705, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1983; Acts 2023, No. 455, §2, eff. June 28,
2023.
