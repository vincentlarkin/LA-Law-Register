§98.9.  Cooperation with other agencies
The department is authorized to collaborate and enter into agreements with local,
state, federal, and private entities to facilitate efficient and effective water resource
management and conservation efforts.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
