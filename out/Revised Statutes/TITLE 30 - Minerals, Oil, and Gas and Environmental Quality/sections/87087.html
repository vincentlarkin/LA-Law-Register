§2050.13.  Hearing officers; employment

The secretary may employ one or more hearing officers to perform such duties relative to adjudications as the secretary assigns to them.  A hearing officer shall be a full-time employee in the classified service of the state.

Acts 1995, No. 947, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
