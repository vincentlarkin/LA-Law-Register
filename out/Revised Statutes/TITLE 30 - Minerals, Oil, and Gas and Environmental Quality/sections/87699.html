§807.  Regulations of board

The mineral board is hereby authorized to incorporate such additional customary provisions in a state geothermal lease and to adopt such additional regulations governing the administration and management of such leases that are not inconsistent with the provisions of this Chapter.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 784, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 134, §1.
