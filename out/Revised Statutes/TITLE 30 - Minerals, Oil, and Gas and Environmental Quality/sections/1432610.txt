§98.8.  Immunity from liability
The department and its officers, employees, agents, and designated representatives
acting in the discharge of their duties under this Chapter shall be immune from civil liability
for actions taken in good faith in compliance with established rules and regulations.
Acts 2025, No. 458, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
