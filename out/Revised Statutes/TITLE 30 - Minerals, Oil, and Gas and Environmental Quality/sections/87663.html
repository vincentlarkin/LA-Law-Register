§681.4.  Participation by state, federal and private agencies; funds

A.  The department is authorized to utilize the expertise, knowledge, equipment, and personnel available from other state agencies, from the federal government, or from the private sector in carrying out its responsibilities under this Subpart, either from a voluntary standpoint or under contract from funds made available therefor, and to that extent the commissioner is authorized to enter into contracts for services to be provided in connection with the goals of this program.

B.  In addition to funds made available by the legislature the department is authorized to accept gifts, grants, and donations from whatever source available.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 735, §1.
