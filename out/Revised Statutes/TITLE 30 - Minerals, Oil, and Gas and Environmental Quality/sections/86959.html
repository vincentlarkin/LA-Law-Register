CHAPTER 15.  ENERGY IMPACTED AREA ASSISTANCE

ACT OF 1981

§1351.  Short title

This Chapter may be cited as the Energy Impacted Area Assistance Act of 1981.  

Added by Acts 1981, No. 824, §1, eff. Aug. 2, 1981.
