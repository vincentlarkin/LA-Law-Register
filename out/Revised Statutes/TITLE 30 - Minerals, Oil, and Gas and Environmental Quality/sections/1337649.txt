§1162.  Preservation of rights
The owner of land burdened by a renewable energy lease and the lessee of a
renewable energy lease shall exercise their respective rights with reasonable regard for those
of the other.  Subject to the laws of registry, the lessee of a renewable energy lease shall not
unreasonably interfere with the rights of others lawfully exercising their rights in the land.
Acts 2023, No. 455, §1, eff. June 28, 2023.
