§2243.  Rate

The one time tax levied in this Chapter shall be levied upon the hazardous waste content of land in Louisiana at the rate of two dollars a dry weight ton of hazardous waste content.  The one time tax shall be determined and shall be due at the time and in the manner provided in this Chapter.  

Acts 1984, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 8, §1, eff. March 27, 1984.
