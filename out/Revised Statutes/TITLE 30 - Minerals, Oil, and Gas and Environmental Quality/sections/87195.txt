§2152.  Policy; purpose
The legislature finds and declares that the disposal and utilization of solid waste is a matter of vital concern to all citizens of this state, and that the safety and welfare of the people of Louisiana require efficient and reasonable regulation of solid waste disposal practices as well as a coordinated statewide resource recovery and management program.
Acts 1979, No. 449, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.
