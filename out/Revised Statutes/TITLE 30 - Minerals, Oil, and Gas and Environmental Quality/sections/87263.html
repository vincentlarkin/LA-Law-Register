CHAPTER 10.  INACTIVE AND ABANDONED

HAZARDOUS WASTE SITES

§2221.  Citation

This Chapter, consisting of Sections 2221 through 2226 of Title 30, may be cited as the "Louisiana Inactive and Abandoned Hazardous Waste Site Law."

Added by Acts 1983, No. 547, §2, eff. July 14, 1983.
