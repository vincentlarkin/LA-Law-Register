§2482.  Claims against third parties
If a responsible person alleges a defense under R.S. 30:2481 the responsible person shall pay all removal costs and damages; however, the responsible person shall be subrogated to any rights or cause of action belonging to those to whom such payment is made.
Acts 1991, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §1, eff. April 23, 1991.
