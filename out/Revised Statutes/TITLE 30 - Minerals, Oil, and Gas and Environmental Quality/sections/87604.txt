§550.  Compilation and publication of information by commissioner
The commissioner shall be empowered to gather, analyze, maintain, and publish information on intrastate natural gas pipelines and producers, transporters, distributors, and users of intrastate natural gas and may serve as a clearinghouse to provide information to users of natural gas regarding the availability of supplies of natural gas.
Added by Acts 1973, Ex.Sess., No. 16, §1, emerg. eff. Dec. 8, 1973, at 9:55 A.M.  Amended by Acts 1979, No. 539, §1.
