§1114. Groundwater quality monitoring

            The owner or operator of a storage facility shall conduct periodic testing and monitoring of ground water quality above the confining zone and shall report the testing and monitoring conducted to the commissioner semiannually.

            Acts 2024, No. 702, §1, eff. June 19, 2024.
