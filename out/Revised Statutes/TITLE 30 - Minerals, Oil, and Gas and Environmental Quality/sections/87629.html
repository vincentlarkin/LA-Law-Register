PART V.  NATURAL GAS PRICE REGULATION

§591.  Definition

As used in this Part, unless otherwise clearly indicated, the following term has the meaning ascribed to it below:

(1)  "Natural gas company" means a person engaged in the production or sale of intrastate natural gas.

Added by Acts 1973, Ex.Sess., No. 16, §1, emerg. eff. Dec. 8, 1973 at 9:55 A.M.
