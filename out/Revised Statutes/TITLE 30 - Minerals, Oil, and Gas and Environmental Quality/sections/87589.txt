§523.  Transfer of records, money and equipment
All books, paper, records, money, choses in action and other property of every kind and description, movable and immovable, real and personal, heretofore possessed, controlled or used by any of the agencies affected by this Part, in the exercise of functions hereby transferred, are hereby transferred to the State Department of Conservation.
Added by Acts 1973, Ex.Sess., No. 16, §1, emerg. eff. Dec. 8, 1973, at 9:55 A.M.
