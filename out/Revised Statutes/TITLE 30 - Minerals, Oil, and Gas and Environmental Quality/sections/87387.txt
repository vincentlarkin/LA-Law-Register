§2351.56.  Relationship to federal law
A.  Regulations promulgated pursuant to this Chapter shall be no less stringent than any minimum standards established under federal law or regulations.
B.  If a provision of this Chapter conflicts with a federal law pertaining to lead hazard reduction activities, the provision shall not apply to the extent that it is preempted by the federal law.
Acts 1993, No. 224, §1.
