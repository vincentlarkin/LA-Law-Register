§2381.9. Data communication
Any release or communication of the collected monitoring data shall include clear
explanations of data interpretation, appropriate context, including the applicable or
comparable ambient air standard data limitations, and relevant uncertainties.
Acts 2024, No. 181, §1, eff. May 23, 2024.
