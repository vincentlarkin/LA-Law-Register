§1175.  Dissolution may be partial or entire
A renewable energy lease may be dissolved partially or in its entirety.  A decree of
partial dissolution may be made as to those portions of the land subject to a renewable energy
lease on which construction of the renewable energy facility has not commenced within ten
years of the effective date of the lease, or in any other fashion which may provide appropriate
justice in the judgment of the court.
Acts 2023, No. 455, §1, eff. June 28, 2023.
