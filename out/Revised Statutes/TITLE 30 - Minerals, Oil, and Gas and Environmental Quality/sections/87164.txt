§21.2.  Bohemia Spillway Cost Recovery
Upon the final disposition of each claim filed with the Department of Conservation
and Energy pursuant to Act No. 233 of the 1984 Regular Session of the Legislature, the
secretary shall condemn one or more of the parties to the claim to pay the actual cost of
administering the claim and may apportion the cost among the parties. The funds received
pursuant to this Section shall be deposited immediately into the state treasury.
Acts 1987, No. 644, §1, eff. July 9, 1987; Acts 1992, No. 984, §§9, 18; Acts 2023,
No. 150, §5, eff. Jan. 10, 2024.
