§83.  Creation of mineral royalty by conditional owner

A mineral royalty may be created by one whose title terminates at a particular time or upon the occurrence of a certain condition, but it is extinguished at the specified time or on occurrence of the condition divesting the title.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §83, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
