§60.  Suspension effective as to all minerals
An obstacle to drilling or mining operations or to production of any mineral covered by an act creating a mineral servitude suspends the running of prescription as to all minerals covered by the act.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §60, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
