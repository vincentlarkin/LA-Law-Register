PART 6.  CONVENTIONAL ALTERATION OF THE

LEGAL INCIDENTS OF CREATION OF A MINERAL SERVITUDE

§72.  Parties free to contract except as specifically limited

Parties to an act creating a mineral servitude may alter the applicable legal rules subject to the limitations provided in Articles 73 through 79.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §72, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
