§212.1.  Sales relative to minerals after they are severed are subject to the laws of registry

Contracts by a landowner or owner of a mineral right disposing of minerals after they are severed, if filed for registry in the conveyance records at the situs of the land or mineral right from which produced, are subject to the laws of registry.

Added by Acts 1979, No. 269, §2.  Acts 1993, No. 948, §5, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
