§102.  Rule regarding effect of partial conveyance; applicability to mineral royalty
Article 69 is applicable as between the owner of land or of a mineral servitude and the owner of a mineral royalty burdening either.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §102, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
