§112.  Right of nonexecutive to operate

When the executive right is separated from a mineral servitude or ownership of the land, the land or servitude owner has the right, with the consent of the owner of the executive right, to conduct drilling or mining operations on the land.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §112, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
