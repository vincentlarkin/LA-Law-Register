§186.  Owner of right derived from all co-owners of land unaffected by licitation
If the rights of an owner of a mineral right or rights or interest or interests therein derive from all of the co-owners of the land, whether by single or separate acts, his rights are unaffected by the licitation, and he has no interest in the proceeds of the sale.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §186, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
