§113.  Executive right as appendage of another mineral right

When a mineral right is created and is accompanied by an executive right on the subject land or mineral right, the executive right is an appendage of the mineral right that it accompanies and is extinguished with it.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §113, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
