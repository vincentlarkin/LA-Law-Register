§184.  Effect of failure to appraise mineral right or interest therein
If an appraisal of any mineral right or interest therein is not made as herein required, the partition is not invalid, but the right or any interest therein is not extinguished or otherwise affected.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §184, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
