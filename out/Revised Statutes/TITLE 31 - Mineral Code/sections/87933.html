§25.  Right of conditional landowner to create mineral servitude

A mineral servitude may be created by a landowner whose title terminates at a particular time or upon the occurrence of a certain condition but it is extinguished at the specified time or on occurrence of the condition divesting the title.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
