PART 2.  WHO MAY GRANT MINERAL LEASES

§116.  Who may grant a mineral lease

A mineral lease may be granted by a person having an executive interest in the mineral rights on the property leased.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §116, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
