SUBPART C.  PARTITION OF MINERAL RIGHTS WHICH ARE THE
SUBJECT OF CO-OWNERSHIP
§172.  Mineral servitudes and royalties subject to partition
Mineral servitudes and royalties are subject to partition.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §172, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
