§163.  Acquisitive prescription; unavailability to mineral servitude owner

The owner of a mineral servitude cannot by any form of possession perfect an acquisitive title against the owner of a mineral royalty burdening his servitude.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §163, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
