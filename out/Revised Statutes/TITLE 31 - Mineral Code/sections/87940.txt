§31.  Operations beyond prescriptive date; effect as interruption
Actual drilling or mining operations commenced within the prescriptive period interrupt prescription although the operations are not completed until after the date on which prescription would have accrued.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §31, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
