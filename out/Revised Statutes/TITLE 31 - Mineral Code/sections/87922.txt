PART 2-A.  PRODUCTION PAYMENTS AND ROYALTY
PAYMENTS TO OTHER THAN MINERAL LESSOR;
REMEDIES OF OBLIGEE
§212.21.  Nonpayment of production payment or royalties; notice prerequisite to judicial
demand
If the owner of a production payment created out of a mineral lessee's interest or a
royalty owner other than a mineral lessor seeks relief for the failure of a mineral lessee to
make timely or proper payment of royalties or the production payment, he must give his
obligor written notice of such failure as a prerequisite to a judicial demand for damages.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 249, §1; Acts 2020, No. 76, §1.
