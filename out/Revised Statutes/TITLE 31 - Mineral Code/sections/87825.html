PART 6.  TERMINATION AND REMEDIES FOR VIOLATION

§133.  Termination of mineral lease

A mineral lease terminates at the expiration of the agreed term or upon the occurrence of an express resolutory condition.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §133, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
