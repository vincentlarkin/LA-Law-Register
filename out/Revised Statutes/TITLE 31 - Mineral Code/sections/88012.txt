SUBPART D.  SUSPENSION OF PRESCRIPTION
§97.  Prescription not affected by minority or other disability
The prescription of nonuse is not suspended by the minority or other legal disability of the owner of a mineral royalty.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §97, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
