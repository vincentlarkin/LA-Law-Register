PART 6.  CONVENTIONAL ALTERATION OF THE

LEGAL INCIDENTS OF CREATION OF A MINERAL ROYALTY

§103.  Freedom of contract; application of rules to mineral royalty

Articles 72 through 75 regarding conventional alteration of the legal incidents of creation of mineral servitudes are applicable to mineral royalties.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §103, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
