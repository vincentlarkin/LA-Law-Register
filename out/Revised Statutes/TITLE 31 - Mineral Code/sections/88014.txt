§99.  Obstacle to production from servitude suspends prescription as to dependent royalty
An obstacle to the actual production of minerals that suspends the prescription of nonuse running against a mineral servitude burdened by a mineral royalty also suspends the prescription of nonuse running against the royalty.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §99, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
