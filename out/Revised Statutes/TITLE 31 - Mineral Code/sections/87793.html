PART 7.  TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING OUTSTANDING

MINERAL ROYALTIES

§104.  Rules applicable to transactions involving outstanding servitudes; applicability to mineral royalties

Articles 76 through 79 concerning transactions involving outstanding mineral servitudes are applicable to mineral royalties, including those created by a mineral servitude owner.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §104, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
