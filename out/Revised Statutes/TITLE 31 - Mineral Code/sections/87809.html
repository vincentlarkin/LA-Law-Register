PART 3.  THE OBLIGATIONS OF THE LESSOR

§119.  Obligations of lessor

A mineral lessor is bound to deliver the premises that he has leased for use by the lessee, to refrain from disturbing the lessee's possession, and to perform the contract in good faith.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §119, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
