§98.  Suspension of prescription by obstacle
An obstacle to actual production of minerals that would be sufficient to suspend the prescription of nonuse if the owner of a mineral royalty were the owner of a mineral servitude suspends the prescription of nonuse running against the royalty until the obstacle is removed.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §98, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
