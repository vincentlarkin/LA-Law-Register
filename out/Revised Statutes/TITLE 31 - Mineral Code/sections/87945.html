§36.  Production as interruption of prescription

Prescription of nonuse is interrupted by the production of any mineral covered by the act creating the servitude.  The interruption occurs on the date on which actual production begins and prescription commences anew from the date of cessation of actual production.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §36, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
