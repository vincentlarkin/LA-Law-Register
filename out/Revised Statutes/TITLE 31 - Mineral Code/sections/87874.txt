SUBPART D.  RIGHTS AND CONSEQUENCES ARISING
FROM CO-OWNERSHIP OF MINERAL RIGHTS
§174.  Use by one co-owner inures to benefit of all
A use or possession of a mineral right inures to the benefit of all co-owners of the right.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §174, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
