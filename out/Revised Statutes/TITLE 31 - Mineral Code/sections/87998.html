§84.  Creation of mineral royalty by usufructuary

A usufructuary of land whose usufruct includes mineral rights, or the usufructuary of a mineral servitude, may establish a mineral royalty for the period of his usufruct.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §84, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
