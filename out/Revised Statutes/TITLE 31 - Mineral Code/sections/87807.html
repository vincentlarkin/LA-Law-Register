§117.  Granting of mineral lease by owner under conditional title

A mineral lease may be granted by the owner of an executive interest whose title is extinguished at a particular time or upon the occurrence of a certain condition, but it terminates at the specified time or on occurrence of the condition divesting the title.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §117, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
