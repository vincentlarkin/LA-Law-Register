PART 4.  COMMENCEMENT OF PRESCRIPTION
SUBPART A.  GENERAL PRINCIPLES
§28.  Commencement of prescription of nonuse
Prescription of nonuse of a mineral servitude commences from the date on which it is created.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
