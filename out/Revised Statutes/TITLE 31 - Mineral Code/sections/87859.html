§160.  Perfection of title by prescription as including mineral rights

When title to land is perfected by a possessor on the basis of acquisitive prescription, the title includes mineral rights to the extent that his possession included mineral rights for the required prescriptive period.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §160, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
