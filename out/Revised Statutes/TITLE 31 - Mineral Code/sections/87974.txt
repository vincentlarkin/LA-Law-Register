PART 5.  INDIVISIBILITY OF THE MINERAL SERVITUDE
§62.  Mineral servitude indivisible except as specifically provided
Except as provided in Articles 63 through 71, the rights and obligations of the owner of a mineral servitude are indivisible.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §62, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
