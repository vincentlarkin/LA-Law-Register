§132.  Demands by lessor; effect on assignee or sublessee
An assignee or sublessee is bound by any notice or demand by the lessor on the lessee unless the lessor has been given written notice of the assignment or sublease and the assignment or sublease has been filed for registry.  If filing and notice have taken place, any subsequent notice or demand by the lessor must be made on the assignee or sublessee.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §132, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
