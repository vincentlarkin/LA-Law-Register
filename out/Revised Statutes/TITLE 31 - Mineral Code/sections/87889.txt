CHAPTER 11.  RIGHTS OF USUFRUCTUARIES IN MINERALS
§188.  Mineral rights not included in usufruct of land except as specifically provided
Except as specially provided in Articles 189 through 191, the usufruct of land does not include the landowner's rights in minerals.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §188, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
