§53.  Adoption possible only as specifically provided

Articles 44 through 52 provide the only means by which the prescription of nonuse may be interrupted by operations conducted by persons other than those designated in Article 42.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §53, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
