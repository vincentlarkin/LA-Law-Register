PART 7.  TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING OUTSTANDING
MINERAL SERVITUDES
§76.  Expectancy of extinction not an article of commerce
The expectancy of a landowner in the extinction of an outstanding mineral servitude cannot be conveyed or reserved directly or indirectly.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §76, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
