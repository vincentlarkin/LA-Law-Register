§47.  Compulsory unit operations; adoption unnecessary

When drilling or mining operations or actual production otherwise sufficient to interrupt prescription takes place on a compulsory unit including all or a part of the land burdened by a mineral servitude, an interruption of prescription takes place without formal adoption by the owner of the servitude.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §47, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
