§107.  Interruption of prescription accruing against executive right

The prescription of nonuse of an executive right existing independently is interrupted by an act or event that would be sufficient to interrupt prescription accruing against a mineral servitude.  

Acts 1974, No. 50, §107, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
