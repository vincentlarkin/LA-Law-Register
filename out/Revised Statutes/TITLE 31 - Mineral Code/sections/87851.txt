CHAPTER 9.  POSSESSION AND ACQUISITIVE PRESCRIPTION
PART 1.  POSSESSION OF MINERAL RIGHTS
§153.  How mineral rights are possessed
Mineral rights are possessed by their use or exercise according to their nature.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §153, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
