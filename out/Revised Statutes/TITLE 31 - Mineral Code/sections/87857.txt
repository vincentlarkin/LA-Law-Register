PART 4.  THE EFFECT OF ACQUISITIVE PRESCRIPTION
ON MINERAL RIGHTS
§159.  Mineral rights not established by acquisitive prescription
Mineral rights may not be established by acquisitive prescription.
Acts 1974, No. 50, §159, eff. Jan. 1, 1975.
