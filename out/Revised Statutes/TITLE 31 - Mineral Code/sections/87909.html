§204. Security interest in minerals and proceeds thereof

            The Uniform Commercial Code - Secured Transactions governs the manner of creation of security interests in minerals produced and the proceeds from their sale or other disposition, as well as the rights of the holders of these security interests against obligors and third persons.

            Acts 1974, No. 50, §204, eff. Jan. 1, 1975; Acts 1989, No. 137, §10, eff. Sept. 1, 1989; Acts 2023, No. 88, §1.
