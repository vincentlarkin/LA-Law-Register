§2205.  Nondiscrimination of policy

All organizations receiving grants under this Chapter shall provide the required transportation services without regard to the race, religion, sex, national ancestry, place of residence or income level of the persons needing the assistance.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 785, §1.
