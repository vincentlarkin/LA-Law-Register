§155. Incapacity required

           The Louisiana Department of Health shall require proof of either physical or mental incapacity prior to certification of any applicant who applies for public assistance under the provisions of R.S. 46:153 or R.S. 46:154.

           Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978.
