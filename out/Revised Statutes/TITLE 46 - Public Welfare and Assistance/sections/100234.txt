§1132.  Scholarships; amount; number
Each board desiring to award scholarships shall establish a policy setting out the maximum amount and number.  This policy may be amended by the board as necessary.  The number of scholarships granted and the amount of each scholarship granted pursuant to this Chapter shall not exceed the number and amount set forth in the policy.
Acts 1988, No. 54, §1.
