§1655.  Transfer of records, money and equipment

All books, papers, records, money, choses in action and other property heretofore used or possessed by any agency in the exercise of functions hereby transferred shall be transferred to the Louisiana Board of Institutions.  

Added by Acts 1960, No. 374, §5.
