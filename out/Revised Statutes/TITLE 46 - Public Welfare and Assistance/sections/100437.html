§1831.7. Amount of reparations award

            Awards payable under this Chapter shall not exceed sixty thousand dollars per occurrence, in the aggregate, for all claims arising out of the same crime. No survivor shall recover more than fifteen thousand dollars on behalf of each victim.

            Acts 2023, No. 451, §2.

NOTE: Pursuant to R.S. 46:1831.16, this Chapter will terminate Aug. 1, 2027.
