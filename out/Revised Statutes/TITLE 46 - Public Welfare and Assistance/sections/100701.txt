CHAPTER 39.  WOMEN'S POLICY
§2521.  Governor's office on women's policy
There is hereby created the office on women's policy within the office of the governor.  The office shall exercise the powers and duties hereinafter set forth or otherwise provided by law.  The office shall be administered by an executive director who shall be appointed by the governor, subject to confirmation by the Senate, to serve at his pleasure.  The executive director shall employ necessary staff to carry out the duties and functions of the office as provided in this Chapter or as otherwise provided by law.
Acts 1985, No. 772, §1, eff. July 19, 1985; Acts 2003, No. 668, §2, eff. June 27, 2003; Acts 2010, No. 774, §1, eff. June 30, 2010.
