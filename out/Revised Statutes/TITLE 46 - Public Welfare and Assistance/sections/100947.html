PART III.  DESTITUTE PERSONS OVER FIFTY WITH DISABILITIES

§541.  Destitute persons over fifty with disabilities

Parish governing authorities may provide annually in their budget for residents of their respective parishes, who are over fifty years of age, who have had a physical disability from birth, who have never been convicted of any offense in the courts of this state, and who are in destitute and necessitous circumstances.

Acts 2014, No. 811, §24, eff. June 23, 2014.
