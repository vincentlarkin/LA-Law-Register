§2201. Persons who are elderly and persons with disabilities; capital acquisitions; definitions

            As used in this Chapter, the terms "persons who are elderly", "persons with disabilities", and "capital acquisitions" shall be defined in accordance with the appropriate federal law and the regulations and definitions in effect thereunder.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 785, §1; Acts 2014, No. 811, §24, eff. June 23, 2014.
