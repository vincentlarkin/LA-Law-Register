§1255.  Funds
The Department of Public Safety and Corrections is hereby authorized to expend whatever funds that may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 118, §5.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1984, No. 567, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 1985, No. 965, §2; Acts 1988, No. 607, §2, eff. July 14, 1988.
