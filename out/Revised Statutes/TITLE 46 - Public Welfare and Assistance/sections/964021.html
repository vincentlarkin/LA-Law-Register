§1863. Immunity from liability

            A. A person providing services to a victim at a family justice center pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter shall not be liable for civil damages while acting in the official scope of his duties if the person, in good faith, makes a recommendation, gives an opinion, or releases or uses information for the purposes of protecting or providing services to the victim.

            B. This limitation of civil liability does not apply if the person acted with gross negligence or in bad faith.

            Acts 2015, No. 327, §1.
