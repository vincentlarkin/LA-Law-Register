§2414.  Compensation; support services
A.  Members of local citizen review boards shall receive compensation only for mileage costs and to the extent that the legislature appropriates funds for such costs.
B.  Secretarial and support services for each local citizen review board shall be provided by the staff of the state citizen review board.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 610, §1; eff. July 1, 1984.
