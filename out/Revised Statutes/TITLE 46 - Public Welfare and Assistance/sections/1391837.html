§1445.11. Legal representation in adoption proceedings

            Nothing in this Chapter shall be interpreted to violate the rights and protections afforded to a birth parent or an expectant parent that are enacted in the Children's Code, the Civil Code, or in any other provision of law.

            Acts 2024, No. 515, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2025.
