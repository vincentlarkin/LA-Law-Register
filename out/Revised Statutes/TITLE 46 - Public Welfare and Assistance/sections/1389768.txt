§1445.4.  Adoption Awareness Program created
The Adoption Awareness Program is hereby created in the Department of Children
and Family Services, and the department shall contract with eligible vendors or organizations
to provide the supportive services as described in R.S. 46:1445.5.
Acts 2024, No. 515, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2025.
