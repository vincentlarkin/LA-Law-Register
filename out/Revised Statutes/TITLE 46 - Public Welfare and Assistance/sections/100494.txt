§1941.3.  Definitions
Words and terms used in this Part shall mean the following:
(1)  Repealed by Acts 2004, No. 555, §2.
(2)  "Youth" means a person seventeen years of age or younger.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 300, §1; Acts 1990, No. 696, §1; Acts 2004, No. 555, §2.
