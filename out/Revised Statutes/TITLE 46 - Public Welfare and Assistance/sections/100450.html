CHAPTER 22. DIVISION OF YOUTH SERVICES

PART I. IN GENERAL

§1901. Definitions

            A. For purposes of this Chapter, "department" shall mean the Department of Public Safety and Corrections.

            B. When used in this Chapter, unless the context otherwise requires, the terms used herein shall have the meaning ascribed to them by the Children's Code.

            Added by Acts 1974, No. 558, §1, emerg. eff. July 12, 1974. Acts 1984, No. 567, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 1985, No. 965, §2; Acts 1988, No. 607, §2, eff. July 14, 1988; Acts 2021, No. 158, §6.
