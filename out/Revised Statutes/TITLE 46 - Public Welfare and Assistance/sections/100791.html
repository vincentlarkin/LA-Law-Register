§2703.  Sale of unsafe children's products prohibited

A commercial user shall not remanufacture, retrofit, sell, contract to sell or resell, lease, sublet, or otherwise place in the stream of commerce, on or after January 1, 2002, an unsafe children's product.

Acts 2001, No. 1164, §1.
