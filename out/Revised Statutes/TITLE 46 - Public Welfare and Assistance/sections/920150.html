§2147.  Meetings

A.  The commission shall hold public meetings quarterly except as otherwise provided by vote of the commission or by order of the chairman.

B.  A simple majority of the commission membership shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business.

C.  The commission may establish subcommittees within the commission and appoint members to those subcommittees, including persons outside of the commission membership, as it deems necessary and appropriate to accomplish its goals.

D.  The Department of Children and Family Services shall provide to the commission such clerical, administrative, and technical assistance and support as may be necessary to enable the commission to accomplish its goals.

Acts 2014, No. 663, §2.
