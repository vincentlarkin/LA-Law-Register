§2575.  Cooperation by other state entities
[Effective Date: Section terminated June 30, 1991.]
All departments, commissions, boards, agencies, officers, and
institutions of the state and all subdivisions thereof shall cooperate with the
task force in carrying out the powers and purposes of this Chapter.
Acts 1989, No. 294, §1, eff. June 27, 1989.
