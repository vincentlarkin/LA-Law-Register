§2605.6.  Children of Incarcerated Parents and Caregivers Fund
A.  There is hereby created in the state treasury as a special fund the Children of
Incarcerated Parents and Caregivers Fund, hereinafter referred to as the "fund".
B.(1)  The source of monies deposited into the fund shall be any monies appropriated
annually by the legislature, including federal funds; any public or private donations, gifts, or
grants from individuals, corporations, nonprofit organizations, or other business entities; and
any other monies that may be obtained or provided by law.
(2)  Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as monies in the state
general fund and interest earned on investment of monies in the fund shall be credited to the
state general fund.  Unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund at the end of the
fiscal year shall remain in the fund.
C.  Monies in the fund shall be used as directed by the CIP Council solely for the
purposes set forth in this Section and R.S. 46:2605.4 and 2605.5.
