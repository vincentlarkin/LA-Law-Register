CHAPTER 29. TRANSPORTATION AID FOR PERSONS WHO ARE ELDERLY AND PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES

§2200. Transportation assistance for persons who are elderly and persons with disabilities

            The Department of Transportation and Development may establish and administer a program to provide assistance to applicants eligible under the provisions of Section 16(b)(2) of the federal Urban Mass Transportation Act of 1964, as amended (49 U.S.C. 1612(b)(2)).

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 785, §1; Acts 2014, No. 811, §24, eff. June 23, 2014; Acts 2018, No. 206, §5.
