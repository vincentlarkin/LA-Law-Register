PART II.  THE LOUISIANA CHILDREN AND YOUTH
HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM
§977.1.  Short title
This Part may be cited as the "Louisiana Children and Youth Health Insurance Act" and may be referred to as the "Louisiana Children and Youth Health Insurance Program".
Acts 2007, No. 407, §1, eff. July 10, 2007.
