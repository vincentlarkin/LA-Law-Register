§1831.16.  Termination of fund
Unless otherwise extended, the provisions of this Chapter shall be null, void, and
without effect and shall cease to exist on August 1, 2027.
Acts 2023, No. 451, §2.
NOTE:  Pursuant to R.S. 46:1831.16, this Chapter will terminate Aug. 1, 2027.
