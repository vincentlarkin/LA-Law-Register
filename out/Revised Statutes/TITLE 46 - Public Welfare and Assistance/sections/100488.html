PART III.  CHILDREN AND YOUTH PLANNING BOARDS

§1941.1.  Short title

The provisions of this Part shall be known as the "Children and Youth Planning Boards Act".

Added by Acts 1978, No. 300, §1; Acts 1990, No. 696, §1; Acts 2004, No. 555, §1.
