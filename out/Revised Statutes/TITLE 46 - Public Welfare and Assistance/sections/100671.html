§2412.  Local foster care review boards; creation; appointment

In each judicial district there shall be created at least one local citizen review board for every one hundred children referred to in R.S. 46:2417.  Each such local citizen review board shall be composed of five members appointed by the court.  The court may authorize the creation of additional local citizen review boards as needed.  

Added by Acts 1983, No. 610, §1; eff. July 1, 1984.
