§51.3.  Criminal history information; access to federal tax information
The Department of Children and Family Services, office of child support enforcement
and family support, is authorized to perform criminal history records checks of current and
prospective employees, contractors, and subcontractors in accordance with the procedures
provided in R.S. 15:587.5.  Pursuant to this authorization and to implement the requirements
of R.S. 15:587.5, the secretary shall promulgate rules and regulations with regard to this
matter.
