§1403.1. Extended stay for completion of educational courses or other programs

            Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a child housed at a residential home, transitional placing program, or in foster care may stay at such home, transitional placing program, or in foster care until his twenty-first birthday while participating in the extended foster care program set forth in R.S. 46:288.1 et seq., or any other program offered by the residential home.

            Acts 2001, No. 912, §1; Acts 2016, No. 502, §1; Acts 2017, No. 376, §2; Acts 2018, No. 206, §5; Acts 2018, No. 649, §1, eff. June 1, 2018; Acts 2019, No. 400, §2, eff. June 19, 2019.
