CHAPTER 16.  PARISH VOLUNTARY COUNCILS ON THE AGING

§1601.  Authorization

In each parish a voluntary council on the aging may be organized and chartered to function within the parish in accordance with the provisions of this Chapter.  Each such council shall be known and designated as "The (Name of Parish)   Voluntary Council on the Aging."

Added by Acts 1958, No. 342, §1.  Amended Acts 1964, No. 456, §1.
