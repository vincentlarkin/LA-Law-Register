CHAPTER 9-A.  VOCATIONAL COUNSELING FOR FOSTER CHILDREN

§1001.  Legislative findings

The legislature finds that children who are emancipated from or aged out of long-term foster care in this state must be better equipped with the necessary skills to find employment and to become self-supporting and productive.

Acts 2005, No. 82, §1.
