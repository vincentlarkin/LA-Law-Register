§2255.  Construction
Nothing in this Act shall be construed to prohibit or alter any program, service, facility, school, or privilege which is afforded, oriented, or restricted to a person because of his disability, from continuing to habilitate, rehabilitate, or accommodate that person.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 665, §1; Acts 2014, No. 811, §24, eff. June 23, 2014.
