§1111.  Suit for default

Upon default of payment of the scholarship as provided herein the board shall turn the matter over to the attorney general or any parish or district attorney acting for the board for prosecution and suit for the amount due, in the parish of the domicile of the recipient, the parish in which the medical school at which the recipient was last enrolled is located, or the parish of East Baton Rouge.  

Added by Acts 1976, No. 130, §1.
