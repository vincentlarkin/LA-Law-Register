§1256.  Additional personnel

Nothing in this Chapter shall be construed as preventing a court or district attorney from employing personnel in addition to that furnished by the Department of Public Safety and Corrections.  

Added by Acts 1952, No. 118, §6.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1982, No. 541, §1; Acts 1984, No. 567, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 1985, No. 965, §2; Acts 1988, No. 607, §2, eff. July 14, 1988.
