§1098.17.  Liberal construction
This Part, being intended to carry out a function of the state to protect the health and
welfare of the inhabitants of the state to be affected thereby, shall be liberally construed by
the courts to effect its purposes.
Acts 2020, No. 365, §1, eff. June 12, 2020.
