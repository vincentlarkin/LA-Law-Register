§1445.13. Adoption awareness; Department of Children and Family Services
A link to the AdoptionOption.La.Gov website as provided for in R.S. 40:1061.17, or
any successor state agency administered website that promotes adoption awareness and
makes adoption resource information available to the public, shall be made available and
placed in a prominent location on the websites administered by the Louisiana Department
of Health and the Department of Children and Family Services. Any other state agency that
administers a website may also provide a link to the AdoptionOption.La.Gov website.
Acts 2024, No. 515, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2025.
