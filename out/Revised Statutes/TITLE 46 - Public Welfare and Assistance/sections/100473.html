§1921.  Receipt of federal funds

The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall, where consistent with the requirements of federal laws and regulations, enter into arrangements with the federal government for the receipt of federal funds to carry out the purposes of this Chapter.  For the achievement of that objective, the department may enter into contracts and agreements with and submit such plans and reports to the federal government as may be required.

Added by Acts 1974, No. 558, §1, emerg. eff. July 12, 1974.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1984, No. 567, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Acts 1985, No. 965, §2; Acts 1988, No. 607, §2, eff. July 14, 1988.
