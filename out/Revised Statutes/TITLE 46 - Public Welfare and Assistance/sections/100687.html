§2428.  Application; appropriations

The provisions of this Chapter relative to the establishment and operation of the local and state citizen review boards shall become effective at such time as funds are appropriated by the legislature and become available for the application of the provisions of this Chapter.  

Acts 1986, No. 79, §1.
