§356. Rulemaking

            The secretary of Louisiana Works, the secretary of the Department of Children and Family Services, the secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health, the state superintendent of education, and the president of the Louisiana Community and Technical College System shall promulgate all rules in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act necessary to implement the provisions of this Part.

            Acts 2018, No. 271, §1; Acts 2022, No. 506, §1, eff. June 16, 2022.
