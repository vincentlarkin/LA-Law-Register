§57.  Other records to be public records

The financial and all other records of the state department and the parish and district offices, except those pertaining to individual recipients of assistance, as provided for in R.S. 46:56, are specifically made subject to the provisions of Title 44.  

Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978.  Amended by Acts 1979, No. 491, §1.
