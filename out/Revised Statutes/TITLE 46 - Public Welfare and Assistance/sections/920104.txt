CHAPTER 8-B.  LOUISIANA FIRST AMERICA NEXT
FREEDOM AND EMPOWERMENT PLAN
§979.1.  Title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana First America Next Freedom and Empowerment Act".
Acts 2014, No. 783, §1, eff. June 19, 2014.
