                        CHAPTER 21-A. VICTIMS OF VEHICULAR HOMICIDE

§1831.1. Short title

            This Chapter may be cited as the "Victims of Vehicular Homicide Act".

            Acts 2023, No. 451, §2.

NOTE: Pursuant to R.S. 46:1831.16, this Chapter will terminate Aug. 1, 2027.
