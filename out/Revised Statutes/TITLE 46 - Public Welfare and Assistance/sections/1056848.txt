§460.86.  Independent reviewers
Each managed care organization shall utilize only independent reviewers who are
selected in accordance with R.S. 46:460.85, and shall comply with the provisions of this
Subpart in the resolution of disputed adverse determinations.
Acts 2017, No. 349, §2.
