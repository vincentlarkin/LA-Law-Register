§1937.  Authority to accept allocated funds
For the purpose of establishing, improving, operating or maintaining the home, the board of governors shall have authority to accept available, federal and/or state funds allocated for that purpose.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 700, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 426, §1.
