PART II.  MULTIPARISH JUVENILE DETENTION

HOME DISTRICTS

§1931.  Creation of multiparish juvenile detention homes

The governing authority of one or more parishes may create a multiparish juvenile detention home district whenever, by joint resolution, they shall deem it in the best interest of the parish or parishes involved.  

Added by Acts 1974, No. 700, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 426, §1.
