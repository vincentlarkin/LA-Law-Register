CHAPTER 14-D.  ACCESS TO CHILD CARE FOR HOMELESS FAMILIES
§1443.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Improving Access to Child Care for Homeless Louisiana Families Act".
Acts 2014, No. 787, §2, eff. June 19, 2014.
