§114.1.  Public Assistance Fraud Hot-Line
To encourage the reporting of incidents of public assistance fraud, the Louisiana
Department of Health shall provide a statewide, toll-free telephone service, known as the
Public Assistance Fraud Hot-Line, when sufficient funds are made available by the
legislature for such purpose, or when funds are available from any other source.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 376, §1.  Amended by Acts 1979, No. 769, §5; Acts 1988,
No. 427, §1, eff. July 9, 1988; Acts 1990, No. 744, §2, eff.  July 24, 1990; Acts 2025, No.
478, §12, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
