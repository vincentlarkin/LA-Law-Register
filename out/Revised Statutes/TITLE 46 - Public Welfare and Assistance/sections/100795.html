§2707.  Revocation or refusal to renew a license or certificate of registration

The department may revoke or refuse to renew the license or certificate of registration of a child care facility or refuse to issue a license or certificate of registration if the licensee, registrant, or applicant does not comply with the provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 2001, No. 1164, §1.
