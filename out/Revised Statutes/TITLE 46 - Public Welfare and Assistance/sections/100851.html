§435.  Costs

The entire cost of the proceedings herein authorized shall not exceed (a) in uncontested cases the sum of $10.00, exclusive of the fee of the special counsel who may be appointed and of the court reporter; (b) and in contested cases shall not exceed $10.00, exclusive of the fee of the special counsel who may be appointed and of the court reporter.  

Added by Acts 1950, No. 166, §1.  Amended by Acts 1956, No. 177, §1; Acts 1960, No. 61, §1; Acts 1961, No. 15 §1.
