§16.  Sale or lease of state-owned facilities; procedure
In the event that any state facility owned or operated by the Department
of Children and Family Services, the Louisiana Department of Health, or the
Department of Public Safety and Corrections should permanently cease
operations or should permanently vacate the premises of one facility and move
to the premises of another facility, the secretary of the appropriate department
is hereby authorized to sell or lease to the highest bidder the property,
buildings, and all other appurtenances of the state-owned facility which has
been permanently closed or vacated.  The respective secretary shall, when such
facilities are offered for sale or lease, follow those procedures set forth in R.S.
41:131 et seq., relative to the sale of public land or R.S. 41:1211 et seq.,
relative to the lease of public land; provided however, the Joint Natural
Resources Oversight Committee approves the proposed sale or lease.
Acts 1986, No. 859, §1; H.C.R. No. 59, 1999 R.S.
