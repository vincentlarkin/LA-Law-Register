§1922.  Making and maintaining written records
The department shall cause to be made and maintained full and complete written records of all studies and examinations and of the conclusions and recommendations based thereon, and of all major decisions and orders concerning the disposition and treatment of every child with respect to whom the department provides, or arranges to have provided, care, treatment, and supervision pursuant to this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 558, §1, emerg. eff. July 12, 1974.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978.
