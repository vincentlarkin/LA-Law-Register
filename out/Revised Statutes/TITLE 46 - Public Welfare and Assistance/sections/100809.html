§2743.  Specialty services; exempted

The various specialty services in the state which provide skilled programs for infectious diseases, neuro-rehabilitation treatment, and technology-dependent care shall be excluded from the provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 2001, No. 694, §1, eff. July 1, 2001.
