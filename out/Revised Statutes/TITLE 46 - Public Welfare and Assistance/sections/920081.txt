§325.  Duties of customers
Each customer in the program shall do all of the following:
(1)  At the time of redetermination of eligibility for SNAP benefits, provide to the
department all requested documentation of participation in a workforce training or education
program.
(2)  Comply with all other requirements for receipt of SNAP benefits as set forth by
the department.
Acts 2014, No. 622, §1; Acts 2025, No. 478, §12, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
