§1819.  Penalty for fraud
No person shall procure or counsel another person to procure reparations under the provisions of this Chapter by any fraud.  The penalty for the violation of the provisions of this Section shall be a fine of not more than five hundred dollars or imprisonment for not more than one year, or both.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 250, §1, eff. July 17, 1982.
