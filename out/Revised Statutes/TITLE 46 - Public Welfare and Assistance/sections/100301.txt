§1416.  Special, temporary, or continued care for children
Any institution, society, agency, corporation, facility, person or persons, or any other group licensed in accordance with the provisions of this Chapter may receive children from their parents or legal guardians or from the courts for special, temporary, or continued care.
Acts 1985, No. 286, §1.
