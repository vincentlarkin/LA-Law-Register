§1603.  Officers

Each parish voluntary council on the aging shall meet within thirty days after the effective date of its charter and shall elect from its membership a chairman and such other officers as it deems necessary.  The terms for which officers shall serve shall be determined by the council.  

Added by Acts 1958, No. 342, §3.  Amended by Acts 1964, No. 456, §1; Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1981, No. 867, §1.
