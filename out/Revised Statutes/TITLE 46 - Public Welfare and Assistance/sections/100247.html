§1140. Repayment not required

            Should full-time positions not be available within the hospital service district and the hospital be unable to offer the scholarship recipient full-time employment within twelve months of completion of his training, the scholarship shall be considered paid in full. No payment on the principal of the scholarship, or the interest, shall be required while the recipient is enrolled in nursing or allied health school.

            Acts 1988, No. 54, §1; Acts 2023, No. 33, §1, eff. June 1, 2023.
