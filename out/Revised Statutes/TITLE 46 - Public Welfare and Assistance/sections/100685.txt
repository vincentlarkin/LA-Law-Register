§2426.  Operational costs for citizen review boards
A.  Operational costs for the local and state citizen review board may be provided by the state legislature.
B.  The state citizen review board may receive additional state or federal funds for the board's activities.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 610, §1; eff. July 1, 1984.
