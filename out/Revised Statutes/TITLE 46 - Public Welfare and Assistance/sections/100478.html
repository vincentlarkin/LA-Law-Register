§193.  Utility relief

Each residential consumer shall receive utility relief in accordance with the provisions of Title III of P.L. 96-223, the "Home Energy Assistance Act of 1980."1  The secretary of the department responsible for implementation of Title III of P.L. 96-223, as designated by the governor, shall have authority to promulgate rules and regulations and take such actions as may be necessary to implement the provisions of this Section.  

Acts 1980, No. 712, §1.

142 U.S.C.A. §8601 et seq.
