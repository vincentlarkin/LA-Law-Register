§1831.14.  Penalty for fraud
No person shall procure or counsel another person to procure reparations pursuant
to the provisions of this Chapter by any fraud.  The penalty for the violation of the provisions
of this Section shall be a fine of not more than five hundred dollars or imprisonment for not
more than one year, or both.
Acts 2023, No. 451, §2.
NOTE:  Pursuant to R.S. 46:1831.16, this Chapter will terminate Aug. 1, 2027.
