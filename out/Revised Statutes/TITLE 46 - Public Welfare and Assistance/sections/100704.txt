§2524.  Budget review
The budget of the governor's office on women's policy shall be submitted to the governor by the director for approval by the division of administration and the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget.
Acts 1985, No. 772, §1, eff. July 19, 1985; Acts 2003, No. 668, §2, eff. June 27, 2003.
