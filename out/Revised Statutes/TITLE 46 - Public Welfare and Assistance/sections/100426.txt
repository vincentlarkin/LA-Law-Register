§1812.  Finality of decision
A decision or order of the board with respect to any application or claim for reparations shall be subject to review in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 13 of Title 49 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 250, §1, eff. July 17, 1982.
