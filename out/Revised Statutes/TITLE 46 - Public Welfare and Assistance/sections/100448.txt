§1843.  Eligibility of victims
A victim has the rights and is eligible for the services under this Chapter regardless
of when the victim reported the crime to law enforcement authorities.
Acts 1985, No. 794, §1; Acts 2017, No. 337, §1; Acts 2022, No. 207, §1; Acts 2022,
No. 411, §1.
