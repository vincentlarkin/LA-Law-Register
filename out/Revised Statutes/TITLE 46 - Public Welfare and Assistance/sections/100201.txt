§1069.  Hospital service district hospitals; contracts for materials, services, or supplies
Hospital service district hospitals may contract for materials, services, or supplies under a shared service or group purchasing arrangement with other hospitals, public and/or private, subject to the provisions of R.S. 38:2212.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 426, §1.
