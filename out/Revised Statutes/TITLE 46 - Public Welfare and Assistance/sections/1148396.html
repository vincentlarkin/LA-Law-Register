§288.7. Internal administrative reviews

            The department shall conduct internal administrative reviews not less than once every one hundred eighty days to determine the status of the following:

            (1) The youth's safety.

            (2) Continuing necessity and appropriateness of placement.

            (3) Extent of compliance with the case plan.

            (4) Projected date when the youth may no longer require extended foster care services.

            Acts 2019, No. 400, §2, eff. June 19, 2019.
