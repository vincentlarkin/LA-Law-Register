§509.  Selection of charitable association for disbursement of funds

The governing body of the parish or the city council of the city, acting separately or together, may in their discretion, select one or more duly incorporated and well recognized charitable associations, which must be non-sectarian, to disburse the revenues arising from the respective alms funds of the parish and city.
