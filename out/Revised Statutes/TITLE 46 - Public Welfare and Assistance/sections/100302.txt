§1417.  Inspections
It shall be the duty of the department, through its duly authorized agents, to inspect
at regular intervals not to exceed one year, or as deemed necessary by the department, and
without previous notice all specialized providers subject to the provisions of this Chapter.
The department shall also develop and facilitate coordination with and among other
authorized agencies making inspections at regular intervals.  A specialized provider shall be
open to inspection only during working hours by parents or legal guardians of children or
youth in care and by authorized inspection personnel.
