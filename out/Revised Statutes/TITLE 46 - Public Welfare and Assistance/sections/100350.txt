§153.1.  Appropriation to state agencies
Any state agency may, with the approval of the division of
administration, transfer to the Louisiana Department of Health any portion of
its appropriation that may be available for federal matching funds in
accordance with the medical assistance program established by the department.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 248, §1, emerg. eff. July 7, 1970, at 1:00 P.M.
Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §6, eff. July 17, 1978.
