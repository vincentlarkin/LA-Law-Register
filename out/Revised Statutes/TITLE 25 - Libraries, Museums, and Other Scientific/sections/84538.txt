§709.  Calendar of events
The Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism shall compile and periodically distribute a calendar of events for historic and cultural activities within the region.  When the department compiles and distributes calendars of events for the entire state, it shall separate those within the region from the others and list those under the heading, "Republic of West Florida Historic Region".
Acts 1990, No. 1021, §1.
