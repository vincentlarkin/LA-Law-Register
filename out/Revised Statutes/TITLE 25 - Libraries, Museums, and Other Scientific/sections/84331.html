CHAPTER 28.  EDDIE G. ROBINSON

MUSEUM COMMISSION

§1241.  Legislative intent; scope

A.  The legislature hereby recognizes that it is appropriate to establish a museum at Grambling State University to honor the legendary football coach, Eddie G. Robinson.

B.  The purpose of such museum shall be to collect, preserve, and exhibit, as an educational resource, objects of art, documents, archives, and other artifacts that reflect the history of the life and coaching domain of Eddie G. Robinson.

Acts 1999, No. 1368, §1.
