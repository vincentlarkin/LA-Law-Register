§262.  Certification by judge of books to be purchased or subscribed for
The judge or judges of the district court of the parish shall certify to the police jury desiring to purchase and maintain a law library for their parish, a list of the books or volumes or sets of books to be purchased or subscribed for; after this list has been certified the police jury shall be authorized to purchase or subscribe for the books or sets of books so certified.
