§380.144.  Operating funds; appropriations by the legislature

The Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism shall include in its annual budget request, required by R.S. 39:32, a request for funds necessary for support of the Louisiana Political Museum and Hall of Fame.  The governor shall include in the executive budget submitted to the legislature sufficient funding for the support of operations and maintenance of the museum and its exhibits.

Acts 2007, No. 415, §1, eff. July 1, 2007.
