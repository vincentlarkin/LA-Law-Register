CHAPTER 4.  LOUISIANA ART COMMISSION
§301.  Creation of commission
There is created a state art commission, to be known as the Louisiana Art Commission.  This commission shall be assigned suitable permanent quarters in the "Old State House" in the City of Baton Rouge.
