§1015. Cooperation by other state entities

            A. The office of state buildings and grounds within the division of administration shall timely perform or provide for all requests for maintenance and repair to the governor's mansion and its grounds made by the commission.

            B. All departments, commissions, boards, agencies, officers, and institutions of the state and all subdivisions thereof shall cooperate with the commission in carrying out the powers and purposes of this Chapter.

            Acts 2022, No. 187, §1.
