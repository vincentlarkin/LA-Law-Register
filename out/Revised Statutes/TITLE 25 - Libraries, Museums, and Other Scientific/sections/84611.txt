§852.  Staffing
The adjutant general shall appoint such employees as are needed, including a director who shall reside in New Orleans, a curator of exhibits who shall be experienced in museum work, establish and use any identifying seal pertaining to museum business, and make an annual report which shall be included as a part of his report to the governor.  The museum shall be open to the public as may be prescribed by the adjutant general.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 688, §1; Acts 1997, No. 467, §1.
