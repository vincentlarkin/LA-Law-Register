§1244.  Donations and grants

The secretary of the state and the commission, jointly or separately, may solicit, accept, use, and dispose of any private or public funds in the form of donations of money, grants, property, or personal services from individuals, corporations, and governments to achieve the purposes of this Chapter.

Acts 1999, No. 1368, §1.
