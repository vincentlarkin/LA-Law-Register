CHAPTER 25-A.  MUSEUM BOARD; JACKSON PARISH

§1215.1.  Museum board authorized

The governing authority of the parish of Jackson is hereby authorized to create, by ordinance, a museum board, referred to in this Chapter as the "board".  The authority of the board shall extend throughout all of the territory of the parish of Jackson.  The board shall be a political subdivision of the state of Louisiana created to establish, maintain, operate, and support public museums and related facilities within the parish.

Acts 2004, No. 424, §1.
