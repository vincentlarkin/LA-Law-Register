§803.  Domicile
The domicile of the department shall be in Baton Rouge, where it shall maintain its offices; in addition, an office shall be maintained in the Louisiana State Museum complex in New Orleans for the assistant director.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 425, §1, operative Jan. 1, 1973.
