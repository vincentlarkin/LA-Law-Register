§380.43. Operating funds; appropriations by the legislature

            The department may include in its annual budget request, required by R.S. 39:32, a request for funds necessary for support of the museum. The governor shall include in the executive budget submitted to the legislature sufficient funding for the support of operations and maintenance of the museum and its exhibits.

            Acts 2022, No. 519, §1, eff. June 16, 2022.
