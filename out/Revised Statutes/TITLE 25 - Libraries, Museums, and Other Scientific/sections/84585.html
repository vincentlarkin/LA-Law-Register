§804.  Transfer of functions

By authority of Section 32 of Article III of the Constitution of 1921, the boards, commissions, councils, agencies, and offices enumerated in R.S. 25:802 are hereby transferred to the State Department of Art, Historical and Cultural Preservation, and all of the functions, programs, and operations of every kind of said boards, commissions, councils, departments and agencies, sometimes hereinafter referred to as agencies, hereafter shall be exercised and be performed by the director.  

Added by Acts 1972, No. 425, §1, operative Jan. 1, 1973.
