§954. Prohibited acts

            Except as otherwise permitted by law:

            (1) The possession of human remains is prohibited.

            (2) It shall be unlawful to trade in, discard, or destroy human remains.

            Acts 2016, No. 531, §1, eff. June 17, 2016.
