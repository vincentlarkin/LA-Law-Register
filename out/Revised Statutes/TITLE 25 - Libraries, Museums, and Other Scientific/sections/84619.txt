§874.  Donations and grants
The Wedell-Williams Memorial Aviation Museum of Louisiana may directly, or indirectly, through the board of directors of the Louisiana State Museum or the Louisiana Museum Foundation, receive donations and grants from individuals, corporations, private foundations, and local, state, and federal governmental agencies, entities, or subdivisions in order to further the provisions of this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 99, §1; Acts 1995, No. 291, §1.
