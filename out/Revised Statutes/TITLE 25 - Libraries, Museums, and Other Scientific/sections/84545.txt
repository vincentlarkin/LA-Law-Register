§735.  Termination of study committee
The historic preservation study committee shall cease to exist following the completion, presentation or filing of its report and recommendations with the governing body of the governmental unit or filing of its report and recommendations with the planning or zoning agency or commission if one exists or after having considered and acted finally on any suggested changes or amendments.
Acts 1970, No. 147, §5.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 804, §5.
