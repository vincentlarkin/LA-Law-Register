§1211.  Audit

The financial records of the board shall be audited pursuant to R.S. 24:513.  A written report of this audit shall be made to the board and to the governing authority.  Audit costs shall be borne by the board.  

Acts 1991, No. 120, §1, eff. June 30, 1991.
