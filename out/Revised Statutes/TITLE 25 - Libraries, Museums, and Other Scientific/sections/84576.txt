§783.  Office of cultural development; division of historic preservation
The division of historic preservation, office of cultural development, Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism, hereinafter referred to as the office, shall administer the provisions of this Part.
Acts 1983, No. 687, §4; Acts 1997, No. 785, §1.
