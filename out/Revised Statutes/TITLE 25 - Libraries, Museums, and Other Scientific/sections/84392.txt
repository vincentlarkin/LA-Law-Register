§296.1.  Membership; tenure
The members of the commission shall consist of the Calcasieu Parish Police Jury.  The commission shall request that the Southwest Bar Association's executive committee appoint an advisory committee of seven active members of the association who shall make recommendations to the commission concerning the operation of the law library.
Acts 1993, No. 730, §1.
