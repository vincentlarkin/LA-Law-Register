§344.  Operating funds
The legislature shall provide sufficient funds for the proper professional operation
of the properties of the Louisiana State Museum, according to accepted standards of museum
operations and practices as established by the American Alliance of Museums, including a
competent professional, technical, and administrative staff, all members of which shall be
subject to the civil service laws.
Acts 1983, No. 687, §4; Acts 1997, No. 318, §1, eff. June 18, 1997; Acts 2025, No.
450, §1.
