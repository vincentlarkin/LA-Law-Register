§374. Domicile; powers and duties of the board

            A.(1) The board shall meet at least three times a year, unless additional meetings are called by the secretary of state.

            (2) The board shall adopt bylaws establishing policies and procedures for its governance.

            (3) The board, in cooperation with any nonprofit corporation established to support the Old State Capitol, may seek private funding to support programs for the Old State Capitol.

            (4) The domicile of the board shall be the city of Baton Rouge.

            B. The board shall advise the secretary of state on all matters relating to the operations of the Old State Capitol and shall have such other functions as are otherwise provided in this Chapter and R.S. 36:919.

            Acts 1990, No. 726, §1; Acts 2020, No. 212, §1, eff. June 11, 2020.
