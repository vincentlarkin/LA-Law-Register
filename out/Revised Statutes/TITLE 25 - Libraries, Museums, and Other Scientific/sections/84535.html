§706.  Historic map of region

The Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism shall provide for the creation and periodic distribution of an historic map of the region, which shall indicate the important events which occurred in the region during the colonial period and during the existence of the Republic of West Florida.  

Acts 1990, No. 1021, §1.
