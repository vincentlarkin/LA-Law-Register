CHAPTER 25.  WEST BATON ROUGE MUSEUM BOARD

§1201.  Museum board authorized

The governing authority of West Baton Rouge Parish may create, by ordinance, a museum board to establish, maintain, operate, and support public museums and related facilities within the parish.  

Acts 1991, No. 120, §1, eff. June 30, 1991.
