CHAPTER 7.  UNION CATALOG OF LOUISIANA ITEMS
§451.  Catalog established
There shall be established a Union Catalog of Louisiana Items (exclusive of state documents and state archives) to be found in all public libraries (state, parish and municipal) in the libraries of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College, in the libraries of the higher institutions of learning subject to the direct supervision of the state board of education, and in all other libraries which may be willing to participate.
Acts 1956, No. 361, §1.
