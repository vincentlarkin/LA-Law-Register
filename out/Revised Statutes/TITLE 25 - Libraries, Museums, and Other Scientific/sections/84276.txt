CHAPTER 23.  LOUISIANA GOVERNOR'S MANSION ADVISORY COMMISSION
§1011.  Establishment; purpose
The Louisiana Governor's Mansion Advisory Commission, hereafter in this Chapter
referred to as the "commission", is hereby created and established as an agency in the office
of the governor for purposes of providing recommendations on the protection and
enhancement of the governor's mansion and its contents, furnishings, and grounds.  The
purpose of the commission is to ensure a continuum in the appearance and the preservation
of contents, furnishings, and surrounding areas of the governor's mansion consistent with the
historical significance and importance of the governor's mansion.
Acts 2022, No. 187, §1; Acts 2024, No. 237, §1, eff. May 23, 2024.
