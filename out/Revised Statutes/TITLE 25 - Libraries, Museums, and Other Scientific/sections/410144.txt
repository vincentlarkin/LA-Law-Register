CHAPTER 33.  DELTA BIKE TRAIL COMMISSION
§1301.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be referred to as the "Delta Bike Trail
Commission", which shall be created to be a body politic and corporate and a political
subdivision of the state with all of the powers of a political subdivision and with such further
powers and functions as are set forth in this Chapter.
Acts 2022, No. 336, §1.
