§798.  Definitions
As used in this Part, unless the context requires otherwise, the following terms shall have the following meaning:
(1)  "Board" means the board of commissioners of the French Quarter Management District.
(2)  "City" means the city of New Orleans.
(3)  "District" means the French Quarter Management District.
Acts 2007, No. 280, §1, eff. July 6, 2007; Acts 2011, No. 304, §1.
