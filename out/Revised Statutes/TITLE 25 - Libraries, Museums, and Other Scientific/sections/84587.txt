§806.  Transfer of records, money and equipment
All books, papers, records, money, choses in action and other property heretofore possessed, controlled or used by any board, commission, council, department or agency in the exercise of functions hereby transferred are hereby transferred to the State Department of Arts, Historical and Cultural Preservation.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 425, §1, operative Jan. 1, 1973.
