§214.6.  Allen Parish Library Board of Control; per diem
A.  The members of the Allen Parish Library Board of Control may receive
a per diem paid from funds of the Allen Parish Library for their attendance at regular
or special meetings of the board.
B.  Members of the board may receive a per diem in an amount not to exceed
one hundred dollars, and the president of the board may receive a per diem in an
amount not to exceed one hundred fifty dollars.
Acts 2024, No. 472, §1.
