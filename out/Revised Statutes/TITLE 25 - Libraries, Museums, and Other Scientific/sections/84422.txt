CHAPTER 5-A.  OLD STATE CAPITOL
§371.  Definitions
For purposes of this Chapter the following terms shall have the meaning indicated unless the context clearly provides otherwise:
(1)  "Board" shall mean the Advisory Board of the Old State Capitol.
(2)  "Department" shall mean the Department of State.
(3)  "Secretary" shall mean the secretary of state.
Acts 1990, No. 726, §1.
