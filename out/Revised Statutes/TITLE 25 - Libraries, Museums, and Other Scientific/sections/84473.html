CHAPTER 6.  ORLEANS PARISH LANDMARKS COMMISSION

§381.  Orleans Parish Landmarks Commission

The Orleans Parish Landmarks Commission is hereby created to be composed of seven members to be appointed by the governor for terms of four years.  All members of the commission shall be residents of the parish of Orleans one of whom shall be a member of the Louisiana Landmarks Society, Inc.  

Acts 1956, No. 85, §1.  Amended by Acts 1962, No. 496, §1.
