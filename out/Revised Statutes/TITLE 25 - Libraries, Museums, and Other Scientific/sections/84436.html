§379.6.  Use of collections

Any collection of the Louisiana State Exhibit Museum may be lent in part, subject to approval of the board.  The board shall insure proper safeguards for its maintenance and return and shall insure that proper records are kept.  The collections shall be available for use in educational projects, subject to the approval of the board.  

Acts 1991, No. 9, §1, eff. June 6, 1991.
