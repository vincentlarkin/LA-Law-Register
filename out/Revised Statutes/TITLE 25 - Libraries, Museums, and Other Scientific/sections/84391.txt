PART VII.  CALCASIEU PARISH LAW LIBRARY COMMISSION
§296.  Creation; purpose
There is hereby created the Calcasieu Parish Law Library Commission to establish, maintain, and operate a law library for the use and benefit of the judiciary, the members of the bar, and the general public of Calcasieu Parish.
Acts 1993, No. 730, §1.
