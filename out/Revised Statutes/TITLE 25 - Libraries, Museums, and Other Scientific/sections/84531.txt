§702.  Baton Rouge as "Birthplace of Louisiana Democracy"
The city of Baton Rouge is designated as the "Birthplace of Louisiana Democracy", because it is the site of the first democratically-elected government in Louisiana, the Assembly of the British Colony of West Florida.
Acts 1990, No. 1021, §1.
