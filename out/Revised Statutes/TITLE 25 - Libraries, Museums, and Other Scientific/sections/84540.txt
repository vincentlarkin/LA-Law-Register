§711.  Relations with other states
All entities of the state are authorized to undertake joint projects with entities of the states of Mississippi, Alabama, and Florida, portions of which were formerly part of the Republic of West Florida, in order to preserve, commemorate, and develop the history and culture of the region.
Acts 1990, No. 1021, §1.
