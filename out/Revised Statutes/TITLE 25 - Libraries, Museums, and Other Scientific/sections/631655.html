CHAPTER 35.  LEEVILLE FISHING VILLAGE AND CULTURAL

PRESERVATION COMMISSION

§1321.  Legislative intent

The legislature hereby recognizes that it is appropriate to establish a commission to preserve the culture of and promote tourism for the Leeville Fishing Village in Lafourche Parish.

Acts 2013, No. 128, §1.
