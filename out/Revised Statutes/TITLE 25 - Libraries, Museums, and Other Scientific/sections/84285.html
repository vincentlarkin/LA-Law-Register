§1024.  Funding; donations and grants

The commission may solicit, accept, use, and dispose of any private or public funds in the form of donations of money, grants, property, or personal services from individuals, corporations, and governments for such purposes.  However, the commission shall first adopt rules and regulations under which it may accept such donations or grants of money, property, or personal services, including the procedure for determining the value of the donation.  

Acts 1988, No. 4, §1.
