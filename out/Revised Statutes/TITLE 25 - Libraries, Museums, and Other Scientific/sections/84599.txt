CHAPTER 17-B.  LOUISIANA CYPRESS SAWMILL MUSEUM
§831.  Establishment and location
The Louisiana Cypress Sawmill Museum is hereby established as the official state cypress sawmill museum.  The museum shall be domiciled in Patterson, Louisiana, and shall be operated, managed, and funded by the office of the state museum of the Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism.
Acts 1997, No. 722, §1.
