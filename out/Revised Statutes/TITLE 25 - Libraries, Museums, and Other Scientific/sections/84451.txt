§380.26.  Use of collections
Any collection of the Louisiana State Oil and Gas Museum may, subject to approval of the board, be lent in part.  The board shall insure proper safeguards for its maintenance and return and shall insure that proper records are kept.  The collections shall be available for use in educational projects, subject to the approval of the board.
Acts 1997, No. 1091, §1; Acts 2001, No. 130, §1.
