PART IV.  LAFAYETTE PARISH LAW LIBRARY COMMISSION

§281.  Creation; purpose

There is hereby created the Lafayette Parish Law Library Commission to establish, maintain and operate a law library for the use and benefit of the judiciary, the members of the bar and the general public of Lafayette Parish.

Added by Acts 1968, No. 416, §1.
