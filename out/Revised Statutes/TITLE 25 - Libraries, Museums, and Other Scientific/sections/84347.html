§15.  Authority to do acts required by federal laws

The State Library of Louisiana is authorized to file any and all applications and make any and all reports and keep and render any and all accounts required or specified by federal law or regulation with reference to securing, administering, and using all such funds and monies for said purposes in the state of Louisiana.  

Acts 1991, No. 938, §2.
