§378.  Use of collections

The collections of the Old State Capitol may be lent in part subject to approval by the secretary.  The secretary shall insure proper safeguards for their maintenance and return and shall insure that proper records are kept.  The collections shall be available for the use of educational projects, subject to the approval of the secretary.  

Acts 1990, No. 726, §1.
