§380.56.  Use of collections
Any collection of the Louisiana Delta Music Museum may, subject to approval of the board, be lent in part.  The board shall ensure proper safeguards for its maintenance and return and shall ensure that proper records are kept.  The collections shall be available for use in educational projects, subject to the approval of the board.
Acts 2001, No. 850, §1.
