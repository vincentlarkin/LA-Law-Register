§811.  Intergovernmental cooperation

The State Art, Historical, and Cultural Preservation Agency may exercise and perform any authorized power and function jointly or in cooperation with one or more historic preservation districts created pursuant to R.S. 25:732 or with the United States or its agencies.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 592, §2, eff. Aug. 1, 1975.
