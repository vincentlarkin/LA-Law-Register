§346.  Use of museum's collections
The collections of the Louisiana State Museum may be lent in part subject to and in
accordance with the collections management policy approved by the board. The office of the
state museum shall ensure proper safeguards for their maintenance and return and shall
ensure that proper records are kept.
Acts 1983, No. 687, §4; Acts 2025, No. 450, §1.
