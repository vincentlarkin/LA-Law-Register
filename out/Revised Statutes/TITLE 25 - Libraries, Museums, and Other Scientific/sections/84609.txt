§844.  Donations and grants
The Louisiana Civil Rights Museum may directly, or indirectly through the board of directors of the Louisiana State Museum or the Louisiana Museum Foundation, receive donations and grants from individuals, corporations, private foundations, and local, state, and federal governmental agencies, entities, or subdivisions in order to further the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 1999, No. 385, §1; Acts 2004, No. 731, §1, eff. July 1, 2004.
