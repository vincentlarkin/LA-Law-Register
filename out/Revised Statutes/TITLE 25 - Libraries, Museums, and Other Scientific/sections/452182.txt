§221.1.  Library donations; spending restrictions
Notwithstanding any provision of this Part or other law to the contrary, the  West Baton Rouge Parish Library Board of Control shall not spend any portion of the principal of the Judge Paul B. Landry, Jr. Memorial Fund, but may spend only the investment earnings of the fund.
Acts 2007, No. 25, §1.
