PART III.  ORLEANS PARISH LAW LIBRARY COMMISSION
§271.  Orleans Parish Law Library; creation; purpose
There is hereby created the Orleans Parish Law Library for the use and benefit of the judiciary, the members of the bar, and the general public of Orleans Parish.
Acts 1964, No. 284, §1; Acts 1995, No. 1132, §1.
