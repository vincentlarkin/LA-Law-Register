§1305. Advice

            The parish planning and zoning departments of Concordia, Tensas, Madison, and East Carroll parishes, and all its subcommittees, shall serve in an advisory capacity to the commission, relative to the trail or any projects contemplated by or to be undertaken by the commission.

            Acts 2022, No. 336, §1.
