§380.15.  Use of collections
Any collection of the Edward Douglass White Historic Site may be lent in part,
subject to and in accordance with the collections management policies approved by the
board. The board shall ensure proper safeguards for its maintenance and return and shall
ensure that proper records are kept. The collections shall be available for use in educational
projects, subject to the approval of the board.
Acts 1993, No. 507, §2, eff. July 1, 1993; Acts 2025, No. 450, §1.
