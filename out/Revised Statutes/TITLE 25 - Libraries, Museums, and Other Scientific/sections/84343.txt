§126.  Publications to be furnished the Senate secretary and House clerk; quantity
The secretary of state shall deliver to the secretary of the Senate and the clerk of the House of Representatives ten copies each of the following public documents as soon as practicable after they are printed from time to time:  (1) the roster of officials, (2) the constitution, (3) supplements to the constitution, (4) proposed constitutional amendments, and (5) compilations of laws.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 238, §1; Acts 1987, No. 224, §1.
