§1422.  Application of Subpart

None of the provisions of this Subpart apply to materials that are to be used in the manufacture of mixed fertilizer nor to fertilizers processed or manufactured in this state intended for sale or distribution in other states nor to fertilizers being transported through this state and destined for use in other states.

Acts 2010, No. 579, §1.
