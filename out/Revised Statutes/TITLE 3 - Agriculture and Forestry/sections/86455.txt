§4230.  Application
The requirements of this Chapter shall apply to persons, firms or corporation establishments, animals and articles regulated under the Federal Meat Inspection Act* or the Federal Poultry Products Inspection Act only to the extent provided for in Section 408 of said federal acts.**
Acts 1968, No. 376, §28.  Amended by Acts 1970, No. 615, §1; Acts 1985, No. 73, §1.
*21 U.S.C.A. §71 ET SEQ.  (TRANSFERRED; SEE NOW, 21 U.S.C.A. §601 ET SEQ.).
**21 U.S.C.A. §301 ET SEQ.
