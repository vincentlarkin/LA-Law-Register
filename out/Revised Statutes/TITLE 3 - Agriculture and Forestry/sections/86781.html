§642.  Commissioner of agriculture and forestry to adopt regulations

The commissioner of agriculture and forestry may adopt rules as to the determination of the amount of the bond or other security and the filing of it.

Amended by Acts 1950, No. 109, §2; Acts 2009, No. 24, §1, eff. June 12, 2009.
