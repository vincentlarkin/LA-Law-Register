§556.10.  Confidentiality
The information received by the department from the reports required by R.S. 3:556.8 and the collections required by R.S. 3:556.9 shall be confidential with respect to the person submitting the report and the person remitting the collections.  The figures for the total amount of artificial bait and crawfish bags sold and the total collections of each assessment shall be public record.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 679, §1; Acts 1985, No. 330, §1; Acts 1985, No. 492, §1; Acts 1986, No. 547, §1.
