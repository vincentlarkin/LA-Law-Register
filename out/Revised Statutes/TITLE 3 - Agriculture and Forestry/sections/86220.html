§3272.  Cooperative agreements

In order to accomplish the objectives of this Part, the commissioner may enter into cooperative agreements with appropriate governmental agencies.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 198, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983; Acts 1990, No. 136, §1.
