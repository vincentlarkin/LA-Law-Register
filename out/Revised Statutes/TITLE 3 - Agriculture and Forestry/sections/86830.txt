§735.  Prohibited activities
A.  No person shall use any brand or mark for branding or marking animals unless
the brand or mark is recorded with the commission.
B.  No person shall intentionally offer livestock for sale without providing the true
and legal name of the owner of the livestock for the purpose of livestock theft or violation
of a quarantine issued by the Louisiana Board of Animal Health.
Acts 1982, No. 113, §1; Acts 2018, No. 429, §1.
