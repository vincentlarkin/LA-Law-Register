§3305.  Monitoring

The commissioner shall establish a program for monitoring the waters of the state for the purpose of determining the levels of pesticides in those waters.  The provisions of this Part shall not preclude or prevent the monitoring of the waters of the state by any other agency.

Added by Acts 1988, No. 349, §1.
