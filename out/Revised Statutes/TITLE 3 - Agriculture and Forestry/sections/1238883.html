§2072. Name and purpose

            A. The name of the program created and organized by this Chapter shall be the Louisiana Equine Promotion and Research Program.

            B. The purpose of the program is to support the growth and development of the equine industry in Louisiana by enhancing research, education, promotion, facilities, tourism, events, and equine-related activities throughout the state.

            Acts 2021, No. 168, §1, eff. June 11, 2021.
