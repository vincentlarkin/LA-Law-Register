CHAPTER 15-A. LOUISIANA EQUINE PROMOTION AND

RESEARCH PROGRAM

§2071. Legislative intent

            The legislature intends by this Chapter to authorize the equine industry to establish a self-governed program to support the growth and development of the industry in Louisiana.

            Acts 2021, No. 168, §1, eff. June 11, 2021.
