§1404.  Evidence
In any controversy or prosecution arising under the provisions of this Part, a certificate of the state chemist or other state employee making analyses or inspection, duly sworn to by the state chemist or employee, shall be prima facie evidence of the facts therein certified.
Acts 2010, No. 579, §1.
