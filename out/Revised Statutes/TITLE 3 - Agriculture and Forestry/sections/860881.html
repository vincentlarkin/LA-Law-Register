PART III-A.  SWEET POTATO PESTS AND DISEASES

§1736.1.  Certificate of inspection required for sweet potato shipments

All sweet potatoes, vines, plants, and slips, shipped or moved into, out of, or within the state shall be accompanied by a certificate of inspection signed by the commissioner.

Acts 2013, No. 332, §1, eff. June 17, 2013.
