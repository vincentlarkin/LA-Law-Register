§2223.  Rules and regulations for brucellosis eradication

The Louisiana Board of Animal Health may promulgate necessary rules and regulations to carry out the eradication of brucellosis, and necessary quarantine regulations to prevent the reinfection of livestock.

Amended by Acts 1966, No. 291, §1; Acts 2008, No. 920, §1, eff. July 14, 2008.
