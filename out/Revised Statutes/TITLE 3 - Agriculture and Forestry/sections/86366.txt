§3814.  Meetings of the commission
The commission shall hold regular meetings twice each year.  The commission may meet at other times upon the call of the chairman or of any three members.  The commission shall not meet more than six times in any calendar year.
Added by Acts 1965, No. 127, §2.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 621, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982; Acts 1985, No. 120, §1; Acts 1990, No. 112, §1.
