§4703. Adoption of certification marks
The terms "Cajun," "Louisiana Creole," and any derivative or combination of either term are hereby adopted, appropriated and established by the legislature as certification marks to be owned by the state of Louisiana.
Acts 2006, No. 124, §1, eff. June 2, 2006.
