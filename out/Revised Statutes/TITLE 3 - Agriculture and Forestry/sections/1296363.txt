§4351.12.  Authorization of the Department of Insurance to employ investigators
The department shall have authority to employ investigators to investigate complaints
received against the group self-insurance fund authorized to do business in this state and
against any unauthorized group self-insurance fund that is reported to be operating in this
state.
Acts 2022, No. 586, §1, eff. June 17, 2022; Acts 2022, No. 598, §1, eff. June 18,
2022.
