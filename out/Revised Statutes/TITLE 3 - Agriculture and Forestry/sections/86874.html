§852.  Analysis by state chemist; reports

Samples received by the commissioner shall be transmitted to the state chemist who shall perform any necessary chemical or physical analysis.  The results of such analyses shall be reported to the commissioner who in turn shall report them to all interested parties.

Acts 1956, No. 316, §2.
