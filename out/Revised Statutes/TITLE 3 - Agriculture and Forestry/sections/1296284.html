PART VII. LOUISIANA INDUSTRIAL HEMP PROMOTION AND

RESEARCH PROGRAM

§1491. Name and purpose

            A. The name of the program created and organized by this Part shall be the Louisiana Industrial Hemp Promotion and Research Program.

            B. The purpose of the program is to support the growth and development of the industrial hemp industry in Louisiana by enhancing research, education, promotion, facilities, and industrial hemp related activities throughout the state.

            Acts 2022, No. 462, §1.
