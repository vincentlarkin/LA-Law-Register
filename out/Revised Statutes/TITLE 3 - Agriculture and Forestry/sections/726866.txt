§1430.6.  Labeling
Each package of agricultural liming materials sold, offered for sale, or distributed in this state shall contain a conspicuous and legible label attached to the package.  If the materials are sold in bulk form, a copy of the label shall be attached to the invoice which is delivered to the person purchasing the materials.  The label shall contain the following information:
(1)  The name and the address of the principal office of the manufacturer.
(2)  The brand name of the material.
(3)  The type of the material.
(4)  If the material is dolomitic limestone, the content of elemental magnesium.
(5)  If the material is ground shells, the name of the mollusk from which the shells were obtained.
(6)  The net weight of the material in the package.
(7)  Such other information as the commission by rule may require.
Acts 2010, No. 579, §1.
