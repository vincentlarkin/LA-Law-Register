PART II.  DOMESTIC FARM-RAISED RATITES

§3111.  Legislative findings

The purpose of this Part is to classify and regulate the growing of nonnative wildlife species of domestic farm-raised ratites including but not limited to ostrich, rhea, emu, and cassowary for the purpose of producing meat, skins, hides, feathers, oil, and progeny.

Acts 1993, No. 134, §1.
