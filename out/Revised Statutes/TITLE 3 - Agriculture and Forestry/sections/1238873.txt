§1473.  Centralized website
The Louisiana State University Agricultural Center shall develop a centralized
industrial hemp website that provides to the public information, resources, and educational
opportunities concerning industrial hemp.  The agricultural center shall develop the website
in collaboration with the Southern University Agricultural Center, the Louisiana Department
of Agriculture and Forestry, the Louisiana Department of Health, the office of alcohol and
tobacco control, Louisiana Economic Development, and relevant industry associations.
Acts 2021, No. 336, §1.
