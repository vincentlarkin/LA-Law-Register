§4675.  Containers or pumps from which petroleum products not conforming to specifications are sold, to be labeled
If any petroleum product which does not conform to the minimum specifications adopted by the commissioner in accordance with this Subpart is sold or dispensed in this state, the container or pump from which the petroleum product is to be dispensed shall be clearly, distinctly, and conspicuously labeled.
Acts 2003, No. 139, §1, eff. May 28, 2003.
