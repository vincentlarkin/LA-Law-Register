§3653.  Applicability
The provisions of this Chapter shall apply to security devices affecting farm products, including timber.
Acts 1987, No. 451, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 1987; Acts 1988, No. 323, §1; Acts 1990, No. 123, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1991; Acts 2010, No. 378, §1.
