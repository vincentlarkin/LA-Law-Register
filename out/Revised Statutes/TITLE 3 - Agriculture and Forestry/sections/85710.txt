§1106.  Authority to sell animal or agricultural products of farms
The board of control may market, sell, trade, or dispose of any of the animal or agricultural products of demonstration farms, whether it be for the maintenance thereof or with the aim toward proper and commensurate compensation to aid in the promotion of the agricultural and stock raising industries of the state.
