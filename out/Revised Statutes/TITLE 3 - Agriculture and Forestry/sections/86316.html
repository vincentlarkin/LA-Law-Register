§3425.  Limitations of applicability

The provisions of this Chapter shall not apply to, and the commission shall not have jurisdiction over, the following:

(1)  Cold storage facilities.

(2)  Facilities which store only commodities which are imported from outside the boundaries of the continental United States.

(3)  Repealed by Acts 1997, No. 1034, §2.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 563, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983; Acts 1997, No. 1034, §2.
