§4162.  Confidentiality of information

Information which is obtained by any person pursuant to this Part is confidential and shall not be disclosed to any other person except to a person with the same right to obtain the information or any attorney employed to give legal advice upon it, or by any court order.

Added by Acts 1983, No. 526, §1, eff. July 8, 1983; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8L, eff. June 12, 2009.
