§3521.  General rules and regulations
A. The commissioner may establish such general rules and regulations as may be necessary to facilitate the administration and enforcement of such marketing orders and agreements.
B.  The provisions of R.S. 3:3520 relative to publication and time of taking effect are applicable to any such general rule and regulation which is established pursuant to this Section and applicable to marketing orders.  Such notice shall be mailed to the advisory board for each marketing order or marketing agreement which is in active operation.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 500, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8J, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 861, §3.
