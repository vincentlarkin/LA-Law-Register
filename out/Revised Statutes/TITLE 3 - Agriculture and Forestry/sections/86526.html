§4416.  Competitive research and cooperative extension grants

A.  In order to provide for research and cooperative extension activities to enhance reforestation, increase productivity, and further  knowledge regarding the proper application of forestry principles, a competitive grant process shall be created.

B.  The commissioner shall adopt rules and regulations to establish procedures for awarding competitive grants under this Part.

Acts 1997, No. 1377, §1, eff. July 1, 1998.
