PART II.  CO-OPERATIVE MARKETING ASSOCIATIONS
§121.  Declaration of policy
The purpose of this Part is to promote, foster, and encourage the intelligent and
orderly marketing of agricultural products through co-operation.
Acts 2017, No. 65, §1.
