§3253.  Notice
A.  The commissioner shall notify each person who is affected by a suspension or revocation of a license or certificate, or by probation, or by civil penalties, by certified mail, return receipt requested.
B.  Each person whose license or certificate has been revoked or suspended shall return the license or certificate to the commissioner within fifteen days of the date on which the notice was received.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 198, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983.
