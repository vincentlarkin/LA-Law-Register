PART X.  STRAWBERRIES

§730.1.  Purpose

The purpose of this Part is to promote the general well-being of the strawberry industry of the state of Louisiana by promoting increased production and quality in Louisiana strawberries through marketing and research; by expanding the market for Louisiana strawberries through promotion and marketing; by increasing the consumption of strawberries in Louisiana through advertising, promotion, and marketing; by providing producers, distributors, retailers, and consumers with educational information as to the nutritional and health value of strawberries; and by performing any other act deemed advisable in promoting this industry and the general welfare of the people of this state.

Added by Acts 1958, No. 485, §1. Amended by Acts 1968, No. 365, §1; Acts 2003, No. 1253, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8D, eff. June 12, 2009.
