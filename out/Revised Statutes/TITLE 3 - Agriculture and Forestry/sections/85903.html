§2005.  Obtaining list of approved pedigree or registry associations

The Louisiana Board of Animal Health shall, upon the request of the police jury of any parish, furnish a list of pedigree or registry associations as satisfy the requirements of R.S. 3:2004.

Acts 2008, No. 920, §1, eff. July 14, 2008.
