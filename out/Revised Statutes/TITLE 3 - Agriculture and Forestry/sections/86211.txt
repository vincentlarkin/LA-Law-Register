§3250.  Applications
A.  Each application for examination shall be in writing and shall be on forms prescribed by the commissioner.  Each application shall be accompanied by the examination fee and such information and documents as the commissioner may require.
B.  Each application for a license, certificate, or registration shall be in writing and shall be on forms prescribed by the commissioner.  Each application shall be accompanied by the license, certificate, or registration fee, proof of passing the examination, proof of financial responsibility where required, and such other information and documents as the commissioner may require.
C.  Each applicant shall indicate on each application the street address of his place of business.  If the place of business does not have a street address, the applicant shall provide sufficient information to establish the physical location of the place of business.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 198, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983.
