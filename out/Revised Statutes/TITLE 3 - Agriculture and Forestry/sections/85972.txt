PART V.  TUBERCULOSIS CONTROL AND ERADICATION
§2261.  Rules and regulations; quarantine
Power and authority is hereby delegated to the Louisiana Board of Animal Health to promulgate the necessary rules and regulations, including quarantine regulations, to carry out a tuberculosis control and eradication program for cattle.
Acts 1950, No. 179, §1; Acts 2008, No. 920, §1, eff. July 14, 2008; Acts 2012, No. 204, §§2, 3(E).
