§2004.  Scrub bull defined

For the purpose of this Part the term "scrub bull" means any bull not entitled to pedigree or registration, as such terms are ordinarily understood in registry associations, societies, or companies recognized by the United States Department of Agriculture and approved by the Louisiana Board of Animal Health.

Acts 2012, No. 811, §1, eff. July 1, 2012.
