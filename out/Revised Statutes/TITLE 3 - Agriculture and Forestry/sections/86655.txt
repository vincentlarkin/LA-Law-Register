§838.  Officers and employees
A.  The members shall annually elect a chairman and such other officers as deemed necessary.
B.  The commission shall employ a director and assistant director who shall be appointed by the commission, subject to the approval of the commissioner.  The director and assistant director shall be in the unclassified service.  The commissioner is authorized to employ such other personnel necessary for the efficient and proper administration of this Part.  All employees of the commission shall be under the direction and supervision of the commissioner.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 441, §1. Amended by Acts 1970, No. 495, §2; Acts 1986, No. 447, §1, eff. July 1, 1986; Acts 2003, No. 523, §1, eff. June 20, 2003; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8F, eff. June 12, 2009.
