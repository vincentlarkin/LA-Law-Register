§2264.  Branding and disposition of reactors

All cattle reacting to the official test for tuberculosis shall be branded with the letter "T" on the left jaw and shall be disposed of by slaughter, according to the rules and regulations of the Louisiana Board of Animal Health and the United States Department of Agriculture, APHIS Veterinary Services.

Acts 1950, No. 179, §4; Acts 2008, No. 920, §1, eff. July 14, 2008; Acts 2012, No. 204, §§2, 3(E).
