§903.  Fees for inspection, classification, and grading
The commissioner may collect fees for the inspection, classification, and grading of
farm products, fruits, and vegetables.  The fees shall not exceed the actual cost necessary to
provide for the proper inspection, grading, and classification of the products.
Acts 1978, No. 242, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8A, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2017, No.
39, §1.
