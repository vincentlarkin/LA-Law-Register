§644.  Producers' rights under bond or other security

Any producer of milk to whom money is due for milk sold to any manufacturer, pasteurizer, or distributor, shall have full recourse on the bond or other security that shall be required by the commissioner of agriculture and forestry, as provided by this Part, for all such amounts.

Amended by Acts 1950, No. 109, §4; Acts 2009, No. 24, §1, eff. June 12, 2009.
