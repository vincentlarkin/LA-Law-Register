CHAPTER 23-A. THE LOUISIANA RENEWABLE FUELS

PRODUCTION ACCOUNTABILITY ACT

§3711.  Title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as "The Louisiana Renewable Fuels Production Accountability Act."

Acts 2006, No. 656, §1, eff. July 1, 2006.
