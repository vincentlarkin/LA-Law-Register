§4013.  Falsifying tests or weights or measures or records thereof prohibited

No person shall underread, overread, or in any way manipulate any approved butterfat test so that other than the true butterfat percentage is obtained; or any weighing or measuring device so that other than the true weight or measure of the milk or cream purchase is obtained.

Acts 1974, No. 306, §1; Acts 1985, No. 74, §1.
