§669.  Issuance of charter; special provision
The owner of any livestock market operated and conducted as such on April 1, 2019,
shall be issued a charter for the operation of said market.
Added by Acts 1966, No. 142, §1; Acts 2020, No. 306, §1, eff. June 12, 2020.
