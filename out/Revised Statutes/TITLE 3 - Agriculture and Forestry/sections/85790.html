§14.  Executive counsel and assistants; qualifications

The commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry may employ an executive counsel and such assistants as he finds necessary.  The executive counsel and any assistants shall be duly qualified and admitted to practice law in Louisiana.  The commissioner may fix the compensation of each.

Acts 2012, No. 152, §1, eff. May 14, 2012.
