§3014.  Saving clause
All parish or ward stock laws or ordinances in effect on July 30, 1958, shall remain in full force and effect, it being the intention of the legislature to provide by this Part an additional method by which stock laws may be voted and enforced.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 399, §1.
