CHAPTER 4-A.  LOUISIANA RURAL DEVELOPMENT LAW

PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§311.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be referred to as the "Louisiana Rural Development Law".

Acts 1990, No. 216, §1; Acts 1991, No. 396, §2; Acts 1991, No. 449, §1.
