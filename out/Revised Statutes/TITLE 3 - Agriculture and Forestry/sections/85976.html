PART VI.  REGULATION OF APIARIES

§2301.  Purpose of Part

The purpose of this Part is to prevent the introduction into and dissemination within this state of contagious and infectious diseases of honeybees by providing for the registration, inspection, and control of honeybees and apiaries, which activity is hereby found and declared by the legislature to promote agriculture in the state of Louisiana.

Amended by Acts 1984, No. 529, §1.
