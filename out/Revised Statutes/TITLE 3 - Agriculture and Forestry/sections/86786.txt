§652.  Penalty; enforcement
Failure to comply with R.S. 3:651 shall result in the loss of operating license.  It shall be the duty of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry to determine compliance with R.S. 3:651 and to enforce its provisions.
Acts 1958, No. 110, §3; Acts 2009, No. 24, §1, eff. June 12, 2009.
