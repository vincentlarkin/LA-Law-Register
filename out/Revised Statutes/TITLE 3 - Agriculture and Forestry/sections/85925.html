§2092.1. Domicile

            The board shall be domiciled in the city of Baton Rouge, and no suit may be filed against the board at any place other than the parish of East Baton Rouge. In suits against the board, all service shall be made on the commissioner of agriculture.

            Added by Acts 1981, No. 169, §1. Acts 1982, No. 443, §7, eff. Jan. 1, 1983; Acts 2013, No. 200, §1.
