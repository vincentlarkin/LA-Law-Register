CHAPTER 21-A.  COMMODITIES MARKETING

§3501.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known as "Louisiana Commodities Marketing Law".

Added by Acts 1978, No. 500, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8J, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 861, §3.
