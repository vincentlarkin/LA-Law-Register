§830.  Eggs must be candled; permitted tolerance

A person offering eggs for sale to any one other than a wholesale dealer shall first candle all eggs and remove all inedible eggs.  A two percent tolerance for inedible eggs will be permitted for each case of eggs at the time of inspection.
