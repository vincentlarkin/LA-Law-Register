§4633. Inspection of mini-sacks, sacks, and barrels; standards contents

            A. The department may also inspect the sacks and barrels in which the oysters arrive.

            B. Each mini-sack of oysters shall contain one-half bushel; each sack of oysters shall contain one and one-half bushels; and each barrel shall contain three bushels. Mini-sacks, sacks, and barrels shall contain correct measurements at the time of delivery to the consignee, buyer, or importer and at the time of delivery to any subsequent buyer after arrival.

            Acts 1992, No. 55, §1; Acts 2018, No. 172, §1.
