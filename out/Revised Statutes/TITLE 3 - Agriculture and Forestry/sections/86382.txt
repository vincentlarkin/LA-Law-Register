§4012.  Purchaser shall use only correct weight and measures
No person purchasing milk or cream on the basis of its butterfat content shall use any weight, measure, or butterfat test thereof other than the correct weight, measure, or butterfat test; nor shall such person have in his possession any inaccurate, defective, or untrue weighing or measuring device.  Also such a person shall not make any misrepresentation as to any weight, measure, or test.
Acts 1974, No. 306, §1; Acts 1985, No. 74, §1.
