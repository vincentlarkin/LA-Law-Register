§1772.  Responsibility for law enforcement
The commissioner of agriculture and forestry and the state entomologist shall carry out all provisions of the law relative to pests and diseases affecting agricultural and horticultural plants and plant products and quarantines and embargoes resulting therefrom.
Acts 2009, No. 24, §1, eff. June 12, 2009.
