§3682.  Imported agricultural products
No person in the state shall sell any agricultural product which was grown or produced in any other state and which is falsely represented for sale as a product of this state.
Whoever violates this Section shall be fined not less than one hundred dollars nor more than one thousand dollars.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 232, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8E, eff. June 12, 2009.
