§3556.  Use of funds
A.  The Louisiana Soybean and Grain Research and Promotion Board shall plan and conduct a program of research and advertising designed to promote the soybean, wheat, corn, and grain sorghum industries in Louisiana.  The board is authorized to use the funds derived from any assessment imposed by this Chapter for these purposes, including basic administration expenses of the plan.  Use of these funds may be applied, as prescribed in this Section, within or without the state of Louisiana, including regional, national, and international applications.
B. The board is authorized to apply for and receive monies from the national assessment for grain sorghum pursuant to 7 CFR 1221.
Added by Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 45, §1; Acts 1985, No. 390, §1, eff. July 10, 1985; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8G, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 25, §1, eff. July 1, 2010; Acts 2010, No. 861, §3; Acts 2014, No. 205, §1.
