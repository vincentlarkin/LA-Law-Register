PART II. FOREST TREE SEEDLING NURSERIES

§4301. Forest tree seedling nurseries; authorization

            The office of forestry may purchase land, install watering systems, and construct and equip the buildings necessary for the operation of forest tree seedling nurseries.

            Amended by Acts 1981, No. 736, §1; Acts 1981, No. 838, §1; Acts 2015, No. 249, §1, eff. June 29, 2015.
