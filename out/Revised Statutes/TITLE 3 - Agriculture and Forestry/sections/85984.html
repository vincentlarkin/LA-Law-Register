§2309.  Assessment on bees, enforcement

For the purpose of enforcing this Part, the commissioner may annually levy and collect a fee of fifteen cents per colony of bees within the state, provided that such assessment shall not be levied on noncommercial apiaries.

Acts 1984, No. 529, §1.
