§2438.1.  Horse aftercare; definition
"Horse aftercare" means the providing for the retraining or retiring of thoroughbreds
and quarter horses that have participated at licensed race tracks in Louisiana.
Acts 2019, No. 221, §1; Acts 2019, No. 328, §1.
