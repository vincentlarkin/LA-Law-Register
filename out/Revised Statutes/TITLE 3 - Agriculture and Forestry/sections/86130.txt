SUBPART C.  SWINE
§2891.  Swine prohibited from running at large
No person owning swine shall knowingly, willfully, or negligently permit his swine to run at large upon public property or upon private property of another person.
Added by Acts 1954, No. 130, §1.  Amended by Acts 1972, No. 419, §1; Acts 2010, No. 980, §1.
