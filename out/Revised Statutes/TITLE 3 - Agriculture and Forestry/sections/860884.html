§1736.4.  Investigation to discover diseased or infested sweet potatoes or plants

The commissioner, his agents and employees, may enter any depot, express office, storeroom, warehouse, or premises for the purpose of inspecting any sweet potatoes, vines, plants, and slips, therein or thought to be therein for the purpose of ascertaining whether the sweet potatoes, vines, plants, and slips are infected with any contagious or infectious diseases or pests that they may have reason to believe have been or are being transported in violation of the provisions of this Part.

Acts 2013, No. 332, §1, eff. June 17, 2013.
