§4634.  Duty of having baskets and oysters inspected

All buyers, sellers, importers, and exporters of oysters may, upon arrival have their baskets inspected and stamped and the oysters inspected and measured, in accordance with R.S. 3:4632 and 4633.

Acts 1992, No. 55, §1.
