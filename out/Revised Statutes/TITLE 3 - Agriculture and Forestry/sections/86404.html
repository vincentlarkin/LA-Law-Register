§4105.  Sales of dairy products not subject to price regulation

Sales of all dairy products covered by this Subpart by retailers, distributors, and processors shall not be subject to price regulation; in no event shall the Dairy Stabilization Board have power or authority to regulate or establish wholesale or retail prices; provided that any such sales shall be subject to the condition of open market competition in compliance with the disruptive trade practice provisions of this Subpart.

Added by Acts 1974, No. 31, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 695, §1; Acts 1985, No. 75, §1.
