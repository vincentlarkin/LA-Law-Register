§16.  Fees for chemical and other analyses

The director of agricultural chemistry, with the approval of the commissioner of agriculture and forestry, may establish fees to be charged for all chemical or other analyses conducted or performed by the agricultural chemistry laboratory. The fees shall be established by rule adopted in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act. The amount of the fee shall be based on the costs incurred in conducting or performing the analyses.

Acts 1988, No. 158, §1.
