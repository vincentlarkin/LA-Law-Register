§1736.2.  Sale of sweet potatoes for certain purposes prohibited
In order to prevent the spread of sweet potato weevils, no person shall sell or provide raw sweet potatoes for the purpose of feeding, offering to feed, or depositing for food the sweet potatoes to wild game quadrupeds.
Acts 2013, No. 332, §1, eff. June 17, 2013.
