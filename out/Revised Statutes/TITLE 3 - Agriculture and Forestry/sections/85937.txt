§213.  Terms defined
In this Chapter whenever the word "corporation" is used, "agricultural credit corporation" as organized under this Chapter is meant; and whenever the words "borrower" or "member" are used, they shall include persons, co-partnerships, or corporations owning stock in the corporation.
