PART IV. LOUISIANA LIVESTOCK MARKET CHARTER LAW

§661. Short title

            This Part shall be known and cited as the Louisiana Livestock Market Charter Law.

            Added by Acts 1966, No. 142, §1; Acts 2020, No. 306, §1, eff. June 12, 2020.
