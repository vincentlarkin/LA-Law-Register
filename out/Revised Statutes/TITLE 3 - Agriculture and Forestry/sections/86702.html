§3504.  Compliance; defense to civil proceedings

Proof that an act was done in compliance with the provisions of this Chapter shall be a complete defense to any civil action or proceeding arising therefrom.

Added by Acts 1978, No. 500, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8J, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 861, §3.
