§904.  Penalty for violations; injunctive relief; costs
A.  Whoever violates this Part or the regulations promulgated under this Part shall
be fined not less than twenty-five dollars nor more than five hundred dollars, or imprisoned
for not less than ten days nor more than six months, or both.
B.  In addition to the penalties authorized in this Section, the commissioner may
apply for injunctive relief restraining violations of this Part or institute necessary actions for
failure to pay accounts due the department.  The person condemned in any such proceeding
shall be liable for the costs of court and for any additional costs incurred by the department
in gathering the necessary evidence, including reasonable attorney fees and expert witness
fees.
Acts 1978, No. 242, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8A, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No.
495, §1, eff. June 24, 2010; Acts 2017, No. 39, §1.
