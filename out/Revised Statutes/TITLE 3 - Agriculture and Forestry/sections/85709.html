§1105.  Record of activities; issuance of bulletins

The board of control of the state penitentiary shall keep an accurate and detailed record of all work, experiments, demonstrations, and enterprises undertaken or conducted by it, which record shall at all times be accessible to the general public.  It shall at stated intervals issue bulletins advising the public of its operations and the results thereof, and shall publish the bulletins through the press or otherwise.
