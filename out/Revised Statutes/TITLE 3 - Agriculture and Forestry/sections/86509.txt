§4362.  Expenditures for management and utilization of areas acquired; disposal of products from land
When lands are acquired or leased under R.S. 3:4361 the commission may:
(1)  Make expenditures from any funds not otherwise obligated, for the management, development, and utilization of such areas;
(2)  Sell or otherwise dispose of products from such lands; and
(3)  Make rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purposes of this Part.
