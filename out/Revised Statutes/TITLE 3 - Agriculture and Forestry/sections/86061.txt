§2573.  Fees and costs
The fees and costs under this Part shall be as follows except for sheep and goats: for impounding, one dollar; for keeping, fifty cents per head, for each day the stock are kept by the impounder or keeper; for making the sale, fifty cents per head for each animal sold; for proceeding, as in case of estrays, such costs as are now allowed by law in cases of estrays in the several parishes.  For sheep and goats the charge for impounding shall be ten cents per head; for keeping, five cents per head for each day; for making the sale, ten cents per head.
