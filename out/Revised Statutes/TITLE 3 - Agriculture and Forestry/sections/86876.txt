§854.  Extent of sampling and analysis
The extent to which sampling and analysis is carried out shall be determined by the commissioner according to the need therefor; provided, that any party to a transaction involving agricultural products sold upon basis of composition may request sampling and analysis to be performed.
Acts 1956, No. 316, §4.
