§4023.  Access to records and information

The commissioner may, by lawful means, obtain information pertaining to the dairy industry which he deems necessary to carry out the purposes of the provisions of this Chapter and the Southern Dairy Compact.  Such information may be utilized by the commissioner, the delegates, and the Southern Dairy Compact Commission.

Acts 1997, No. 11, §1.
