§1430.3.  Applicability

A.  The provisions of this Subpart shall apply only to materials which are sold, offered for sale, or distributed in this state for the purpose of decreasing the acidity of soils used for agricultural production.

B.  Substances which are sold, offered for sale, or distributed in this state for use in the construction or building industries are specifically exempt from the provisions of this Subpart.

Acts 2010, No. 579, §1.
