§4738.  Cooperative endeavor agreement
The Louisiana Department of Health and the Department of Agriculture and Forestry
shall enter into a written cooperative endeavor agreement authorizing the Department of
Agriculture and Forestry to perform inspections in order to determine compliance with this
Subpart.
Acts 2009, No. 506, §1, eff. Nov. 1, 2009.
