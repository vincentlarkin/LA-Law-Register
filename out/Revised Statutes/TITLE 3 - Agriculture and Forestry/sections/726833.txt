§1402.  Exemptions
The provisions of this Part shall not apply to any commercial feeds that have been
manufactured or produced by any person for the purpose of feeding his own livestock or
manufactured or registered in accordance with Part VI of this Chapter.
Acts 2010, No. 579, §1; Acts 2021, No. 336, §1.
