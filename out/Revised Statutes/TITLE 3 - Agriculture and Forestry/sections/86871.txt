§84.  License fee and tax liability
Each association organized under this Part shall pay an annual license fee of ten dollars to the secretary of state, and shall be exempt from all franchise or other license taxes, but not from ad valorem property taxes.  The provisions of this Section shall likewise apply to associations and corporations organized under the laws of the United States for the sole purpose of extension of credit to farmers and farmers' co-operative associations.
Amended by Acts 1966, No. 228, §1.
