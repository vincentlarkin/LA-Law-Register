§2265.  Appraisal

Cattle reacting to the official test for tuberculosis shall be appraised by a representative of the Louisiana Board of Animal Health and, when thus appraised, due consideration shall be given to the breeding value as well as the dairy and meat value of these cattle.

Acts 1950, No. 179, §5; Acts 2008, No. 920, §1, eff. July 14, 2008; Acts 2012, No. 204, §§2, 3(E).
