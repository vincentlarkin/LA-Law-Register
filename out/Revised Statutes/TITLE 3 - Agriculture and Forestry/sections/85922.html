§209.  Loans to non-members

The corporation may enter into and execute all contracts and agreements and loans, as authorized in this Chapter, with a borrower who is not a member of the corporation provided

(1)  that the borrower is a tenant or lessee of a member or the payment of the loan of the borrower has been guaranteed by a member, and

(2)  that the amount of capital stock required by the corporation as part security for the loan is pledged and delivered by the member to the corporation; and in each case the act executed between the corporation and borrower shall state the position of the borrower, who is not a member, and the member lessor or guarantor shall intervene in the act for the purpose of substantiating the averments and making the pledge and pawn and delivery of the stock as required.
