§4015.  Penalties

Any person violating any of the provisions of this subpart or the rules and regulations promulgated by the Commissioner shall be fined not less than one hundred dollars, nor more than five hundred dollars, or imprisoned for not less than thirty days nor more than six months.

Acts 1974, No. 306, §1; Acts 1985, No. 74, §1.
