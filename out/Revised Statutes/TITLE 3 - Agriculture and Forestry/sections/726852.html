§1416.  Cancellation of registration; causes for

Repeated failure of a registrant to meet the guaranteed weight or analysis of a fertilizer, or to fail to make payment of the inspection fee, or to fail to pay penalties assessed shall be considered just cause for cancellation of a manufacturer registration.

Acts 2010, No. 579, §1.
