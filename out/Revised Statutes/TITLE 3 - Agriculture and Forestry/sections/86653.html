§3555.  Exclusion from coverage of Chapter

The provisions of this Chapter shall not apply to any person who purchases one thousand or less bushels of soybeans, wheat, corn, or grain sorghum in any calendar year.

Added by Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 45, §1; Acts 1985, No. 390, §1, eff. July 10, 1985; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8G, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 861, §3.
