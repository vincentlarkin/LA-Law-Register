§927. Louisiana Department of Health; authority

            A. Nothing in this Part shall be construed as eliminating or diminishing the authority of the Louisiana Department of Health to regulate food as provided in Title 40 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.

            B. The Louisiana Department of Health is hereby designated as the state agency responsible for cooperating with the secretary of the United States Department of Health and Human Services regarding provisions of the FDA Food Safety Modernization Act that fall within its authority granted in Title 40 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.

            Acts 2017, No. 327, §1, eff. June 22, 2017.
