§4117.  Construction
The authority of the commissioner to regulate bulk milk and of the board to regulate dairy products moving in interstate commerce shall be construed to be as great as, but not to exceed, the limits imposed by the United States Constitution.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 31, §1; Acts 1985, No. 75, §1.
