§2063.  Severability clause

If any provision in this Part is held invalid or suspended, such invalidity or suspension shall not affect other provisions, and to this end the provisions of this Part are hereby declared severable.

Acts 1995, No. 298, §1; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8K, eff. June 12, 2009.
