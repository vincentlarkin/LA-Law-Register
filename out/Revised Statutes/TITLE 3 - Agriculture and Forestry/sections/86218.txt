§3257.  Reciprocal agreements
The commissioner may waive all or part of the examination requirements for applicants for certification on a reciprocal basis with any other state which has substantially the same requirements for examinations.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 198, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983.
