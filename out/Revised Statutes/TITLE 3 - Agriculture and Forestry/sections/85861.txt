PART V.  NOXIOUS PLANTS
§1791.  Chinese tallow; declaration as a noxious plant; destruction
The Chinese tallow (sapium sebiferum) is hereby recognized as a noxious plant harmful to growth and development of beneficial plants and pasture and may be destroyed wherever found in this state.
Acts 1995, No. 497, §1.
