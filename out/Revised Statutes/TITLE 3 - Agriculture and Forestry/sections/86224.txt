§3276.  Public participation
A.  Citizen complaints concerning the treatment, storage, or disposal of pesticide wastes may be filed with the Department of Agriculture and Forestry at any time during normal working hours.  Complaints shall be in writing and shall be signed by the complainant.
B.  The Department of Agriculture and Forestry shall investigate any citizen complaint involving the treatment, storage, or disposal of pesticide wastes.  The investigation shall be initiated within thirty days of the receipt of the complaint.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 198, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1983; Acts 1986, No. 509, §1; Acts 1990, No. 136, §1.
