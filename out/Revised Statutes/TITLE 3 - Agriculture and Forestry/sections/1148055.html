PART III. TRUTH IN LABELING OF FOOD PRODUCTS

§4741. Short title

            This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Truth in Labeling of Food Products Act".

            Acts 2019, No. 273, §1, eff. Oct. 1, 2020.
