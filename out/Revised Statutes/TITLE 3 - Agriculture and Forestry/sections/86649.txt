CHAPTER 21-D.  LOUISIANA SOYBEAN AND GRAIN RESEARCH
AND PROMOTION BOARD
§3551.  Purposes
The purpose of this Chapter is to promote the growth and development of the soybean, wheat, corn, and grain sorghum industries in Louisiana by research and advertisement, thereby promoting the general welfare of the people of this state.
Added by Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 45, §1; Acts 1985, No. 390, §1, eff. July 10, 1985; Acts 2009, No. 24, §8G, eff. June 12, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 861, §3.
