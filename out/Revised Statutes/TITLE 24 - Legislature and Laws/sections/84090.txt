§31.2.  Expenses of clerk of House and secretary of Senate
A.  The clerk of the House of Representatives shall be paid an expense allowance of five hundred dollars per month for expenses in connection with the operation of his office and for attending to the business thereof between sessions.  The expense allowance shall be withdrawn from the treasury on the warrant of the clerk of the House.
B.  The secretary of the Senate shall be paid an expense allowance of five hundred dollars per month for expenses in connection with the operation of his office and for attending to the business of the Senate between sessions.  The expense allowance shall be withdrawn from the treasury on the warrant of the secretary of the Senate.
Added by Acts 1952, No. 214, §1.  Amended by Acts 1960, No. 349, §1; Acts 1969, No. 11, §7.
