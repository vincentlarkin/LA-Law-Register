§585.  Local auditee database
The  auditor shall establish a schedule to include fiscal information regarding local
auditees on the website.  The schedule and the fiscal information requested from local
auditees shall be subject to the approval of the Legislative Audit Advisory Council.  After
approval, local auditees shall furnish the information requested by the auditor in the format
specified by the auditor.
