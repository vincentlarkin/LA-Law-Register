§172.  Proposed constitutional amendments; printing and publication
Whenever constitutional amendments proposed by the legislature have been submitted to and voted upon by the people, those which have been ratified and adopted in the election shall be printed and published as laws of the State of Louisiana in the next volume of the acts of the legislature printed thereafter.
