§105. Staff support

            The staffs of the Senate, House of Representatives, legislative fiscal office, legislative auditor, office of the governor, and division of administration shall, upon request, provide staff support and otherwise assist the commission as requested by the commission. The commission may submit a written request to the president of the Senate, the speaker of the House of Representatives, the legislative fiscal officer, the legislative auditor, or the commissioner of administration, for specific support and assistance to be provided by the staffs of their respective agencies.

            Acts 2009, No. 491, §1, eff. July 10, 2009; Acts 2020, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 24, §1, eff. July 13, 2020.
