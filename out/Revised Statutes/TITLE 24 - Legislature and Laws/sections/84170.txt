§606.  Assistance and cooperation of other agencies; boards; use of facilities
All of the agencies, boards, commissions and departments of the state and of any of its political subdivisions are hereby directed to assist the office in its work and to furnish such information, reports, aid, services and assistance as may be requested, all without any cost or charge of any nature to the office.  The facilities of the state library and of Louisiana State University shall be made available for use by the office.  It shall be the duty of the attorney general to give assistance to the office and to render his opinion in writing on any subject requested by the legislative fiscal officer.
Added by Acts 1973, No. 169, §1, eff. July 1, 1974.
