§674. State agencies, political subdivisions; assistance to committee

            A. All agencies, boards, commissions, and departments of the state and of any of its political subdivisions, shall assist the committee in its work and furnish such information, reports, aid, services, and assistance as may be requested by the committee.

            B. In order to assist the committee in its study and examination of information technology required by this Chapter, each public entity shall provide to the committee, upon request, any information the committee requests regarding the entity's information technology.

            Acts 2020, No. 301, §1.
