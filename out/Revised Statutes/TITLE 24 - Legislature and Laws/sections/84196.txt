§675.  Powers
The committee shall have the power and authority to hold hearings, subpoena
witnesses, administer oaths, require the production of books and records, and do all other
things necessary to discharge its duties and responsibilities, including the power to punish
for contempt and to initiate the prosecution, in accordance with the laws of this state, of any
individual who refuses to testify or is charged with false swearing or perjury before the
committee.
Acts 2020, No. 301, §1.
