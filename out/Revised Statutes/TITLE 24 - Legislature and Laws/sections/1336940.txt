§992.  Redistricting website
A.  The legislature shall provide for, maintain, and update as necessary a redistricting
website.
B.  At a minimum, the redistricting website shall contain the following information:
(1)  Any documents or material presented or distributed by the legislature at a
redistricting public hearing.
(2)  Any written testimony, materials, or redistricting plans submitted by the public
at a redistricting public hearing or in the manner prescribed by the legislature.
Acts 2023, No. 240, §2, eff. June 9, 2023.
