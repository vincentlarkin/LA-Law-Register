§113. Staff support; agency cooperation and assistance
A. The staff of the Senate Committee on Health and Welfare shall provide support
and assist the commission as requested by the chairman.
B. Each agency and political subdivision shall furnish aid, services, and assistance
as requested by the commission.
Acts 2017, No. 187, §1, eff. June 12, 2017.
