§583.  Performance database
A.  The auditor shall ensure that the website includes a performance database that
is electronically searchable by the public.
B.  The executive branch shall provide information pursuant to this Section.
C.  The performance database shall include the Louisiana Performance
Accountability System, the electronic performance database that tracks performance
standards, interim quarterly performance targets, and actual performance information for
executive branch departments and agencies required under the Louisiana Government
Performance and Accountability Act, R.S. 39:87.1 et seq.
Acts 2023, No. 446, §1, see Act.
