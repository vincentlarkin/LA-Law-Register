§552.  Advise auditor
The council shall meet from time to time as it deems necessary.  The council shall advise and consult with the legislative auditor with respect to all matters arising out of his functions, duties, and responsibilities to the legislature.  The council shall make such recommendations to the legislative auditor and to the legislature as it deems necessary or expedient.
Acts 1978, No. 357, §2.
