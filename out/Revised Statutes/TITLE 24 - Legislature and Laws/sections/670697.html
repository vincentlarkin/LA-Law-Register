§108. Effect on other law

            This Part shall not be deemed to supercede or limit the provisions of Part XII of Chapter 1 of Title 49 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.

            Acts 2009, No. 491, §1, eff. July 10, 2009; Acts 2020, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 24, §1, eff. July 13, 2020.
