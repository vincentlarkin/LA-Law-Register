CHAPTER 9.  FISCAL SERVICES

§601.  Legislative fiscal office; creation; purpose

There is hereby created the legislative fiscal office, hereinafter referred to as the office, which shall have for its purpose the provision of service, research and technical staff assistance concerning fiscal matters of any kind to the members of the legislature and to the committees thereof.

Added by Acts 1973, No. 169, §1, eff. July 1, 1974.
