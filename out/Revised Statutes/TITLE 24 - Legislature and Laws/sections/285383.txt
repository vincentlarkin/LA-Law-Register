CHAPTER 19. LOUISIANA COMMISSION ON CIVIC EDUCATION
§971.  Louisiana Commission on Civic Education; creation; purpose
A.  The Louisiana Commission on Civic Education, hereinafter referred to as "commission", is hereby established.
B.  The purposes of the commission shall include the following:
(1)  To educate citizens in the community as well as students in schools on the importance of citizen involvement in a representative democracy.
(2)  To promote communication and collaboration among organizations in the state that conduct civic education programs.
Acts 2004, No. 596, §1, eff. July 5, 2004; Acts 2005, No. 107, §1, eff. June 21, 2005.
