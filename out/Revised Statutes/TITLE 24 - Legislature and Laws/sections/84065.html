§174.  Distribution of R.S.1950 to members of legislature

The Secretary of State is authorized to furnish to each member of the legislature for the term 1952-1956, except members who were reelected to succeed themselves and have been furnished the Revised Statutes, one set of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, including indices and all supplements.  

Acts 1952, No. 311, §1.
