§106. Agency cooperation and assistance

            A. Each agency and political subdivision shall furnish aid, services, and assistance as may be requested by the commission.

            B. To the extent permitted by and in accordance with R.S. 44:1 et seq., each officer, agency, and political subdivision shall make available all facts, records, information, and data requested by the commission and in all ways cooperate with the commission in carrying out the functions and duties imposed by this Part.

            Acts 2009, No. 491, §1, eff. July 10, 2009; Acts 2020, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 24, §1, eff. July 13, 2020.

NOTE: See R.S. 24:108 and 109 relative to effect and effectiveness of Part.
