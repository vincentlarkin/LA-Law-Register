§205.  Reports; advisory capacity; printing and distribution of reports
The Louisiana State Law Institute, in submitting reports to the legislature shall act
solely in an advisory capacity.  Its reports, studies, and recommended publications shall be
public and available as provided by law.
Acts 2015, No. 383, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
