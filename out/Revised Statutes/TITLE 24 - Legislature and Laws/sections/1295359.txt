§683.  Employees; salaries
The director of capitol security shall make recommendations of employment for all
security officers and other necessary employees and shall recommend salaries to the Capitol
Security Council for approval by the president of the Senate and the speaker of the House of
Representatives.
Acts 2022, No. 507, §1, eff. June 16, 2022.
