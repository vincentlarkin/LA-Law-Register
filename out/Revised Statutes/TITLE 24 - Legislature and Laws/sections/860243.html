§982.  Funding

To achieve the purposes of this Chapter, the Institute for Public Health and Justice may seek, accept, and expend monies from any source, including donations, state appropriations, and federal grants and may seek, accept, and use services from individuals, corporations, and governmental entities.

Acts 2013, No. 3, §1.
