§74.  Quorum and vote requirements
In the event of an attack:
(1)  Quorum requirements for the legislature shall be suspended, and
(2)  Where the affirmative vote of a specified proportion of members for approval of a bill, resolution or other action otherwise would be required, the same proportion of those voting thereon shall be sufficient.
Acts 1963, No. 113, §14.
