§505.  President of the Senate; president pro tempore
The president of the Senate and the president pro tempore of the Senate shall be elected from among the membership of the Senate by the membership thereof in accordance with the rules of the Senate governing the election of the president and president pro tempore.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 605, §1, eff. May 10, 1976.
