PART III.  CODES AND COMPILATIONS
§191.  Repugnance between codes
In case the Code of Practice as enacted in the year 1870, contains any provisions contrary or repugnant to those of the Civil Code as enacted in the year 1870, the latter shall be considered as virtually repealed or thereby amended in that respect.
