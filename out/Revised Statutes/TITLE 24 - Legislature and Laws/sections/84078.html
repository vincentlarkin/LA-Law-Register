CHAPTER 5.  CONTINUOUS REVISION

§251.  Continuous revision under supervision of Louisiana State Law Institute

The Louisiana State Law Institute, as the official advisory law revision commission of the State of Louisiana, shall direct and supervise the continuous revision, clarification and co-ordination of the Louisiana Revised Statutes in a manner not inconsistent with the provisions of this Chapter.
