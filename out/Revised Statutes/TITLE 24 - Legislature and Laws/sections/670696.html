§107. Finances

            A. The commission may apply for, contract for, receive, and expend for purposes of this Part any appropriation or grant from the state, its political subdivisions, the federal government, or any other public or private source.

            B. The books and records of the commission shall be subject to audit by the legislative auditor pursuant to R.S. 24:513.

            Acts 2009, No. 491, §1, eff. July 10, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 861, §10; Acts 2020, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 24, §1, July 13, 2020.

NOTE: See R.S. 24:108 and 109 relative to effect and effectiveness of Part.
