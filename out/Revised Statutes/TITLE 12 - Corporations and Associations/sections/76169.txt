§1112.  Corporate name
The corporate name shall consist of the full or last name or names of one or more voting shareholders, former voting shareholders, or members of a predecessor optometry partnership or firm.  It may include "Limited" or "Ltd.," and shall end with one of the phrases which may be in parenthesis "A Professional Optometry Corporation," "A Professional Corporation," or "An Optometry Corporation." The name need not contain "Incorporated" or "Inc."
Added by Acts 1980, No. 404, §1.
