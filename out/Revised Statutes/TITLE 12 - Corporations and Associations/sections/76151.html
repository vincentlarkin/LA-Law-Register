§1087.  Professional corporation

A corporation formed under Chapter 1 of this Title for the purpose of practicing architecture shall be subject to all of the provisions of Chapter 1, as the same may from time to time be amended, except to the extent that such provisions are inconsistent with the provisions of this Chapter.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 225, §1, eff. July 9, 1979.
