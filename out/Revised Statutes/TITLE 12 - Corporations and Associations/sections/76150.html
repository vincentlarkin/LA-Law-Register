CHAPTER 16.  PROFESSIONAL ARCHITECTURAL

CORPORATIONS

§1086.  Terms defined

As used in this Chapter:

A.  "Professional architectural corporation" means a corporation organized pursuant to this Chapter for the practice of architecture as provided for by R.S. 37:141 through R.S. 37:158, inclusive, or a corporation which is certified to be in compliance therewith by the Louisiana State Board of Architectural Examiners.  

B.  All terms used both in this Chapter and in Chapter 1 of this Title should have the same meaning when used in this Chapter, as when used in Chapter 1.  

Added by Acts 1979, No. 225, §1, eff. July 9, 1979.
