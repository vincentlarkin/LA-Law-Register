PART VII.  DISSOLUTION
§1334.  Dissolution
Except as provided in the articles of organization or a written operating agreement, a limited liability company is dissolved and its affairs shall be wound up upon the first to occur of the following:
(1)  The occurrence of events specified in writing in the articles of organization or operating agreement.
(2)  The consent of its members in accordance with R.S. 12:1318.
(3)  Repealed by Acts 1997, No. 717, §2, eff. July 8, 1997.
(4)  Entry of a decree of judicial dissolution under R.S. 12:1335.
Acts 1992, No. 780, §2, eff. July 7, 1992; Acts 1993, No. 475, §4, eff. June 9, 1993; Acts 1997, No. 717, §2, eff. July 8, 1997.
