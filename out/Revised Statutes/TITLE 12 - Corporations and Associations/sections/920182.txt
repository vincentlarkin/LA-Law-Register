§1-121.  Forms
A.(1)  The secretary of state may prescribe and furnish on request forms for any of
the following:
(a)  An application for a certificate of existence and standing.
(b)  A foreign corporation's application for a certificate of authority to do business
in this state.
(c)  A foreign corporation's application for a certificate of withdrawal.
(d)  The annual report.
(2)  If the secretary of state so requires, use of these forms is mandatory.
B.  The secretary of state may prescribe and furnish forms for other documents
required or permitted to be filed by this Chapter.
Acts 2014, No. 328, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015; Acts 2019, No. 19, §2, eff. May 28, 2019.
