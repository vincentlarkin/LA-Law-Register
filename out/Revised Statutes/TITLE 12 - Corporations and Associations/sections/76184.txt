CHAPTER 18.  PROFESSIONAL PSYCHOLOGY CORPORATIONS
§1130.  Terms defined
As used in this Chapter:
A.  "Professional psychology corporation" means a corporation organized pursuant to this Chapter for the practice of psychology.
B.  All terms used both in this Chapter and in Chapter 1 of this Title shall have the same meaning when used in this Chapter, as when used in Chapter 1.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 523, §1.
