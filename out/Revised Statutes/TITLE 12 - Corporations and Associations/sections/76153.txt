§1089.  Corporate authority
A professional architectural corporation shall engage in the business of the practice of architecture and may engage in any business not in conflict with such business activity.  It may hold, own, lease, or otherwise deal in property for investment or in connection with its architectural practice.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 225, §1, eff. July 9, 1979.
