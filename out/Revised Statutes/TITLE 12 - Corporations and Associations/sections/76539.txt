§444.  Persons who may organize
Ten or more persons, a majority of whom are residents of this state, engaged in the catching, gathering, or the production of seafood products and skins and furs, may form an association, with or without capital stock, under the provisions of this Part.
Renumbered from R.S.1950, §12:354 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969; Acts 1999, No. 341, §1.
