§972.  Savings clause
Nothing herein contained shall be interpreted or construed as disapproving any act, action, or conduct which is or has been or may be in accordance with existing law, prior to amendment, nor shall anything herein contained be interpreted or construed as approving any act, action, or conduct which is or has been or may be in violation of existing law.
Acts 1984, No. 890, §1, eff. July 20, 1984.
