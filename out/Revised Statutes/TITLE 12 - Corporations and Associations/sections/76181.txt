§1123.  Regulation by Louisiana State Board of Optometry
Professional optometry corporations shall be subject to the discipline of the Louisiana State Board of Optometry Examiners and its authority to adopt rules and regulations governing the practice of optometry.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 404, §1.
