§914.  Regulation by Louisiana State Board of Medical Examiners
Professional medical corporations shall be subject to the discipline of the Louisiana State Board of Medical Examiners and to its authority to adopt rules and regulations governing the practice of medicine or podiatry.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 27, §1; Acts 2005, No. 304, §1.
