§429.  Construction

This Part shall be construed liberally.  The enumeration of any object, purpose, power, manner, method, or thing shall not be deemed to exclude like or similar objects, purposes, powers, manners, methods or things.  

Renumbered from R.S.1950, §12:329 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969.
