PART IV.  FINANCE

§1321.  Contributions to capital

The contribution of a member to a limited liability company may take the form of cash, property, services rendered, or a promissory note or other binding obligation to contribute cash or property or to perform services.  

Acts 1992, No. 780, §2, eff. July 7, 1992.
