§417.1.  Corporate records; disclosure; right of members to inspect

A cooperative shall maintain for inspection by any member of the cooperative or his attorney during regular office hours every contract or agreement of a value in an amount in excess of fifteen thousand dollars.  Each person or legal entity which is a party to any such contract or agreement with a cooperative shall submit a sworn statement disclosing any business or personal relationship which exists with a director or officer of that cooperative.  The sworn statement shall be attached to all such contracts or agreements.  

Added by Acts 1983, No. 636, §1, eff. July 19, 1983.
