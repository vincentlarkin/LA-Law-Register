§502.  Supplementary general principles of law

Provisions of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, and other principles of law supplement this Chapter unless displaced by a particular provision of this Chapter.

Renumbered from R.S.1950, §12:402 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969; Acts 1985, No. 907, §1; Acts 2008, No. 877, §1.

NOTE:  Repealed by Acts 1985, No. 270, §1; however, superceded and reenacted by Acts 1985, No. 907, §2.
