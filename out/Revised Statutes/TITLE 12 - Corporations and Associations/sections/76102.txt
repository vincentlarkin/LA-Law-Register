§1013.  The corporate name
The corporate name shall consist of the full or last name or the names of one or more voting shareholders, former voting shareholders, or members of a predecessor accounting firm, may include "limited" or "Ltd.", and shall end with one of the phrases which may be in parenthesis "A Professional Accounting Corporation", "A Professional Corporation", or "A Corporation of Certified Public Accountants".  The name need not contain "Incorporated" or "Inc.".
Added by Acts 1970, No. 191, §1.
