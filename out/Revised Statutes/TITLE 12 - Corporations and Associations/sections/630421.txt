§513.  Involuntary proceedings for dissolution and appointment of liquidator
An involuntary proceeding for dissolution may be instituted against an unincorporated association by a member or by a creditor whose claim has been reduced to judgment, on which execution has been issued and returned "nulla bona".
Acts 2008, No. 877, §1.
