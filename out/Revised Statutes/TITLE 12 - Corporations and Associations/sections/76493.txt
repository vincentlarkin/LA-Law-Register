§318.  Pre-filing procedure
Any document to be filed with the Secretary of State by a foreign corporation may be delivered to the Secretary of State in advance, for filing as of any specified date (and, if specified upon such delivery, as of any given time on such date) within thirty days after the date of delivery.
Acts 1968, No. 105, §1.
