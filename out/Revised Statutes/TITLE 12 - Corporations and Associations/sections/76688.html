§984.  Corporate and limited liability company authority

A professional dental corporation or limited liability company shall engage in no business other than the practice of dentistry, but may hold property for investment or in connection with its dental practice.

Added by Acts 1970, No. 127, §1.  Acts 1995, No. 488, §1.
