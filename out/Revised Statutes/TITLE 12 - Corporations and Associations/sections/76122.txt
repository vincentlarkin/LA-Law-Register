§1053.  The corporate name
The corporate name shall consist of the full or last name or names of one or more voting shareholders, former voting shareholders or members of a predecessor chiropractic partnership, may include "Limited" or "Ltd.", shall end with one of the phrases (which may be in parentheses) "A Professional Chiropractic Corporation", or "A Professional Corporation."
The name need not contain "Incorporated" or "Inc."
Added by Acts 1976, No. 686, §1.
