§1137.  Action of shareholders
Any action by, or requiring the assent of, the shareholders of a
professional psychology corporation may be taken on the affirmative vote of
a majority (or such greater proportion as the articles of incorporation may
specify) in interest of the voting shareholders present or represented at a
meeting duly called and held on due notice at which a quorum is present or
represented.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 523, §1.
