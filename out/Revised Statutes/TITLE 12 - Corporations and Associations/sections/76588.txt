§503.  Territorial application
Immovable and movable property in this state may be acquired, held, encumbered, and transferred by an unincorporated association, whether or not the unincorporated association or a member has any other relationship to this state.
Renumbered from R.S.1950, §12:403 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969; Acts 1985, No. 907, §1; Acts 2008, No. 877, §1.
NOTE:  Repealed by Acts 1985, No. 270, §1; however, superceded and reenacted by Acts 1985, No. 907, §2.
