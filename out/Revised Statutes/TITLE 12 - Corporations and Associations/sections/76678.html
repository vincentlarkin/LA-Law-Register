§967.  State development company; federal definition

Any corporation organized under the provisions of this Chapter shall be a state development company, as defined in the Small Business Investment Act of 1958, Public Law 85-699, 85th Congress,1 or any other similar federal legislation, and shall be authorized to operate on a statewide basis.  

Acts 1969, No. 49, §17.
