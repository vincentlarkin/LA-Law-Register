§988.1.  Action of holders of membership interest

Any action by, or requiring the assent of, the holders of a membership interest in a dental limited liability company may be taken on the affirmative vote of a majority, or such greater proportion as the dental limited liability company operating agreement may specify, in interest of the membership interest holders present or represented at a meeting duly called and held upon due notice, at which a quorum is present or represented.

Acts 1995, No. 488, §1.
