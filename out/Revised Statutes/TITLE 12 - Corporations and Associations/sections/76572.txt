§493.  Name of Part
This Part shall be known and may be referred to by the short title: "Real Estate Investment Trust Law."
Added by Acts 1962, No. 330, §1.  Renumbered from R.S.1950, §12:397 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969.
