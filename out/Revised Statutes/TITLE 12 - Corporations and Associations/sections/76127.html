§1058.  Action of shareholders

Any action by, or requiring the assent of, the shareholders of a professional chiropractic corporation may be taken on the affirmative vote of a majority (or such proportion as the articles of incorporation may specify) in interest of the voting shareholders present or represented at a meeting duly called and held on due notice, at which a quorum is present or represented.  

Added by Acts 1976, No. 686, §1.
