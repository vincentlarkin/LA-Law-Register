PART IX.  MERGER
§1357.  Merger or consolidation
Any one or more domestic limited liability companies may merge or consolidate with or into a domestic limited liability company, partnership in commendam, partnership, or business or nonprofit corporation.  Any one or more domestic limited liability companies, partnerships in commendam, partnerships, or business or nonprofit corporations may merge or consolidate with or into a domestic limited liability company.
Acts 1992, No. 780, §2, eff. July 7, 1992; Acts 1995, No. 847, §3, eff. June 27, 1995.
