§1164.  Regulation by Louisiana Board of Veterinary Medicine

Professional medical corporations shall be subject to the discipline of the Louisiana Board of Veterinary Medicine and to its authority to adopt rules and regulations governing the practice of medicine.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 218, §1; Acts 2012, No. 811, §2, eff. July 1, 2012.
