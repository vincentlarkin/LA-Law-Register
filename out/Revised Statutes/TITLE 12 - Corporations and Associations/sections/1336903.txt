§1867.  Departmental complaint directives; failure to comply; fines; hearing
A.  Any person subject to the regulatory authority of the department who fails to
comply with any directive issued by the department in connection with a consumer complaint
filed pursuant to this Chapter shall be fined an amount not to exceed two hundred fifty
dollars for each occurrence.
B.  Any person against whom a fine has been levied shall be given ten days' notice
of the action. Upon receipt of this notice, the person aggrieved may apply for and may have
an administrative hearing conducted in accordance with the provisions of the Administrative
Procedure Act.
Acts 2023, No. 259, §§1, 3A, eff. June 12, 2023.
