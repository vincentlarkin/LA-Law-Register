§1-1410.  Certain sections in Subpart A applicable to all dissolved corporations
R.S. 12:1-1405 through 1-1409 apply to a dissolved corporation regardless of whether the dissolution is voluntary or judicial.
Acts 2014, No. 328, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
