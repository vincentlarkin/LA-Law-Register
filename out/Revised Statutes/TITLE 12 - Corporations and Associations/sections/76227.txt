§1180.  Compliance by existing corporation
If upon the effective date of this Chapter a previously existing engineering corporation or professional architectural corporation desires to operate as an architectural-engineering corporation, it may do so by compliance with the provisions of this Chapter and shall be entitled but not required to form a separate corporation for that purpose.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 465, §1, eff. July 6, 1983.
