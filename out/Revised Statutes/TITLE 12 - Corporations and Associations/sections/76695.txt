§988.  Action of shareholders
Any action by, or requiring the assent of, the shareholders of a professional dental corporation may be taken on the affirmative vote of a majority, or such greater proportion as the articles of incorporation may specify, in interest of the voting shareholders present or represented at a meeting duly called and held on due notice, at which a quorum is present or represented.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 127, §1.
