§1084.  Regulation by Louisiana State Board of Nursing

Professional nursing corporations shall be subject to the discipline of the Louisiana State Board of Nursing and to its authority to adopt rules and regulations governing the practice of nursing.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 730, §1; Acts 2012, No. 811, §2, eff. July 1, 2012.
