§1094.  Shareholders' agreements
A.  Any lawful provision regulating the affairs of a professional architectural corporation or the rights and liabilities of its shareholders, which is not required to be set forth in the articles of incorporation, may be set forth in an agreement among all of the shareholders.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 225, §1, eff. July 9, 1979.
