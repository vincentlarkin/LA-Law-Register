§812.  Merger and consolidation

Professional law corporations may be merged into or consolidated only with other professional law corporations.  

Added by Acts 1964, No. 354, §1.  Renumbered from R.S. 1950, §12:912 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969.
