PART 17.  TRANSITION PROVISIONS
§1-1701.  Application to existing domestic corporations
This Chapter  applies to all domestic corporations in existence on its effective date that were incorporated under the laws of this state for a purpose or purposes for which a corporation might be formed under this Chapter.
Acts 2014, No. 328, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
