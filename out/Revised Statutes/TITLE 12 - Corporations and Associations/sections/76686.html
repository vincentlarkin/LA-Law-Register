§982.1.  Professional limited liability companies

One or more natural persons of full age and duly licensed to practice dentistry in this state may form a limited liability company under Chapter 22 of this Title for the purpose of practicing dentistry.  Such limited liability company shall be subject to the provisions of Chapter 1 as the same may from time to time be amended, except to the extent that the provisions are inconsistent with provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 1995, No. 488, §1; Acts 2001, No. 476, §1, eff. June 21, 2001.
