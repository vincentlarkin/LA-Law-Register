§1407.  Action of shareholders
Any action by, or requiring the assent of, the shareholders of a professional social work corporation may be taken on the affirmative vote of a majority, or such greater proportion as the articles of incorporation may specify, in interest of the voting shareholders present or represented at a meeting duly called and held on due notice at which a quorum is present or represented.
Acts 1997, No. 1169, §1.
