§815.  Regulation by Supreme Court
Professional law corporations shall be subject to the discipline of the Supreme Court, and to the Supreme Court's authority to adopt rules and regulations governing the practice of law.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 354, §1.  Renumbered from R.S. 1950, §12:915 by Acts 1968, No. 105, §3, eff. Jan. 1, 1969; Acts 1991, No. 225, §1.
