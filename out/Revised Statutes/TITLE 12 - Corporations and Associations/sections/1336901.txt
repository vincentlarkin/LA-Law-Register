§1865.  Authorization of the department to employ investigators
The department may employ investigators to investigate complaints received against
the group self-insurance fund authorized to do business in this state and against any
unauthorized group self-insurance fund that is reported to be operating in this state.
Acts 2023, No. 259, §§1, 3A, eff. June 12, 2023.
