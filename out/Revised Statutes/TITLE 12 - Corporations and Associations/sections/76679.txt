§968.  Tax exemptions, credits and privileges applicable
Any tax exemptions, tax credits, or tax privileges granted to banks, savings and loan associations, trust companies, and other financial institutions by any general laws are granted to corporations organized pursuant to this Chapter.
Acts 1969, No. 49, §18.
