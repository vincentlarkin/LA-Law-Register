§319.  Exemption from provisions of the Insurance Code

Health care sharing ministries shall be exempt from all provisions of the insurance laws of this state unless the ministries are expressly provided for in such provisions or unless the provision is specifically made applicable by this Subpart.

Acts 2014, No. 20, §1.
