§2116.  No charges or discrimination by associations

No charge of any kind shall be imposed, for services rendered by an association in the public interest and, in the efforts to protect and save life and property at and during any fire, no discrimination shall be made between property which is insured and that which is uninsured.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1556 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
