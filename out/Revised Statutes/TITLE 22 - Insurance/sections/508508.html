§1528.  Injunctions

The commissioner may seek temporary and permanent injunctions restraining violations or attempted violations of this Part in the Nineteenth Judicial District Court.

Acts 1989, No. 773, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1990; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2091.8 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1528 redesignated as R.S. 22:718 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
