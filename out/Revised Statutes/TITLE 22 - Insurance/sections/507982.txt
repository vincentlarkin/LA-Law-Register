§1190.  Administrative procedure
Regulations adopted pursuant to this Subpart shall be adopted in accordance with the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 2004, No. 780, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2005; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1740 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
