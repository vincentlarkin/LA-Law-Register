§1660.6.  Examination authority
The commissioner may cause an examination, as prescribed by the provisions of
Chapter 8 of this Title, of any pharmacy services administrative organization when in the
opinion of the commissioner it is necessary for such an examination to be made.
Acts 2021, No. 192, §1.
