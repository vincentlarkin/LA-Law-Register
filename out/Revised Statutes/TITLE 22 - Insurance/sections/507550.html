§767. Schedule of experience

            Any insurer transacting any liability or workers' compensation insurances shall include in its annual statement filed with the commissioner of insurance, a schedule of its experience thereunder in such form as the commissioner of insurance may prescribe.

            Acts 1958, No. 125. Acts 1983, 1st Ex.Sess., No. 1, §6; Redesignated from R.S. 22:900 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
