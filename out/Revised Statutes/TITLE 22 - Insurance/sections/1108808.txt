§2469.  Limitation of liability
An insurance producer shall not be subject to civil liability for any harm or injury that
occurs because of a party's election to receive any notice or document by electronic means
or by an insurer's failure to deliver or a party's failure to receive a notice or document by
electronic means.
Acts 2018, No. 132, §1.
