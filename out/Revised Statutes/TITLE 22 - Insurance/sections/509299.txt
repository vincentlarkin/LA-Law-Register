§2195.   Notice of wrongful conduct; opportunity to show cause
If any person is entitled to a hearing pursuant to any provision of this Code, the
commissioner shall issue a notice of wrongful conduct prior to the taking of any regulatory
action. The notice shall contain allegations of fact describing the wrongful conduct and cite
the provisions of law that the commissioner deems to have been violated. The notice shall
also inform the person of the opportunity to show cause, in a manner specified in the notice,
as to why regulatory action should not be taken.
