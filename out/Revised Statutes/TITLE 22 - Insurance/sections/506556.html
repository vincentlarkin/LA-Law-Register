§348.  Penalties

Any foreign or alien insurer, other than life, which violates any provision of R.S. 342 through 349 shall, for each offense, be fined five hundred dollars.

Acts 1958, No. 125.  Amended by Acts 1958, No. 419, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1586 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:348 redesignated as R.S. 22:208 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
