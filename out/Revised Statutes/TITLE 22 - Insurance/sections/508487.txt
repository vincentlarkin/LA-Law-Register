§1506.  Appeals of underwriting or rates
Insurers using credit information to underwrite or rate risks shall provide a process for the consumer to appeal the underwriting or rating of risks for which credit scoring may be an inappropriate factor.  Nothing in this Section shall be construed to provide a consumer or other insured with a cause of action that does not exist in the absence of this Section.
Acts 2003, No. 1256, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1486 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
