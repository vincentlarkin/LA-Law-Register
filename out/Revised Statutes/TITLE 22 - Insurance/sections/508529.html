§1555.  Liability of producers on binder

The commissioner of insurance may suspend or revoke the license of any producer issuing or purporting to issue any binder on behalf of any insurer for which he is not then authorized to bind.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:632 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
