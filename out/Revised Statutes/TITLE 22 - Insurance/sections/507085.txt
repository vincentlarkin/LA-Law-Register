§467.  Duties of commissioner; rules and regulations
The commissioner shall promulgate such rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act as are necessary to effectuate the provisions and purposes of this Subpart.
Acts 1990, No. 902, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3017 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
