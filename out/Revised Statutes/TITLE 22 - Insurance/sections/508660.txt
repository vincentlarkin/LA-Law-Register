§1655.  Rules and regulations
The commissioner may adopt those rules and regulations as are necessary to implement this Chapter.
Acts 1993, No. 144, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3045 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
