§1260.3.  Registration
No discount medical plan organization shall conduct business or otherwise operate in this state unless it is registered with the commissioner.
Acts 2008, No. 442, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated by Acts 2009, No. 503, §3.
