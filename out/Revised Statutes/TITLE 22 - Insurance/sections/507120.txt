§525.  Title insurance producer; inventory maintenance
The title insurance producer shall maintain an inventory of all numbered policy forms or policy numbers assigned to the producer by the title insurer.
Acts 1999, No. 192, §1, eff. June 9, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.8.5 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
