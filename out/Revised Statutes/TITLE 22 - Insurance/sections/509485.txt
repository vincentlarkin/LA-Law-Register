§550.3.  Applicability of other provisions
A.  The terms and conditions set forth in R.S. 22:96 and Chapter 9 of this Title, R.S.
22:2001 et seq., pertaining to rehabilitation, liquidation, conservation, dissolution, and
administrative supervision, apply to captive insurance companies licensed pursuant to this
Subpart.
B.  The provisions of R.S. 22:72 regarding stock and mutual conversions apply to
captive insurance companies licensed pursuant to this Subpart.
C.  The provisions of acquisition of control or merger with a domestic insurer
provided for in the Insurance Holding Company System Regulatory Law, R.S. 22:691.1 et
seq., apply to captive insurance companies licensed pursuant to this Subpart.
