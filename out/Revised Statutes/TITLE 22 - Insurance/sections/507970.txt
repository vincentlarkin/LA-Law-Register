§1166.  Rulemaking
The commissioner shall promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the
Administrative Procedure Act as necessary to implement the provisions of this Subpart,
including but not limited to definitions of eligible clinical dental services, activities to
improve dental care quality, and administrative and overhead cost expenditures.
Acts 2024, No. 357, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2025.
