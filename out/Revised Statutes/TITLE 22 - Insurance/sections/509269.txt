§2112.  Formation of fire insurance patrol associations
A.  Two-thirds of the fire insurance companies regularly licensed and authorized to do business in this state, may voluntarily organize, in any city of fifty thousand or more population, an association for the purpose of protecting life and property from fire in such cities.  The association shall be known as the fire insurance patrol of the city in which it is organized.
B.  Every fire insurance company regularly licensed and authorized to do business in the city in which the association has its domicile shall be a member of the association and shall have one vote.
