§1767.  Insurance charges
Notwithstanding any other provision of this Part or any rule adopted by the commissioner, a limited licensee pursuant to this Part shall not be required to treat monies collected from renters purchasing such  insurance when renting vehicles as funds received in a fiduciary capacity if the charges for coverage shall be itemized and be ancillary to a rental transaction.  The sale of insurance not in conjunction with a rental transaction is prohibited by the provisions of this Part.
