§550.26.  Conflict of interest
Each captive insurance company shall adopt a conflict of interest policy whereby
officers, directors, and key personnel annually file a conflict of interest disclosure statement
with the board of directors.
Acts 2008, No. 403, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated by Acts 2009, No. 503, §3;
Acts 2025, No. 313, §2.
