SUBPART F. HEALTHCARE COVERAGE FOR LOUISIANA

FAMILIES PROTECTION ACT

§1121. Short title

            This Subpart shall be known and may be cited as the "Healthcare Coverage for Louisiana Families Protection Act".

            Acts 2019, No. 412, §1, eff. June 11, 2019.
