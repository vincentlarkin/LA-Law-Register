§1260.38.  Rules and regulations
The commissioner of insurance shall promulgate all rules and regulations which are necessary and proper to carry out the provisions of this Subpart.  All rules and regulations promulgated pursuant to this Subpart shall be in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 2014, No. 174, §1.
