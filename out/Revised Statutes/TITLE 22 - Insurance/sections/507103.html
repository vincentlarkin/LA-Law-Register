§496.  Rules and regulations

The commissioner may establish and, from time to time, amend such rules relating to risk retention groups as may be necessary or desirable to carry out the provisions of this Subpart.

Acts 1987, No. 462, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1987; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2084 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
