§1771.  Applicability of this Part
All limited licensees under this Part shall be subject to all other applicable provisions of this Title unless specifically exempted by this Part.
Acts 1999, No. 1278, §1, eff. July 12, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2111 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
