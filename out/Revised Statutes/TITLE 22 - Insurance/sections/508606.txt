§1626.  Examination authority; account reports; financial examinations; required notices to
department
A.  The acts of the MGA are considered to be the acts of the insurer on whose behalf
it is acting.  An MGA may be examined as if it were the insurer.
B.  As the commissioner considers necessary, an MGA shall submit to an
examination by the commissioner of the MGA's financial condition.
C.  The MGA shall pay the examination expenses in an amount that the
commissioner certifies as just and reasonable.
