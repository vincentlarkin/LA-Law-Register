§147.  Policies under standard non-forfeiture law excepted
The provisions of R.S. 22:146 shall not apply to any policy issued by any industrial insurer under the provisions of R.S. 22:936.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:257 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
