CHAPTER 11.  COMMISSIONS AND ASSOCIATIONS

PART I.  FIRE INSURANCE PATROL ASSOCIATIONS

§2111.  Definitions

For purposes of this Part, the term "association" means a fire insurance patrol association organized under this Part, except where the context clearly indicates otherwise.
