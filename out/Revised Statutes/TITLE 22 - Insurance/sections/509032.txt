SUBPART D. HEALTH CARE CONSUMER BILLING
AND DISCLOSURE PROTECTION ACT
§1871.  Short title
This Subpart shall be known and may be cited as the "Health Care Consumer  Billing  and Disclosure Protection Act".
Acts 2003, No. 1157, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004; Redesignated from R.S. 22:250.41 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
