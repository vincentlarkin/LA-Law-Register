§2044.  Uniformity of interpretation
This Uniform Insurers Liquidation Law, R.S. 22:2038 through 2044, shall be so interpreted and construed as to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law of those states that enact it.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:763 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2012, No. 271, §1.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:2044 redesignated as R.S. 22:404 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
