§388.1.  Filing of contracts
No property residual value insurance policy or application form, where written application is required and is to be attached to the policy, or any rider or endorsement of such a contract shall be issued, delivered, or used unless it has been filed with the commissioner of insurance.  Each submission shall be accompanied by the fees provided for in R.S. 22:821.
Acts 2008, No. 883, §1; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
