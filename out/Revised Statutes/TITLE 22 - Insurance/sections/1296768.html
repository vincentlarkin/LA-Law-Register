CHAPTER 22. HURRICANE PROPERTY INSURANCE CLAIM

ALTERNATE DISPUTE RESOLUTION PROGRAM

§2651. Short title

            This Chapter shall be known as the "Hurricane Mediation Program", hereinafter referred to as the "program".

            Acts 2022, No. 591, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2023.
