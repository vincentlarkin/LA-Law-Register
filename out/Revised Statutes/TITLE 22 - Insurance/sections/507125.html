§530.  Title insurer; agency licensing and errors and omissions insurance requirements

The title insurer shall have on file evidence that each appointed title insurance producer is licensed by the state and maintains the errors and omissions insurance required by this Subpart.

Acts 1999, No. 192, §1, eff. June 9, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.9.4 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
