PART III.  LOUISIANA BASIC HEALTH
INSURANCE  PILOT PROGRAMS
§2241.  Louisiana Basic Health Insurance; title
This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Basic Health Insurance", and any program created under R.S. 22:2243 shall be referred to as LaChoice.
Acts 1993, No. 660, §1; Acts 1999, No. 294, §1; Acts 2004, No. 751, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:244 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
