§2346.  Annual report
The governing committee of the plan shall file in the office of the commissioner of insurance each year a statement which shall summarize the transactions, conditions, operations, and affairs of the plan during the preceding fiscal year ending September thirtieth.
Acts 2004, No. 761, §1, eff. July 6, 2004; Acts 2007, No. 459, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 2008; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1441.25 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
