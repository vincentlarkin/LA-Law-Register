§1732.  Rules and regulations

The commissioner may adopt reasonable rules and regulations for the implementation and administration of the provisions of this Part.

Acts 1992, No. 811, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.31 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1732 redesignated as R.S. 22:1182 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
