§2117.  Right of way enroute to fire

The officers and men of a fire insurance patrol association, with all of their apparatus, have the same right of way enroute to a fire as the fire department of the city and any violation of the street rights of associations shall be punished by the city in the same manner as a violation of the rights of the fire departments.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1557 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
