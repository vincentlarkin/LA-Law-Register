§1340.  Applicant or insured disclosure requirement; group homes
Upon application for a new homeowners' insurance policy or at any time for an
insured that has a homeowners' insurance policy in effect, the applicant or insured shall
disclose to the insurer that the property is to be used or is being used as a group home.
Failure to disclose this information to an insurer provides the insurer cause to cancel or
nonrenew the homeowners' insurance policy pursuant to R.S. 22:1333(B).
Acts 2024, No. 397, §1, eff. May 28, 2024.
