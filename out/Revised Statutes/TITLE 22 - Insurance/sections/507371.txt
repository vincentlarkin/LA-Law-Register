§660.  Unauthorized insurers
It shall be unlawful for a domestic insurer to assume reinsurance from another insurer on lines of insurance such domestic insurer is not authorized to write in the state of Louisiana.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 316, §1; Acts 1995, No. 1182, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:946 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:660 redesignated as R.S. 22:1442 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
