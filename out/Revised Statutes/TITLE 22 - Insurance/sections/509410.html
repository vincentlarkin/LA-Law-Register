§2309.  Operation of the Coastal and FAIR Plans; requirements

The governing board of the corporation shall provide property insurance pursuant to this Chapter which shall include any property easily accessible by road for emergency vehicles.

Acts 2004, No. 665, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1430.18 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
