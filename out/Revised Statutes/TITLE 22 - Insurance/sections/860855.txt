§2442.  Hold harmless for external review procedures
No independent review organization or clinical peer working on behalf of an independent review organization or an employee, agent, or contractor of an independent review organization shall be liable in damages to any person for any opinions rendered or acts or omissions performed within the scope of the organization's or person's duties under the law during or upon completion of an external review conducted pursuant to this Part, unless the opinion was rendered or act or omission was performed in bad faith or involved negligence or gross negligence.
Acts 2013, No. 326, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
