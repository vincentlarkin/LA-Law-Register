§2511.  Defenses
A licensee that satisfies the provisions of this Chapter may assert an affirmative
defense to any cause of action arising in tort that is brought pursuant to the laws of this state
or in the courts of this state and that alleges that the failure to implement reasonable
information security controls resulted in a data breach concerning nonpublic information.
Acts 2020, No. 283, §1.
