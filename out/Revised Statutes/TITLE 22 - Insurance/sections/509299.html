§2195. Notice of wrongful conduct; opportunity to show cause

            If any person is entitled to a hearing pursuant to any provision of this Code, the commissioner shall issue a notice of wrongful conduct prior to the taking of any regulatory action. The notice shall contain allegations of fact describing the wrongful conduct and cite the provisions of law that the commissioner deems to have been violated. The notice shall also inform the person of the opportunity to show cause, in a manner specified in the notice, as to why regulatory action should not be taken.

            Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1355 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2015, No. 15, §1, eff. May 26, 2015.
