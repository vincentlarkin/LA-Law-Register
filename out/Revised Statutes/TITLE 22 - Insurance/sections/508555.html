§1583.  Licensing and fees

Except as provided by the regulations authorized in this Subpart, bail enforcement agents shall be subject to the same licensing and fee requirements as bail bond producers.

Acts 1999, No. 1037, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1514.2 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1583 redesignated as R.S. 22:345 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
