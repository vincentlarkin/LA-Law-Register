§185.  Application of other sections
Except as otherwise provided in this Subpart, every reciprocal insurer shall be subject to other applicable provisions of this Code.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:456 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
