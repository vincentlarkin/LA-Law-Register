§368.  Contracts not in compliance
Any vehicle mechanical breakdown insurance policy issued in violation of any provisions of this Subpart shall be an enforceable and valid contract unless otherwise invalid.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 520, §1, eff. July 12, 1978; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1807 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
