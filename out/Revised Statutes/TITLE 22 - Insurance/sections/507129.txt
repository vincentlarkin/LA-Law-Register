§534.  Louisiana Insurance Code; applicability to title insurers, title insurance producers
All title insurers and title insurance producers shall be subject to all other applicable provisions of this Title unless specifically exempted by this Subpart.
Acts 1997, No. 1427, §1; Acts 1999, No. 192, §1, eff. June 9, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.13 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
