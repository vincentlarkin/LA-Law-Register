§909.  Life insurance; prepayment of certain benefits

Rules and regulations concerning accelerated benefits relating to life insurance as provided in R.S. 22:47(1) shall be promulgated by the commissioner.

Acts 1991, No. 909, §1; Acts 1999, No. 1312, §1, eff. July 12, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:644 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
