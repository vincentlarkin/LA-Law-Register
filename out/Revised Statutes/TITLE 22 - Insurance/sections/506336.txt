SUBPART F.  RECIPROCAL INSURERS
§161.  Scope of Subpart
A.  This Subpart shall apply to all reciprocal insurers organized or authorized to transact business in this state.
B.  As used in this Subpart, "subscriber" means the participant or policyholder; "attorney-in-fact" means the representative of the subscribers through whom reciprocal insurance is exchanged; and "reciprocal insurer" means the organization or group of all the subscribers.
