SUBPART D.  FINANCIAL INSTITUTION SALES
§1591.  Short title
This Subpart may be cited as the "Financial Institution Insurance Sales Law".
Acts 1997, No. 1475, §2, eff. July 15, 1997; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3051 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
