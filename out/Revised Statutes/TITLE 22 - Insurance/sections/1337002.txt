§919.  Insurance producers; standards; sale of annuities; rulemaking
The commissioner shall promulgate and adopt rules, in accordance with the
Administrative Procedure Act, to govern the standards which insurance producers shall
follow in the sale of annuity products.
Acts 2023, No. 73, §1.
