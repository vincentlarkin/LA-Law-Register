§1768.  Representations
No limited licensee pursuant to this Part shall advertise, represent, or otherwise hold itself or any of its employees or agents out as licensed insurers or insurance producers.
Acts 1999, No. 1278, §1, eff. July 12, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2108 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
