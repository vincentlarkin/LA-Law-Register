PART V.  PUBLIC ADJUSTERS

§1691.  Purpose

This Part governs the qualifications and procedures for the licensing of public adjusters.  It specifies the duties of and restrictions on public adjusters, which include limiting their licensure to assisting insureds in first-party claims in a manner which avoids the unauthorized practice of law as defined in R.S. 37:212 and 213.

Acts 2006, No. 806, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.91 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
