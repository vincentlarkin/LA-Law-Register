§312.  Injunction

No application or petition for injunction against any domestic, foreign, or alien society, or lodge thereof, shall be recognized in any court of this state unless made by the commissioner of insurance.

Acts 1958, No. 125.  Acts 1987, No. 470, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Redesignated from R.S. 22:572 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
