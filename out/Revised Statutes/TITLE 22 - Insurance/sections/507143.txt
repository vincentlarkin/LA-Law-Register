PART II.  BUSINESS TRANSACTED WITH
PRODUCER CONTROLLED INSURER LAW
§551.  Title
This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Business Transacted with Producer Controlled Insurer Law".
Acts 1992, No. 811, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.1 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:551 redesignated as R.S. 22:291 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
