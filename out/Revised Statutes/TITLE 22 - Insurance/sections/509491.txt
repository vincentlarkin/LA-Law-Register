§550.9.  Change of information submitted with application
A captive insurance company shall notify the commissioner of any change to the plan
of operation or other information submitted with the application within thirty days of the
adoption of the change.
Acts 2008, No. 403, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated by Acts 2009, No. 503, §3;
Acts 2025, No. 313, §2.
