§315.  Review
All decisions and findings of the commissioner of insurance made under the provisions of this Subpart shall be subject to review in any court of competent jurisdiction of this state.
Acts 1987, No. 470, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988; Redesignated from R.S. 22:576 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
