§2367. Authorized insurers

            Although a surplus lines insurer may apply for a grant, the insurer shall obtain a certificate of authority to do business in Louisiana before it may receive the grant funding. The commissioner may reallocate funds allocated to a surplus lines insurer if it does not apply on a timely basis, as specified in the regulations, or is not approved for a certificate of authority.

            Acts 2007, No. 447, §1, eff. July 11, 2007; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3307 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2022, No. 754, §1.
