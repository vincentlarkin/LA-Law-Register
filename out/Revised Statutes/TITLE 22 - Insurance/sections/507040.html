§414.  Coinsurance and reinsurance

Authorized trust funds may insure or reinsure their obligations and liabilities with insurance companies authorized to do business in Louisiana or with companies similarly authorized in any other state of the United States.

Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2054 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
