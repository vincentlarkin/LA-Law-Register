§135.  Incorporation of service insurers prohibited
No domestic service insurance company may be organized and no alien or foreign service insurer may be qualified by this Subpart to do business in this state after twelve o'clock noon of August 1, 1964.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 151, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:295 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
