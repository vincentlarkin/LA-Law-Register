§334.  Trust deposit resolution
An alien insurer shall file with the commissioner of insurance a certified copy of the resolution of its governing board by which the trust deposit was established, together with a certified copy of any trust agreement under which the deposit is held.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:984 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:334 redesignated as R.S. 22:194 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
