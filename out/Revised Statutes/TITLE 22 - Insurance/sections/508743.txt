§1749.  Enforcement
The provisions of this Part may be enforced by the commissioner pursuant to R.S. 22:1967 through 1970 and other applicable provisions of this Title.
Acts 2001, No. 1150, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.59 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
