§1300.8. Contribution against indemnification

            A motor vehicle insurer that defends or indemnifies a claim against a shared vehicle that is excluded under the terms of its policy shall have the right to seek contribution against the motor vehicle insurer of the peer-to-peer car sharing program if the claim is both of the following:

            (1) Made against the shared vehicle owner or the shared vehicle driver for loss or injury that occurs during the car sharing period.

            (2) Excluded under the terms of its policy.

            Acts 2020, No. 277, §1.
