§1132. Definitions

            As used in this Subpart, the following definitions apply:

            (1) "Commissioner" means the commissioner of insurance.

            (2) "Program" means the Louisiana Guaranteed Benefits Pool.

            Acts 2019, No. 412, §1, eff. June 11, 2019.
