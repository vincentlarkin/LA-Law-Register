§1807.3. Enforcement

            A. The commissioner may take any actions necessary or appropriate to enforce the provisions of this Part and the commissioner's regulations. The commissioner may order a person to cease and desist from committing violations of this Part or the commissioner's regulations, issue an order prohibiting a person from acting as an appraiser or umpire, and rescind the registration of any person who violates this Part or the commissioner's regulations or orders.

            B. A person aggrieved by a decision, act, or order of the commissioner pursuant to the provisions of this Part may demand a hearing in accordance with Chapter 12 of this Title, R.S. 22:2191 et seq.

            Acts 2012, No. 96, §1; Acts 2024, No. 625, §1.
