§916.  Inapplicability to reinsurance

R.S. 22:931, 942, and 951 shall not apply to contracts of reinsurance.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:181 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
