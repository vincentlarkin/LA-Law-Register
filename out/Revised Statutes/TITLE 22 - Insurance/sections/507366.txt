§658.  Certificate of fees and commissions paid
Whenever a reinsurance agreement is submitted to the commissioner of insurance for his approval, there shall be filed with him a certificate with regard to fees, commissions and other compensations or valuable considerations paid or to be paid to any person directly or indirectly in connection with the agreement.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:944 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:658 redesignated as R.S. 22:1892 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
