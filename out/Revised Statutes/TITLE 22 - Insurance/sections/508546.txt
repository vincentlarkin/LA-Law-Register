§1565.  Other laws
All laws and parts of laws of this state inconsistent with this Subpart are hereby superseded with respect to matters covered by this Subpart.
Acts 2001, No. 158, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2002; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1151 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
