SUBPART P.  USE OF CREDIT INFORMATION

§1501.  Purpose

The purpose of this Subpart is to regulate the use of credit information for personal insurance so that consumers are afforded certain protections with respect to the use of such information.

Acts 2003, No. 1256, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1481 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
