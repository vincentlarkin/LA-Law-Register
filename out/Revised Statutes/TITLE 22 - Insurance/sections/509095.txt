§1988.  Failure to pay expenses; penalty
Should any insurer fail or refuse to pay the expenses of examination as billed by the commissioner of insurance after fifteen days upon receipt of such billing or after final judgment where a rule has been taken as provided in R.S. 22:1987, then the commissioner of insurance may revoke the certificate of authority of such insurer to do business in this state until the full amount of the bill is paid.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1307 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2012, No. 271, §1.
