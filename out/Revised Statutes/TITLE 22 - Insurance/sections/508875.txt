§1803.  Unfair trade practices
A violation of this Part shall be considered an unfair trade practice under Part Part IV of Chapter 7 of this Title subject to the penalties contained in that Part.
Acts 2003, No. 343, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:199 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1803 redesignated as R.S. 22:364 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
