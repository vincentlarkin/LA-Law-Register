§118.  Impairment of surplus; how made good
Any impairment in the initial minimum surplus required of a mutual insurer may be made good by funds obtained in accordance with R.S. 22:116(2), provided the transaction is submitted to and approved by the commissioner of insurance.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:126 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
