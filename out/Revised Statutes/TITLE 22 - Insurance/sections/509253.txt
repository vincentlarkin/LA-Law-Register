§2095.  Tax exemption
The association shall be exempt from payment of all fees and all taxes levied by this state or any of its subdivisions except taxes levied on immovable property.
Acts 1991, No. 998, §1, eff. Sept. 30, 1991; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1395.15 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
