§2465. Contracts and policies not affected

            The legal effectiveness, validity, or enforceability of any contract or policy of insurance executed by a party shall not be denied solely because of the failure of the insurer to obtain electronic consent or confirmation of consent of the party in accordance with the provisions of this Chapter.

            Acts 2018, No. 132, §1.
