SUBPART D. RESERVES FOR HEALTH INSURANCE

§786. Minimum standard for accident and health insurance contracts

            For accident and health insurance contracts issued on or after the operative date of the valuation manual, the standard prescribed in the valuation manual is the minimum standard of valuation required under R.S. 22:751(A). For health and accident insurance contracts issued on or after July 1, 1948, and prior to the operative date of the valuation manual, the minimum standard of valuation is the standard adopted by the commissioner by regulation.

            Acts 2021, No. 370, §1.
