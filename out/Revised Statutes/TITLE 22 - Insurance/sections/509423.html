§2325.  Board of directors of the plan

The governing committee of the plan shall be a board of directors which shall consist of seven representatives of participants in the plan.

Acts 2004, No. 761, §1, eff. July 6, 2004; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1441.4 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
