§2120.  Revocation of license of delinquent members; reinstatement

The commissioner of insurance shall revoke the license of any fire insurance company, association, or underwriter who fails to comply with his demands as provided in R.S. 22:2119 after fifteen days' written notice to do so and during such interval of fifteen days, the fire insurance company, association, or underwriter may urge any valid or legal reason why its license should not be revoked.  The commissioner of insurance shall, at the expiration of the fifteen days, determine whether or not the licenses shall be revoked, and, if he decides in the affirmative, the revocation shall be self-operative.  Reinstatements may be made at the discretion of the commissioner of insurance, provided the demands authorized to be made by him in R.S. 22:2119 have first been complied with.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1560 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
