§21.  Agreement on producers' compensation prohibited

No insurer shall enter into any combination or agreement with another insurer to prevent its legally authorized producers and representatives in this state from accepting a higher compensation than that paid by any party or parties to such agreement.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1452 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:21 redesignated as R.S. 22:2221 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
