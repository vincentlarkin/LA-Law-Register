§180.  Annual statements
The annual statement of a reciprocal insurer shall be made and filed by the attorney-in-fact.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:451 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:180 redesignated as R.S. 22:903 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
