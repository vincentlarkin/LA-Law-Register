PART V.  COLLISION DAMAGE WAIVER LAW

§1521.  Title of Part

This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Collision Damage Waiver Law".

Acts 1989, No. 773, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1990; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2091.1 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1521 redesignated as R.S. 22:915 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
