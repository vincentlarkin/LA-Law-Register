§1514.  Applicability
This Subpart shall apply to personal insurance policies either written to be effective or renewed on or after nine months from August 15, 2003.
Acts 2003, No. 1256, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1494 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1514 redesignated as R.S. 22:1581 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
