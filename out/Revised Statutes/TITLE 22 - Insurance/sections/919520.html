§1260.36.  Waiver prohibited

The provisions of this Subpart cannot be waived by contract.  Any contractual arrangements in conflict with the provisions of this Subpart or that purport to waive any requirements of this Subpart are null and void.

Acts 2014, No. 174, §1.
