§529.  Title insurer; inventory maintenance
The title insurer shall maintain an inventory of all numbered policy forms or policy numbers allocated to each title insurance producer.
Acts 1999, No. 192, §1, eff. June 9, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.9.3 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
