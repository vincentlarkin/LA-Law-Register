§419.  Trust funds not insurance

A trust fund established under this Subpart shall not be considered an insurance company or to be in the business of insurance, nor shall it be subject to regulation by the commissioner, except as provided for in this Subpart and in R.S. 22:977 and 978.

Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Acts 1991, No. 933, §1; Acts 2006, No. 396, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2059 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
