§2208.  Administrative hearings

As provided in Chapter 13-B of Title 49 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, R.S. 49:991 et seq., the division of administrative law shall conduct any hearings required by any provision of this Chapter.

Acts 2003, No. 933, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1368 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 317, §1; Acts 2012, No. 271, §1.
