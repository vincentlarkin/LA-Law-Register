§2032.  Publication and transmittal of assessment order

The commissioner of insurance shall cause a notice of such assessment order setting forth a brief summary of the contents of such order to be both:

(1)  Published in such manner as shall be directed by the court.

(2)  Enclosed in a sealed envelope, addressed and mailed postage prepaid to each member or subscriber liable thereunder at his last known address as it appears on the records of the insurer, at least twenty days before the return day of the order to show cause provided for in R.S. 22:2031.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:753 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2012, No. 271, §1.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:2032 redesignated as R.S. 22:1165 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
