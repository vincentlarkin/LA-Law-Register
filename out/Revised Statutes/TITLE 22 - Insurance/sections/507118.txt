§523.  Title insurance producer; claims
It shall be the duty of the title insurance producer to immediately report and forward to the title insurer all claims reported to the producer by policyholders or other persons.
Acts 1999, No. 192, §1, eff. June 9, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.8.3 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
