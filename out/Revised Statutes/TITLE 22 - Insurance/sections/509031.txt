§1862.  Applicability
The provisions of Subpart B of Part II of this Chapter, R.S. 22:1831 et seq., shall not apply to this Subpart.
Acts 2004, No. 876, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2005; Redesignated from R.S. 22:250.62 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
