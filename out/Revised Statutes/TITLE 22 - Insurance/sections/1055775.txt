§41.2.  Submission of contact information
A.  Every person licensed by the commissioner shall provide accurate contact
information to the commissioner and shall annually inform the commissioner by electronic
means of the name, mailing address, phone number, and electronic mail address of each
individual responsible for each of the following:
(1)  Receipt of and response to consumer complaints.
(2)  Receipt of rules, regulations, and other directives from the commissioner.
(3)  Any other information that the commissioner deems necessary to the exercise of
his authority.
B.  In addition to the requirements of Subsection A of this Section, every authorized
risk-bearing entity shall provide accurate contact information to the commissioner and shall
annually inform the commissioner by electronic means of the name, mailing address, phone
number, and electronic mail address of each individual responsible for each of the following:
(1)  Receipt and filing of inquiries regarding the financial condition of the entity.
(2)  Receipt and filing of inquiries regarding tax payments.
C.  Every person licensed by the commissioner and every authorized risk-bearing
entity shall inform the commissioner within thirty days of any change in the information
required to be submitted pursuant to this Section.
Acts 2017, No. 10, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2018; Acts 2024, No. 614, §1.
