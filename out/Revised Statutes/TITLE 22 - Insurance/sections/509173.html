§2054. Construction

This Part shall be construed to effect its purpose under R.S. 22:2052, which shall constitute an aid and guide to interpretation.

Added by Acts 1970, No. 81, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1378 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 959, §1, eff. July 6, 2010.

NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:2054 redesignated as R.S. 22:414 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
