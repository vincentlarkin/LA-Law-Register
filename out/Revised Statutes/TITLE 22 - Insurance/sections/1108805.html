§2466. Withdrawal of consent

            A. A withdrawal of consent by a party shall not affect the legal effectiveness, validity, or enforceability of a notice or document delivered by electronic means to the party before the withdrawal of consent is effective.

            B. A withdrawal of consent by a party shall be effective within a reasonable period of time after receipt of the withdrawal by the insurer.

            C. Failure by an insurer to comply with any provision of R.S. 22:2462 or 2463 may be treated, at the election of the party, as a withdrawal of consent for purposes of this Chapter.

            Acts 2018, No. 132, §1.
