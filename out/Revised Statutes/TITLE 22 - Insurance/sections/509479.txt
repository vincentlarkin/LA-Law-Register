§1260.8.  Notice of change in name or address
Each discount medical plan organization shall provide the commissioner at least thirty days advance notice of any change in the discount medical plan organization's name, address, principal business address, mailing address or Internet website address.
Acts 2008, No. 442, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated by Acts 2009, No. 503, §3.
