§553. Applicability

            This Part shall apply to licensed insurers as defined in R.S. 22:552, either domiciled in this state or domiciled in a state that is not an accredited state having in effect a law substantially similar to this Part. All provisions of the Insurance Holding Company System Regulatory Law, R.S. 22:691.1 et seq., to the extent they are not superseded by this Part, shall continue to apply to all parties within holding company systems subject to this Part.

            Acts 1992, No. 811, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.3 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1; Acts 2022, No. 161, §1.

NOTE: Former R.S. 22:553 redesignated as R.S. 22:293 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
