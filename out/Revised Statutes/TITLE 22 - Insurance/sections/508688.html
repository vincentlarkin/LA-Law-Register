§1678.  Regulations

The commissioner of insurance may adopt reasonable rules and regulations as are necessary or proper to carry out the purposes of this Part.

Acts 2006, No. 783, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.87 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
