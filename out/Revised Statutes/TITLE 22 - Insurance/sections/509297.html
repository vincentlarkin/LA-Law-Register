§2193.  Hearing place

All division of administrative law hearings shall be held at the place designated by the division of administrative law and in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, R.S. 49:950 et seq.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1353 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 317, §1.
