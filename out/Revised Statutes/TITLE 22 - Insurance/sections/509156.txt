§2037.  Payments to Insurance Guaranty Association
The commissioner of insurance shall make periodic partial payments to the Insurance Guaranty Association.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 83, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:756.2 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
