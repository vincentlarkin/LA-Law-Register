§1092.1.  Grandfathered health coverage; rating practices

The rating practices and rating methods and the rating restrictions imposed by law upon grandfathered health coverage in the individual market and small group market that are in effect on the day that this Section takes effect, including the restrictions on rate increases and required notices for such increases, shall remain binding upon such grandfathered health coverage.  Such grandfathered coverage is exempt from the provisions of this Subpart, unless specifically provided for otherwise.

Acts 2014, No. 718, §1, eff. June 18, 2014.
