SUBPART F.  AUDITED FINANCIAL REPORTING
§671.  Title
This Subpart shall be known and may be cited as the "Audited Financial Reports Law".
Acts 1990, No. 264, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1321 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:671 redesignated as R.S. 22:1291 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
