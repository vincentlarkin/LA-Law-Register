§494.  Duty on producers to obtain license

Any person acting, or offering to act, as a producer for a risk retention group or purchasing group which solicits members, sells insurance coverage, purchases coverage for its members located within the state, or otherwise does business in this state shall obtain a license from the commissioner pursuant to R.S. 22:1541 through 1554 and 1556 through 1565.
