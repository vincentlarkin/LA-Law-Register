PART VII.  PROFESSIONAL EMPLOYER ORGANIZATIONS
§1741.  Purpose
The purpose of this Part is to provide for professional employment services by defining such services, requiring registration of persons providing such services, providing for employee benefits plans and workers' compensation coverage for participants of such services, and providing for enforcement of this Part.
Acts 2001, No. 1150, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.51 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:1741 redesignated as R.S. 22:1191 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
