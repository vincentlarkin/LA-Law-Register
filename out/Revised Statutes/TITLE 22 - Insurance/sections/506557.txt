§349.  Fire insurance associations not incorporated under laws of Louisiana
No fire insurance association not incorporated under the laws of this state shall carry on any fire insurance business in this state, save and except upon compliance with the conditions imposed in R.S. 22:342 through 348 as well as all other conditions which may be now or hereafter imposed by law.
Acts 1958, No. 125.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 218, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1977; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1587 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
NOTE:  Former R.S. 22:349 redesignated as R.S. 22:209 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
