§691.54.  Rules and regulations
Pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act and this Title, the commissioner may
promulgate rules, regulations, and orders necessary to carry out the provisions of this
Subpart.
Acts 2015, No. 304, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
