§2332.  Reports of inspection

All reports of inspection performed by or on behalf of the governing committee of the plan shall be made available to the participants in the plan and the commissioner of insurance.  An applicant or his representative shall be entitled to a copy of any inspection report on property in which the applicant has an insurable interest.

Acts 2004, No. 761, §1, eff. July 6, 2004; Acts 2007, No. 459, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 2008; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1441.11 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
