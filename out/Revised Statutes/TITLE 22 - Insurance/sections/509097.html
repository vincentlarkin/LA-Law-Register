§1990.  Production of books and records

Every insurer being examined, its officers, employees and representatives, shall produce and make freely accessible to the commissioner of insurance the accounts, records, documents and files in its possession or control relating to the subject of the examination, and shall otherwise facilitate the examination.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1309 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
