§408.  Pooling prohibited
No trust fund shall enter into an agreement with any other trust fund whereby the risks assumed by each are pooled or shared.
Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2048 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
