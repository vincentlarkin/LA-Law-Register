§388.  Contracts not in compliance
Any property residual value insurance policy issued in violation of any provisions of this Subpart shall be an enforceable and valid contract unless otherwise invalid.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 472, §1, eff. July 19, 1981; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1907 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
