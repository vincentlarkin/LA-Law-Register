§765.  Loss records
Every insurer shall maintain a complete and itemized record showing all losses and claims as to which it has received notice, and all notices received of the occurrence of any event which may result in a loss.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:894 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
