SUBPART A-3. PROTECTING PATIENT ACCESS TO
PHYSICIAN-ADMINISTERED MEDICATIONS
§1020.51. Purpose and intent
The purpose and intent of this Subpart is to ensure patient access to
physician-administered drugs and related services furnished to persons covered under a
health insurance contract. This Subpart shall ensure that health insurance issuers do not
interfere with patients' freedom of choice with respect to providers furnishing
physician-administered drugs and ensure that patients receive safe and effective drug
therapies.
Acts 2021, No. 50, §1, eff. June 1, 2021.
