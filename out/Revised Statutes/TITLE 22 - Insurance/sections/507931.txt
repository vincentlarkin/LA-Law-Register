§1129.  Conflict of laws
In case of any conflict between the provisions of this Subpart and any other provision
of law, the provisions of this Subpart shall control unless application of this Subpart results
in a reduction in coverage for any insured.
Acts 2019, No. 412, §1, eff. June 11, 2019.
