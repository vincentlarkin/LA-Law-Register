§413.  Contracts with risk management service providers

Authorized trust funds may enter into contracts with risk management service providers, actuarial consultants, or other vendors as are necessary to ensure the effective and efficient operation of such trust funds. Fees paid to vendors for services provided shall not be excessive.

Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2053 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
