§372.  Rules and regulations
The commissioner may adopt such administrative rules as are necessary to implement the provisions of this Subpart.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 520, §1, eff. July 12, 1978; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1811 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
