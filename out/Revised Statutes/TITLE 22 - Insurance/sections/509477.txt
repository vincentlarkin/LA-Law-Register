§1260.6.  Provider listing requirements
Each discount medical plan organization shall maintain an up-to-date list of the names and addresses of the providers with which it has contracted directly or through a provider network.  This Section applies to those providers with which the discount medical plan organization has contracted with directly as well as those providers that are members of a provider network with which the discount medical plan organization has contracted.
Acts 2008, No. 442, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated by Acts 2009, No. 503, §3.
