§2344.  Rates, rating plans, and rate rules applicable
Rates shall be in accordance with the rating plans approved by the commissioner of insurance for use by the Property Insurance Association of Louisiana; however, such rates may include rules for classification of risks insured hereunder and rate modifications hereof.
Acts 2004, No. 761, §1, eff. July 6, 2004; Acts 2007, No. 459, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 2008; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1441.23 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
