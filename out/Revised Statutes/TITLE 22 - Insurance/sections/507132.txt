§537.  Limits of scope
Nothing contained in this Subpart shall be deemed to amend, supersede, or repeal any provision of R.S. 37:212 and 213.
Acts 1997, No. 1427, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.16 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
