§367.  Reserves
Each vehicle mechanical breakdown insurer shall maintain loss reserves in such amounts as the commissioner deems sufficient.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 520, §1, eff. July 12, 1978; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1806 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
