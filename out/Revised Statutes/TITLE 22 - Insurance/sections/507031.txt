§405.  Bylaws and plan of operation benefits
Every trust fund shall establish in its bylaws and plan of operation a schedule of benefits, to be approved by the commissioner, governing the indemnification of beneficiaries of the trust.  The schedule of benefits shall include all conditions, limitations, and exclusions relevant to indemnification.
Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2045 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
