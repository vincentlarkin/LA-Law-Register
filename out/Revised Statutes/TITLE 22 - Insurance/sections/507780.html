§910.  Life insurance and annuities; replacement

Rules and regulations concerning replacement of life insurance and annuities as provided in R.S. 22:47(1) and (17) shall be promulgated by the commissioner.

Acts 1999, No. 1312, §1, eff. July 12, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:644.1 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
