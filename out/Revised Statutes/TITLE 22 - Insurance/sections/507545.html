§763.  Reserve for marine and transportation (inland marine) insurance

In the case of policies of marine or inland navigation or transportation insurance the unearned premium reserve, to be charged as a liability shall be fifty percent of the amount of the premiums upon risks covering not more than one passage not terminated and shall be upon a pro rata basis for all other policies.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:892 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
