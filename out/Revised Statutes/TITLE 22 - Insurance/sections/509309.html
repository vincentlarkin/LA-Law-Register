§2205.  Appeal

All appeals from a decision of the division of administrative law shall be in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, R.S. 49:950 et seq.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1365 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 317, §1; Acts 2012, No. 271, §1.
