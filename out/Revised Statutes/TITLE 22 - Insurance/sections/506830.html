SUBPART H-2.  CONVERSIONS OF DOMESTIC MUTUAL NON-LIFE

INSURERS AND MUTUAL INSURANCE HOLDING COMPANIES

§237.  Corporate reorganization

The conversion of a mutual non-life insurer or a mutual non-life insurance holding company pursuant to R.S. 22:71 and 72 shall also comply with the provisions of this Subpart.  "Mutual non-life insurer" and "mutual non-life insurance holding company" shall have the meanings as set forth in R.S. 22:237.2.

Acts 2009, No. 234, §1.
