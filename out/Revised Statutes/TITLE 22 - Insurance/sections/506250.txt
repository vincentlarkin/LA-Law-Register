§87.  Stock dividends
Stock dividends may be declared and paid in the manner specified in R.S. 22:85 except that publication of the notice and the payment in cash shall not be required.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:75 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
