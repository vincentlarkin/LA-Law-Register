§1645.  Insurer; underwriting; advertisement

An administrator may use only such advertising pertaining to the business underwritten by an insurer that has been approved in writing by the insurer in advance of its use.

Acts 1993, No. 144, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3035 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
