CHAPTER 20.  LOUISIANA HEALTH INSURANCE INNOVATION
AND STABILIZATION PROGRAM
PART I.  STATE INNOVATION WAIVER
§2471.  Authority of the commissioner
The commissioner may apply on behalf of the state of Louisiana for a state innovation
waiver pursuant to Section 1332 of Public Law 111-148, 42 U.S.C. 18052, and establish and
implement a reinsurance program administered pursuant to the state innovation waiver.  No
reinsurance program authorized by this Section shall be created until the application for a
state innovation waiver is approved by the appropriate departments or agencies of the federal
government.
Acts 2018, No. 684, §1, eff. May 30, 2018.
