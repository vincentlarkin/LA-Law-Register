§1356.  Travel administrators
Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Title, no person shall act or represent
himself as a travel administrator in this state unless that person meets one of the following
conditions:
(1)  Is a licensed producer for property and casualty insurance in this state.
(2)  Holds a valid managing general agent license in this state.
Acts 2017, No. 225, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2018.
