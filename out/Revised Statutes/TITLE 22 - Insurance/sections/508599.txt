§1605.  Unfair trade practice
Failure to comply with any of the provisions of this Subpart shall be an unfair method of competition and an unfair or deceptive act or practice subject to regulation by the commissioner of insurance as provided by law, including R.S. 22:1554 and 1967 through 1972.
Acts 1997, No. 1475, §2, eff. July 15, 1997, H.C.R. No. 4, 2002 1st Ex. Sess.; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3065 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
