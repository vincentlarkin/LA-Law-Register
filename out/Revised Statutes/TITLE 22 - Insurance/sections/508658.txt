§1653.  Annual report; filing fee
A.  Each administrator shall file an annual report for the preceding calendar year with
the commissioner on or before March first of each year, or within such extension of time
therefor as the commissioner may grant for good cause.  The report shall be in the form and
contain all information as the commissioner requires.
B.  The annual report shall include the complete names and addresses of all insurers
with which the administrator had an agreement during the preceding fiscal year.
C.  At the time of filing its annual report, the administrator shall pay a filing fee in
the amount set forth in R.S. 22:821.
