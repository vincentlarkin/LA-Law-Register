§517.  Title insurance producers; licensing and reporting requirements
Every title insurance producer licensed in the state shall provide, in a timely fashion, each title insurer with which it places business all information the title insurer may request in compliance with the licensing and reporting requirements of the department.
Acts 1997, No. 1427, §1; Acts 1999, No. 192, §1, eff. June 9, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2092.6 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
