§691.33. Risk management framework

            An insurer shall maintain a risk management framework to assist the insurer with identifying, assessing, monitoring, managing, and reporting on its material and relevant risks. This requirement may be satisfied if the insurance group of which it is a member maintains a risk management framework applicable to the operations of the insurer.

            Acts 2015, No. 196, §1, eff. Jan 1, 2016.
