§418.  Administrative rules
The commissioner may adopt rules to enforce and administer the requirements of this Subpart, according to the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2058 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
