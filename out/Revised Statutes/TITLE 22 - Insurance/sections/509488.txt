§550.6.  Certificate of authority required to transact insurance
A captive insurance company shall not transact the business of insurance in this state
unless the captive insurance company first obtains a certificate of authority from the
commissioner.
Acts 2008, No. 403, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated by Acts 2009, No. 503, §3;
Acts 2025, No. 313, §2.
