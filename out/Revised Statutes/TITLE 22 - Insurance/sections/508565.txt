§1596.  Licensure requirement
Any solicitation for the purchase or sale of any insurance product by any person, including a financial institution, its employees or agents, shall be conducted only by persons who have complied with all applicable state insurance licensing and appointment laws and regulations.
Acts 1997, No. 1475, §2, eff. July 15, 1997; Redesignated from R.S. 22:3055 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
