CHAPTER 5.  PRODUCERS AND OTHER REGULATED ENTITIES

PART I.  PRODUCERS

SUBPART A.  QUALIFICATIONS AND LICENSING

§1541.  Purpose and scope

This Subpart shall govern the qualifications and procedures for the licensing of insurance producers.  These provisions shall apply to any and all lines of insurance and types of insurers as set forth in this Subpart.

Acts 2001, No. 158, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2002; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1131 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2011, No. 94, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2012.
