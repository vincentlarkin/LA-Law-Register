§416.  Commissioner's authority to examine

The commissioner may examine authorized trust funds to the same extent and with the same purpose as is provided, with respect to insurance companies.

Acts 1986, No. 574, §2; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2056 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
