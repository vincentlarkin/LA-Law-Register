PART II.  MANAGING GENERAL AGENT

§1621.  Title

This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Managing General Agents Law".

Acts 1990, No. 265, §1, eff. midnight Dec. 31, 1991; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1201 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
