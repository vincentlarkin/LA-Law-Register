§2142.  Purpose

The purpose of the consortium shall be to promote the development of academic courses in insurance and financial services, to encourage the establishment of a subspecialty degree program in insurance and financial services, and to develop, promote, and administer continuing education courses and professional development for the insurance industry.

Acts 1995, No. 710, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1995; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1194.1 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
