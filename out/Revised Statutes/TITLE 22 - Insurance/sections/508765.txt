§1766.  Authorized employees and agents
A.  Any limited license issued under this Part shall also authorize any employees and
any authorized agents of the limited licensee to act individually on behalf, and under the
supervision, of the limited licensee with respect to the kinds of coverage specified in this
Part.
B.  Each rental company licensed pursuant to this Part shall conduct a training
program in which employees and authorized agents being trained shall receive basic
instruction about the kinds of coverage specified in this Part and offered for purchase by
prospective renters of rental vehicles.
C.  The limited licensee shall keep a list of all persons who are authorized or who are
selling insurance as provided in this Part.  The list shall be provided to the commissioner
within two weeks of written demand from the commissioner.
