§1300.9.  Insurable interest
A.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a peer-to-peer car
sharing program shall have an insurable interest in a shared vehicle during the car sharing
period.
B.  Nothing in this Section creates liability on a peer-to-peer car sharing program to
maintain the coverage mandated by R.S. 22:1300.3.
C.  A peer-to-peer car sharing program may own and maintain as the named insured
one or more policies of motor vehicle liability insurance that provides coverage for any of
the following:
(1)  Liabilities assumed by the peer-to-peer car sharing program under a peer-to-peer
car sharing program agreement.
(2)  Any liability of the shared vehicle owner.
(3)  Damage or loss to the shared motor vehicle.
(4)  Any liability of the shared vehicle driver.
Acts 2020, No. 277, §1.
