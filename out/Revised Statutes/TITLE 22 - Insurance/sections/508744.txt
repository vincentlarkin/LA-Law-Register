§1750.  Implementation
The department may promulgate such rules and regulations, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act, as are necessary to implement this Part.
Acts 2001, No. 1150, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1210.60 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
