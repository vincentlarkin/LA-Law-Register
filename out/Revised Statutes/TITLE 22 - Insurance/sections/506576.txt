§383.  Exceptions
Nothing in this Subpart shall have any effect upon contracts of casualty or property insurance issued on property in this state.
Added by Acts 1981, No. 472, §1, eff. July 19, 1981; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1902 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
