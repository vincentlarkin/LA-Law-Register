§232.8. Failure to give notice

            If the mutual insurance company complies substantially and in good faith with the notice requirements of R.S. 22:232.1, the mutual insurance company's failure to give any policyholder any required notice does not impair the validity of any action taken pursuant to R.S. 22:231 or this Subpart.

            Acts 1997, No. 1482, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1004.8 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Redesignated from R.S. 22:702 by Acts 2012, No. 294, §3; Acts 2022, No. 161, §1.
