§50.  Reimbursement to a unique provider of health services
Health insurance issuers shall provide coverage and reimbursement to a unique
provider of health services for catastrophically ill children, as defined by R.S. 40:1077.1(2),
located outside the state of Louisiana in accordance with the terms and conditions of the
policy of insurance between the insured and the insurer.
Acts 2009, No. 419, §1.
