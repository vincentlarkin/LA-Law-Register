§691.14.  Receivership
If at the discretion of the commissioner, it appears that any person has committed a violation of this Subpart which so impairs the financial condition of a domestic insurer as to threaten insolvency or make the further transaction of business by it hazardous to its policyholders, creditors, shareholders, or the public, then the commissioner may proceed pursuant to the provisions contained in R.S. 22:73, 96, 731 et seq., 2001 et seq., as well as Subpart H of this Part and Chapter 9 of this Code.
Acts 2012, No. 294, §1.
