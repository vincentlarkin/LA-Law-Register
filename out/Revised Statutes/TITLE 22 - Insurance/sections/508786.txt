§1772.  Rules and regulations; promulgated by department
The commissioner shall issue such rules, regulations, and directives, in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to implement the provisions of this Part.
Acts 1999, No. 1278, §1, eff. July 12, 1999; Redesignated from R.S. 22:2112 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
