CHAPTER 3.  DEPOSITS, ASSESSMENTS, FEES AND TAXES
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§791.  Exemption from franchise or capital stock tax
No insurer paying the taxes levied under this Part shall be liable for any franchise or capital stock tax.
Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:1069 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
