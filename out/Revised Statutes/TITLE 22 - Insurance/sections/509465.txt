§2371.  Insure Louisiana Incentive Fund
There is hereby created in the state treasury as a special fund the Insure Louisiana
Incentive Fund, referred to in this Chapter as the "fund". Monies appropriated or transferred
to the fund shall be deposited by the state treasurer after compliance with the provisions of
Article VII, Section 9(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana. Monies in the fund shall be
invested in the same manner as monies in the state general fund and any interest earned on
the investment of monies in the fund shall be credited to the fund. All unexpended and
unencumbered monies in the fund at the end of the fiscal year shall remain in the fund.
Monies in the fund shall be used by the department to provide grants pursuant to the
provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 2022, No. 754, §1.
