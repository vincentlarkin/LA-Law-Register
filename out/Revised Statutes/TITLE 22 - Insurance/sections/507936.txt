§1134.  Operation of program
A.  The commissioner shall establish the Louisiana Guaranteed Benefits Pool with
a framework and operation similar to other state best practices.
B.  The program may be administered by either the commissioner or by an
independent nonprofit organization.
Acts 2019, No. 412, §1, eff. June 11, 2019.
