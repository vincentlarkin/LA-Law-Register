§206.  Expenses

The expenses of necessary printing, stationery, postage, office supplies and expenses, clerical hire, statutory fees, and examination fees may be paid by the association.  The total of all expenses shall not exceed twenty-five percent of each assessment and shall be paid out of the general fund of the association.

Acts 1958, No. 125; Redesignated from R.S. 22:346 by Acts 2008, No. 415, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2009, No. 503, §1.
