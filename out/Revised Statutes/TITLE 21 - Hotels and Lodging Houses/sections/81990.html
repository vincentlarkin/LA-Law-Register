§2.  Screens

Every hotel or innkeeper shall properly screen with wire cloth or gauze, mesh not to be less than sixteen to the inch, the doors and windows of the kitchen and dining-room, and all openings therein, of such inn or hotel.  

Whoever fails or refuses to comply with the provisions and requirements of this Section shall be fined not less than five dollars nor more than twenty-five dollars.
