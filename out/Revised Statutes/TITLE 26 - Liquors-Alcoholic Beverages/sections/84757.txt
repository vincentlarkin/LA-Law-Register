§456.  Concealing violations or hindering enforcement of Chapter
No person shall use any artful device or deceptive practice to conceal any violation of this Chapter or to mislead the secretary or any agent of the secretary in the enforcement of this Chapter or do any other act tending to defraud the state of its revenue.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
