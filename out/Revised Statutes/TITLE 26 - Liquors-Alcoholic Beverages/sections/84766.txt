§495.  Copies of pertinent local ordinances to be furnished commissioner
The governing authority of each parish or municipality adopting, under the authority of this Chapter ordinances pertaining to alcoholic beverages shall furnish, in each instance, a certified copy thereof to the commissioner.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
