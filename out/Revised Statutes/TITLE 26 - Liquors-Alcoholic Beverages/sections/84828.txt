§811.  Nonwaiver
No right or cause of action authorized by Louisiana law shall be waived by the supplier or wholesaler unless specifically waived in this* agreement.
Acts 1993, No. 132, §1, eff. May 26, 1993.
*As appears in enrolled bill.
