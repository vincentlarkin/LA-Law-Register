§350.  Maximum stock of beverages of high alcoholic content in dealer's bonded stockroom

Every manufacturer or wholesaler may carry in his bonded stockroom or warehouse beverages of high alcoholic content with a taxable value not in excess of his surety bond.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
