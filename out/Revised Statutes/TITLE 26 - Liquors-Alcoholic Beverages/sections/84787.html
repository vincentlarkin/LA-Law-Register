§653.  Analysis of beverages in possession of arrested person

Any officer who has made an arrest and found alcoholic beverages in the possession or control of the party arrested may have a sample of the beverages so found analyzed by the state board of health.  The board shall make a report of its findings to the officer who furnished the sample.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
