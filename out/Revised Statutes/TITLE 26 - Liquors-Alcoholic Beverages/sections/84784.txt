CHAPTER 4.  MISCELLANEOUS PENAL PROVISIONS
PART I.  DEFINITIONS
§641.  Definitions
For purposes of this Chapter, the terms "alcoholic beverages", "beverages of low alcoholic content", and "beverages of high alcoholic content" have the same meanings ascribed to them in R.S. 26:241.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
