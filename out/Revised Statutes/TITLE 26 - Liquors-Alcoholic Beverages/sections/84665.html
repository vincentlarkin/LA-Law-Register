§152.  Forms for default notices and delinquent lists; rules for filing and publication

The commissioner shall prescribe forms for notices of default and for delinquent lists and shall make and publish rules and regulations to govern the filing of such notices and lists and the publication thereof.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
