§89.  Form of application for renewal of permit

The commissioner may, by regulation, prescribe the form of application to be used when the applicant is not seeking a new permit but only the renewal of a current permit held by him.  Nothing in this regulation shall alter the qualifications required in this Chapter.

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1; Acts 2011, No. 259, §1.
