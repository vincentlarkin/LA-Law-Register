CHAPTER 3.  LOCAL OPTION
§581.  Definitions
For purposes of this Chapter, the following words have the following meanings:
(1)  "Alcoholic beverages", "beverages of low alcoholic content", and "beverages of high alcoholic content" have the meanings ascribed to them in R.S. 26:241.
(2)  "Election district" means a district from which a parochial officer is elected but "election district" does not include a district located entirely within an incorporated municipality.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1; Acts 1990, No. 10, § 1, eff. Jan. 1, 1991; Acts 1997, No. 330, §1.
