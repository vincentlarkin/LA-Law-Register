PART II.  POISONOUS BEVERAGES

§651.  Manufacture or preparation of poisonous beverages; penalty

A.  No person shall manufacture, concoct, or prepare for sale any alcoholic beverage containing lye, nicotine, wood alcohol, india berries, or any other poisonous substance.  

B.  Whoever violates this Section shall be fined not more than one thousand dollars and imprisoned for not more than five years, with or without hard labor.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
