§105.  Decisions to withhold, suspend, or revoke permits final unless appealed and reversed

Decisions of the commissioner in withholding, suspending, or revoking permits and of local authorities in withholding permits are final and binding on all parties unless appealed in the manner provided in R.S. 26:106 and finally reversed by the courts.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
