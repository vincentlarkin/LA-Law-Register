§925. Additional requirements

            To the extent that they do not conflict, the provisions of this Chapter that provide for the regulation of tobacco products shall also apply to vapor products and alternative nicotine products. Such provisions shall include but are not limited to provisions governing the responsible vendor program, server liability, and the sale of all such products.

            Acts 2019, No. 424, §1, eff. June 20, 2019.
