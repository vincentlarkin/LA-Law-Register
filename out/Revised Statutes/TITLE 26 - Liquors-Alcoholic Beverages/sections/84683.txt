§283.  Authority of commissioner and local authorities to withhold permits
The commissioner with respect to state permits and municipal authorities or parish
governing authorities with respect to local permits may withhold the issuance of permits in
the manner and under the terms and conditions specified in this Chapter.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1, Acts 1992, No. 976, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993; Acts 2015, No.
382, §1.
