§356.  False invoices of beverages of high alcoholic content
No person shall make or use, present, or exhibit to the secretary or agent of the secretary any invoice of beverages of high alcoholic content that bears an untrue date or falsely states the nature or quantity of the goods involved.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
