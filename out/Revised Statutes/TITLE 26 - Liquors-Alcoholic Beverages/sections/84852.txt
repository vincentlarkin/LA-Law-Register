§913.  Violation
No person shall perform any action for which a permit is required by this Chapter unless he holds the proper permit.  Each day of business which is conducted without such a valid, unsuspended permit shall constitute a separate violation of this Chapter.
Acts 1997, No. 1370, §2, eff. Oct. 1, 1997; Acts 2012, No. 143, §1.
