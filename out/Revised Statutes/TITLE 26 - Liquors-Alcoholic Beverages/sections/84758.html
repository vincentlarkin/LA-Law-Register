§457.  Special agents of secretary and commissioner

The secretary and commissioner shall commission agents in their departments to carry out the provisions of this Chapter and shall provide them with appropriate credentials and badges of authority.  These agents have the powers of peace officers in the performance of their duties.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
