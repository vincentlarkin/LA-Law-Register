§369.  Carriers to file report showing alcoholic beverages handled
All common or contract carriers doing business or making deliveries within the state shall file with the secretary monthly, on or before the fifteenth day of the month following the period covered by the statement, reports showing in detail the number of gallons of alcoholic beverages shipped or delivered by them at points of ultimate destination, or otherwise, whether in car lots or otherwise, with the date of the delivery and by whom and to whom shipped and delivered.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
