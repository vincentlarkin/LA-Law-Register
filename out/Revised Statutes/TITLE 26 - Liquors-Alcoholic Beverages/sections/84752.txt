PART V.  ADMINISTRATION
§451.  Secretary to collect taxes
The secretary shall collect, supervise, and enforce the collection of all taxes, penalties, and interest that may be due under the provisions of this Chapter.  To that end, he is vested with all the power and authority conferred by this Chapter.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
