§149.  Refilling used containers prohibited; exception

No person shall refill an original container of alcoholic beverages with an alcoholic beverage or have in his possession any original container that has been so refilled, except for three gallon keg type containers of sparkling wine.  

Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
