§291.  Cause necessary to withholding, suspending, or revoking of permit
No permit shall be withheld, suspended, or revoked except for causes specified in this Chapter.  If a person holds more than one permit and any one of them is suspended or revoked, the commissioner may suspend or revoke all of his permits.
Acts 1987, No. 696, §1.
