§1243.  Enforcement of Chapter through court action
The board may institute any action in a court of competent jurisdiction to enforce compliance with any provision of this Chapter or with any regulation, subpoena, or order of the board made pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 1999, No. 767, §1.
