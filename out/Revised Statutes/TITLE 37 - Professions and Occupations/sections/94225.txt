§358.  Student ages
Barber colleges shall not admit as students persons under the age of sixteen years
unless the student is enrolled in a board-approved barber college that operates within a state
high school, in which case the student may enroll in the barber college as a high school
freshman.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1; Acts 2024, No. 140, §1.
