PART I-A. MEDICATION ATTENDANTS IN LICENSED LONG-TERM CARE

FACILITIES

§1026.1. Purpose

            The purpose of this Part is to create a program that shall authorize certification of medication attendants to perform certain functions in licensed long-term care facilities. This program shall be administered by the Louisiana Department of Health.

            Acts 2007, No. 293, §1, eff. July 9, 2007; Acts 2011, No. 50, §1; Acts 2022, No. 112, §1.
