§3473.  Applicants from other states; waiver of inspection
The board may waive the inspection provided in R.S. 37:3470, if the applicant
presents to the board a satisfactory certificate of registration or license from an entity which
licenses distributors of the same type in another state, and if the standards adopted and
enforced by such entity are comparable to those provided in this Chapter.
