§377.  Report to governor
The board shall submit annually to the governor and the legislature a full statement of its work during the year together with any recommendations it thinks necessary and proper.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Amended by Acts 1966, No. 41, §1; Acts 1972, No. 244, §1; Acts 1976, No. 424, §11; Acts 1979, No. 52, §1; Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
