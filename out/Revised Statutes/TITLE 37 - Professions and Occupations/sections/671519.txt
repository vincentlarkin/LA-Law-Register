PART VI.  MEDICAL PSYCHOLOGISTS
§1360.51.  Short title
This Part may be cited as the "Medical Psychology Practice Act".
Acts 2009, No. 251, §11, eff. July 1, 2009; Acts 2021, No. 238, §2.
NOTE:  Acts 2021, No. 238, §2 provides that Acts 2009, No. 251 may be cited as
"The Dr. James W. Quillin, MP, Medical Psychology Practice Act".
