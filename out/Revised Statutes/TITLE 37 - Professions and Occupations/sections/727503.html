§711.21.  Continuing professional development for professional geoscientists

Professional geoscientists may, by rule of the board, adopted in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, be required to earn annually up to fifteen hours of board-approved continuing professional development.

Acts 2010, No. 974, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
