§1210.  Continuing education in pharmacy
The board shall establish requirements for continuing education in pharmacy, including the determination of acceptable program content.  The board shall adopt rules and regulations necessary to carry out the stated objectives and purposes, to enforce the provisions of this Section.
Acts 1999, No. 767, §1.
