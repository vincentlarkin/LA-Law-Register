§1114.  Privileged communications
Testimonial privileges, exceptions, and waiver with respect to communications
between a licensed professional counselor or a provisional licensed professional counselor
and his client are governed by the Louisiana Code of Evidence.
Acts 1987, No. 892, §1, eff. July 20, 1987; Acts 1992, No. 376, §6, eff. Jan. 1, 1993;
Acts 2014, No. 484, §1, eff. May 1, 2015.
