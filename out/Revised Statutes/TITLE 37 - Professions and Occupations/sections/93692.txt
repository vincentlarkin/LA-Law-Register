§2458.  Meetings of board
The Louisiana Board for Hearing Aid Dealers shall meet at least once each year at
a place and time determined by the chairman.  The board shall also meet at such other times
and places as are specified by the chairman to carry out the purpose of this Chapter.
Acts 1968, No. 302, §18, eff. Jan. 1, 1969; Acts 2018, No. 206, §3.
