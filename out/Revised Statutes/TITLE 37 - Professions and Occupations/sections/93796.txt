§2815.  Display of license or certificates
Licenses and renewal certificates issued under the provisions of this Chapter shall be conspicuously displayed in the principal office of the licensee.  Any intern who is practicing under the provisions of this Chapter shall display his certificate in the office in which he is practicing.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 39, §1.  Acts 1986, No. 231, §1.
