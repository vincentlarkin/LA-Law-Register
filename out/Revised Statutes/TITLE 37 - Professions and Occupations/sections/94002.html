§3215.  Renewal of license

A.  Every person licensed under the provisions of this Chapter shall renew his license every two years.  Upon the receipt of the application for renewal and the renewal fee, the board shall verify the accuracy of the application for renewal and issue to the applicant a certificate of renewal.

B.  Renewal of a license issued under this Chapter may be contingent upon the fulfillment of continuing education requirements as promulgated by the board.  

Acts 1984, No. 485, §1.
