CHAPTER 33.  FINANCIAL PLANNING AND
MANAGEMENT SERVICES
§2581.  Declaration of policy
The business of rendering financial planning and management services to individuals is a matter of public interest and concern and is subject to regulation and control in the public interest.
Acts 1970, No. 423, §1.
