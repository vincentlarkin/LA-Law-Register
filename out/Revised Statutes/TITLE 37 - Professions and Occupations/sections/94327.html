§574.  Organization of board; meetings; quorum

A.  The governor shall designate one member of the board to serve as chairman, and the chairman shall designate one member of the board to serve as vice chairman.

B.  A majority of the members of the board physically present at a board meeting shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business.

Acts 2001, No. 907, §2, eff. June 26, 2001; Acts 2010, No. 728, §1.
