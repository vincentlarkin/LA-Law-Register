§3015.  Advisory committee; meetings
A statewide advisory committee established by the board in carrying out its duties
pursuant to this Chapter may conduct and its members may attend and participate in a
meeting via electronic means in accordance with R.S. 42:17.3.
Acts 2022, No. 408, §1; Acts 2023, No. 263, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2024.
