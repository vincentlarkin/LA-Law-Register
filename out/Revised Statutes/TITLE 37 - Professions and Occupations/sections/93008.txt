§104.  Duties of the society
Within fifteen days of the receipt of the request for review, the society shall do the following:
(1)  Confirm by mail to the claimant or the claimant's attorney that the filing has been officially received.
(2)  Notify all named defendants that a filing has been made against them and a request made for the formation of a public accountant review panel and forward a copy of the request for review to each named defendant at his last and usual place of residence or his office.
(3)  Repealed by Acts 1999, No. 611, §1.
Acts 1997, No. 747, §1; Acts 1999, No. 611, §1.
