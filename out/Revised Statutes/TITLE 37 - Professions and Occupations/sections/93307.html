§1467.  Agency

A.  Licensees shall provide the parties to a real estate transaction with an agency disclosure informational pamphlet, and where applicable, a dual agency disclosure form as mandated under R.S. 9:3897.

B.  The commission may prescribe such agency disclosure forms or pamphlets as it deems necessary for the enforcement of this Section.

Acts 1991, No. 354, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1997, No. 32, §1.
