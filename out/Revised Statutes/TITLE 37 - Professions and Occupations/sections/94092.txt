CHAPTER 51.  LOUISIANA  REAL ESTATE
APPRAISERS LAW
§3391.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Real Estate Appraisers Law".
Acts 1987, No. 472, §1; Acts 1995, No. 690, §2; Acts 2001, No. 833, §2.
