§351.  Employment of barbers
No person shall permit any person in his employ or under his supervision or control to practice as a barber unless the latter person has a certificate of registration as a registered barber, an apprenticeship certificate, or a certificate of registration as a cosmetologist issued by the Louisiana State Board of Cosmetology, or holds a valid permit from the Board of Barber Examiners issued pursuant to R.S. 37:368.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 424, §3; Acts 1978, No. 57, §1; Acts 1984, No. 238, §1; Acts 1995, No. 226, §1, eff. July 1, 1995.
