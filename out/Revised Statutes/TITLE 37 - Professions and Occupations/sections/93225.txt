§1363. Officers of the board
A. The board shall elect from its members a chairman, a vice chairman, and a
secretary-treasurer for two year terms; the election to be held within thirty days of August
first in each odd-numbered year.
B.(1) The chairman and vice chairman shall each be an active master plumber listed
as the responsible master plumber for his company.
(2) The chairman shall vote only in case of a tie.
Amended by Acts 1964, No. 498, §1; Acts 1977, No. 276, §1, eff. July 7, 1977; Acts
2025, No. 438, §1, eff. June 20, 2025.
