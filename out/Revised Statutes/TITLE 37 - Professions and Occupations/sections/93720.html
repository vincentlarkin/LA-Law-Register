§2560.  Fines

After investigation and hearing and upon a finding of a violation of the provisions of this Chapter or other laws regarding the practice of shorthand reporting, the Board of Examiners of Certified Shorthand Reporters may impose a fine not to exceed one thousand dollars.

Acts 1997, No. 635, §1.
