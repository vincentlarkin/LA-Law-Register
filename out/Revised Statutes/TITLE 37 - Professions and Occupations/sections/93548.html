§2107.  Examination and license fees

A.  The board shall require each applicant for an examination to deposit the sum of fifty dollars as a nonrefundable examination fee.

B.  When an applicant meets the qualifications and passes the examination, he shall be issued a license which shall be renewed annually as provided by this Chapter.

C.  The board is authorized to charge a fee of twenty-five dollars for the issuance of each license.

Added by Acts 1954, No. 371, §7; Acts 2001, No. 807, §1.
