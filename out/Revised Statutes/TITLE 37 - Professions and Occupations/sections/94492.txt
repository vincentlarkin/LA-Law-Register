PART II.  PRENEED FUNERAL PLANS
§861.  Preneed funeral plans
It shall be unlawful for a firm, partnership, corporation, an association of individuals, or anyone to enter into a preneed funeral contract other than a duly licensed funeral establishment.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 5, §1.  Amended by Acts 1982, No. 592, §1; Acts 1983, No. 454, §1; Acts 2008, No. 799, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
