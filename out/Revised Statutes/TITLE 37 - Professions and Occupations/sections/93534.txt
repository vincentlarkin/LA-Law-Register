PART IV.  ITINERANT VENDORS
§1920.  Purpose
The purpose of this Part is to protect the public from improper sales techniques by itinerant vendors, and to provide a method of processing warranty claims on merchandise sold by itinerant vendors.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 815, §1.
