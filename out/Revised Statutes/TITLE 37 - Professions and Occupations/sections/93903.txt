§3076.  Penalty
Whoever violates any provision of this Chapter, upon conviction shall be fined not less than three hundred dollars or more than five hundred dollars, or be imprisoned for not more than six months, or both.  Each day of violation shall constitute a separate offense.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 748, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.  Acts 1993, No. 819, §1.
