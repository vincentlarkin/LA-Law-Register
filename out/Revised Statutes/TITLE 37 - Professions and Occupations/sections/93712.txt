§2552.  Terms of office
A.  Members of the board of examiners shall be appointed for three-year terms, except that any member appointed to fill a vacancy occurring prior to the expiration of the term shall be appointed for the remainder of such term only.  Members of the board shall continue in office until their successors are appointed and qualified.
B.  Reporter members of said board shall be appointed from among the holders of certificates issued under this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 456, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 110, §1; Acts 1983, No. 612, §1; Acts 1986, No. 365, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1987; Acts 2009, No. 438, §17(A).
NOTE:  See Acts 1986, No. 365, §4.
