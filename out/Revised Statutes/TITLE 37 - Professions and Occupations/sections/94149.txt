§3464.  Qualifications of board members
Each member of the board shall be at least twenty-one years of age, of good moral
character and temperate habits, and a resident of this state and shall have engaged in
distribution as defined by this Chapter.
Acts 1988, No. 852, §1; Acts 1995, No. 1152, §1, eff. June 29, 1995; Acts 2015, No.
443, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
