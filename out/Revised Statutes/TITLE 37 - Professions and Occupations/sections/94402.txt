CHAPTER 2.  ACCOUNTANTS
PART I.  IN GENERAL
§71.  Short title
This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Accountancy Act".
Acts 1979, No. 510, §1; Acts 1991, No. 880, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992; Acts 1999, No. 473, §1, eff. June 18, 1999.
