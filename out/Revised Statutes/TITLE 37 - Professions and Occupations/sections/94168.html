§3480. Unauthorized sales

             Distributors shall sell or distribute legend drugs or legend devices only to a person who is authorized, by law or regulation, to procure or possess legend drugs or legend devices.

            Acts 1988, No. 852, §1; Acts 2008, No. 597, §1; Acts 2015, No. 443, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
