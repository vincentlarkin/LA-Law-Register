§759.  Report of board

The board shall report its proceedings and the items of its receipts and disbursements for the previous fiscal year to the governor before September first in each year.

Amended by Acts 1964, No. 133, §4; Acts 1995, No. 920, §1; Acts 1999, No. 1358, §1.
