§3211.  Licensure
The board shall issue a license to and authorize the use of the title "Licensed Radiologic Technologist" by each applicant who has successfully passed the certification examination or has otherwise been qualified under the provisions of this Chapter and has paid all fees required by this Chapter.
Acts 1984, No. 485, §1; Acts 2014, No. 250, §1.
