§3475.  Annual renewal of license
All licensed distributors shall pay to the board a renewal fee as shall be determined
by the board.
Acts 1988, No. 852, §1; Acts 1995, No. 1152, §1, eff. June 29, 1995; Acts 2015, No.
443, §1, eff. July 1, 2015.
