§378.  Obtaining certificate by fraud

No person shall obtain or attempt to obtain a certificate of registration for money other than the required fee or for any other thing of value or by fraudulent misrepresentation.  

Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
