§932.  Collection of criminal evidence

Nothing in this Part shall prohibit a registered nurse from collecting blood or urine from a person suspected of violating the provisions of R.S. 14:32, 32.1, 39.1, or 98, at the request of a law enforcement officer.  

Acts 1990, No. 1011, §1.
