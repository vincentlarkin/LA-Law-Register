§3313.  Display of license

A license and renewal issued pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter shall be conspicuously displayed in the principal office of the licensee.  Licensees shall, upon request, present proof of state licensure when engaged in professional activities as a licensed athletic trainer (LAT).

Acts 2014, No. 418, §1.
