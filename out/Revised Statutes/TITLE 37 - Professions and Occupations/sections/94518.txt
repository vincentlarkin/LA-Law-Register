§928.  Disposition of Funds
A.  All amounts paid to the Louisiana State Board of Nursing shall be held subject to the order of the board to be used only for the purpose of meeting necessary expenses incurred in the performance of this Act, and the duties imposed thereby.
B.  All monies which may have accumulated to the credit of the Board under the Act which is now repealed shall be continued for use by the board in the administration of this Part.
Acts 1976, No. 351, §1.
