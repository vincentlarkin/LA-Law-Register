§370.  Display of certificate of registration
Every holder of a certificate of registration shall display the certificate in a conspicuous place adjacent to or near his work chair in his place of employment.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 424, §8; Acts 1978, No. 114, §1; Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
