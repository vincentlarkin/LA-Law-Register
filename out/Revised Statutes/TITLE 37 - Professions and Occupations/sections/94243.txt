§374.  Record of proceedings
The secretary of the board shall keep a record of all the proceedings of the board.  The records relating to the issuance, refusal, renewal, suspension, and revocation of certificates of registration shall contain the name, place of business, and residence of each barber and barber college and the date and number of his or its certificate of registration.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
