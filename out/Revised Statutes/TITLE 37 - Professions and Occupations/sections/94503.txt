§915.  Removal of board members
Any member may be removed from the board by the governor or a majority vote of
the board after notice and a hearing by the board wherein grounds for removal have been
established.  Grounds for removal shall include but not be limited to incompetence, neglect
of duty, or unprofessional or dishonorable conduct.
Acts 1976, No. 351, §1; Acts 1995, No. 633, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996; Acts 2018, No.
515, §2.
