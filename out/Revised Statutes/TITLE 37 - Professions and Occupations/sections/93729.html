§2589.  Denial of license

Any application for a license hereunder shall be approved or denied within sixty days of the filing of such application with the director.  If license shall be denied, then the applicant shall be so notified by United States mail, registered, and shall be given an opportunity to be heard thereon, within sixty days of such denial of license.  

Acts 1970, No. 423, §9.
