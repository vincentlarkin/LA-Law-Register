§1205.  Duplicate certificates
In case a certificate of a licensed pharmacist is lost, destroyed, or otherwise missing, the board shall issue a duplicate thereof, upon receipt of an affidavit attested to by the pharmacist setting forth the facts and circumstances surrounding the loss of the certificate and payment of the fee or fees as specified by the board.
Acts 1999, No. 767, §1.
