§367.  Issuance of certificate of registration

A.  When the provisions of this Chapter have been complied with, the board shall issue a certificate of registration as a registered barber or as an instructor as the case may be.  Each member of the board shall sign every certificate.

B.  No certificate of registration issued under this Chapter shall contain the complete social security number of the person to whom such certificate was issued.  However, the certificate may contain the last four digits of the person's social security number if the board deems it necessary.

Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1; Acts 2005, No. 470, §1.
