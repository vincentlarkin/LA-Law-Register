§368.  Permits for barbers pending examination
Any person who holds a valid current license to engage in the practice of barbering issued by any state of the United States or any foreign country may be issued a temporary permit to engage in the practice of barbering in this state pending examination by the board upon payment of the permit fee set by the board.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 424, §6; Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
