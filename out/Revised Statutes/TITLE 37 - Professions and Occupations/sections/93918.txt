§3094.  Representation of the board by the attorney general
The attorney general shall designate one of his assistants to act as attorney for the board, without additional salary, who shall counsel and advise the board and shall prosecute any violation of the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 1999, No. 1055, §1.
