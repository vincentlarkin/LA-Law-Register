§2664.  Prohibitions
No person shall:
(1)  Sell, barter, or offer to sell or barter a license or registration.
(2)  Purchase or procure by barter a license or registration with intent to use it as
evidence of the holder's qualifications in the practice of speech-language pathology or
audiology.
(3)  Alter a license or registration materially.
(4)  Use or attempt to use a license or registration which has been purchased,
fraudulently obtained, counterfeited, or materially altered.
(5)  Willfully make a false, material statement in an application for a license or
registration or for renewal of a license or registration.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 260, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1973; Acts 1995, No. 892, §2; Acts
2017, No. 302, §1.
