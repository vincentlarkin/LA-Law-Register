§58. Exceptions

            A. This Chapter does not apply to an occupation regulated by the state supreme court.

            B. This Chapter shall not apply to peace officers as defined in R.S. 40:2402.

            C. This Chapter does not apply to water and wastewater operator licenses.

            Acts 2024, No. 253, §1; Acts 2024, No. 568, §1.
