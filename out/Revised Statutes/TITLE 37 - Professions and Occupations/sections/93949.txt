PART III.  LIVESTOCK AUCTIONEERS
§3134.  Livestock auctioneers
The provisions of this Chapter shall not apply to and the board shall not have
jurisdiction over auctioneers who are engaged in the auction business exclusively as an
employee or agent of a Louisiana livestock market which is regulated by the Louisiana Board
of Animal Health and who are registered with that board as required by R.S. 3:571.
Acts 1987, No. 157, §2; Acts 2012, No. 811, §12, eff. July 1, 2012; Acts 2020, No.
306, §3, eff. June 12, 2020.
