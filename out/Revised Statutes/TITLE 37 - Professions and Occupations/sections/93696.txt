§2462.  Notice and hearing on revocation or suspension
Any person whose license or temporary training permit is sought to be revoked or
suspended pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter shall be given thirty days' notice, in
writing, enumerating the charges and specifying a date for public hearing thereon.  The
hearing shall be held in the parish where the person's business is conducted.  The board may
issue subpoenas, compel the attendance and testimony of witnesses, and place them under
oath, in the same manner as in a district court in the parish where the hearing takes place.
Acts 1968, No. 302, §22, eff. Jan. 1, 1969; Acts 2005, No. 261, §1; Acts 2022, No.
271, §2.
