§1228.  Equipment required of pharmacy
The board shall determine, by regulation, all standards for professional, technical, equipment and any other requirements deemed necessary for a pharmacy to possess, in order to operate in the best interest of the health, safety and welfare of the public.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 373, §1; Acts 1999, No. 767, §§1, 3.
