§1370.  Renewals
Every holder of a license or endorsement of such license issued under this Chapter shall renew his license and, if applicable, endorsement each year by payment of the annual renewal fee established by the board pursuant to R.S. 37:1371.
Amended by Acts 1964, No. 498, §1; Acts 1995, No. 824, §1.
