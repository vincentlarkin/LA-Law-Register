§2586.  Renewal of license

Each licensee under the provisions of this Chapter may on or before December 1, make application to the director for renewal of its license, which said application for renewal shall be on the form prescribed by the director and shall be accompanied by a fee of one hundred dollars together with a bond as required in the case of an original application.  

Acts 1970, No. 423, §6.
