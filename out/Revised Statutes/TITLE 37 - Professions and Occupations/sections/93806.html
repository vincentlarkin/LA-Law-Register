§2825.  Return to practice

Within such time after applicant has completed chiropractic college, as the Louisiana Board of Chiropractic Examiners shall provide by regulation, the scholarship recipient shall return to the state of Louisiana to practice chiropractic.  The recipient shall remain in the state for a period of one year as consideration for each chiropractic scholarship awarded.  

Added by Acts 1978, No. 705, §1.
