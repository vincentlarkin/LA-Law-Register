§2812.  Statistical certification
Chiropractors shall observe and be subject to all federal, state, parish and municipal regulations with regard to public health and all other information required by law as coming within their knowledge.  Chiropractors shall sign certificates and statements pertaining to public health insofar as they relate to chiropractic, but nothing in this Chapter shall be construed to permit any chiropractor to execute or register certificates of birth or death.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 39, §1.
