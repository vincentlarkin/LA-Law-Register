PART II.  BOARD OF PHARMACY
§1171.  Louisiana Board of Pharmacy; creation
The Louisiana Board of Pharmacy is hereby created within the
Louisiana Department of Health and is subject to the provisions of R.S.
36:803.  The board shall carry out the purposes and enforce the provisions of
this Chapter.
Acts 1999, No. 767, §1.
