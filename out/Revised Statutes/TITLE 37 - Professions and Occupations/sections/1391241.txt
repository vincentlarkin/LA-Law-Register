§57. State laws and jurisdiction
A person who obtains an occupational license or certification pursuant to this Chapter
is subject to the laws regulating the occupation in this state and the jurisdiction of the board
in this state.
Acts 2024, No. 253, §1; Acts 2024, No. 568, §1.
