§151.  Roster of architects to be published annually
The board shall publish annually a roster of registered architects with their addresses.  This published roster shall be received as evidence in court that the names it contains are duly registered architects of this state as of a specific date on the roster.
Amended by Acts 1958, No. 524, §1; Acts 1972, No. 69, §1.
