§2814.  Waiver of renewals while in the military service
The board shall waive the requirements of R.S. 37:2810 for any chiropractor licensed under this Chapter while on active duty in the military service of the United States or any of its allies, upon notification by the licensee to the board.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 39, §1; Acts 2001, No. 375, §2.
