§3141.  Information required in advertisement
Any licensed auctioneer who advertises to hold or conduct an auction shall indicate in such advertisement his name, business addresses, license number, and such other reasonable information as may be required by rule.
Acts 1986, No. 564, §6, eff. July 2, 1986.
