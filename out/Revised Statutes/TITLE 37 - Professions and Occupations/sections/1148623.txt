§23.3.  Prohibited agreements and actions of professional licensing boards and commissions
A.  No professional or occupational licensing board or commission shall:
(1)  Enter into a consent decree with a licensee, permittee, or certificate holder if such
decree contains a nondisparagement clause. Such a nondisparagement clause contained in
a consent decree is contrary to public policy of this state and shall be null, void, and
unenforceable.
(2)  Initiate disciplinary action against a licensee, permittee, or certificate holder for
providing testimony or records to a legislative body.
B.  For the purposes of this Section, a "legislative body" shall include but not be
limited to a legislative committee, subcommittee, or task force.
Acts 2019, No. 179, §1.
