§384.  Inspection of shops and schools by board
Any member of this board or any employee designated for the purpose shall have access to and may enter and inspect at all reasonable hours and at any time during business hours any barber shop or barber college or place where barbering is being carried on.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
