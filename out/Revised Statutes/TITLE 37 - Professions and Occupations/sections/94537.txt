§970.  Qualifications of applicants
An applicant for a license to practice as a practical nurse shall:
(1)  Be of good moral character.
(2)  Be a permanent resident or citizen of the United States.
(3)  Satisfy either of the following requirements:
(a)  Successfully complete an education program for practical nurses at a program
approved by the board.
(b)  Successfully complete an education program for registered nurses at a program
approved by the Louisiana State Board of Nursing.
Amended by Acts 1954, No. 315, §2; Acts 1968, No. 80, §1; Acts 2010, No. 553, §1;
Acts 2023, No. 80, §1.
