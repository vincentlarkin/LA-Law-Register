§389.  Enforcement of Chapter through court action

The board may institute any actions in the courts that appear necessary to enforce compliance with any provision of this Chapter or with any rule, subpoena, or order of the board made pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter.

Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1; Acts 1995, No. 226, §1, eff. July 1, 1995.
