§347.  Salaries and expenses; how payable
All salaries, per diem, and expenses due or incurred by the board shall be paid by vouchers drawn by the secretary.  All such vouchers are payable only out of the barbers' board fund provided for in R.S. 37:376.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
