§755.  Compensation of members

Out of the funds of the board, each board member shall receive one hundred fifty dollars per day and actual reimbursement for reasonable traveling expenses for each day actually engaged in the performance of his official duties.  In addition, the president shall be paid a salary in connection with performing the official duties of his office in an amount to be set by the board.

Amended by Acts 1958, No. 490, §1; Acts 1962, No. 17, §4; Acts 1972, No. 209, §2; Acts 1982, No. 278, §1; Acts 1991, No. 504, §1, eff. July 15, 1991; Acts 1997, No. 83, §1.
