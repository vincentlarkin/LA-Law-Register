§2355. Records

            A. The board shall keep a record of its proceedings and a register of all applications for licensure.

            B. The board shall publish or cause to be published annually a list of psychologists, licensed psychological associates, and licensed specialists in school psychology licensed in accordance with the provisions of this Chapter.

            Added by Acts 1964, No. 347, §5; Acts 1987, No. 915, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1987; Acts 2014, No. 136, §1; Acts 2022, No. 271, §2; Acts 2024, No. 286, §1.
