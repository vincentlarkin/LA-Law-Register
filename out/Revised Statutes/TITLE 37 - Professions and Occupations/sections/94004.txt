§3217.  Penalty
Any person who violates the provisions of R.S. 37:3213 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and, upon conviction, shall be fined not more than five hundred dollars or imprisoned for not more than six months, or both.
Acts 1984, No. 485, §1.
