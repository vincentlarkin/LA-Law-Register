§1022.  Drugs which may be administered
Nonlicensed personnel who have successfully completed and passed the drug administration course may administer oral medication, ointments, suppositories, but shall not administer any medication by intramuscular, intravenous, or subcutaneous routes.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 558, §1.
