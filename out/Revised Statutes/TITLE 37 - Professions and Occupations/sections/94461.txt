§795.  Fees and costs
A.  The board is authorized, by rule, in accordance with the Administrative Procedure
Act, to establish fees and costs to be imposed for the purpose of implementing and enforcing
the provisions of this Chapter.  However, the board may, by a majority vote, reduce the
amount of or waive the collection of any such fees.  The fees established under the provisions
of this Section shall be paid to the secretary-treasurer.
