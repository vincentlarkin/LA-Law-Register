CHAPTER 13.  MENTAL HEALTH COUNSELORS
PART I.  SHORT TITLE AND PURPOSE
§1101.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Mental Health
Counselor Licensing Act".
Acts 1987, No. 892, §1, eff. July 20, 1987; Acts 2022, No. 341, §2A.
