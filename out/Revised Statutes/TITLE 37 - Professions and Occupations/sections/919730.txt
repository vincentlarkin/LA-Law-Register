§1360.86.  Direct practice not an insurer
A direct practice that complies with the provisions of this Part is not a health insurance insurer and not subject to the provisions of nor the regulations under Title 22 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
Acts 2014, No. 867, §1.
