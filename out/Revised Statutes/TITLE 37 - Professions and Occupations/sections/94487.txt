§85.  Single act evidence of practice
Evidence of the commission of a single act prohibited by this Part or any rules or
regulations adopted pursuant to this Part is sufficient to justify additional fees, assessment
of reasonable costs, a penalty, fine, injunction, restraining order, or conviction without
evidence of a general course of conduct.
Acts 1979, No. 510, §1; Acts 1999, No. 473, §1, eff. June 18, 1999; Acts 2016, No.
553, §1.
