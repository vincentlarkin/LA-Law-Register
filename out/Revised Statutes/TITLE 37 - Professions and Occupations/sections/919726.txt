§1360.82.  Prohibition on discrimination
Except as provided in R.S. 37:1360.85, no direct practice shall decline to accept any person solely on account of race, religion, national origin, the presence of any sensory, mental, or physical disability, education, or economic status.
Acts 2014, No. 867, §1.
