§3476.  Failure to renew license; penalties

The failure to pay the renewal fee required by R.S. 37:3475 shall result in an automatic revocation of the license.  In such cases, the person shall be reinstated if he files an application for reinstatement with the board within one year after the revocation and pays a reinstatement fee and all delinquent charges as provided by the board.

Acts 1988, No. 852, §1; Acts 1995, No. 1152, §1, eff. June 29, 1995.
