§1448.2.  Revocation or suspension of registration
A timeshare registrant will within seventy-two hours of notification by the commission of the final action on the revocation or suspension of a registration issued to a timeshare interest salesperson or a timeshare developer return such registration to the commission.
Acts 1989, No. 655, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
