§356.  Certificate of registration for barber college required
A.  No person shall operate a barber college unless he holds a certificate of registration in good standing therefor issued by the board and unless such certificate is displayed at all times in a conspicuous place on the premises.
B.  The board shall issue a certificate of registration for each college that complies with this Chapter and the regulations of the board.
Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Amended by Acts 1976, No. 424, §4; Acts 1978, No. 58, §1; Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
