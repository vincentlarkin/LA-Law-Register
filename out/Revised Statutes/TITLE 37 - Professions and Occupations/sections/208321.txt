§934.  Prepayment and post-payment utilization review
Nothing in this Part shall prohibit a registered nurse who is properly licensed and recognized by the board from performing prepayment or post-payment utilization review of claims submitted for reimbursement under the Louisiana Medical Assistance Program provided that such review is performed under the direction of a licensed physician.
Acts 2003, No. 673, §1.
