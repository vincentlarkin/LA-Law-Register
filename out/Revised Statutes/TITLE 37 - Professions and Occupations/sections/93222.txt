§1360.38.  Exemptions
A.  The provisions of this Part shall not apply to:
(1)  Practitioners of allied health fields duly licensed in accordance with other laws
of this state.
(2)  A physician assistant administering medical services in cases of emergency.
(3)  A physician assistant student enrolled in a physician assistant educational
program accredited by the Accreditation Review Commission on Education for the Physician
Assistant.
(4)  A physician assistant employed by the federal government while performing
duties incidental to that employment.
B.  Nothing herein shall prohibit or limit the authority of physicians to employ
auxiliary personnel not recognized under this Part.
Acts 1993, No. 662, §1, eff. June 16, 1993; Acts 1997, No. 316, §1; Acts 2015, No.
