§3282.  Notification of changes

The licensee shall notify the board within ten days of any change in its officers, directors, or other material change in the information previously furnished or required to be furnished to the board, or immediately upon any occurrence which could reasonably be expected to affect the licensee's right to a license under this Chapter.  

Acts 1984, No. 505, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985; Act 1991, No. 315, §1.
