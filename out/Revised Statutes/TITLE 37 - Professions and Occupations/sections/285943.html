§§3388.3. Applicability

            A. It is the intent of the legislature that there be a single authority issuing credentials for addictive disorder counseling and prevention professionals in Louisiana. The authority for issuing and recognizing credentials for professionals engaged in the providing of counseling and prevention services for those who suffer from addictive disorders shall be the Addictive Disorder Regulatory Authority.

            B. Repealed by Acts 2020, No. 328, §3.
