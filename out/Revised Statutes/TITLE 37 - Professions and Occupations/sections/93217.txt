§1360.33.  Violations
The board may, exercising due process, discipline any physician assistant, as
provided in R.S. 37:1360.34, who:
(1)  Fraudulently or deceptively obtains or attempts to obtain a license.
(2)  Fraudulently or deceptively uses a license.
(3)  Violates any provision of this Part or any regulations adopted by the board
pertaining to this Part.
(4)  Is convicted of a felony.
(5)  Is a habitual user of intoxicants or drugs to such an extent that he is unable to
safely perform as a physician assistant.
(6)  Has been adjudicated as mentally incompetent or has a mental condition that
renders him unable to safely perform as a physician assistant.
(7)  Repealed by Acts 2024, No. 644, §2.
(8)  Represents himself as a physician.
Acts 1993, No. 662, §1, eff. June 16, 1993; Acts 2024, No. 644, §2.
