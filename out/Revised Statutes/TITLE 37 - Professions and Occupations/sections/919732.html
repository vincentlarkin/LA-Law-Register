§1360.88.  Misrepresenting the terms of a direct agreement

A person shall not make, issue, circulate, or cause to be made, issued, or circulated, a misrepresentation of the terms of any direct agreement, the benefits or advantages promised thereby, or use the name or title of any direct agreement misrepresenting the nature thereof.

Acts 2014, No. 867, §1.
