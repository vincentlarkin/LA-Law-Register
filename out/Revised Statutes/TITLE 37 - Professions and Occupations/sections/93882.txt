§3004.  License required
No person shall practice occupational therapy or hold himself out as an occupational
therapist or an occupational therapy assistant, or as being able to practice occupational
therapy or to render occupational therapy services in the state unless he is licensed in
accordance with the provisions of this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 566, §1; Acts 2023, No. 263, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2024.
