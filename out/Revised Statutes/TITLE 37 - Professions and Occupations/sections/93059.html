§1115.  Applicability

The licensing of individuals under the provisions of this Chapter shall not be construed to authorize those individuals to practice medicine or psychiatry in violation of the provisions of Part I of Chapter 15 of Title 37 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.  

Acts 1987, No. 892, §1, eff. July 20, 1987.
