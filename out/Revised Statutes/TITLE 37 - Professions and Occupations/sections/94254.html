§385.  Information not to be divulged

No member of the board and no officer, agent, or employee thereof shall divulge to any person the contents of any document, paper, or record examined by him in the performance of his duties under this Chapter or any information obtained by him in the course of his investigations except when necessary to carry out the purpose of this Chapter.  

Acts 1964, No. 309, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 238, §1.
