§1257. Pharmacy benefit managers; rules and regulations; reporting requirements; fees

            A. The board shall promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to implement the provisions of this Part and the applicable provisions of the Pharmacy Benefit Manager Licensing Law, R.S. 40:2861 et seq.

            B. The board may promulgate rules and regulations to specify permit renewal reporting requirements for pharmacy benefit managers.

            C. The board shall charge fees for the issuance of pharmacy benefit manager permits in the amounts authorized by R.S. 37:1184.

            Acts 2019, No. 124, §2, eff. July 1, 2020; Acts 2020, No. 202, §2, eff. July 1, 2020.
