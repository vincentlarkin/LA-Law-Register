§2829.  Reciprocity certification
The board may grant certification of proficiency to any person who is certified in the taking of chiropractic x-rays by the American Chiropractic Registry of Radiologic Technologists or who, by examination, holds a certificate of proficiency in chiropractic x-ray procedures from any state.
Acts 1984, No. 249, §1.
