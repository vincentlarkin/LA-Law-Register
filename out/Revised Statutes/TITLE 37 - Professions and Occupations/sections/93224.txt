§1362. Term of office
The members of the board shall serve for six years and shall remain serving after their
term expires until their successors are appointed by the governor.
Acts 1990, No. 752, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1991; Acts 2025, No. 438, §1, eff. June 20, 2025.
