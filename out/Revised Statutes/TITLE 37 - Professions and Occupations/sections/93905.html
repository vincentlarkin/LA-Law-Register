CHAPTER 41.  DIETITIANS AND NUTRITIONISTS

§3081.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Dietetics/Nutrition Practice Act of 1987".  

Acts 1987, No. 574, §1, eff. July 9, 1987.
