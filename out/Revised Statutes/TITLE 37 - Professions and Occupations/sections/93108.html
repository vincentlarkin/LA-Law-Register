§1213.  Notification of change of business place or employment

A pharmacist, pharmacy technician, pharmacy intern, or pharmacy technician candidate shall notify the board, in writing, of any change of employment within a time frame determined by the board by rule.

Acts 1999, No. 767, §1; Acts 2012, No. 357, §1.
