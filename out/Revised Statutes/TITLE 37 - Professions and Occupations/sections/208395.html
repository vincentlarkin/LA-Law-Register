§1869.1.  Prohibitions; sale of certain uniforms

No secondhand dealer, including those secondhand dealers exempted by R.S. 37:1861(B) shall sell or offer for sale any article of clothing, including but not limited to hats, shirts, pants, or jackets, which exhibits or displays the insignia of any law enforcement agency or public utility.

Acts 2003, No. 1162, §1.
