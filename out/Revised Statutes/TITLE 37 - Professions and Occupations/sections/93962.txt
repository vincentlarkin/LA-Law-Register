§3153.  Examinations
The board shall hold not fewer than four examinations per year for the two years following the effective date of this Chapter for the purpose of examining candidates for certification.  After the initial two-year period, the board shall hold not fewer than two examinations per year.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 543, §1.
