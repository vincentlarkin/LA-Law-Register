§1211.  Pharmacy intern

The board shall establish an internship program for the purpose of providing the practical experience necessary for licensure as a pharmacist.  The board shall adopt rules and regulations regarding the standards and qualifications for internship programs.

Acts 1999, No. 767, §1.
