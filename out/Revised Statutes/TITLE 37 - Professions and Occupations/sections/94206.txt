CHAPTER 57.  MASSAGE THERAPISTS AND ESTABLISHMENTS
§3551.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Massage Therapists and Massage Establishments Act".
Acts 1992, No. 753, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1992.
