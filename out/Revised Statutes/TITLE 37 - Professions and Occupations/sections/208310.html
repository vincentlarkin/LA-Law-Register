§881.  Refusal to grant or renew licenses; revocation or suspension; grounds; hearings

A.  The board may refuse to grant or refuse to renew, or may suspend or revoke any license when the applicant or licensee is found guilty of any of the acts or omissions set forth in R.S. 37:846(A).

B.  When considering the revocation or suspension of a license, petitions shall be filed, notices shall be given, and hearings shall be conducted in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 37:846(B).

Acts 2003, No. 1243, §2.
