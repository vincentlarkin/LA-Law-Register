§1316. Receipts and disbursements

            All monies collected by the board pursuant to this Part shall be deposited in the treasury of the board. All committee expenses shall be paid out of such funds only and shall not be charged to or be payable by the state. The financial records of the board shall be audited in accordance with state law by the legislative auditor or an independent auditor approved by the legislative auditor.

            Acts 1993, No. 396, §2, eff. Aug. 1, 1993; Acts 2022, No. 653, §1.
