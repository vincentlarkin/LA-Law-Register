§839.  Quorum

Four members of the board shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of all its business and the performance of all its duties.  

Amended by Acts 1966, No. 19, §1, eff. June 15, 1966 at 10:30 A.M.  Acts 1986, No. 782, §1, eff. noon, July 11, 1988; Acts 1988, No. 275, §1, eff. July 1, 1988.
