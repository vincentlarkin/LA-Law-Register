§3390.5.  Penalties
Whoever violates any provision of this Chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be punished by a fine of not less than one hundred dollars nor more than five hundred dollars, or imprisonment for not more than six months, or both.
Acts 2004, No. 803, §3, eff. July 8, 2004.
