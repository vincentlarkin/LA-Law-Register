CHAPTER 1-C. LICENSES FOR EX-OFFENDERS

§31. Short title

            This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Licenses for Ex-Offenders Act".

            Acts 2014, No. 809, §1, eff. June 19, 2014; Acts 2017, No. 262, §1.
