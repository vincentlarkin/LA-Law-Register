§23.1.  Licenses, permits, or certificates for individuals with an ADA-recognized disorder
Any board or commission created and provided for within this Title may develop a
process to issue a license, permit, or certificate outside the national examination for those
individuals with a disorder which is recognized by the Americans with Disabilities Act.
Acts 2018, No. 515, §2; Acts 2018, No. 655, §1.
