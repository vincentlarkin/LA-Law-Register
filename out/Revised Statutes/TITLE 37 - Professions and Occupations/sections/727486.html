§711.11.  Rosters and lists

A.  A roster showing the names and addresses of all professional geoscientists and the geoscience discipline under which each professional geoscientist has been listed, and the names and addresses of all geoscientists-in-training may be published at the discretion of the board.

B.  The board shall maintain a list of each state or foreign country in which the requirements and qualifications for licensure or registration are comparable to those established in this state and with which a reciprocity agreement exists.

Acts 2010, No. 974, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
