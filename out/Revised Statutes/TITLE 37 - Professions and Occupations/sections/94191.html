§3515.  Training of investigators; requirements

A.  The board shall approve all training programs.  

B.  All training required by this Chapter shall be administered by a licensee who:

(1)  Is approved by the board.  

(2)  Meets the qualifications of an applicant required by R.S. 37:3507.  

(3)  Has a minimum of three years supervisory experience with a contract investigator company or proprietary investigator organization.  

Acts 1992, No. 245, §2.
