§1879.  Prohibited practices
An antique dealer shall not:
(1)  Accept cemetery artifacts or used building components from any natural person under the age of eighteen.
(2)  Knowingly accept an object that they know or should have known has been stolen, and the original use of which was a cemetery artifact or used building component.
(3)  Falsify, obliterate, remove from the records, or fail to include in the report required in this Part any accounts relating to the antique dealer's transactions involving objects which the antique dealer knew or should have known were cemetery artifacts or used building components as defined in this Part.
Acts 1999, No. 218, §1; Acts 2003, No. 1184, §1, eff. July 3, 2003.
