§2362. Advertisement of psychological services

            The board may promulgate regulations to prohibit deceptive advertisements and representations concerning psychological services and may enforce this Section and its regulations in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 37:2361.

            Added by Acts 1964, No. 347, §12; Acts 1987, No. 915, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1987; Acts 2022, No. 271, §2.
