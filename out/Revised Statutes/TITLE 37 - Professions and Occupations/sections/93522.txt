§1880.  Retention of purchases
It shall be unlawful for an antique dealer to sell, exchange, barter, or remove from his place of business any cemetery artifact or used building component that has been bought, exchanged, or deposited with him by a seller for a period of thirty days from the date of the antique dealer's acquisition.
Acts 1999, No. 218, §1; Acts 2003, No. 1184, §1, eff. July 3, 2003.
