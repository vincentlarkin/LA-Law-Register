§74.1. Fees

            The board is authorized to adopt rules in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to impose and collect fees which shall not exceed the following:

            (1)       Original or reciprocal certification application          $250.00

            (2)       Reinstatement application                                          $500.00

            (3)       Notice under substantial equivalency                         $200.00

                        applicants and registrants                                           $100.00

                        CPA-Inactive status                                                    $100.00

            (7)       Registration and renewal fee for

                        CPA-Retired status                                                     $50.00

                        or evaluate education courses and

                        qualifications                                                              $200.00

                        February 1                                                                  $200.00 per month
