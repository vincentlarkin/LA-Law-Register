§3434. Advisory committee; meetings

            A statewide advisory committee established by the board in carrying out its duties pursuant to this Chapter may conduct and its members may attend and participate in a meeting via electronic means in accordance with R.S. 42:17.3.

            Acts 2022, No. 408, §1.
