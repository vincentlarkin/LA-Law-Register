§1369.  Temporary working permits

Temporary working permits to perform work as a journeyman plumber may be issued as required by R.S. 37:1376 and may be issued to a holder of a state license as a journeyman plumber from a state with like examinations, should that state recognize the Louisiana license, or where other bona fide evidence shows that the applicant's past experience would be capable of protecting the public from defective plumbing.  Each temporary permit must be signed by both the chairman and secretary of the board.  A reasonable fee may be charged for the issuance of temporary permits, as fixed by the board.  Temporary permits shall not be issued to master plumbers.  

Acts 1990, No. 752, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
