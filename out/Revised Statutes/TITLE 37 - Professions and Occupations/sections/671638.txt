CHAPTER 51-A.  APPRAISAL MANAGEMENT COMPANY
LICENSING AND REGULATION ACT
§3415.1.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Appraisal Management Company Licensing and Regulation Act".
Acts 2009, No. 502, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2010.
