§3156.  Violations; operation without certification
A.  On or after four years following the effective date of this Chapter, it shall be unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation to operate a solid waste disposal facility which has been classified by the board unless the board duly certifies the competency of the operator under the provisions of this Chapter.  Furthermore, it shall be unlawful for any person to perform the duties of an operator, as defined herein, without being duly certified under the provisions of this Chapter.
B.  Certificates shall be prominently displayed at the operators facility.  Failure to do so shall be a violation of this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1983, No. 543, §1.
