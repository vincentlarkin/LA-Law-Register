CHAPTER 20.  MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS COMMON TO

CERTAIN PROFESSIONS

PART I.  PHYSICIANS, PHARMACISTS AND DENTISTS

§1661.  Proof of registration when applying for state license

State tax collectors, sheriffs, or other officers exercising the duties of a state tax collector shall require satisfactory proof of registration in accordance with the provisions of this Title from all practitioners of medicine, pharmacy, and dentistry when applying for state license.
