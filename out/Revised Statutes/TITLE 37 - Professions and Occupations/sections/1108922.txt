§1208.1.  Waiver of license or certificate renewal fee for military spouse
Upon written request of any licensed pharmacist or certified technician who is the
spouse of an active-duty member of the armed forces of the United States, the board may
waive the requirement of the fee for the renewal of a pharmacist license or technician
certificate in accordance with the rules promulgated by the board.
Acts 2018, No. 63, §1.
