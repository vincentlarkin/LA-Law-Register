§762.  Licenses; data on

All licenses issued by the board shall bear a serial number, the full name of the licensee, the date of issuance, and the seal of the board; and shall be signed by the president and members of the board.  

Amended by Acts 1979, No. 310, §1, eff. July 10, 1979.
