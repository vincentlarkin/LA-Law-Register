§1965.  Change in location to be noted on license
If after issuance and delivery of a license under the provisions of this Chapter any change is made in the location of the place of business designated therein, the business shall not be conducted at the new location or under such license, until the official issuing the license notes the change on the license, and the superintendent of police of the city or the sheriff of the parish in which the scrap metal recycling facility is doing business is notified in writing of the change.
Acts 2012, No. 827, §1, eff. June 14, 2012.
