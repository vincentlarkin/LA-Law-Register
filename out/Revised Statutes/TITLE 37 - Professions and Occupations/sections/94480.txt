§843.  Registration and display of certificate
A.  The board shall register each certificate in the office of public health of the
Louisiana Department of Health.
B.  Each certificate holder shall display his certificate in a conspicuous place in his
office or place of business.
Amended by Acts 1977, No. 283, §3; Acts 1977, No. 684, §3; Acts 2018, No. 206,
§3.
