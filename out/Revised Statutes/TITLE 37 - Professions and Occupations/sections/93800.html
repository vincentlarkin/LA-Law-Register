§2819. Louisiana State Board of Chiropractic Examiners; chiropractic scholarships; creation

            There are hereby created annual chiropractic scholarships to be administered by the Louisiana Board of Chiropractic Examiners which may be awarded each fiscal year, beginning July 1, 1978, to persons recommended by the Louisiana Board of Chiropractic Examiners for the study of chiropractic leading to the attainment of the degree of Doctor of Chiropractic.

            Added by Acts 1978, No. 705, §1.
