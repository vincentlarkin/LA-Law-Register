§1267. Quorum

            Six members of the board constitute a quorum for all purposes including the holding of examinations, the granting of licenses and permits, rulemaking, and, except as provided in R.S. 37:1285.1, the adjudication functions of the board.

            Acts 1975, No. 350, §1; Acts 2003, No. 209, §1, eff. June 5, 2003; Acts 2015, No. 441, §1, eff. July 1, 2015; Acts 2018, No. 599, §1.
