§2846.  Change of business address

Notice in writing shall be given to the secretary by the certified polygraphist of any change of principal business location within thirty days of the time he changes the location.  A change of business location without such notification to the secretary shall automatically suspend the certificate theretofore issued.  

Added by Acts 1980, No. 761, §1.
