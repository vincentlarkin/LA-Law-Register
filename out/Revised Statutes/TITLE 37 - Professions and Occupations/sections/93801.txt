§2820.  Scholarships; funding
The board is authorized and empowered to accept any federal, state, parish or private funds, grants, or appropriations which may be used to award scholarships to qualified persons to study chiropractic in an approved chiropractic college.  The Louisiana Board of Chiropractic Examiners shall have the authority to make reasonable rules and regulations for carrying out the provisions of this act.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 705, §1.
