CHAPTER 53.  REHABILITATION COUNSELORS

§3441.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Rehabilitation Counselor Licensing Act".  

Acts 1988, No. 555, §1, eff. July 14, 1988.
