§3713. Continuing education

            A.(1) A licensed behavior analyst shall annually complete a minimum number of hours of continuing education relevant to the practice of behavior analysis as established by the board.

            (2) A certified assistant behavior analyst shall annually complete a minimum number of hours of continuing education relevant to the practice of behavior analysis as established by the board.

            B. The board shall develop the requirements for continuing education.

            C. The continuing education classes shall be approved by the board.

            Acts 2013, No. 351, §2.

            NOTE: See R.S. 37:3718 regarding termination date of Chapter on July 1, 2028.
