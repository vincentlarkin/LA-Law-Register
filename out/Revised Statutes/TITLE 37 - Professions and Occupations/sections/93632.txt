CHAPTER 28.  PSYCHOLOGISTS
PART I.  LICENSURE AND REGULATION OF PSYCHOLOGISTS
§2351.  Declaration of purpose
It is hereby declared that the creation of a state board of examiners of psychologists
is necessary in order to safeguard life, health, property, and the public welfare of this state,
and in order to protect the people of this state against unauthorized, unqualified, and
improper application of psychology.
Added by Acts 1954, No. 371, §14; Acts 2018, No. 206, §3; Acts 1964, No. 347, §1;
Acts 1987, No. 915, §2, eff. Sept. 1, 1987; Acts 2004, No. 11, §2; Acts 2018, No. 206, §3.
NOTE:  See Acts 1987, No. 915, §3.
