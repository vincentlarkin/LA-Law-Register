§923.  Appeal from board decision
Any decision of the board may be appealed to the court of competent jurisdiction within thirty days after mailing of notice of the decision by certified mail.
Acts 1976, No. 351, §1; Acts 1995, No. 633, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
