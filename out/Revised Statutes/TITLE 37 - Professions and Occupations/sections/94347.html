§597.  Display of certificate of registration

Every holder of a certificate of registration issued by the board shall display it in a conspicuous place in his principal place of business or employment.  Additionally, any facility licensed by the board shall display any other documents required to be displayed by the board's rules.

Acts 2001, No. 907, §2, eff. June 26, 2001.
