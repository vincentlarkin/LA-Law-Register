TITLE 37 PROFESSIONS AND OCCUPATIONS

CHAPTER 1.  DEPARTMENT OF OCCUPATIONAL STANDARDS

§1.  Definitions

As used in this Chapter:

(1)  "Department" means the Department of Occupational Standards; and

(2)  "Director" means the Director of Occupational Standards.
