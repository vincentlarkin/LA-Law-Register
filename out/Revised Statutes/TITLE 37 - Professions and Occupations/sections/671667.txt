§3415.21.  Rulemaking authority
A.  The board may adopt any rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act necessary for the enforcement of this Chapter.
B.  Repealed by Acts 2014, No.764, §1.
Acts 2009, No. 502, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2010; Acts 2012, No. 429, §1, eff. May 31, 2012; Acts 2014, No. 764, §1, eff. June 19, 2014.
