§1365.  Domicile of the board

The domicile of the board shall be in the city of Baton Rouge; however, the State Plumbing Board may, in its discretion, maintain and establish district offices elsewhere in the state of Louisiana.  

Amended by Acts 1968, No. 298, §1; Acts 1984, No. 608, §1.
