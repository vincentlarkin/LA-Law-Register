§1966.  Hours of business
An operator shall not enter into any transactions for the purchase of scrap metal before the hour of 7:00 a.m. nor after the hour of 7:00 p.m. except on Saturdays and during the month of December, during which time the hour of 7:00 p.m. shall be extended to 10:00 p.m.
Acts 2012, No. 827, §1, eff. June 14, 2012.
