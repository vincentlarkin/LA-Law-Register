§2367.  Orders to nurses
Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, including but not limited
to the provisions of Chapter 11 of this Title, it shall be within the scope of the practice of
nursing as defined in Chapter 11 of this Title for a registered nurse, licensed practical nurse,
and any other healthcare provider licensed in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 11
of this Title to execute and effectuate any order or direction otherwise within the scope of
practice of that healthcare provider when the order is within the scope of practice of
psychology and given to him by a psychologist licensed in accordance with the provisions
of this Chapter and, when given in an institutional setting, the order is within the scope of
the privileges granted to the psychologist by that institution.
Acts 1995, No. 1302, §1, eff. June 29, 1995; Acts 2022, No. 271, §2.
