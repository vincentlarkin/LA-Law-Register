§3010.  Display of license or certificates
Licenses and renewal certificates issued under the provisions of this Chapter shall be
conspicuously displayed in the principal office of the licensee.
Acts 1979, No. 566, §1; Acts 2023, No. 263, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2024.
