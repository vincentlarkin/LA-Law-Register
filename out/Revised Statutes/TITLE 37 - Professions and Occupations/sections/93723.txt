§2583.  Requirement of license
On or after thirty days from the effective date of this Chapter, it shall be unlawful for any person to operate a financial planning and management service or engage in said business as herein defined except as authorized by this Chapter and without first having obtained a license as hereinafter provided.
Acts 1970, No. 423, §3.
