CHAPTER 58-A. PUBLICATION OF INFORMATION BY PROFESSIONAL

OR OCCUPATIONAL LICENSING BOARDS

§3611. Definitions

            For purposes of this Chapter, "professional or occupational licensing board" means any state agency, board, commission, or substantially similar entity involved in the licensing, certification, or registration of any regulated profession or occupation within this state.

            Acts 2022, No. 483, §1.
