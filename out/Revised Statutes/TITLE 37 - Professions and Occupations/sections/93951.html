PART IV.  ADVERTISING AUCTION SALES

§3140.  Statement in proces verbal of manner, time, and place of making advertisement

In all auction sales made by sheriffs, auctioneers, or others authorized to sell at public auction that are required by law to be preceded by advertisement, the officer making the sale in his proces verbal or act of sale shall state the manner, time, and place of making the advertisements.  This statement shall be proof of the manner, time, and place of making the advertisement.  

Acts 1986, No. 564, §6, eff. July 2, 1986.
