§191.1.  Operating motorcycles on roadways laned for traffic
A.  All motorcycles are entitled to full use of a lane and no motor vehicle shall be
driven in such manner as to deprive any motorcycle of the full use of a lane.  This Subsection
shall not apply to motorcycles operated two abreast in a single lane.
B.  The operator of a motorcycle shall not overtake and pass in the same lane
occupied by the vehicle being overtaken.
C.  No person shall operate a motorcycle between lanes of traffic or between adjacent
lines or rows of vehicles.
D.  Motorcycles shall not be operated more than two abreast in a single lane.
E.  Subsections (B) and (C) shall not apply to police officers in the performance of
their official duties.
F.  Penalties imposed by this Chapter shall be applicable to any driver or operator of
a vehicle, as defined in R.S. 32:1, who is found guilty of or pleads guilty or nolo contendere
to a violation of the provisions of this Chapter, including this Section, or any regulation of
the department, secretary, and commissioner made pursuant thereto.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 538, §5; Acts 1999, No. 631, §1; Acts 2022, No. 739, §1.
