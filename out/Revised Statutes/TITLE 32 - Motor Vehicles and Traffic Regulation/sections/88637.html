§905.  Owner may give proof for others

Whenever any person required to give proof of financial responsibility hereunder is or later becomes an operator in the employ of any owner, or is or later becomes a member of the immediate family or household of the owner, the commissioner shall accept proof given by such owner in lieu of proof by such other person to permit such other person to operate a motor vehicle for which the owner has given proof as herein provided.  The commissioner shall designate the restrictions imposed by this Section on the face of such person's license.

Acts 1952, No. 52, §26.
