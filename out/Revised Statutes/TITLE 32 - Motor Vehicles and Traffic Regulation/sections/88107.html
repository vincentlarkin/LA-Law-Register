§1517.  Personnel and expenses

A.  The secretary shall employ such persons as he considers qualified, consistent with applicable civil service regulations, to carry out the provisions of this Chapter.

B.  The secretary may incur such expenses as may be required to carry out the provisions of this Chapter.

Added by Acts 1979, No. 83, §1.
