§396.  Commissioner to publish records
As often as practicable, but at least once each month, the commissioner shall either publish or post upon public bulletin boards in each of his offices, a record of stolen and recovered motor vehicles and of suspensions and revocations of operators' and chauffeurs' licenses.  He also shall furnish copies of such records to the police departments and sheriffs' offices throughout the state.
Acts 1962, No. 310, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 113, §1, eff. June 22, 1977.
