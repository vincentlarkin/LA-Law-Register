§715.  Change of engine in vehicle

The commissioner is authorized to adopt and enforce such registration rules and regulations with respect to the change or substitution of one engine or motor in place of another in any vehicle as may be deemed necessary and consistent with the purposes of this Chapter.

Acts 1950, No. 342, §18.
