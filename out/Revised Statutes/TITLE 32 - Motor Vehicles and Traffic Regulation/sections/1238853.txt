§1270.41.  Exclusiveness
This Part provides exclusive remedies, warranties, and peremptive periods as between
the manufacturer, dealer, and consumer, relative to nonconformity defects as defined in this
Part, and no other provisions of law relative to recreational vehicle warranties and redhibitory
vices and defects shall apply. Nothing herein shall be construed to affect or limit any
warranty of title.
Acts 2021, No. 220, §1.
