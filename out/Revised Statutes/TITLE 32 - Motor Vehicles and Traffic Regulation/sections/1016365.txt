§127.3.  Golf carts; Lake Fausse Pointe State Park
A.  Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, a golf cart may be
operated between sunrise and sunset on the roadways within the boundaries of Lake Fausse
Pointe State Park by any operator who is in possession of a valid driver's license and liability
insurance.
B.  For the purposes of this Section, the provisions of R.S. 32:53(D), 261, and
Chapter 5 of this Title shall not apply, and golf carts shall not be considered a vehicle for
purposes of defining "equipment" as referenced in Part V of Chapter 1 of this Title.
C.  For the purposes of this Section, "golf cart" means a vehicle that was originally
intended for use off-road on golf courses and other green spaces and that has a maximum
speed of twenty-five miles per hour.
