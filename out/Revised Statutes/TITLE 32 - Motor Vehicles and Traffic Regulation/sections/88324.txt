§385.1.  Recreational vehicles; exemptions
Recreational vehicles as defined in R.S. 32:771 and truck campers may exceed the maximum width provided in R.S. 32:380 if such excess width is attributable to an appurtenance that extends no more than six inches beyond the body of the vehicle.
Acts 2001, No. 1207, §1.
