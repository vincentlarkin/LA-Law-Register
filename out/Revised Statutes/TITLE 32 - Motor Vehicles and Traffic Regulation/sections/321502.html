§867.  Private parking lots

A.  The provisions of this Part shall apply to the operation of a motor vehicle in any privately owned parking lot that is utilized for commercial or retail activities.

B.  The provisions of this Part shall not apply to the operation of a motor vehicle on private land on which activities related to agriculture, as is defined in R.S. 3:263(2), are performed provided that the motor vehicle used in the activity is not operated on a public road or highway of the state of Louisiana.

C.  The provisions of this Part shall not apply to any legally parked vehicle.

Acts 2005, No. 265, §1.
