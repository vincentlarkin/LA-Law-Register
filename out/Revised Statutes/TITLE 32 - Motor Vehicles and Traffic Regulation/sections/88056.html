§1309.  Fee for inspection certificates

The fees collected for the issuance of certificates of inspection and approval shall be deposited in the state treasury upon receipt and shall be credited to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund as required by Article VII, Section 9 of the Constitution of Louisiana.

Acts 1960, No. 405, §8.  Acts 1975, No. 610, §2, Amended by Acts 1983, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 33, §2; eff. Jan. 19, 1983; Acts 1986, No. 52, §1; Acts 1992, No. 984, §10.
