PART VIII.  METHOD OF CITATION

§399.  Citation

Chapter I of Title 32 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes as amended and revised shall be known as the Louisiana Highway Regulatory Act.  The provisions hereinafter may be referred to or cited either as Sections of the Highway Regulatory Act or as Sections of the Revised Statutes.  Thus Section 5 of the Highway Regulatory Act may also be referred to or cited as R.S. 32:5.

Whenever reference is made herein to a Section of the Highway Regulatory Act, the same shall also relate to the corresponding Section of the Revised Statutes.

Acts 1962, No. 310, §1.
