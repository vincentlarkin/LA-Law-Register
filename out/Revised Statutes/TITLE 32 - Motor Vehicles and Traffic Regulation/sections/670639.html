§668.1.  Procedure following revocation or denial of license; discovery

Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary, prehearing discovery, in conjunction with the administrative hearing provided for in R.S. 32:668, shall not include depositions of law enforcement witnesses.

Acts 2009, No. 287, §1.
