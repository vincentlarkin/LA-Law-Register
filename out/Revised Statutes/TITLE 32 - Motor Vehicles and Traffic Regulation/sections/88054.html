§1307.  Improper representation as official station

A.  No person shall in any manner represent any place as an official inspection station unless such station is operating under a valid permit issued by the department.

B.  No person other than a duly authorized officer or employee of inspection station shall issue a certificate of inspection and approval.

Acts 1960, No. 405, §7.
