§264.  Authority to regulate traffic through tunnels

A.  The department may provide regulations to govern the use of all tunnels, including regulations excluding undesirable types of traffic or loads.  No over-weight or over-sized permits may be granted for passage through a tunnel.

B.  Regulations adopted pursuant to this Section shall be posted in a conspicuous place near each vehicular entrance to the tunnel.

Acts 1962, No. 310, §1.
