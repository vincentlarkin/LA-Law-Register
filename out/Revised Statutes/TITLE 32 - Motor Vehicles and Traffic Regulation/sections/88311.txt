§376.  Special lights for certain department vehicles
The department is authorized to install on vehicles used by it incidental to the painting of stripes on highways, two fixed white lights, directed to the rear, which lights may be illuminated while the vehicles are in use upon the highway.
Added by Acts 1963, No. 33, §3.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 113, §1, eff. June 22, 1977; Acts 2006, No. 11, §2.
