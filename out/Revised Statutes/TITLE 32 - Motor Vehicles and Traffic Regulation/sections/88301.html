§366.  Required equipment of tow cars

Tow cars shall be equipped with the following:

(1)  One or more brooms, and the driver of the tow car engaged to remove a disabled vehicle from the scene of an accident shall remove all glass and debris deposited upon the roadway by the disabled vehicle which is to be towed.

(2)  A shovel, and whenever practical the tow car driver engaged to remove any disabled vehicle shall spread dirt upon that portion of the roadway where oil or grease has been deposited by such disabled vehicle.

(3)  A fire extinguisher of at least two quart capacity of a type capable of extinguishing a fire of flammable liquid.

Acts 1962, No. 310, §1.
