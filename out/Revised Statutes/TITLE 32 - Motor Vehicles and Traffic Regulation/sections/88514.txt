§703.  Offices of commissioner
The vehicle commissioner shall maintain an office in the State Capitol at Baton Rouge and such other places in this state as he shall deem necessary, to properly carry out the provisions of this Chapter.  The custodian of the capitol building shall provide therein adequate quarters for the offices and records of the vehicle commissioner.
Acts 1950, No. 342, §6.
