§57.2.  Fee for expenses incurred for renewal or reissuance of suspended operator's license; office of the prosecuting authority

Each person seeking renewal or reissuance of a suspended driver's license, as provided in R.S. 32:57.1, shall pay to the office of the prosecuting authority an additional fee of twenty-five dollars to defray the administrative costs incurred by the office of the prosecuting authority in handling such renewal or reissuance of the suspended driver's license.

Acts 2003, No. 966, §1.
