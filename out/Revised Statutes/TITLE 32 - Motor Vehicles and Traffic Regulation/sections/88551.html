§734.  Signature to documents

Whenever the commissioner is required to sign any document under this Chapter he may, at his discretion, perform the act of signing in person or by duly authorized agent, and may use or cause to be used a signature machine for the purpose of affixing his signature to such documents.

Acts 1950, No. 342, §37.
