PART III. DISMANTLERS AND PARTS RECYCLERS; MOTOR VEHICLE

CRUSHERS; AND SCRAPPED MOTOR VEHICLE DEALERS

§801. Definition

As used in this Part only:

            "Motor vehicle" means any motor-driven vehicle required to be registered that was used, is used, or is designed to be used for the transporting of passengers or goods for public, private, commercial, or for-hire purposes.

            Acts 2006, No. 440, §1; Acts 2018, No. 435, §1, eff. May 23, 2018.
