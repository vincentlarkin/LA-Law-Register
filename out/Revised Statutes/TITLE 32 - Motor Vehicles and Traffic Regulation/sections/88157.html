§1732.  Third party surcharges

Whenever a motor vehicle is towed to a location other than a facility owned and operated by the tow company, the surcharge levied by the third party receiving the motor vehicle shall not exceed twenty-five percent of the amount charged for the initial tow of the vehicle, including other related towing charges such as storage and administration fees.

Acts 1992, No. 1089, §1; Acts 2001, No. 400, §1.
