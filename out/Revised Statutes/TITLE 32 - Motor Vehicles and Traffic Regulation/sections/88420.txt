§428.  Failure to enforce requirement
Notwithstanding the testing, licensing, and reporting requirements herein, it shall not be a breach of duty to the public or any individual if the department inadvertently fails to enforce said requirements or waives same pursuant to the authority granted herein.
Acts 1989, No. 293, §1, eff. June 27, 1989.
