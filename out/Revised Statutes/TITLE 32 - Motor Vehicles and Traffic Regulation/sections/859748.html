§1524.3.  Contract carriers; penalties

A.  The provisions of R.S. 32:1524, 1524.1, and 1524.2 shall be considered minimum standards and shall not be construed to supercede or abrogate any law, rule, or regulation which imposes stricter standards or regulations upon the operation of contract carriers which transport railroad employees.

B.  Any person, corporation, or entity that violates the provisions of R.S. 32:1524, 1524.1, and 1524.2 or any rule promulgated thereto, shall be subject to a civil penalty in an amount not more than two thousand dollars for each offense or violation.  Each violation shall constitute a separate and distinct offense.  In the event of continuing violations, each such violation shall constitute a distinct and separate offense.

Acts 2013, No. 193, §1.
