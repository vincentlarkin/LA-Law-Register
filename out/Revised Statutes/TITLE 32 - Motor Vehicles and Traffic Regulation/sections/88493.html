§630.  Violations; penalties

It is unlawful to manufacture, sell or distribute any brake fluid which is adulterated or misbranded, or which is not registered, or which is without a valid permit as required by this Part.  Violation of any provision of this Part is a misdemeanor.  The first offense shall be punishable by a fine of not more than $1,000.00 and the permanent cancellation of the offender's permit.

Acts 1958, No. 279, §10.
