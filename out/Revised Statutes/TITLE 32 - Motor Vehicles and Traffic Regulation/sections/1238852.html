§1270.40. Notification of nonconformity remedy; dealer responsibility

            Prior to or during the delivery of the recreational vehicle to the consumer, the dealer shall inform the consumer in writing of the remedy for a nonconformity defect as provided in this Part.

            Acts 2021, No. 220, §1.
