§400.4. Owners and operators of an autonomous commercial motor vehicle

            The automated driving system of an autonomous commercial motor vehicle and the person or entity required to submit a statement to the Department of Transportation and Development pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 32:400.3(C) shall be subject to all applicable laws, rules, ordinances, and statutes of this state and will be considered to be licensed to operate the vehicle. The person or entity operating the autonomous commercial motor vehicle may be issued a traffic citation or other applicable penalty if the vehicle fails to comply with any traffic or motor vehicle laws of this state.

            Acts 2019, No. 232, §1.
