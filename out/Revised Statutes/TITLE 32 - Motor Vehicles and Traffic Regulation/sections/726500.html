§387.9.1.  Special permits; vehicles hauling agronomic or horticultural crops; authorization to travel upon segment of Interstate 49 as alternate route to US 71 and US 167

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, and provided the federal government does not raise any objection to such permitted weight limit, any vehicle issued an annual special permit pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 32:387.9 may travel upon that segment of Interstate 49 between its interchange with US 71 and US 167 (MacArthur Drive) south of the city of Alexandria and its interchange with Rapides Station Road north of the city of Alexandria as an alternate route to US 71 and US 167.

Acts 2010, No. 847, §1.
