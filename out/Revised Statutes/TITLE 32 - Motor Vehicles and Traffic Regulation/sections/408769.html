§803.  Other licenses not required

A licensed dismantler and parts recycler shall not be required to obtain a separate license as a used motor vehicle dealer, an automobile accessory dealer, an automobile garage or shop, or a storage garage or yard to engage in the business of a dismantler and parts recycler as set forth herein.

Acts 2006, No. 440, §1.
