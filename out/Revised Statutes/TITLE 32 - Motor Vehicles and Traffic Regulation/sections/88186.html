SUBPART H.  PEDESTRIANS' RIGHTS AND DUTIES

§211.  Pedestrians subject to traffic regulations

Pedestrians shall be subject to traffic-control signals at intersections as provided in R.S. 32:233 unless otherwise required by local ordinance, but at all other places pedestrians shall be accorded the privileges and shall be subject to the restrictions stated in this Part.

Acts 1962, No. 310, §1.
