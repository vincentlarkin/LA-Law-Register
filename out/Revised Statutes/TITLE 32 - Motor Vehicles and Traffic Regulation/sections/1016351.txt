§44.  Required notification for red light cameras
A.  Local municipal authorities or local parish authorities shall post signs, indicating
that a red light camera is present, within five hundred feet of each red light camera, in such
a manner as to be clearly visible to traffic approaching the red light camera.
B, C. Repealed by Acts 2025, No. 107, §3.
