§1511. Illegal discharge of hazardous materials

            No person shall intentionally discharge or cause to be discharged the contents of any transport vehicle containing hazardous material between the points of origin and the points of billed destination, except as authorized by representatives of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections or the Department of Conservation and Energy.

            Added by Acts 1981, No. 180, §1, eff. July 10, 1981; Acts 2023, No. 150, §7, eff. Jan. 10, 2024.
