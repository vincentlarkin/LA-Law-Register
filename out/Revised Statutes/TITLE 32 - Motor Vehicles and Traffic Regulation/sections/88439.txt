§456.  Registration; licensing
No vehicle which has been registered and licensed as a street cruiser pursuant to this Part and R.S. 47:463.33 shall be subject to the regular motor vehicle licensing requirements.
Acts 1992, No. 189, §1, eff. June 8, 1992.
