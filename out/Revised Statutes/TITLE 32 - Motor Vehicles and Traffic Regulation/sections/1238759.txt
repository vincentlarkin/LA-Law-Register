§210.1.  Applicable law
A.  The operation of a personal delivery device is governed by the provisions of this
Subpart and Federal Aviation Administration airport regulations.
B.  For the purpose of this Subpart, a personal delivery device operated in compliance
with this Subpart shall not be considered a vehicle.
Acts 2021, No. 214, §1, eff. June 11, 2021.
