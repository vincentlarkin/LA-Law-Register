§286.  Following authorized emergency vehicles prohibited
The driver of any vehicle other than one on official business shall not follow any authorized emergency vehicle traveling in response to an official call of duty closer than five hundred feet or drive into or park such vehicle within the block where such vehicle has stopped in answer to an official call.
Acts 1962, No. 310, §1.
