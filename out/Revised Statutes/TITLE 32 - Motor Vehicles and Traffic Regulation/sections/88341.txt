§387.8.  Vehicles requiring steering axle permit; interstate highways
All vehicles requiring a steering axle permit to travel on state highways shall also be entitled to travel on interstate highways without an additional permit, provided the single axle vehicles requiring such permit do not exceed twenty-four thousand  pounds in weight.
Acts 1997, No. 176, §1.
