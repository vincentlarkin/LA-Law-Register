§400.5.  Reporting an accident
If an accident occurs involving an autonomous commercial motor vehicle while the
automated driving system is engaged, the autonomous commercial motor vehicle shall
remain at the scene of the accident and the operator or any person on behalf of the operator
of the autonomous commercial motor vehicle shall comply with the provisions of R.S.
32:398 relative to contacting the appropriate law enforcement agency and furnishing all
relevant information.
Acts 2019, No. 232, §1.
