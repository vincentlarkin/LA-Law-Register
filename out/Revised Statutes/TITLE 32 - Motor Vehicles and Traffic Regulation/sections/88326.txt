§386.1.  Blanket oversize yearly permit; fees
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the Department of Transportation and Development shall establish a blanket oversize yearly permit for vehicles the width of which does not exceed twelve feet, the height of which does not exceed fourteen feet and four inches, a length not to exceed ninety feet, and the front and rear overhang of which does not exceed twenty-five feet.  The fee for this yearly permit shall be five hundred dollars.  The secretary shall promulgate rules and regulations to provide for the implementation of this Section.
Acts 1997, No. 292, §1; Acts 2014, No. 49, §1.
