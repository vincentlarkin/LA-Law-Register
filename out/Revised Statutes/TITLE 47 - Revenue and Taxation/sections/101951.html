§3205. Rules and regulations

            Louisiana Economic Development may make and promulgate such rules and regulations consistent with the provisions of this Chapter, as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this Chapter.

            Acts 1966, Ex.Sess., No. 12, §6. Amended by Acts 1976, No. 381, §1; Acts 1989, No. 491, §1; Acts 2005, No. 403, §1.
