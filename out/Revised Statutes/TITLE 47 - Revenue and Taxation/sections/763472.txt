§338.28.  Repeal
All laws or parts of laws in conflict herewith be and the same are hereby repealed, but nothing herein contained shall amend, repeal or modify any special laws relative to the taxing power of particular municipalities.
Acts 1950, No. 285, §5.  Amended by Acts 1950, 2nd Ex. Sess., No. 18, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2715 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
