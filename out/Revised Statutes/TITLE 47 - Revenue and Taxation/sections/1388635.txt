§820.5.4.1. LA 1 recreational vessel toll payments
For purposes of proper classification in the LA 1 toll schedule, any vehicle towing
a recreational vessel that exceeds the Class 1 specifications shall pay the applicable Class 2
toll rate.
Acts 2024, No. 361, §1.
