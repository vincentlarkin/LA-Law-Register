§1695.  Rules and regulations
The department may promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the
Administrative Procedure Act as are necessary to implement the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 2019, No. 247, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 2019.
NOTE:  See Acts 2019, No. 247, re: tax year applicability.
