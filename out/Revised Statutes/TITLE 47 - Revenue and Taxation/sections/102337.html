§533.  Office of commissioner

The vehicle commissioner shall maintain an office in the State Capitol at Baton Rouge and in such other places in this state as he shall deem necessary, to properly carry out the provisions of this Chapter.  The custodian of the capitol building shall provide therein adequate quarters for the offices and records of the vehicle commissioner.  The equipment for these offices shall be purchased with funds appropriated for the purpose of carrying out the provisions of this Chapter.
