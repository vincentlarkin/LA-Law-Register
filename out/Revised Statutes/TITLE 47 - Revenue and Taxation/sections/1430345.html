§1992.2. Board of review; complaints for review; Calcasieu Parish

            Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 47:1992(C), in Calcasieu Parish, the board of review shall only consider complaints for review of the correctness of an assessment by an assessor if the complaint is timely filed in person at the board of review's office or if the complaint is timely filed by means of certified mail. 

            Acts 2025, No. 9, §1, eff. July 1, 2025.
