§469.2.  Taxi cabs; reconstructed title prohibition

No vehicle issued a reconstructed title as provided in R.S. 32:707 or which has ever been issued a title marked as salvage, rebuilt, junk, total loss, or any equivalent certificate of title in this state or any other state shall be issued a registration certificate or license plate to operate as a taxicab.

Acts 2012, No. 566, §3, eff. Oct. 15, 2012.
