§332.5.  Disposition of certain collections in Natchitoches Parish
The avails of the tax imposed by R.S. 47:331 from the sales of services as defined in
R.S. 47:301.3(1) in the parish of Natchitoches under the provisions of R.S. 47:331(C) and
332, as applicable, shall be credited to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund, and after
a sufficient amount is allocated from that fund to pay all the obligations secured by the full
faith and credit of the state which become due and payable within any fiscal year, the
treasurer shall pay the remainder of such funds into the Natchitoches Historic District
Development Fund as provided in and subject to R.S. 47:302.10(C).
Acts 1992, No. 977, §1, eff. July 1, 1992; Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 154, §2, eff.
July 1, 1998; Acts 2025, No. 384, §8(B), eff. June 20, 2025.
