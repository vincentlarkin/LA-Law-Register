§1925.5.  Enforcement of millage rollbacks; resolutions of taxing authorities
The legislative auditor shall have the authority to and responsibility for enforcing the mandatory adjustment of millages by each taxing authority as provided under this Part.  The tax assessor shall submit a copy of the resolution of each taxing authority levying taxes hereunder for approval by the legislative auditor no later than the first day of August in the year following the year in which the district is implemented.
Acts 1984, No. 223, §1; H.C.R. No. 88, 1993 R.S., eff. May 30, 1993; H.C.R. No. 1, 1994 R.S., eff. May 11, 1994.
