§1925.10.  Iberia Parish Assessment District; automobile expense allowance

In the Iberia Parish Assessment District, the assessor may receive an automobile expense allowance equal to fifteen percent of his annual salary provided the assessor maintains three hundred thousand dollars of automobile insurance per accident for bodily injury and one hundred thousand dollars of automobile insurance per accident for property damage.

Acts 2009, No. 532, §1, eff. July 14, 2009.
