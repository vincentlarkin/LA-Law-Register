§1873.  Qualifications

The commission may guarantee loans for an assessor only when that assessor has demonstrated the need for and the amount necessary to complete the task of reappraisal and reassessment in his parish.

Added by Acts 1977, No. 571, §1, eff. July 15, 1977; H.C.R. No. 88, 1993 R.S., eff. May 30, 1993; H.C.R. No. 1, 1994 R.S., eff. May 11, 1994.
