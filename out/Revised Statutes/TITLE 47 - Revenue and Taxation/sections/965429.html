§1925.12. Jefferson Davis Parish Assessment District; automobile expense allowance

            In the Jefferson Davis Parish Assessment District, the assessor may receive an automobile expense allowance equal to fifteen percent of his annual salary provided the assessor maintains three hundred thousand dollars of automobile insurance per accident for bodily injury and one hundred thousand dollars of automobile insurance per accident for property damage.

            Acts 2015, No. 252, §1, eff. June 29, 2015.
