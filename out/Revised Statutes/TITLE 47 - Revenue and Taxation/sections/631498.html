§820.5.5.  Interoperability

A.  All public toll facilities in this state shall operate on an interoperable basis for toll collection and toll violation enforcement.

B.  The secretary of the Department of Transportation and Development, notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, is hereby authorized and empowered to develop and promulgate the policies and procedures for the implementation of toll and toll violation interoperability.

Acts 2008, No. 171, §1, eff. June 12, 2008.
