§1523.  Reciprocal agreements; federal government
The secretary is authorized to enter into reciprocal offset of indebtedness agreements with the federal government, pursuant to which the state shall agree to offset from state tax refunds and payments otherwise due to vendors and contractors providing goods or services to state agencies, non-tax debt owed to the federal government, and the federal government shall agree to offset from federal payments to vendors, contractors, and taxpayers debt owed to the state.
Acts 2010, No. 960, §1, eff. July 6, 2010.
