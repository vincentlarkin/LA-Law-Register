§4352.  Program administration
There is hereby established a program to implement the exemption provided by
Article VII, Section 21(L) of the Constitution of Louisiana.  The program shall be
implemented and administered by Louisiana Economic Development, which shall adopt and
promulgate such rules as are necessary for the administration of the program in compliance
with the Administrative Procedure Act except that the department may promulgate such rules
only after approval of the House Committee on Ways and Means and the Senate Committee
on Revenue and Fiscal Affairs meeting jointly within sixty days of publication of such
proposed rules in the Louisiana Register.
Acts 2012, No. 499, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2013; Acts 2013, No. 220, §21, eff. June 11,
2013.
