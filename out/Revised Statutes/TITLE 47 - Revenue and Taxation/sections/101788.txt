§299.33.  Offset request
In accordance with the provisions of this Part, any claimant who has a debt owed to them by an individual may make an offset claim to the secretary against any refund or overpayment of Louisiana individual income tax in which the individual owing the debt has an interest.
Acts 1992, No. 735, §1.
