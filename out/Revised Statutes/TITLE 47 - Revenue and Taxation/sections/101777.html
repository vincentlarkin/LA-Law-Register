§299.14.  Form of contest

An individual whose individual income tax refund, or part thereof, has been offset and remitted to an agency may contest the offset by notifying the claiming agency in writing of his intention to contest the offset and his reasons therefor.  

Acts 1983, No. 288, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
