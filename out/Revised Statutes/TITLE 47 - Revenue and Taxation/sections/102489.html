§726.1.  Gasoline and other motor fuels; advertised price; requirement

The advertised price of gasoline and other motor fuels sold at the pump shall include all taxes levied and collected on such fuel.  Any advertisement of a price shall also clearly state whether the price is a "cash price" or a "credit price".  

Acts 1990, No. 733, §1.
