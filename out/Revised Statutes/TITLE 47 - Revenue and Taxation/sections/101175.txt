§1404.  Employees of the board
The board shall appoint as its principal assistant a secretary-clerk who shall be custodian of its files and records, and one private secretary who may also be the stenographer-reporter for the board.  The board shall also appoint any other employees necessary for the performance of the functions herein delegated.  The board shall fix the salaries of the secretary-clerk, stenographer-reporter and other employees.
