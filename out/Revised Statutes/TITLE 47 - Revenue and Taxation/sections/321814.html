§818.5.  Louisiana Truck Center, authorization

The secretary of the Department of Revenue shall provide the personnel and equipment required to fully implement the provisions of R.S. 32:390.23 as it relates to taxes and fees assessed and collected by this department.

Acts 2005, No. 252, §1, eff. July 1, 2006.
