§2238.8.  Donation of adjudicated property
The provisions of this Subpart shall apply to the unredeemed property adjudicated to the city of Monroe for five years or more that is subject to donation to a nonprofit organization, which is recognized by the Internal Revenue Service as a 501(c)(3) or 501(c)(4) nonprofit organization.
Acts 2009, No. 146, §1, eff. June 25, 2009.
