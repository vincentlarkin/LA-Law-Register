§335.3.  Policy
The Legislature of Louisiana finds that this state should enter into an agreement with one or more states to simplify and modernize sales and use tax administration in order to substantially reduce the burden of tax compliance for all sellers and for all types of commerce.
Acts 2001, No. 72, §1, eff. May 24, 2001.
