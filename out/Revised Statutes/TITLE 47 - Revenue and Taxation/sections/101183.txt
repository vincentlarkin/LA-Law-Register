§1411.  Documents to be served by registered mail
All papers or documents filed with the board which are required by law to be served upon opposing party or which because of a prayer contained therein, are asked to be served upon the opposing party or any counsel of record representing the taxpayer, or upon the collector or his counsel of record, may be served by registered mail, return receipt requested, and such service shall be valid for all purposes under this Chapter.
