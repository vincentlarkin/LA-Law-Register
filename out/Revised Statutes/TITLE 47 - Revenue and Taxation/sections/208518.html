§337.25.  Authority of collectors to sue

Each collector of each taxing authority of this state, who is charged by law with the duty of collecting a tax, shall be the proper party to bring suits in his official capacity for the collection of such taxes.

Acts 2003, No. 73, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
