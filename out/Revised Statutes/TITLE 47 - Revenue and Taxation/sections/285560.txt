§337.93.  Public information; adoption of rules; availability of rules and orders
The board shall make available for public inspection all rules, preambles, responses to comments, and submissions and all other written statements of policy or interpretations formulated, adopted, or used by the board in the discharge of its functions and publish an index of such rules, preambles, responses to comments, submissions, statements, and interpretations on a regular basis.
Acts 2004, No. 469, §1, eff. July 1, 2004.
