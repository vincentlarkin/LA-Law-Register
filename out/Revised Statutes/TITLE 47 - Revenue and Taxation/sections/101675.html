PART II-A.  LOUISIANA CORPORATION INCOME TAX

SUBPART A - INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS,

TAX LEVY, RATES OF TAX; CREDITS

§287.2.  Short title

This Act shall be known as and may be cited as the "Louisiana Corporation Income Tax Act".  

Acts 1986, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 16, §1, eff. Dec. 24, 1986.
