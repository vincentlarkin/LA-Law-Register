§297.5.  Exemption from income; payments received from the Louisiana Military Family Assistance Fund

Payments or awards made to activated military personnel or family members of activated military personnel under Part III of Chapter 2 of Title 46 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, the Louisiana Military Family Assistance Fund, are exempt from the tax imposed by this Part.

Acts 2005, No. 151, §3, eff. June 28, 2005.
