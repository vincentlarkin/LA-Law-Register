SUBPART D.  SALE OF PROPERTY
ADJUDICATED TO THE CITY OF MONROE
§2238.1.  City of Monroe
The provisions of this Subpart shall apply to any tax adjudication of residential or commercial property within the city limits of Monroe.
Acts 2009, No. 146, §1, eff. June 25, 2009.
