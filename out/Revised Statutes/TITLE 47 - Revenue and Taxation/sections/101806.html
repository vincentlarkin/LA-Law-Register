§300.11.  Inconsistent provisions

The provisions of this Part shall supersede the provisions of Part I and Part II of this Chapter to the extent that they are inconsistent or in conflict herewith.  The provisions of Part I and Part II of this Chapter shall remain in effect to the extent that they are not inconsistent or in conflict with this Part.

Acts 1996, No. 41, §1, eff. for taxable periods beginning after Dec. 31, 1996.
