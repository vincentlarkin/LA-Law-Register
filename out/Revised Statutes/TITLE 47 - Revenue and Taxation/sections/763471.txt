§338.27.  Ordinance imposing tax; purposes of tax
The ordinance imposing said tax, authorized by R.S. 47:338.1, and any amendments thereto, shall specify the purpose or purposes for which said tax is imposed.  The revenues derived from said tax shall be dedicated and used solely for said purposes.
Acts 1950, No. 285, §4; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2714 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
