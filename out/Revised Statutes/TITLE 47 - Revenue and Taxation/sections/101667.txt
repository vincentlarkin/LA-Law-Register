SUBPART H.  ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS
§281.  Administration; general
Except as specifically provided to the contrary in this Chapter, all matters pertaining to the administration of this Chapter shall be governed by the provisions of Chapter 18 of this Sub-title.*
*R.S. 47:1501 et seq.
