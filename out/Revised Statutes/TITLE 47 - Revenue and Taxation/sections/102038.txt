§332.41.  Disposition of certain collections in West Feliciana Parish
The avails of the tax imposed by this Chapter from the sales of services as defined
by R.S. 47:301.3(1) in West Feliciana Parish under the provisions of R.S. 47:331(C) and 332
shall be credited to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund, and after a sufficient amount
is allocated from that fund to pay all of the obligations secured by the full faith and credit of
the state which become due and payable within any fiscal year, the treasurer shall pay the
remainder of such funds into the St. Francisville Economic Development Fund as provided
in and subject to R.S. 47:302.46.
Acts 1997, No. 1319, §1, eff. July 1, 1997; Acts 2025, No. 384, §8(B), eff. June 20,
2025.
