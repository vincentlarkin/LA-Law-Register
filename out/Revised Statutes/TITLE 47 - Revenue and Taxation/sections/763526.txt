§338.82.  Purpose for tax
The ordinance imposing the tax, and any amendments thereto, shall specify the purpose or purposes for which said tax is imposed.  The revenues derived from said tax shall be dedicated and used solely for said purposes.
Acts 1962, No. 276, §4; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2735.4 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
