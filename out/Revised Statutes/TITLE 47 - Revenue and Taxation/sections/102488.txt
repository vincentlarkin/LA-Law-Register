§726.  Gasoline and other motor fuels dispensing machines; requirements
Each machine through which gasoline or other motor fuels are dispensed shall display only one sign relating to taxes and it shall state "ABOVE PRICE INCLUDES ALL LOCAL, STATE, AND FEDERAL TAXES."
Amended by Acts 1950, No. 26, §1; Acts 1952, No. 5, §2: Acts 1957, No. 16, §1; Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 7, §3.  Acts 1984, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 11, §1, eff. July 1, 1984.
