§513.1.  New residents; notice
The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor vehicles, shall notify new residents upon the issuance of a driver's license pursuant to R.S. 32:401 et seq. or a special identification card pursuant to R.S. 40:1321 that an owner of a motor vehicle shall have thirty days from the date of notification to pay such taxes that may be due before penalties and interest begin to accrue.
Acts 2014, No. 414, §1.
