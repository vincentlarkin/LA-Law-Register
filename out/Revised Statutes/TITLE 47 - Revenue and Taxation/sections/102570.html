§815.  Special fuels dispensing machines; requirements

Each tank through which a special fuel is dispensed shall have clearly displayed on it only one sign which refers to taxes and it shall state "ABOVE PRICE INCLUDES ALL LOCAL, STATE, AND FEDERAL TAXES."

Acts 1964, Ex.Sess., No. 3, §2.  Amended by Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 11, §2; Acts 1984, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 12, §1, eff. July 1, 1984.
