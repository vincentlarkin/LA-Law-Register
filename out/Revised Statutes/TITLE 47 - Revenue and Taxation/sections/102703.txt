§9081.  Legal shipments of lottery devices into state
All shipments of lottery supplies, equipment, and devices into any parish of the state of Louisiana within which lottery operations are authorized shall be deemed legal shipments of such devices for purposes of federal law.
Acts 1990, No. 1045, §1, eff. Nov. 7, 1990.
