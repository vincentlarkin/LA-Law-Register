§4306.  Rules and regulations
Louisiana Economic Development and the Board of Commerce and Industry may
make and promulgate such rules and regulations consistent with the provisions of this
Chapter as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 773, §1; Acts 2005, No. 403, §1.
