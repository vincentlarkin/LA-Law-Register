PART IV.  ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS

§531.  Director of public safety to be vehicle commissioner

The director of public safety is designated as vehicle commissioner of the state; he shall have all powers and perform such duties as are herein imposed upon the vehicle commissioner.  

Amended by Acts 1972, No. 96, §7, eff. Jan. 1, 1973.
