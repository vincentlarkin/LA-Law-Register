§1925.9.  Evangeline Parish Assessment District; automobile expense allowance

In the Evangeline Parish Assessment District, the assessor may receive an automobile expense allowance equal to twelve percent of his annual salary provided the assessor maintains three hundred thousand dollars of automobile insurance per accident for bodily injury and one hundred thousand dollars of automobile insurance per accident for property damage.

Acts 2008, No. 358, §1, eff. June 22, 2008.
