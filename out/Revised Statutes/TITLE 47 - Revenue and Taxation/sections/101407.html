§1925.6.  Disbursements from special district funds

All funds collected by the special district shall be paid into the assessor's salary and expense fund, which shall be disbursed by the assessor in accordance with law.  Expenditures from the fund shall be subject to the public bid laws of the state of Louisiana.

Acts 1984, No. 223, §1; H.C.R. No. 88, 1993 R.S., eff. May 30, 1993; H.C.R. No. 1, 1994 R.S., eff. May 11, 1994.
