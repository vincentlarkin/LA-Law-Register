PART V. EXTINGUISHMENT OF LIEN

SUBPART A. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§2241. Redemptive period preemptive

            All redemptive periods provided in the Constitution of Louisiana shall be peremptive.

            Acts 2008, No. 819, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2008; Acts 2024, No. 774, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2026; Acts 2025, No. 411, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2026.
