§4.  Same; authority of foreign officials to sue

A certificate of the Secretary of State of the other state that a certain official has the authority to collect the taxes in issue shall be conclusive proof of that authority and of his authority to bring a suit for such taxes in his official capacity.  

Acts 1950, No. 19, §2.
