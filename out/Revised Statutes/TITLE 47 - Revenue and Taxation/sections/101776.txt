§299.13.  Waiver of right to contest offset
The failure of an individual to file a contest of the offset with the claiming agency within forty-five days of the date of mailing of notice of offset shall be deemed a waiver of the right to contest the offset.
Acts 1983, No. 288, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
