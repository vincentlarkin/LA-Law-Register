§338.101.  Purpose of tax; dedication of revenues
The proposition submitted at the election and the ordinance imposing said tax and any amendments thereto shall specify that the tax shall be levied and used for the purpose of constructing, maintaining and operating levees and drainage facilities in said parish, paying debt service on any bonds heretofore or hereafter assumed by said parish and/or for any lawful corporate purposes for which any funds of the parish may be expended, and the revenues derived therefrom shall be dedicated and used solely for said purposes.
Acts 1968, No. 83, §4; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2737.34 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
