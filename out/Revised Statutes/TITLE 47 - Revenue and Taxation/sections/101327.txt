§1688.  Suspension of dealer's permit
Whenever the secretary determines that a dealer holding a permit under R.S. 47:1683 has violated any of the provisions of this Part, he shall suspend the permit of such dealer for a period of not less than six months nor more than one year; provided that any dealer aggrieved by such action on the part of the secretary may appeal suspensively to the Board of Tax Appeals.
Acts 1950, No. 371, §8; Acts 2014, No. 198, §1, eff. July 1, 2014.
