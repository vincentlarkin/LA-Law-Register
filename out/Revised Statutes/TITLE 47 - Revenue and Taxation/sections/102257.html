§464.  Road tractors

On each road tractor, an annual registration or license tax, on the basis of seven dollars and fifty cents ($7.50) per thousand pounds gross weight of such vehicle shall be imposed, provided that the minimum charge thereon shall not be less than twenty-five dollars ($25.00).
