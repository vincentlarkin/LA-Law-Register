§815.1.  Special fuel; advertised price; requirement
The advertised price of special fuels dispensed by a retail dealer shall include all taxes levied and collected on such fuel.  Any advertisement of a price shall also clearly state whether the price is a "cash price" or a "credit price".
Acts 1990, No. 733, §1.
