§338.103.  Proceedings validated

The proceedings pursuant to which the existing one-half of one per cent (1/2%) sales tax is now being collected by the governing authority of the Parish of St. Bernard are hereby validated, ratified and confirmed.

Acts 1968, No. 83, §6; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2737.36 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
