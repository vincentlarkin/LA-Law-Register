CHAPTER 2-D.  UNIFORM LOCAL SALES TAX CODE

PART A.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§337.1.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known as and may be cited as the "Uniform Local Sales Tax Code".

Acts 2003, No. 73, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
