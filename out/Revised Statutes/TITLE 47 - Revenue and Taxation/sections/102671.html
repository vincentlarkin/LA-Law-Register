§9020.  Bulk sale and purchase of on-line lotto lottery tickets; syndicates; investment

The board shall promulgate administrative rules that define and prohibit the bulk sale of on-line lotto lottery tickets by a retailer and the bulk purchase of such tickets by an investment syndicate, investment group, or individual for investment purposes.  

Acts 1992, No. 953, §1; Acts 1993, No. 749, §1.
