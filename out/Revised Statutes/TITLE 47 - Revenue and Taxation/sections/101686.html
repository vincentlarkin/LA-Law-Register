§287.526.  Shipowners' protection and indemnity associations

There shall not be included in gross income the receipts of shipowners' mutual protection and indemnity associations which are not organized for profit and no part of the net earnings of which inures to the benefit of any private shareholder; but such corporations shall be subject to the tax imposed by this Part on their Louisiana taxable income from interest, dividends, and rents earned within or derived from sources within this state, for a taxable year, the same as any nonexempt corporation.  

Acts 1986, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 16, §1, eff. Dec. 24, 1986.
