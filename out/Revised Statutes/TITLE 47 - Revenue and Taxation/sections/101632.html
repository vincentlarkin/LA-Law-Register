PART II.  ESTATE TRANSFER TAX

§2431.  Levy of tax

There is hereby levied an estate transfer tax upon all estates which are subject to federal estate taxation under the Federal Internal Revenue Code.

Amended by Acts 1972, No. 315, §2; Acts 1997, No. 818, §2, eff. July 1, 2004.
