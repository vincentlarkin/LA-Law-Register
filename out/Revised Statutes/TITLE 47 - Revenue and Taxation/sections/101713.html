§287.681.  Administration

Except as specifically provided to the contrary in this Part, all matters pertaining to the administration of this Part shall be governed by the provisions of Chapter 18 of this Subtitle.  

Acts 1986, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 16, §1, eff. Dec. 24, 1986.
