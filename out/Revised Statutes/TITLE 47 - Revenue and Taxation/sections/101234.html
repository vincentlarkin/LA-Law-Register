§1515.1.  Sales tax refund; new housing construction

A.  Any person who restores, renovates, or rehabilitates an existing structure or builds or causes the building of a new house and associated improvements in an approved housing development area pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 40:582.1 through 582.7 shall be entitled to a refund of the amount of local sales tax paid and collected under the provisions of R.S. 47:1515, if any, as a consequence of the purchase of materials used in the construction of such new house upon showing that he has complied with the provisions of R.S. 40:582.7.

B.  The secretary of the Department of Revenue is authorized to prescribe the forms and regulations for use in carrying out the provisions of this Section.

Acts 1984, No. 292, §2; Acts 1997, No. 658, §2.
