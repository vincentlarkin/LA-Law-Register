§337.16.6.  Collector to be furnished list of taxpayers; records made available
In the event such agreement is reached, pursuant to R.S. 47:337.16.1, it shall provide that the commissioner of finance of the city of New Orleans shall transmit to the collector of revenue at a time specified in the agreement a current list of taxpayers under each ordinance to be enforced by the collector.  All records of the city relating to taxes due under the ordinances to be enforced by the collector of revenue shall be preserved and made available to the collector upon his request.
Acts 1950, 2nd Ex.Sess., No. 11, §6; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2752 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
