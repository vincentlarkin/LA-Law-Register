§502.  Exemption from registration
Farm tractors, self-propelled farm equipment, rubber-tired farm wagons and carts and drawn rubber-tired farm equipment which are designed primarily for field use and which are operated or moved only incidentally on the highways of this state, and which are used for farm purposes only need not be registered under this chapter.  Road rollers and road machinery temporarily moved upon the highways are also exempt from registration.
Amended by Acts 1972, No. 533, §1.
