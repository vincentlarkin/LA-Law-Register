§2308.  Louisiana Tax Commission; tables; classifying land; public hearings
Whenever the Louisiana Tax Commission or its successor is required to prepare and publish a table or make a determination or define classifications of land, it shall do so only after public hearings held pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 702, §8, eff. Aug. 4, 1976; H.C.R. No. 88, 1993 R.S., eff. May 30, 1993; H.C.R. No. 1, 1994 R.S., eff. May 11, 1994.
