§9019. Legislative oversight of corporation rules and regulations

           Legislative oversight of corporation rules and regulations shall be in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 49:966.

           Acts 1990, No. 1045, §1, eff. Nov. 7, 1990; Acts 1995, No. 1022, §1, eff. June 29, 1995.
