§337.56.  Collection by distraint and sale authorized

When any taxpayer fails to pay any tax, penalty, and interest assessed, the collector may proceed to enforce the collection thereof by distraint and sale.

Acts 2003, No. 73, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
