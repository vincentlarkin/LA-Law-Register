§2136.  Duty of assessors and Louisiana Tax Commission to amend tax roll to conform to
proration of ad valorem taxes
All assessors throughout the state of Louisiana and the commission are authorized
and directed to adjust and amend all tax rolls and records within their respective offices in
order to properly carry out the intent and purposes of R.S. 47:2135 through 2137.
Acts 2008, No. 819, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2024, No. 774, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2026.
