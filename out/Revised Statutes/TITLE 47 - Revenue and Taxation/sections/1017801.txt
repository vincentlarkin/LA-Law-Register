§502.4.  Exemption from registration; mobile construction equipment
Mobile construction equipment including but not limited to bulldozers, forklifts, road
graders, and excavators are not required to be registered pursuant to this Part unless
otherwise expressly required to be registered.  Additionally, such equipment exempted from
registration by this Section shall also be exempt from the compulsory motor vehicle security
requirements of R.S. 32:861 et seq.
Acts 2016, No. 468, §1.
