§2210.  Liability of owner of adjudicated property

While property remains adjudicated property, the current owner remains liable as owner of the property under applicable law.  The political subdivision is not liable solely as owner of the property.

Acts 2008, No. 819, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
