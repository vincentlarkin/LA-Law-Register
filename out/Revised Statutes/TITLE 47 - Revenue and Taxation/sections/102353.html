§6.  Definitions

The term "tax" or "taxes" as used in R.S. 47:3 through 47:5 shall include (1) any and all penalties lawfully imposed pursuant to a taxing statute, and (2) interest charges lawfully added to the tax liability.  

Acts 1950, No. 19, §4.
