§1515.2.  Authority to collect fees; office of conservation
A.  The secretary of the Department of Revenue is hereby authorized to enter into an
agreement with the Department of Conservation and Energy, office of conservation, at the
request of the commissioner of conservation, to collect fees assessed by the office of
conservation.
B.  The agreement between the Department of Revenue and the Department of
Conservation and Energy, office of conservation shall be executed by the secretary of the
Department of Revenue and the commissioner of conservation.  The agreement shall provide
the manner of collection, the fees to be collected, and the costs of collection, if any, to be
paid by the office of conservation, and such other terms and conditions necessary to
effectuate the agreement.
Acts 1995, No. 98, §1; Acts 1997, No. 658, §2; Acts 2023, No. 150, §18, eff. Jan. 10,
2024.
