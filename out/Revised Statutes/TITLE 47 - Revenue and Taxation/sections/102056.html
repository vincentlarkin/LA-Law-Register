CHAPTER 2-C.  UNIFORM SALES AND

USE TAX ADMINISTRATION ACT

§335.1.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known as and referred to as the "Uniform Sales and Use Tax Administration Act".

Acts 2001, No. 72, §1, eff. May 24, 2001.
