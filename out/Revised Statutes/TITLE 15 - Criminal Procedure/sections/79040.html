§303.  Maximum fine or imprisonment when not specified

Whenever the punishment of fine and imprisonment are left by law to the discretion of any court, the fine shall not exceed one thousand dollars, nor the imprisonment two years.  

Acts 1966, No. 311, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1967.
