§846.  Prisoners

Any person convicted of misdemeanors and felonies not necessarily punishable by hard labor may be confined in the center.  A judge of the Seventh Judicial District shall have full authority to sentence any party convicted of such offenses in the Seventh Judicial District to imprisonment in the center.  

Added by Acts 1974, No. 690, §1; Acts 1985, No. 447, §1.
