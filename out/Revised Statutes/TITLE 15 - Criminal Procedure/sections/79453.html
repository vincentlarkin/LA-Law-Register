§903.  Separate facilities for males and females

The Department of Corrections is authorized to provide separate facilities for male and female juveniles committed to it.  These separate facilities may be provided at whatever location or locations the Department of Corrections deems advisable.  

Added by Acts 1970, No. 353, §1.
