PART XIV.  PRISON ENTERPRISES

§1151.  Definitions

As used in this Part, the following words shall have the following meanings ascribed to them:

(1)  "Board" means the Prison Enterprises Board.  

(2)  "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Corrections.

(3)  "Secretary" means the secretary of corrections.  

Added by Acts 1983, 1st Ex.  Sess., No. 59, §1, eff. Feb.  7, 1983.
