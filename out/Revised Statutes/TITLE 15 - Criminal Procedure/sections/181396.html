§571.51.  Evaluation of program effectiveness

Each judicial district shall evaluate the job intervention program effectiveness in writing.  These evaluations shall be compiled annually and transmitted to the judicial administrator of the Supreme Court of Louisiana.

Acts 2003, No. 803, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
