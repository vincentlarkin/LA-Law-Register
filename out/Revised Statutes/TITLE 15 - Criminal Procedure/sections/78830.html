DETENTION CENTER AUTHORITY

§1106.  Bossier/Caddo Juvenile Detention Center Authority; creation; jurisdiction

The Bossier/Caddo Juvenile Detention Center Authority is hereby established as a political subdivision of the state, with a territorial jurisdiction throughout the parishes of Bossier and Caddo.

Acts 1997, No. 1445, §2, eff. July 15, 1997.
