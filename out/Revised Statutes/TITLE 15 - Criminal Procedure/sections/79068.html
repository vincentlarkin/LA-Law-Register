§430.  Proof of truth of recitals in copy of registry of birth, baptism or circumcision

In all criminal prosecutions for the violation of any law relative to the employment of children or young persons, a copy of the registry of the birth, baptism or circumcision of such child or young person, duly certified to by the person in whose custody such registry may be kept, shall be prima facie evidence of the truth of the facts recited in such certified copy.
