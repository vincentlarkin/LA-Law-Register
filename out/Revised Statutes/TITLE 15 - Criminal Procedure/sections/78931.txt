§1252.  Statement of purpose
The legislature enacts this Chapter to provide for a program to be administered by the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and the Administration of Criminal Justice, hereinafter in this Chapter referred to as the commission, intended to upgrade parish jail facilities to standards promulgated by the commission.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 753, §1, eff. July 31, 1980.
