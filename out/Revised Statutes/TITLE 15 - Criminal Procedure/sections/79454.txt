§904.  Superintendent; powers and duties
The secretary of public safety and corrections shall appoint a superintendent for each juvenile institution under the administration of the department and shall enumerate his powers and duties.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 353, §1; Acts 1991, No. 467, §2.
