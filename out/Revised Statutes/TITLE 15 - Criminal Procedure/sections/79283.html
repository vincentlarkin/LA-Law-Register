§606.  State DNA data bank

There is hereby established the state DNA data bank.  It shall serve as the repository of DNA samples collected under this Chapter.

Acts 1997, No. 737, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1999; H.C.R. No. 40, 1999 R.S., eff. June 21, 1999.
