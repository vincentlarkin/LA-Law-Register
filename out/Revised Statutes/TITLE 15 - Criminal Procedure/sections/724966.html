§562.6.  Duration of registration and notification period

A person required to register pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter shall comply with the requirement for  ten years from the date of initial registration for a first offense and for the duration of the lifetime of the offender for a second or subsequent offense, unless the underlying conviction is reversed, set aside, or vacated.

Acts 2010, No. 796, §1; Acts 2014, No. 140, §1.
