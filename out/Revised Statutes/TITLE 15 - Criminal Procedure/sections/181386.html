§571.47.  Employer participation; report

An employer participating in the job intervention program shall report the following changes or conditions to the district attorney at any periodic reporting period specified by the court:

(1)  The probationer fails to comply with program rules and expectations.

(2)  The probationer refuses to engage constructively in the employment program.

(3)  The probationer terminates his or her participation in the program.

Acts 2003, No. 803, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
