§571.33.  Extension or alteration of pilot; approval of the budget committee; statewide implementation
The pilot project undertaken as required in this Part may be varied, extended, or dissolved prior to evaluation only if approved by the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget and the governor.
Acts 1990, No. 553, §1.
