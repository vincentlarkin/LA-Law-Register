PART XI.  REGIONAL JUVENILE FACILITIES

SUBPART A.  IN GENERAL

§1091.  Regional system of shelter care and detention facilities; alternative shelter care programs

The Department of Public Safety and Corrections may develop or assist in the development of a regional system of approved and licensed shelter care and detention facilities and certified shelter care programs and may make payments for the care and treatment of children in approved and licensed detention and shelter facilities and programs.  

Acts 1966, No. 123, §1.  Amended by Acts 1978, No. 786, §2, eff. July 17, 1978; Acts 1979, No. 680, §1; Acts 1988, No. 609, §1.
