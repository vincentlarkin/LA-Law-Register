§1226.  Juvenile Delinquency and Gang Prevention Act of 1993
The Juvenile Delinquency and Gang Prevention Act of 1993, as provided for in Chapter 13 of Title 15 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, and its administration are hereby placed under the jurisdiction of the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice.
Acts 1993, No. 461, §1.
