§573. Sessions of Board of Pardons open

            A. All sessions of the Board of Pardons shall be public except as provided in Subsection B of this Section. No action shall be taken by the board on any pardon application at any time other than during a meeting that is open to the public. Any pardon or commutation of sentence granted outside of an open, public meeting of the board shall be null, void, and of no effect.

            B. The victim, or the spouse or next of kin of a deceased victim, shall be allowed to present testimony in executive session at his request.

            Acts 1999, No. 246, §1; Acts 2025, No. 128, §2.
