§571.52.  Record of participation
A record of the fact that an individual has participated in a job intervention program shall be sent to the office of the attorney general and shall be made available upon request to any district attorney for the purpose of determining if an individual has previously participated in such a program.
Acts 2003, No. 803, §1, eff. July 1, 2003.
