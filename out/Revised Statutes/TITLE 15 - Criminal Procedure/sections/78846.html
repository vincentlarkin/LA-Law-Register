§113.  Duty of Orleans registrar to furnish jury commission lists of voters added to or deleted from rolls

The registrar of voters for the Parish of Orleans shall prepare and furnish to the jury commission for the Parish of Orleans quarterly a list containing the names of persons added to and deleted from the voter registration rolls for the Parish of Orleans during the preceding quarter, which list the jury commission may use, together with other information, for the purpose of discharging its duties.  

Acts 1966, No. 311, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1967.
