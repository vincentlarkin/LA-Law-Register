§844.  Suits; service of process

Suit may be instituted against the Northeast Louisiana Correctional Center at its domicile and service of all legal documents shall be made on the president or secretary of the board of governors.  

Added by Acts 1974, No. 690, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 678, §2; Acts 1985, No. 447, §1.
