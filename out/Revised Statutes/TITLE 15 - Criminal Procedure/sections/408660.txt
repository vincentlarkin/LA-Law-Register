§561.6.  Adoption of rules
A.  The Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall adopt all rules necessary to implement the provisions of this Chapter.
B.  All rules shall be adopted pursuant to the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act.
Acts 2006, No. 242, §1.
