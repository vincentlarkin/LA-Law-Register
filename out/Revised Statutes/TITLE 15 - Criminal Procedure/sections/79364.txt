§836.  Cooperation and agreements with other agencies; federal government; local government
The department shall cooperate with public and private agencies and officials to assist in fulfilling its functions.  The department may enter into agreements with other departments of this state or any political subdivision thereof, with private agencies and with the federal government with respect to the discharge of their respective responsibilities.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 192, §1.
