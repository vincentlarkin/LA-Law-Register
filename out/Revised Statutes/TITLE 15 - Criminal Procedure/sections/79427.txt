§866.  Payment to released prisoners
The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall promulgate rules and regulations to determine which prisoners are eligible upon release from prison to receive a temporary maintenance payment not to exceed the sum of twenty dollars.  Payments shall be made from a source determined by the secretary.
Acts 1988, No. 377, §1.
