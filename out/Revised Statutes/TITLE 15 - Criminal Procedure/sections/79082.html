SUBPART II.  ELECTRONIC RECORDING: PROTECTED PERSONS

§440.1.  Purpose

It is declared to be in the best interest of the state that protected persons be spared from crimes of violence, and that persons who commit such crimes be prosecuted with a minimum of additional intrusion into the lives of such protected persons.

Acts 1984, No. 563, §1; Acts 2004, No. 241, §1.
