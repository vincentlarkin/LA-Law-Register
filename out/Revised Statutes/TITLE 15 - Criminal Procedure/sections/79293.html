§616.  Confidentiality of records

Unless otherwise provided, all DNA profiles and samples submitted to the state police pursuant to this Chapter shall be confidential.

Acts 1997, No. 737, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1999; H.C.R. No. 40, 1999 R.S., eff. June 21, 1999.
