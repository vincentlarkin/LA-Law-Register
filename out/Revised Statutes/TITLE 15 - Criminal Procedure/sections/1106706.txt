§777.  Effectiveness
This compact shall enter into force and become effective and binding upon the states
so acting when it has been enacted into law by any two states.  Thereafter, this compact shall
become effective and binding as to any other of such states upon similar action by such state.
Acts 2018, No. 440, §1.
