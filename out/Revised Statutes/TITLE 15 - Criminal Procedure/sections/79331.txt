§808.  Prisoners confined in multiparish prisons
All persons convicted of misdemeanors and felonies punishable with or without hard labor may be confined in the multiparish prisons, and in misdemeanor cases, may be ordered to participate in a court-approved workday release program as provided for in R.S. 15:708.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 461, §2; Acts 1986, No. 704, §2.
