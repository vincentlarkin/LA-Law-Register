§809.  Preexisting facilities

The sheriff of a parish already the domicile of a prison facility may make the prison facilities under his jurisdiction available to one or more parishes on a per prisoner fee basis.  The prison facilities shall in no way come under the requirement of this Part.  

Added by Acts 1970, No. 461, §2.
