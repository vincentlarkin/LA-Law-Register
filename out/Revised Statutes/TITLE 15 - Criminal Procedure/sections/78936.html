CHAPTER 10.  ELECTRONIC SURVEILLANCE

PART I.  GENERAL

§1301.  Short title

This Chapter may be cited and referred to as the "Electronic Surveillance Act".  

Acts 1985, No. 859, §1, eff. July 23, 1985; Acts 1991, No. 795, §4.
