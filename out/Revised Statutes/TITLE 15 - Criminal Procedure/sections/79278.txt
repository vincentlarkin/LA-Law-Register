CHAPTER 6-A.  DNA DETECTION OF SEXUAL AND
VIOLENT OFFENDERS
§601.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known as the "DNA Detection of Sexual and Violent Offenders Act".
Acts 1997, No. 737, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1999; H.C.R. No. 40, 1999 R.S., eff. June 21, 1999.
