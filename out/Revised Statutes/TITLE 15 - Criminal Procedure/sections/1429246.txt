§589.1.  Handling of information; juvenile criminal conduct
Any information on juvenile criminal conduct obtained by the bureau under this Title
shall be handled in accordance with the provisions of Children's Code Articles 412, 414, and
917 through 926 and shall be made available pursuant to such provisions.  Such access and
use shall include an audit trail to maintain the integrity of the records and ensure
accountability.  Such trail shall include all of the following:
(1)  Who accessed the system.
(2)  When the system was accessed.
(3)  What actions were performed during access.
Acts 2025, No. 364, §2.
