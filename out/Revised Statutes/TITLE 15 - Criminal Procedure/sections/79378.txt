§847.  Additional facilities
Any prison, correctional institute, or other type of prison facility which is operated by the Concordia or Catahoula Parish Police Jury may be made available by said police jury to the Northeast Louisiana Correctional Center on a per prisoner fee basis.  When any such facility is made available, the facility shall become a part of the Northeast Louisiana Correctional Center and subject to all the requirements of this Part.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 690, §1.  Amended by Acts 1975, No. 678, §2; Acts 1985, No. 447, §1.
