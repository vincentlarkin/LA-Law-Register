§1205.  State Law Enforcement Planning Agency

Within the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice, there shall be a State Law Enforcement Planning Agency (SLEPA), which shall serve as the staff of the commission.

Added by Acts 1976, No. 592, §1.
