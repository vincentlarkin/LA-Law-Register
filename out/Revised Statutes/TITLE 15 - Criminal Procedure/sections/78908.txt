§1223.  Crime Victims Reparations Board
The Crime Victims Reparations Board as further provided for in Chapter 21 of Title 46 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 is hereby placed under the jurisdiction of the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice.
Acts 1984, No. 844, §1, eff. July 13, 1984.
