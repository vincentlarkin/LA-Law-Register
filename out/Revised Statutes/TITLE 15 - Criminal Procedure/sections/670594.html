§972.  Definitions

For purposes of this Part:

(1)  "Evidence-based practices" shall mean practices which have been documented based upon scientific research to demonstrate effectiveness in terms of a reduction in recidivism rates among participants.

(2)  "Office" shall mean the Department of Public Safety and Corrections, youth services, office of juvenile justice.

Acts 2009, No. 170, §1, eff. when sufficient funds are appropriated.
