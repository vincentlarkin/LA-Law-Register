§256.  Fees where more than one case on same day
Except as provided in R.S. 15:255, no person shall be allowed, for attendance as a witness in criminal cases, more than is allowed in one case on the same day, or mileage for more than one case at a time.  The provisions of this Section shall not apply to the city of New Orleans.
Acts 1966, No. 311, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1967.  Amended by Acts 1980, No. 690, §1.
