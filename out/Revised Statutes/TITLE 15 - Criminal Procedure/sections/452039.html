§742.  Definitions

For the purposes of this Part:

(1)  "Correctional facility employee" means any employee of any jail, prison, or correctional facility.

(2)  "Private correctional facility" means a correctional facility owned and operated by a private entity.

Acts 2007, No. 241, §1.
