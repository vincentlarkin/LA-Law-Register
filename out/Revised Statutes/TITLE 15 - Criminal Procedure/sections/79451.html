§902.  Juvenile institutions

The Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall establish, operate, and maintain the Louisiana Training Institute for juveniles at locations situated to meet the regional needs of the state.  

Added by Acts 1970, No. 353, §1.  Acts 1989, No. 4, §1, eff. June 1, 1989; Acts 1991, No. 467, §2.
