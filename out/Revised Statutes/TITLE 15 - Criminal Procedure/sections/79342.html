§824.1.  Deputy marshals, town of Jackson; Department of Corrections to provide funds

The Department of Corrections, out of its appropriated funds, shall annually allocate fifteen thousand dollars to the governing authority of the town of Jackson, for use solely to provide funds for the employment of two additional deputy marshals to provide extra law enforcement in and around the town of Jackson made necessary by reason of the location of Dixon Correctional Institute.  

Added by Acts 1978, No. 698, §1.
