CHAPTER 8.  LOUISIANA COMMISSION ON LAW

CRIMINAL JUSTICE
§1201.  Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice
The Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice is hereby created and established.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 592, §1.
