§1199.25. Mentor program for Veterans Court probation program; job assistance

            While participating in the Post-Conviction Veterans Mentor Program, the offender shall serve, upon written approval of the presiding judge, as a mentor in the Veterans Court probation program in the parish of the transitional work release program in which the offender is housed.

            Acts 2018, No. 273, §1; Acts 2020, No. 228, §1.
