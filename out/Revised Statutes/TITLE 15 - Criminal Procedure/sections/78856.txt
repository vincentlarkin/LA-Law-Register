§1152.  Prison enterprises
The department may engage in any agricultural, aquacultural, silvicultural, industrial enterprise, or service in accordance with the provisions of this Part.
Added by Acts 1983, 1st Ex.  Sess., No. 59, §1, eff. Feb.  7, 1983; Acts 2007, No. 289, §1.
