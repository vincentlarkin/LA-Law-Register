§1036.  Duties of board of commissioners; appointment, term, and compensation of superintendent; report

The board of commissioners shall elect a superintendent for the school; fix his term of employment and his compensation, enumerate by proper by-laws his powers and duties, and employ and discharge the superintendent and all necessary employees at the will of the board.  The board shall in each year render a full report to the governing authority of the parish of the operations of the industrial school accompanied by any recommendations which the board may desire to make in reference to management, expansion, and otherwise.
