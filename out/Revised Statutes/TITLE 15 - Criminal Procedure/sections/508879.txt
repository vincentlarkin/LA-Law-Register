PART XVIII.  REENTRY ADVISORY COUNCIL AND OFFENDER
REHABILITATION AND WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT
§1199.1.  Short title
This Part may be referred to and may be cited as the "Reentry Advisory Council and Offender Rehabilitation Workforce Development Act".
Acts 2008, No. 106, §1, eff. June 6, 2008; Acts 2010, No. 836, §1.
