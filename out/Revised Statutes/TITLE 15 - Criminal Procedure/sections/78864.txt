§116.  Preparation of general venire list; assistance
The clerk of court and the jury commission may avail themselves of the services of a deputy clerk or of a stenographer in the preparation of the general venire list, the slips for use in the general venire box, the proces verbal of the meetings and in all other proceedings of the jury commission.  All names, lists, slips, or other papers may be typewritten.
Acts 1966, No. 311, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1967.
