§1228.4.  Expenses

Legislative members of the policy board shall receive for attendance at meetings the same per diem and travel allowances as are provided by their respective houses for attendance at interim meetings or standing committees.  Other members of the policy board shall serve without per diem but may receive travel expenses in accordance with applicable travel regulations.

Acts 1999, No. 864, §1.
