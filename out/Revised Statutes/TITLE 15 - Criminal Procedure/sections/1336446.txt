§1109.7.  Immunity from civil liability
A.  The members of the board of commissioners of the Acadiana Regional Juvenile
Justice District shall be immune from suit and liability, either personally or in their official
capacity, for any claim for damage to or loss of property or personal injury or other civil
liability caused, connected to, or arising out of any actual or alleged act, error, or omission
that occurred within the course and scope of their actions, duties, or responsibilities for or
on behalf of the district or commission. However, nothing in this Section shall be construed
to protect any such person from suit or liability for any damage, injury, liability, or loss
caused by the intentional or willful and wanton misconduct of any such person.
B.  The immunity provided by this Section is in addition to any other immunity
provided by law.
Acts 2023, No. 166, §1, eff. June 7, 2023.
