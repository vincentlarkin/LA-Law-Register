§149.2. Offices; meetings

            The office shall maintain an office in East Baton Rouge Parish but may maintain such branch offices as it deems necessary to provide for the efficient and thorough regulation and governance of public defender services under its jurisdiction.

            Acts 2007, No. 307, §1; Acts 2013, No. 175, §1, eff. June 7, 2013; Acts 2024, 2nd Ex. Sess., No. 22, §1, eff. March 20, 2024.
