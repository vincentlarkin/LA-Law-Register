§622.1.  Sexual assault collection kits; prohibited use in criminal investigation of victim
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, DNA obtained by a
criminal justice agency from a sexual assault collection kit as defined in R.S. 15:622 shall
not be compared with other DNA records as defined in R.S. 15:603 for the purpose of
investigating the victim of the sexually oriented criminal offense who submitted the DNA
if that victim is charged with or suspected of committing any criminal offense.
Acts 2022, No. 625, §1.
