§1210.  Law enforcement planning district advisory councils; composition
A law enforcement planning district advisory council is hereby created and established for each law enforcement planning district agency.  These councils shall be composed in accordance with the guidelines prescribed by the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 592, §1.  Amended by Acts 1980, No. 753, §1, eff. July 31, 1980.
