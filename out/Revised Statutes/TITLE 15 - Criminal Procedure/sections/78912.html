§1228.  Integrated Criminal Justice Information System Policy Board

The Integrated Criminal Justice Information System Policy Board, hereinafter known as "policy board", is established under the jurisdiction of the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice, office of the governor.

Acts 1999, No. 864, §1.
