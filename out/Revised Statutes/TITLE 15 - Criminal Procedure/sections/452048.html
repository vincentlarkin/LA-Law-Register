§183.  Effect of signing of pleadings

The provisions of Code of Civil Procedure Article 863 shall be applicable to any petition for post-conviction relief and related or responsive pleadings filed by counsel for petitioner or respondent pursuant to the provisions of Code of Criminal Procedure Articles 924 through 930.8.

Acts 1999, No. 1012, §1; Acts 2007, No. 307, §16.
