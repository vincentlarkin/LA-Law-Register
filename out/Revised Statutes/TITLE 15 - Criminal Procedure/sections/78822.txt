§1105.  Central Louisiana Juvenile Detention Center Authority; creation; jurisdiction
The Central Louisiana Juvenile Detention Center Authority is hereby established as a political subdivision of the state, with a territorial jurisdiction throughout the parishes of Avoyelles, Catahoula, Concordia, Grant, LaSalle, Vernon, and Winn.
Acts 1997, No. 1445, §1, eff. July 15, 1997.
