§186.4. Standards and guidelines for representation of indigent children in custody; rulemaking

            A. The board shall adopt all rules necessary to implement the provisions of this Part.

            B. The rules shall include mandatory statewide standards and guidelines for the representation of indigent children in the custody of the office of juvenile justice to be provided in a uniform manner and consistent throughout the state.

            C. All rules and regulations shall be promulgated in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act and subject to legislative oversight by the House Committee on Health and Welfare and the Senate Committee on Health and Welfare.

            Acts 2016, No. 617, §2.
