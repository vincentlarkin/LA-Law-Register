§744.8.  Return to prison
After childbirth or any pregnancy-related medical distress and upon return to the prison, the prisoner may, upon her request, have a member of the prison's medical staff present during any "strip search", "visual body cavity search", or "body cavity search" as defined by department rules and regulations.
Acts 2012, No. 761, §1.
