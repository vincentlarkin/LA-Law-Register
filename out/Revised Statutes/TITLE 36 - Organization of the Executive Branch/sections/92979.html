CHAPTER 25.  DISTRIBUTION OF POWERS

§960.  Membership in certain departments and agencies prohibited

No member of the legislature shall hold office or serve in any of the departments or agencies of the executive branch of state government as enumerated and contained in this Title.  

Acts 1976, No. 513, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 83, §1, eff. June 22, 1977.
