§932.  Organizational meeting; officers

The committee created in this Chapter shall hold its organizational meeting within ninety days after adjournment sine die of the 1997 Regular Session of the Legislature on the call of the speaker of the House of Representatives and the president of the Senate, acting jointly.  At such organizational meeting the committee shall elect such officers as it shall find necessary.

Acts 1997, No. 1045, §1.
