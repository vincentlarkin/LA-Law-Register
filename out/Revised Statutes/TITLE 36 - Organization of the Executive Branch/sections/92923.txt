§853.  References in laws and documents
Wherever any agency transferred hereby is referred to or designated by the constitution or by any law or contract or other document, after the effective date of the transfer of such agency such reference or designation shall be deemed to apply to the department to which the transfer is made and/or its secretary.
Acts 1976, No. 513, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 83, §1, eff. June 22, 1977.
