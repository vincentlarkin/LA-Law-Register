§650.  Office of student financial assistance; purposes and functions

A.  The office of student financial assistance shall perform the functions of the state relating to the programs of financial assistance and the certain scholarship programs for higher education, all in accordance with applicable law.

B.  Repealed by Acts 2012, No. 251, §9; Acts 2012, No. 811, §17, eff. July 1, 2012.
