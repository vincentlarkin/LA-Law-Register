§692. Office of licensing; functions

            There is hereby created an office of licensing in the department of insurance. Such office shall be under the direction of the deputy commissioner for licensing. The duties and functions of the office of licensing and the deputy commissioner of licensing shall be as provided in this Title and as assigned by the commissioner.

            Acts 1992, No. 477, §1; Acts 2015, No. 274, §2, eff. June 29, 2015.
