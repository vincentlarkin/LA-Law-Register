§102. Officers of Louisiana Economic Development; compensation for one office only

            A. The officers of Louisiana Economic Development shall be the secretary and the deputy secretary if a deputy secretary is appointed, each of whom shall be selected and shall perform functions as provided in this Title.

            B. No person serving as a secretary or deputy secretary shall receive any additional salary from the state other than that salary which the person receives by virtue of serving in any one of such offices. Neither the secretary nor the deputy secretary shall be a statewide elected official.

            C. Notwithstanding any provision herein to the contrary, subject to approval of the governor, any person serving or appointed to serve as a secretary or deputy secretary may receive additional compensation for part-time services rendered as an instructor in post-secondary educational institutions or as a member of the National Guard.

            Acts 1976, No. 513, §1. Amended by Acts 1977, No. 83, §1, eff. June 22, 1977; Acts 2024, No. 590, §1, eff. June 11, 2024.
