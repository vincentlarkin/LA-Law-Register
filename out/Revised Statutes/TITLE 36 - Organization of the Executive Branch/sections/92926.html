§856.  Transfer of property

All books, papers, records, money, actions, and other property of every kind, movable and immovable, real and personal, heretofore possessed, controlled, or used by each agency transferred in accordance with this Part are hereby transferred to the department to which the agency is transferred.  

Acts 1976, No. 513, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 83, §1, eff. June 22, 1977.
