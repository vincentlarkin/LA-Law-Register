§305.  Deputy secretary
There may be a deputy secretary of the department, who shall be appointed by the
secretary with consent of the Senate and who shall serve at the pleasure of the secretary at
a salary fixed by the secretary, which salary shall not exceed the amount approved for such
position by the legislature while in session. The duties and functions of the deputy secretary
shall be determined and assigned by the secretary. If appointed, he shall serve as acting
secretary in the absence of the secretary.
Acts 1976, No. 513, §1.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 83, §1, eff. June 22, 1977; Acts
2008, No. 743, §3, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2018, No. 380, §2, eff. June 30, 2018.
