§284.  No effect on suit to enforce

The appointment of a receiver shall have no effect on the subsequent prosecution of the action to enforce the security interest.

Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:684 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
