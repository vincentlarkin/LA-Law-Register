CHAPTER 5.  THE LOUISIANA ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT
AND GAMING CORPORATION LAW
PART 1.  LEGISLATIVE INTENT AND POLICY
§201.  Title and citation
This Chapter shall be cited and referred to as the "Louisiana Economic Development and Gaming Corporation Act".
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:601 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
