PART V.  GENERAL CORPORATION GAMING OPERATIONS
§230.  Specific powers and duties of the corporation relating to casino gaming operations
A, B.  Repealed by Acts 2001, No. 1222, §2, eff. July 2, 2001.
C.  The casino gaming operator and no person who is licensed or has a direct or indirect financial interest in an entity licensed as a manufacturer of gaming devices and slot machines shall be licensed as a distributor of gaming devices or slot machines.
D.  The corporation shall issue licenses or permits to such other persons as it deems necessary for the protection of the citizens of this state or as may be necessary to carry out the requirements of this Chapter.
E.  The securing of any license or permit required under the provisions of this Chapter shall be a prerequisite for conducting, operating, or performing any activity regulated by the corporation.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:630 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 2001, No. 1222, §2, eff. July 2, 2001.
