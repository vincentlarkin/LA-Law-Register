§409.  Winning percentage
Each device must have dual electronic accounting devices to verify net device revenue and winning percentages, and access to the stored data must be readily available to the division.
Acts 1991, No. 1062, §1, eff. July 30, 1991.  Redesignated from R.S. 33:4862.3 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 2012, No. 161, §§2, 4.
