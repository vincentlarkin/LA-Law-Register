§203.  Official gaming establishment

The official gaming establishment shall be located in Orleans Parish.  The particular facility shall be determined as provided for in R.S. 27:241.

Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:603 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 2011, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 19, §1.
