§251.  Fees for licenses
Except as otherwise provided in this Chapter, the annual fee for a license issued under the provisions of this Chapter will be as set forth in the rules and regulations promulgated by the board.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:651 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 2001, No. 1222, §1, eff. July 2, 2001.
