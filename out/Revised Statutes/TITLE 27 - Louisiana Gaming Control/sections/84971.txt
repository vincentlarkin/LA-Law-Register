§276.  No cause of action against the corporation or the state
No rule or regulation and no provision in a contract executed by the corporation pursuant to authority granted in R.S. 27:275 shall be the basis for any cause of action in contract or in tort against the state or the corporation, its board of directors or its agents, attorneys, or employees.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:676 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
