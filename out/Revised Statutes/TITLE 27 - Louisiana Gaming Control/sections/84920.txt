§23.  Contracts
Unless otherwise authorized in this Title, all contracts for services,
goods, supplies, and major repairs entered into by the board shall be made in
accordance with Chapter 17, the Louisiana Procurement Code, of Title 39 of
the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §1, eff. May 1, 1996.
