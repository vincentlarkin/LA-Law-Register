PART XII.  MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS
§111.  Exemptions
Commercial cruise ships, as described in R.S. 14:90(B), shall be exempt from the provisions and requirements of this Chapter.
Acts 1991, No. 753, §1, eff. July 18, 1991.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:560 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
