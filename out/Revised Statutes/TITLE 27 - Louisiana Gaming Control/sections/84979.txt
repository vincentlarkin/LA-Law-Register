§283.  Operations by receiver
The actions of the receiver during the term of the receivership shall be governed by and taken in strict accordance with rules and regulations to be promulgated by the corporation.  The receiver shall be required to account to both the court and the corporation during his tenure at such times and in such detail as may be required by the rules and regulations of the corporation.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:683 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
