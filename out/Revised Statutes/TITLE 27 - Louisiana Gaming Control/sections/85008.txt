§315.  Charging for inactive accounts
A.  An operator shall not charge a player for an inactive account.
B.  An operator shall charge players only for entry fees placed or contests entered.
No player shall be charged for failure to enter on a fantasy sports contest or for failure to
deposit certain amounts of cash or cash equivalent into any account.
Acts 2020, No. 141, §1, eff. June 9, 2020.
