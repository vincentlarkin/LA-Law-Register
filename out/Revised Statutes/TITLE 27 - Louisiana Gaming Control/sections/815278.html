§450.  Holder of video draw poker employee permit

The holder of a video draw poker employee permit is authorized to work for any video draw poker licensee in the capacity specified on the video draw poker employee permit.

Acts 2003, No. 1268, §1; Acts 2012, No. 161, §4.
