§441.  Illegal lottery devices
Notwithstanding any provision of the law to the contrary, no video draw poker device licensed for play at a licensed establishment according to the provisions of this Chapter shall be considered an illegal lottery device for purposes of R.S. 47:9075.
Acts 1991, No. 1062, §1, eff. July 30, 1991.  Redesignated from R.S. 33:4862.15 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 2012, No. 161, §4.
