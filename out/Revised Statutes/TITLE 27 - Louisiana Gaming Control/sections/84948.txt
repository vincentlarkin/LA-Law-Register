§257.  Duty of corporation to initiate proceedings
The corporation, through the president, shall initiate appropriate proceedings or actions to enforce the provisions of this Chapter and may request or recommend that a district attorney or the attorney general prosecute any public offense committed in violation of any provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:657 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
