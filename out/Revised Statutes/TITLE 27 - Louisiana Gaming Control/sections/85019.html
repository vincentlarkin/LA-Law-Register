CHAPTER 7.  PARI-MUTUEL LIVE RACING FACILITY ECONOMIC

REDEVELOPMENT AND GAMING CONTROL ACT

PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS

§351.  Title

This Chapter shall be referred to as the "Louisiana Pari-mutuel Live Racing Facility Economic Redevelopment and Gaming Control Act".

Acts 1997, No. 721, §1, eff. July 9, 1997.
