§74.  Licenses; term

The term of any license to conduct gaming operations shall be five years.

Acts 1991, No. 753, §1, eff. July 18, 1991.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:534 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 1999, No. 1384, §1; Acts 2001, No. 1222, §1, eff. July 2, 2001.
