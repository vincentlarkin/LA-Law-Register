§222.  Corporation and board to act through president
The acts of the president shall be considered the acts of the corporation.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:622 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
