§206.  Venue in civil proceedings
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the venue for any civil proceeding by, against, or to which the corporation is a party shall be the parish of East Baton Rouge.
Acts 1992, No. 384, §1, eff. June 18, 1992.  Redesignated from R.S. 4:606 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996.
