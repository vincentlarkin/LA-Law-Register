§355.  Requirements for slot machines
Slot machines shall:
(1)  Be inspected by the board or its designee for certification and compliance.
(2)  Have a serial number or other identification number permanently affixed to the device by the manufacturer.
(3)  Be connected to a central computer operated and maintained as directed by the board.
(4)  Offer the same minimum winning percentage and payout as slot machines on riverboats.
Acts 1997, No. 721, §1, eff. July 9, 1997.
