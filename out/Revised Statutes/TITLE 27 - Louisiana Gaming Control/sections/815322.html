§453.  Toll-free telephone assistance for compulsive gamblers; posting of signs on premises

The division shall require the posting of one or more signs on licensed premises at points of entry to the areas where devices are located to inform customers of the toll-free telephone number available to provide information and referral services regarding compulsive or problem gambling.  Failure by the owner of the licensed premises to post and maintain such a sign or signs shall be cause for the imposition of a fine not to exceed one thousand dollars per day.

Acts 1993, No. 200, §3.  Redesignated from R.S. 33:4862.20 by Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 7, §3, eff. May 1, 1996; Acts 2012, No. 161, §4.
