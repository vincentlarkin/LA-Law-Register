§374.  Illegal lottery devices
Notwithstanding any provision of the law to the contrary, no slot machine operated according to the provisions of this Chapter shall be considered an illegal lottery device for purposes of R.S. 47:9075.
Acts 1997, No. 721, §1, eff. July 9, 1997.
