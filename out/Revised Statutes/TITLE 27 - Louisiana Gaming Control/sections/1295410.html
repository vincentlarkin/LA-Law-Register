§629. Pari-mutuel wagering; commissions, fees, and other deductions

            Any commissions, fees, and other deductions on racehorse wagering shall be in accordance with Chapter 4 of Title 4 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.

            Acts 2022, No. 530, §2.
