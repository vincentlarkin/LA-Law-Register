§1410.64.  Approval of petitions in bankruptcy court

No petition in relation to the readjustment of the debts of any agency of the state of Louisiana included in the provisions of R.S. 39:1410.60 shall be received or filed in the courts of bankruptcy of the United States unless accompanied by the written consent of the commission, and no such plan of readjustment shall be put into temporary effect or finally confirmed without the written approval of the commission of such plans.

Acts 1990, No. 500, §1, eff. July 18, 1990.
