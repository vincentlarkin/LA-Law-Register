§88.4. State agencies; compliance

            Each state agency shall comply with the provisions of the collection policy and procedure manual and is authorized to establish and maintain internal controls not inconsistent with the provisions included in the manual.

            Acts 2001, No. 904, §1, eff. June 26, 2001; Acts 2012, No. 500, §2.
