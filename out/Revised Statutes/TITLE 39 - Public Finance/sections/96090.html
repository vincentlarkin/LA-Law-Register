SUBPART D. TYPES OF CONTRACTS

§1611. Cost-plus-a-percentage-of-cost contracts

            The cost-plus-a-percentage-of-cost system of contracting shall not be used.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
