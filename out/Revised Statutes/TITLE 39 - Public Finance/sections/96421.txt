§461.8.  Allocation from state general fund
An amount sufficient to pay indebtedness incurred during the preceding fiscal year under the authority of Article VII, Section 7 of the Louisiana Constitution shall be allocated, as a first priority, each year from the state general fund.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 502, §3.
