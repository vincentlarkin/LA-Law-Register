§471.1.  Certain home rule charter parishes
Pursuant to Article VI, Section 6 of the Constitution of Louisiana, in any parish which operates under a home rule charter which provides for the governing authority of any road, subroad, waterworks, subwaterworks, sewerage, gravity drainage, subdrainage, fire protection, hospital service, or other special district lying wholly within the parish, such charter provisions and any other charter provisions relating to the organization and structure of parish governance shall supersede the provisions of R.S. 39:471 or any other statutory provision.
Acts 1999, No. 1079, §1.
