§21.2.  Use of official information by state agencies

Each state agency shall use the official information developed by the consensus estimating conferences in preparing its annual operating and capital outlay budget requests and for carrying out its duties under the state planning and budgeting system.

Acts 1990, No. 966, §2, eff. July 1, 1990.
