PART VII.  ENERGY MANAGEMENT ACT OF 2001
§251.  Short title
This Part may be cited as the "Energy Management Act of 2001".
Acts 1984, No. 662, §1, eff. July 1, 1984; Acts 1986, No. 255, §1, eff. July 1, 1986; Acts 2001, No. 1184, §1, eff. June 29, 2001.
