§1554.1.  Federal block grants
The provisions of this Chapter shall be applicable to any goods and services procured
with funds pursuant to the federally enacted community services block grant or community
development block grant.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 603, §1; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
NOTE:  Redesignated in part and amended by Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff.
Jan. 1, 2015.
