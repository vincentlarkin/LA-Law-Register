§1410.53.  Construction with other statutes

The powers conferred by this Part constitute an additional and separate grant of powers for the issuance and payment of the notes and all other acts in connection therewith authorized by this Part.  If there is any inconsistency between the provisions of this Part and any other law, the provisions of this Part shall control.

Acts 1991, No. 827, §1.
