§1363.  Incorporation into bond acts
Any bond act may adopt the provisions of this Chapter by reference to its title or legal citation, and such reference shall serve to incorporate the provisions of this Chapter into said act as though set out in full therein.
Added by Acts 1968, Ex.Sess., No. 27, §1.
