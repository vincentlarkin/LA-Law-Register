§343.  Accounts, vouchers, and documents to be preserved; copies
The commissioner of administration shall preserve in his office all accounts, vouchers and documents settled by him, and give copies thereof, duly authenticated, to any interested person.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 649, §1.
