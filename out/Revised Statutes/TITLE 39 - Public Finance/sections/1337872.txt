§121.1.  Timely submission of invoices
Any entity administering a capital outlay project included in the comprehensive
capital outlay budget may require all parties performing any portion of planning and
designing, including engineers and architects, or parties contracted with for the construction
of a capital outlay project to submit invoices for the payment of services rendered or
performed within one hundred eighty days of the date that the services were rendered or
performed.  Failure of a party to timely submit an invoice for services rendered or performed
shall result in the party's claim for reimbursement or payment from the state being denied.
Acts 2023, No. 82, §1, eff. July 1, 2024.
