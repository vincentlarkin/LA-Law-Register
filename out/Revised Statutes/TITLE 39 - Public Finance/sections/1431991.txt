SUBPART F. TRANSPARENT RESPONSIBLE USE OF STATE
TAX-DOLLARS (T.R.U.S.T.) ACT
§89.1. Short title
This Subpart shall be known as the "Transparent Responsible Use of State Tax-dollars Act" or "T.R.U.S.T. Act".
Acts 2025, No. 467, §1, eff. June 30, 2025.
