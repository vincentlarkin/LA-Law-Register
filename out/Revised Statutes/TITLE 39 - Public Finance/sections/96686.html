§97.5.  Construction of Subpart

Nothing in this Subpart shall or shall be construed to create, extinguish, revive, transfer, or otherwise affect any existing or future claim or cause of action or any objections, exceptions, or defenses thereto.

Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 140, §1, eff. May 5, 1998.
