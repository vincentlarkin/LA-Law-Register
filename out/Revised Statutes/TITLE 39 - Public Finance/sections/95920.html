§1433.  Application of provisions

The provisions of this Chapter shall supersede all other laws of the state of Louisiana relating to the issuance, execution, registration, transfer, and payment of securities of public entities in fully registered form.

Added by Acts 1983, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 34, §1, eff. Jan. 19, 1983.
