§1038.  Defeasance of bonds
The provisions of Chapter 14 of Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 shall be applicable to all bonds issued by the City and its public entities.
Acts 1985, No. 674, §8, eff. July 16, 1985.
