SUBPART D.  LOUISIANA ASBESTOS
DETECTION AND ABATEMENT FUND
§97.1.  Short title
This Subpart shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Asbestos Detection and Abatement Act".
Acts 1998, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 140, §1, eff. May 5, 1998.
