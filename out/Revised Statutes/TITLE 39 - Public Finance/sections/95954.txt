§1472.  Application of provisions
The powers and rights conferred by this Chapter shall be in addition to the power and rights conferred by any other general or special law.  The provisions of this Chapter shall be liberally construed for the accomplishment of its purpose.
Acts 1984, No. 102, §1, eff. June 15, 1984.
