SUBPART B. CONTRACT CONTROVERSIES

§1716. Contract controversies

            Under a cooperative purchasing agreement, controversies arising between an administering public procurement unit and its bidders, proposers, or contractors shall be resolved in accordance with Part VI of this Chapter, where the administering public procurement unit is a state public procurement unit or otherwise subject to Part VI.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
