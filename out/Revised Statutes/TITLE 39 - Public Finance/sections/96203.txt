§1798.3.  Functions of corporation
There is hereby authorized the formation and incorporation of a nonprofit corporation, the purpose of which shall be the financing and acquisition, purchase, construction, renovation, improvement, or expansion of public facilities for lease to the state of Louisiana.
Acts 1989, No. 805, §1, eff. July 8, 1989.
