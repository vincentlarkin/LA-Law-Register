§514. Bond validation

            Bonds issued under this Part may be validated in accordance with Part XVI of Chapter 32 of Title 13 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, or any successor laws relating to suits to determine validity of governmental bonds.

            Acts 2018, No. 569, §1, eff. July 1, 2018.
