§1565.  Duties of the attorney general
The attorney general shall be the chief legal adviser to the state chief procurement
officer.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan.
1, 2015.
