§1335.  Submission of budget
Not later than the first day of January in each year, each licensing
agency shall submit a copy of its proposed budget for the ensuing fiscal year
to the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget, to each chairman of a
standing committee of the legislature having jurisdiction as listed in R.S.
49:966, to the legislative auditor, and to the legislative fiscal office.
Acts 1989, No. 751, §2, eff. July 8, 1989.
