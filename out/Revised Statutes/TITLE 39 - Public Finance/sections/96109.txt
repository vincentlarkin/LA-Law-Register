PART IV.  SPECIFICATIONS
§1651.  Duties of the commissioner of administration
The commissioner shall promulgate regulations governing the preparation,
maintenance, and content of specifications for supplies, services, and major repairs required
by the state.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan.
1, 2015.
