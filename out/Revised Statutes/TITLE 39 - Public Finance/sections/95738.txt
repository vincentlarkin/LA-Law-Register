§1122.  Saving clause; pending proceedings
Nothing contained in this Chapter shall affect any proceeding heretofore begun by the governing authority of any subdivision to issue bonds under the authority of the constitution of 1913, or of this constitution and any statute heretofore in force in this state.  The proceedings may be completed under this Chapter so far as it can be made applicable thereto.
