§1798.1.  Construction of Chapter; supplemental and additional nature
This Chapter shall be deemed to provide a complete, additional, and alternative method for doing the things authorized hereby and shall be regarded as supplemental and additional to powers conferred by other laws.
Acts 1989, No. 805, §1, eff. July 8, 1989.
