PART V.  FIVE-YEAR BASE-LINE BUDGET PROJECTION

§171.  Base-line projection; purpose

A five-year base-line budget projection, hereinafter referred to as "base-line projection", shall be established to provide state policymakers with a financial tool against which to gauge revenue and expenditure proposals.

Acts 1992, No. 827, §1, eff. Aug.  1, 1992.
