SUBPART E. ADMINISTRATIVE APPEALS PROCEDURES

§1681. Authority of the commissioner of administration

            The commissioner of administration shall have the authority to review and determine any appeal by an aggrieved person from a determination by the state chief procurement officer or his designee which is authorized by R.S. 39:1671, R.S. 39:1672, or R.S. 39:1673.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
