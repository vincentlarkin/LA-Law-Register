§99.43.  Bonds exempt from taxation
The bonds, their transfer and the income therefrom, including any profit made on the
sale thereof, shall at all times be free and exempt from taxation by the state of Louisiana and
by any other political subdivision of the state.
Acts 2007, No. 249, §1, eff. July 6, 2007; Acts 2017, No. 357, §1.
