§1312.  Governing authority's failure to make appropriation

If, at the end of any fiscal year, the appropriations necessary for the support of the political subdivision for the ensuing fiscal year have not been made, then fifty percent of the amounts appropriated in the appropriation ordinance or resolution for the last completed fiscal year shall be deemed reappropriated for the several objects and purposes specified in such appropriation ordinance or resolution.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 504, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1980.  Acts 1984, No. 186, §2; Acts 1999, No. 775, §2, eff. July 2, 1999.
