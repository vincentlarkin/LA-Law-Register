§1959.  Monitoring of compliance
The division shall independently monitor the participation of enterprises
in the contracts let by each state agency and educational institution.
Acts 1984, No. 653, §1, eff. July 1, 1984; Acts 1987, No. 916, §1; Acts
1988, No. 397, §1; Acts 1992, No. 797, §2, eff. July 1, 1992.
