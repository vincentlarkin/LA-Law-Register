§1342.  State agencies, political subdivisions; assistance to committee
All agencies, boards, commissions, and departments of the state, whether budget or nonbudget units, and of any of its political subdivisions, shall assist the committee in its work and furnish such information, reports, aid, services, and assistance as may be requested by the committee, all without any cost or charge of any nature to the committee.
Acts 1989, No. 751, §2, eff. July 8, 1989.
