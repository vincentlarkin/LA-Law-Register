§1333.  Legislative intent

It is the intent of the legislature that this Chapter shall serve to provide for full disclosure of the proposed budgets and financial information of licensing agencies through review by the legislature of submitted budget documents.

Acts 1989, No. 751, §2, eff. July 8, 1989.
