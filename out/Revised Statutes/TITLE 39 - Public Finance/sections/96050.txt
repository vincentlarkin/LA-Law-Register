SUBPART C.  RECORDS; PUBLIC ACCESS
§1557.  Public access to procurement information
Procurement information shall be a public record to the extent provided in Chapter 1 of Title 44 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 and shall be available to the public as provided in such statute.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
