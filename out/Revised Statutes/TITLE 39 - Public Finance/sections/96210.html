CHAPTER 17-E.  LOUISIANA CORRECTIONS

PRIVATE MANAGEMENT ACT

§1800.1.  Title

This Chapter shall be referred to and may be cited as the "Louisiana Corrections Private Management Act".

Acts 1989, No. 360, §1, eff. June 28, 1989.
