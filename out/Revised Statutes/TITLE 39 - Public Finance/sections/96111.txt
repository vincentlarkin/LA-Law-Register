§1652.  Duties of the chief procurement officer
The chief procurement officer shall prepare, issue, revise, and monitor the use of specifications for required supplies, services, and major repairs.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
