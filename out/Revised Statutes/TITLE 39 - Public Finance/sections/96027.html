§1534.  Transfer of personnel and funds

All personnel and funds provided to the Division of Administration in the ancillary enterprise bill for operation of the property insurance section are hereby transferred to the office of risk management.

Added by Acts 1980, No. 520, §1, eff. July 1, 1980.
