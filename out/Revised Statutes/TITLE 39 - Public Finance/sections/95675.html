TITLE 39.  PUBLIC FINANCE

SUBTITLE I.  STATE FINANCE

CHAPTER 1.  DIVISION OF ADMINISTRATION

PART I.  DIVISION OF ADMINISTRATION

SUBPART A.  ORGANIZATION

§1.  Division of administration

A division of administration is hereby created as a division of the office of the governor and shall exercise those powers and functions as hereinafter set forth and as may be further provided by law.

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 58, §1; Acts 1956, No. 42, §1; Acts 1989, No. 836, §1, eff.  July 1, 1989.
