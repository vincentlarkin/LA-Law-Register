§1651.1.  Shrimp specifications
Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, regulations promulgated by the commissioner of administration or other purchasing entity, governing the purchase or use of shrimp shall require that the bid specify the count size of such shrimp and not specify size as "jumbo", "extra large", "medium", "small" or any other similar term or nomenclature used in the shrimping industry.
Acts 2001, No. 706, §1; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
