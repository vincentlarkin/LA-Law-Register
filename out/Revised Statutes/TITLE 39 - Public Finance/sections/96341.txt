§325.  Inventory reports; head of agency to furnish information
Upon completion of the initial inventory each property manager shall submit an inventory report to the commissioner and the Legislative Auditor containing a list of all property items in his custody, together with descriptive information as set forth in R.S. 39:324.  Upon completion of each subsequent annual inventory, each property manager shall submit to the commissioner and the Legislative Auditor a certified report containing all exceptions or discrepancies found in relating physical and perpetual inventory records.  The annual report also shall include a listing of idle or surplus items available for transfer or disposition.
Added by Acts 1964, No. 115, §1.
