§1677.  Remedies prior to an award
If it is determined prior to award that a solicitation or proposed award of a contract is in violation of law, then the solicitation or proposed award shall be cancelled.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
