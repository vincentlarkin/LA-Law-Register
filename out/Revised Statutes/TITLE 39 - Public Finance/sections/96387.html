PART XIV. STATE RESOURCES ACCOUNTABILITY

SUBPART A. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§366.1.  Short name

This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Accountability for State Resources Act".

Acts 2005, No. 329, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
