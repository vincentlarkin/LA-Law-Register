SUBPART H. INSURANCE

§1631. Direct purchase of insurance

            Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, the state may purchase insurance policies covering any property or insurable interests or activities of the state directly from insurers or underwriters, without the necessity for signature or countersignature of such policies, and in lieu thereof such policy shall be signed by an official or designated representative of the company issuing the policy.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980. Acts 2008, No. 415, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
