§1968. Noncompliance with Chapter by division

           Any person aggrieved by any act or inaction of the division may seek judicial relief by a writ of mandamus or by injunction, to require compliance with the provisions of R.S. 39:1953(B) and R.S. 39:1955.

           Acts 1984, No. 653, §1, eff. July 1, 1984; Acts 1992, No. 797, §2, eff. July 1, 1992.
