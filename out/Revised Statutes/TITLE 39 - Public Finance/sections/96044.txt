CHAPTER 17.  LOUISIANA PROCUREMENT CODE
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
SUBPART A.  SHORT TITLE, PURPOSES,
CONSTRUCTION, AND APPLICATION
§1551.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known as and may be cited as the Louisiana Procurement Code.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
