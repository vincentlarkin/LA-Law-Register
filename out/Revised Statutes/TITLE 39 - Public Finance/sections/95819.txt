§1313.  Budget filing
The chief executive or administrative officer shall retain and file certified copies of the adopted budget, budget adoption instrument, duly authorized budget amendments, and copies of supporting schedules and correspondence related to the budget at the domicile of the governing authority.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 504, §1, eff. Sept. 1, 1980.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 643, §1; Acts 1982, No. 116, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 186, §2; Acts 1999, No. 775, §2, eff. July 2, 1999.
