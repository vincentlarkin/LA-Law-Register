§1410.42.  Definitions

As used in this Part, unless the context otherwise requires:

(1)  "Commission" means the State Bond Commission of the state.

(2)  "Fund" means the state general fund.

(3)  "Note" means any note or other evidence of borrowing made under the authority of this Part.

Acts 1991, No. 827, §1.
