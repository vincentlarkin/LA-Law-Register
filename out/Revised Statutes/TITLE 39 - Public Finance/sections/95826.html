§1332.  Definitions

For the purposes of this Chapter:

(1)  "Licensing agency" shall mean any agency transferred as provided in R.S. 36:803.

(2)  "Committee" shall mean the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise.

Acts 1989, No. 751, §2, eff. July 8, 1989.
