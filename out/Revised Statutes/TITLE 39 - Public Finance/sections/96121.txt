SUBPART D.  SOLICITATIONS OR AWARDS
IN VIOLATION OF LAW
§1676.  Applicability of this Subpart
The provisions of this Subpart apply where it is determined administratively, or upon
administrative or judicial review, that a solicitation or award of a contract is in violation of
law.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan.
1, 2015.
