§100.95.  Grants for Grads Program; rulemaking

The Louisiana Housing Corporation shall establish rules in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this Subpart.

Acts 2008, No. 748, §1, eff. July 6, 2008; Acts 2011, No. 408, §5(B), eff. July 5, 2011.
