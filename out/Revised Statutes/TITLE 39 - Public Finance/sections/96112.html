§1653. Exempted items

            Specifications for supplies, services, or major repairs exempted pursuant to R.S. 39:1572 may be prepared by a purchasing agency in accordance with the provisions of this Part and regulations promulgated hereunder by the head of the governmental body granted authority to promulgate regulations by R.S. 39:1581.

            Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
