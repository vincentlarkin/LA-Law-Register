§257.  Reporting requirements

The division of administration shall report annually to the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget and the Legislative Fiscal Office the results of the energy cost-saving measures undertaken pursuant to this Part and the savings generated by such measures.

Acts 1984, No. 662, §1, eff. July 1, 1984; Acts 1986, No. 255, §1, eff. July 1, 1986; Acts 2001, No. 1184, §1, eff. June 29, 2001.
