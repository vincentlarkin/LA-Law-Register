§3.  Organization
The division of administration shall consist of the position of commissioner of administration, and such other subdivisions or sections as are deemed necessary, in the opinion of the governor, to carry out the functions of the division of administration.
Amended by Acts 1952, No. 58, §3; Acts 1956, No. 42, §1; Acts 1986, No. 765, §1, eff. July 1, 1986; Acts 1989, No. 836, §1, eff. July 1, 1989.
