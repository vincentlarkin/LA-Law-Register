§1633.  Authorization constitutes public record
Such written authorization as required by R.S. 39:1632 shall constitute a public
record as defined in Chapter 1 of Title 44 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 715, §1, eff. July 1, 1980; Acts 2014, No. 864, §2, eff. Jan.
1, 2015.
