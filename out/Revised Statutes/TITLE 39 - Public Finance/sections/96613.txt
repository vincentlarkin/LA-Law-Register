§814.  Election procedure
Elections under this Part shall be called and conducted and the returns thereof canvassed in accordance with the procedures provided by law for special tax elections, and wherever such law is inapplicable, in accordance with the general election laws of this state.
Added by Acts 1975, 1st Ex.Sess., No. 28, §1, eff. Feb. 20, 1975.
