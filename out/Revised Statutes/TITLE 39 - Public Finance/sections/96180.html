§1784.  Membership of board of directors; vacancies; compensation; expenses

A.  The governor shall appoint five persons to serve as the directors and members of this corporation.

B.  Members of the board of directors shall serve without compensation, but the corporation may reimburse such members for necessary expenses incurred in the discharge of their duties.

Acts 1985, No. 893, §1, eff. July 23, 1985.
