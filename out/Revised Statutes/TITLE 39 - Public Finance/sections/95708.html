§104.  Capital outlay budget preparation

A.  The division of administration then shall prepare the capital outlay budget for the next five years.

B.  The budget shall include a list of projects requested to be undertaken within the time period, projects recommended by the division of administration to be undertaken within the time period, the projected expenditures for each project, and the reasons for rejection of any requested project.

C.  The projects shall be listed by requesting agency and shall indicate whether the funds will be used for acquisitions, planning, construction, or purchase of equipment.

Acts 1989, No. 836, §1, eff. July 1, 1989; Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 133, §1, eff. July 1, 1994.
