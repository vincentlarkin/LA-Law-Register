§1410.49.  Investment of proceeds; income therefrom
The state treasurer is authorized to invest and reinvest the proceeds of the notes in any securities which are legal investments for the state general fund.  The income from any such investment shall be credited to the state general fund and shall be retained therein for application for any purpose for which said fund is created or used to pay debt service on the notes.
Acts 1991, No. 827, §1.
