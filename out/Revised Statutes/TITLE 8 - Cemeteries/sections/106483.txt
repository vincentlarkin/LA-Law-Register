§809.  Waiver of right of interment
Any surviving spouse, parent, child, or heir having a right of interment in an
interment space may waive such right in favor of any other relative or spouse of a relative
of the deceased record owner.  Upon such waiver, the remains of the person in whose favor
the waiver is made may be interred in the interment space.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
