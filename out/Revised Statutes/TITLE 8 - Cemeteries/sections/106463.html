§703. Compliance with ordinances and specifications

            The development of the undeveloped land shall comply with the laws, ordinances, building codes, and any and all other lawful requirements of the state, parish, and municipality in which the land is located.

            Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
