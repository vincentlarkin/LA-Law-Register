§902.  Underground burials; depth
Any cemetery sexton or other person digging graves for underground interments shall
dig sufficiently deep to allow for at least two feet of soil to cover the entire area of the casket,
unless the interment is in a burial vault, coping, or lawn crypt.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
