§803. Descent of title to cemetery space

            Except as otherwise provided in this Chapter, every right of interment and cemetery space shall be subject to the laws of this state pertaining to community property, inheritance, including but not limited to the laws of intestacy, donations inter vivos and mortis causa, and successions.

            Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
