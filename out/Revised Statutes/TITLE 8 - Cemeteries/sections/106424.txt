§604.  Improper construction a nuisance; penalty
Every owner or builder of a structure erected or converted in violation of this Title
shall be guilty of maintaining a public nuisance and, upon conviction, shall be punishable by
a fine of not less than five hundred dollars nor more than five thousand dollars or by
imprisonment for not less than one month nor more than six months, or both.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
