§605.  Construction in compliance with existing laws
The penalties of this Chapter shall not apply to any structure that is in existence on
July 31, 1974, which at the time of construction was constructed in compliance with the laws
then existing, provided its continued use is not in violation of the laws for the protection of
the public health.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
