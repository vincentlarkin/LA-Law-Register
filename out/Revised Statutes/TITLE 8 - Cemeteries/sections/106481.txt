§807.  Co-owners; identification
An affidavit by any informed but disinterested person having knowledge of the facts
setting forth the fact of the death of one co-owner and establishing the identity of the
surviving co-owner named in the deed to any cemetery space or right of interment, when
filed with the cemetery authority, shall constitute complete and sufficient authorization to the
cemetery authority to permit the use of one unoccupied portion of the space in accordance
with the directions of the surviving co-owner.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
