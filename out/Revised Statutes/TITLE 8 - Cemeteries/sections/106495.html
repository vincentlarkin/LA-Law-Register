§906.  Additional ad valorem tax; Evangeline Parish

Notwithstanding the rate limitations of R.S. 8:105 and of R.S. 33:2740.1 but otherwise in accordance with R.S. 8:104 and 105, the governing authority of Evangeline Parish may levy an ad valorem tax not in excess of two mills when the proposed tax is approved by a majority of the qualified electors voting on the proposition at an election held for that purpose and conducted in accordance with the Louisiana Election Code.

Acts 1996, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 27, §1.
