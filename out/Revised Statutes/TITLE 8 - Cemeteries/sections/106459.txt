§69.1.  Service of pleadings upon board
Upon commencement of any action wherein a person or cemetery authority is alleged to be operating or conducting a cemetery business is named defendant
1
the plaintiff's attorney shall mail a copy of the petition to the board within ten days of filing the action.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 428, §1.
1
The language of this section appears as in the enrolled bill.
