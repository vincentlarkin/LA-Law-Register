CHAPTER 10-A.  LOUISIANA UNMARKED HUMAN

BURIAL SITES PRESERVATION ACT

§671.  Short title

This Chapter shall be known as the "Louisiana Unmarked Human Burial Sites Preservation Act".

Acts 1991, No. 704, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
