§507.  Nonwaiver of provisions of Chapter
Any provision of any contract for the sale of personal property or the performance of
services contemplated in this Chapter which waives any of the provisions of this Chapter
shall be void.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
