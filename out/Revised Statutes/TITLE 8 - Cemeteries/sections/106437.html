§656.  Effect of authorization

Any person signing any authorization for the interment of any remains warrants the truthfulness of any fact set forth in the authorization, the identity of the person whose remains are sought to be interred, and his authority to order the interment.  Such person shall be personally liable for all damage occasioned by or resulting from breach of such warranty.

Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2003, No. 1243, §1.
