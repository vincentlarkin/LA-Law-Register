§704.  Improper use a nuisance; penalty
Every landowner who sells or otherwise disposes of or causes or permits the sale or
other disposition of undeveloped land for use for the interment of human remains in violation
of this Section shall be guilty of maintaining a public nuisance and, upon conviction, shall
be punishable by a fine of not less than five hundred dollars nor more than five thousand
dollars or by imprisonment for not less than one month nor more than six months, or both.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
