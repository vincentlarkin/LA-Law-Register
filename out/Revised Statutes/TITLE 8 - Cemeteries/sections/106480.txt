§806.  Rights of co-owners
When the record title to a cemetery space or right of interment is in the names of two
or more persons, each shall be considered as having a vested right therein and no conveyance
or other disposition of the space or right shall be recognized without the written concurrence
of each record owner, but each owner shall have the right of interment in any unoccupied
portion of the cemetery space at the time of death.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
