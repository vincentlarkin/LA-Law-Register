§317.  Certain cemetery lands exempt from taxes
Property dedicated for cemetery purposes, including cemetery spaces and the land on
which they stand, shall be exempt from all taxation to the fullest extent permitted by the
Constitution of Louisiana and laws of this state.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
