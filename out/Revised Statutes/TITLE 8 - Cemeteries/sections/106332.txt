§107.  Gifts, donations, and contributions
The municipal governing authority may accept gifts for purposes of establishing or
maintaining a public cemetery, provided there is no condition thereto inconsistent with the
purposes set forth in this Chapter.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
