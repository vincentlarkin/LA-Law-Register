§705.  Construction in compliance with existing laws
The penalties of this Title shall not apply as to any land that is being used for the
interment of human remains if the continued use of such land is not in violation of the laws
for the protection of public health.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
