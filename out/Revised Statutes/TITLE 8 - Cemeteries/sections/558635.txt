§206.  Contracts required; disclosure
A cemetery authority offering to provide burial rights or a cemetery authority or other entity offering cemetery related merchandise and services shall provide to the customer, upon purchase, a written contract which complies with the requirements set forth in this Title and the rules and regulations of the Louisiana Cemetery Board.
Acts 2008, No. 188, §2, eff. June 13, 2008.
