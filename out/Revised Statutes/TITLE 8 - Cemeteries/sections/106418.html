§509.  Trustee's duty of care

The trust shall be administered in conformity with R.S. 8:465 with respect to the nature and character of the trust investments and the duties of the trustee.

Acts 1974, No. 417, §1.  Amended by Acts 1980, No. 428, §1; Acts 2004, No. 67, §1.
