CHAPTER 6.  CEMETERY SALES AND
MANAGEMENT ORGANIZATION
§401.  License to engage in business
No person shall engage in the business of a cemetery sales organization or a cemetery
management organization except as authorized by this Title and without first obtaining a
license from the board.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
