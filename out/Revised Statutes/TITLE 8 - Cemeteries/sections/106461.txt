CHAPTER 11.  UNDEVELOPED LAND
§701.  Application
This Chapter applies to all undeveloped land of a cemetery authority that is used,
intended to be used, or converted into use for the interment of human remains.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
