§462. Examination expense; effect of refusal to pay; disposition

            If a cemetery authority refuses to pay examination expenses, the board may refuse it a certificate of authority and may revoke any existing certificate of authority.

            Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2015, No. 222, §1.
