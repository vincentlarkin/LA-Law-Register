§460.  Penalties
Whoever violates any of the provisions of this Chapter, shall, upon conviction, be
fined not more than one thousand dollars, or imprisoned for not more than six months, or
both.
Acts 1974, No. 417, §1; Acts 2022, No. 574, §1.
