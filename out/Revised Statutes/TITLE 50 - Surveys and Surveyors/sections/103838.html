§10. Use of Louisiana coordinate system names

            Use of the terms "Louisiana Coordinate System of 1927", "Louisiana Coordinate System of 1983", and "Louisiana Plane Coordinate System" on any map, report of a survey, or other document shall be limited to coordinates based on the coordinate systems defined in this Chapter.

            Acts 1968, No. 299, §2; Acts 1985, No. 913, §1; Acts 2024, No. 626, §2.
