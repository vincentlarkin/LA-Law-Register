§334.1.  Revenue utilization; Atchafalaya Basin Levee District
Nothing shall prohibit the Atchafalaya Basin Levee District from expending tax funds directly for tidal flood control projects within the Atchafalaya Basin Levee District.
Acts 1988, No. 687, §1.
