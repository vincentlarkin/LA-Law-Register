PART XVIII.  MILL CREEK RECREATION AND WATER
CONSERVATION DISTRICT
§3087.51.  Creation
There is hereby created a recreation and water conservation district to be known as the "Mill Creek Recreation and Water Conservation District".
Acts 1995, No. 443, §2, eff. June 17, 1995.
