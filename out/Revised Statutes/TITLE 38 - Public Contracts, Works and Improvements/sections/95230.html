§305.  Employment of attorney

 Except as provided in R.S. 38:330.5, each levee board and levee and drainage board for a levee district or levee and drainage district may employ one or more attorneys to represent it and to offer advice and assistance of a legal nature and one or more engineers.  The salary of each attorney shall be determined by the board.

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985; Acts 2006, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 1, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
