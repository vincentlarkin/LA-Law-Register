§3108.  Eminent domain

Nothing contained in this chapter shall be construed as creating, in any condemnation proceedings brought under the power of eminent domain, any element of value or of damage not in existence on the date of enactment of this Chapter.  Any payments herein authorized are gratuities and are vested in the discretion of the agency head.  

Added by Acts 1971, No. 116, §1, emerg. eff. June 14, 1971.
