§393.  Grant as additional authority
The right to take possession and title in advance of final judgment as provided herein is in addition to any right or authority conferred by the laws of this state under which expropriation or appropriation proceedings may be conducted and shall not be construed as abrogating, eliminating, or modifying any such right or authority.
Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
