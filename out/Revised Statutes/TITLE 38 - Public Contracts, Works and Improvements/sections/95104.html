PART V.  JOHN K. KELLY GRAND BAYOU RESERVOIR DISTRICT

§2701.  Creation

There is hereby created a recreation and water conservation district to be known as the "John K. Kelly Grand Bayou Reservoir District".  

Acts 1958, No. 474, §1; Acts 1989, No. 176, §1; Acts 1993, No. 120, §1.
