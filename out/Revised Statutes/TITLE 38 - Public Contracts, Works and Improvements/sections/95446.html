§3109.  Fund availability

Funds dedicated, appropriated or otherwise available to any state agency or unit of local government for the acquisition of real property or any interest therein for a particular program or project shall be available also for obligation and expenditure to carry out the provisions of this Chapter as applied to that program or project.  

Added by Acts 1971, No. 116, §1, emerg. eff. June 14, 1971.
