§2294.  Separate bids
A.  Each closed specification authorized by the approving authority shall be offered for bid as a separate item.
B.  No contractor or sub-contractor shall accept a bid on a closed specification unless the item is priced separately in the bid.
Added by Acts 1965, No. 40, §1.
