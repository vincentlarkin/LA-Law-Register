§433.  Refunding of outstanding bonds

The refunding of the outstanding bonds of a levee district or levee and drainage district into the bonds authorized by this Subpart may be effected by a direct exchange of bonds or by a negotiation of the bonds authorized by this Subpart, and the application of the proceeds to the payment of redemption of the bonds now outstanding.  

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
