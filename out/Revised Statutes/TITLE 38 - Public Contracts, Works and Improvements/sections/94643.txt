§1612.  Record of proceedings of board
The board of commissioners of drainage districts shall cause to be kept a well bound book, entitled "Record of Board of Commissioners of (Here insert name of drainage district)" in which shall be recorded the minutes of all meetings, all proceedings, certificates, oaths of commissioners, bonds of employees and contractors, and any and all corporate acts.  The records shall be in the possession of the secretary of the board and shall be open to inspection at all times by any person interested.
