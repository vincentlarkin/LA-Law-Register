PART XIX.  CADDO LAKE WATERSHED DISTRICT

§3087.71.  Creation; location

There is hereby created a recreation and water conservation district to be known as the "Caddo Lake Watershed District" to be comprised of all of Ward 1 of Caddo Parish.

Acts 1995, No. 345, §1.
