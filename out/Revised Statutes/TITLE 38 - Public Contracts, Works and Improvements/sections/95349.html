PART XVII.  KEPLER CREEK RECREATION AND WATER

CONSERVATION DISTRICT

§3087.31.  Creation

There is hereby created a recreation and water conservation district to be known as the "Kepler Creek Recreation and Water Conservation District".

Acts 1995, No. 443, §1, eff. June 17, 1995.
