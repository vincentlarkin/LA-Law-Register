§322.  Annual audit; report

The legislative auditor or a certified public accountant shall conduct a biennial audit of each levee board and levee and drainage board pursuant to R.S. 24:513(A) and shall issue and distribute all audit reports pursuant to R.S. 24:516(A).  However, at the request by such a board, there shall be an annual audit.  

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985; Acts 1986, No. 130, §1.
