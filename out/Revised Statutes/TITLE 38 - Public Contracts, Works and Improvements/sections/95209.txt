§3003.  Board of commissioners created
The Board of Commissioners of the Jackson-Bienville Parishes Dugdemona Watershed District, within its powers and duties as defined herein, is hereby created and established as the governing board of the Jackson-Bienville Parishes Watershed District.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 600, §1.
