§1591.  Certificates as negotiable paper

 Drainage certificates issued hereunder shall be deemed to possess all the legal requisites of negotiable paper, shall not be invalid for any irregularity or defect in the proceedings for their issuance, sale, or delivery, and shall be incontestable in the hands of bona fide purchasers or holders for value thereof.  

Added by Acts 1983, No. 708, §1.
