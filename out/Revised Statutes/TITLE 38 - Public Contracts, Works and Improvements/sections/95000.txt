§2316.  Applicability
Except as provided in R.S. 38:2311(C), the provisions of this Part shall not
apply to professional service contracts in which the estimated project budget cost is
one million dollars or less.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 721, §1.  Amended by Acts 1982, No. 618, §1; Acts
1999, No. 921, §1, eff. July 7, 1999; Acts 1999, No. 1013, §1, eff. July 9, 1999; Acts
2024, No. 427, §1, eff. June 3, 2024.
