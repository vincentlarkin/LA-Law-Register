§317.  Acquisitive prescription shall not run against levee districts

The prescription by which the ownership of property is acquired as defined by Article 3446 of the Louisiana Civil Code shall not run against any levee district or levee and drainage district or against the board of commissioners of any levee district or levee and drainage district.  

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
