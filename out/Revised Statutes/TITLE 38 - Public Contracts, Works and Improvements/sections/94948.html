PART II-B.  TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND DATA PROCESSING

PROCUREMENT BY POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS

§2234.  Short title

This Part shall be known as the "Political Subdivisions Telecommunications and Data Processing Procurement Law".  

Acts 1988, No. 523, §1; Acts 1992, No. 486, §1.
