§395.  Divesting of title

The plaintiff shall not be divested by court order of any title acquired under these provisions except where such court finds that the property was not expropriated for a public purpose.  In the event of such findings, the court shall enter such judgment as is necessary to compensate the defendant for the period during which the property was in the possession of the plaintiff and to recover for the plaintiff any award paid.  

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
