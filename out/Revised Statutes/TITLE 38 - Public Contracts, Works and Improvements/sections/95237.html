§3056.  Assistance; advisory subcommittees

The director of public works and the parish attorney shall serve in an advisory capacity to the commission, subject to call by the commission.  The office of parish clerk shall furnish such clerical assistance as may from time to time be required by the commission.  The commission shall be, and is hereby authorized to appoint such advisory subcommittees as may be deemed appropriate for the purpose of working with the commission in the study of various phases of water supply, usage, ground water conditions and special reports of salt water encroachment.  

Added by Acts 1970, No. 682, §6.
