§1504.  Mortgage certificates

In furnishing a mortgage certificate in the name of any owner of any land located in any drainage or sub-drainage district within any parish, the clerk of court and ex-officio recorder of mortgages of the parish shall not set forth on the mortgage certificate any of the proceedings incident to the organization or bond issue of the drainage or sub-drainage district, but when the tax roll of any drainage or sub-drainage district has been filed with him for record, the clerk of court and ex-officio recorder of mortgages shall set forth on the mortgage certificate any delinquent or unpaid drainage tax owing by the land owner, just as he would report any delinquent state or parish tax on the mortgage certificate.
