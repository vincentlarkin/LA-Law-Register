§3098.8.  Chapter supplementary to other laws and regulations
This Chapter shall be supplementary to laws, rules, and regulations of the state of Louisiana, or any of its political subdivisions, and of any other state agencies or commissions, except insofar as such conflict may exist.
Added by Acts 1980, No. 715, §1.
