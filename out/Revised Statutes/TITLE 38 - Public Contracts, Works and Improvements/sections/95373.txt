§3087.56.  Election of officers
Immediately after the members of the board of commissioners have been appointed, or as soon as thereafter is practicable, they shall meet and organize by electing from their number a president, vice president, and secretary who shall perform the duties normally required of such officers.
Acts 1995, No. 443, §2, eff. June 17, 1995.
