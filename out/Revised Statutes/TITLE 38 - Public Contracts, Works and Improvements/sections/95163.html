§2838.  Attorney general as counsel

The attorney general of the State of Louisiana and his assistants shall be and are hereby designated as counsel for the State Department of Public Works and the said district in the execution of the purposes of this Part and are hereby charged with the responsibility of representing the said State Department of Public Works and the board of commissioners in any and all matters when called upon to do so.  

Added by Acts 1966, No. 262, §18.
