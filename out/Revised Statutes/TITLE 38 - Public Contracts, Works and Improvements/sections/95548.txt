§369.  Examination of juror
The court shall permit the parties or their attorneys to conduct the examination of a prospective juror and may itself conduct an examination, which shall be limited to ascertaining the qualifications of the juror.
Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
