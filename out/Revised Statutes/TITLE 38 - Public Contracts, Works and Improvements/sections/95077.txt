§2571.  Grant of state lands to district; right of mineral leases unabridged
All lands belonging to the state of Louisiana which are enclosed in the said area, as provided in R.S. 38:2551, and which are necessary to the erection and maintenance of said Bayou D'Arbonne Lake are hereby granted to the Bayou D'Arbonne Lake Watershed District for the purposes herein set out, but this grant shall in no wise abridge the right of the state to lease the said land for the production of oil, gas and other minerals under the general laws of the state as now provided.
Added by Acts 1956, No. 9, §21.
