§3087.222.  Location

The district shall be comprised of the area of Lake St. Joseph and the Black River Area and all of the land encompassed by Louisiana Highways 605 and 608 surrounding Lake St. Joseph and the Black River Area.

Acts 2003, No. 328, §1.
