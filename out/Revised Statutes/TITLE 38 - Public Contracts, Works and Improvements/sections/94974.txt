§2252.  Requests for bids and proposals to contain reference to preference
All requests for bids and proposals for any purchase shall contain the words: "Preference is hereby given to materials, supplies and provisions, produced, manufactured or grown in Louisiana, quality being equal to articles offered by competitors outside of the state".
Added by Acts 1958, No. 318, §2.
