§1851.  Right to vote
Only property taxpayers qualified as electors under the constitution and laws of this state shall be entitled to vote at any election hereunder.  The qualifications of the taxpayers as voters shall be those of age, residence, and registration as voters, as prescribed by the constitution and statutes, without regard to sex.  There shall be no voting by proxy.
