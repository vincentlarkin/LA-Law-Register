§90.8.  Notification to proper authority of project status by office of engineering

After receipt of the list of projects adopted by the legislature, the office shall determine if each project lies solely within the jurisdiction of one authority or is partially located within other authorities.  The office shall notify each authority affected by the projects in the plan of the status of each project.

Added by Acts 1982, No. 351, §1; Acts 1999, No. 999, §1, eff. July 9, 1999; Acts 2006, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 6, §4; Acts 2012, No. 753, §5.
