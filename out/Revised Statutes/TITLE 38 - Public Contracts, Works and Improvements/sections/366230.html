§330.13.  Public contracts; Louisiana Initiative for Small Entrepreneurship

The board of commissioners of the Southeast Louisiana Flood Protection Authority-East and Southeast Louisiana Flood Protection Authority-West Bank shall comply with the provisions of R.S. 39:2001 et seq., when advertising and letting public works contracts and procurement contracts.

Acts 2006, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 1, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
