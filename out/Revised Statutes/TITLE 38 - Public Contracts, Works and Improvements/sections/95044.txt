§2361.  Emergency projects
A.  Notwithstanding any provision of this Chapter, in the event of a declared
emergency under R.S. 29:721 et seq., a project within the jurisdiction of the authority may
proceed immediately upon written certification by the governor that the project is necessary
to preserve public health, safety, critical military infrastructure, or essential federal
navigation or flood control interests.
B.  Projects authorized under this Section shall be submitted for ratification in the
next annual plan but may proceed prior to such approval.
Acts 2025, No. 418, §1.
