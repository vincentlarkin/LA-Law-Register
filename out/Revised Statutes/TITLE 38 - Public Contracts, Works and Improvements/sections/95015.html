CHAPTER 11.  SABINE RIVER AUTHORITY

§2321.  Creation

All the territory in the parishes of DeSoto, Sabine, Vernon, Beauregard, Calcasieu and Cameron, lying within the watershed of the Sabine River and its tributary streams, shall be embraced in the limits of and shall constitute a conservation and reclamation district to be known and styled "Sabine River Authority, State of Louisiana".  

Added by Acts 1950, No. 261, §1.
