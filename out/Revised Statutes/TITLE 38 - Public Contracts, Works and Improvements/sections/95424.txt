§3098.1.  Qualifications for license
In order to be licensed as a drilling contractor, as provided in R.S. 38:3098, in the
state of Louisiana, the applicant must possess the following qualifications:
(1)  Be at least eighteen years of age.
(2)  Be of good moral character.
(3)  Have a minimum of two years drilling experience.
(4)  Demonstrate to the satisfaction of the Louisiana State Licensing Board for
Contractors a reasonable knowledge of this Chapter and the rules and regulations adopted
by the board and the Department of Conservation and Energy under the provisions of this
Chapter.
