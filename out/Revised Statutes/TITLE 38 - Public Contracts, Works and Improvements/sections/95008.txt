§2319.3.  Equipment-lease-purchase contracts with nonprofit lessors authorized; term
Local governmental entities may enter into equipment-lease-purchase contracts directly with nonprofit lessors, as provided in this Part, for a term not to exceed ten years or the reasonably expected economic life of the equipment, whichever is less, notwithstanding any other law to the contrary.
Acts 1985, No. 758, §1, eff. July 17, 1985.
