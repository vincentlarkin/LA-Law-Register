§3087.35.  Oaths
Before entering upon his official duties each member of the board of commissioners shall take the oath of office provided by Article X, Section 30 of the Constitution of Louisiana before an officer authorized by law to administer oaths.
Acts 1995, No. 443, §1, eff. June 17, 1995.
