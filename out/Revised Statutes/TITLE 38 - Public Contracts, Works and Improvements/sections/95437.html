§310.  Expenses of levee board members as delegates to flood relief conventions; dues

The levee boards may defray the actual, properly-documented expenses of their members and executives when traveling as delegates to conventions and meetings held in relation to flood relief and control.  The levee boards may pay the dues in associations or conventions related to flood relief and control.  

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
