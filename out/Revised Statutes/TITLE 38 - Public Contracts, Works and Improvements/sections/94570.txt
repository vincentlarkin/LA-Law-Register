§142. Drainage of levee and parallel or contiguous road
Local governing bodies shall thoroughly drain all public roads which are parallel or
contiguous to any public levee, shall cut across public roads all ditches and drains necessary
to effect the complete and perfect drainage of the roads and levees, and shall connect the
drains and ditches with those on private property abutting on the road.
Acts 2025, No. 452, §2.
