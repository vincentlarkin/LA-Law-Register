§1842.  Designation of districts; political subdivisions of state
The consolidated gravity drainage districts so created shall be known and designated as "Consolidated Gravity Drainage Districts No. _____ of the parish of _____, Louisiana.  Consolidated gravity drainage districts created under the authority of this Part shall be political subdivisions of the state and shall have all the rights, powers and privileges granted under the Constitution and laws of this state.
