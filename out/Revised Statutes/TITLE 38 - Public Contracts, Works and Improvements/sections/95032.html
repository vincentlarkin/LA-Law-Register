§2335.  Toll bridges and ferries

In addition to the powers imposed in the Authority under the provisions of R.S. 38:2332 above, the Authority shall have the right to own and operate such toll bridges over or ferries and ferry service across any of the waters under its jurisdiction as may in its opinion be necessary to provide adequate facilities for the traveling public.  

Added by Acts 1956, No. 432, §6.
