CHAPTER 13.  WATER CONSERVATION
PART I.  POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE LOUISIANA
WILDLIFE AND FISHERIES COMMISSION
§2501.  Louisiana Wildlife and Fisheries Commission; powers; duties; functions
The Louisiana Wildlife and Fisheries Commission shall have sole authority to control and regulate all aspects of hunting, fishing, and boating in all water conservation districts enumerated in this Chapter.
Acts 1993, No. 830, §1.
