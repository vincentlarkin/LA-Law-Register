§324.  Protection of obligations

This Chapter is in no way and to no extent intended to nor shall it be construed in any manner which will impair the contractual or other obligations of any levee board or levee and drainage board of the state of Louisiana.  

Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
