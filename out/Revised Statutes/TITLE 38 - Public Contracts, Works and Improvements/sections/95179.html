§2872.  Contracts of the district

Contracts of the district shall be subject to the provisions of Chapter 10 of this Title, relative to public contracts (R.S. 38:2181 et seq.).

Added by Acts 1966, No. 299, §12; Acts 2005, No. 81, §1.
