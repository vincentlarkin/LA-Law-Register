PART XXVIII.  LAKE ST. JOSEPH RECREATION AND WATER
CONSERVATION DISTRICT
§3087.221.  Creation
There is hereby created a recreation and water conservation district to be known as the "Lake St. Joseph Recreation and Water Conservation District".
Acts 2003, No. 328, §1.
