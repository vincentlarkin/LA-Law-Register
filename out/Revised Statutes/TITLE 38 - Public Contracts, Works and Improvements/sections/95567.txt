§388.  Trial according to Code of Civil Procedure and the general expropriation laws
Except as provided in this Part, these suits shall be tried in accordance with the provisions of the Code of Civil Procedure and general expropriation laws.
Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
