PART XXXIV.  LAKE ST. JOHN RECREATION AND WATER

CONSERVATION DISTRICT

§3087.341.  Creation

There is hereby created a recreation and water conservation district to be known as the "Lake St. John Recreation and Water Conservation District".

Acts 2005, No. 214, §1, eff. June 29, 2005.
