§2254.  Delivery tickets and itemized invoices
All persons or companies selling to the state or any of its agencies, boards, districts or commissions shall comply with the following provisions:
(1)  A delivery ticket must accompany the things sold when delivered and
(2)  A complete itemized invoice must be sent to the proper place immediately.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 318, §4.
