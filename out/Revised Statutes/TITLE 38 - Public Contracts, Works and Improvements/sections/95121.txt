PART VI.  BAYOU DESIARD-BAYOU BARTHOLOMEW CUT-OFF
LOOP WATER CONSERVATION BOARD
§2751.  Creation
There is hereby created a water conservation board to be known as the Bayou DeSiard-Bayou Bartholomew Cut-Off Loop Water Conservation Board of Ouachita and Morehouse Parishes, Louisiana.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 308, §1.
