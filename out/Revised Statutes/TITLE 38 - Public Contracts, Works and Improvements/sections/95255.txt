§3084.  Aiding and abetting violation
Whoever knowingly and willfully aids or abets a person in the violation of a provision of this part, or any rule, regulation, or order made hereunder, shall be subject to the same penalties provided herein for the principal violator.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 678, §1.
