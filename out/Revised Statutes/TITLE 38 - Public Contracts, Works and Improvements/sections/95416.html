CHAPTER 13-A.  UTILIZATION OF GROUND WATER RESOURCES

§3091.  Legislative findings; purpose

The utilization of ground water resources is hereby found and declared to be a matter of public interest.  

It is the purpose of this chapter to provide for the efficient administration, and gathering of data concerning ground water resources of the State of Louisiana.  

Acts 1972, No. 535, §§1, 2.
