§3102.  Administration of relocation assistance programs

In order to prevent unnecessary expenses and duplication of functions, the agency may make relocation payments or provide relocation assistance or otherwise carry out the functions required under this Chapter by utilizing the facilities, personnel, and services of any other federal, state or local governmental agency having an established organization for conducting relocation assistance programs.  

Added by Acts 1971, No. 116, §1, emerg.  eff. June 14, 1971.
