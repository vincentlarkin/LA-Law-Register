PART XI.  JACKSON-BIENVILLE PARISHES
DUGDEMONA WATERSHED DISTRICT
§3000.  Creation; location
The Jackson-Bienville Parishes Watershed District is hereby created out of the watershed of Dugdemona Bayou located in Jackson and Bienville parishes.
Added by Acts 1970, No. 600, §1.
