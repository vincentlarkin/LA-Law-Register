§3087.342.  Location

The district shall be comprised of the area of Lake St. John and all of the land between the centerlines of Louisiana Highways 568 and 569 and Lake St. John located within the parish of Concordia.

Acts 2005, No. 214, §1, eff. June 29, 2005.
