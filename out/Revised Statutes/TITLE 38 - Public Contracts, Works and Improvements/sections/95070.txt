§2564.  Supervisory control over board
All the powers and duties conferred upon the board of commissioners herein shall be subject to the supervisory control of the department of public works of the state of Louisiana and to the wildlife and fisheries commission of the state of Louisiana or its successor, with jurisdiction by the latter over the fish, game and wildlife of the state of Louisiana and of the said Bayou D'Arbonne Lake Watershed District.
Added by Acts 1956, No. 9, §14.
