PART X.  JACKSON PARISH WATERSHED DISTRICT

§2900.  Creation; location

The Jackson Parish Watershed District is hereby created out of the watershed of all streams located in Jackson Parish, and more particularly defined as all of Jackson Parish, Louisiana.  

Added by Acts 1968, No. 589, §1.
