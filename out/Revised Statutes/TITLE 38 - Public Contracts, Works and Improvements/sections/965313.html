§2573. Bayou D'Arbonne Lake dam and spillway; name

            A. The dam and spillway at Bayou D'Arbonne Lake is hereby named the "T.T. Fields Dam and Spillway".

            B. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby directed to erect and maintain appropriate signage of this designation.

            Acts 2015, No. 33, §1.
