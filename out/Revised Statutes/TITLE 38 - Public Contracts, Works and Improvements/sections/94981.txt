§2259.  Contracts in violation of Act null and void
Any contract awarded or executed, or purchase made in violation of this Part shall be null and void and shall not be enforced in the courts of this state.
Added by Acts 1958, No. 318, §9.  Amended by Acts 1960, No. 502, §1.
