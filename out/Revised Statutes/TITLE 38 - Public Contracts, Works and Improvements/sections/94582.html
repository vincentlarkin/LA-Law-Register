PART IV.  BOND ISSUES

§1541.  Funding or refunding bonds

Any drainage district created or organized prior to the year 1921, or any sub-district of a drainage district, may, issue funding or refunding bonds, for the purpose of adjusting, readjusting, funding, refunding, extending, or unifying any or all of its authorized indebtedness, bonded or otherwise, or both, incurred in draining and reclaiming by means of levees and pumps any or all of the lands in the drainage district or sub-district.
