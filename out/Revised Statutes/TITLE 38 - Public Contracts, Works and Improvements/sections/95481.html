§3308.  Authority to issue bonds

The Amite River Basin Drainage and Water Conservation District shall have authority to issue bonds or other evidences of indebtedness in the manner provided by Part III and Part IV of Chapter 4 of this Title.  

Added by Acts 1981, No. 896, §1.  Acts 1984, No. 907, §1.
