§443.  Existing rights to issue bonds not limited
Nothing contained in this Subpart shall be construed as depriving the levee districts and levee and drainage districts of the right or power to issue their bonds in the manner and form already authorized if the boards of commissioners of the district so prefer.
Acts 1985, No. 785, §1, eff. July 22, 1985.
