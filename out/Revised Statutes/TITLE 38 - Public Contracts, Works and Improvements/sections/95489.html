§3325. Officers; vacancies; compensation

            A. The commissioners shall elect from their membership a chairman, a vice chairman, and a secretary-treasurer. The duties of the officers shall be fixed by the bylaws adopted by the board.

            B. Any vacancy which occurs in the membership of the board shall be filled in the same manner as required for the member being replaced.

            C. The commissioners shall serve without compensation, except per diem or expense reimbursement to which they may be individually entitled as members of their constituent organizations.

            D. All commissioners shall be subject to the Code of Governmental Ethics.

            Acts 2023, No. 323, §1.
