§204.  Advertisements to be in English; duplication in French permitted
A.  When advertisements are required to be made in relation to judicial process, or in the sale of property for unpaid taxes, or under judicial process or any other legal process of whatever kind, they shall be made in the English language and may in addition be duplicated in the French language.
B.  State and local officials and public institutions are reconfirmed in the traditional right to publish documents in the French language in addition to English.
Amended by Acts 1968, No. 256, §1.
