§33.1.  Mailing lists for printed matter; requirements

A.  Each branch, department, agency, official, employee, or other entity of state government shall maintain a separate mailing list for each publication printed by such branch, department, agency, official, employee, or entity.  Each mailing list shall be purged at least once every two years, and, except for mailings required by law, shall be replaced by a list of persons actively requesting receipt of that particular publication.

B.  This Section shall not apply to any printed publication for which federal funds have been provided.

Acts 1995, No. 1298, §1.
