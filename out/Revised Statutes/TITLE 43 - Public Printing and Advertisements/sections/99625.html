§85. Information in bids; price

            All bids for printing as provided for under the provisions of this Chapter shall contain the price per agate line, and shall name a daily newspaper possessing the qualifications enumerated in R.S. 43:81, as the medium of publication, but no bid shall be accepted and no contract shall be let which allows a price per agate line in excess of the maximum price per agate line permitted to be paid for advertisements in judicial proceedings or any other legal proceedings in the Parish of East Baton Rouge.

NOTE: §85 as amended and redesignated from R.S. 49:1305 by Acts 2025, No. 374, eff. July 1, 2027.

§85. Applicability

             For purposes of this Chapter, "boards and commissions" shall have the same meaning as provided in R.S. 42:1124.2.1(D)(1)(a)(i) and shall include any committee, subcommittee, or panel of any board or commission.
