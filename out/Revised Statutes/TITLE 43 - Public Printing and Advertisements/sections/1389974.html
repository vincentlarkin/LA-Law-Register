§147.3. Cost of publication

            The official journal of a political subdivision shall include a statement of the cost charged to the political subdivision for publication with any proceeding, notice, or other information that it publishes for the political subdivision pursuant to a legal requirement that the information be published. The official journal shall not charge the political subdivision for printing this statement of cost.

            Acts 2024, No. 464, §1.
