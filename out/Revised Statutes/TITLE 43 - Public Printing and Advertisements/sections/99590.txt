§20.  Bills and resolutions; contract provisions
Bills and resolutions of the Senate and House shall be printed in accordance with contracted specifications for such printing.  The secretary of the Senate and the clerk of the House of Representatives shall order the requisite number of copies needed by the Senate and House, respectively.
Amended by Acts 1980, No. 671, §1.
