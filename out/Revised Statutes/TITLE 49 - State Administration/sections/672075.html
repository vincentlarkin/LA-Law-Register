§214.5.7.  Legal representation of the board; attorney general

The attorney general or his designee shall be the legal advisor to the board, shall counsel and advise the board, and shall represent the board in all legal proceedings.

Acts 2009, No. 523, §3, eff. July 10, 2009; Acts 2012, No. 604, §3, eff. June 7, 2012.
