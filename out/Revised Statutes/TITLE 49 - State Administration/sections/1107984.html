SUBPART B-1. ATCHAFALAYA BASIN PROGRAM

§214.8.1. Purpose

            The provisions of this Subpart are intended to establish an Atchafalaya Basin Program within the Coastal Protection and Restoration Authority to serve as the authority on behalf of the state to work in partnership with the United States Army Corps of Engineers and other public entities, and coordinate state and local activities, in developing and implementing the federally sponsored and funded Atchafalaya Basin Floodway System, Louisiana Project.

            Acts 2018, No. 570, §3, eff. July 1, 2018.
