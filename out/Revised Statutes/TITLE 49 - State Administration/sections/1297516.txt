§979.  Appeals
An aggrieved party may obtain a review of any final judgment of the district court by
appeal to the appropriate circuit court of appeal.  The appeal shall be taken as in other civil
cases.
Acts 1966, No. 382, §15, eff. July 1, 1967; Acts 2022, No. 663, §1; Redesignated
from R.S. 49:965.
