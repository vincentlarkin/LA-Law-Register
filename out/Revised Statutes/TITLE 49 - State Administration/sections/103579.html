§190.1.  Termination dates of statutory entities

Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the date for the termination of a statutory entity shall be July first of an odd-numbered year.

Acts 1995, No. 712, §1; Acts 2004, No. 477, §1, eff. June 30, 2004.

NOTE:  See Acts 2004, No. 477, §2, relative to Act superseding conflicting provisions of R.S. 49:192 and 193.
