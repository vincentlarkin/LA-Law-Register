§1119.  Funding sources

The commission may accept funds and in-kind services from private sources at national, state, or local levels.  The commission may receive federal funds as may be available.  No state general fund monies or other funds appropriated to the commission shall be used to displace, replace, or supplant any federal funds appropriated to the commission in any prior fiscal year.  Nothing in this Section shall preclude the commission from receiving or accepting any in-kind services from a state agency.

Acts 1995, No. 742, §2, eff. June 27, 1995; Acts 2003, No. 219, §1.
