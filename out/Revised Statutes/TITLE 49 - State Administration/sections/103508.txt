PART VII.  PUBLIC BUILDINGS--ERECTION AND MAINTENANCE
SUBPART A.  ERECTION
§131.  Board created; members
There is created the Public Buildings Board, to consist of the Governor as chairman, and the Director of Public Works, the Commissioner of Administration, the Director of Highways and the Superintendent of State Buildings as members.
Amended by Acts 1964, No. 263, §1.
