§320.1.  Notification upon opening banking accounts
Any state depositing authority which deposits state funds in a bank designated as a
fiscal agent bank by the Interim Emergency Board shall provide written notice to the state
treasurer and the cash management review board upon opening any type of account at the
fiscal agent bank. The state treasurer shall annually provide a listing to the Joint Legislative
Committee on the Budget of all accounts reported under these provisions. The information
the state treasurer reports herein shall be in addition to the report required pursuant to the
provisions of R.S. 39:372(D).
Acts 1990, No. 664, §1, eff. July 1, 1990; Acts 2016, No. 587, §2, eff. June 17, 2016.
