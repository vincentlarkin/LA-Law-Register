§202.1.  Lieutenant governor; expenses

The lieutenant governor shall be entitled to reimbursement of his actual expenses for all travel out of state in connection with his official duties and shall be entitled to be reimbursed for actual expenses incurred within the state of Louisiana, including but not limited to travel and lodging expenses and for attendance at meetings, conferences, and appearances in connection with his official duties.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 676, §1, eff. May 10, 1976.  Amended by Acts 1977, No. 463, §1, eff. July 13, 1977; Acts 1981, No. 220, §1.
