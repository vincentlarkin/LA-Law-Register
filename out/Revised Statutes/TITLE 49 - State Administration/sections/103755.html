§652.  Appointment of collector; term; compensation

The collector of revenue shall be appointed by and shall serve at the pleasure of the governor.  He may be removed from office by the governor for any cause by him deemed sufficient.  His compensation shall be fixed by the governor.  

Added by Acts 1975, No. 156, §1.
