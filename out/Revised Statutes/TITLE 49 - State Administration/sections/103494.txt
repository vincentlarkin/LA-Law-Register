§1116.  Commission staff
The commission may employ an executive director, an administrative assistant, and other staff, consultants, attorneys, and other such persons the commission may deem necessary in carrying out its mandate.  The executive director and the administrative assistant shall be in the unclassified civil service.
Acts 1995, No. 742, §2, eff. June 27, 1995.
