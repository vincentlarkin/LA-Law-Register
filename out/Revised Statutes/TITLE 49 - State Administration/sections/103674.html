§226.  Overpayment of fees

The Secretary of State shall not be required to make refunds of overpayments of fees, except upon request, when such overpayments are for an amount less than two dollars.  

Added by Acts 1981, No. 189, §1.  Amended by Acts 1981, No. 508, §1.
