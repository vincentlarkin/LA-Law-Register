§200.1.  State agencies; requests
A.  In any civil proceeding, no state agency shall be obliged by subpoena to provide any document, record, or any other item for which a reasonable cost or fee has not been paid.
B.  For the purpose of this Section, a "state agency" is defined as any board, commission, department, division, agency, office, or other entity within the executive branch of state government.
Acts 1999, No. 1291, §1.
