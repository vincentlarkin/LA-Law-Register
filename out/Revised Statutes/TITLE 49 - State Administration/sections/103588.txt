§197.  Legislature may legislate otherwise
Nothing in this Part shall be construed to prohibit the legislature from terminating an entity covered by these provisions at a date earlier than that provided herein, nor to prohibit the legislature from considering any other legislation relative to such an entity.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 277, §8.
