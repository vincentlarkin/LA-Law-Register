§974.6.  Conflicts of law
Nothing in the Small Business Protection Act shall be construed to conflict with or
supersede any applicable federal law, rule, or regulation.
Acts 2008, No. 820, §1; Acts 2019, No. 204, §1, eff. Feb. 1, 2020; Acts 2022, No.
