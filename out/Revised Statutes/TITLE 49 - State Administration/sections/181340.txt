§149.27.  Bobby Fletcher Agricultural Center, Covington
The building situated in the city of Covington, having the municipal address of 1304 Columbia Street, and generally known as the St. Tammany Livestock Show Facility is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the Bobby Fletcher Agricultural Center.
Acts 2003, No. 329, §1.
