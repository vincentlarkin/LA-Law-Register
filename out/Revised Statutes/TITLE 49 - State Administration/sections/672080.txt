§214.6.4.  Functions and responsibilities; continuation of certain flood control projects
Nothing in this Chapter is intended to interfere with or impede the design, planning, construction, or financing of any flood protection project undertaken by any levee district within the coastal area which was initiated prior to January 1, 2006.
Acts 2009, No. 523, §3, eff. July 10, 2009.
