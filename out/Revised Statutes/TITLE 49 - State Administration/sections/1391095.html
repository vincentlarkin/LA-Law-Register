§1504. Permitted uses of digital assets

            A governing authority shall not prohibit, restrict, or otherwise impair the ability of an individual or business to do either of the following:

            (1) Accept digital assets as a method of payment for legal goods and services.

            (2) Self-custody digital assets using a self-hosted wallet or hardware wallet.

            Acts 2024, No. 700, §1.
