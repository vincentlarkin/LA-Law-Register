§214.39.  Effective date
This Subpart shall become effective on January 1, 1979, except that the coastal use permit program established pursuant to R.S. 49:214.30 shall not commence until thirty days after the adoption of guidelines pursuant to R.S. 49:214.27.
Acts 1978, No. 361, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1979.
