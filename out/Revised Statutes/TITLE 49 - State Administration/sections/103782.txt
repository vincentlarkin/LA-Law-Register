§858.  Disputes
Any dispute concerning a question of fact arising under this Chapter with respect to an office in the executive branch of the state government, except a dispute of fact relative to the office of governor shall be adjudicated by the governor, or other official authorized under the constitution or laws to exercise the powers and discharge the duties of the office of governor and his decision shall be final.
Acts 1963, No. 111, §8.
