PART XI.  SPECIAL DUTIES AND PROHIBITIONS

§185.  Governmental restriction of choice prohibited

Every person in this state needing eye care shall have complete freedom to use the services of an individual licensed under either R.S. 37:1041 through R.S. 37:1066 or R.S. 37:1261 through R.S. 37:1290.  No state, parish or municipal board or agency, nor any officer or employee thereof, shall either directly or indirectly limit or restrict the freedom of any person to choose the services of a person so licensed under either authority.

Added by Acts 1970, No. 170, §1.
