PART IX. LOUISIANA STATE POET LAUREATE
§171.  Poet laureate; creation
The position of Louisiana State Poet Laureate is hereby created within the office of the governor.
Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 55, §1.
NOTE:  See Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 55, §3, relative to continued service of past laureate in office.
