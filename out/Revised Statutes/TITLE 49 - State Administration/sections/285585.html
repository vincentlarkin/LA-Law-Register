§214.64.  Prayer of the petition; ex parte order of taking

The petition shall conclude with a prayer that the property be declared taken for public purposes and, upon presentation of the petition, the court shall issue an order declaring that the property described in the petition has been taken for public purposes.

Acts 2004, No. 633, §1, eff. July 5, 2004.
