§974.7.  Notification
Upon receiving the electronic transfer of information from the office of the state
register, as provided in R.S. 49:961, the commercial division of the Department of State shall
perform all of the following items:
(1)  By the fifteenth day of the month, the commercial division shall post information
on the internet page of the commercial division under the heading of "Proposed State Rules
and Regulations that May Affect Your Business".
(2)  No later than the sixteenth day of the month, the division shall electronically
transfer the information to the Louisiana Association of Business and Industry, the Louisiana
Chapter of the National Federation of Independent Business, and to each person who has
made a timely request of the department for this information.
