§152.  United States flag to be displayed
The flag of the United States shall wave from sunrise to sunset every day, over the State Capitol and the public departments and institutions of the state and over the court houses in the several parishes during the sessions of the courts.
Public officers shall enforce and execute the provisions of this Section.
Amended by Acts 1954, No. 449, §1.
