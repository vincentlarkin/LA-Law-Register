SUBPART C.  USE OF BUILDINGS
§145.  Use of public buildings; discrimination
No person shall be denied access to any public meeting in any public building or
facility used or owned by the state or any political subdivision of the state because of race,
color, creed, military status, or physical or mental disability.  For purposes of this Section,
a public meeting is a meeting which is advertised as being open to the general public.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 700, §1; Acts 1987, No. 277, §1; Acts 2025, No. 100, §3.
