CHAPTER 9.  DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE
§651.  Creation of department, powers, and duties of collector
There is hereby created the Department of Revenue, which shall be directed and controlled by a collector of revenue, who shall have and exercise all authority and power in relation to the collection of taxes that is prescribed by law.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 156, §1.
