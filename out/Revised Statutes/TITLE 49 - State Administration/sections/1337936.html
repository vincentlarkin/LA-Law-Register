§149.36. Governor P.B.S. Pinchback Building

            The state building situated in the city of Baton Rouge, having the municipal address of 1201 Capitol Access Road, and generally known as the Department of Transportation and Development headquarters building, is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the Governor P.B.S. Pinchback Building.

            Acts 2023, No. 95, §1.
