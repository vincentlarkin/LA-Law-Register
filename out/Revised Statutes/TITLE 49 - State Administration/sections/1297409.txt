§1140.  Inland water cleanup
In order to fulfill the obligations and responsibilities under this Chapter, the secretary
may promote and encourage inland water cleanups whereby a waterway cleanup may be
conducted annually by local groups to clean rivers, bayous, lakes, streams, and other
waterways encouraging beautification through removal of litter and debris.
Acts 2022, No. 16, §2.
