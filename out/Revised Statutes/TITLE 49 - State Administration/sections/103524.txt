SUBPART F.  NAMES OF BUILDINGS
§149.21.  Governor Sam Houston Jones Building, New Orleans
The state office building situated in the city of New Orleans and having the municipal address of 325 Loyola Avenue is hereby named, and shall hereafter be known as, the Governor Sam Houston Jones Building.
Added by Acts 1978, No. 182, §1.  Acts 1993, No. 153, §2.
