§149.29.  J.C. Willie Building

The state building situated in the city of Baton Rouge, having the municipal address of 9071 Interline Avenue and wherein the primary administrative offices of the Louisiana Real Estate Commission are located, is hereby named, and shall hereafter be known as, the J.C. Willie Building.

Acts 2008, No. 557, §1, eff. June 30, 2008.
