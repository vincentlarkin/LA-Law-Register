§214.69.  Effect of appellate proceedings
In any expropriation suit brought by the department pursuant to the authority set forth in this Part, no appellate proceedings or review shall operate to prevent or delay the vesting of title in the plaintiff.
Acts 2004, No. 633, §1, eff. July 5, 2004.
