§149.35.  J. Burton Angelle Building
The state building situated in the community of Grand Chenier, having the municipal
address of 5476 Grand Chenier Highway, and generally known as Rockefeller Wildlife
Refuge main office building, is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the J. Burton
Angelle Building.
Acts 2022, No. 418, §1, eff. June 15, 2022.
