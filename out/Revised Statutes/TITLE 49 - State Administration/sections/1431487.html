CHAPTER 23. STATE SERVICES AND BENEFITS LEGAL

STATUS AND ACCOUNTABILITY ACT

§1511. Short title

            This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "State Services and Benefits Legal Status and Accountability Act".

            Acts 2025, No. 419, §1, eff. June 20, 2025.
