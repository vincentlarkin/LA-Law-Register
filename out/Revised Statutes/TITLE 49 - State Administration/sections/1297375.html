§149.34. Carl W. Aron Building

            The state building situated in the city of Baton Rouge, having the municipal address of 3388 Brentwood Drive, and generally known as the Louisiana Board of Pharmacy building, is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the Carl W. Aron Building.

            Acts 2022, No. 409, §1, eff. June 15, 2022.
