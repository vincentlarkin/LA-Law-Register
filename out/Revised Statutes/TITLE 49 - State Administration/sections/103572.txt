§170.7.  State saltwater fish
There shall be an official state saltwater fish.  The official state saltwater fish shall be the spotted sea trout, or speckled trout (
Cynoscion nebulosus
).  Its use on the official documents of the state and with the insignia of the state is hereby authorized.
Acts 2001, No. 165, §1.
