§1505. Permitted uses of home digital asset mining; exceptions

            It shall be legal in this state to do both of the following:

            (1) Participate in home digital asset mining as long as the person engaging in home digital asset mining complies with all local noise ordinances.

            (2) Have a digital asset mining business in any area that is zoned for industrial use, provided that they comply with all current ordinances.

            Acts 2024, No. 700, §1.
