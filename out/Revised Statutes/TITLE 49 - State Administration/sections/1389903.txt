§24.  Jurisdiction of certain international organizations
The World Health Organization, United Nations, and the World Economic Forum
shall have no jurisdiction or power within the state of Louisiana.  No rule, regulation, fee,
tax, policy, or mandate of any kind of the World Health Organization, United Nations, and
the World Economic Forum shall be enforced or implemented by the state of Louisiana or
any agency, department, board, commission, political subdivision, governmental entity of the
state, parish, municipality, or any other political entity.
Acts 2024, No. 395, §1, eff. May 28, 2024.
