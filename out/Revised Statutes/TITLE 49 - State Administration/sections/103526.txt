§149.23.  Executive offices of the Department of Children and Family Services
Any floor of any state building upon which the executive offices of the Department of Children and Family Services are located shall be dedicated to the honor and memory of Mya George and all victims of child abuse.
Acts 1995, No. 727, §1; Acts 2006, No. 585, §1; Acts 2007, No. 154, §1, eff. June 25, 2007.
