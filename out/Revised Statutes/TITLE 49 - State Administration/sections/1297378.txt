§160.2.  State steam locomotive
There shall be an official state steam locomotive.  The official state steam locomotive
shall be the 2-8-2 Mikado class of steam locomotives.  Its use on official documents of the
state and with the insignia of the state is hereby authorized.
Acts 2022, No. 191, §1.
