§170.21.  Thoroughbred Breeding Capital of Louisiana; St. Tammany Parish
St. Tammany Parish is hereby designated as the "Thoroughbred Breeding Capital of
Louisiana".
Acts 2022, No. 245, §1, eff. May 31, 2022.
