§149.26.  Pike Hall, Jr., Courthouse, Shreveport
The state building situated in the city of Shreveport, having the municipal address of 430 Fannin Street and generally known as the Second Circuit Court of Appeal Building, is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the Pike Hall, Jr., Courthouse.
Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 72, §1.
