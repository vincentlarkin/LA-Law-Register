§228.  Direct computer access to certain public records; fee
The secretary of state is authorized to provide direct computer access to public records in the custody of the Department of State.  The department is authorized to assess a fee for such access pursuant to the provisions of R.S. 49:222.  Said fee shall not exceed the cost of providing the service.
Acts 1990, No. 278, §1.
