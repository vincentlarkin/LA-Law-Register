§1054.  Duties of the Louisiana Geographic Information Systems Council
The Louisiana Geographic Information Systems Council shall have the following
duties:
(1)  To advise the chief information officer, office of technology services, on the
activities and duties of and provide oversight to the Louisiana Geographic Information
Center (LAGIC), established to provide various GIS services to the state.
(2)  To make recommendations to the chief information officer on the establishment
of GIS policies, procedures, and guidelines for the sharing of data and technology among
federal, state, and local agencies.
(3)  To provide a forum for the coordination and cooperation of federal, state, and
local GIS efforts.
(4)  To advise state agencies on the acquisition and implementation of GIS and
related activities.
