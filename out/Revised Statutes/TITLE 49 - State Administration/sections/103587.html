§196.  Claims against or in favor of terminated entities

Nothing in this Part shall be construed to effect the termination or dismissal of any claim or right of a citizen against an entity terminated by its provisions, nor to effect the termination or dismissal of any claim or right of the entity.  Such claims and rights shall be assumed by the state of Louisiana.  

Added by Acts 1976, No. 277, §7.
