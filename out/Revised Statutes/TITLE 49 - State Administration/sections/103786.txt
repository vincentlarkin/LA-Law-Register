CHAPTER 13.  ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURE
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§950.  Title and form of citation
This Chapter shall be known as the Administrative Procedure Act and may be cited
as the Administrative Procedure Act.
Added by Acts 1982, No. 129, §1.
