§170.25.  State children's Christmas book
There shall be an official state children's Christmas book.  The official state children's
Christmas book shall be "Cajun Night Before Christmas", a literary composition written by
J.B. Kling, Jr. and illustrated by James Rice.
Acts 2025, No. 172, §1.
