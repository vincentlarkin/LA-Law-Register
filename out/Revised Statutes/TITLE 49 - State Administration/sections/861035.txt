§149.63.  Michael C. Voisin Hatchery
The bivalve hatchery situated on Grand Isle owned by the Department of Wildlife and Fisheries is hereby named, and shall hereafter be known as, the "Michael C. Voisin Hatchery".
Acts 2013, No. 2, §1.
