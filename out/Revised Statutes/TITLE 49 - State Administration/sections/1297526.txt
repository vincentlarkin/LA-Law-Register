§974.1.  Short title
R.S. 49:974.1 through 974.8 may be cited as the Small Business Protection Act and
are referred to therein as "this Act".
Acts 2008, No. 820, §1; Acts 2019, No. 204, §§1, 2, eff. Feb. 1, 2020; Acts 2022, No.
663, §1; Redesignated from R.S. 49:978.1.
