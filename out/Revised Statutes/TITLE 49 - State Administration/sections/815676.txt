§149.30.  Reverend Avery C. Alexander Academic Research Hospital
The new University Medical Center/Louisiana State University Hospital currently under construction in the city of New Orleans is hereby named, and shall hereafter be known as, the Reverend Avery C. Alexander Academic Research Hospital.
Acts 2012, No. 769, §1, eff. June 12, 2012.
