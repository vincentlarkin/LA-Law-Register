§149.31.  Senator Chris Ullo Building
The state building situated in the city of Harvey, having the municipal address of
2150 Westbank Expressway Trail, and generally known as the office of motor vehicles of
Harvey, is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the Senator Chris Ullo Building.
Acts 2015, No. 148, §1, eff. June 23, 2015.
