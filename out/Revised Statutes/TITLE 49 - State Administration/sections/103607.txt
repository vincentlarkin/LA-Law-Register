§211.  Commissions; formalities
All commissions shall be in the name and by the authority of the state of Louisiana and shall be sealed with the state seal, signed by the governor and countersigned by the secretary of state.
Added by Acts 1975, 1st Ex.Sess. No. 46, §1, eff. Feb. 20, 1975.
