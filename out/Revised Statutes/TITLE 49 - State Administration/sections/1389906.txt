§170.24.  Louisiana Vietnam Veterans Memorial
The Vietnam Veterans Memorial located at Veterans Memorial Park in the city of
Lake Charles is hereby designated as the "Louisiana Vietnam Veterans Memorial".
Acts 2024, No. 760, §1, eff. June 19, 2024.
