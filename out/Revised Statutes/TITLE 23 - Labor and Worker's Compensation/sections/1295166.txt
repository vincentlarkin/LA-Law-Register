§1310.5.1.  Continuances
If the parties, whether represented or unrepresented, agree to a continuance of a
mediation, hearing, or trial by filing a joint motion to continue or an uncontested motion to
continue, the workers' compensation judge shall grant the continuance.
Acts 2022, No. 435, §1.
