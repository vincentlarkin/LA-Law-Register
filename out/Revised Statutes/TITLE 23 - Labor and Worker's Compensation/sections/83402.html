§1173. Rules and regulations

            A. The secretary shall have the authority to promulgate rules and regulations to implement the provisions of R.S. 23:1170 and 1171.

            B. All such rules and regulations, including all fees otherwise authorized by this Part shall be subject to legislative oversight pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act.

            Acts 1988, No. 938, §2, eff. July 1, 1989; Acts 2008, No. 743, §7.
