§1410.  Fraud division

The corporation shall establish a fraud division to investigate and take any actions to remedy and prevent employer or employee fraud regarding workers' compensation.  The division shall provide an annual report to the board to be submitted with the report provided for in R.S. 23:1411(C).

Acts 1991, No. 814, §1, eff. Nov.  20, 1991; Acts 1992, No. 374, §1.
