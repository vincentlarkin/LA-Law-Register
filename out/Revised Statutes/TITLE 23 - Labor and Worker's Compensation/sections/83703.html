§1743.  Legal effect of assessments; when collectible

All assessments under this Subpart shall be tantamount to and the equivalent of judgments of courts.  The assessments are final when made subject only to modification by an appeal as provided in R.S. 23:1629 through 1634 or reassessment as provided in R.S. 23:1742.  Assessments are immediately collectible when made and any claimant may waive any delays and notices provided for in this Subpart.

Acts 1989, No. 350, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
