§1599.  Waiver of certified mail requirement
An employer, his duly authorized representative, or the claimant may waive the right
under this Chapter to receive written notices or determinations by certified mail.  The waiver
shall be in writing and shall be mailed or transmitted electronically to the office of
unemployment insurance administration within Louisiana Works.  If the right to receive
written notices and determinations by certified mail has been waived, written notices or
determinations may be transmitted by first class mail or by electronic delivery.  A notice or
determination is deemed delivered when it has been mailed or electronically transmitted.
Acts 2013, No. 39, §1.
