§904.  Agricultural pursuits exempt
R.S. 23:900 through R.S. 23:903 shall not apply to farming, agricultural pursuits, or the handling or primary processing of perishable raw agricultural commodities, or to those engaged therein.
Acts 1962, No. 365, §4.
