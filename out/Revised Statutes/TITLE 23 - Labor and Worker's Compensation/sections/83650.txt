PART VII.  ADMINISTRATION
§1651.  Office of unemployment insurance administration; creation
There is created the office of unemployment insurance administration, which shall be administered in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 36:304(A)(6).
Amended by Acts 1968, No. 42, §3; Acts 1992, No. 447, §1, eff. June 20, 1992; Acts 1997, No. 1172, §4, eff. June 30, 1997; Acts 2007, No. 113, §1.
