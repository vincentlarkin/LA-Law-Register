§2206.  Report

A board shall periodically provide a report summarizing by occupation the labor demand to the following:

(1)  Each public postsecondary institution providing vocational, technical, and professional education.

(2)  Each entity under contract to the board to provide workforce training and services in a workforce development area.

Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
