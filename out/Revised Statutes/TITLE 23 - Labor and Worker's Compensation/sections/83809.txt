§2055.  Rulemaking authority
The council, in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, shall promulgate such rules and regulations as necessary to carry out the provisions of this Chapter.
Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
