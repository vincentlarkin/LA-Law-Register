SUBPART C.  EMPLOYMENT CERTIFICATES
§181.  Secretary to furnish forms
The secretary shall prescribe and furnish all forms to be used in
connection with the issuance of employment certificates by each issuing
authority.
Acts 2008, No. 743, §7.
