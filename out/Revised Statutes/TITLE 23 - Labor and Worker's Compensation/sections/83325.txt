§1015.3.  Rulemaking authority
The secretary of Louisiana Works may adopt, amend, suspend, repeal, and enforce
rules and regulations to carry out the provisions and purposes of this Part.
Acts 1993, No. 790, §1; Acts 2008, No. 743, §7, eff. July 1, 2008.
