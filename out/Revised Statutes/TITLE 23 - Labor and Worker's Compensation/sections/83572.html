§153. Regulations

            The secretary shall have power to regulate and supervise the administration of minor labor laws and to make, repeal, prescribe, and enforce orders, rules, and regulations to effectuate the provisions and purpose of the Chapter.

            Acts 1993, No. 621, §1, eff. June 15, 1993; Acts 2018, No. 380, §1, eff. June 30, 2018.
