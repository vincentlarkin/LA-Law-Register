§3002. State treasurer as custodian of federal funds

            The state treasurer is designated and appointed custodian of all monies received by the state of Louisiana from appropriations made by the Congress of the United States for the vocational rehabilitation of persons disabled in industry or otherwise, and is authorized to receive and provide for the proper custody of the same and to make disbursement therefrom upon the approval of the legislature and the requisition of Louisiana Works.

            Acts 2010, No. 939, §2, eff. July 1, 2010.
