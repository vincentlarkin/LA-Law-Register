§20.  State and local planning process; local workforce development boards
The secretary shall design and implement a state and local planning process for
workforce training and services, including the certification of business/career solution centers
and the chartering of local workforce development boards, provided through the programs
under the jurisdiction of the office of workforce development.
Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2015, No. 426, §2.
