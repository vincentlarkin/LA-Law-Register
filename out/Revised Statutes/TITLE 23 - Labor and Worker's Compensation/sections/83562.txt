§15.  Posting of labor laws
Every employer shall keep conspicuously posted in or about the premises wherein any
worker is employed, a printed copy or abstract of those labor laws which the secretary or may
designate, in a form to be furnished by the secretary.
Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
