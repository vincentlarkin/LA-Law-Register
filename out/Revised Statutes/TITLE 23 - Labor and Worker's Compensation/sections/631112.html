§19. Delegation of functions

            The secretary shall, to the extent allowed under state or federal law, delegate all or part of the administration of a program integrated pursuant to R.S. 23:17 that is eligible for funding to a local workforce development board in an area in which a board has been certified and a local plan approved by the governor, or to another appropriate state or local entity in an area in which a local workforce development board has not been certified and a local plan approved by the governor. Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2015, No. 426, §2.
