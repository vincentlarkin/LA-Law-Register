§1612.  Effect of other provisions of this Chapter relating to regular benefits on claims for, and the payment of, extended benefits

Except when the result would be inconsistent with the provisions of R.S. 23:1611 through R.S. 23:1616 of this Chapter, as provided in the regulations of the Administrator, the provisions of this Chapter which apply to claims for, or the payment of, regular benefits shall apply to claims for and the payment of, extended benefits.

Added by Acts 1971, No. 136, §15, eff. Jan. 1, 1972.
