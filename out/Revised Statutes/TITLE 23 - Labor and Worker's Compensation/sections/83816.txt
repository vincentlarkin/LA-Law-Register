§2066.  Job placement information; occupational information
A.  The council shall establish, supervise, and control a comprehensive labor market information system as specifically provided for in Part IV of Chapter 1 of this Title.
B.  The council shall use the information developed as provided in this Section and other information to determine whether a specific workforce development program is effective and whether to recommend to the governor to continue or discontinue the program.
Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
