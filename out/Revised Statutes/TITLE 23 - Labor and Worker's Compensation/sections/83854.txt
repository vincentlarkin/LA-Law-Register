§255.  Bond as prerequisite to issuance of permit
The secretary may exact from the applicant, under such conditions and
stipulations as he may determine, and as a condition precedent to the issuance
of the permit, a bond not exceeding two thousand dollars, executed in favor of
the state, conditioned to secure and guarantee the proper tuition and the moral
and physical health of the minor while in such employment.  This bond may
be forfeited upon a breach of the conditions thereof in the State of Louisiana
or elsewhere, and in any proceedings for such forfeiture testimony may be
taken as provided by law in civil matters.
Acts 1989, No. 731, §2, eff. July 8, 1989; Acts 2008, No. 743, §7.
