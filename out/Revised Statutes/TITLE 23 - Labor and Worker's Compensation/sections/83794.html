§2024.  Findings and recommendations; termination of commission

A.  The commission shall make a final report of its findings and recommendations on or before February 1, 2006, to the House and Senate Committees on Labor and Industrial Relations.

B.  The authority and operation provided by this Chapter shall expire on July 1, 2006.

Acts 2004, No. 795, §1.
