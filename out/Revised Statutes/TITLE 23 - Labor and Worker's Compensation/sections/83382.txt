§115.  Enforcement of this Part through court action
The
assistant
secretary may institute any action in the courts necessary to
enforce compliance with any provisions of this Part or with any rule, subpoena, or
order of the
assistant
secretary made pursuant to the provisions of this Part.  In
addition to any other remedy, the
assistant
secretary may apply to the appropriate
district court for injunctive relief.
Acts 1981, No. 732, §1.
