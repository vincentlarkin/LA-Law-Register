§1712.  Violations of provisions, regulations or orders when penalty not otherwise prescribed

Whoever knowingly violates any provision of this Chapter, or any order, rule, or regulation thereunder and for which a penalty is neither prescribed in this Chapter nor provided by any other applicable statute, shall be fined not less than twenty dollars nor more than two hundred dollars, or imprisoned for not less than ten days nor more than sixty days, or both.  Each day such violation continues shall constitute a separate offense.
