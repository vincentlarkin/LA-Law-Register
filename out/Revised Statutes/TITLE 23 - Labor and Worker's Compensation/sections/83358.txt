§109.  Revocation of license; effect
A.  Upon the revocation of any license, the
assistant
secretary shall take
possession of the same and cancel it.
B.  No license shall be issued to a person whose license has been revoked,
until the expiration of the time for which the revocation was made.
Acts 1981, No. 732, §1; Acts 1987, No. 791, §1.
