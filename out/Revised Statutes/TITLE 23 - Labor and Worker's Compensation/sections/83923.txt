§5.  Right of entry
The secretary and his authorized representatives may enter any place of employment
at any reasonable time for the purpose of collecting facts and statistics relating to the
employment of workers, and of making inspections for the proper enforcement of all labor
laws.  No employer or owner shall refuse to admit the secretary or his authorized
representatives to any place of employment.
Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
