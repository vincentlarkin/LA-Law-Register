§2197. Removal of workforce development board members

            The chief elected official with the authority to appoint board members may remove any appointed member from the board for cause, including misconduct, incompetence, neglect of duty, or absence from more than half of the regularly scheduled board meetings in any calendar year.

            Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2015, No. 426, §2.
