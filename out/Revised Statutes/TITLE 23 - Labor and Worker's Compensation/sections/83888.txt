§354.  Notices to be posted
Every employer, employment agency, and labor organization shall post and keep
posted in conspicuous places upon its premises a notice, to be prepared by Louisiana Works,
setting forth information as the department deems appropriate to effectuate the purposes of
this Part.
Acts 1997, No. 1409, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §7, eff. July 1, 2008.
