§1168.5.  Deposit of monies collected
The monies collected by the
assistant
secretary
shall be deposited in one or more state
or national banks, savings banks, trust companies, or savings and loan associations chartered
to do business in this state.  Any depository so selected shall provide the same security for
such deposits as that required for the deposit of state funds pursuant to the provisions of R.S.
49:321.  The
assistant
secretary
r may in his discretion deposit such monies or any part
thereof in a national bank or trust company as a trust fund.  For the purposes of this Section,
the
assistant
secretary
shall be considered a "depositing authority" pursuant to R.S. 49:321.
Acts 2006, No. 49, §1, eff. May 16, 2006.
