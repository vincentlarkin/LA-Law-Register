PART X.  SCHOOL AND DAY CARE CONFERENCE
AND ACTIVITIES LEAVE
§1015.  Short title; citation
This Part shall constitute and be known as the "Louisiana School and Day Care Conference and Activities Leave Act" and may be cited as such.
Acts 1993, No. 790, §1.
