§42. Membership of board

            This board shall consist of four members, one of whom shall be the chief inspector of the boiler inspection division, Louisiana Works, and the other three shall be special inspectors holding commissions as inspectors of steam boilers issued by the Louisiana Works.

            Acts 1989, No. 512, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990; Acts 1992, No. 447, §1, eff. June 20, 1992; Acts 2008, No. 743, §7, eff. July 1, 2008.
