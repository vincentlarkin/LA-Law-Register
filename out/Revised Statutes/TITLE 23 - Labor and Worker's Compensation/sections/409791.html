§1168.6. Appointment of assistants

            A. The assistant secretary shall have the power to contract with or employ such clerks or assistants as may by him be deemed necessary and to give each such powers to assist him as he may consider wise.

            B. The assistant secretary may contract with the department of insurance for the administration of claims.

            C.(1) The attorney general shall provide representation for the assistant secretary in all matters involving the seizure and distribution of the security.

            (2) Attorneys employed by the attorney general for purposes of this Section shall be named by the assistant secretary with the approval of the attorney general, and shall perform their duties under the supervision of the attorney general.

            Acts 2006, No. 49, §1, eff. May 16, 2006.
