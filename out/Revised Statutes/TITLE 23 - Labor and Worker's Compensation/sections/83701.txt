§1741.  Assessment
At the expiration of the delay periods as provided in R.S. 23:1629 through 1634, or
at the expiration of such additional time as may be necessary to consider any application for
waiver of overpayment as provided in R.S. 23:1713(B), the administrator shall proceed to
assess an overpayment that he determines to be due pursuant to this Chapter.  This
assessment shall be evidenced by a writing in any form suitable to the administrator which
states the name of the claimant and the amount determined to be due.  This writing shall be
retained as a part of the administrator's official records.
Acts 1989, No. 350, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990; Acts 2018, No. 314, §1.
