§1222.  Probable duration of disability not basis for award
Supplemental earnings benefits shall not be awarded or payable for probable duration of loss of wages.  However, this Section does not prohibit the parties from entering into a compromise or lump sum settlement, in accordance with the Workers' Compensation Law.
Amended by Acts 1983, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 1, §1, eff. July 1, 1983; Acts 1988, No. 938, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1989; Acts 1989, No. 260, §1, eff. June 26, 1989.
