§1625.  Notice of determination on claims

If a disqualification is alleged, or appears to exist, notice of the determination together with the reasons therefor shall be promptly delivered to the claimant and to the employer from whose employment the disqualification issue arose or mailed to their last known address.

Amended by Acts 1952, No. 400, §1; Acts 1968, No. 350, §2.
