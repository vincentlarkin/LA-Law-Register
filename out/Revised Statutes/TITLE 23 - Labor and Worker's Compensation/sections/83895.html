§383. Director of apprenticeship

            The secretary or his designee shall appoint a director of apprenticeship. The secretary is further authorized to appoint and employ such clerical, technical, and professional help as shall be necessary to effectuate the purposes of this Chapter.

            Acts 2010, No. 791, §1.
