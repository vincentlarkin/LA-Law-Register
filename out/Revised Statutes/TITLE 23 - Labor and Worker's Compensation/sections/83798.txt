§2044.  Qualifications
Each member of the council shall be a registered voter in and a domiciliary of
Louisiana and shall have completed the same training as provided to local workforce
development board members pursuant to R.S. 23:2194.
Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008;
Acts 2015, No. 426, §2.
