§113.  Violations of provisions; penalty

A person who acts as an employment service without a license as provided in this Part shall be punished by a fine of not less than fifty nor more than one thousand dollars per violation, by imprisonment for not more than six months, or both.

Acts 1981, No. 732, §1; Acts 2010, No. 307, §1.
