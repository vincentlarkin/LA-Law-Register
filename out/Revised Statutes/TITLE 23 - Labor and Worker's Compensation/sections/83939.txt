§545.  Penalties
No boiler in this state, the city of New Orleans excepted, shall be installed, moved, or reinstalled without a permit issued to a licensed person by a chief boiler inspector.  Whoever fails to comply with the provisions of R.S. 23:543 and 544 shall be fined not more than one thousand dollars or imprisoned for not more than one year, or both.  Each violation hereunder shall constitute a separate offense.
Acts 1988, No. 382, §1.
