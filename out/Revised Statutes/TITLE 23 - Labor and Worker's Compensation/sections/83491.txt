§1310.14.  Securing information
Every employer shall furnish the
assistant
secretary
, upon request, any
information required by him to carry out the provisions of the Worker's
Compensation Act.
Acts 1988, No. 938, §2, eff. July 1, 1989; Acts 1989, No. 260, §1, eff. Jan.
1, 1990.
