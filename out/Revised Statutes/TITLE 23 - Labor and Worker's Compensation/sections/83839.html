§2152.  Provision of services by state agencies

A.  An affected agency shall provide workforce development services in accordance with the council's plans and recommendations and shall implement rules and policies consistent with the plan or recommendation.

B.  Affected agencies are prohibited from adopting any official plans or proposals without obtaining approval from the council prior to such adoption.

Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
