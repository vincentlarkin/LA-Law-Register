PART X.  ALTERNATIVE COLLECTION PROCEDURES

AND ASSESSMENTS

SUBPART A.  OUTSTANDING CONTRIBUTIONS

§1721.  Alternative remedies for the collection of contributions

In addition to any of the remedies provided in the various sections of this Chapter, the administrator may, within his discretion, proceed to enforce the collection of any contributions or other payments due under this chapter by means of assessment and executory procedure as set forth in this part.

Added by Acts 1972, No. 336, §1.  Acts 1989, No. 350, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
