§1657.1.  Criminal history information; access to federal tax information
Louisiana Works, office of unemployment insurance administration, is authorized to
perform criminal history records checks of current and prospective employees, contractors,
and subcontractors in accordance with the procedures provided in R.S. 15:587.5.  Pursuant
to this authorization and to implement the requirements of R.S. 15:587.5, the secretary shall
promulgate rules and regulations with regard to this matter.
Acts 2017, No. 147, §2, eff. June 12, 2017.
