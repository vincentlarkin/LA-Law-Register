§192.  Certificates as evidence of age of minors

Employment certificates issued in accordance with the provisions of this Subpart shall be conclusive evidence of the age of the minor for whom issued in any proceeding involving the employment of the minor subsequent to the issuance thereof.

Acts 2011, No. 177, §1.
