§105.  License fee; renewal
Effective January 1, 1993, no license shall be granted until the license
applicant has paid the
assistant
secretary a license fee of two hundred dollars per year
for each location.  Upon initial license application, the applicant shall pay a three
hundred dollar investigation fee and an examination fee of one hundred dollars.  An
active licensee shall not be subject to such fees when opening new offices.
Acts 1992, No. 633, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
