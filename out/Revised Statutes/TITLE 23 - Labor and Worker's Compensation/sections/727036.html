PART IV. INDEPENDENT LIVING

§3061. Louisiana Works of Federal Independent Living Program

            Louisiana Works shall administer Rehabilitation Act independent living programs, including independent living, Part B, and independent living for older individuals who are blind.

            Acts 2010, No. 939, §2, eff. July 1, 2010.
