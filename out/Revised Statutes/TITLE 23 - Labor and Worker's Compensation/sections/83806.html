§2052.  Compensation

No council member shall receive compensation for his services.  However, members may be reimbursed expenses incurred for necessary travel when attending to official business of the council as provided for state employees by division of administration regulations.

Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
