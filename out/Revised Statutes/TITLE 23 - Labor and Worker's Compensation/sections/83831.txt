§2100.  Federal support and waivers
The standing committees, through the council, shall assist the department in seeking
federal support and waivers, if necessary for implementing the provisions of this Part.
Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
