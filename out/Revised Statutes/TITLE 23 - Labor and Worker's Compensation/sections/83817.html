§2067.  Integration of job training programs

Annually the council shall prepare and submit to the governor and the legislature a report that describes how additional job training programs not under the jurisdiction of a standing committee created pursuant to Part III of this Chapter can be consolidated into a more integrated and accountable workforce development system that better meets the needs of employers, employees, and job seekers.

Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
