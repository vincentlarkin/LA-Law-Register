§1168.12.  Cooperation of officers, owners, and employees; civil penalties
A.  Any officer, manager, director, trustee, owner, employee, or agent of any self-insured employer, or any other persons with authority over or in charge of any segment of
the employer's affairs, shall cooperate with the
assistant
secretary
in the seizure of the
security and administration of claims.
B.  No person shall provide to the
assistant
secretary
statements, documents, or
records which are fraudulent or materially false.
C.  The Nineteenth Judicial District Court shall have jurisdiction to issue such orders,
including injunctive relief, as appropriate, for the enforcement of this Section.
D.  Failure to comply with such court order shall subject the violator to a fine not to
exceed one hundred thousand dollars.
Acts 2006, No. 49, §1, eff. May 16, 2006.
