§1731.  Injunction to arrest execution of assessment made executory
The execution of an assessment made executory under this part may be arrested by injunction only if the judgment is extinguished or otherwise legally unenforceable.  No temporary restraining order or a preliminary writ of injunction may be issued, however, unless the applicant therefor furnishes security in an amount of one and one-half times the amount of the assessment including contributions, interest and penalty.
Added by Acts 1972, No. 336, §1.
