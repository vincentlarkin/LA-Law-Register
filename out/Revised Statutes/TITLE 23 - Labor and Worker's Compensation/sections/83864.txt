CHAPTER 3-A.  PROHIBITED DISCRIMINATION
IN EMPLOYMENT
PART I.  GENERAL PROVISIONS
§301.  Short title
This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Louisiana Employment Discrimination Law".
Acts 1997, No. 1409, §1, eff. Aug. 1, 1997.
