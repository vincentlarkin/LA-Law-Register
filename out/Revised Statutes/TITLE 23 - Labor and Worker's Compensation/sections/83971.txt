§74.  Customer information
For the customer information component of the system, there shall be a user-friendly
formatted inventory of available training opportunities and, to the extent possible,
employment opportunities.  This component of the system shall be available for access on
the internet.
Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008;
Acts 2025, No. 478, §7, eff. Oct. 1, 2025.
