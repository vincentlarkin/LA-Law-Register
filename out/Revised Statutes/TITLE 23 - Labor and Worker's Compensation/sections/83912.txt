§47.  Revocation of certificate and commission
Any certificate of competency and commission as a boiler inspector may be
revoked by the secretary for misconduct, or untrustworthiness on the part of the
holder thereof, or for wilful falsification of any matter or statement contained in his
application, or in any report prepared by him; but the holder of the certificate of
competency and commission shall first be entitled to a hearing before the secretary
to show cause why such certificate and commission should not be revoked.
