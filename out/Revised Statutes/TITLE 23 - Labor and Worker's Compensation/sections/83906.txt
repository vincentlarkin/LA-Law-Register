PART II.  STATE BOARD OF BOILER
INSPECTOR EXAMINERS
§41.  Louisiana state board of boiler inspector examiners
There is created and established as part of Louisiana Works, the Louisiana State
Board of Boiler Inspector Examiners.
Acts 1989, No. 512, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990; Acts 1992, No. 447, §1, eff. June 20, 1992;
Acts 2008, No. 743, §7, eff. July 1, 2008.
