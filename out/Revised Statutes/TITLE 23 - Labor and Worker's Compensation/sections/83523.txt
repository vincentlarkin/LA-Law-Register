§1375.  Personnel
A.  The board shall appoint, fix the compensation and prescribe the duties of an
executive director, who shall be in the unclassified service.  The executive director shall
devote full time to his duties and may not accept or engage in additional employment of any
kind.
B.  The executive director shall employ and supervise all such personnel, who shall
be in the classified service, necessary for the operation of the business of the board.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 165, §1; Acts 2006, No. 453, §1, eff. June 15, 2006.
