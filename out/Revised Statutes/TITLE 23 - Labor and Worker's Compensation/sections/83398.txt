§1171.2.  Default of employer; additional liability
The amount of weekly compensation provided in this Chapter shall be
increased by fifty percent in any case where the employer has failed to provide
security for compensation as required by R.S. 23:1168.
Acts 1995, No. 368, §1, eff. June 16, 1995.
