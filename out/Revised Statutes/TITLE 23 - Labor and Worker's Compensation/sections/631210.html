§2203.  Components of local workforce development system

The local workforce development system shall be comprised of two major components, as follows:

(1)  Business solutions using a strategic approach to develop and maintain relationships and partnerships with the business community, and ensuring services and service delivery methods meet business demands, including improvement of existing services and development of new business services.

(2)  Career solutions using funding and staff integration to advance efficiency and maximize the resources of the business/career solution center processes, moving from a focus and delineation on programs to well-structured, customer-appropriate sets of services.

Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008.
