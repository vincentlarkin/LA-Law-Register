§1046.  Chapter inapplicable to uncompensated officers and uncompensated members of the board of directors of certain nonprofit organizations
The provisions of this Chapter are inapplicable to uncompensated officers and uncompensated members of the board of directors of bona fide, nonprofit veterans and other bona fide, nonprofit organizations which are charitable, educational, religious, social, civic or fraternal in nature including, but not limited to, the Young Men's Christian Association, the Young Women's Christian Association and all scouting associations of the United States.
Added by Acts 1976, No. 295, §1.
