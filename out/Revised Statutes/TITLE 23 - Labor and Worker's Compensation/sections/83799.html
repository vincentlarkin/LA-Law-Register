§2045.  Chairman and vice chairman

The governor shall appoint the chairman of the council from members representing business and industry appointed pursuant to R.S. 23:2043(A)(11).  The council shall elect the vice chairman.  The chairman and vice chairman shall serve one-year terms, beginning July first and ending June thirtieth of the following calendar year.

Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2012, No. 21, §1.
