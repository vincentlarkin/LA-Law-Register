§2056. Executive organization

            For the purposes of executive branch organization, the council is placed within the executive office of the secretary, pursuant to R.S. 36:301(C)(1).

            Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008; Acts 2008, No. 831, §6, eff. July 1, 2008.
