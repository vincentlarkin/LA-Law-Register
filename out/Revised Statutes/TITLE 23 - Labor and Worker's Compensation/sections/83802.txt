§2048.  Removal of members
The governor may remove any appointed member of the council for cause including
misconduct, incompetency, neglect of duty, or absence from any two out of four consecutive
meetings.
Acts 1997, No. 1, §1, eff. April 30, 1997; Acts 2008, No. 743, §2, eff. July 1, 2008;
Acts 2015, No. 426, §2.
