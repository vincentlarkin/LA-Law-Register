§1540.  Appeal of liability or tax rate determination
An employer may apply for review of any liability determination and any tax rate resulting from that determination in accordance with the time delays and procedures provided in R.S. 23:1541(E).
Acts 1989, No. 351, §1, eff. June 28, 1989; Acts 2014, No. 529, §1.
