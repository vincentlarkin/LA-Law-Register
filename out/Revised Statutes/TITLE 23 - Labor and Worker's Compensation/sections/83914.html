CHAPTER 5.  HEALTH AND SAFETY

PART I.  DIVERS, TUNNEL AND CAISSON WORKERS

§481.  Facilities for protection; duty to provide

All employers of divers, tunnel and caisson workers who work in compressed air, shall provide ample facilities to protect the health of such employees.
