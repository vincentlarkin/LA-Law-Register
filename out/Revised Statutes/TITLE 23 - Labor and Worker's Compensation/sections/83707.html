§1747.  Injunction to arrest execution of assessment made executory

The execution of an assessment made executory under this Subpart may be arrested by injunction only if the judgment is extinguished or otherwise legally unenforceable.

Acts 1989, No. 350, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
