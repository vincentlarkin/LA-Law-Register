§1749.8.  Reissuance of license
The licensing authority shall issue, reissue, renew, or otherwise extend an obligor's license, in accordance with any applicable reinstatement fees or applicable rules, upon receipt of a certified copy of a compliance release from the commission.
Acts 2012, No. 263, §1.
