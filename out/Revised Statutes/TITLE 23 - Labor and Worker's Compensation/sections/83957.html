§641.  Liability of publishers; employing agents' failure to pay wages or commissions of door to door solicitors

Upon proof that any distributor, wholesaler or local agent has failed in an obligation to tender payment in the form of wages or commissions to any door to door solicitor engaged for the purpose of selling magazines or periodicals or selling subscriptions to magazines or periodicals, such obligation shall become the responsibility of the publisher of the periodical or magazine being sold, jointly and in solido with that of his agent.

Added by Acts 1966, No. 541, §1.
