§1372.  Louisiana Workers' Compensation Second Injury Board; creation, domicile,
membership
The Louisiana Workers' Compensation Second Injury Board, hereinafter referred to
as the board, is created.  The board, which shall be domiciled in Baton Rouge, Louisiana,
shall be composed of five members or their designee, who shall be the secretary of state, the
state treasurer, the commissioner of insurance, the secretary of the Department of Children
and Family Services, and the assistant secretary of the office of workers' compensation
administration.
Acts 1988, No. 997, §1; Acts 2006, No. 453, §1, eff. June 15, 2006.
