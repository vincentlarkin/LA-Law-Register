§1174.  Certificate of compliance

Whenever a certificate of compliance is properly applied for with the office of worker's compensation, that office shall respond by issuing a certificate of authority without a charge or by rejecting the application within ten days of receipt of the application.

Acts 1992, No. 765, §2, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
