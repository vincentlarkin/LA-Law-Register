§188.  Records kept by issuing authority
A copy of each employment certificate shall be retained in the office of the issuing authority together with the papers required to be submitted by the applicants under R.S. 23:184.  The issuing authority shall also keep a record of all applications denied.
Acts 2003, No. 671, §1, eff. June 27, 2003.
