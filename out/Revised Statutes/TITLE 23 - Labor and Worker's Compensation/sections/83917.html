§484.  Shifts; maximum pressure

The working time in any twenty-four hours shall be divided into two shifts under compressed air with an interval in open air.  Persons who have not previously worked in compressed air shall work therein but one shift during the first twenty-four hours.  No person shall be subjected to pressure exceeding fifty pounds except in emergency.
