§546.  Disposition of fees

All fees collected pursuant to R.S. 23:537, 541, 543, and 544  shall be used exclusively for the operation and maintenance of the boiler section within the office of the state fire marshal, code enforcement and building safety.

Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 90, §1.
