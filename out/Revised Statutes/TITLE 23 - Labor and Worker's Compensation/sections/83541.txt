§1404.1.  State reporting
Upon its extinguishment, the full faith and credit guarantee shall no longer operate as a state liability, contingent or otherwise, for the state's accounting, budgeting, financial, or reporting purposes.
Acts 1999, No. 1256, §1, eff. July 12, 1999.
