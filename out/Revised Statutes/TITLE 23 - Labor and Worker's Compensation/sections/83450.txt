§1224.  Payments not recoverable for first week; exceptions
No compensation shall be paid for the first week after the injury is received; provided, that in cases where disability from injury continues for two weeks or longer after date of the accident, compensation for the first week shall be paid after the first two weeks have elapsed.
Acts 2012, No. 860, §1.
