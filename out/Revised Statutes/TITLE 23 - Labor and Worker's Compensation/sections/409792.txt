§1168.7.  Priority of claims
The priorities of distribution of the security shall be as follows:
(1)  Claims by injured employees of the self-insured employer.
(2)  The
assistant
secretary
's costs and expenses of administration and any claims
handling expenses.
(3)  All other claims.
Acts 2006, No. 49, §1, eff. May 16, 2006.
