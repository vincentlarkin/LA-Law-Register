§1161.2.  Domicile; quorum; officers
A.  The commission shall be domiciled in Baton Rouge, but may meet, conduct investigations, and render orders elsewhere in the state.
B.  Three commissioners shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business at any regular or special meeting.  All official action of the commission shall require the favorable vote of a majority of the elected membership.
C.  At its first regular or special meeting of each calendar year the commission shall elect a chairman and a vice chairman from among its membership.
Added by Acts 1975, No. 4, §1, eff. June 13, 1975.
