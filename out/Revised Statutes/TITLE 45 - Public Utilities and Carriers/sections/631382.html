§1371.  Customer service standards

Any holder of a state-issued certificate of franchise authority shall comply with 47 CFR 76.309(c).  No franchising authority or local governmental subdivision shall have the power to require a holder of a state-issued certificate of franchise authority to comply with any customer service standards other than those set forth in this Section.

Acts 2008, No. 433, §1.
