CHAPTER 12.  RADIO COMMON CARRIERS
§1500.  Regulation of radio common carriers
The Louisiana Public Service Commission shall exercise over and in relation to radio common carriers the powers conferred by this Chapter.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 634, §1.
