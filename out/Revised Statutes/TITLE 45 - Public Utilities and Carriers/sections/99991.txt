§309.  Inspections
In furtherance of the purpose set forth in R.S. 45:308(A), the commission shall have the authority to promulgate rules and regulations providing for (1) the inspection by the permanent staff of the commission of pipeline facilities and the practices of public utilities in the transportation of gas, and, (2) record maintenance, reporting and inspection of pipeline facilities by public utilities.
Added by Acts 1968, No. 81, §1.
