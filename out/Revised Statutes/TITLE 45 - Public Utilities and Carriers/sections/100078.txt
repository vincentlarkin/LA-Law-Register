PART III.  PERSONNEL
§711.  Motormen and conductors; instruction; qualification in certain cities
No person shall act as motorman or conductor on any electric railway unless he shall have first received at least ten days instruction under a competent instructor or instructors employed by the company in whose services he intends to enter.
In cities of less than twenty-five thousand, the experience and qualification of conductors and motormen shall not be less than five days.
