§1100.  Prescription on actions against carriers for freight overcharge and damage to shipments
All actions by or against common carriers for the collection or recovery of erroneous freight charges, and all actions for loss of or damage to shipments of freight, shall be prescribed by the lapse of two years, from the date of shipment.
