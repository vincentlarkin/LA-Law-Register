§5.  Amount of bond
If the person engaged in the business of carrying persons for hire by airplane uses in the business only one airplane which is used or intended to be used for the purpose of carrying passengers for hire, then the amount of the bond shall be fifteen thousand dollars, and for every additional airplane that such person may so use, the amount of the bond shall be increased by one thousand dollars.
