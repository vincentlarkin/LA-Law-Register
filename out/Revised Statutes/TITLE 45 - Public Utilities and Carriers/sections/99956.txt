§200.12.  Name of owner and number of certificate to be painted on vehicle
The full name of the owner of each public carrier vehicle operated as a taxicab, and the number of the vehicle's certificate, shall be painted conspicuously on each side of the vehicle in letters and figures at least two inches high.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 404, §1.
