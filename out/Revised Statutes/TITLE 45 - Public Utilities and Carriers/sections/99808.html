§1162.  Salaries of members

The salary of each of the members of the Louisiana Public Service Commission shall be forty-five thousand dollars per annum, payable monthly on the warrant of each commissioner.

Amended by Acts 1950, No. 60, §1; Acts 1952, No. 171, §1; Acts 1959, No. 117, §1; Acts 1965, No. 14, §1; Acts 1970, No. 417, §1; Acts 1979, No. 236, §6, eff. Sept. 1, 1979; Acts 1980, No. 376, §6, eff. Sept. 1, 1980; Acts 1981, No. 636, §9, eff. Sept. 1, 1981; Acts 1997, No. 466, §1.
