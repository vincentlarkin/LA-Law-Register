§200.11.  Other automobile laws applicable
Nothing in R.S. 45:200.1-45:200.15 shall exempt any person owning or operating any public carrier vehicle from complying with all applicable laws and municipal and parochial ordinances relating to the ownership, registration and operation of automobiles in this state.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 404, §1.
