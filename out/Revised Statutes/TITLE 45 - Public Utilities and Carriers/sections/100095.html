§787.  Stipulations for shorter prescription or notice to be void

All printed or written stipulations contrary to R.S. 45:786 attempting to provide a shorter prescription period, or attempting to limit liability to cases in which written demand has been made within a shorter period than four months after the telegram is filed with the company for transmission are void.
