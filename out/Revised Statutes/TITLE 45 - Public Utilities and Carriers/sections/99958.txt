§200.14.  Enforcement
The provisions of R.S. 45:200.1-45:200.15 may be enforced by appropriate civil remedy in any court of competent jurisdiction by governmental authority or by any person having an interest affected, directly or indirectly, by any violation thereof.
Added by Acts 1962, No. 404, §1.
