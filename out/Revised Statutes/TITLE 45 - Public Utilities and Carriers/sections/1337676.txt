§1604.  Requirement to provide service
No water cooperative shall arbitrarily deny a request for water service to a properly
permitted residential or commercial structure that is located within the cooperative's service
area.  This Section does not prohibit a cooperative from discontinuing water service to a
customer who fails to pay service charges or for other just cause.  Notwithstanding R.S.
45:1603, this Section applies to all water cooperatives.
Acts 2023, No. 360, §1.
