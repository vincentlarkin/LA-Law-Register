§844.6.  Renewal clause; continuation of services

Each company which provides wireless telephone service in this state shall be prohibited from automatically renewing a consumer's service contract. Upon expiration of a wireless telephone service contract, the consumer's service shall continue on a month-to-month basis under the same terms as the original contract. Either party may terminate such contract at any time. Nothing in this Section shall prohibit a party from entering into a new contract.

Acts 1999, No. 1205, §1.
