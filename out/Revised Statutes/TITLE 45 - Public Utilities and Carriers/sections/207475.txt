§844.8.  Wireless telephonic solicitation; rules and regulations
The Louisiana Public Service Commission shall promulgate such rules and regulations which comply with federal regulations that prohibit a telemarketer from making wireless telephonic solicitations.
Acts 2003, No. 988, §1, eff. July 2, 2003; Acts 2003, No. 991, §1.
