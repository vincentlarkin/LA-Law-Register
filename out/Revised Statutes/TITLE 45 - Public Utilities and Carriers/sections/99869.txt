§1202.  Violation of commission's order; exception
The provisions of R.S. 45:1200 and 45:1201 shall not in any manner prevent the commission from proceeding against the railroad company, telephone company, sleeping car company or other person operating railroad, express lines, steamboats or other water craft, telephone or telegraph lines, or sleeping cars for such offense as now provided by law.
