§812.  Agreement of consent to receive calls
A person may give consent to a call made with ADAD equipment when a live operator introduces the call and states an intent to play a recorded message, or when the ADAD has a feature that allows the person to indicate consent.  Any such consent shall apply only to a particular call and shall not constitute prior consent to receive further calls through the use of such ADAD equipment.
Acts 1991, No. 917, §1.
