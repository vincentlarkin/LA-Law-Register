§1190.  Appearance before Interstate Commerce Commission
The commission through any of its commissioners, or its secretary, or by a duly authorized attorney, shall appear before the Interstate Commerce Commission at Washington, D.C., or at any other place where the Interstate Commerce Commission might be holding a session, whenever, in the judgment of the Louisiana Public Service Commission the interests of shippers or consignees in Louisiana may require it.
