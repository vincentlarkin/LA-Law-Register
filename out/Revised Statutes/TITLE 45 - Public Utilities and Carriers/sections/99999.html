§322.  Copies of railroad's books and records as evidence

Copies of all books and records kept by the several railroad companies in Louisiana, including extracts from the stock books and minutes of the proceedings of the directors, certified by the secretaries of said companies, under the seal of the company, shall be received in all the courts of Louisiana as evidence in place of the originals.
