CHAPTER 8-M. INTERNET ACCESS AT PUBLIC AIRPORTS
§844.81. Internet access; public airports
Any public airport may provide free access to broadband internet, as defined by the
Federal Communications Commission, solely to patrons at the airport.  Such broadband
internet shall be procured in accordance with applicable state and local procurement laws and
rules.
Acts 2017, No. 322, §1.
