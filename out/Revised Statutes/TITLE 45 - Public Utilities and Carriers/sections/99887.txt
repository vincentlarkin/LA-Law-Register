§1352.  Defamatory statements by or on behalf of, or in opposition to, candidate for public office
In no event, shall any owner, licensee or operator, or the agents or employees of any such owner, licensee or operator of such a station or network of stations be held liable for damages for any defamatory statement uttered over the facilities of such station or network by or on behalf of, or in opposition to, any candidate for public office.
Acts 1950, No. 468, §2.
