§814.  Use of access lines prohibited

It shall be unlawful for any person making use of a telephone access line to use, to employ or direct another person to use, or to contract for the use of ADAD equipment or the United States mail for the purpose of soliciting any person to call such telephone access line.

Acts 1991, No. 917, §1.
