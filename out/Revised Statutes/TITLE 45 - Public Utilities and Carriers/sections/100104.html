§796.  Design of systems

All systems shall be designed to meet the requirements of each community and public agency served by the system.  Every system, whether basic or sophisticated, shall be designed to have the capability of utilizing at least three of the four methods specified in Paragraphs (3) through (6) of R.S. 45:792, in response to emergency calls.

Added by Acts 1974, No. 94, §1.
