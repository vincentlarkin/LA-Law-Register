§69.  Telephone, telegraph and electric lines
Corporations, described in R.S. 45:64, have the right to construct and maintain along the route of such canal or canals telephone and telegraph lines, and to erect poles for the transmission of electric power for all uses incident to and connected with the operations and purposes of these canals, and hydro-electric line or system.
