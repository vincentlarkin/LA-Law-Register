§844.3.  Violations; penalties
The commission shall investigate any complaints received concerning violations of this Chapter.  If, after investigating any complaint, the commission finds that there has been a violation of this Chapter, the commission may impose a civil penalty not to exceed five hundred dollars for each violation against the telephone solicitor.
Acts 1999, No. 518, §1.
