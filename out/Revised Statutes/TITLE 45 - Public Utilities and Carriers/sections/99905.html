§146.  Rules and regulations

The Public Service Commission shall promulgate rules and regulations requiring owners or operators of overhead electric utility lines to promote public awareness of the requirements of this Chapter, including but not limited to annual inserts in all customers' billing statements.

Acts 2001, No. 176, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2002.
