§1195.  Contest of acts of commission; effect of rescission or alteration of order
If the commission rescinds the order complained of, the suit shall be dismissed; if it alters, amends or modifies the order, the altered, modified, or amended order shall take the place of the original order complained of and judgment shall be rendered thereon, as though made by the commission in the first instance.  If the original order is not rescinded or changed by the commission, judgment shall be rendered by the court upon the original order.
