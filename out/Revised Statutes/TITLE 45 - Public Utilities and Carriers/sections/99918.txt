§167.  Name and address of operator and certificate or permit number to be displayed
There shall be displayed upon every vehicle operated intrastate under the authority of R.S. 45:161 through 172 the name and address of the operator and his certificate or permit number in such a manner as to be plainly discernible.
Acts 2008, No. 724, §2, eff. July 6, 2008.
