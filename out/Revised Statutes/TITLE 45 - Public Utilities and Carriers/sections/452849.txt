§1327.  Status of corporation
The corporation, any issuer, and any financing party shall not be considered a utility
or person providing electric service or natural gas service by virtue of engaging in the
transactions described in this Part.  The corporation is not an agent of any utility.
Acts 2007, No. 55, §3, eff. June 18, 2007; Acts 2021, No. 293, §4, eff. June 14, 2021.
