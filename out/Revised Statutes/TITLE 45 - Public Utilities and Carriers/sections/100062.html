CHAPTER 7.  STREET RAILROADS

PART I.  RIGHT OF WAY AND ROADBED

§671.  Acquisition of railroad property by street railway company

Any railroad company, domestic or foreign, may sell, lease, or grant the use of any portion of its railroad, property or franchise or both, as may be situated within the limits of any parish or municipality to any street railway company existing under the laws of this state and carrying passengers under municipal franchises.
