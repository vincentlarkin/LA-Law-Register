§1097.1.  Freight loss or damage; time for settlement
Motor carriers shall settle, either by payment or by denial of liability, any written notice or claim for damages to goods resulting from transporting property for compensation or hire within sixty days after the filing of written notice or claim.
Acts 1972, No. 70, §2.
