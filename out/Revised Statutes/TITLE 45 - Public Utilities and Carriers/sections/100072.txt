§694.  Sandboxes; violation of law
All officers and directors of railway companies, refusing or neglecting to comply with the provisions of R.S. 45:693 shall be fined not less than one hundred dollars nor more than five hundred dollars, or imprisoned for not less than thirty days nor more than ninety days.
