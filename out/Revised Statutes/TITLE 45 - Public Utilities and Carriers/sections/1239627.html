SUBPART C. ADDITIONAL POWERS

§1343. Corporation participation

            The corporation may, in each instance, subject to the prior authorization of the commission, participate in financing transactions contemplated by the Louisiana Electric Utility Storm Recovery Securitization Act, R.S. 45:1226 through 1240.

            Acts 2021, No. 293, §3, eff. June 14, 2021.
