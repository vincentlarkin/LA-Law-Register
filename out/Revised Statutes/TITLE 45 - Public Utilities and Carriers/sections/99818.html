§1164.13.  Promulgation of elections results

The governing authority of the political subdivision calling the election shall promulgate the result by resolution or ordinance adopted at its first regular meeting after the election and shall publish it in the official journal of such political subdivision.

Added by Acts 1975, No. 328, §2.
