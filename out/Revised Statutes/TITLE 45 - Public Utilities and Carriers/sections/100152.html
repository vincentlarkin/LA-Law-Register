CHAPTER 8-G.  WIRELESS TELEPHONE CONTRACTS

§844.5.  Required information

Prior to the execution of a written contract for wireless telephone service, each company which provides such service within this state shall provide to a consumer the terms of the contract, in writing.  Such information shall include but not be limited to the length of the contract.  Each executed contract shall include a provision indicating that the consumer has received the information required by this Section.

Acts 1999, No. 1205, §1.
