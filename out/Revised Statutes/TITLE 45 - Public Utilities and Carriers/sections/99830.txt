§1166.2.  Cramming
A.  For purposes of this Part, the term "cramming" shall be defined as the inclusion of any charges on a subscriber's telephone bill that the subscriber did not authorize.
B.  The Louisiana Public Service Commission shall adopt rules and regulations including but not limited to the adoption of fines and sanctions prohibiting any telecommunications service provider or any person acting on behalf of any telecommunications service provider from engaging in the billing and collecting of cramming.
Acts 1999, No. 410, §1.
