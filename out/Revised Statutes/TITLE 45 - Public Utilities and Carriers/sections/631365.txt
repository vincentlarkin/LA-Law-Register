§562.  Authority to inspect railroad facility
A.  In performing an inspection in accordance with this Section, the commission shall not require a railroad facility owner or operator to alter or cease rail operations.
B.  Any inspection, investigation, or surveillance performed on the site of a manufacturing facility shall be performed in compliance with the safety rules or regulations of the facility, including security clearance at the front gate if appropriate.
Acts 2008, No. 753, §1.
