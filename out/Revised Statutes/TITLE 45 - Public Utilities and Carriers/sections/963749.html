§201.11. Violations

            Any person who shall operate a vehicle upon any street in violation of R.S. 45:201.6 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor, and shall be fined not less than one hundred dollars nor more than one thousand dollars, or imprisoned for not less than sixty days nor more than one year, or both.

            Acts 2015, No. 266, §1, eff. June 29, 2015.
