§620.  Laws and regulations applicable

Railroad companies availing themselves of the provisions of R.S. 45:613 through 45:619 shall maintain a domicile and main and general office in Louisiana and shall be subject to the control and regulation of the laws of Louisiana and the Louisiana Public Service Commission.
