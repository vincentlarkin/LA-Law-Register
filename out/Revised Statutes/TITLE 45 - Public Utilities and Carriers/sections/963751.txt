§201.12.  Construction
The legislature hereby declares that R.S. 45:201.3 through 201.9 are remedial in
nature and shall be construed liberally in favor of the public in general, and the users of
transportation network company services in particular, and to exact strict compliance by
transportation network companies and their participating drivers with the requirements of
R.S. 45:201.5 through 201.9.
Acts 2015, No. 266, §1, eff. June 29, 2015.
