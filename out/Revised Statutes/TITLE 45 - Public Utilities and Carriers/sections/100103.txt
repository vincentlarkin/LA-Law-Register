§795.  Primary emergency telephone number; 911
The digits 911 shall be the primary emergency telephone number within the system, but a public agency or public safety agency may maintain a separate secondary backup number, and shall maintain a separate number for non-emergency telephone calls.
Added by Acts 1974, No. 94, §1.
