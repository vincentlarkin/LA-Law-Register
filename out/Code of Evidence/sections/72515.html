Art. 805.  Hearsay within hearsay

Hearsay included within hearsay is not excluded under the hearsay rule if each part of the combined statements conforms with an exception to the hearsay rule provided by legislation.

Acts 1988, No. 515, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1989.
