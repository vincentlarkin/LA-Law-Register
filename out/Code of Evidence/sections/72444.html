Art. 1104.  State v. Prieur; pretrial; burden of proof

The burden of proof in a pretrial hearing held in accordance with State v. Prieur, 277 So.2d 126 (La. 1973), shall be identical to the burden of proof required by Federal Rules of Evidence Article IV, Rule 404.

Acts 1994, 3rd Ex. Sess., No. 51, §2.
