Art 610.  Religious beliefs or opinions
Except as provided in Article 613, evidence of the beliefs or opinions of a witness on matters of religion is not admissible for the sole purpose of showing that by reason of their nature his credibility is impaired or enhanced.
Acts 1988, No. 515, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1989.
