Art. 3396.20.  Removal of succession representative and termination of independent administration

The court on motion of any interested person, after a contradictory hearing, may remove an independent administrator for any of the reasons provided in Book VI for which a succession representative may be removed from office.  In addition, the court on motion of any interested person, after a contradictory hearing, may for good cause order that the letters of independent administration be withdrawn and that the succession thereafter be administered under the procedures set forth elsewhere in Book VI, other than those contained in this Chapter.

Acts 2001, No. 974, §1.
