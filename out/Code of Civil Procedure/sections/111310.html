Art. 1795. Jury request to review testimony

            If the jury, after retiring for deliberation, requests a review of certain testimony, they shall be conducted to the courtroom. After giving notice to the parties, the court may have the requested testimony read to the jury.

            Acts 1983, No. 534, §7; Acts 2021, No. 259, §2.
