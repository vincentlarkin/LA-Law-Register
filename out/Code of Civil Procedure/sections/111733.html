SECTION 3.  JUDGMENT OF POSSESSION

Art. 3381.  Judgment of possession

A judgment of possession shall be rendered and signed as provided in Article 3061.  The judgment shall be rendered and signed only after a hearing contradictory with the succession representative, unless he joins in the petition, in which event the judgment shall be rendered and signed immediately.
