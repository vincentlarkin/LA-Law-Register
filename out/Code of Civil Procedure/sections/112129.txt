Art. 4922.  Notice of judgment; justice of the peace courts; district courts with concurrent
jurisdiction
Notice of the signing of any final judgment shall be given as required by Article
1913, except that if the party is personally served with the judgment in open court, no further
notice shall be required.
Acts 1986, No. 156, §1; Acts 2001, No. 512, §1; Acts 2015, No. 424, §1.
