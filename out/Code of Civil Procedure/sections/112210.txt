Art. 684.  Interdict
A.  A person fully interdicted or a person whose limited interdiction specifically
restricts the procedural capacity to sue does not have the procedural capacity to sue.
B.  Except as otherwise provided in Articles 4431, 4554, and 4566, the curator is the
proper plaintiff to sue to enforce a right of a person fully interdicted or a person whose
limited interdiction specifically restricts the procedural capacity to sue.
Acts 2025, No. 250, §3.
