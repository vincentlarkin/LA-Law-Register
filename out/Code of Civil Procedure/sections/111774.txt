TITLE VI
PARTITION OF SUCCESSIONS
Art. 3461.  Venue; procedure
The petition for the partition of a succession shall be filed in the succession proceeding, as provided in Article 81(2).
In all other respects and except when manifestly inapplicable, the procedure for partitioning a succession is governed by the provisions of Articles 4601 through 4614.
