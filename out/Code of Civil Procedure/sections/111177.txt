Art. 1352.  Restrictions on subpoena
A witness, whether a party or not, who resides or is employed in this state may be
subpoenaed to attend a trial or hearing wherever held in this state.  No subpoena shall issue
to compel the attendance of such a witness unless the provisions of R.S. 13:3661 are
complied with.
Amended by Acts 1961, No. 23, §1; Acts 2021, No. 259, §2.
