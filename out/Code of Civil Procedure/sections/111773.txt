Art. 3443.  Sale of succession property; publication of notice of sale
A. Notice of the public sale of property, movable or immovable, by the
succession representative of a small succession shall be published once and only in
the parish where the succession is pending, and the property shall be sold no less than
ten days nor more than fifteen days after publication.
B. Notice of the application of the succession representative of a small
succession to sell succession property, movable or immovable, at private sale shall
be published once and only in the parish where the succession proceeding is pending
and shall state that any opposition to the proposed sale shall be filed within ten days
of the date of publication.
Acts 2024, No. 90, §1.
