Art. 1459.  Interrogatories to parties; scope; use at trial

Interrogatories may relate to any matters which can be inquired into under Articles 1422 through 1425, and the answers may be used at trial to the extent permitted by rules of evidence.

Acts 1976, No. 574, §1.
