Art. 2165.  Appeals deemed abandoned

An appeal is abandoned when the parties fail to take any step in its prosecution or disposition for the period provided in the rules of the appellate court.

Amended by Acts 1966, No. 36, §1; Acts 1982, No. 186, §2.
