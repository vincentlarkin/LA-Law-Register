Art. 1436.1.  Depositions by telephone
If agreed upon by every party to an action or if ordered by the court, a
deposition may be taken by telephone or other remote electronic means.
Acts 1986, No. 205, §1; Acts 2003, No. 545, §1; Acts 2024, No. 371,
§1.
