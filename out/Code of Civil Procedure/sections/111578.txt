Art. 2952.  Descriptive list of property, if no inventory
A.  If no inventory of the property left by the deceased has been taken, any heir,
legatee, or other interested party shall file in the succession proceeding a detailed descriptive
list, sworn to and subscribed by him, of all items of property composing the succession of
the deceased, stating the actual cash value of each item at the time of the death of the
deceased.
B.  The detailed descriptive list shall be sealed upon the request of an heir or legatee.
C.  If the detailed descriptive list is sealed, a copy shall be provided to the decedent's
universal successors and surviving spouse.  Upon motion of any successor, surviving spouse,
or creditor of the estate, the court may furnish relevant information contained in the detailed
descriptive list regarding assets and liabilities of the estate.
Acts 2020, No. 19, §2.
