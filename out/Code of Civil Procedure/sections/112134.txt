CHAPTER 3.  APPEALS FROM CITY AND PARISH COURTS
Art. 5001.  Appeals from city and parish courts
A.  An appeal from a judgment rendered by a parish court or by a city court shall be
taken to the court of appeal.
B.  Appeal shall be on the record and shall be taken in the same manner as an appeal
from the district court.
Acts 1986, No. 156, §1; Acts 2001, No. 1134, §1; Acts 2021, No. 259, §2.
