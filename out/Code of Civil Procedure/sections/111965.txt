Art. 4274.  Compensation of tutor
The court shall allow the tutor reasonable compensation for his services annually, which shall not exceed ten percent of the annual revenues of the minor's property, unless increased by the court upon proper showing that this would be inadequate.
Amended by Acts 1966, No. 36, §1.
