Art. 695.  Absent or mentally incompetent managing spouse
If the managing spouse with respect to the community right sought to be enforced is an absentee or a mental incompetent, then the other spouse is the proper plaintiff to enforce that community right.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 711, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.
