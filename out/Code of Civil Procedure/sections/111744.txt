Art. 3396.13.  Testamentary prohibition of independent administration
A testator may expressly provide that no independent administration of his estate may be allowed.  In such case, his estate, if administered, shall be administered in accordance with the other provisions of Book VI.
Acts 2001, No. 974, §1.
