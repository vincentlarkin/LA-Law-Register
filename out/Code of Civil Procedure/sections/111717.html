Art. 3332.  Final account

A.  A succession representative may file a final account of his administration at any time after homologation of the final tableau of distribution and the payment of all estate debts and legacies as set forth in the tableau.

B.  The court shall order the filing of a final account upon the application of an heir or residuary legatee who has been sent into possession or upon the rendition of a judgment ordering the removal of a succession representative.

Acts 1997, No. 1421, §3, eff. July 1, 1999.
