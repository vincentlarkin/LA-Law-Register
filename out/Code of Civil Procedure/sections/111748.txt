Art. 3396.17.  Accounting
An independent administrator is not required to file an interim accounting.  Nevertheless, any person interested in the estate may demand an annual accounting from the independent administrator as provided in Article 3331.  Further, the court on application of any interested person may require an independent administrator to furnish accountings at more frequent intervals.
Acts 2001, No. 974, §1.
