Art. 690.  Domestic corporation;  insurer; limited liability company
Except as otherwise provided in Articles 692 and 693, a domestic corporation, a domestic insurer, and a domestic limited liability company has the procedural capacity to sue to enforce its rights in the corporate or company name.
Acts 1999, No. 145, §2.
