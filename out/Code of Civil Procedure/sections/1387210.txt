SECTION 4. RETENTION OF TESTAMENTS
Art. 2911. Retention of testaments
The clerk of court shall retain in perpetuity the original of a testament that is probated or
ordered to be filed and executed. Until the order probating the testament or ordering the
testament to be filed and executed becomes final and definitive, the clerk of court shall also
retain the originals of all other testaments filed in accordance with Article 2853.
Acts 2024, No. 501, §1.
