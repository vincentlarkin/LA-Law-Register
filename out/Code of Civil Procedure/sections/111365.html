Art. 1979.  Summary decision on motion; maximum delays

The court shall decide on a motion for a new trial within ten days from the time it is submitted for decision.  The time may be extended for a specified period upon the written consent or stipulation of record by the attorneys representing all parties.  When the court grants a motion for a new trial, it shall specify each of its reasons in the order.

Acts 2013, No. 78, §1.
