CHAPTER 12.  SMALL TUTORSHIPS
Art. 4461.  Small tutorship defined
For the purposes of this Chapter, a small tutorship is the tutorship of a minor whose property in Louisiana has a gross value of fifty thousand dollars or less.
Amended by Acts 1976, No. 437, §1; Acts 1979, No. 71, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980; Acts 2013, No. 118, §1, eff. June 5, 2013.
