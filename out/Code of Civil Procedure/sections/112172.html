CHAPTER 5. PAYMENT OF STATE INHERITANCE TAXES

Art. 5181. Privilege of proceeding without prior payment of costs

            A. Except as provided in Paragraph B of this Article, an individual who is unable to pay the costs of court because of poverty and lack of means may prosecute or defend a judicial proceeding, including a succession proceeding, in any trial or appellate court without paying the costs in advance or as they accrue or furnishing security therefor.

            B.
