Art. 3396.15.  Rights, powers, and duties; performance without court authority

Except as expressly provided otherwise in this Chapter, an independent administrator shall have all the rights, powers, authorities, privileges, and duties of a succession representative provided in Chapters 4 through 12 of this Title, but without the necessity of delay for objection, or application to, or any action in or by, the court.

Acts 2001, No. 974, §1.
