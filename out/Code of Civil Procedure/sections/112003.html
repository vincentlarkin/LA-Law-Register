Art. 4542.  Venue

Venue for an interdiction proceeding is the parish where the defendant is domiciled, where he resides if he has no domicile in this state, or where he is physically present if he has no residence in this state.

Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 25, §3, eff. July 1, 2001.
