Art. 743.  Absent or mentally incompetent managing spouse
If the managing spouse with respect to the obligation sought to be enforced against community property is an absentee or a mental incompetent, then the other spouse is the proper defendant in a suit to enforce that obligation.
Added by Acts 1979, No. 711, §4, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.
