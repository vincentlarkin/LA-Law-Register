TITLE II
REAL ACTIONS
CHAPTER 1.  ACTIONS TO DETERMINE
OWNERSHIP OR POSSESSION
Art. 3651.  Petitory action
The petitory action is one brought by a person who claims the ownership of, but who
does not have the right to possess, immovable property or a real right therein, against another
who is in possession or who claims the ownership thereof adversely, to obtain judgment
recognizing the plaintiff's ownership.
Amended by Acts 1981, No. 256, §1; Acts 2023, No. 421, §2.
