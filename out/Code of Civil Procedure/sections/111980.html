Art. 4363.  Security instead of mortgage

Instead of the mortgage provided in Article 4362, the parent may furnish security as provided in Article 4132 or a special mortgage as provided in Article 4133.  The amount of the security or special mortgage shall be equal to the price of the adjudication.
