Art. 4629. Articles applicable to partition by licitation or private sale

            Article 4603, the first paragraph of Article 4605, and Articles 4607 and 4614 are applicable to a partition by licitation or a partition by private sale under the provisions of this Chapter.

            Acts 2020, No. 281, §2, eff. June 11, 2020.
