Art. 3396.1.  Scope
Upon qualification of a succession representative and compliance with the provisions
of this Chapter, the clerk shall issue letters of independent administration or letters of
independent executorship, as appropriate, certifying that the independent administrator has
been duly qualified.
Acts 2001, No. 974, §1; Acts 2020, No. 107, §1, eff. June 9, 2020.
