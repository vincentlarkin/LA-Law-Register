CHAPTER 3.  RECUSAL OF JUDGES; APPOINTMENT
OF JUDGES AD HOC
Art. 4861.  Recusal of judges
A parish court or city court judge or justice of the peace may recuse himself or be
recused for the same reasons and on the same grounds as provided in Article 151.
Acts 1979, No. 46, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 1980; Acts 2021, No. 143, §1.
