CHAPTER 3.  JUDICIAL PROCEEDINGS
Art. 3441.  Acceptance without administration; procedure
Except as otherwise provided by law, all of the rules applicable to the judicial
opening of a succession, and its acceptance by the heirs or legatees without an
administration, apply to the small succession.
