Art. 4303.  Perishable property; crops
Upon the petition of the tutor as provided in Article 4321 or 4341, the court may order the immediate sale of perishable property and growing crops either at public auction or private sale, without appraisal, and without advertisement, or with such advertisement as the court may direct.
