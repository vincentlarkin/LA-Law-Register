Art. 4464.  Court costs

In proceedings under this Chapter, court costs shall be one-half of the court costs in similar proceedings in larger tutorships, provided that the minimum costs in any case shall be one hundred dollars.

Acts 2013, No. 118, §1, eff. June 5, 2013.
