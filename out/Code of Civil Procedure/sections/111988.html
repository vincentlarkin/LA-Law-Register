Art. 4397.  Deceased or interdicted tutor

If a tutor dies, an account of his administration may be filed by his succession representative or heirs.  If a tutor is interdicted, such an account may be filed by his curator.

The court shall order the filing of such an account in either case, on the petition of an interested person.
