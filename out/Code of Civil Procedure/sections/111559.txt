Art. 2882.  Ex parte probate if objection
An objection to the ex parte probate of a testament may be presented in an opposition.
The opposition shall comply with the provisions of Article 2902 and shall be filed.
Amended by Acts 1968 No. 130, §1; Acts 2025, No. 34, §1.
