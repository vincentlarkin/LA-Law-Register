Art. 691.  Foreign corporation; foreign limited liability company; foreign or alien insurance corporation
Except as otherwise provided in Articles 692 and 693, a foreign corporation, a foreign or alien insurer, and a foreign limited liability company has the procedural capacity to sue to enforce its rights in the corporate or company name.
Acts 1999, No. 145, §2.
