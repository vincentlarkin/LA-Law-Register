CHAPTER 3.  MANDAMUS
Art. 3861.  Definition
Mandamus is a writ directing a public officer, a corporation or an officer thereof, or
a limited liability company or a member or manager thereof, to perform any of the duties set
forth in Articles 3863 and 3864.
Acts 2017, No. 419, §1.
