Art. 4548.  Burden of proof

The petitioner in an interdiction proceeding bears the burden of proof by clear and convincing evidence.

Amended by Acts 1961, No. 23, §1; Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 25, §3, eff. July 1, 2001; Acts 2003, No. 1008, §2.
