Art. 4568. Removal of curator or undercurator

            On motion of any interested person, or on its own motion, the court may remove a curator or undercurator from office for good cause. Good cause may include but not be limited to a violation of Article 4566(J). Unless otherwise ordered by the court, removal of the curator or undercurator by the court is effective upon qualification of the appointed successor.

            Acts 2000, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 25, §3, eff. July 1, 2001; Acts 2016, No. 110, §2, eff. May 19, 2016.
